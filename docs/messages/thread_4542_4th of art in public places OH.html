Reaching for the Stars
Placed 2/18/02
Westerville, Ohio Franklin County
Difficulty: 1
Micro box

The 4th box placed in the series: Art in Public
places. To find the area where to look for these
letterboxes all you have to do is go to
www.sculpturecenter.org/oosi/oo.asp. Once here you go
to searching oosi, you can either enter the artist
name or the name of the letterbox title under advance
query. This will take you to the site about this
sculpture and where it is located. Once you find out
where to head then read the clues to find the
letterbox.

Kenneth S. Foltz finished this piece in 1990 and it
was placed in a park honoring Americas Astronauts.
There are three parks in this area and over on
Cleveland Avenue is Knights Ice Cream, stop and try
one of their Knight sticks.

Letterbox clues: Standing looking at the front of the
sculpture were the inscription starts: America I
took star.. Stand in the second ring of bricks
surrounding the sculpture. Go 28 paces (1paces=2
steps) at 310 degrees. Turn and look to your rights.
About two steps away is a stump. The mini box is in
the stump. Be careful the middle is completely gone.
You need to take your own pen and stamp pad. Hope you
enjoy the area.
