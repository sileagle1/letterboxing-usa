We're not upstate NY, we're not western NY, or Mid Atlantic. Many of us
live in or near an urban area. If you live in one of the five boroughs
of New York City, or Westchester, Rockland, Suffolk, Orange, Nassau, or
Putnam County, this will be a small, private group for those who love
letterboxing and want to perhaps meet and greet other boxers nearby for
exchanges or boxing in this area or others. Maybe you are looking for
boxing buddies? Maybe you just want to talk about your favorite hobby?
Join us.
