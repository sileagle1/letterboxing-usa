We had a lovely time hiking and finding A Holiday Puzzle on Sunday. It
hasn't had too many visitors, which is a shame because the hike was just right for
a winter walk, and the stamps were terrific! We noticed that Knitwit Mom and
Puppy Boy had been there earlier in the day. As we were looking for the last
box, checking out every downed tree to see if it had fungi and twists, someone
called out to us, "If you're looking for what I think you're looking for,
it's farther down the trail." We asked if by any chance she was a nitwit? (Boy,
it would have been rude if she weren't!) It was Knitwit Mom and Puppy Boy!
We had fun exchanging stamps and holiday greetings. Happy New Year to all! D
and Shoo
