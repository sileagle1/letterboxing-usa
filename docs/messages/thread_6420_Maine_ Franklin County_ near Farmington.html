Center Hill Picnic Area
Weld, Maine  in Franklin County  near Farmington
Easy
under a mile
planted by :Pine Tree on July 11, 2002


Follow rte 156 into Weld Maine in the center of town at the intersection turn right on to Center Hill road and proceed to the Center Hill Picinic area which is part of Mt. Blue State Park ....the road turns to dirt and the park is on the left.

Park in the parking lot and enjoy the view.
Follow the Interpretive Tour( Hike)
Take the stone steps to the scenic trial  sign  near the shelter.
Continue past the shelter.
Follow the numbered self tour of the nature trail.
Past #1, #2.................................
Follow signs to Overlook Ledges and  take in the view.
Proceed back on the trail and head towards #6 info stop.
There are 2 benches there and 2 picnic tables here too.
#7 is a bog..........keep going.
After #8 be sure to discover the bench at the Mt. Blue Scenic area.
Your treasure is hidden behind the fallen tree directly to the right of #9 info station.
Stamp quietly
continue on to # 10 and #11 down towards the parking lot.
