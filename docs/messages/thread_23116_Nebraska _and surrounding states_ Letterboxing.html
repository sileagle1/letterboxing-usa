The Letterboxing Nebraska website (http://www.letterboxingne.com/
) was
recently updated to ensure all pages are working properly. Not all
boxes have been listed yet; as the webmaster of the site, this is an
ongoing process for me. However, anyone with a registered Wiki username
and password can add listings for letterboxes.

discussion forum for Nebraska letterboxers, and letterboxingne.com now
also offers free 2 GB e-mail addresses.

Anyone who wants one of the free @letterboxingne.com e-mail accounts can
learn more about it and get an address here:
