Hello Jay -

The new Southern New England Region pages look great. I organize my
personal clue stash in a similar manner (by town); it makes it easier to
plan multi-box outings. I looks as though others have tried out the
links. I, however, found a few discrepancies in the clues that I though
I'd bring to your attention. I hope you don;t think I'm being picky
because I really appreciate the effort you and the other webmasters put
in to keep us all up to date and informed. "The Good Earth" and "Froggy
Went a Courtin'" boxes are listed as being in Mansfield, Rhode Island
instead of Connecticut. Also when we went looking for the "Turtle
Creek" box we noticed that the right hand turn is onto Watrous Point
Road (not Waltrons); you may want to verify this and not just take my
word.

On another note, I just wanted to let you know that we enjoyed your
Celestial Motion boxes at Beebe Pond. Half of the "Elements of Hiking"
asked my daughter, Meg and I to join them in looking for these boxes.
Our little group was the first to find them. (We were wondering whether
you were clever enough to align the location of the boxes!) Meg and I
also got to meet Dan and Melissa and make our first exchanges. We also
found the "Letter from a Traitor" boxes and the Avery Light box. Look
forward to meeting you (and all) at the get together on Sunday.
