* How I discovered letterboxing !

From: capecodletterboxer (capecodletterboxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-17 11:33:49 UTC

Ackletterboxer called me and told me she read this artical in a
Nantucket mag. After going to the LBNA web site and talking with
her, I was starting to get it. After making a trip to nantucket in
August I went with her to find her first box. (great point light
house) and got hooked. came home and went and found every box close
to home. can you say obsessed? i've since carved 8 stampes, planted
5 boxes and have found 23. can't wait to go down cape today for a
great day of boxing. I have always took my kids for nature walks on
trails and whatnot. its soo cool to know we can still hike but now
we can find treasure along the way...and i've chatting with some
great people (boxers i've meet) too.
