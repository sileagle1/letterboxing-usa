Chicken-counters in Victoria and new hitch-hiker

From: Matthew_Colvin (mac47@cornell.edu) | Date: 2002-07-18 15:07:16 UTC
At least, 4 out of 5 of us are in Victoria, B.C. for a few weeks.

We found the Bear and Berry letterbox on the Galloping Goose
yesterday. A bit prickly -- watch out for stinging nettles as well as
berry bushes! The box was in good shape except for the notebook -- the
pages that had been previously stamped were all coming out. :-( If it
is convenient, whoever placed that box might want to replace the book
with a more durable one.

We left a new hitch-hiker there: Rambling Rose. Please email us
mac47@cornell.edu if you take her somewhere else! I will set up a
web-page to track her progress when I get home to Ithaca. It will be
interesting to see how far she goes.

Looking forward to finding some more Victoria letterboxes and maybe
placing a few while we're here.

Sora Colvin,
with kiddos but without dh :-( in beautiful Victoria
