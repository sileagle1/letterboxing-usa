From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2003-10-09 22:45:21 UTC
*nod* This box has NOTHING to do with Martha. :)

The Letterbox in Your Letterbox (also known as Autumn and Mark's
postal letterbox) had a printed distribution list enclosed with the
box. The last person that I know that had the box was schizokeet
from VA. That was in August, and I haven't heard any word since
then. I did email her to ask if she had sent it along, and to whom.

To correct any mis-conceptions, there was no "order" to the list.
The idea was that you could send to anyone on the list, and when
you'd get it - and from whom - would be part of the mystery.

Unfortunately, it means that I now don't have a clue who has it.

Schizokeet if you're out there - please let me know when you sent it,
and to whom.
Thanks,
Autumn
