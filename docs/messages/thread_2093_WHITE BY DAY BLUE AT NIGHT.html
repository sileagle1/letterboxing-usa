PLACED BY DON,3-18-01,LONG BEACH,LOS ANGELES COUNTY,CALIF.
 AT THE FOOT OF A PINE ENTER THE CUL DE SAC GUARDED BY LIONS. PARKING IS LIMITED HERE.MORE PARKING CAN BE FOUND EAST OF SHORELINE VILLAGE. FROM THE FOOT OF PINE WALK SOUTH TO THE BRICK PATH.PASS THE FISH POND ON YOUR RIGHT.WHEN YOU HAVE A CHOICE TAKE THE BOARDWALK EAST. PASS THE SHIPS WHEEL AT DUE NORTH AND THE CANOE BENCH AT DUE SOUTH. STOP AT THE L SHAPED WOODEN BENCH. SIT ON THE WEST END AND FACE WEST. AT THE JUNCTION OF THE BOARDWALK AND THE BOARDS UNDER THE BENCH(APPROX. 12 INCHES FROM YOUR LEFT FOOT) YOU WILL FIND THE LETTERBOX HIDDEN IN THE CORNER UNDER THE BOARDWALK.THE BEST TIME TO SEARCH FOR THIS BOX WOULD BE AT NIGHT. DO NOT TRY TO LOOK FOR IT DURING GRAN PRIX WEEKEND!!!!!!
