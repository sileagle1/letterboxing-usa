Kite Mystery Letterbox

Somewhere in Connecticut

Number of boxes: 2

Placed by sadie & russ

March 20, 2003

 

The Kite Club met on a blustery day at one of their favorite locations.  A gust of wind snapped my kite string and my kite dove out of sight.  Where did it land?

 

A friend called, “Jimmie, I located your kite.  From your front sidewalk, walk west to the Handicapped Sitting Area sign.  Then head in a northerly direction about Savinty steps around the base of the Rock.  Your kite is caught in some rocks right behind the three- sister tree.”
