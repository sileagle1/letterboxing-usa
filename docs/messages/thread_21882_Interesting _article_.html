From: Rick Simpson (simpson.rick@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-05-04 12:36:11 UTC-07:00
@ funhog1, Here, here!

I've heard there's an amendment to the bill which would place a "sin
tax" on letterboxing and geocaching in addition to restricting the
activities. It's being called the "Prevention of Invaders at Grampa's
Grave" amendment.

Since LB and GC are subversive, secret groups, the plan is to apply
the "PIGG" tax to stamping supplies and GPS devices. The amendment is
in committee debate right now, but is expect to pass. The "PIGG" tax
is expected to increase the cost of stamping supplies by 87 - 106%.

Since the tax is so arbitrary, the Scrapbooking Community and Greeting
Card Hobbyists--fearing the tax's impact on their activities--are now
working aggressively against the tax amendment. They've formed a
political action committee to lobby the SC legislature against the
tax. The PAC is called "Hobbyists for the American Way and
Godliness", "HAWG" for short.
