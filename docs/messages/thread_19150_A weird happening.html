A weird happening

From: dave & diane (vonderinsel@cox.net) | Date: 2004-06-12 07:46:55 UTC-04:00
Dave and I got a very nice letter from a fellow letterboxer.
You've heard us lament over the loss of "A Major Award HH"?
Well, they found the logbook at "Celebrations" down in Mouse World in Florida!
Unfortunately the stamp was not there. Also, it seems the log book for "Celebrations" was not there. They thought perhaps it was geocachers, I do not concur Dr. Watson.
I've been boxing with multi boxers and HH's and keeping all the stamps and logbooks and ink pads straight can be confusing. I beleive that somewhere out there is the Major Award stamp and the Celebrations logbook hitchhiking around the US. A bit like Nomad and Veeger, huh? (Star Trek fan alert)
So, that's my theory.
By the way, we have asked that the logbook be returned to us and I think that Dave is going to carve a replacement stamp - we'll start it off again.
Diane
