100 Mile Wilderness
Monson, Maine
on your way to Moosehead Lake

Planted Aug.6, 2001
by Floater and Floating Feather

EASY

Drive Rt. 15 North. Park in lot 4 1/2 miles outside of Monson-the last
town for Appalachian Trail thru-hikers to resupply before entering the
100 mile wilderness. It is their last trek before summiting Mt. Katahdin
Have No Fear- your destination is only 100 paces(1 pace = 2 steps). Stop
at the base of a lone pine tree at the edge of Spectacle Pond and before
the bridge.
