FL new box: Clay Acorn

From: Mountain Scorpia (mountainscorpia@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-02-10 23:18:13 UTC-05:00
Volusia County
nearest city: DeLand

First find Swamp Owl and Clay Dog, Nos. 1 & 2 of Seven from the Florida
Ancients

NO. 3 - Clay Acorn

There is a large cut log at the beginning of a lazy L boardwalk. At the
far end of the boardwalk, take 15 steps, then right 18 steps to large
straight tree with smooth knees and big feet. The Clay Acorn is planted
behind.

Return to the trail and turn right to continue.
