"Hogg" Letterbox

Rusk, Texas Cherokee County Eastern Texas Pineywoods Region
Jim Hogg State Historical Park

Date of Placement: 1/30/03
Difficulty: Easy
Total Distance to letterbox: About 150 yards
Placed by Baby Bear

Texas History Letterbox Series

The "Hogg" letterbox is dedicated to James Stephen Hogg, the first
native born governor of Texas. The day-use only park is located on
the land of his birth. A scale replica of his dog-run birth home
serves as the museum. The cemetery is the actual family burial
site.
