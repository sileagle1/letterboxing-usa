Re: [LbNA] Re: CA , California- Missing Letterbox in Monrovia?

From: Marc Lebendig (mrl@mrlebendig.com) | Date: 2003-04-26 06:34:10 UTC-07:00
The Descanso Garden series is ours (4 boxes) and we have one at Eaton Canyon
(although we haven't checked on it recently). There is another box at Eaton
Canyon if memory serves, and Don and Gwen have a high-traffic box in
Pasadena as well. These are all listed on letterboxing.org.

Many people list their clues on their own sites, but usually these are
linked from the main site. You might also try Wes Garrison's site, which I
m sure you've read the messages about in recent days.

Good luck!

Threedigs
Marc, Jen, and Emma
