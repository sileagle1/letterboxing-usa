Hi Jay,
Thanks so much for this great series. I was quite shocked to
see no one had been to the first box since August! Was the park
closed or something? I found all six boxes plus the other three
in Haley. I did have to hurry a bit as I didn't start until noon.
Boxes 2 & 5 of Sound View were wet but no damage to the
logs. I used my own ink pad for those two as yours were a bit
waterlogged. Only #5 had visitors since the summer and those
people just stumbled onto the box. The foundation is getting
quite a bit of briar but that is up to the State. The area around #6
is turning into a maze of bike trails. I really needed the compass
to figure out which ones to take.
There looked to be enough clue sheets, especially with one
boxer every six months... How about a box 4a or 5a to break up
the railroad stretch? There is a really nice loop to a bench at the
very top of the cove. It gets you out of the industrial zone for a
while.
I tried to find the Hex box on the way home but gave up after
nearly falling off the cliff in the dark.
You are the DrewClanMan for all your work in the sport.

Regards,
WaldenRun (Geoff)
