Hi Everyone,

I thought this sounded kind of cool so I decided to check it out
(especially since it was kind of a rainy Sunday afternoon). Their
letterboxing chat room was really a lot of fun!

It took me a little while to figure out how to get into it -- MSN
wanted me to choose a nickname but everything I tried was already
taken. Well, I finally got in. I didn't always understand what
they were talking about, but it was still a lot of fun! They have
it every Sunday (evening for them, but afternoon for us). Check it
out if it ever happens that you're not out boxing at this time of
day!
