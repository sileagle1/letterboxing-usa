Okay, some of you may remember that I inadvertently "stole" the stamp from
the Celebration letterbox back in August. I sent it to another letterboxer, who

before I could get there. Unfortunately, it appears she hasn't had a chance
to do it yet. I was back in Orlando for a karate tournament this weekend, and
checked on the box. It was stampless.

So I got a stamp for it--not exactly like the original, but in the same
category, and better than nothing for those who find the box.

And Kat-- If you still have that original stamp, when you get the chance to

original condition again. And if anyone's going after this box, the logbook is very

through the book. I didn't have a spare one to replace it with.

And in other news, we tried to find the box at the Wilderness Lodge, only we
didn't find it. We're 99.9 percent sure we found the right stump, but there
was no letterbox inside it. :-( If anyone's found it recently, I'd love to
know about it!

Then we ran out of time (had to be back at the tournament) so we weren't able
to try for any more boxes.
