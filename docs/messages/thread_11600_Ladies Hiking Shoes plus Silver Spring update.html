From: lizardbuttsfamily (mmebt@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-03-31 06:23:13 UTC
My favorite is Vasque's lightweight boots. I never put on the boots
until I start the hike, and I remove them immediately when the hike
ends. I also bring a change of socks with me for long(5 miles +)
hikes. Sometimes I hike in sneakers, but I always change my shoes
before and after a hike. I also like to take breaks at streams, and
soak the feet in the icy water while basking in the warm sun. Vasque
are a bit $$$$, but your feet will love you for it(as well as the
rest of your body).

Monica

Vasque. Go to their website, www.lowaboots.com. If you don't have an
