It's been 4 months since I found out about letterboxing and this week proves I'm hooked on this new sport. I've been out 3 of the past 4 nights hunting letterboxing and for the past week carving new stamps for a series I'm working on. My two newest finds are Chase-a-Butterfly, Lincoln, RI and Birchwod Farm in W. Wretham, Mass. Both are placed by "Scout" and I ended up stamping in second on both.

Thanks for the new way to keep me on the trail.
Trail Turtle
