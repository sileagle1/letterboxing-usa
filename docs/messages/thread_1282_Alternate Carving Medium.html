In reading through the list I noticed you are interested in finding
another carving medium -- I own small art rubber stamp store in
Essex, CT - don't know if that's too far for you to travel - but I am
now carrying 3 sizes of Staedler Carving Blocks (Marscarve) as I am
trying to promote Lbing to my customer base via my newsletters. One
size is 4x6 so you could get 3 2x4 stamps from one block. I have not
done a lot of carving yet but in my estimation this is not at all
crumbly,is very easy to work with and makes a wonderful impression. I
offer mail order as part of my business services.

Kathleen,

Being a Stamp Professional I hope you can answer this question.

What rubber material is used in laser cutting stamps? I have a laser
engraver at work which I used to carve the red material that came
with my Speed Cut started kit. It worked pretty good, but have not
found that 4x6" block sold by itself.

Erasers really stunk up the lab when I cut my "Locust Shade" stamp...

I really like hand carving the erasers, but I also enjoy the
challenge of graphic design on the computer which can then be turned
into a stamp. Tom Cooch mentioned he found a place that he can send
graphic files which the company then produces a laser cut stamp for
about $10 each....
