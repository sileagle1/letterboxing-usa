Journey of the Saints

Hidden by Mama Bunny of seven bunnies
On 4/12/04
Carved by RTRW

This saint was the youngest of 5 girls. She was her papa's little queen. Her
dog's name was Tom. She entered the convent when she was only 15 and died at
the age of 24. Do you know who she is?

Clues: To begin this series you need to go into the woods, Woods Park that
is!!!! Woods Park is at 401 South 33rd Street. There is another entrance on
O street.
Park in the lot at the end of the indoor tennis courts. Go to the sidewalk
between the picnic tables and the lot. Make a left. continue 'til you come
to the second white dome of the courts. Look to your left and see the lamp
post at the corner of the dome. Count 3 large evergreens. Make a left and
walk between the 3rd and the 4th. Walk to a smaller evergreen. The box is
under wood chips at the tree's base.

Rehide well. Thank you and enjoy. Be sure to put what area you are from when
you stamp in. The kids are interested to see where visitors are from.
