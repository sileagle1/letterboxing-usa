Park at N 39.6940 W 84.1140. You may use a GPS to find this parking
lot in Kettering, Ohio, or use the www.topozone.com website to make a
topographic map of the area at large scale for driving and at small
scale for walking. The topographic map does not show the lake that
has been added to the park.

Once at the park, find the very high spot next to the lake. All the
high school and college cross country races climb this "mount" at
least twice. I have run all over the park many times chasing one or
another of my children as they raced. If you are here when the
ground is white, take care for your life. Sleds come down the hill
at regular intervals.
