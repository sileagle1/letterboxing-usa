"There's Always A Catbird" Hitch-hiker
 
www.psychokiwi.org/letterboxing/catbird.htm
 
Have you noticed how there is always a catbird skulking around in the bushes while
you're letterboxing? We started to wonder whether it was the same catbird each time
- perhaps Sylvester from our back garden? Perhaps he hung onto the roof-rack as we
set off on each new adventure? With that theory in mind, we prepared a special
catbird hitch-hiker so Sylvester could have even more adventures. If you come across
him, please let us know.
 
Sylly started his adventures somewhere in NE CT in November 2002.

The Psychokiwis
Judi, Linton, and The Bunny
www.psychokiwi.org/letterboxing
