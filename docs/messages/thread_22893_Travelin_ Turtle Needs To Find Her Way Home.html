The Journey Begins

Travelin' Turtle is hitchhiking her way across the country. Released in the new
Pennsylvania Postcard 3 box in Spokane, WA, Travelin' Turtle seeks to return to her home in
Eastern Pennsylvania by the good graces of Letterboxers across America.

You can follow her progress on the Travelin' Turtle blog: http://
travelinturtle.blogspot.com. Pictures, sitreps and wellwishers are welcome.

If you find the Travelin' Turtle hanging out in a letterbox near you, please move her
along as quickly as possible to another high-traffic box that is a little bit closer to home.
When you get a chance please update us on her progress by posting to the blog or sending
an email to nattybumppolbna@aol.com.

Bring 'er on home!
