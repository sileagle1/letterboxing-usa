Hi VA Boxers,
About the time that someone in CA posted the start of their
Redwall Series of boxes, I had been working on starting one myself.
The first box (Redwall Abbey)has been hidden and posted in the NoVa
section. I have another one to go out,"Martin the Warrior".
I seem to remember another VA boxer who was interested in
Redwall. I thought it would be fun to do an informal series around
the state. Whoever wants to make a Redwall box, go for it! Just label
it "Redwall Series: (Fill in the blank). We could have multiple boxes
of the same character if desired, just make the name of the box
different.
If you have not read this great series of books, please do!
Adults love them as much as children.
