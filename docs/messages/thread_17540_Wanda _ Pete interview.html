From: (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2004-01-12 19:55:10 UTC-05:00
In a nutshell, I think we letterboxers must be somewhat alike in our
interests yet, there are a few of you out there that make me wonder if I belong. What
would make a person want to hike thousands of miles? Making a goal of 2000 Fs
in a year, who would accomplish something like that? Believe me, I'm nothing
like these people!! But behind these passions are 2 gentle, and quietly
enthusiastic individuals that make me grateful I'm involved in the same pastime.

The Wanda and Pete interview was as interesting as any I've read, maybe more
so since I've been intrigued by their adventures, both together and apart, for
a long time now. I remember visiting Rhode Island and finding a box with a
WandaandPete stamp. WOW, THAT was a score!!

Thanks for the great effort, Pepe. Like Pete said, gatherings are all about
meeting fellow letterboxers. Short of traveling a couple thousand miles for a
face-to-face, we feel we've met Wanda and Pete in this interview,
