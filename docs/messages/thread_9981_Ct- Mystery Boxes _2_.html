Happy New Year To All- Here are Two Not-So-Difficult Mystery Boxes
We are like Butterfly- we want you to get them ;D

Difficulty: EASY
Time: Slow Camel can do it quickly but does not like to rush !
Take your time and let your imagination wander. Take it all in.

Slow Camel and the Coach have planted 2 new letterboxes.
Please go to the top of White Oak Lane and follow to its end. Turn
and head back 25 steps. On the south side of the wall (sinister), you
will find the prize you seek. Be careful of the carriages which may
come through. We wouldn't want you to get run over.
Now find the Westbourne Road wall where the orange meets the blue.
Follow the wall in a southwesterly fashion. There is no trail per se
but you will not get lost. Go to the wall's end which faces the
athletic fields and turn around to head back. Go 15 steps to the tree
by the wall. The "Looking Back Letterbox" is tucked 'neath the tree's
sister who is now only a stump of her former self.
We wish you all Good Health, Happiness and Peace in 2003 and many
wonderful letterboxing adventures.
