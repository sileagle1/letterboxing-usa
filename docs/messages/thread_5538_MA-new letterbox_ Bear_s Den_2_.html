Bear’s Den #2, The Path of Life Letterbox

New Salem, Massachusetts

Planted by David Besnia & Deanne Bellefeuille-Besnia

May 25, 2002

Follow Bob & Ginger’s excellent clues to Bear’s Den and after finding the bear in his den and replacing him, go back to the path along the water. Follow it for a short distance until you come to a fallen tree where the river bends to the left. Look to where the end of the tree points to a stone wall and rocky knoll in the woods. Climb up the knoll and find a twin trunk birch tree emerging from a crop of rocks. Look at the base of the tree for a crack in the rocks that holds the Path of Life. Return to your vehicle along the same path you entered.
