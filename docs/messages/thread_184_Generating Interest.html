Prayer Rock Letterbox Map & Clues  
PRAYER ROCK LETTERBOX, BRISTOL, VT, USA  
CLUES: 
From a well known incantation, follow the text 190 feet, as the crow flies towards the water, thence 30 feet upstream to a pile of rocks in the cobble. 

REMARKS: Some agility required; Good place to fish, picnic, and enjoy the river; Box will be removed from snow fall to snow melt. 
 
 
 
 

History of Prayer Rock: Local lore has it that, as the early woodsmen brought their logs to Bristol in the spring, there was a very bad mudhole near the rock. The woodsmen would utter considerable profanity trying to get their log teams through through the mud, and this was the reason for having the prayer chiseled in the rock. 

Before you set out read the waiver of responsibility and disclaimer.

Note: Prayer Rock Letterbox was the first Letterbox placed in the United States.
