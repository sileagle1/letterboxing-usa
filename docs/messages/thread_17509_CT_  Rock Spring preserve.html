I re-read the history of this refuge in Country Walks in Ct. (a
guide to Nature Conservancy Preserves). If anyone would like more
specific info. about this book, I will be glad to provide it of
course.
Rock Spring is an incredible place to hike. The diversity of
flora, fauna, terrain is incredible. The pine woods area referred to
is a small pine plantation approx. 50 years old. Most of the trees
are red pines.At the northern end, there are some white pines.
A magical place to hike, if I may say so.
