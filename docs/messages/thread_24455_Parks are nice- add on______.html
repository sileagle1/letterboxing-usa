in Virginia we have the www.timetravelers.org group
which uses stamps to entice your K-12 kids to stuffy
old (wait a minute-lets make that air-conditioned)
museums. the kids collect stamp impressions from the
admission desk or whatever passes for such, mail a
copy of the page off to Richmond for a certificate,
and if the kids visited "special" sites they get a
patch. Its not all museums, and some sites likely have
letterboxes on teh grounds... but its the closest ive
been able to get to boxing this year. THEY say its too
hot.

Enjoy!
