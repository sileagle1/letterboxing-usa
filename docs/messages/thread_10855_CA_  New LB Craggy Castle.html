Craggy Castle Letterbox

Placed by: dvn2r ckr, rnrB & trkr
On: 17 Feb 2003

Castella
(3 miles South of Dunsmuir or 45 miles North of Redding)
Shasta County
California

Directions: Take I-5 to Castella, Exit #724. Head West, contribute
4 George Washingtons and head up hill to the Vista Point parking area.

Notes: Bring grey & green markers for added effect. This is a
steep, winding and narrow one car road at points. Drive carefully.
The trek itself is short (the kids were tired at the time) but don't
dare leave the area without at least first visiting the scenic
overlook for an incredible view if you don't take any other treks
during your visit. Sorry for the boring clues, hopefully the great
views and the box will provide much compensation.

CLUES: From the aCbRcAdGeSf Trailhead, find the 1st burnt, jagged
stump on your left (hint: a1b6c4d steps from beginning). Then a9b5c
steps later, halt! a1b2c steps N to a mossy stump 2.5'x3' is the
castle in which I reside.

This letterbox is an orphan. Please email me at blee@pobox.com when
you find it.
