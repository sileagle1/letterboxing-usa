The favorite season for letterboxing is...

From: Baker (knightbaker63@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-09-21 02:16:33 UTC
...Fall!

My poll I just ran last week on my blog asked "Which Season do you do
MOST of your boxing or caching in?" and 68% of you preferred fall as
the time that you like to letterbox.

I was a little surprised as I get a lot of activity during the summer,
however most of that activity is comming from newbies with a lot of
storebought stamps and finger prints During the fall I noticed that the
numbers are not as high, but the quality of stamps better and there are
more homemade stamps during the fall. I think that is when the serious
lb's do their work
