Hi All!

While visiting Utah last week we were able to connect with two
letterboxes!

The first one we got was Delicate Arch in Moab. Still there and in good
condition.

Hitchhiker logbook for Sockeye Salmon by Buckeroo was picked up by me,
but there was no stamp :(

I will probably copy the image of what the stamp looks like, carve it up
and then put together the replaced stamp and logbook with a pen and a
couple ziplocs and go out "visiting" letterboxes with it before dropping
it someplace for its next driver to find.

I'm very happy to report that Der Mad Stamper's Golden Spike Letterbox
is alive and well in Promontory, Utah. It has been rarely visited, but
the stamp is worth going out of the way for. The historic site nearby
is interesting and the hike is windy but nice.

Best regards,
