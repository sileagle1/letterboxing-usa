Hi all,

To commemorate my favorite artist, I have carved a stamp to celebrate
Salvador Dali's 100th birthday this May. His piece is called "soft
watch at the moment of first explosion". But I have named one newly
carved stamp "warped time".

I will be limiting this postal letterbox to 50 people. I am "hoping"
that each person will be able to mail it on to the next person within
a week so that it may return home within a year. I am considering
carving another of his pieces or maybe even another artist.

Please email me off list with your name and address and once the 50
names are collected I will mail this letterbox out.


Thanks for participating,
