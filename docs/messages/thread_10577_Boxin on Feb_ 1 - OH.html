Boxin on Feb. 1 - OH

From: Dog Scouts Troop (DogScouts@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-02-02 10:44:07 UTC-05:00
Yesterday, I spent the day boxin' with Franzsolo. With his guidance (so I didn't get totally lost of the East side), we trekked around East Fork Park & Mariemont. We had East Fork almost to ourselves. It was a bit cold, but bearable. Myself & my dogs had a great time and slept soundly last night! Other than one box, all were in good shape. I have several very beautiful stamps added to my collection. I think we walked about 10 miles and most of it seemed to be uphill :-) It felt so good to get back out and walk in the woods. I've been so busy with our move that I just haven't had the time & the dogs were going stir crazy. Thanks Franzsolo for a great day!
