Greetings everyone -

Okay so usually I don't sign the bottom of my posts with a
F/P/E/X/blah blah blah count because I really feel that some people
get a bit competative about that and so I just figure no big deal no
reason for me to do it. But I got so excited these weekend after
hitting my 54th find that it just made me do a little happy dance and
I decided to share it with the list.

We had a great weekend letterboxing - Team Chaos which now consists
of Iron`Bear, myself and Sim Kitty. Boy do I gotta tell you, we
startet out for the Multnomah Falls series and set out rather late on
Saturday so after a little over a 2 mile hike all up hill we nabbed
two boxes and then turned around and went back. Yesterday however we
started over, passed the two boxes we had already nabbed and then
snatched 2 more. By the time we had finished we had completed
roughly a 6 mile hike at a 3200 Ft. Elevation gain. Wow not only was
I proud of myself, but Sim Kitty was a real real trooper, walked the
whole thing herself, didn't complain until we were on the last leg of
the hike and all and all just did really really well with the whole
thing!

Iron`Bear and I just love the Multnomah Falls area. He has lived here
most his life and done a ton of hiking and it is one area we have
found so far that he has never explored. It has been fun to explore
it together.

So we ended up getting 3 of the Der Mad Stamper (And the Ryan
Replacements) for this area, and then we Managed to nab Fun Hog's
Octopus.

We will be hitting the area again looking for some more soon!

Hope you all have a blast boxing!
