I have placed a new box in southwestern New Mexico. Here are the clues:

Lordsburg Letterbox

Placed by: Azroadie
Placement date: December 25, 2003
State: New Mexico
County: Hidalgo
Nearest city: Lordsburg
Difficulty: Clues & walk very easy

This box is for weary travelers who need to stop and stretch their
legs. It is located at the New Mexico Visitor Information Center and
Rest Area. From I-10 take Exit 20 and go to the Information Center on
the south side of the interstate highway and park there.

At the "Pet Area" sign face west and walk at about 350 degrees to a
lone moderate size Yucca cluster. The box is at the base of the Yucca
under a small concrete slab.

This is a very public and highly visible site; so, please be very
discreet when retrieving and re-hiding the box.

I will not be able to check on this box; so, please let me know if you
find it. Also, let me know if it is missing so that I can delete the
clues:

http://home.att.net/~azroadie/letterboxing.html

This is the first box in my "I-10 Series".

- Azroadie
