Santa Barbara/Golita box at Nojoque Falls Park, California

From: Gwen & Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2001-06-07 11:53:19 UTC-07:00
Placed by Don and Gwen,on June 4, 2001 in Santa Barbara County. The nearest cities are Santa Barbara, Goleta and Solvang. Box Name: Nojoque Falls and the Turtle. The hike is easy, dogs on leashes are OK. The Park is open 8:00 A.M. to Sunset with a big picnic area. It could be very busy on weekends but so worth your visit as waterfalls (even misty ones) are scarce in So. Cal. Combine this box with a longer visit to Solvang, the quaint Danish tourist destination.(The road to the park is a loop to Solvang).
