My boxes are listed as being placed by "Webmaster" and I want them back.
First, you need an account.
After creating an account, just send an email to the webmasters, listing your
boxes and your account name. We'll switch your boxes over to you and let you
know when we're finished. Then you can edit them just like a box you'd entered
yourself.

How do I edit my box?
When you're logged in, you can use the "My Letterboxes" link in
Member Services to view
your boxes. Each of your boxes will have a link to edit that box. These links
will also show up when you view the box from the state page, but you're the only
one that can see them.

I had to remove my box. Can I delete my box?
When you're logged in, you can use the "My Letterboxes" link in
Member Services to view
your boxes. Each of your boxes will have a link to delete that box. These links
will also show up when you view the box from the state page, but you're the only
one that can see them.

I have a lot of boxes that I want to add. Is there some automated process so I
don't have to type in each one?
Sure! The easiest way is if they're in Excel format, with each row representing
a box. Make sure to include all of the information that's on the
Add Box page. Once you've got
the info together, contact the webmasters (webmasters@letterboxing.org) and
they'll arrange a way to get your boxes put in all at once.

Do I need to create an account?
Not necessarily. You don't need an LbNA account to view boxes or get on the
lbox-announce yahoo group. If
you're going to add boxes, then you need an account.
