Clues:
Sandy Point Discovery Center is in Greenland, NH. You can access the
park by following the signs off of Rte. 33 near Stratham Hill Park.
Head down the trail at Sandy Point behind the Discovery Center until
you reach the boardwalk.
There will be posts along the boardwalk that have numbers on them. Go
until you find the one with the number 9 on it.
Face the post that has the number 9 on it and go left.
Keep going until you find an oak tree with a split middle. Do not go
past the information board that talks about "Most Valuable Land in NH"
It is under the inside part of the boardwalk about three feet away
from the tree.
