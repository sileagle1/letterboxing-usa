Re Hitch Hikers:
We can tell you there are not very many in British Columbia, in fact we
followed one around last week - note was made in one box saying someone had
planned to drop off a HH but that the box was too small, ( might be a tad
difficult to drop one off in a micro box !! ) later we came across another
box - with a message saying they had picked up a HH and were taking it to
Virginia, USA ! I think that is the cool part - a rubber stamp being packed
around, from country to country. Hey ! that is one question never asked at
the border isn't it? " Are you packing a Hitch Hiker ? "
