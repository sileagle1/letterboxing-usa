500Th Find

From: Dan (danzad_99@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-30 23:22:11 UTC
Today we got our 500th Find at G.H.R. Letterbox in Coventry Ct. We
first started by bringing Little Piggy to her letterbox Friendship in
Pomfret. She would like to thank Wanda & Pete for dropping off the
Saving Private Piggy H.H. and for Jessie & Humming Bird for stamping
it and leaving it for her. She was very excited when she opened the
box. She will drop it off soon. Then when went and did the first
box of Paige's Tale, we already found box 2. Then off to Coventry
for #500. Call it a day and headed home. Teach & Preach, Little
Piggy, and Nappy Kitty
