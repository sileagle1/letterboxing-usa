From: (cscm@edgenet.net) | Date: 2001-10-22 17:34:21 UTC
I did a bit of traveling this weekend and picked up a few stamps on
the way.

Started Saturday morning by snagging Kancamangusdabull's new H2O Fire
box, in Providence, RI.

Stopped off in Voluntown, CT, to try to drop in on the gathering
taking place there, but I spotted no letterboxers -- only hoards of
boy scouts. The Rhododendron Preserve was a great little place and
the Drew Clan's Father's Day was my first Connecticut box.

On my way back from a party in New Jersey Sunday, I hunted down
Steven Jachera's Campgaw box in Mahwah, my first for that state. The
weather and the foliage were incredible. It was nice to have a
positive experience in New Jersey for once!

I had hoped to return to RI with enough time to find Wanda's
Polish#1, but torturous traffic prevented me. Maybe next weekend!
