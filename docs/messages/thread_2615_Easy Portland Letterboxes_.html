Some of the boxes at the Tualatin Hills Nature Park are located very near
paved paths.

The Rock Creek series are mostly on dry ground. You can definitely bag them
without serious hiking gear.

I have hunted several of Amanda's boxes. "Who Let the Dogs Out" is a fairly
easy hiking path.

You might get a little dusty, a blackberry bramble or two and dirty knees,
but beyond that, I can't imagine too much trouble in street clothes....
depending on your definition of street clothes!
