Title: Pooh Trail #3. Location: Chillicothe, Ross County, Ohio.
Placed 11-03-03 by Salamander.Difficulty: Easy. To find this
letterbox, you must go to the " Butterfly Garden" which was planted
by the Ross County Master Gardeners. (This is just off Camp Sherman
Rd. and is very near the High Street Dairy Mart store).
CLUES: Stand in front of the sign which depicts 2 black butterflies
and take a compass reading of 100 degrees. Go 38 paces at that
heading to a blue and gray man-made object. Standing approximately 4
feet to the west of this object, take a reading of 80 degrees. Four
paces at this heading will take you to a double-trunked tree. The
box is hidden in a nest of rocks and bark at the base of the tree.
