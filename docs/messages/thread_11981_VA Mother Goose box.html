From: (gbecket@aol.com) | Date: 2003-04-15 14:49:38 UTC-04:00
I previously emailed the list as I had gone to check on Mother Goose at
Greenspring Gardens Park and hadn't been able to find her. I received a very
kind email today from the director of the park, Chris Strand, who found my
address inside and said that the box had been turned into the lost and found
at the park by a visitor. Mr. Strand reported that he would put it back
where it came from and that I was welcome to place other boxes in the park.
I definitely have plans! Those of you in NoVA look for more boxes there
soon! Mother of Crickets
