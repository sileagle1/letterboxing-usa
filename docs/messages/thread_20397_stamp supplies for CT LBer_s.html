I was chasing down some of the "pink stuff" to make
some new stamps at our local AC Moore over the
weekend. After searching with two store employees, we
spoke with the manager, who said that they aren't
carrying the "linoleum supplies" (apparently that's
what they classify the Speedball materials as)
anymore.

I'm not sure if that's just our local store, or all AC
Moores in general, but it may be worth checking out
and saving yourself a trip if they don't carry it.

But at least there was a happy ending ... I hopped in
my car and drove down the street to Michaels where
they still had some :)
