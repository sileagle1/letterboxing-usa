I was recently introduced to letterboxing by an online (U.S.) friend
who seems to have become addicted to the pastime. When I checked
the website, I found that there was one - ONE! - letterbox in my
vicinity (Nanaimo, BC), so my husband and I went to check it out.
We took along a ballpoint pen with which to sign the guestbook, but
by the time we found the box, the pen had disappeared. Nonetheless,
a fine time was had by all. We'll have to go back and sign - or
stamp - in.

Today I put together my first letterbox (called "Imagine") and I
plan to place it tomorrow. I look forward to producing the clues.

Great idea.

Woo Hoo!!! Welcome aboard! It's so exciting to hear about new
letterboxes in the area--now an even better reason to go play on
Vancouver Island!!! Now, if we could only get some free time to

just waiting for new visitors!
