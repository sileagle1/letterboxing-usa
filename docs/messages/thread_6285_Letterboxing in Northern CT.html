Greetings,

After having neglected household chores for the last two "weekends" (which,
for us, is Monday and Tuesday), we took one day off, and today went to CT for
the first time.

Our first stop was the Cute Clucker, a box originally put out for a fair in
2001 in Windsor, CT. After miscounting the trees once, we found the right
fallen log, and stamped in. The box is in excellent shape, though it hasn't
seen a lot of visitors since it's initial placement at the fair.

Next up, East Windsor Intermediate School. This box was placed as a project
by a third grader to get classmates into letterboxing. In honor of the nature
of the box, we sent our four year old off to get it. The box is only a month
old, but has seen a goodly number of visitors, so the project is clearly
working!
