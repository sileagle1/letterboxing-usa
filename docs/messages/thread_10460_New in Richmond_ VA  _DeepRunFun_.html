Location: Deep Run Park, Henrico County, Richmond VA
Difficulty: Very easy walk in the woods
Dogs on leash okay.

Things to do in Deep Run Park...#1 SOCCER

Follow main road into park to the soccer fields. At the SW corner of
the parking lot, begin paved trail to the right of the Castle
Playground. At the T-intersection go right onto unpaved trail.
Cross footbridge, following trail through woods...the soccer fields
will be on your right. At fork go right. As you see the end of the
trail leading onto the soccer fields, notice the last double trunk
tree on your right. Follow a straight path directly east (90
degrees) into the woods until a hollow tree stump blocks your path.
Hidden inside the hollow stump is the Soccer Fun Stamp. Rehide and
cover well, as the stump is shallow and the box very visible if not
disguised.

Watch for more fun things to do in Deep Run Park, but in the
meantime, have a "Food Fight" by Boxitup!
