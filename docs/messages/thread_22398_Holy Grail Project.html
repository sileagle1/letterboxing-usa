Holy Grail Project

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-05 17:37:13 UTC
The Great Lakes group is currently undertaking a large project based
on Monty Python's The Search for the Holy Grail. You can find current
information at the following site:

http://www.geocities.com/the_lb_dragon/Search_for_the_Holy_Grail.htm

As the project manager, I am about to close the window on letterboxers
getting involved in this project, but I'm willing to take on a few
more interested letterboxers if you get back to me by Sunday night.

If you are a letterboxer in the Great Lakes region that, as of until
now, has not heard of the project but would like to get involved, you
may get involved as a stamp creator and/or box planter. Email me with
what you'd like to do!

If you are a letterboxer outside the Great Lakes region that would
like to create a box for the series to be sent to someone in the Great
Lakes region to hide the box for you, let me know!

At the above link, you'll see a listing of suggested box/stamp themes
and an indication of those which have already been signed-up for.

Thanks!
