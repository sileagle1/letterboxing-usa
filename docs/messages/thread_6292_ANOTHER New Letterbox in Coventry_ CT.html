Enter the parking lot at Nathan Hale Homestead on South St.
At the back of the parking area there is a trail.
Follow the blue trail.
You will pass an entrance to a corn field on the right and a large, old, twisted stump on the left.
 
Look for a tall tree with a hollowed out trunk visible from the path.
Good luck!
 
Please be discrete and replace it just as you found it so others may enjoy the hunt!
