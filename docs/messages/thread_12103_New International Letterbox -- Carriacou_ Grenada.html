From: Barry (moloch515@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-19 06:31:01 UTC
On March 25, my brother was kind enough to place a letterbox on his
trip to the tiny (and I mean tiny) island of Carriacou, in the South
Carribean. The link for the clue is at:

http://www.geocities.com/moloch515/carriacou.html

Ryan may think he's the only Oregonian placing boxes in desolate,
remote locations like Africa, Mt. Defiance, and Jack-In-The-Box
restaurants. This one may even be a bit more remote than his odd-
ball boxes! I suspect that this box will decompose before anyone
actually makes the trip down there to nab it. And I think I can say
with a great deal of confidence that there aren't many letterboxers
on the island of Carriacou!

Barry
