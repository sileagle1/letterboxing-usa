Dingman's Ferry-Pa

From: joie christensen (see_joy29@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-12-01 17:33:16 UTC-05:00
Burr!! At 28 degrees it was nippy for letter boxing but I just had to get
out of the house today or go crazy!

Lockmaster and I drove north about 2 hours to Dingman's Ferry, PA. to try to
locate the 3 boxes that Silent Doug had planted there this past August.
After some confusion on my part as to the correct parking area we were on
our way to the treasure! The falls were just beautiful!! Too bad
Lockmaster's hands and feet were numb with cold by the time I located the
second box... This is a place to take your time and enjoy the view but it
was just a wee bit too cold for much contemplation today.
I haven't done much 'boxing this year so it was nice to place three well
carved stamps in my book. All three boxes were in great shape tho #3 had
some condensation in the box. Thank you Silent Doug for such nice well done
boxes!
Enjoy Life!
'Box On!
