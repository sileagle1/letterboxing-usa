We're taking a road trip from NY to MA next week and I hope to fit in a few
LB attempts along the drive. I'll be mapping some possibilities over the

Bennington, VT to Rockport, MA along VT Rt. 9 and MA Rt. 2, then 128N to 127

touristy tips about places we shouldn't miss would be welcome as well
