Monument Valley, Lake Powell, Indian Head & Navajo Bridge missing?

From: Jason Nicoll (jaenicoll@yahoo.co.uk) | Date: 2004-06-23 21:36:21 UTC
Hi y'all

My girlfrind and I have been touring thru the 4 corners region
checking out the sites and catching a few letterboxes enroute.
However, we fear that someone has been thru the Northern Arizona
boxes and cleared them (either that, or we really are THAT bad at
letterboxing).

The 4 boxes in question - Monument Valley, Lake Powell, Indian Head
& Navajo Bridge all have relatively straight forward clues too. We
visited them all over the 19th to 20th June.

At the Lake Powell site we even found a pen (from Doubletree Hotel)
in the location described. It looked like it had been deliberately
left there.

Has anyone else been out to the 4 corners region recently and had a
similar experience or found them?

Cheers

Jae
