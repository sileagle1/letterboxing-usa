WA : Kopachuck SP

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2002-12-01 00:33:18 UTC
I visited this long neglected ( by me) venue today.

I would STRONGLY ADVISE anyone considering this site have a regional
map with them. I had a hard time finding the park and finally pulled
my local map out. A vital road sign at an important junction is
missing. The standard SP road signs get you away from the freeway
but then you will miss the crucial turn off to the park. That
junction sign is missing. Who knows how long it will be down. I
assume not long as not only is the SP sign missing but the street
name sign is down in the bushes.

Once there be prepared for a somewhat challenging navigation effort
to the first box.

"Trail and Error" always crafts clues that teach you ( once again for
me) to not take for granted what something means and assume all
possible meanings of a word.

Even when I got to the tree, the box is not where I would have
(logically) placed it. Even that was a little challenge.
