I don't want to rain on your parade, but before you get too far along
in your planning, most letterboxers would recommend that you find a
20 or 30 or 40 or 50 letterboxes -- and try but fail to find a few
more -- before you start planting your own. Even if you read as much
as you can about letterboxing, it's really hard for many beginners to
grasp the nuances of what makes a good hiding place, the importance
of using quality waterproof containers and baggies, how nice it is to
find a hand-crafted journal and hand-carved stamp in a letterbox, how
frustrating it is to try to find a letterbox with poorly-written
clues, how very satisfying it is to find a mystery box. All these
things you'll only learn as you letterbox.

I hope you enjoy letterboxing.

SD
