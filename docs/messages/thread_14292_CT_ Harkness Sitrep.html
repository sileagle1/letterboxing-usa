True Soccer Fan and I went out to the beach at Harkness yesterday (True
Soccer Fan really wanted to go to the beach rather than to the Giant). While we
were there, we checked on the four Harkness boxes. I am sad to report that the
Eolia Mansion box may be missing. It was not in its hiding place when we
checked on it. However someone may have had the box away from the area to stamp
in.

If you were at Harkness yesterday and had the box for stamping in, please let
me know! If I don't hear from anyone, I'll start carving the stamp and
replacing the box.

All of the other boxes are in good shape. McCann Reunion box is now on the
other side of the rock in the brush, since the park workers actually weedwacked
around the box. The box had no covering (no rocks or anything) except for
weedwacked grass. It's amazing that the box is still there. Obviously the
Harkness workers don't mind the boxes being there!

Happy Hunting everyone!
