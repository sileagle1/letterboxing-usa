Mississippi State Stamp

From: (needeeps@webtv.net) | Date: 2003-03-23 19:12:28 UTC-05:00
Mansfield Center Conn. Rated Moderate
Placed By The NeeDeeps 3-23-03
Directions: From RT.44 take Cod Fish Falls RD.when you come to a stop
sign,go straight across onto Chaffeeville RD.(Follow the signs that say
grist mill.) Soon you will make a right turn onto Stone mill RD. go down
to the bottom of the hill and you will see the grist mill on the
right,keep going straight down the road and you will pass a white house
then their will be a pull off area on the left with the blue blaze trail
on the right.(Don't forget to bring the clues for Grist in the mist this
box is right down the road.) Enter the trail and keep following the blue
blazes all along the river.You will come to a intersection where the red
trail crosses over the blue but you stay true to the blue.Soon you will
come to a place where you have to cross over a little stream and go up a
banking,continue on a little more and you will see a place where the
area opens up with lots of evergreen trees.You can see this place from
where you climbed up the banking.Before you enter this clearing notice
the four oak trees growing from the same base the Mississippi LB. is in
the middle of the four.Please rehide well this is a busy trail.The
NeeDeeps.
