Freebie Bin - "It's New To You"

There will be "Freebie Bin" setup at the SSLF (South Sound Letterboxing
Fest).

Please bring your no-longer-used letterboxing goodies and drop them in the
freebie bin... Look thru the bin and take any goodies you can use. It's
kinda like the "leave a penny... take a penny" dish at the mini mart.

Items you might find / leave:
~ Stamp pads
~ Carving tools / blades
~ Pink Stuff / White Stuff etc
~ Tupperware containers
~ Blank log books
~ Compass
~ Bug repellant
~ Purple socks
~ Maps
~ Hiking books
~ ETC!

See you soon!
