Hello
I do not know what 'number' the Healing Staff was for me when I found
it, but it was and is a wonderful story and puts one in such a quiet
place. I must tell you all that letterboxing is in truth a healing
hobby. The Nature Preserves and Forrests that the thoughtful clues take
us to in the search, are in themselves a healing place. Please know that
the faces of letterboxing friends in time of sorrow have brightened up
my heart. and uplifted my soul. Thank you all for giving me that
precious gift of a caring friend. Very soon, I promise this butterfly
will be flying into the woods again with a light and happy heart amoung
friends.
Butterfly
