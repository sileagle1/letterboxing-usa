In a lot of cases where WOM clues are used, planters don't necessarily
care if there are hoards of finders. Often these clues are
distributed WOM because it is a sensitive area and they don't want a
lot of visitors or to provide an extra perk for people in their region
or people they have come to know through the hobby. The proliferation
of letterboxes over the past couple of years gives a pretty good
chance that when you travel you will have a selection of publicly
listed boxes to find, even if you don't have clues to WOM boxes in
that area.
