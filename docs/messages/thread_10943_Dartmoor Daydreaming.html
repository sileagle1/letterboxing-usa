Dartmoor Daydreaming

From: letterboxer2002 <letterboxer2002@yahoo.com> (letterboxer2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-02-26 18:12:19 UTC
Sigh ...

Work is simply not as fun as vacation. Especially when you consider
the vacation I just got back from. On Valentine's Day I flew from JFK
to the UK and spent five fantastic days letterboxing in Dartmoor ...
and then three days in London. What a trip!!

On Saturday evening, members of the Dartmoor Letterbox Club met me at
The Plume of Feathers pub for drinks, clues, and lots of fun stories.
On Sunday I went out to Cranmere Pool (the letterbox that started it
all) with The Furzgood's and Tailchaser. I'm glad they came with me.
Letterboxes on the moor are totally different than I expected. On
Monday and Tuesday, Alan took me out and showed me many beautiful areas
of Dartmoor. It didn't hurt that we found 36 boxes in one day!

Wednesday ... Wednesday ... Wednesday ... what a great day! Dartmoor
Chris took me to so many places. I must have taken 150 pictures in one
day! By 5:30 we had found so many letterboxes that I had a total of
over 100 finds!!!!!! We headed over to the The Dolphin Hotel in Bovey
Tracey for the letterboxing meeting which happens every Wednesday
evening. While I was there I met many friendly letterboxers. The two
woman who are photographed in the Smithsonian article were there as
well as 'God' who has over 50,000 finds (that is not a misprint!).
That night I became the first American to join the Dartmoor 100 club!

Thursday, I was allowed to go out on the moor alone! :) I ran out to
Nun's Cross and Fox Tor and found about 15 boxes. In total I took over
400 pictures and every one is of a great memory of the best vacation I
have ever taken!

Keep an eye out for some new letterboxes to be released once the snow
has melted. I hope to plant a series called Dartmoor Daydreams ...
which will all have clues and be sited like boxes out on the moor. In
the meantime, check out my web page. I will be posting new pictures
from my trip each week. They were very high quality prints so it may
take a little while to download. I hope you all had a great vacation
too!
http://letterboxer2002.tripod.com/DartmoorPics.htm

Safe letterboxing-
Letterboxer2002 (Alan)
