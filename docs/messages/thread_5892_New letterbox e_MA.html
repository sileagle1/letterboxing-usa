From: Funhog (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2002-06-26 09:01:00 UTC-07:00
On June 23, 2002 Funhog place the Heron's Haven Letterbox in the town of
Yarmouthport, Barnstable County, Massachusetts. This letterbox is hidden
along one of the Town of Yarmouth's walking trails. As you walk along the
marsh be on the lookout for egrets and herons. This is a fragile little
stamp. Please treat it kindly.

To the Trailhead: From Route 6a go north on Homestead Lane across from
house #365, The Friday Club. The road will curve to the left. Go right on
Kingsbury for about a block to a small parking area on the right.

To the letterbox:
With a left-hand choice you shall not fail.
Cross a ramp, grasp a rail.

A narrow path through the trees,
A right-hand turn, a marshy breeze.

The pathway here is twice as wide,
A leaning tree just beside.

Its head is gone and roots are showing,
A friend reclines, don't keep going!

Behind the log and on the ground,
Beneath some bark, the box is found.
