Dave here is a listing of various sites you can research to find
most of the answers to your questions.

http://www.letterboxing.org/faq/faq.html

Silent Doug's site:
http://www.letterboxing.info/
Doug probably has the best info site on Hitchhikers and links to
regional chatlists.

AtlasQuest:
http://www.atlasquest.com/
Ryan has a link set up "Code of Conduct" that is really good for all
boxers to read

newboxers' group:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/
Where there is no dumb question, and is "the" place for many
newletterboxers.

In addition to these sites you can find listings of other sites on
the left hand side of the chatlist home page, as well as listings on
the LBNA home page. You should be advised that many times there are
hidden clues lodged in informational sites as well as the great info
that can be found. Many times things are not what they seem, and
that includes past "flames" listed in the archives of this list.
