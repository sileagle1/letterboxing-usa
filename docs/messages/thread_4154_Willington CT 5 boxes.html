Willington CT 5 boxes

From: Straub (cstraub01@snet.net) | Date: 2002-01-10 20:46:18 UTC-05:00
Just wanted to express my opinion on the 5 boxes planted in the Ruby Fenton Park in Willington CT.
Makes a fine walk. Had a great time. Wish I could make stamps like they did. Boxes well hidden and excellent clues. I would recommend this walk but would be best in a time without snow. Trail is slippery this time of year. Also, all 5 stamps make a fine series representing the state of Connecticut. Thanks for planting these boxes......Chuck Straub in Mansfield CT
