Hello Everyone-

As you may have heard I am teaching a class for the Trumbull
Continuing Education entitled "Hiking In Connecticut". I plan on
introducing my eight students to all sorts of outdoor hiking
opportunities including letterboxing, geocaching, and orienteering.
On Saturday, March 13th my eight students will be meeting at Putnam
Memorial State Park in Redding to find their first letterboxes and
geocaches. Afterwards we will be heading over to Huntington State
Park for lunch and to find the Peanuts, Physics 101, and Colis P.
Huntington letterboxes, as well as several geocaches. Anyone
interested in meeting my class to exchange stamps and take a fun
hike with us are more than welcome to attend. Please plan on
meeting at Huntington State Park at 12:00 to exchange stamps and
chit-chat over bagged lunches. We will try to hit the trail by
12:45 (ish). I hope to see all of my good friends in letterboxing
as well as some newbies.

Safe Letterboxing- Letterboxer2002 (Alan)
