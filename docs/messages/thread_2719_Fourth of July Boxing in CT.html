Fourth of July Boxing in CT

From: Miner Peter R NPRI (MinerPR@npt.nuwc.navy.mil) | Date: 2001-07-06 11:29:19 UTC-04:00


> Hope everyone else took advantage of their day off!

We enjoyed our day off climbing up and down the ridges in Southington, CT and collecting stamp images from 5 of the 6 ESAK boxes. Great stamps ESAK. There has been some logging at the beginning of the way to Sunset Rock but we got there following a pinkish path a little above the south shore of the reservoir. We have also found Jay Drew's boxes at Thrill Ride and Barb & Larry's Ledge. All were in good shape and waiting for more visitors.

> ESAK asks "If anyone knows of any of these [New London County, CT] boxes that may be out of commission, please let us know. "

We have been to all these boxes over the last couple of months and found them in good condition. I don't recall any problems with finding them as there wasn't anything particularly tricky in the clues. The areas are all delightful walks.

Also, we had great "sailing" weather the day before in Newport, RI.

Wanda and Pete
