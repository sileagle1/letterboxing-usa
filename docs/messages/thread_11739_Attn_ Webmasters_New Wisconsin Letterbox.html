Buena Vista Park Letterbox

Placed: 4/5/03
Where: Alma, WI
Placed by: Leapin' Lizards
Difficulty:Easy
Terrain: Moderate

Clues: After visiting the scenic overlook, walk to the the northeast
and find the hiking trail. Walk 150 paces from the start of the trail.
To the left is a small trail leading to an outcropping. Standing on
the outcropping, the dam is due west. At 320 degrees, spot the old
birch who's limbs have grown together. Search the hollow in the trunk
for your treasure. Have fun!

Leapin' Li
