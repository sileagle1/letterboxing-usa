(ok, so far I'm a fair-weather boxer...), we headed up to the

memorial letterbox. Stamped up on the stone railing
surrounding the capitol on a beautiful spring Wisconsin afternoon.
I must say that the notion of the letterbox coming to me
is a very fine one. If anyone else would like to place a letterbox
in Madison just let me know!
