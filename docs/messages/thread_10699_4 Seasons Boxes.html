Mark Pepe wanted a universal symbol to denote a box being findable in all weather. In the font "Wingding" the capital "R" (R) could mean a good weather only box and the lower case "v" (v) or "bracket" ( ] ) (which is ] in Wingding) could mean all seasons. Are we all capable of getting to that font? How about our intrepid webmasters?   For that matter, is everyone's computer reading symbols in this email?
