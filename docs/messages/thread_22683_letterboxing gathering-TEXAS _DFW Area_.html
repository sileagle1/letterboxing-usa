I'd like to announce a gathering in Texas October 22nd
at Bardwell Lake, just south of Dallas in Ellis
County.

This is a Barnraising, sort of a reverse gathering -
rather than getting together to find all the boxes in
a given area, we are getting together to hide boxes in
a given area that is sorely in need of boxes!

While we would love to see fellow boxers from anywhere
come to the gathering, we realize some of y'all may
want to participate but can't make the trip. That's
fine - we're accepting boxes from absentee boxers to
be planted during the gathering.

We are also doing a recipe exchange - boxers are
encouraged to bring their favorite recipe and stamp it
with their personal stamp to make a double exchange -
good food and good stamps.

For more information, email me directly offlist, or go
to this website:

www.geocities.com/barefootlucy/JENBarnraising
