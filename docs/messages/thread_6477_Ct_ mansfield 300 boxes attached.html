From: Carolyn Stearns (cstearns07@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-07-26 09:05:23 UTC-04:00

Thanks to all who came to the Historical Society workshop yesterday. There
were 40 or so in attendance and about half were letterboxers half historical
Soc. friends and a few seem to be picking up the hobby. I planted several
boxes for folks to enjoy and now they will be available to all. They are
easy short wet your whistle boxes each with a history lesson. ( Some longer
ones coming soon.) One box caled plane spotter was unique i that among the
audience was a real plane spotter! It was so nice to hear a first hand
detail of the WWII job. Anyway have fun with these and as always be Safe.
Leader of the Pack
