DC War or Peace Mystery box found

From: thedoubtfulguests (thedoubtfulguests@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-04 01:34:46 UTC
What could be a better July 4th Eve activity than looking for the War
or Peace mystery box. The setting was very appropriate and beautiful
on a summer evening with the sun down low for dramatic lighting.

Once I had guessed the general location it didn't take me long to
confirm some of the clues before we set out. Once there the clues
were fun to follow.

We were the first finders, somehow beating Mary from Virginia who is
usually just before us everywhere. :-) As the first finders Acorn
chose peace over war but Cozy decided we should takes pictures and
leave the first finders prizes for others to enjoy.

When I replaced the box I covered it a little with leaves and such
for fear of it being discovered by fireworks partyers tomorrow.

Thanks to Knit-wit-Mom for the fun boxing evening, the art, and the
very nice stamp.
