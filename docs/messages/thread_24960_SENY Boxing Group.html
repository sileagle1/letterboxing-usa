Hi everyone,

It seems like new members join the usa group all the time, so I thought
I'd repost about our SENewYork group. There aren't many of us because,
well, there aren't that many boxers in the area. Many of us have to make
road trips to do much of our boxing. But we're here and we love to
letterbox, however we have to do it, locally or by hitting the highways
of nearby (and not so nearby) areas.

Three of us met recently at a gathering in Mr. Kisco, New York, held at
the Westmoreland Sanctuary and Museum. It's always great to put a face
with the trailname.

If you live in one of the five boroughs of New York City, or
Westchester, Rockland, Suffolk, Orange, Nassau, or Putnam County, this
is a small, private group for those who love letterboxing and want to
perhaps meet and greet other boxers nearby for exchanges or boxing in
this area or others. Maybe you are looking for boxing buddies? Maybe you
just want to talk about your favorite hobby?

We're a friendly group and welcome new people.

So if you're in our area, please join us in our favorite hobby.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/SENYBoxing/
