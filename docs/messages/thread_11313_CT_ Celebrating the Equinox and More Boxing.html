From: bluebird2boxes (debby_butler@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-03-20 20:59:02 UTC
The sunrise celebration at Lantern Hill with the Westerly Morris
Men was very, very special. We climbed when it was still dark
(love my headlamp) and then enjoyed their dancing as the sun
rose. I was expecting to see more letterboxers. although it was
great to meet Churchill and Moonshine from RI. We did the two
boxes on that trail, had breakfast with the celebrators and I also
had time to do the Little Dam series. All were dry and in fine
shape. Later Butterfly and I got to 2 of the DEP boxes. All before it
rained! Next year, maybe more boxers will make the early
morning climb! Bluebird
