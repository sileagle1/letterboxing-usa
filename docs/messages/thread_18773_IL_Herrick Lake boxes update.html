For those of you seeking the boxes of Herrick Lake in Dupage Co. IL,
please note that the Herrick Lake Box has been stolen by vandals.
As this has been an ongoing problem in recent months (the park's
proximity to a high school may have something to do with this),
we've decided not to replace the box. HOWEVER-- we are keeping the
clues on the LbNA website because they are necessary to find the
remaining box, "Lost and Found". Notations reflecting this
interdependence have been made on both clue pages.
