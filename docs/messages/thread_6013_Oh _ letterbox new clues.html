Night Moves

Woodland Mound Park
Near Cherry Grove Ohio, Hamilton County
Difficulty : easy
Placed : July , 6 2002 ~ by franzsolo...
(motofranz@webtv.net)

Note : This Microbox ** does not have a stamp pad, and a compass would
be beneficial.

Letterbox clues:
Enter the park from Old Kellogg Road.
After you enter the park there is a small parking place on the left side
of the road.
Walk back down the hill a bit to the park entrance.

Start at the silver colored pole at the right side of the road.
Go 0 (due North ) to the "Pets must be on leash...etc, etc. sign.
Go 345 for 20 paces
(1pace=2 steps ; about 5 feet )
to the speed limit sign ( 25 MPH )
Last head due North again to a Tall Cedar tree.
about 8 paces,There will be a few rocks behind that tree.
Seek Night Moves under those rocks !

email me if you are successful,(or not )
Thanks,
franzsolo...
