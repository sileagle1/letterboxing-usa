WA: Good news about "authorities"

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2002-12-03 23:14:48 UTC
I asked for and got permission to place some LB's in the University
of Washington's Pack Experimental Forest.

http://www.cfr.washington.edu/facility.pack/index.html

I am excited about scouting this 4000+ acre venue near Mt Rainier.
In their e mail they expressed pleasure at being able to introduce
their site to as many visitors as possible. Some of the woods are
classed as Old Growth.

It is my understanding that the site should be available year round.
They rarely get snowed out, which is news that amazed me, given their
proximity to Mt Rainier.
