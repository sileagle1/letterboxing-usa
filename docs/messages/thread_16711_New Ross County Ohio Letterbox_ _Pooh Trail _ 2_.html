Title: Pooh Trail # 2. Placed 11-03-03 by Salamander. Difficulty:
Easy. Location: Ross County Ohio, near Chillicothe. This 2nd
installment of the Pooh Trail series is hidden at Camp Sherman
Memorial Park, just north of the intersection of Rts. 104 and 35,
Chillicothe, Ohio. Clues: Go to Camp Sherman Park. You must find a
Star of David symbol somewhere in the park. Standing approximately 6
feet in front of this symbol, take a compass reading of 337 degrees.
Go 38 paces at this heading to a large tree. Then take another
compass reading of 240 degrees. 21 paces at this heading will bring
you to another tree. The box is hidden in a hollow space at the base
of the trunk.
