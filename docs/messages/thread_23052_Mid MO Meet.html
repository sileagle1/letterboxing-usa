Mid MO Meet

From: Clyde Bentley (clyde.bentley@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-02-07 17:40:56 UTC
Fifteen members of the Mid Missouri Letterboxers gathered in Columbia
Saturday to discuss their hobby. The group exchanged both stamps and
tips and created a joint box for children that will be hidden in a park.

There are approximately 150 boxes in Missouri and 36 boxes in
Missouri's Boone and Calloway counties near Columbia. For information
about the Mid Missouri Letterboxers, see midmoletterboxing.blogspot.com.
