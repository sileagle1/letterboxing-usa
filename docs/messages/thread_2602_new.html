I'm from Arkansas, and don't know a lot about GA, but looked on the

amount in all of Arkansas, so you should have no problem getting into
the hobby. :-) Anyway, here's a link to that search, and you can
look for yourself. I made this a "Tiny URL" at tinyurl.com (a place
that takes long URL's, and makes them small, since email tends to
truncate URL's, and make them hard to access with a click) but it
will (hopefully) lead you to the search I made on AtlasQuest.com. Here it is:

Hope this works out for you... Atlas Quest has LOTS of information
about letterboxing, etiquette, etc, that one will want to read up
on. Another place you can search for letterbox clues is:

http://www.letterboxing.org/
