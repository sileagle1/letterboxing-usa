Another Star Letterbox

Placed by franzsolo (with the help of A-bear) on 2
January 2003
Star, ID
Ada County

Directions: Off of Highway 44 at the Star MERC head
south on Main St to the southern most point where you
can park on the dirt near some large boulders. You
will be able to see a rope swing and a bridge from the
parking spots.

Description: Easy walking, but can be muddy.

Clues: Start at the three large water turnkeys by the
river. From the southern most key walk 15 paces
(pace=2 steps) at 240 degrees. Take the road at 320
degrees that follows the creek. You will eventually
see a 2 board walk bridge on your right. Continue 19
paces to a large boulder on your left. Below it lies
a 10 diameter concrete slab. In between the two lies
Another Star.
