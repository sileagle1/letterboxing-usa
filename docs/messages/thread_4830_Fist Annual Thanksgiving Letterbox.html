After Ryan posted about the 1st Annual Thanksgiving Letterbox being in place as of March 17, my husband and I went back to Priest Point Park today to try again.  After we got on the correct trail, we found the correct tree and then the letterbox!  Yes!  Now I was hooked.  We felt so good about it we decided to drive to Tacoma and we found another one at the Tacoma Nature Center.  So I got my first two stamps in my log book today.
