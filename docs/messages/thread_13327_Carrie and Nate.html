Carrie and Nate:

Good to have new letterboxers in Nor Cal!!

The Natamos Oak Microbox is a shady treat. Bring a picnic lunch or walk to
local restaurants. The infamous Davis Letterbox has had a couple of problems and
I don't know its status right now but I'll look into it. (It was my first and
is still the best!) Folsom Powerhouse is a good one too. I've never been able
to find the Auburn box even though I've tried 4 times! There's a new one,
Davis to Winters, which would be great on a bike but I took a car.
It's probably just as far for you to go to the Contra Costa Series boxes in
the Walnut Creek, Martinez area (east Bay Area) as the ones I've mentioned.
I've gotten #2 and #3 and they're wonderful, short hikes. I intend to get the
others soon.

Hope to see you at the future Nor Cal Gathering!
