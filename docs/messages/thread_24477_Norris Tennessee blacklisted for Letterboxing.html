Norris Tennessee blacklisted for Letterboxing

From: generalsans (mlaforest05@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-07-27 20:43:59 UTC
In late June, 2007, 4 letterboxes were planted in/near Norris, Tennessee, in hopes of
introducing boxing in that community. Today, after inspection, three of those boxes have
been destroyed with contents having been reported earlier to me as scattered. The
remaining box required taking a compass bearing and may have been saved because of that
minor technical factor.
Personally, I am blacklisting Norris as a boxing area. I am sad about this, because the area
has much to offer for hiders and seekers alike. I have 'retired' them from AQ and LbNA lists.

Michel
