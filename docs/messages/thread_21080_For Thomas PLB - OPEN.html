For Thomas PLB - OPEN

From: Debbi Scott (dscott5377@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-12-10 16:01:11 UTC-07:00
As many of you know, back in October, I started a donation PLB to raise
funds for my friend's son to get hippotherapy lessons. This box has
been VERY successful and has raised $470! (lessons are $115/hr)

I am almost at the end of the list for those who have asked to be put on
the list to get this box. So, now I would like to reopen it for anyone
who would like to sign up.

If you want to learn about Thomas and why he needs these lessons, please
visit my website: http://www.geocities.com/dscott5377/thomas.html

the requirements for this box are as follows:

- I am asking that you donate $5.00. However, if you are unable to meet
that amount, I understand. And, of course, if you want to donate more,
that would be very appreciated. Each 1 hour session at Pegasus costs
$115.00
- You must be willing to mail this Priority Mail w/ Delivery
Confirmation
- You must email me with the deliver confirmation number when you mail
the box out.
- You will need to email me for the next name on the list - I will not
include a mailing list in the box
- I will be emailing each of you individually as the box gets close to
your name. If you do not respond, you will NOT get the box. In this
email, I will also ask you to confirm your mailing address.
- This box MUST be mailed out within 3 days of its arrival at your
house. (I can always change the line up if the box will be coming to you
when you will be on vacation, etc)

Thank you so much for signing up for this box. I am so happy that with
your help I will be able to give Donna and her family this gift for
Thomas.

Debbi
