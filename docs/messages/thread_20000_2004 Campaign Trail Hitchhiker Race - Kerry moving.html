Rushed right out after I saw this announcement to 'cast my vote' --
but the first box had the wrong candidate. . . er stamp. . . so I
moved on (of course I hadn't been to either of these boxes so I
actually had to look for them.) At the second box I picked up Kerry
and he will be riding with Mary of Virginia and I to Lancaster PA
where we will make a campaign stop before we meet up with mizscarlet
and hikers-n-hounds. Hopefully Kerry can hitch a ride to another
campaign stop in DE. I wouldn't endeavor to force my politics on
others. . . but a stamp . . . that I would. Hopefully these Red-Hat
Box'rs will play along and Kerry won't be left glad handing in
Lancaster! I haven't check'd the voting demographics, but I thought
it best to move Kerry North where there are at least a lot of
campaign stops he can make. So within 48 hours of hitting the trail,
Kerry could possibly have 4 stamps, 2 stops, and 2 states = 8 votes!

I guess his opponent will just have to wait it out in the bush until
some fair weather boxer/voter happens along.
