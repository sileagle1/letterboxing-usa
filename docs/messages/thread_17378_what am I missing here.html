From: Rayvenhaus (rayvenhaus@myndworx.com) | Date: 2003-12-30 09:45:26 UTC-08:00
There was (And still is at times) a large debate about 'other' databases
other than the 'official' one at the www.letterboxing.org site. There are
many arguments about the pros and the cons, so here's a good point to
remember.....

IF it's not your box, don't put it in any other database anywhere. Only the
placer of a box should put the box in a database.

Make any status reports to the owner of the box, either through the 'Contact
the Placer' at the main website, or via direct email if they put that in the
letterbox and or here, to this mailing list.

Those simple guidelines should make everyone happy.


And, unless you have been adding boxes you found to a database elsewhere,
then you've not been doing anything wrong at all.

Just my humble 2 cents worth.
