Lucy,

Munro bagging is the process of climbing a list of Scottish mountains over
3000 feet. Sir Hugh T. Munro made up the list of these mountains in 1891,
and since then zealous amblers have been trying to get to the top of all of
them. Check out the book "The Munros" to get the "official" list of
mountains to be bagged.
