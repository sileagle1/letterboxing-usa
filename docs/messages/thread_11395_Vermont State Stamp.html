Mansfield Center Conn. Rated Moderate 3/4 of
a mile Placed By The NeeDeeps 3-23-03
Directions: On RT.195 in Mansfield pull into the old Town Hall &
Historical Society across from Spring Hill RD. Go to the back of the
building and park,then enter the blue blaze trail.(Don't forget the
clues for other great boxes like the Plane spotter,the bed rug,and
Inherit the earth.) Walk all the way down and in awhile you will come to
a wooden bridge,cross over and you will come to a T.You will turn right
here keep going and you will come to a place with a beautiful view.Go to
the clearing that has the best view, go over and take a moment to enjoy
the scenery.Now from where you are standing facing the view take reading
of 180 degrees,that should be to your left.their will be a stone wall
off a ways go over to it and walk down the back side of it. you will
come to a stump with a medium size tree next to it on the other side of
the tree opposite of the stump you will find your Vermont LB. in the
wall behind a stone door.The NeeDeeps.
