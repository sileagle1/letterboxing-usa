From: Cherie Mortazavi (cheriescorner@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-04-12 02:38:01 UTC
We took a Spring Break trip from Vancouver,WA to Disneyland. I had
asked for a few easy boxes to find and we were successful. If you
want to find a few easy boxes off of the freeway check out: Tip Of
Shasta, W B Ide Adobe and The Source of Sacramento River boxes along
I-5 in California. They were all alive and very well. The Ide
Adobe box is hidden in a wonderful historic park. It is just
beautiful there. I would of never stopped there if I had not been
letterboxing. As was the park where the Source of Sacramento is
hidden. I wish we would of taken our lunch there. Beautiful places
to experience while we do this wonderful hobby. My husband is a non-
letterboxer so far, but he was quite impressed with these 3 finds.
I will get him out there sooner or later. We talked about his trail
name and I have an idea how to carve his stamp. He will have it in
his blood by the time summer is through. Willow and family.
