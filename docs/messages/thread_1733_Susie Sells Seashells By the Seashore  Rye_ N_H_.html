A fun letterbox at our favorite picnic spot. Rye Harbor State Park
Rte 1 Rye N.H. Seasonal fees or park down the road walk in or wait
til off season. Bring a picnic you need only a few minutes to gather
the box but the view of the harbor is something to savor over a good
picnic or some hot fish and chips from nearby Ray's Seafood.
Outside the ladies room take a magnetic bearing of 80 degrees NE
. Spy a large bush seaside. your quest lies wedged and weighted
behind. Weigh Suzie down again so the sea won't rob her in a
hurricane. This is a very popular park if that picnic area is busy
scramble along the water and come up to it that way or wait for the
picnic to finish and take the near table for yourselves a picnic is
great cover for your activity. Hope you enjoy! Planted by : The
Leader of the Pack - Cock-A-Doodle-Moo 4-H Club the Cowboy and
bear.Rated easy
