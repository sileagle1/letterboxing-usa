Back in 2001, when I first started letterboxing, there were *zero*
letterboxes in my neck of the woods. So I started planting like
mad. Shortly thereafter, another boxer who was apparently thrilled
that there were now boxes in the area planted his own. At the bottom
of his clues he wrote something to the effect of "and don't forget
Warrior Woman's nearby boxes" and he listed all my boxes. He never
asked permission. My thoughts? Cool! Free publicity!

Recently, in 2004, I have seen new letterboxes planted in my area
which start with "Start at the same starting point as Warrior Woman's
XYZ boxes". No one asked permission. My thoughts? Cool! They
referenced my starting point without revealing its location, and the
finder will still have to do the research necessary to find the
starting point of my boxes! How wonderful!

Personally, I take it as a compliment. Thus far it has not been
abused.

YMMV.

Warrior Woman
