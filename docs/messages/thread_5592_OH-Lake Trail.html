Name:Lake Trail at East Fork Lake

Location: Batavia, Ohio

Address: Take SR 125 Amelia from I-275 about 11 miles, Bantam Road is Left at the
light and SR 222 is to the Right. Goto The Left, The park is about .5 Miles to the
Left. Once on the Park Road, Be on the lookout as after you pass Newman Trail
you will need to start looking for The Lake Trail

Level: 3 (Dirt Trail, Hills)


Need: Stamp Pad, Pen

Clues: Find the Water Fountin, Look west and you will see a sign that reads
(Lake Trail) You will take this trail. You will stay on this trail as you will be
hiking about Half a mile and 3 bridges. While standing on the 3rd Bridge
there is a stump at 210'. Just to the left if a tree at 200'. At the base of
that tree and under a rock you will find your box.
