From: Alafair
Subject: CT-Bear's Rock near Durham

Last evening, my letterboxing partner and I took the short hike to Bear's
Rock near Durham. Since it stays light so late in the evening, this Drew
Clan letterbox can easily be done after work. When we were there, there was
not another soul around.

The Mountain Laurels were in full bloom and the trail was a fairy-land of
white and pale pink. In addition, the view is not to be missed.
