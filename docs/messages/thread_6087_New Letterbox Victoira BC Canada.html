Another Currency series box to add. This one is based on the 20 dollar bill....it is pretty easy once you have found the starting point which is nicely described in the first clue...

The Currency Series continues in the province of British Columbia.

Location Victoria BC

Placed: July 4th by Canada Goose

Letterbox name: 20 Bucks

Clues:

Find the beacon on the hill historic marker.

Look yonder green with envy and pace out 3.5 x 20 paces to a rest stop.

Next pace out 20x2 toward the "Sorry" sign and head 20 additional paces toward the water and stairs.

Go 20 x 2 more steps and you will see the next rest stop.

Have a rest. Count to 20 and by your feet you will find what is it you seek.

Be sure to search all around and watch for on lookers. This is a popular trail. You may want gloves.

Questions or comments feel free to email me. Just keeping track of the 20 counts is all you need...
