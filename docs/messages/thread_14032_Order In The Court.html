Order In The Court

Make your way to the courthouse in Jackson, Ohio. Once there, make
your way to the base of the southern-most column at the front of the
building. (You may stand down on the sidewalk beside it.) Trying
not to look like a terrorist lest you land in the shiny new jail
around the corner, take a bearing of 100 and go about 9 paces (1
pace = 2 steps) to the corner of a 1993 bench. From there, go
directly North, 0, about 10 paces to a shrub. Again trying not to
look like a terrorist, look around behind the bush to find Order In
The Court hiding under a piece of concrete.
