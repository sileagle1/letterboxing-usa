From: Eric J. Eurto (enicltrbxr@netzero.net) | Date: 2001-10-18 00:16:32 UTC-04:00
Okay, so this is darned late notice to all you out there that may not know about it already. This Saturday the 20th at 10am, a joint get-together between folks from the Connecticut Forest and Parks Association and EMS Shoppers/letterboxers will take place at Patchaug State Park off route 49 in Voluntown, CT. I invite any and all to attend as there will be new and old letterboxers alike. We'll check out Father's Day Flowers and Misery Loves Company while we're there and chat a bit about Letterboxing ettiquette, where to place them, how to place them, Leave No Trace ethics, and also place a box or two or three while we're there! Oh, yeah, we'll also probably chat a bit about some orienteering (or land navigation with compass). Hope to see some of you there if you can make it. I hear the weather should be cooperating.
