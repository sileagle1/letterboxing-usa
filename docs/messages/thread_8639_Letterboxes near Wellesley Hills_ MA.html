would also suggest the "Heron of the Charles" letterbox, which is near Nonantum/Watertown Square.  There are a couple of letterboxes in Dover (Noanet Woodlands and Chase Woodlands) which are worth seeking out (though perhaps a longer hike than you are looking for).  The Birdhouse letterbox in Needham is also well worth seeking out.  There is at least one more box with a Mystery Clue that is within a half-hour of Wellesley Hills, but I won't name it, since I don't want to spoil the pleasure of the hunt!
