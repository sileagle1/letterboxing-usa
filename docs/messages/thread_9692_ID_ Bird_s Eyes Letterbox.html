Bird's Eyes Letterbox
Boise, Idaho
Ada County
Placed by A-Bear on 15 December 2002

Directions: Take Exit 50 south from I84 onto Cole Road. Follow signs
to the World Center for Birds of Prey. Drive about 5 miles until
Cole Road ends (you'll drive only a short ways passed the entrance to
the World Center for the Birds of Prey.) Park at the end of Cole
Road.

Level of Difficulty: Easy & extremely short, but not easily
accessible to handicapped as one must cross a ditch and rocks to
locate the letterbox. You want to spend the rest of the time at the
actual World Center for the Birds of Prey.

Bird's Eyes Clues:
Start from the street entrance to the World Center for the Birds of
Prey where you see the two large boulders (one with World Center for
the Birds of Prey and the other with The Peregrine Fund). Follow the
street (Cole Road) south with your eyes on the wooden fence and brick
wall that parallel each other on your right. When they both stop,
spot the shrub on the east side of the brick wall. Behind and under
that shrub and under a pile of rocks you will find the Bird's Eyes
Letterbox. Please hide the owl well and then drive the rest of the
1.5 miles to the World Center for the Birds of Prey if you like
(entrance to that is $4, but finding the letterbox before that is
free).
