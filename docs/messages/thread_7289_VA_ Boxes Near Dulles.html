VA: Boxes Near Dulles

From: mlgainey (mlgainey@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-08-30 20:16:53 UTC
Am I correct in figuring that the following boxes are the closest to
the Dulles airport:

Frying Pan Park in Herndon
Christmas Tree Graveyards in Reston

I'm going to Alaska in September, and on the trip home from Dulles to
Richmond, I won't be in any rush, so I was going to take a few hours
and do some letterboxing. But I've spent most of my life in central
Virginia and have a fuzzy idea at best of where all the 'burbs are in
relation to each other and in relation to Dulles in NoVA. Any advice
from residents would be greatly appreciated.

From: The Schumacher's (schubass@myeastern.com) | Date: 2002-08-30 16:42:30 UTC-04:00
Hi Marsha ~
 
Both are within 10 minutes of each other and the airport.  When coming from the airport on the Dulles Toll Road, Christmas Tree Graveyard is off exit 13 and Frying Pan Park is exit 10 (if I'm not mistaken ~ it may be exit 11).  We picked up the Christmas Tree Graveyard box while visiting my sister in Reston a few weeks ago.  Time was not on our side so we were not able to get any of the others in the area ~ not even the ones in DC that I had hoped to get.  Oh well, maybe next time.  Not sure when we'll make the trip from Connecticut down there again ~ maybe next year.
 
Enjoy your trip to Alaska and your Letterboxing side trip!
