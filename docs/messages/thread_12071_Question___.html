From: Rachel Henry (remcat@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-12-09 22:31:36 UTC-05:00
A boxing buddy is like a pretend friend, who has his/her/its own log book
and sig stamp. Because the BB is pretend, you are responsible for printing
and reading clues, hiking, carrying the BB's logbook and stamp, and doing
all stamping in. They tend to travel with a letterboxer for a while, and
then move on.
