Congratulations. Our SP Rangers are very a protective lot. They
take their parks very seriously and want to do everything to stay on
the good side of those who sign their checks ( many of whom have
never been to the park :-p )

Our Ranger at Bridle Trails was thrilled with the presence of the
LB. She signed into the log book using the stamp that they use for
the Junior Ranger Program. It is quite a nice one, each stamp
specific for the park.

I look forward to returning when the LB's are back in place and
finding them at last. I hope Amanda will do the same.

I was disheartened to see all the graffiti on the historic
buildings. I am sure it troubles the park staff as well.

It is nice that the community of Pt Townsend supports the park by
financing the parking fee. This community is suffering with the
current economy and unemployment. They recognize the importance of
that park.

I wonder who I can tweak in the community of Bellevue /Kirkland /
Redmond to do the same for Bridle Trails. Hummm who do we know there
who has money????
