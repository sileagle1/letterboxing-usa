3000 letterbox finds!

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-25 19:41:48 UTC
Just had to say that I reached F3000 yesterday! Seems like just the
other day (actually June 1st) that I was reaching F2500 at "Universal
Language" in VA, and here it is, less than 2 months later, and I've
rather haphazardly collected together another 500 boxes - most of
them NOT IN CT!!! This last batch of 500 came mostly from my recent
trips to places in OH, VA, MD, PA, NJ, IN, KY, NH, VT, MA, and RI,
with just a few of those super CT series tacked on at the end! My
3000th box was the CT mystery "Healing Staff", which I thought was
really cool, since we could probably all use healing from some of
life's wounds! Letterboxing at its best can help provide some of that
kind, uplifting spirit, so thanks again to everyone who helps to keep
it alive!

Wanda from RI P53 F3001 X380 (no virtuals,repeats or multiples!)
