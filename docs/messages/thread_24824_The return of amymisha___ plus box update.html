The return of amymisha... plus box update

From: Amy (amymisha@localnet.com) | Date: 2008-04-20 12:49:00 UTC
Hi all...
I am back. I'm sure there are a lot of new faces on the scene, but
some of you old crusty dogs may still be out there in boxing land.
Hopefully for good this time. I needed to take a nice long break from
everything.

I am saddened to say the Mr,. or Ms Potato Head in East Boothbay, ME
is no longer findable. Unless you are insanely persistent. I hadn't
checked up on it in 2 years. *Gasp* because every time I was home it
was hunting season. Not a good time to be walking around.
Unfortunately, I just checked it today and they have done so much
trail work, and so much has changed that the clues are useless. I will
leave them up for persistent souls who like a good challenge, but do
me a favor and don't bring your kids. I don't want to be responsible
for heartbreak. I'll put some new ones out today though, to make up
for it.

OK. I can promise I probably won't post much and will mostly lurk, but
I am here. I am in Maine for now, but will be moving back to
Boston/Cambridge very soon. Maybe I'll even be social and attend a
gathering... all these Maniac boxers up here keep trying to get me to
go to one and I haven't been in about 2 years.

Amymisha
