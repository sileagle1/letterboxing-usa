Butterfly sent a gentle note that I had missed her recent letterboxes
in CT, and I must say that I've had this nagging suspicion that I
missed a half-week's worth of letterboxes by accidently over-writing

see that I missed a bunch of Perfect Circle boxes too. There must be
others! They call me a "webmaster," but I'm not very proficient with
html and I'm especially not very detail-oriented, which is a key
attribute for this business.

I try and do updates twice a week. I'm an unorganized Virgo, so I'm
not real consistent, but to help with your planning, my schedule
works best when I do What's New on Tuesday and Saturday-or-Sunday. I
try and also make the "courtesy" clue pages at the same time for the
states I cover (CT MA RI CA NV UT NM AZ TX). So if you don't see your
boxes up within a week, please let me know. I tried for awhile to
post people individual notes that "your box is up," but my computer
is so slow and rickety that I gave up on that.

Sorry for any delay and I'll patch things right up!

Jay in CT
