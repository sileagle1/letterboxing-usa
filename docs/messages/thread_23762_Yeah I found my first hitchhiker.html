Yeah I found my first hitchhiker

From: Chedd (chedderfish@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-09-04 02:53:36 UTC
I was in Colorado and the friend I was visiting wanted to go to Pikes
Peak. She had never been letterboxing and I figured there was proabbly
a good chance there was one in the area. I called up my honey back at
home and had him e-mail the clues to one in the park to my blackberry
so - She found her first letter box but also ended up finding Armchair
Detective's Bless my Little Heart HH. It was so random but I was
confused becuase the box it was at didn't have a stamp. We sorted
everything out - the original boxes journal had tons of notes asking
about where the stamp was. I didn't have my stamp but I signed it and
so did my friend. I wound up bringing the thing all the way back to
the east coast. I feel so lucky- this was only my 5th box ever anyway.
