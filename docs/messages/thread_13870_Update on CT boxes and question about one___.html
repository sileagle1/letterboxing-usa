Update on CT boxes and question about one...

From: meadow77lark (meadow77lark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-27 23:53:06 UTC
This has been a great week for letterboxing - other than the
outrageous amount of mosquitos and ticks... Here's an update on
some CT boxes:
North Windham Station - Perfectly intact - Chuck does such a great
job putting boxes together! Trails are very wet though. Lots of
hitchhikers had been there, but none for us! :(
You Otter See This - Intact, but very difficult to find. At least
we found it on the first try, unlike several others had noted in the
logbook.
Paddle to the Sea - Intact, but difficult to find (two trails on the
right....so many pine trees....so many stumps....)
Fab Four - Got the first 3 stamps back in October, but was getting
too dark for the others. Got George and Ringo this time. Ringo's
box was wet inside, but the logbook and stamp were okay within the
baggy.
Zoom Zoom Zoom - Intact. The first log book had been filled and
someone had added a second. Is this box supposed to be in full-view
like that?

If anyone has found the "And Then There Were Three" box, please
email me privately at meadow77lark@yahoo.com so I can ask you a
question. We were pretty sure we were in the right location, but
didn't find it...
