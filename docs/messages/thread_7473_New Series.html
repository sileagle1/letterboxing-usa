New Series

From: Kristoffer Buquet (KBuquet@ApertureFoto.com) | Date: 2002-09-06 06:31:35 UTC-04:00
Hello everyone,

If there is going to be anyone in the Shenandoah Valley of VA this
weekend I would like to recommend your attendance at the
Shenandoah Vineyards Annual Harvest Festival.

There is going to be wine tastings, local crafts, kids events, a
BMW car show, etc...

Most important, to ME at least, there I will premier the *FIRST in
a series* of a new series of LetterBoxes that I am currently
working on.

I have teamed up with a few of the local vineyards in the release
of what I call the "Virginia Wine Trail LetterBoxes".

It is a unique mixture of the outdoors, treasure hunting, and
great local wines.

I will not actually *post* the clues until Saturday, sometime
around 1-2 pm, HOWEVER if you are at the Harvest Festival you will
not be able to miss the box. If you would like more details feel
free to contact me.

P.S. All of these boxes will not only win you a stamp
representative of the individual vineyard, but also two others:

a. A Vineyard Specific *First Finder* certificate
b. If you collect all of the stamps in the series, you are then
elidgable to receive a commemorative poster of the series.

Happy Hunting and Tasting!
