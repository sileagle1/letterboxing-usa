Okay, I can see that some areas of the country need more hitchhikers.
Or maybe I just get lucky and happen upon boxes with hitchhikers more
often than other folks. :-) I do enjoy what they do for my "F" count.

And as a reminder, I do NOT suggest that there should be some rule
about hitchhikers.

And you folks who find the hitchhikers (myself included) need to make
an effort to contact the "parents" of these wayward boxes. Part of
the fun of the hitchhiker is seeing where it's been and some people
have not heard from their hitchhikers since they set them free.

Go on over to the letterbox_travellers board and let folks know who
and where and when!

Just get out there and letterbox.
Amanda from Seattle
(who will definitely start spreading those hitchhikers coast to coast-
-silly me was just dropping them at the next box)
