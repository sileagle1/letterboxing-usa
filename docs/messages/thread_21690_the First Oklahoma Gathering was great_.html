the First Oklahoma Gathering was great!

From: ltrboxingrichters (ltrboxingrichters@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-04 20:55:32 UTC

I wanted to brag a few minutes on Mandy. She planned our Cabin
Fever Gathering last Saturday. It was great.

There were at least 22 actual letterboxers there and 15 or more
others. Not bad for a first try. We even had visitors from Rhode
Island, Wanda and Pete traveled down.

There were aprox. 50 boxes hid for the event, food, prizes and a lot
of visiting.

Mandy had done so much planning and preparing. She had many
surprises and had solicited some of you for boxes. Thanks for those
that sent them. They were fun.

Anyway, it was very encouraging and we're pumped to "get out there
and box."

Thanks again Mandy, it wouldn't have happened without you!

the Letterboxing Richters
