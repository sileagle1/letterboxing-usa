From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2003-06-26 02:25:58 UTC
If you have the map / picture / etc in the computer, click on
the "Files" or "Photo" section on the left side of the Yahoo! Groups
page (under home, messages, chat links). You will have to either
create a folder or add the picture to an existing file. Use the
little "browse" button to locate the picture on your disk, then click
upload. Thats it!

If the map or photo you want to put online is not in your PC, you
will either have to take a picture of it with a digital camera or
scan it to get it into picture mode. I would suggest Jpeg format, as
the bitmaps take up a HUGE amount of space and there is already a
shortage of that within the space allotted for our group.

-Steve
