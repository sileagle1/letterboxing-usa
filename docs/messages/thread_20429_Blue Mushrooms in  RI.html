They are growing in leaf litter on top of moist to wet soil. Anyway, please email me privately with any pictures or info that you may have. I would consider you a "fun-gi" or girl!!

Seth,

It sounds like a Spotted Cortinarius mushroom or Spotted Cort for short.
They are bright purple when they emerge and slowly fade to lavendar with
whitish spotted areas. They are not edible...if my memory serves me...but
they sure are pretty.
