Beautiful boxin' day in Southern Ohio

From: BigGuy_45640 (bigguy_45640@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-29 03:08:58 UTC
CardinalMom, Pack Rat, Trail Buddy, and I took advantage of a few
hours of this beautiful afternoon to find a few more of franzsolo and
Salamander's great boxes. We managed to find LeOToO, Fred, A Rose By
Any Other Name, Taking 'Stock #1, 'Shroom With A View, and Grandview
Cemetery without great difficulty. The boxes are all dry and in
great shape, the stamps are wonderful, and the locations beautiful.
We've come to expect no less from these two. Sadly, we were still
unable to locate Kick Up Your Heels but the brush has gotten nearly
inpenetrable - maybe we'll try it again in the fall. We were also
disappointed that the Geometry Frog mystery box seems to be missing.
We're pretty sure we had the right spot - has anyone found this box
lately??? Hope you all got a chance to box today!

Bigguy

PS. We checked on a couple of our boxes today and were honored to see
that Wanda had stamped in on her way home last weekend. Glad we
could add slightly to that awesome F number!
