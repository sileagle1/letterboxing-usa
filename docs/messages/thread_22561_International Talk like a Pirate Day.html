Well, I have to say that we jumped on the idea of planting Pirate
boxes. Thanks for the idea!!!

My husband loves Pirates and we do Renissance faires as Pirates sooo
he has taken this Pirate thing to a new level.

Here is the new box clues called Cap'n Scarneck's Ahoy Matey!! Box.

North Middleton Park, 1701 Waggoners Gap Road, Carlisle, PA 17013

Ahoy ye Scurvy Dogs! T' Dreaded Cap'n Scarneck has hidden some of his
booty on shore fer ye t' find.

Now, I's know yer wonderin' who's Cap'n Scarneck an' what kind of
booty did he be hiden'?!? Well, he be a shameless Pirate who travels
all along the east coast hiden' his wench's carvins'. We don't know
too much else `bout him, `cept he's got a scar on his neck from a
firey battle and 3 salty sea dogs that help him pillage and plunder
everything in sight. Ye never know when `t Cap'n will show up!!!

Now, `t first box - o - loot t' Cap'n Scarneck hid is in t' Ol' Town
of Carlisle. On `t outskirts of town, `t Cap'n an his Wench docked
their boat. ( Arrg....t' Cap'n wasn't pleased t' see t' No Pets
sign... `t salty dogs had to stay at home). He got out of his boat an
walked 40 paces from t' waters edge along t' wooded side, on his
starboard side ther' be a row of trees. He be walkin' 5 steps from t'
blacktop toward t' tree with a small V that starts about 3 feet up.
Behind that tree on t' ground be t' treasure, under some bark. Then
t' Cap'n quickly ran back to his boat an' they took off down t'
creek. Now, be careful ther' be a lot of other pirates launchin'
their boats an' they might be lookin' t' steal ye treasure!!

Happy Treasure Hunting!!
-Ye Dreaded Cap'n Scarneck
