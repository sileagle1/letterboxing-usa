From: Deb T (dlbt123@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-10 17:17:48 UTC-07:00
I also live in RI and know of a couple of libraries
that have boxes in them. I haven't been to find them
yet but I've heard good things about them. I don't
know of any plans to include all libraries (that
sounds very ambitious!). In my travels recently, I was
chatting with a woman who had never heard of
letterboxing before and who was very intrigued with
the whole idea. She is on the board of a local
library and seemed interested in planting a stamp in
the library she is affiliated with. It would be great
to have more "rainy day" boxes to seek!
