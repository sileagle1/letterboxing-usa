southern Florida letterbox report

From: Janet (moonstone_baby@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-03-26 22:13:35 UTC
Mr. Grumpy Turtle & I just came back from 9 glorious days in sunny (&
hot!) southern Florida. I am happy to report that the following
letterboxes were found to be in great shape:

Shh! Its a Secret -- in Ft Lauderdale
Snowflakes are falling in Florida -- in Deerfield Beach
The Conch Republic Challenge -- Key Largo & Key West (boxes 1 - 3)

We could not find the following 2 boxes. This does not mean they are
missing. It just means we could not find them in the time we had
with the clues that were given.

Edge of the Swamp & Deep in the Swamp -- both in Everglades Nat. Park
