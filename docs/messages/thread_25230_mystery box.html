I do not know what box in particular you are talking about, but as a general rule these are some of the things (questions I ask myself)I do to work on solving them:

-does it list a county
-does it list a town
-are there misspellings (sometimes they are intentional)
-is there a play on words
-are some letters capitalized that should not be
-are there key words that can be googled

sometimes in reading a mystery something jumps out at you, but generally you have to play around wit them and see what happens.

I hope I helped you out a little.

Flutterby
