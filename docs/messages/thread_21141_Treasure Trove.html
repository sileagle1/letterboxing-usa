I can't believe no one has mentioned the new book that is out by
Michael Stadther, "A Treasure's Trove". In the story are clues to
hidden treasures in the Continental US. Find the coin and cash it in
for a beautiful designed gem-filled bug worth thousands of dollars.
All total 1 million dollars.

Now that's my kind of hunting!

At least one L'boxer should find a valuable bug.
