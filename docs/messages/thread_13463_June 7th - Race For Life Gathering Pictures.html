Hello All-

On Saturday, Butterfly and Bluebird were nice enough to take me out.
We were quickly joined by Musicwoman. We went galavanting all over
the countryside looking for letterboxes. By 11:00 we had found quite
a few boxes and arrived at the mini-gathering at Montville High
School. It was great to see everyone and swap stories. I have been
out of letterboxing for only a few months but it feels like years. I
loved hearing everyones new stories. It was great! I hope to see
everyone out on the trail soon. Safe Letterboxing!
