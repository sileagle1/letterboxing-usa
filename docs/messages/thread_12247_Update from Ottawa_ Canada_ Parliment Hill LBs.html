Update from Ottawa, Canada. Parliment Hill LBs

From: (letterbox@upperclassglass.com) | Date: 2003-04-23 09:50:00 UTC+01:00
I was up in Ottawa last weekend for a Bonspiel and managed to get part of a day to head out to Parliment Hill for some boxing.

The area is under construction and my friends and I were unable to find the start of the clues for the 3 box set on Parliment Hill - however after much walking and some great luck found the match for the 3rd box in that set.

We found the third box (GREAT hiding spot!) and the first box (we rehid better than we found it, it was partially exposed), but not the 2nd box. I am not willing to say that the second was MIA, because we were out of time and I wasn't able to focus my full attention on a careful search for it.

We also did not have time for searching out the "10 bucks" box - maybe next trip.

We also found the bonus left by Legerdemaine (spelling?)

Thanks to the Secret Gardeners and the Canadian Goose (and Legerdemaine) for a fun adventure!
