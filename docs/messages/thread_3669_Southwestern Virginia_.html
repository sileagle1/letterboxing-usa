From: Jay (ae4mk@1bigred.com) | Date: 2001-11-12 08:54:52 UTC-05:00
Welcome Ben,
I am up in Fredericksburg and take care of the clues on the LbNA site for VA and a dozen more states. The state map on letterboxing.org is up to date. You are correct is stating that there aren't any boxes near you. I was the one who planted the Mt. Rogers boxes last Feb. I think the lack of boxes nearby is a real problem for some people, but my advice is to do what I did. Plant some. My count is P47 F26 right now because I made a decision to get out in my area and plant. This has slowly brought a few more boxes into my area also, not as many as I would like but a few.
 
And this is how LBing will grow in your area.  Like the movie saying..   If you plant them they will come..  Personnelly I enjoy the planting and clue writting almost as much as adding another stamp to my log.
 
Jay C.
