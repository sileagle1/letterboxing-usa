Letterbox Gather

From: Wendell Jones (mstrwndl@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-17 00:27:10 UTC
I just wanted to take a second and a few lines to remind everyone
that "The One In Tenn. Letterbox Gather" in Nashville, TN is coming up
this spring. The date on this event is March 17th. It is St. Patty's
Day. There are several event stamps (including the official event stamp
that will travel afterwards), lots of PT's, 20 or more traditional
boxes, and several St. Patrick's Day boxes. It is not going to be
anything fancy; no bells or whistles, justa place for boxers to gather
and exchange. The only requirment is that you sign up on the AQ
website. There are going to be geocachers present to help us learn more
about that hobby and hopefully so they can learn more about
letterboxing. The Metro parks director is inviting some of her friends
who are geocachers. In order to keep the numbers at a reasonable level,
we ask that those who plan to attend sign up on AQ so that we can make
ssure we have clues especially for you. Those not signed up will not
have a clue package, and there will not be spares.
