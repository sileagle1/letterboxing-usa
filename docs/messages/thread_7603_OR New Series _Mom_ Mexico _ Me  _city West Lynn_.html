Mom, Mexico & Me.

This series is a stamp collection nothing compared to the likes of Lynn Canyon stature (tee hee)could not resist Ryan...but is to celebrate the great trip my mom and I had in Mexico.

Location: MARY S. YOUNG STATE RECREATION AREA
City: West Lynn
County: Clackamas County
Seven Letterboxes
Stamp Quality: Is in the eye of the beholder.
You will need a black marker to fill in the coconuts.

Placed: September 9, 2002 by the Canada Goose

Park in the main lot at the end of the road. Find the wood map and head onto the trail toward the River ViewPoint.

Past twins with bent necks you will hang a right.
