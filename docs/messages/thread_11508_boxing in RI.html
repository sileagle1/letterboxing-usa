I'm so happy to finally have a report for y'all. On Monday, I
visited Moonstone Baby's "The Dog Days of Summer" at the Great Swamp.
I had a great time. This series was lots of fun. The day was
glorious; lots of activity on the impoundment, barely any mud, really
cute stamps. I can't wait to meet Katie, Indy and Skye in person.

well. As luck would have it, I ran into the illustruous Butterfly on
my travels and had a petie tour of her neighborhood State Park. Lots
of reasons to go back.
Last Friday, I planted a box "Strong to the Finish" on the banks of
the Blackstone River. Clues will be up shortly.
It feels good to be outside again.

Refreshingly,
