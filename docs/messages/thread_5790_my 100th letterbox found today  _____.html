my 100th letterbox found today !!!!!

From: moonstone_baby (moonstone_baby@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-06-18 02:00:32 UTC
I went out for a great day of letterboxing with Butterfly & Angel.
We went up to meet the Oxen Traveler on the trail. We caught up with
the oxen on the trail just before Rt 203. The oxen were moving right
along and were very well behaved. This was my first hike with
oxen... a new experience. Best of luck to Carol & her "pack" on the
rest of their journey.

After the oxen hike we headed down to Norwich to Mohegan Park for the
Indian and the Fountain boxes. If you have not been to this park it
is a lovely place. Sadly I must report that box #2 appears to be
missing. The other boxes are all ok.

And ......

I found my 100th found letterbox here! I started letterboxing
exactly 4 months ago.

Happy trails to all,

Moonstone_baby

P5 F100 X53
