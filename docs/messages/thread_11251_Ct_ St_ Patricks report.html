Ct. St. Patricks report

From: mothermoo2001 (cstearns07@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-03-17 12:24:44 UTC
The Erin Go Bragh series was our quest yesterday myself litle Cody
and the Cowboy ventured out. What a sliipery wet walk we snagged all
five boxes while fighting bears with sticks, seeing and imaginary
Irish Wedding and searching for an elusive Pot O' Gold! What fun!
Of course between stomping feet then the Cowboys rather large fall
into water we had to race back to the car to get warm and dry so the
Pa. Quarter is for another day. The water Cowboy dipped into must
have been mighty cold. His humor stayed with him as we went back and
the bag of out grown clothes got Cody dry and my spare socks etc
from my pack warmed the Cowboy. It was a great series all in good
shape. I'll go back and nab the quarter with Pink Trotters one day.
We had planned all the new Coventry but opted home and dry then went
on to get the KJ's best for Cody and Daybreak along with ice cream. A
great day. Hallelujah for 60 degrees! Leader of the Pack
