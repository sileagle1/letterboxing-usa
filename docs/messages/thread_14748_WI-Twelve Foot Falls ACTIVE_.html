Before I forget to announce it again, for all you WI letterboxers, I DID
FIND the Twelve Foot Falls LB on my trip a few weeks ago. I was the first
there in over two years. The box is in surprisingly good shape, but the
stamp isn't too thrilling. If you attempt to go find it, be aware that you
must travel on dirt roads for a few miles, and they remind one of driving on
a washboard. The falls are nice. The clues are vague as suggested. If you
want more detailed clues, just drop me an email.
