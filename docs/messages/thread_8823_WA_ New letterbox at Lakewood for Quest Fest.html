There is yet another new letterbox at the site of the Quest Fest in
Lakewood!

Fledgeling Letterbox
Planted by Amanda from Seattle
Clues:
Find the Old Insane Asylum Building. As you are facing the broken
down old building, start walking around it counter-clockwise. Take
the first trail to your right. Directly ahead, you will see a tall
stump (or a short snag?!). When the tree fell, it looks like it split
into pieces on the ground behind the stump. At the "V" where these
pieces meet under a large piece of bark is the Fledgeling Letterbox.
