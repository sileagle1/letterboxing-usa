Hi Mike!

As for Letterbox #2 in the Tualatin Hills Nature Park series. I did the
same thing you did and thought the box was missing....but it's there.
You must keep going and going and looking for the rock with the
predator. Eventually, you'll figure out why you didn't see the rock
before -- because it's not where you were looking. Same as me.

Tom Cheney did a great job on this series. I've heard some of the boxes
are missing. I've gotten the first four, but have yet to hunt for the
others.

My Rick's Farm series is at the same park and, hopefully, most of them
are easily gotten (except for the missing three).

Another great series that is supposed to be a pretty nice bunch is the
Rock Creek Neighborhood series placed by Der Mad Stamper and Thom
Cheney. I haven't nabbed those myself, but I hear they are great finds!

"Who Let The Dogs Out" in Forest Park is fun and has gotten high marks
especially from children! It's not too long a hike and the letterboxes
are not too tough. That series was kid-tested by my nephew Travis and
he really enjoyed it. Other kids have really had a good time seeking
out this series. Your kid will probably enjoy it, too! Dog #2, Rover
has been missing since spring, so forget about him. I hear the others
are still there, though!

Happy Letterboxing!
