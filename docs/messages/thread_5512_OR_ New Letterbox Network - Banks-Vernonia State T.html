OR: New Letterbox Network - Banks-Vernonia State Trail

From: Brett (bcostley@agalis.net) | Date: 2002-05-27 11:48:16 UTC-07:00

On May 26, 2002 Brett and Penny started the Banks-Vernonia State Trail Letterbox Network in the counties of Washington and Columbia, OR near the towns of Banks and Vernonia. There are currently four boxes in the network. Clues to one box are placed on the internet, you must find this box to continue exploration of the network.

 

See: http://home.agalis.net/bcostley/Letterbox/banksvernonia.htm for details on the network concept and the clues to the starting box.

 

I’d like to here what others think about this concept – please email or post!

 

Thanks
