The Star Letterbox.
Placed by Aryn of Eagle on 3 September 2002
Star, ID
Ada County

Directions: Off of Highway 44 at the Star Merc head
south on Main St to the southern most point where you
can park on the dirt. You will be able to see a rope
swing and a bridge.

Description: Easy walking or you can float down the
river for this letterbox. If you choose to float to this box,
beware the rapids along the way (they're bordered by concrete
structures and large rocks).

Clues: Swing off the rope swing and float to the
bridge or walk from the rope swing in search of the
three large water turn keys. Continue West on
whichever trail you want and go under the bridge
(beware of all the cobwebs). Under the Northwest side
of the bridge there is a small opening under the
concrete with more spiderwebs hiding the STAR
letterbox.

Please replace the rocks to hide the letterbox out of
sight. Go back to the rope swing and hang out with
the locals and swing into the refreshing Boise River.
One of our favorite hangouts.
