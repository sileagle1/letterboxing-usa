Condition of boxes at Rocky Neck

From: Thomsen, Karen CONT (CSG2) (thomsenk@csg2.navy.mil) | Date: 2001-06-04 08:03:21 UTC-04:00
Hi Everyone!

Yesterday while 'boxing at Rocky Neck (East Lyme, CT) I found that Box # 1
had water in it. I tried to dry it out as best I could. The stamp and the
log book were still OK. Only the info sheet was ruined.

I was not able to find Box # 2 at all. I searched for a long time and
finally gave up. But I could have been looking in the wrong place, I don't
know.

I located Box # 3 with no problem, but I found it covered with ants. I
mean, *swarming* with them! They had built their nest in the dirt under and
around the sides of the box. It was disgusting to watch them scurrying to
save their ugly little white eggs! I felt that I had to replace the box in
the same place or else no one else would be able to find it. Maybe I
stirred them up enough that they will go build their nest elsewhere!

Also in Box # 3, the cover of the ink pad is broken, with one side entirely
missing.

Karen
