From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2003-10-07 10:36:28 UTC
Kids Day 2002 was also our first dabbling in letterboxing. And Rocky
Neck is a special place for us as we held our wedding reception there
in October last year as well!

What I was kind of thinking was something like a "Connecticut
Icebreaker" in say... January or February? I'm just not sure where
to have such an event as you will have to have some indoors facility
for folks to get warmed up in -- Heidi and I don't really look
forward to having to use our EMT skills : ) We think Nov 2nd is a
bit quick to plan an event and it seems that Ginger Meets Molly was
the big event for the fall. Maybe it is even best to wait until
spring thaw? I know we are very busy for the next 2-3 weeks with work
and life in general (and I know many other boxers who are busy
too!).

Also a consideration is that once November swings in there will be a
lot of folks getting busy with the holidays etc. If we can come to a
consensus on the "theme" and a general time frame it will give folks
a lot of cold days and nights to get carving by the fireside!

-Steve
