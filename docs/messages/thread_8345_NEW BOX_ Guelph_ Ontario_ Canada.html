Silvercreek Trail Letterbox

In Guelph, Ontario. From the 401 take either exit 299 (Brock Rd 46)
or exit 295 (Hanlon Hwy 6). From Edinburgh Road (north/south)and
College Ave (east/west). Drive north on Edinburgh. From Edinburgh and
Wellington. Drive south a few yards to the parking areas.

3 parking options.
Driving north on Edinburgh, just before the pedestrian lights:
Option 1) turn right into the park.
Option 2) turn left onto Manor Park Crescent
Option 3) drive over the Speed River, turn right into park just before
Wellington Road.

The letterbox is 350m from the first parking lot listed above.
