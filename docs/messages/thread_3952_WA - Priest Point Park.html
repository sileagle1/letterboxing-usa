Olympia, Washington
Thurston County
Priest Point Park
Lost Loon Letterboxes
These birds ventured away from the water and
lost their way among the maze of trails.
Here are the clues to help you find them.

Take Interstate 5 to Port of Olympia exit.
Plum St. SE will become East Bay Dr.
Follow East Bay Dr. to Priest Point Park Entrance.

Start at the Ellis Cove Trailhead.

If choices there be, take the trail with the rail.....

Along the trail look for the tree with the black bear cub.

Continue on the trail until you come to a three section bridge-like structure.
Each section is a different direction.
At the beginning of the structure on the right is a tree that Y's near the base.
The box is on the ground in the back.

Continue your trek until you reach a white capped 'Trail Head' post.
Go 280 degrees from the top of this post.

Shun 'Service' in favor of 180 degrees.

Go 270 when you can.
