I just knew that somehow we'd be getting back to Naked
Letterboxing.....

.....my .02 regarding the religious HH....I think that
it's perfectly fine to plant your own religious themed
letterbox that you plan to maintain, care for, etc.
It's your box, you can do what you like. However, when
I find religious themed calling cards in my box, that
"I" plant and maintain...then it's not okay. Totally
overstepping a boundary. I don't appreciate my box
being used as a "soapbox" by anyone but myself! When
the calling cards appear in my boxes and boxes I
maintain...they go straight to the trash....I don't
care if I agree or not. It's my box. Unless it's a HH,
don't put it in my box. If it's a religious themed HH,
move it along quick......or I will certainly send it
to someone near a religious themed series where like
minded people can share it.

--Armadillo Jo
