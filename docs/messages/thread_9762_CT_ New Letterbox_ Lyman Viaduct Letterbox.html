Lyman Viaduct Letterbox
Planted: December 21, 2002 by Music Woman
Town: Colchester, CT
Difficulty: Easy, flat railroad bed
Length: 1/2 mile or so round trip

To Start from Bull Hill Road: (from Points North): Take Route 2 East to exit
13. At the end of the ramp, take a right onto Route 66. At your first
traffic light, take a left onto South Main Street. About 1/2 a mile down,
South Main will veer to the left. Turn right (kinda more of a straight) onto
South Road. Take a right onto Bull Hill Road. (Once on Bull Hill, look to
your right, there is an private airport community. All (or most) of the
houses have hangers attached. Cool huh?) Keep on Bull Hill Road for
approximately three miles. Just after the christmas tree farm on your right,
turn right into the Air Line Trail parking lot.
