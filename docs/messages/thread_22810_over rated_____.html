I'll just put a note in with my hitchhikers requesting that they never be
transported to New Hampshire. Hopefully that should lessen my chances of
ending up with any hijacked hitchhikers. Since some folks seem to respect

thru any unwritten courtesies based on respect for an artist, putting such a
note in with my hitchhikers should work, right?

gingerbreadjunk, maybe I'd likely just go ahead and attempt to restore it to
it's original travelling condition. If he feels he can change an artist's
work, I guess I could feel that I could restore it. If he feels he's some

in different ways, y'know? :-)
