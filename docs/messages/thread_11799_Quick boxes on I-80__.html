In NJ, these boxes are along the i-80 corridor and don't involve more than
an hour of hiking round-trip:
Mount Hope; Mount Hope, NJ
The Tourne; Boonton, NJ
There's a mystery box that's also very close to I-80.

Yesterday, I placed a box in West Paterson, right off I-80, and I ought to
have the clues done in a week or two.

When you get to the NJ-PA border , you'll drive right between the Mt.
Tammany (NJ) and Mt. Minsi (PA) boxes, but those involve a bit more of a
climb than you're probably looking for. The Delaware Water Gap box in
Blairstown, NJ, though, is about a one-hour hike RT.

Then, just inside PA, my Pinebrook Park box is an easy one not too far from
Exit 307, then 10 minutes north on Route 191.

In Illinois, check out the Lockport I&M Canal Letterbox in Lockport (I've
not found this one, but was doing some planning last week for some upcoming
travel and marked it on a map, and I see it's close to I-80).

In Nebraska, the Esperanto and Homing Pigeon Letterboxes are literally
adjacent to I-80. The park is an interesting place, and ought to help you
acclimate to Californians! :-o
