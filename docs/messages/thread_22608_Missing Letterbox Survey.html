From: Rick in Boca (rick_in_boca@bigfoot.com) | Date: 2005-09-20 22:26:08 UTC
Letterboxes go missing all the time and we seem to just accept it.

I'd propose that we get a bit more scientific about how these go
missing, and how to prevent it.

If you have ever planted a letterbox and had it go missing, I've
designed a ten-question survey for you. You may respond for as many
letterboxes as have gone missing, if the circumstances are different.

At the conclusion, we should know more about good planting.

If you have ever had a letterbox go missing, click here to take a
survey (multiple responses are allowed):
http://www.surveymonkey.com/s.asp?u=830251344185

After 100 responses, I will post the results in the Files section of
the Letterbox-USA Yahoo Group.
