Just posted to the LbNA website is a new Central NY letterbox:

Dalliance Letterbox is a micro-box
Note: there are other boxes in this park, watch for more to be posted
here. The LbNA website also has "Tom's Letterbox".
Placed: 7/31/03 By: catbead, The Fly Doc and their trusty friend The
Mollusk

Terrain: this is an easy walk, take the kids & push the stroller. Some
very minor climbing is involved for the retrival. Less than 2 miles
round trip, in fact it's probably less than a mile. How long it takes
depends on how fast you move.

Where: Green Lakes State Park
7900 Green Lakes Rd.
Fayetteville, NY 13066
#315-637-6111

There is a fee ($6-7.00) to enter the park, or free with your N.Y.
Empire Pass. The fee is charged in the summer months. Household pets
only are allowed; caged or on a leash not more than 6'. Pets are not
allowed in the bathing areas. The park has 10 miles of trails and an
18-hole golf course designed by Robert Trent Jones. Camping, cabins,
boat rentals, biking, beach, picnic, pavilions, fishing, cross country
skiing and more.

Syracuse is centrally located in New York State, at the cross roads of
New York Thruway 90 (east & west) and Interstate 81 (north & south).
Green Lakes State Park in Fayetteville is to the east of Syracuse, NY.
