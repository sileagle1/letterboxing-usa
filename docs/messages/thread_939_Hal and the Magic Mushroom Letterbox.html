Hal and the Magic Mushroom Letterbox
Washington, DC

Once upon a time in the Land of Mary, there was a lad named Hal who went
in search of the magic mushroom. It is told that the magic mushroom
would lead to his one true love and lots of fun. He began his journey in
the Capital City with the second wisest wizard of the land. To find his
home Hal was told to go to Mass and Observe, there he would find the way
to the old seafaring stargazers home and the second wisest wizard. When
Hal arrived at the Naval Observers gate he met the wizard who said to
"look beyond the gray road and follow the yellow brick road to
Nordstrom’s Place through the woods. When you come to an M carved in a
tree, kiss it three times, turn around and if your heart is true, your
declaration of your love will appear." From where he declared his love
for Mary, a gray squirrel whispered “what you seeks can be found under
the white stone in the death log just 30 paces from here at 130 degrees.
Go there and stamp the mushroom two times and see what fun you have
found in the land of your love."

Placed by twograysquirrels@aol.com on June 3, 2000.

Good luck, Squirrel
