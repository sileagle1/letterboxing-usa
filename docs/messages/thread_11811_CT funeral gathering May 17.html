From: (cherieph@aol.com) | Date: 2003-04-08 20:57:42 UTC-04:00
All are invited to come and pay your last respects to poor Gerbil Anon who
will have a fitting burial after we gather to remember his short life.
Join us at Hop Brook Park in Middlebury, Ct on May 17 near noon for some
funeral food and libation. Then, after we celebrate Gerbil's life, hunt for
some Presidents, share stamps and prizes, you will have the opportunity to
caravan to the cemetary for his interment.
Please bring food to share (crock pots are OK-we have electricity there), HH
to disburse, and anything else that suits your fancy for such an event.
Let me know who and how many of you will be coming so the proper amount of
paper products may be obtained in advance.
There is a per car charge for this park; it may be $4, so be prepared.
Additions and corrections to this info will be posted as it becomes
available.
BookWorm, JustBeth, RTRW, and other mourners
