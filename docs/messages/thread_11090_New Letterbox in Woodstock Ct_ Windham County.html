The Troop 25 Letterbox

Placed: 3-8-03
Placed by: Teach & Preach
Location: Woodstock Ct.
Rated: Easy 5-10mins.

In Woodstock go 171 west. Turn right onto Roseland Park Rd. Go
straight at the four corners. Look for a sign on your right that
says Roseland Park 1876 pull in there. At the Y go left, park just
past the Restrooms on your right in a small parking area. Looking
out towards the water, go right. Head towards the second grill in
the middle section. Take a reading of 255 degrees to a stone slab.
The front right corner hide the Troop 25 Letterbox. This is a public
park for fishing, bringing the kids to play on the playground, and
BBQ's. Best time is in the spring and summer. The park does close
at sundown. Have fun. Teach & Preach
