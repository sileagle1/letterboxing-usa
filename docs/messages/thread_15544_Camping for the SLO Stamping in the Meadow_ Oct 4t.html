From: Sir Balthazar (neovolatile@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-03 23:15:00 UTC
For those of you who wish to tent camp at the October 4th, SLO
Stamping in the Meadow event. Here is some information:

San Luis Obispo Parks - El Chorro (just outside San Luis Obispo)
would be the best, I think but you might also like some other locales
like Oceano or Lopez Lake, are at
http://SLOCountyparks.com I believe their fees are about $19 a night
and are suitable for 6-8 people, maybe 4 tents.

Sand and Surf at http://www.sandandsurf.com/ is a private one in
Oceano. They are $27 a night, no more than 4 people, 1 tent and 1
car. They have many more facilities like swimming pool and laundry.

Morro Bay State Park at http://www.parks.ca.gov/default.asp?
page_id=594
also has campsites for tents. This number and site also includes
camping at Montana del Oro. These sites are first come, first served.
Phone 805-772-2560

Hope this helps some,
Sir Balthazar
