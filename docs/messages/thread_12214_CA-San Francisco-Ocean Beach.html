CA-San Francisco-Ocean Beach

From: james_blu22 (james_blu22@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-04-22 00:32:43 UTC-07:00
Hey check this one out! It's beautiful!

Difficulty: Easy
pets: with leash on trail

To the big city,
Where it's nice and windy
Park in the lot,
And hope there's a spot!
Take the main trail
Where the man holds his dog by a tail
Go over the hump and to the left
To the bench, but don't rest yet
Head down to the ocean and to the square,
Off to your right the Golden Gate is there!
Enjoy for a moment the crashing wave
And look behind you at the tree cave.
Look for the sideways tree where,
Underneath it you'll find me there!

Good luck, let me know if you have any troubles.
jaymez
