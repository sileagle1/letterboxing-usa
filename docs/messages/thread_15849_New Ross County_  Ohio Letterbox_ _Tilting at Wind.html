Title: Tilting at Windmills. Placed 9-17-03 by Salamander. Location:
Chillicothe, Ross County, Ohio. Difficulty: Easy. If my memory
serves me correctly, I believe the phrase "tilting at windmills"
refers to a pointless pursuit. But I am hopeful that you will find
that this quest is not at all pointless and that you will be pleased
will the results. CLUES: Go to the upper parking lot near the
gymnasium at Ohio University- Chillicothe. A stairway leads from
here to the street below and to a lower level entrance to the
building. Go down the stairs. At the bottom of the stairway, turn
left and walk 14 paces. Stop. You should be standing at the end of
the sidewalk, with the westernmost edge of a brick wall immediately
to your left. The letterbox is hidden under the rocks at the base of
the opposite side of the wall, about one foot in from the edge.
