From: Connie Terry (connie@lavernia.net) | Date: 2004-09-30 17:50:00 UTC-05:00
I haven't tried it on MasterCarve, but I think you should be able to
slice through it with dental floss-loop the dental floss around the
piece right where you want the cut to be and then slowly pull the ends
to collapse the loop. You might have to get someone to hold the block
steady for you because you are trying to cut on a small edge to begin
with.

Corazon
