Pirate Ship spotted on Lake Michigan!

From: dagonell2001 (salley@klaatu.canisius.edu) | Date: 2004-04-23 14:46:41 UTC
I was Chicago last weekend for the "11th Annual Midwest Artificial
Intelligence and Cognitive Science Conference" (In my personal life,
I'm a buccaneer pirate; in my professional life, I'm about as geek as
they come! :D) While I didn't have a whole lot of free time because
of the conference, I did manage to do some letterboxing.

Architectural Mystery -- alive and well

Cars-On-A-Stick -- probably AWOL. I know I was at the correct rock
and bush, but I simply couldn't find it and I scratched my arm up
looking for it.

Fallen Star -- alive and well

Bee's Knee's #3 -- alive and well I was briefly confused for a couple
of minutes. The clue "Defy the next arrow you see" needs to be
changed to "Defy the second arrow you see".

Painted Turtle -- alive and well I also found a traveller, "Soaring
Seagull" For one moment, I had this vivid image of the BC cartoon of
the turtle with the bird on his back. :D The seagull returned with me
for a long voyage home in the crow's nest of my ship. I could tell
you where I released him, but then I'd have to make you walk the plank! :D
