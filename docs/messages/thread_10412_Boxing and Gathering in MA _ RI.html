Boxing and Gathering in MA & RI

From: Brian, Ryan & Lori <bconnoll@marksonrosenthal.com> (bconnoll@marksonrosenthal.com) | Date: 2003-01-21 16:40:57 UTC
TeamGreenDragon had the best time at the MA gathering, thank you Kim
and Chris.
We picked up all of the temporary event stamps, got a ton of
exchanges (Thanks All), and even logged a few Hitchhikers.
Also we found the new permanent "Billings Barn" box (This is a great
stamp, Jay!!) and one we had missed last time we where here "Fertile
Frond".
Finding "Sister Road, Sister Town" in the dark with Wanda & Pete,
Music Women, LetterBoxer2002, and Moonstone Baby was a great treat
and our son Ryan was thrilled. We found the following Sunday, "A
Little Butterfly", "North Camp", "Fallen Leaves" (A very nice
trail), "Carolina North", "Carolina South", and finally found "Frosty
Drew". We picked up the "Swift Passage" HH at "Carolina South" and
will give it a good home next week.

Brian
TeamGreenDragon

P.S.

Being mentioned in a news article is going to make Ryan so happy!!
