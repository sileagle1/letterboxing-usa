Mass. Mud Season!

From: adelcoll (adelcoll@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-03-29 23:30:59 UTC
Our crew went out to find the Angel Bear Letterbox (Wilson Mountain,
Dedham) and the Tree for All Season box (Chase Woodlands, Dover).

We found both but the mud and water on the trails at Chase Woodlands
were daunting! We had a little trouble following the number signs on
the way to the box -- I think our eyes were focused on the ground to
watch for mud puddles. What are probably seasonal little streams are
flowing over the trail in several places. The pretty stamp was worth
the hike but I recommend wearing your old hikers if you go any time
soon!

There is also some mud at Wilson Mountain park, but most of the time
it could be avoided by sticking to the side of the trail.

You know it was a long winter when we're willing to slog through the
mud to enjoy the warming weather!
