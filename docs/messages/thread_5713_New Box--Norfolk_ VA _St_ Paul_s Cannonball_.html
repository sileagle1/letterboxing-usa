"St. Paul's Cannonball"
Date:  June 11, 2002
Location:  VA -- Norfolk (Southeastern VA)


"St. Paul's Cannonball" is located in downtown Norfolk.


Long ago Dunmore lost his cool
forgot to live by the golden rule.
Lost a fight before the fall
fired his cannon into St. Paul's wall.

Today in the churchyard, next to a mall
if you search real hard, you'll find the ball.
There are two surprises for you to find
one is Lord Dunmore's, the other is mine.

Find sweet John who was only seven,
he's next to Fredrick, they are both in heaven.
Next to the two, grows an unusual tree
and there inside is the gift from me.

 
Enjoy your search!
