DIRECTIONS TO Lacy Woods Park:

From West: Take I66 East exit to Glebe road.  Turn left and head N. on Glebe road.  Take a left on to Washington Blvd (2nd stop light). Turn left on to George Mason Dr; this will be the first traffic light.  Park along the street by the Lacy Woods picnic pavilion sign.

From East:  Take I66 West exit at Glebe road.  Turn Left and head S. on Glebe.  Take a Right onto Washington Blvd (1st stop light).  Turn left on to George Mason Dr; this will be the first traffic light.  Park along the street by the Lacy Woods picnic pavilion sign.

Directions

        Starting at your car walk up the steps to the green fence.

        Take 48 medium steps from the fence down the path towards the pavilion. 

        Stop

        Turn right (There should be a small tree directly behind you  ~3 feet off the path)

        Take 16 steps 311 degrees towards a 6 foot Holly tree.  Behind the Holly is a very tall tree.

        Between the Holly and a log under sticks and leave is what you seek.

        Happy Chinese New Year.  Please make sure its hidden well when you leave.
