Ct. Birds update

From: NLS (jsperrazza@snet.net) | Date: 2003-04-18 18:02:35 UTC-04:00
As announced , the" Rocky Neck Winter Camping is for the Birds" have
began their flight to the Northwest Park. The Birds have new log books,
some of the old stamps have been re worked and there are two new birds
and one surprise added to the mix. We , friends, want to thank everyone
who visited the series this winter. It proved popular and the log books
were full of wonderful comments and images of stamps that we will all
treasure. Now as they move north and form a new series, we hope they
will give enjoyment to many more folks, and we invite old friends to
check them out for the additions, and changes to the stamps. Their new
home is a great place with other letterboxes on the trails within the
park..
Watch for them , the clues soon to be posted as a new series with a new
name, on the Rainbow Trail.
