Patterson Park Baltimore City

Patterson Park is a unique combination of borders, and allows for a place for
all baltimoreans to congregate and enjoy its rolling hills and teasing views
of the waterfront. It has recently undergone a renaissance thanks to the
friends of patterson park. The pagoda finished renovations in 2002 and the
boat lake was reopened in April 2003. This box is to celebrate the boat
lake and its return of the patterson park turtles.
