Blossom is on a mission! Help Her save the World before Bedtime!! This lettercache has two matching schoolbox size containers and is in an ammo box.

Park here: N 38d 44.25__ W 89d 55.71 . At the parking lot at the south side of the Rte 162, Old Troy Road intersection.


Parking on any of the side roads or closer to the cache will most likely lead to
TOWING.
This is known as the SchoolHouse trail.
Head South.
Round the bend.
Past the old farm equipment and lame bugs.
Keen ears will pick up on the quail's whistle.
Pass the yield sign & private access.
Pass the stop sign and street crossing ...
Hold your kids' hands, the cars don't stop.
Pass the 3960' marker.
As you near the location, you'll pass a box culvert. The upstream end of which is in someone's back yard.
Begin counting 38 strides.
(remember a stride is 2 foot falls).
This is the spot!
Turn left, SOUTH.
You should be in a cut section of the trail ... like a valley with the banks sloping up on both sides.
The hiding place is at the base of a Power Pole covered with branches.
