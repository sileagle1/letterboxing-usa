Log into www.yahoogroups.com and pull up the letterbox-usa group
(click on the 'mygroups' link and it'll show you all the groups to
which you've subscribed. Then when you get to the homepage for it,
look for the icon somewhere on the page that asks "Edit Membership"
(just above the dark blue line that says letterbox-usa on it). Pull
up the options there and you can modify your subscription from
individual emails to digest form (one email of a days' worth of
messages or some fixed number of messages) or set it to no emails at
all and then you can peruse the postings on the message board
instead (the one I prefer because you can skip over messages that
don't appeal to you).

You can do this for any/all of your yahoogroups to which you belong
and it will really cut down on your email inbox traffic.

Hope this helps,
