And on my list of interesting stories is a box from the rose park "Elizabeth Park"
in Hartford, Ct. Having previously lived in CT I was delighted to return to the
area in search of boxes. Well, it was one of those instances where the clue was
written so beautifully that it brought you around a scenic tour of the entire park
showing me places that I didn't even know (having lived nearby) and ultimately
finding the box just steps from where we initially began - and never even
realizing it until long afterwards. It was after this box that I began to understand
the fine art of writing a clue so that you enjoy the spot to the fullest! Thank you
to whomever planted that box - this story was long overdue!
