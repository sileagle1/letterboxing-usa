THE DUCK DUCK GOOSE LETTERBOX

Loantaka Reservation, Morris Twp
Walk is approx. half a mile, paved, level.

Clue:
Where Woodland Avenue ends in South Street
take a left on the latter.
(for a map of the area, or to find another way:
search maps.yahoo.com or mapquest
for "Continental Ave, Morristown NJ",
you will easily see Woodland and South).

Soon, after the stables on your left,
you will see the main entrance
of the Loantaka Reservation.
Park here, put on warm gear (if it's cold)
and get ready for your journey of half a mile.

Walk on the paved path away from South St,
past the playground. Follow the paving
when it curves right, goes over a tiny bridge,
and left into the woods.
