Re: [LbNA] Re: Mystery boxes

From: Eric Polk (ericpolk@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-07-12 10:34:37 UTC-07:00
Not every letterbox gets listed on this site. Some people list theirs on their own private websites. Other boxes are only circulated by word of mouth or as bonus boxes when you find another box. I have heard of sporting goods stores that maintain a binder with clues in it.

You could also try checking the old site (the link can be found on the landing page of the current site) to see if any are listed there. I would also try searching the Yahoo! group archive to see if there are any clues posted there.
