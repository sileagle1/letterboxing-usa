From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2003-04-30 22:40:14 UTC-07:00
Hey Everyone!

I'm thinking it might be about time for another Letterboxing gathering
in the Portland area. It's been a while. We had a wonderful gathering
in Portland in August 2002 at Upper MacLeay Park organized and hosted by
Funhog, which was a blast.

In November 2002 Bethany, a.k.a. Dvntrcker or however she spells it,
hosted an event that exceeded expectation, food, and letterboxing fun in
the Olympia/Tacoma area!!! (South Sound Chili is outstanding and easy,
by the way!) Lots of great letterboxers showed up for that and God
blessed us with a beautiful day and a wonderful view of Mt. Rainier.

I'm thinking Oxbow Park in the Gresham/Boring area might be a good place
to host. It does cost an admission fee of $3 per car, though, unless
you have a Metro Parks pass, and if that is cost-prohibitive I'd be
willing to take other suggestions. There are two letterboxes there
already -- Cara's Oxbow Letterbox #1 -- (#2 has been missing forever)
and Funhog's Riverbend Box. It's a large and wonderful park that would
have lots of good hiding of letterbox opportunities. It is right on the
Sandy River, too, for those who enjoy canoeing, kayaking, innertubing or
swimming.

I'd appreciate input on what you think of the idea and appropriate dates
for such an event. The end of July is out for me, otherwise, I'm open
to all ideas.

Best regards,

Amanda Briles
The Paisley Orca

Hello one and all! I am the nakedone living the wonderful life in
Portland. I have been completely enraptured by the idea of
letterboxing now that I know of it. A friend showed me the site while

some local names in the few letterboxes I've found in the Greater
Portland area.

little "treaure hunt" makes it all the better.

If there's a gathering, let me know and I'll be there!

Hope to hear from you soon!
