Dearest Jena, My Dragonfly, My Blue Lady,

First you say you love me for my misquoting Gorey - *Blush* I had
done an admirable job of getting my daughter registered for school
and then was finishing a PowerPoint presentation I needed for today's
class, so I was tired - now you tell me of this boyfriend, who
obviously does not appreciate you nearly enough. So what if you are
insane? Joan of Arc heard voices, too! I know I am insane about you.
*Sigh*

Meanwhile, why use pen, at all? I do them blindfolded or just
mentally. If one has to use a pen, I find a good cane or goose quill
flat nib is just fine. And yes, I really can do uncial, black letter,
and a few other scripts.

Hoping that this lover's spat of ours is not the thing causing the
bickering about the 'base on the board. Surely we shall never be so
base as to be bored with each other!

Stop me before I open a vein with a Two Cherries 0.5 mm gouge,
Sir Balthazar
