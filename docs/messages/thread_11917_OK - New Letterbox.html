Prairie Pug Letterbox
Elmer Thomas Park
Lawton, OK
Comanche County
Placed: April 12, 2003
Placed by: The Pug Lovers

Difficulty: Easy
Time: 10 minutes

Elmer Thomas Park is located off of Hwy 44 and Cache Road. Park on the
south side of the park at 6th St. and Ferris next to the Museum of the Great
Plains. Go to the sign for Elmer Thomas Park that contains the rules of the
park. Take the concrete path north. Pass the log fortress to the west and
enjoy the many prairie dogs that inhabit the park. Take the dirt path at
250 degrees towards the bridge. As you approach the bridge, the box is
under the closest left corner. You need to squat down facing east to see
it. Thanks for visiting the Prairie Pug Letterbox:)
