What an addictive little hobby. Me and the gang only found out about this
last Thursday and we have already placed 2 boxes and found 4. (P2F4, how
cool that you have your own language!)

Thankfully, one of our gang is very talented with carving. (Go Sarah! She
also makes fabulous fairy dolls if anyone is interested) We are currently
working on a trio of stamps for the South Mountain State Battlefield and hope
to plant them soon. We were searching for the Washington Monument stamp late
Saturday evening and I drove us to the battlefield in error. My car battery
died but luckily there was a commemorative event going on so a helpful ranger
jump-started my car. Since we have already been poking around and no one has
yet to "box" the joint, we are more than happy to oblige.
