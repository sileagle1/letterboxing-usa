Dear DrewClan -
Just wanted to let you know that my daughter and I found your
letterbox at Great Bay in Durham, NH on New Year's Day. I think we
were the first ones to find it because all the other stamps appeared
to belong to Drew Clan members! It was harder because of a foot of
fresh snow, but we didn't want to get caught in the storm on the way
upstate on Saturday and post-poned our search until Monday. We
really enjoyed stomping around in the snow, and were glad that we
were still able to follow the clues. We just had to watch out for
the duck hunters!
Hope to do more Drew Clan letterboxes soon, even if we need to
dig in the snow!

Deena and Shelley
