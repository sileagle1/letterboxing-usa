From: roberta a stillson (Rubaduc@juno.com) | Date: 2003-06-26 17:22:48 UTC-04:00
I took the bull by the horns and went to Mansfield today. Mansfield is
very intimidating to someone as unorganized and scatter brained and your
truly. I usually end up driving around and around, getting very few of
the treasures, but today I went straight to the historical society
building and successfully found The Rock Climber, The Vermont Quarter (my
first quarter), Inherit the Earth, Bed Bug and Plane Spotter. All were
wonderful and in good shape except Inherit the Earth needed, new baggies
and a drying out. It's in fine shape now and I put it in a freezer bag as
an extra precaution.
thanks all
