Central New York is Lock n Lock territory! I have planted over 200
boxes with the majority of those being packed inside Lock n Locks and
I have only had one failure. Someone somehow managed to break off one
flap! No worries...I use it in my pack now to carry those little
square ink pads.

Our grocery stores seem to rotate this product in and out. Wegman's is
currently carrying it, as well as Price Chopper. A few price choppers
are even discounting it as they reach certain sales levels. (says the
manager of my local store) P & C seems to jump in with product around
the Thanksgiving holidays. If only they knew how many boxes weren't
going to be holding leftovers! lol
