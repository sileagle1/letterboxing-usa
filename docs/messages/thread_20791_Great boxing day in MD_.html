Great boxing day in MD!

From: bassackwardsfrommd (bassackwardsfrommd@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-24 23:30:30 UTC

Hello! The Basses had a very productive boxing day today! We found
the Box On and Zodiac series in Black Hill park. Except we
accidently skipped over the directions for Deanno's Capricorn, so
we'll have to go back sometime. Still have to get the Birder Series
anyway. We also found the Blair Letterboxing Project box. We got 8
stamps in all. While hiking back to the car, from finishing the
Zodiac series, a huge owl swooped out in front of us, landing in a
tree. You just never get to see (at least I don't!) owls out there,
so it was incredible to watch this thing in action! He kept flying
fron tree to tree so close to us. It was amazing that he never made
the smallest sound. They are so silent. It wasn't a horned owl, we
could see there were no "horns," but he was huge. His wing span had
to be at least 4 feet. It was very cool!
Keep an eye out for a new MD mystery box, coming soon. I am also
working on an idea for an Artemis Fowl series. Can't wait to get
started on it. (hands rubbing together, maniacal smile...)
