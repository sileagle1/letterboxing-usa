How to tell another letterboxer has been discussed, but what I recall is:
a) You can tell because the person is holding clues, and a compass & looking
lost (or determined) :-)
b) Look for ink on the fingers (harder now that everyone is wearing gloves)
c) Ask them if they know what LBNA is, if they don't know, you can either
tell them what it is or say never mind.
d) Some people have the logo patches on their pack, coat or hat

Hope that helps,
