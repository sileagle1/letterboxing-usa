Thought it would be a good time to crawl out from under my webmaster
rock where I've been lurking and break ranks with the hidden cabal:

our hands up together with our thumbs against the opposite index
finger as an enigmatic signal. Try it: it's empowering and it makes a
special shape appropriate to boxers and now everyone can do it.

But seriously, I want to "out" myself as a supporter of Eric and what
he is trying to do for the letterboxing community. If you look at
anybody's "links" pages, you'll see a growth in people supporting the
pastime online. My personal favorites include the Mapsurfer pages,
the RuthAnn Zaroff pages, Jolly G-Man's work, RIBNAG, Kurdziel's
three-leafed pages, and the new Perfect Circle Letterboxing Guild. As
I've said before about letterboxes, they're like chocolate chip
cookies: you can never have too many. Same with the websites that tie
us together as a community. The more options and creativity and
excitement, the better.

My hat is off to Eric and his love of letterboxing. Go for it, doc!
