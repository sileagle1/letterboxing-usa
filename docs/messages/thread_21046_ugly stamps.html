From: Armadillo Jo (jes7o@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-01 17:12:23 UTC-08:00
This is one Bipolar list! All right, i'll fess up...it
was I who placed the lithium in the coffee urn....

...and i'm a stamp snob too. I left my sig stamp at
home once and needed a replacement fast. I looked at
the store-bought stamps and just could not bring
myself to do it. I bought an eraser and an x-acto
knife (for a whopping total of two dollars at
Wal*Mart) and carved the most heinous stamp known to
man while my mom drove. The armadillo is missing an
ear and the JO part of the stamp has a deformed O.
There are 8 boxes in NW Ohio that bear the "ugly
armadillo" stamp. That stamp may be ugly, but I made
it and that's the important part. Try carving. It's
not nearly as hard as it seems. Just don't do it while
in a car. You'll totally "van gogh" your armadillo!

--Armadillo Jo
