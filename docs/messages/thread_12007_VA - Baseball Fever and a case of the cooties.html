From: be ma (bema57@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-04-16 19:00:12 UTC

The kids might not recognize "who's on first" as the classic routine, but,
they know the letterbox when they see it! I can't believe this is our first
find during Spring Break (not counting the NC box). How many days have we
wasted? Oh well!

We found the little baseball field after walking all around the park in the
other direction (It's been a long time since we found Reiku -- the first
ones!)

Be loved the stamp because it fits so nicely with his personal stamp!

We were also thrilled to find the Cooties HH! The kids don't know what
cooties are either, but they are adorable (both the stamp images and my
little ones -- if I do say so myself...)

What a great history for this HH. It's been to CA, NC and now VA. We are
going to do our best to replant it somewhere in MD or DC so it can add
another state to its travel log. It was great to see that it had been in
the hands of the notorious Tar heel Amanda from Seattle and Randy (whose
Pokemon stamps we are going to plant very soon -- they've been by the
computer all winter watching and waiting). Then, the last person to plant
the stamp was Mary, Daughter of the Old Dominion. How odd it is to feel so
excited to connect to people that you have never met face to face!

Speaking of face to face, we're still game for a mini gather this week....
How does Thursday or Friday at Riverbend look to anyone?
