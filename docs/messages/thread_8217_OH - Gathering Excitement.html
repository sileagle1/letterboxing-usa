It was a blast to meet many new Ohio boxers! Thanks to Dave aka
DocMan for getting it all organized and putting out special boxes
just for us! It was especially nice to meet franzsolo face to face.
It has been almost exactly one year since I joined his solo resident
letterboxing act in Ohio.

It got me fired up to do some more boxing, now that the weather is
cooling off a bit.

I did put two letterboxes back out that had been removed a month or
so ago. Each have new clues.

For fun, here is the clue poem from the new Lost Fishie Mysterie:
*********************************************8
Oh where, Oh where could that little fish be?
Oh where, Oh where could he be?

All you have is a little fragment of a map:
And a few notes:

Near the pylon on the east side,
Standing under an absent bridge,
Trees overhead and still,
You can't hide!

Face the noontime sun,
Turn away from the path,
Carrying kids that peddle for fun,
Forward 32 paces.

Pass the Orc road at 20,
Keep going,
To a sycamore tree,
On the righting.

Find the Pisces!
Five pacies West
Look for a cocoon
Flyfisher high.

Tuck 'em back to sleep,
For the next boxing fisher,
Or fishing boxer,
Pisces!

******************************************
