Our first boxes.

From: armchrdtective (armchrdtective@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-17 00:26:24 UTC
My kids and I have been letterboxing since labor day. Today we found
our 9th box and then planted two of our own. We left the "Bless My
Little Heart" hitchhiker in Maine and then planted the "Hollyberry"
Letterbox in Exeter, NH. We are pretty excited about seeing who
visits our hiding place (Culex, Nautilaus, Phyto and Isosoles, my
kids recognize your stamps on sight and look for them everytime we
find a box!). I hope we did everything right and we look forward to
planting some more boxes sometime.

Armchrdtective (&, by proxy, Beep-beep-Bepe, Beef Jerky and GI Jared)
