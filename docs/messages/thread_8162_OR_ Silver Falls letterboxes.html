OR: Silver Falls letterboxes

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2002-10-04 04:57:24 UTC
I just did a sleepover/letterboxing extravaganza at this wonderful park.
The two days showed themselves to be typical Oregon. One was blue and shiny and the other grey and
drippy. It made for a wonderful contrast. The first day some of the falls were almost nonexistent and
the second... water flowing everywhere. Seven of the nine boxes there by Ryan C made themselves known
to me. One of the ones I failed to find was not one of the two I expected to be missing according to info
on his webpage. I replaced the Buddah letterbox while I was there and placed a brand new one while I was in
the neighborhood. Clues will follow soon. These boxes have not been visited often; the vine maples are
beginning to go scarlet and the trails are nearly empty. Go get 'em. Funhog
