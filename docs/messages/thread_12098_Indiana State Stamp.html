Columbia CT. Rated Easy
Planted By NeeDeeps 4-18-03
Directions: From RT.66 in Columbia turn onto Hunt RD. follow the boat
ramp signs.You will travel down this road 1.7 miles,there will be a stop
sign in the middle of this road but just keep going straight after
stopping.You will pass by a dam on your right and just after that is the
entrance to Mono Pond where you pull into the parking area,drive to the
bulletin board and park.( Wear orange blaze during hunting season)Enter
the yellow blaze trail and you will come to a Y go straight and stay on
the yellow in a little while you will pass through a stone wall,and then
you will pass through another stone wall.Then there will be a Y in the
trail take a right and go through a third stone wall.Continue a bit more
and you will see a trail feeding into this one coming from the left,they
both join together and the trail changes to red.You will pass over a
rocky and muddy area and cross a small stream keep going a little
more.Stay on the red trail and you will cross over another small stream
and then a little bigger stream that breaks into three smaller
streams.Once you cross this stream stop,and walk 33 steps down the trail
you will see some dead logs on the ground by a dead tree to your
right.Behind the longest log is the letterbox.Return the way you
came.The NeeDeeps.
