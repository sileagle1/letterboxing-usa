Newgate Prison Update

From: thelionandsanddollar (pmoriarty01@snet.net) | Date: 2003-04-19 00:34:08 UTC
We spent the day in Granby yesterday chasing a prisoner through the
woods. We found all four boxes and the Field Day HH in the last
one. Boxes 1 and 2 were fine, but box 3 was wet inside. We dried
out the box, and fortunately the ziplocs kept everything mostly dry.
The top of the stamp pad and box 4 was broken, so it's a little
messy, but otherwise ok. The logbooks are getting quite full also.
It was a wonderful hike. We also picked up some DEP boxes yesterday,
all in good shape. We found the Salmon River, Tunxis, and People's
SF boxes. And we set the Vagabond Vulture HH loose to fly some.
Hopefully someone will find him soon.
Patrick & Amy
