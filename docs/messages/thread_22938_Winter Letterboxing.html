No spoilers here.

My "Temporary Haiku" mystery letterbox (listed on the LBNA web site) is
actually much *more* accessible now/in snow than in the summer. It
hadn't had many visitors as of early fall, when it was so overgrown as
to be almost inaccessible. That's the last time I checked on it -
figured that if I, the planter, had trouble locating it, it ought to be
fine in situ for quite a while. Barring vandals of any sort since then,
it should be very easy to find & grab from now through early spring.

Happy hunting!
