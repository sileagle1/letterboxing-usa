>Where the hell is Paul?<

Ironic, isn't it, that the letterbox for Paul has turned out to be
the hardest? Most folks dismissed him as the cute one, without the
stunning musicality of Lennon or the mysticism of Harrison, and
lacking the randy bluecollar appeal of Starr. Yet he lives in estate
on the Scottish coast, a member of the Forbes Richest, successful in
marriage and in supporting his partner's heroic fight against cancer,
and was knighted by the Queen. It turns out that there are several
geo-markers in the area although only one is marked on the trail map,
which makes finding Sir Paul a challenge. I had changed the clues a
bit, but a lovely letterboxer a half generation older than I
convinced me to leave them as-is. Paul, it turns out, was never dead
despite all the bizarre historical evidence to the contrary. The box
fits the story...
