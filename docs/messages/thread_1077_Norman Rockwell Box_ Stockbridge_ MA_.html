What'z up:
I was out on a jaunt yesterday and decided to place a
new letterbox with Jen.

Go to the Norman Rockwell museum in Stockbridge, MA.
Park in the visitors parking lot. Go to the light
post in the SSW corner of the parking lot. Find the
little path, formed by drainage, heading in a SW
direction. Follow the path down to the road below the
parking lot. Look to your right there should be a
utility pole numbered 18. From the pole take a
heading of 192 degrees. Sight the tree with 5 lives,
look on the downhill side of the tree to find your
treasure.
Then go to the museum and enjoy some Americana art and
a little bit of New England back in town.

Enjoy your day:
Jen and Stan
