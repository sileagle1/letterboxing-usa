MA and RI box updates

From: cscm88 (cscm@toast.net) | Date: 2002-03-24 16:30:41 UTC
On Sunday, March 17, Kim and I found Two Hearts and Moments to
Celebrate in Lincoln and Concord, MA, respectively. Both boxes are
intact.

Yesterday, March 23, I checked in on Viking Ships at Sunrise and
Ghost Town at Sundown in South Kingstown, RI. Both were present and
accounted for.

However, I regret to announce that the Crossways Crossed Paths box,
which CZF (aka Warrior Woman) and I placed back in August is
missing. I didn't see any pieces strewn around, so I can only assume
it was found by groundskeepers and removed. I have no plans to
replace this box at this time.

CSCM
