"Star Gazer" Letterbox

(Texas Gulf Coast Region Needville, Texas Fort Bend County
Brazos Bend State Park)

Date of Placement: November 16, 2002
Difficulty: Easy
Total distance to letterbox: 250 yards (one way)
Placed by Baby Bear


The "Star Gazer" Letterbox can be found at Brazos Bend State Park,
where Texas colonization was begun by Stephen F. Austin. The park
preserves habitat for many species of birds, and contains many
American Alligators. Also in the park is the George Observatory,
which is open to the public from 3pm to 10pm on Saturdays. Call 979-
553-3400 for more information. The "Star Gazer" letterbox is near
the observatory, but can be found on any day.
