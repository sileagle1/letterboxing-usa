45th Parallel - Mill Creek Letterbox
Polk County, Oregon
Placed by Funhog 3/03
This box is part of the 45th Parallel Series, an attempt to place letterboxes all along the mysterious
invisible line which runs around the earth half-way between the North Pole and the Equator.

To the letterbox: From Salem head west on Highway 22. A little over ten miles after the junction with
99W you will cross Mill Creek. Take the next left on Mill Creek Rd. In about a mile start watching to
the left for a blue roof. Along the edge of the tilled field next door you will find the orange Witness
Post marking the 45th Parallel. From the survey marker, travel .3 miles farther down the road where
you will see a lineup of retired farm workers. They have creaky joints and flat feet. Pick the one who
looks like he'd be fun at a party. This guy mixes well and will open the doors to his heart for you. He
keeps everything above the belt and safeguards the letterbox.
