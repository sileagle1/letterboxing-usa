From: Judi Lapsley Miller (judi@psychokiwi.org) | Date: 2003-07-05 19:33:51 UTC-04:00
There are a number of boxes at Denison Pequotsepos Nature Center in Mystic
that are family friendly. Some by us, some by Melissa & Dan, and one
DrewClan. Check out:
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?boxnum=1036&boxname=DPNC_Nature_Seri
es-Part_1
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?boxnum=1037&boxname=DPNC_Nature_Seri
es-Part_II
http://www.psychokiwi.org/letterboxing/pequotsepos.htm
http://members.aol.com/_ht_a/drewclan/coastal2.htm#Birdhouse
Some of the boxes are on longer walks than others, but you can get a map at
the front desk and plan your attack accordingly.

Captain's Walk is a must-do. You can either do the lovely walk down River Rd
to get there or just drive up :-)
(Make sure you read the logbook too...)
Either way, River Rd is one of the most scenic drives in the area.
http://members.aol.com/_ht_a/drewclan/local5.htm#Captains

Olde Mystic Village is also easy (plus there's loads of good shopping). The
restaurant "Go Fish" is one of the better ones in the area too, if you're
looking for recommendations.
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?boxnum=4304&boxname=Olde_Mystick_Vil
lage

Have fun!! I hope you enjoy your visit.

Judi
