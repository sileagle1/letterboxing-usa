From: dagonell2001 (salley@klaatu.canisius.edu) | Date: 2005-05-09 15:16:03 UTC
I was watching L&O last night. The plot revolved around geocaching
and letterboxing. (I think Hollywood has trouble understanding the
difference) A geo-cacher was being framed for murder. The murderer
planted evidence near a geo-cache to incriminate the suspect. The
detective had to solve the clues to figure out where the cache was so
the surrounding area could be searched for evidence. I thought it was
neat, that our hobbies are being noticed by the mainstream media.
Now, if one of us was to find a finger in a letterbox...
