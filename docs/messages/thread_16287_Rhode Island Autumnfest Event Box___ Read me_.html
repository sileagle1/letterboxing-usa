Greetings all,
Columbus Day weekend is an exciting time here in RI. In
Woonsocket, the beloved Autumnfest fair and parade will take place. In honor
of this always fun event, I have made a letterbox especially for it. If any
of you out there are interested in visiting the box over this weekend, (I
will be placing it Friday night or Saturday morning and removing it on
Monday evening) please feel free to email me off list at
Sailor_Mystique@hotmail.com so that I can get the directions to you.



Happy hunting!

Mystique

P.S. ~ If you happen to see the parade, look for the Burrillville High
School band in Division 3! I'll be in the front row, 2nd in from the right.
