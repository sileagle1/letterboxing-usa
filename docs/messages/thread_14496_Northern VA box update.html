Northern VA box update

From: justplaingeorge (justplaingeorge@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-22 12:37:51 UTC
Just a quick note that the following boxes are alive and well:

Tyson's Witch - went and did this one before work this morning. That
hill just about killed me, but the box is safe and sound. Loved the
story. : ) Even got to see a baby bunny on the way out. : )

Fort Marcy - great box site! Glad we went early in the morning
before it got busy.

McCarty Farm Letterboxes (Danaus plexippus and Honesty)
- we had stamped in to Sir Mooseness last year, but never managed the
other two boxes til now. Wonderfully quiet park - the walk was quite
a hike, but we enjoyed it. Got completely covered with spiderwebs
trying to get to the last box.
