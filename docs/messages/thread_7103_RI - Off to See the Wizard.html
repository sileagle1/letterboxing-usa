From: btrplc2b (Btrplc2b@aol.com) | Date: 2002-08-21 11:05:37 UTC
Early Sunday Morning Sunshine, a RI Wizard who knows Weber's Walks
and Rambles in RI like the back of his hand, and clues to seek the
Wizard...a sure Rx for letterboxing fun. I highly recommend this
series to anyone who has several hours to spend on this delightful
trail. The clues are challenging and the stamps incredibly well
done. Our time ran out before we were able to complete the quest to
the Wizard, but Dorothy & Toto, The Witches, Scarecrow, Tin Man and
Cowardly Lion are all right in place. I'll have to wait until my
next visit to beautiful RI to finish the series, but hope many of you
will take the time to visit this great series!
