SE Gathering in Atlanta was a success!

From: red_bing (redbng@aol.com) | Date: 2004-08-09 02:11:53 UTC
Hi gang,

Yesterday we had the first gathering in the southeast, and it was a
blast! We had fifty-six in attendance (including dogs and infants)
from six states, and had beautiful weather in a great park to
compliment the festivities. We did a rough estimate of how many stamp
images were created and/or collected, and came up with a ballpark
guess of 2300. We'll be posting photos on Tuesday in the Yahoo Group
letterbox-southeast. Wish y'all coulda been there!

Our thanks go out to all who provided how-to tips, online
suggestions, and travelling event stamps. You helped make this a huge
success.

Letterboxing is booming in the southeast, so if you ever find
yourself with vacation plans in this area, stop on in and sit a spell.

Red of Red's Bunch
