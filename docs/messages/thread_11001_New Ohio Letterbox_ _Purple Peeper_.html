Title:" Purple Peeper". Placed 3-2-03 by Salamander. Difficulty:
Easy. Location: Paint Creek State Park, Ross County, Ohio. Driving
directions: US-50 (West of Bainbridge or east of Hillsboro) to Rapid
Forge Road. 2 miles on Rapid Forge Rd. to park entrance. From the
entrance, go .3 mile to the parking area for the Little Pond Trail.
CLUES: Enter the Little Pond Trail near the shelterhouse. When the
trail forks,go to the left, past a small wooden bench.Proceed to a
wooden A-frame bridge. Cross the bridge. When you exit the bridge,
you will go up a small slope. At the top of the slope you will see a
metal trash can. Turn left at the trash can and go 6 paces. Stop.
Just off the trail to your left (East) is a large boulder.The Purple
Peeper is nestled in a hollow formed by this and two other boulders.
