I don't belong to any chat lists up that way, so I wanted to say here
that Norasta and Guitarzan put on a delightful gather (Rocks Locks &
Docks) in Kingston ON.

The RedHat Box'rs caravan'd up from Wayne PA on Fri to Watertown NY -
finding & planting boxes along the way - then drove over the boarder
Sat AM. We had fantastic accommodations in the hospitality suite on
board the Alexander Henry and really enjoyed meeting some new
Canadians (including Paul Apoolooza) and stateside boxers.

Thanks for putting together the clue booklet - even though I had
already printed and mapped all the LBNA clues!! At least we had maps.

With Kingston only 8 hrs away -- I hope we can visit this charming
city again.
