Busy Weekend

From: forestfrogdad (dad@forestfrogfamily.com) | Date: 2002-11-11 13:35:23 UTC
The Forest Frogs had quite a weekend. Though disappointed at not
having the opportunity to meet so many DC-area folks at Firefly's
celebration, we had our busiest weekend yet.

On Saturday, we found the Canadian in DC and Hal and the Magic
Mushroom. Unforunately, the second was completely waterlogged, so we
took it home for a makeover. We also found Egypt, but failed at
finding Orion.

On Sunday, we hit the Loonerism Betterspox, along with (finally!)
finding Get Outta My Stump. Then we headed in the opposite direction
to re-place Hal's new box, and trekked along to find Kid Gloves 2 and
3.

Overall, 7 boxes found, including 1 box rescue, and only 1 failure.
Barring some productive placements, I think there are only 3 left in
DC that we have yet to find. Now we just have to get to work on
placing the ones that we've got in the works.
