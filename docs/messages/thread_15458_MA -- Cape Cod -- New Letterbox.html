Barnstable County. South Wellfleet. Massachusetts.

Stonecroft Letterbox. Planted by Ginnie. 8/27/03
Difficulty: An easy hike.

Go to the Marconi Site, park in the lot and look for the Atlantic White Cedar
Swamp Trail. Follow the trail through the swamp. When you come out of the
swamp, instead of following Old Wireless Road back to the parking lot, turn
sharp right instead and, from the swamp exit, walk 38 steps down the path to
the right to a double-trunk bear oak about 11 steps off the path to the left. On a
bearing of 137 degrees, walk from the double-trunk oak 21 long steps to
another double-trunk bear oak (you'll only see it is double-trunked when you
get to it). From there go 14 steps at 5 degrees to a fallen log. Stonecroft
Letterbox is on the east side of the log under duff, leaves, a rock and a stick.
