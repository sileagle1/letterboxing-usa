I recommend those attending the gathering in Mansfield, and have not been to
Mansfield before, take the clues for these 6 very easy boxes near the area
of the gathering. All 6 are almost drive by's.Ponde Place might take as long
as 15 minutes. All are listed either Mansfield or Mansfield Center.

These two can be done together

Mother/Daughter letterbox
The Oxen Adventure Series box #3 The Village Letterbox

The following 4 boxes in this order

Ye Olde Cemetery
Ponde Place
Mansfield Centre General Store
Population

Hope this helps. There are many others but these were the first real easy
ones I thought of......Chuck and Molly
