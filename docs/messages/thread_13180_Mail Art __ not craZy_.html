nope, not crazy at all. I came to LB through rubber stamping - and
I stamp envelpoes and packages quite frequently just for the fun of
it and the delight of the recipients.

The term "mail art" means different things to different people. It
can be simply stamping an image on an envelope, OR making a work of
art out of it with stamps & collage. OR, for other people it means
sending something unusual through the mail . . . . With a search on
google I did find one site that looks rather helpful & even has some
carving info on it:

http://www.geocities.com/SoHo/5921/
