Clear DayWhile I was down in Atlanta, I had the great opportunity to find
the Easter Egg Letterbox. Surprised, I was, to find 1 stamp from Atlanta and
(counting mine) 2 from New England.
I also discovered a new letterbox enthusiast. He lives in Sacramento, CA. He
signed in at the Easter Egg box, because being brand new he did not have a
stamp. He also pulled up the web site, found several boxes near his home,
vowed to find them, and was talking about placing boxes in and around the
coast of California, on off coast islands, and in Hawaii (Oahu) this summer.
Looks like we will be able to add Hawaii to our list of states soon.
Sorry I missed the gathering, but I was 1500 miles away.
