Well it's time for a sequal to the first letterbox. And Since I
planted the first I guess I'll plant the Sequal. Southport is known
for Movies being made there. "Crimes of the Heart, I Know What You
Did Last Summer, Summer Catch" And others. So the Southport the
Sequal was an interesting concept. Enjoy.

SOUTHPORT THE SEQUAL
Clues:
After coming such a long ways I am sure you are curious about such an
out of the way town. So why not go to the most "Knowledgable"
building in Southport.

Stop awhile and learn more of the history about this small Historical
Town.

Continue on toward the small park which it's "Center" attraction is
one of mother natures "Old" friends.

The gazebo behind this friend shelters the box in which you seek.

Please... This is a busy part of town let none see you take or
replace the box.

Now go and take a look around. I am sure you will find a few things
in the area to peek your interest.
