TeamGreenDragon created a Special Charity Hitchhiker called the "Pink
Ribbon" in memory of those we have lost to Breast Cancer. Our concept
is that for each time the "Pink Ribbon" is found, TeamGreenDragon
will donate money to the American Breast Cancer Foundation for
research. We would like for the "Pink Ribbon" to be a " public "
hitchhiker, meaning that when it is found that the finder would post
to the Yahoo talklist where they found it and where they placed it,
in the hopes that anyone who may be interested in moving it along
will know where to look for it. The quicker this Hitchhiker gets
moved, the quicker breast cancer awareness will become more common
and more money will be donated to help research. (We are not asking
for money - we, TeamGreenDragon - will donate $ but of course,
anybody who wishes to, can also donate $)

The last we have heard - The "Pink Ribbon" was dropped off at
the "Turkey
Trot" letterbox in Harvard, Ma.
by Seekyr, Blaze and Mobius on 1/19/03.

This is a great letterbox. The clues are fun as it tells the story
from the turkey's point of view. We found this box on a cold, snowy
day over the winter, and even then, we enjoyed the location and as it
was our last box of the day, a very nice stroll in the woods. Also
the stamp is just too cute for words. All in all you'll be rooting
for the turkey.

So, We are hoping that someone will see this post and find this
creatively done letterbox placed by Seekyr and give the "Pink Ribbon"
a ride somewhere new.

(If someone else has already moved the "Pink Ribbon" from the Turkey
Trot - we would be grateful if you would post to the Yahoo talk list
where you left it.)

Thanks Everyone!!!
TeamGreenDragon :)
