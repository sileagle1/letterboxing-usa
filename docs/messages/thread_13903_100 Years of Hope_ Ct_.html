I can't hold a torch to all you enthusiastic 500 plus letterboxers
who trecked out in the recent heat to find multiple boxes. However,
I did an afternoon (buggy, buggy) stroll to find Irish Tinker's
lettebox in honor of Bob Hope. I needed to backtrack. Due to the
enthusiastic forest undergrowth, the downed fence posts were hard to
spy. The box was damp, but logbook and stamp were fine. I dried out
the box before replacing it. The stamp is so well done! my first
Irishtinker letterbox find, though several clues sheets are in my
letterbox binder.
