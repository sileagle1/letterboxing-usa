Harkness Memorial State Park Letterboxing Series

Town: Waterford, CT
County: New London
Rated: Easy, but lots of walking on level paved or hard gravel paths. The
first three are wheelchair accessible.
Placed by Pam in honor of the McCann Family Reunion, which was held at the
park on 9/21/02.

Directions to Harkness from NYC and areas south: Take I-95 North to Exit 75;
bear right at end of exit onto Route 1. Go three miles and take a right onto
Avery Lane which will become Great Neck Road (Route 213). Continue for 3
miles. Turn right into the park.
