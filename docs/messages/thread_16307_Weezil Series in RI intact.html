Weezil Series in RI intact

From: Stephanie (rubberchickengirl@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-10-10 18:16:46 UTC
To anyone who has emailed me lately, or plans on finding the Weezil
series in Johnston, RI. All three boxes are in their places,
intact. None are missing. I checked today. There were a few
people who emailed me, saying they couldn't find the first box.
It's there. Also, when it's really wet here in RI, the path to the
first box may be a little muddy. But if you're in love with
letterboxing as much as I am, a little mud is a small price to pay.
Follow the directions closely, pay attention to what you see.
Thanks for the responses, and happy Letterboxing!!!
P.s. The Scituate art festival and Johnston Autumnfest are both
this Columbus day weekend. What a great time to go letterboxing in
Scituate and Johnston!
