Reader/Viewer Participation at LBCentral

From: Bill&amp;Val/LBCentral (Bill@LetterboxingCentral.com) | Date: 2001-07-19 01:46:16 UTC
We have done an update to the Shutterbug page.
As usual,we have added more hikes, but this time we were
asked to add a hike from one of our readers. When
we started this Website, we said that it would become
whatever was needed by the general letterboxing
community - You ! We have decided that we will post
graphics and verbage from your letterboxing experience
for everyone to share.

We ask for the following consideration -

Do not ask us to post anything that would give away
clues or otherwise compromise any of the fun in a
given letterboxing hike. We have previously had to
tailor photographs of our own to accomplish this. The
idea here is to share and entice more participants
without diluting any of the experience.

Submissions should be made by Email to
Bill@LetterboxingCentral.com
Please allow us a week to process and post your photos.

Bill & Val
