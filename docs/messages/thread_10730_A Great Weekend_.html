A Great Weekend!

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-02-09 19:39:03 UTC-05:00
Hello everyone!  We had a great weekend in RI!!!!!

Butterfly, Lobsta Lady, my friend Andy and I decided to stop at Wanda and Pete's for a visit :-)  After stamping into their house box, we went out cross country skiing at the Great Swamp WMA in Richmond/West Kingstown area (?).  There was just enough fluffy white snow to ski, so we ventured out onto the trails to find Moonstone Baby's new series "Dog Days of Summer," and the Holly Impromptu letterbox.  We had a blast out on the loop, and thank goodness Andy doesn't hate me for dragging him along, on borrowed skis no less!  He actually enjoyed his first letterboxing excursion and said that he would like to go letterboxing again.  I don't think he will be as "dedicated" as the rest of us, but that's ok.... another convert is a good thing!

Unfortunately, due to the lack of sunlight (if we just had another hour of sunlight.... sigh) we were unable to get the Adventures of Tin Tin box at the Great Swamp, which means that we will definitely be back!  This was also Butterfly's first letterboxing excursion in RI, and since she totally enjoyed herself, I doubt it will be her last!

This afternoon (Sunday), I stopped by my "local" box at the beach house, the Quonochantaug (I live here, and I STILL can't spell it!!!!) letterbox is alive and well with a new and wonderfully carved stamp by Jay Drew!!!!  Go now, before there are too many people at the breach way.  You don't want one of them calling the police department and complaining that you're wandering aimlessly through the brush..... right Warrior Woman?????  ;-)    (btw... I still get a big kick out of that story!  not that it was funny at the time.....!!!!  but I'm sure that Warrior Woman's laughing about it now!)

Anyway, thanks Wanda, Pete, Lobsta Lady and Butterfly for a great weekend!  Hope to see you again soon!  

Music Woman
from CT and RI
(and Andy too from RI!.... who's still talking about his excursion in the Swamp!)

Hi! Music Woman,

It was Lobsta Lady's first excursion into Rhode Island
Saturday, & I'll be back for sure! And I was impressed
at Andy's dedication to you, to go out like that &
have fun in spite of not knowing what he was getting
in for! I got cold just looking at him with no hat or
gloves & getting wet in his cotton jeans when he fell
because he'd never xc skied before! I was Amazed he
didn't go into hypothermia once the sun got low! It
was Great fun & thanks for the hot chocolate too!
We (Bluebird & Butterfly)did get over to Alan's
anniversary box Sunday (You are one Amazingly Good
carver! and the box & it's other contents were Very
Impressive!)after a fun, though occassionally
frustrating day of snow shoeing to letterboxes. We
found it to be Grim in Griswald & Hopeless at Hope
Pond, but Really enjoyed Max's swimming hole (Great
stamp!)after Butterfly nosed out the box! (She is
Amazing!), and enjoyed Pete's cute kitty cat stamp
honoring Wanda's Great 2,000 boxes in 2 years
Accomplishment. Looking forward to more Great
Adventures out on the trail soon. Enjoy!
Lobsta Lady
