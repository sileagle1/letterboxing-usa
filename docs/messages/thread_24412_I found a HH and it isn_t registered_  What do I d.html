Re: I found a HH and it isn't registered. What do I do?

From: trekkiegal1701d (kjnohr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-06-26 03:14:48 UTC
If it isn't listed on LbNA, then there is nothing you can do. LbNA
doesn't allow you to record finds on unlisted boxes. Atlas Quest
does allow that, but only if you are a premium member. Did you check
to see if the HH is listed on AQ? What is the name of the HH?

RE: [LbNA] I found a HH and it isn't registered. What do I do?

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2007-06-25 22:22:54 UTC-05:00
"Credit" on-line doesn't really matter to a lot of people. What IS
important is sending an email to the creator of the hitchhiker to let them
know when & where you found it. Usually there is an email address on the
logbook for the hitchhiker so you know who to contact.

We enjoy getting messages from people that have found one of the 3
hitchhikers we created.

Wisconsin Hiker

Re: [LbNA] I found a HH and it isn't registered. What do I do?

From: uneksia (uneksia@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-06-25 23:47:04 UTC-04:00
you will find that there is a lot of hitchhikers not listed. there are also
many boxes that are not listed on lbna or on atlas quest.

as for getting credit, the only credit you really need is to yourself. the
logging of finds on either atlas quest or lbna is simply a matter of choice.
there are many letterboxers that only log clues in their logbooks, no where
else.

you might find your questions of this nature more readily answered in the
newboxers list. the url is:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/

smile
uneksia
