Brown's Point Lighthouse Mini Meet

Hi--for anybody out there in the letterboxing community who is
interested in joining us, a few letterboxers from the region will
get together from 2-4 pm (may last longer if the kids want to play
for a while) on 31 July 04 at Brown's Point Park (Lighthouse), WAY-
WAY North Tacoma (practically Federal Way at this point) to meet
with Spacy Holly and her letterboxing family from Southern
California for exchanges.

We plan on meeting at the flag pole in front of (West) of the
lighthouse keepers cottage. The park offers expansive and
incredible views of the Olympic Mountains, Puget Sound, Vashon &
Maury Islands, and depending on the tides lots of opportunity for
beachcombing and boulder scrambling. There's even
enough 'greenspace' to throw a football or frisbee. We plan on
bringing some picnic items, too.

Let us know if you plan on joining us then.

Perhaps we'll see you there?
