Team Weems planted two new boxes this afternoon. Valley Falls is one
of our favorite places. On hot summer days, we can be found walking
through the stream with friends (with our old sneakers on, splashing
away!) We must look strange to other hikers, but try it. It's a
blast! While trying to de-stress, the Mighty Mommy closes her eyes
and pictures the babbling steam and peaceful pond and instantly
relaxes. This is such a nice park, we want to share it with
everybody. We hope you like it as much as we do.

Enjoy!

The Tortoise and The Hare Letterboxes
Valley Falls Park Vernon, CT Tolland County

Planted by Team Weems on 10/7/01.

Should take just under an hour to do both boxes.
Mostly flat, except for one large hill (with stairs!)

Directions: From I-84 West, take exit 66. Take a left off the exit,
then left onto Tunnel Road. Take a left onto Valley Falls Road.
Valley Falls Park will be on your right.
