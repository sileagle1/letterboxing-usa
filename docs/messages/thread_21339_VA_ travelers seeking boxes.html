Re: VA: travelers seeking boxes

From: abacus00 (mahjongg@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-02-13 13:34:41 UTC

As Civil War buffs you cannot miss the Balls Bluff Civil
War Trilogy !
The small park is located in beautiful rural Loudoun County
and is not far from Fairfax and Manassas. These boxes are
well worth the trip.

The park was the
site of a resounding Union defeat that resulted in
bodies washing down the Potomac past Washington, DC.


You may have already seen this pamphlet, but just in case,
it has other Civil War suggestions:
day.pdf>

If you come to Leesburg I would also suggest the Goose Creek
Boxes and A
Day in Leesburg Collection


As to what to wear, I would suggest layers. Except for a
couple snow storms we have had mostly mild weather - lots of
days in the 40s and 50s or even warmer. A t-shirt,
long-sleeved shirt, sweater, and jacket -- pile it all on if
it's freezing and wear less if it is warmer. Or as frequently
happens, start the day wearing everything and then shed as it
warms up. Round it out with gloves, scarf, hat, and boots
that can take you through the mud and you'll be set.

I'd be happy to help, if I can, if you have any other
questions.

--Mahjong
