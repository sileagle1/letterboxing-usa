From: Crappiefisher (crappiefishergirl@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-09 21:20:54 UTC
watch out, it has been said that cinci is the second or third
largest area with regard to letterboxes. we have tons of boxes out
here. franz solo has placed some nice boxes in the city parks...like
mt airy and eden parks. then there's the jedi stuff....a dad and
son who place boxes and caches in the area. so you'll have lots to
choose from!!

let us know when you come in to town. i'm sure that many of us would
like to meet up with you guys and do some exchanges.
