From: xxxxxxxx (BrighidFarm@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-11-28 00:01:59 UTC-06:00
Hey, why not?

My letterboxing sister put a series of videos together for me as a hunt for
my Easter basket one year. She handed me a videotape. When I played it, it
was a clue to a hiding spot. When I found the spot, another tape was
waiting. That clue held the clue to the next hiding spot. At the end of
the sixth clue was my Easter basket. I told her that after all that work,
there had better be nothing but chocolate in that basket. Chocolate and
peeps. And that the ears were going first. (She always hates it when I do
the ears first -- she's a feet first bunnyeater.)

~~ Mosey ~~
