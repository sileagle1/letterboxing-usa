ForceBox #2 
(Area  Cincinnati, Ohio)
 
(Forcebox #2 can be found at Pierce Township Park)
 
(Trail, with a Up/Down hill walk 3/4 Mile Round Trip)
 
  From I-275 take the SR 125 Exit (East to Amelia)
 
  About 6 miles from I-275 or  1/2 West rom Amelia, There is a Water Tower.  The Street at
that water tower is is (White Oak). Drive on White Oak for about a mile or so and there will
be a 4 way Stop. At that stop, Turn Left Onto (Merwin Ten Mile).  You will drive about a  Mile
on Merwin Ten Mile till you get to a Road on your right called (Locust Corner). You will drive
about a Mile and the (Pierce Township Park will be on the left, Can't miss it. (Enter the Parking lot)
 
 From the top of the walking trail (By the Playground) you will see a Trail map. You will take
the RED trail.  on the RED trail you will go down a Dip  (Only Dip on this trail) at the bottom
of the Dip their is a Dirt walking path. (Take this Path) 
 
 You will will this path till you come upon the 2nd steel Drain Pipe that goes under the path
(Can't miss it)  This will also be were the path splits. You will want to take the path on the right.
You will walk about 90 paces and will see a BIG smoth rounded rock that is in the middle of the
trail. Stop on the right. (If you look to the left) you will see a dead tree that runs longway. in the
middle of the tree their is a Rock, under this rock is (Forcebox #2) You should not have to
pick the rock up to get to the box.
 
 (This is Box 2 of 3 of our May 2002 Theme  (Attack of the Clones) Each month for the rest of the
year we will hide at least 3 boxes with different theme for each month).
