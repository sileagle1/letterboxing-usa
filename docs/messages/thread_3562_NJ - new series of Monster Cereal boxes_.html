From: Kimberly Mako (MartianShark@msn.com) | Date: 2001-10-24 21:42:48 UTC-04:00
Hey Gang,
Ok, here's my inspiration for this series of boxes.
Right before Halloween, there always seems to be one store in my area that gets Boo Berry and Frankenberry cereals.  And every year it's a different store!  So far this year, I have tracked down Boo Berry.  And I was that person in line that only had six boxes of Boo Berry cereal that everybody else looked at cross eyed.  I haven't found Frankenberry yet, and am getting a little discouraged since there is only one week left and it will probably be sold out before I find it :-(   Well, while standing in line, I got a good chuckle to myself when I thought that this is exactly what finding some letterboxes is like.  Weeks of research and searching, a couple of failed attempts, and then WHAMO! I've got it, life is now complete!  Personally, I think the Mapsurfer is like CEO at General Mills and is in charge of Monster cereal distribution :) j/k 
And for those of you who always seem to have these cereals in your local grocery store (Count Chocula doesn't count, everyone has that one), please don't let me know, I already feel deprived enough without Frankenberry this year :-(
So anyway, the catch is finding the supermarket, or in this case, the park.  Once you get there, it's a quick jaunt to the cereal aisle and you're good to go.
Here's the link:
www.geocities.com/martianmaggot_k9/mcereal/monster.html
 
Have fun!
Kimberly
