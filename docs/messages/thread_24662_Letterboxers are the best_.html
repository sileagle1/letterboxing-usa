Letterboxers are the best!

From: piloteminuit (piloteminuit@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-11-13 20:54:06 UTC
I just wanted to say a big thank you to all of the letterboxers out
there who make this pastime so great.

I planted for the first time this summer, and included a little
surprise for finders to enjoy while stamping in. I didn't know if it
was reasonable to expect that the little extra something I put in my
l'box would still be there after multiple finders, but upon replacing
the full logbook last week, the "something extra" that I put in was
still there. I am quite pleased to find so many people in a global
pasttime who are so considerate and respectful of each other. So a
big "thank you" to those of you who rehide with care, who leave such
nice comments in logbooks, and who make this such an enjoyable pasttime!
