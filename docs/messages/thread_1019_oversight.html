Folks,

When the site was moved byte for byte to pil.net, the webmasters
asked (well I actually asked) if there are any clues you don't
want on the new server, to let us know. The oversight was we
should have asked -- if there is any of your material (clues
and other stuff) you do not want on the pil.net server, let us
know.

I apologize for this oversight, and the question stands :-)
We were under pressure to solve the problem of the moribund
site from several people, and in the haste to provide some
reasonable level of service, some mistakes were made. All I
can say, for my part, is that I was trying to do the right
thing.

Believe me, I personally did _not_ want to move the site to
pil.net and have to deal with it, but circumstance left little
choice.
