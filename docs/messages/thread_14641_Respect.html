THE LIST.

To the Reverend ... I'm sorry that you are offended by simply the
name of a stamp. Please remember that this hobby is enjoyed by
people who follow many paths. Should you find something you disagree
with - particularly in the way of an HH, then simply disregard it.

To PaganDC ... This is not the place for your request. Might I

privately if you believe they share your views.

Please folks. This sport is about respecting Earth, getting out
there hiking and treasure hunting. Let's try a little respect for
each other in the process.
