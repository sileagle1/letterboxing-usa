New box and website (at last)

From: Judi (Judi@SpaceFamily.net) | Date: 2001-07-18 14:39:35 UTC-04:00
Hello All
    I have finally, finally worked up a website for my letterboxes! Woopie!!!
It has been quite a learning experience, and I am in no position to be called a webmaestra, but merely a web novice. If you go to my website:
www.SpaceFamily.net and click the letterboxing link you can find all my boxes in one place (in cyberspace at least - to find them in reality takes a little more effort)
Webmasters, and any one else who has letterboxing sites let it be known that I give my permission to post this link on any letterboxing related sites.
    I would welcome constructive criticism or comments about the site. I know that some of the pages in the non letterboxing part of the website are not complete, but I wanted to take over the maintenance of my boxes in cyberspace as soon as possible. Thank you, webmasters for minding them up to this point.
SpaceTraveler
