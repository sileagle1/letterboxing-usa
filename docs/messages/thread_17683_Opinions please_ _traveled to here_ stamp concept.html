Hello everyone:

I'll be visiting Portland, OR next month where the very wonderful,
Maiden1974 (Melanie) has offered to play host and driver for a
couple of day of boxing.
Also in February, Bindle Bum and I will be going to Florida for 10
days. So, I'm kind of trying out ideas for some kind of "traveled
to here" stamp that I could carve and bring with me and then to
FLA.

There are no where near the number of boxes in FLA as in OR so I was
thinking of leaving it there (along with the other two I have ready
to go!) as a box plant to help boost the FLA numbers. I still
haven't found anyone local who would be willing to keep an eye on
any boxes that I plant down there though, so that is a concern.

Anyone have ideas on the "traveled to here" concept? Has anyone else
ever done something along this line?

Enjoy!
