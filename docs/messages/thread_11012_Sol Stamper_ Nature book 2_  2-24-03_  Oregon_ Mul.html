Clues:  You are on a Volcano from long ago.  Rise to the sky just as high as you can go.  Meet the man of stone he’ll give you his opinion on which way to go.  If you listened to the stone you’ll be faced with a patch of thorn. At this point you must stop and think “Which way is the sun at noon?”  Walking that way you will find a trail going down hill.  Move slowly down the trail the way gravity pulls looking to the direction that the sun goes to hide every evening. The walk on the trail isn’t far. Keep your sight keen as you look for the large snag with ridged bark go to it.   If you make a turn on the trail you’ve gone to far.  From the snag you can see the water.  Now think of the stars in the sky, Polaris is the direction to have your eye.  Right in front of you is a holly plant.  At its base is a fern who’s tiny leafs look like rows of swords.  Under the leafs  move the bark it’s here what you seek.
