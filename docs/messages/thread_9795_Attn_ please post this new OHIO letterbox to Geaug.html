Big Creek Park Letterbox
Placed: September 11, 2002 in honor of the memory of Kit Faragher.

Big Creek Park, Chardon, Ohio
Geauga County
Nearest Big City: Cleveland

Difficulty: Easy

Big Creek Park is part of the Geauga Park District and is located at 9160
Robinson Road in Chardon, Ohio. Take Ravenna Road north from the Square in
Chardon. Turn right on Woodin Road, then left on Robinson Road. Turn left
into Big Creek Park and follow the signs to the Meyer Center. You may want
to go in to the Meyer Center for a trail map.
