CT. 2nd KIDS DAY GATHER
Kids of all Ages :: This is an Invitation to the Second Kids Day Gather
at Rocky Neck State Park in Southeastern Ct.
On October 20,2002. we will gather at 10:00 am till sundown in the Main
Parking lot at Rocky Neck to exchange, visit and hunt for the many boxes
planted for this special day.
This is a "Newbie" group effort with the boxes being created and planted
by : Angel, Alafair, Bluebird, Butterfly, Lobsta, and The Gal Pals.
Letterboxer 2002 is again creating the event stamp. Bring yourself,
family , friends, and a smile, for we will hunt, stamp, beach walk, and
picnic the day away.
The surrounding towns are rich in letterboxes and there are motels handy
for those who wish to spend more time in our great shoreline
letterboxing area.
Come on down,
Butterfly
