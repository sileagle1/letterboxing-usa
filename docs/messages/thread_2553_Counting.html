I have already recieved positive feedback from the Mansfield Parks
director due to the letterboxing activity. He was most impressed with
the one stat I had at the time. From Sept5 to Presidents day in Feb
49 visitors stamped in at the Wolf Rock box here and that was through
a snowy winter ! I will get out there soon to update the count it is
my first box thus out there the longest and on a popular trail. I
expect the ones in and about Mansfield Hollow State Park will
collect a sizeable following as well , that cluster and local make a
good daytrip. The Friends of Mansfield Hollow ( support /activity
group) have been interested in the response to letterboxing in the
park. I may do a workshop for them in the summer. Checking on who
stamped in is like seeing old friends! I'll post an update where
count is worth noting.
