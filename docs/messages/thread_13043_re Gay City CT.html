Sorry to say that's it's true: since I've gotten a "real" job we
haven't kept up with box maintanence like we should. We had heard
that our Gay City boxes were so trashed that we virtually retired

and hike in Gay City and Case Mountain alot. It's a wonderful area.

stuff, and even complete replacement/overhauls. You guys are the
greatest!

BTW, we're not letterboxingcentral.com. I notice they have a link to
us (among others) on their clue page, though.
