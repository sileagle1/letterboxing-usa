From: thenextrushdie (jelybean@stny.rr.com) | Date: 2003-08-21 23:18:01 UTC
Yes, I got banned a few weeks ago as well. I wrote to the
webmasters and got it reversed. Unfortunately, the Klez virus is
going around this talk list. Klez pulls a random email address out
of the infected user's contact list and sends out spam email with
the chosen address as the return, so spam emails appear to be coming
from you that are not actually sent by you. Perhaps this is what
happened to you as well. In my case, a message entitled "Japenese
lasses sexy pics" got sent to the talk list with my return address,
which got me promptly banned from the list. I posted a message to
the talk list back at the end of July (#30832) imploring people to
scan their machines and providing a link to a free online scanner.
If infected, people can go to the Norton anti-virus web site to
download free cleaning tools that eliminate Klez, and there's also a
tool there now for the latest SoBig worm. Please people, scan and
clean your computers!!!

JeLyBean
