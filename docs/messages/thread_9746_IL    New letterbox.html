Milk Capital Letterbox
County: McHenry
Town:Harvard, IL
Placed 11/30/02 by greenheron with assistance from her sister Hound
Maiden and her brother Inkboy
Difficulty: Easy

Placed to honor the most famous resident of Harvard, Illinois, the
Milk Capital of the World.

McGuire Road. Turn south at a small walled cemetery into the Rush
Creek Conservation Area. Follow the hiking trail from the second
parking area. Your reward is in the far end of a downed tree, 34
paces east-southeast along the trail from marker # 16. Stamp in and
return home to enjoy a tall glass of the cold white stuff!
