Wanda and Pete are pleased to announce another addition to their
list of placed letterboxes. We call it "Chaney" because it is located on
the Chaney ranch in the western section of Montville, CT. It follows
our birdhouse theme as this property is owned by Audubon Society
and is open to those who like to hike the woods and listen to birdsong.
