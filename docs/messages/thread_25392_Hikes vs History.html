From: (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-04-22 14:23:13 UTC-07:00

If it will stop raining in Texas I have multiple new History boxes to place.   Lately, I have found many interesting historical events and figures in Texas small towns, with links to (or since good place to hide) the local cemetery.   This of course leads to short walks to find the box.  I would prefer longer hikes for letterboxes, but just does not fit the theme.   Anyway, I hope the stories off-set missing out on a hike, but many of these cemeteries are fun to walk all around.   More to come.
