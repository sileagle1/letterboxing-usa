A lovely hike at another of Cleveland's great metroparks led me to
BayBoxer's "Nuts" (aka Black Walnut) letterbox. Great Stamp!!! But
sad to say, the box has been discovered by pranksters -- the log book
was missing, although it appeared the used pages had been torn out
and left in the box (but not sure it was all of them, as this box has
been around for a year and a half and there were only a few loose
pages in the box). There were also some unkind words scribbled onto
one of the loose pages from the log book. This is unfortunate as I
concluded from the comments previous finders had made, it was a very
nice handmade book.

I had used my spare log book in DE the day before to place a box I
had not planned on, so I didn't have anything with me to leave
behind. I put the box back into the same spot, but hid it a little
better.
