From: (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2003-07-27 13:58:23 UTC-04:00
The 2 of us drove to Tilden Park in Berkeley (Nor Cal) yesterday and grabbed
Princess Lea's 2 great boxes. Very doable and nice stamps. Since we grew up in
this area, you'd think we'd have gotten to know the fabulous treasure that is
Tilden Park pretty well. But you know how it is when you grow up somewhere:
you need to come back as a tourist (or a letterboxer!) to find the really great
places.

Chalk up another beautiful venue found because of the generous letterboxers
willing to share their favorite places with us all!
