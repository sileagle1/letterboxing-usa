[LbNA] Re: Request for permission

From: Thom Cheney (tcgrafx@imagina.com) | Date: 2000-01-19 08:13:12 UTC-07:00
Dear Mr. Sarjinder,

As one of the contributors of the letterboxing map of the United
States, respectfully, I would like to decline your request to reprint
the map appearing on the letterboxing web site. By granting you
permission without a lot more detail as to its intended use, I'm afraid
in all good concience the idea does not interest me.

There is a proliferation of clip art and stock photography available,
and I believe a map of the United States would be fairly easy to come
by. I would strongly recommend that you pursue that direction in your
search for graphics. The map as viewed on your computer screen has a
resolution of approximately 72 dots per inch. Standard printing methods
prefer a dot pitch of 600-900 dots per inch.

Good luck in the completion of your project.

Sincerely,

--
Thom Cheney
tcgrafx... among other things
