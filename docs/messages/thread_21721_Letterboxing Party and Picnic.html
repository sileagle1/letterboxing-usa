Letterboxing Party and Picnic

From: deepseaamethyst (jandacapps@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-04-07 21:16:15 UTC

Hello,

I wanted to just touch base with everyone. We will be letterboxing
next weekend
(Letterboxing / picnic April 16 12:00 Tawawa Park).

. This is going to be alot of fun. I wanted to prepare everyone.
So, here is a couple of notes.

First of all, this is a picnic and your entire family is invited.
Everyone should bring a covered dish to share.

Second, anyone can come in costume. A prize will be given for the
best costumes, (which will include all).

Third, we will be putting together a couple of boxes to be sent to
children orpaned because of the tsunami. To help in this, please
bring anything you might think a child would enjoy that would fit
inside a shoebox. We will collect the boxes and organize them.
Dana Kittner our treasurer will mail them for us. The items do not
need to be expensive or fancy and it is not necessary to bring more
than you can afford. The idea is just to share our love with these
children.

Fourth, each child/participant should bring a stamp. Homemade,
store bought, thumb prints work well too. The stamp can be anything
and any design. Have fun. If you are not familiar with stamp
making, Shoe cushions work great and the old fashioned brown
erasers.

We will have book stamping, so, bring a notebook, spiral, pad of
paper.... To share stamps and autographs.

Finally, if it is storming we will reschedule.

I look forward to seeing everyone.
Sincerely,

Angel
