Hi,

This happened to me a couple of weeks ago. For some reason Yahoo "bounced" me stating that the reason for it was that my ISP was not accepting incoming mail from Yahoo. Called ISP...no problem there. Then, thanks to A.D., who straightened me out nicely, she told me how to get "un-bounced." CHeck this out before you unsubscribe.

Go to Yahoo/My Groups...then click on the LBNA group...then "Edit My Groups"...then "Email Preferences"...then look at your bounce history. If you are suspended, just follow instruction and reinstatement takes only a short while..but you will have missed the posts since you were bounced.

I am sure the techies know a shorter way to get to the Bounce History, but that's how, with A.D.'s help, I got there.
