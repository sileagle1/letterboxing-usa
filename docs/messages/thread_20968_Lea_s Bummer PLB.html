This PLB is a group effort and there are about 10 spaces left on the list for
those of you who have had one of your precious boxes vandalized. PrincessLea
not only had a box lost but the thief emailed her and said he'd put rocks in
the box and threw it into the nearby lake "har, har". She thought up the box,
Art Trekker carved the stamp and we are keeping the list and such.

If you have had the misfortune of losing a box, send US your story and your
snail mail address and we'll put you on the list. When you get this very
lightweight box (envelope), email us and we'll give you the name and address of the
next person on the list.

HURRY!!
