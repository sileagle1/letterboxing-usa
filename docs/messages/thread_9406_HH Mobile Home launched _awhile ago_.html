I'm not sure if I ever reported this little fellow. The hitchhiker
letterbox 'Mobile Home' was launched during the South Sound
Letterbox Quest Fest on 2 Nov 2002 in Lakewood, WA.

This letterbox is dedicated to children of US military
servicemembers known as 'service brats' who move quite frequently
throughout their childhood and get used to moving from home to home
(the quintessential 'mobile' home) every couple of years as their
parents are stationed at military installations around the world
defending their country.

Happy & safe travels 'Mobile Home'.
