A word of Thanks

From: Wilson Revelle (ph0t0bug@bellsouth.net) | Date: 2005-11-02 21:35:16 UTC-06:00
I just wanted to take a second to say a few words to those who donated to
the Hurricane Katrina Funds that have opened up. Thank you.



It seems in times of distress there are those who call out and hear no
replys...

Voices in the night crying for help and relief from the pain that a natural
disaster can cause..

Sometimes all it takes is a helping hand to lift our spirits up and help us
to regain our strength.

In our time of need, you have been there to be that helping hand and words
of comfort when it looked like our world was destroyed forever.

Someday you may need that same helping hand and I want you to know that ours
will be here for you.

You may be wondering why I am writing this. thinking " They have said thanks
enough." or " We only did what anyone would do." but I don't think that I
can ever be thankful enough for friends such as you.



If you get a chance please try to watch a program that National Geographic
produced called "Inside Hurricane Katrina" I want you to all see what you
are helping and why your aid has been so important to us. If you see a
donation bucket somewhere and can afford the dollar please slip it in.. The
Gulf Coast will need your help for a long time to come. If you cant, thank
you for your previous donations. They mean a lot to those with nothing.



Sunday, November 6, 6:00P is the next showing of Inside Hurricane Katrina



Thanks again from the bottom of my heart,

Wilson

Resident of the Greater New Orleans Area
