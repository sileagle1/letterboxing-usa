From: Debbi Scott (dscott5377@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-06-23 15:05:14 UTC-06:00
Hmmmm... well, since I haven't used the pink stuff or some of the
others, I have to say EZ cut is pretty good. Of course, I prefer PZ Cut
(I think that is it) that Stampeaze used to sell. It is similar to EZ
Cut but thinner and I think it is a tiny bit softer? I just find it
better for carving detail than the EZ Cut. Of course, if I can't get
the PZ Cut, the EZ cut would be my second choice.


Debbi
