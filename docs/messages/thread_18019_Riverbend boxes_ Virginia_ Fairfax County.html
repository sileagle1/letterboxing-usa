I finally was able to get out and check my Riverbend Park boxes. Billy Goat
Gruff is gone; if I decide to replace it, it will not be at the bridge, since that is
clearly too exposed. The others: Possum Trot, Muscrat Ramble, SpryngFlyng,
Who Cooks For You? and Bees Knees are in place and in good condition.
Thanks to all of you visitors who rehid them carefully. A couple of boxes were
left fairly exposed. I implore all you loyal letterboxers with children: Giving
them the job of rehiding is GREAT, but the process should be supervised by a
responsible adult. Please, guys, if a box was hidden under a rock, be sure the
rock is replaced on top of the box, not the reverse! I count on rocks to keep the
forest critters away. Thank you!
