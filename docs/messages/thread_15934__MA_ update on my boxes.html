(MA) update on my boxes

From: K Collins (adelcoll@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-21 18:13:36 UTC-07:00
Dawn of Spring Letterbox (Rocky Woods, Medfield, MA) is back in operation. We replaced the container and logbook due to animal damage, but the stamp is the original.

Small changes were made to the clues of the following boxes, either to add information or to give new landmarks where the old have gone missing, etc.. So, you may want to print out a new set of clues, if you go.

Fly Like an Eagle (Mildred Morse Allen Wildlife Sanctuary, Canton, MA)

Dawn of Spring (Rocky Woods, Medfield, MA)

Cavorting Cavy (Fork Factory Brook, Medfield, MA)

We do try to keep our web page updated to reflect when boxes have been found, so please do report both finds and misses (contact info with our clues).

Happy Trails,
Cavy Lovers
