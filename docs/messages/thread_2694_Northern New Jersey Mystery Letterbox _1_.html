Northern New Jersey Mystery Letterbox (1)
Placed 07/02/01 by Wingfoot
Somewhere in Northern New Jersey

Clues: Difficult
Terrain: Easy
Time: 15 Minutes from the start point

Notes: This is a rather large stamp that requires at least a 2x3 inkpad. You
will need to press very firmly (it was the second stamp I did, so please
don't expect a work of art.) on all parts of the back of the stamp for the
image to come out.

The "Continents of the World" is the place to be.
Africa at Cornu (1682) is a statue you will see.
Birds in winter head in the direction you need to go.
At the gravel road, you'll go left, that's something you'll need to know.
A small trail on the right will appear.
You'll take that trail and a small stream you will hear.
A large rock bridge you will then cross.
Go to the right or you will be at a loss.
Follow the trail until you cross another viaduct.
Underneath the span on the left is what you're searching for, good luck.
