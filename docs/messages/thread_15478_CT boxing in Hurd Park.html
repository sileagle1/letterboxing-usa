CT boxing in Hurd Park

From: (RNSaunders@aol.com) | Date: 2003-09-01 00:45:53 UTC
What a wonderful day today. The weather couldn't have been better.
We started the day at Hurd Park. White Elephant, Purple Serpentine
Dragon, In search of Mokele-Mbembe, and Blue Griffin were all found
and in great shape. Unknown Artist seems to be missing. Not only
could we not find it, but many entries in other logs reported not
finding it, either. What a great view of the river at River Vista!!
and that white rock-WOW!! Home for lunch, and then we went in the
other direction to Whimsy Walk. Butterfly's add-on brought us to the
cute "village" in the bushes- great for kids. Then on to find
Drewclan's 3 boxes. Such a pretty spot, and all boxes were found and
doing well. The Pine Bench was a bit elusive, and it's log is
getting pretty full, but there's still some room. We met some other
LB-ers on the trail, but we'd already exchanged at the Gerbil
Gathering. But they left their stamp on our car, just in case--how
nice!!!Thanks to the Voyageur duo. Great Day!!- Sunshine, Swamp
Yankee, and the Toad Dog :0) P8F239X48
