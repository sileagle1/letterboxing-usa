From: (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2007-06-25 21:13:26 UTC-07:00
Thanks for the heads-up here WiHi...I don't keep up on this list all the time.

This is what I called the "Metra Rail Letterbox Crawl" and featured a bunch of boxes along one of the Chicago commuter routes. I hoped others would jump on the bandwagon (or the train?) and place along the other rail lines. My first box was "Sit-Sip-Stay-Box", but that location closed down (and they never returned my letterbox!!). The three remaining "password protected" letterboxes are "Come Grumpy Go Happy" (ask for a spinach latte at Grumpy's ice cream shop), "Happy Apple" (ask for a happy apple at Lilette candies), and "Rock, Paper, Scissors" (ask to see the rock collection at the Paperie, a stationery store). There are a few other more conventional clue-related boxes also along the RR line.

I recently did something similar at the Great Lakes Gathering in Ohio...to get "Room Service" you had to ask the front desk staff at the hotel I stayed at if they had room service. (That totally confused the one worker who was not told of the letterbox's existance...she thought people were playing a trick on her.) Which reminds me...if anyone wants to borrow this one for an upcoming gathering, contact me offlist...it's available.

I think I still have some pics of the original packaging of S-S-S-B, so let me know if you'd like to see it, C2B2.

--klk/FungusWoman
