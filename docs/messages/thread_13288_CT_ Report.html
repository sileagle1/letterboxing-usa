Such fun today and I am so happy to report on the H.H. Black Bear Paw of
Candlelights . I read in the log: After hitching around in Wa. State,
Amanda from Seattle mailed the Paw to Music Woman in CT. . she planted
it in a box belonging to Bluebird, in Madison CT. and it then got a
ride to Seldon Creek, one of Jay Drew's boxes .After enjoying that great
view of the CT. River, he hitched today to East Lyme and into a box just
planted by Chuck and Molly. Lobsta Lady and I found him early in the
afternoon and he had not even had a change to eat. We carried him to a
really popular Inn for dinner and he liked it so well, just stayed.
What a busy little black bear. I must say, without even planning , he
has visited boxes and been carried by very busy CT.letterboxing folks
and boxes. Great Stamps are in his log, and thanks Candlelight for the
fun. None other than the Butterfly who had such a hard time with one of
your Virtuals and had the joy of you honoring me in such a special way.
Now I get to tell you a big THANKS
Butterfly
