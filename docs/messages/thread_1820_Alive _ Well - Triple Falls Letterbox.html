From: Briles, Amanda (a1b@exchange.gasco.com) | Date: 2001-01-29 07:26:09 UTC-08:00
Hey All!

While the New Englanders were having the time of their lives in Connecticut,
my mate, John and I got into the spirit ourselves and headed out to the
Columbia River Gorge to seek out some of Der Mad Stamper's waterfall series.
We came up empty-handed at Wahkeenah. Same with Pony Tail, though we
suspect our compass skills are to blame on that one. However, we scored up
at the top at Triple Falls! YES!!! That letterbox is alive and well and
has had many, many visitors, letterboxers and considerate non-letterboxers
alike. It was a cold, but wonderful hike and we had a great time.

So, those of you on the East Coast...We weren't able to go to Connecticut,
but we were letterboxing with you all the same! I hope you had a great time
this weekend!

Best regards,
