I went out in Cara's wake to see what she left in my letterboxes at Rock
Creek. I should inform you all that Rock Creek #1.. the original box
that had been misappropriated has been returned!! I was in the act of
placing the replacement box & saw it very near the original location
(not getting too specific about that location). I did a double take &
wondered for a mintute if I had just overlooked it. Upon closer
inspection I found that the contents were intact, but all removed from
the ziplock baggie. The notebook was still usable & still has Mitch's &
Dan'l's stamps and remarks. I added the new stamp I carved for the
replacement (bringing the total stamps included in that box to 3). At
some point I will divide them up & give them their own boxes. We have a
nice wildlife theme going in the neighborhood. You can collect a
raccoon (compliments of Mitch), opposum, canadian goose, little brown
bat, great blue heron & now an otter for your trouble. All local
inhabitants of our little 5 aker wood & vicinity. Not to mention the
troll & dragon downstream (also compliments of Mitch).

So... Rock Creek #1 is back in business... sporting some lovely purple
stampage by Cara (thanks Cara... I went to check on the "Possum's
Penthouse" after reading that you didn't find it... it awaits your
return!!). The woods are leafing out, the trillium are about done
blooming, but some blue lily-looking flowers are popping up all over.
Starting to heavily scout for new hiding spots... something a little
tougher.... hmmmmm....
