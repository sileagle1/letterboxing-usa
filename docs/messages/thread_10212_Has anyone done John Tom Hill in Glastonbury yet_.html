From: John P. Sullivan (suljohn@cox.net) | Date: 2003-01-12 20:32:28 UTC-05:00
Hello Music Woman,
 
Did John Tom Hill a couple of weeks ago.  Nice View from near the box.

When we did the box we parked on the right, heading towards Route 94, just past the new dirt drive to a new cell tower.  It wasn't very 'sheltered' parking and we had to dig some snow out when we left. 
 
There is a trailhead there but no sign.  After you climb up through the gap in the foliage you'll find blazes, etc.  It's harder to find in winter - there really are stone steps but they're covered with snow.
 
Happy Hunting.
 
John

From: defygravity2001 <defygravity@snet.net> (defygravity@snet.net) | Date: 2003-01-15 02:01:40 UTC
When we did this, we parked roadside near the no parking sign. We took
it to mean you couldn't park in the field. We did miss it the first
time by, as it was not as far as we thought it'd be. The Gay City
trail seems to pass in a narrow corridor between two lots of private
land. Look in the blue book: The CT Walk Book for the map. It will
help a lot.

For the record, we did this with snow on the ground and it wasn't too
hard.

Aili
