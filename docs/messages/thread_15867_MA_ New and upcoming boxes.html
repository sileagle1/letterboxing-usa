Greetings!

Well, it took a lot longer than I thought to finally
finish (the last four stamps have been sitting on my
desk mocking me all summer...), but the Zodiac series
is finally complete. All twelve boxes are out. I know
a lot of folks have already been hunting them, and
some of you have asked when the last ones would be
ready. So, consider yourself informed. :-)

Be on the lookout for more new boxes soon. On the list
of plants coming up are a box at a National Historic
Site, a new Pagan Pride Box (the old box is going to
be retired, so if you want to get it, better do so
before the first Sunday in October!), the Celestial
Series, based on the planets, and a box commemerating
a historical figure from my home town.

Mike S.
