My dearest friends,

Quiet travelers from distant shores shall soon walk about our lands
of the Pacific Northwest in search of the perfect specimen of
Pseudotsuga menziesii and the the glorious crest of a white wave.
Whosoever willst welcomest these folk of fine repute in grand
fashion, please join us one `dies solis' during the month of the
snow moon in the 2004th year. On the day of 20 and 1, abandon your
typewriters and join one, join all during the 15th hour beneath the
only pavilion at the lake nearest the aerodrome (less than one
league therefrom).

Bringest with thee victuals, refreshment, letterboxing materiel & a
cheerful countenance. Conversation, exchanges of signatures,
merriment and a new letterbox or two shall be provided. The
youngest of thy clans are wholly welcomed.

Help us welcome these weary travelers with the openest of arms.
Until then, I bid thee well,

the seeker of adventures
