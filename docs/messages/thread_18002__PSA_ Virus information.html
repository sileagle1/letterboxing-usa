From: Wes Garrison (letterboxing@wesgarrison.us) | Date: 2004-03-03 10:54:29 UTC-06:00
Public Service Announcement

As you may or may not know, many viruses can send email from your computer to
everyone in your address book. The webmaster account ( webmasters @
letterboxing.org ) has been deluged with these automatically sent email viruses.
While Yahoo does filter them out, they do get in the way of us answering
questions.

So, if you have our address in your address book, we would GREATLY appreciate it
if you would scan your computer for viruses.

You can do a free scan of your computer from several places on the internet.
Here's a good one:
http://us.mcafee.com/root/mfs/default.asp
(This is not an endorsement. Just a well know company. Neither Wes nor any of
his relatives do not work for McAfee.)

If you don't like that one, here's a list:
http://dir.yahoo.com/Computers_and_Internet/Security_and_Encryption/Viruses_and_
Worms/Online_Virus_Scanners/

Please, take a moment to ensure that you're virus free.

If you have any questions, I'll be more than happy to answer questions.
Just get in touch with me off-list:
http://www.wesgarrison.us/Contact.asp

. . . or ask your favorite friend or relative that deals with computers.

-- Wes
