From: (TeeEmAch@aol.com) | Date: 2000-09-05 08:06:27 UTC-04:00
Hello everyone!
This will serve as my introduction to the list. I'm posting it under this subject line because it was thru the article in the Hartford Courant that I became aware of this very interesting sport. My name is Tina Habegger, I live in central CT. I have been an avid hiker for as long as I can remember, and I enjoy solving puzzles, so as you may well imagine I read the newspaper article with great interest. I checked out the URL listed in the article, did some exploring, and am already experimenting with creating my own stamp. I hope to get out by next weekend to locate and stamp my first letterbox. Can placing a box be far behind?
I'm really excited about this 'new' (new to me) outdoor activity.
Take Care, Tina
