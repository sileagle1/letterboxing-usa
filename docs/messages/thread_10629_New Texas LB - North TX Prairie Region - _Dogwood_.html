"Dogwood" Letterbox

Palestine, Texas Anderson County North Texas Prairie Region
Rusk/Palestine State Park

Date of Placement: 1/30/03
Difficulty: Easy
Total Distance to letterbox: About 100 yards
Placed by Baby Bear


The "Dogwood" letterbox is located in Rusk/Palestine State Park, in
the Palestine Unit. These parks are joined by the 25-mile Texas
State Railroad, and the Palestine Unit is primarily a day-use park
(though it does have some campsites). Take a ride on the trains
running March through October. Dogwood trees are present in the
park, thus the "Dogwood" stamp.
