From: be ma (bema57@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-04-28 18:12:49 UTC


What a fun time we had! Just the mom part of BeMa teamed up with Daughter
of the Old Dominion for our first stamping adventure together! I am happy
to announce that she is a very nice and fun person and not a cyber
psychopath as some had feared (o.k. -- just my mom...)

I told her that my "really bad sense of direction karma" was a potent force,
but I really don't think she believed me... Several u-turns later... But,
our bad directional luck disappeared once we hit the trail!

This was a fun set of boxes, allowing us to use our imaginations as we
followed the clues like true the Sherlock Holmes. I am happy to note that
we apparently had much better luck than Pychomommy -- it was funny reading
her notes of frustration knowing that she had triumphed in the end!

Mary and I worked well together -- seemingly taking turns finding the boxes!

I missed the kids, but, it was nice being able to choose MY OWN colors for
each stamp and Mary didn't whine nearly as much as Be and Ma (only kidding
-- she didn't whine at all!)

Together we found the whole series and even had time for a picnic lunch!

I'll have to go back with the family and show them the nature center and the
spooky swamp -- I think they'd get a kick out of it!

Thanks Mary for the great afternoon of boxing! BeMa
