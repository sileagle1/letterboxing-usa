Hey there,
This is the second box that I planted in Iowa last week.

It is in Iowa City,IA at the Black Angel. To learn more about the
Black Angel go here
www.icgov.org/documents/oaklandhistory.pdf
This is another "drive-by" letterbox.

Once in Iowa City, go to the Oakland Cemetary. Visit the Black Angel.
Underneath her outstretched wing is a small bush. In the middle of
the bush is the Black Angel Letterbox.

I apologize that this is not a homemade stamp. I had not planned on
planting a letterbox in the cemetary, but the friends that I traveled
with convinced me that it was the thing to do! (Peer pressure! :-)...
So we ran off to the local craft store and found a stamp that would
be appropriate!
