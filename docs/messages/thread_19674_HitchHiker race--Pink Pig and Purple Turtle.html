From: Periwinkle (kc7777@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-07-29 21:52:10 UTC-07:00
The Race is On!

Pink Pig (Betty) and Purple Turtle (Elvira) left their starting boxes
in Long Beach this morning, beginning their long trek to New York City
(the crowd cheers!).

Elvira headed up Signal Hill to enjoy one last panoramic view of the
Pacific before heading east. But wait--what's this?! She sputtered out
when she reached the top! She's settled into the Signal Hill Letterbox
for a breather! Well race fans, it looks like it's going to be a long
trip to the east coast for the turtle. Someone had better go pick her
up for the next leg of the journey.

Meanwhile, Betty is making plans to head north up the California Coast
very soon. Stay tuned for news of her 1st pit stop on the race to New
York!

Periwinkle
