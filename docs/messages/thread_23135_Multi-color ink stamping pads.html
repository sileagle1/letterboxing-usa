From: (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2006-03-08 20:36:51 UTC-05:00
We use LePlume brand pens that we buy at Michael's Crafts for a couple
dollars apiece or in sets for about $20. (Hint: in big Sunday papers every other
Sunday or so, Michael's includes a 40% off coupon in it's color ad.)

We take the stamp and use 3-4 colors of pens to color it depending on what
the picture is, then 'huff' on the stamp to remoisten the inks and stamp in.
Another way is to stamp in solid black, let dry on your page then use colored
pens to color in the spaces.

Sure makes for pretty books!
