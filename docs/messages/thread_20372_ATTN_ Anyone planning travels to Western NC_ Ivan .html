ATTN: Anyone planning travels to Western NC. Ivan hit us hard.

From: ncginger2000 (ncginger2000@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-17 23:56:01 UTC
I posted on the SE board but I thought I should give a heads-up over here as well.

Check route closings if you are planning trips that take you through the mountains of
Western NC in the near future. Frances took out whole sections of Blue Ridge Parkway
roadbed. Estimate for repair completion is next year.

Jackson County, NC, (Highlands, Cashiers and Franklin)--US 64 closed due to
mudslides caused by Ivan. Cashiers is completely cut off and without power.

I-40 between TN border and mile 20 in NC closed in both directions due to roadbed
washout from Ivan.

I-26 westbound between Asheville and Hendersonville one lane closed due to a
sinkhole.

Lots of flooding, possible bridge washouts, so check with AAA or something before
you head in our direction.

Knit Wit
