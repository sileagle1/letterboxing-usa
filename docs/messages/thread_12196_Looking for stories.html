Looking for stories

From: justplaingeorge (justplaingeorge@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-21 23:59:50 UTC
I'm getting ready to publish a book on letterboxing and am now
searching for personal stories. They can be about funny things that
happened while searching, unique finds, bizarre events or just
thoughts on the hobby. Clues also would be welcome, but it might be
best that they be letterboxes placed by permission since this will be
in print.

If you do not mind having it included in the book, please state in
the email that you are giving me permission to reprint your story.
Depending on space only part of it may be used... I can be reached
at justplaingeorge@yahoo.com Please do *not* reply to the list!

Also, let me know if you prefer your letterboxing name or real name
to be used.

I've LOVED some of what I've read on this list and think letterboxing
adventures would make a great read.

Please understand that this is not a huge book event. I am self-
publishing it primarily for the fun of putting it together and maybe
introducing a few new people to letterboxing.

Thanks!
Shell
justplaingeorge@yahoo.com
