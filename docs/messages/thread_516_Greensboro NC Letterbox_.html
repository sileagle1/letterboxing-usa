I am pleased to report that there is now a letterbox in
Greensboro North Carolina! I have called it the Lake Daniel
Park Letterbox #1. Here are the clues for anyone who would
like to come to Greensboro and take a nice walk in the park to
find the letterbox:

Starting at the Swimming Pool, begin your journey by following
Mimosa. Enter the park to the left when you see the Bridge over
Buffalo Creek. Take a right on the bike trail (do not cross
Benjamin or you have gone too far!). When you see a bench on your
left you can stop and rest a while.
