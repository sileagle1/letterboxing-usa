The Clothespin Box

I was planted by mlgainey on February 1, 2003, at Hanover Wayside Park
in Hanover County, Virginia. The nearest town is Ashland. Hanover
Wayside Park is on Rt. 301. This should be an easy, quick walk to find me!

Park as close as possible to the shelter that has a wooden causeway
leading down to it.

Walk down the wooden causeway and then to your left.

Take the trail that runs beside the pond.

Cross the bridge.

Go up the wooden steps, staying on the path as it leaves the pond.

Take the right fork.

Take another right fork.

Immediately after you cross the small bridge, go off-trail to your right.

Walk beside the stream until you reach its end.

A long fallen tree is laying across the stream's end.

I am hidden by leaves beside this fallen tree near the root end.

Please help yourselves to one of my brethren in addition to stamping
into me.

Please rehide me well with leaves and leave me "hanging around" for
the next boxer to find!
