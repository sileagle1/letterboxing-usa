From: Randy Hall (randy@mapsurfer.com) | Date: 2002-05-14 17:56:05 UTC-04:00

> Anyway, you know you can turn off the distribution of attachments? The
> Moderator has to do it. Go into

My thoughts on this are in the archives many times over. I may rehash
them on a web page later if I feel like it but don't feel like rehashing
them here.

The bottom line is this -

I'm philosophically against censoring attachments, but if a supermajority
(think in the 5/8 to 2/3 ballpark) of those with an opinion wants them
censored, discuss it, prove it, and it will be so. I will give added weight
to the opinions of old-timers who have been with the list as it is for a long
time. Since no one is chiming in in support of allowing attachments, it may
be a no-brainer.

(As an aside, I feel sending attachments to a mailing list is a poor way to
distribute content. Rather than send large word files or jpegs to 440 people,
consider using the upload file area, or creating a web site and posting links
to
this content. This saves alot of resources, and assures that people who don't
care about your content aren't paying the price to carry it).

(as another aside, consider using a mail program other than outlook. You will
get less worms and viruses, regardless of what this list decides to do, and
you will still have to deal with it even if the list decides to censor
attachments)

Cheers
Randy
List Manager
