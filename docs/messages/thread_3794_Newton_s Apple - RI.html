Debbie,

Newton's Apple was a temporary "simbiant" stamp hosted
by H2OFIRE. The stamp was to commemorate the 2001
Gravity Games held not 50 feet from the location of
the stamp. The Original H2OFIRE and Newton's Apple
have both been removed (by someone other than me...
garbage detail,perhaps?) and the new impression for
H20FIRE has been out for more than a month. Newton's
Apple is no longer there, so if the webmasters would
not mind to take down the link, it would be
appreciated... there will eventually be another
Newton's Apple standalone box!
