Great 'Boxing Day in CT

From: markpepe <mjpepe1@attbi.com> (mjpepe1@attbi.com) | Date: 2002-12-16 02:32:58 UTC
Although a little cool, we spent Sunday, 12/15 visiting the
following letterboxes, which were all in great shape and we
thoroughly enjoyed them. They were all beautifully carved stamps:

The Christmas Mystery Letterbox
You Otter Seet This Letterbox
Paddle to the Sea Letterbox
Christmas Around the World [Series of 6]

Much to our surprise, we were the first to find the "Bohemian
Traveler" Hitchhiker.

We did have one strike-out, World War 1 Silent Night. We found the
foundation of the old building and the flat rock, but couldn't find
the proper tree with the 2 rocks. We looked for about 45 minutes and
it started to get dark. Might try again in the spring.
