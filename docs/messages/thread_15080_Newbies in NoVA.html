From: Tortoise & the Hare (letterbox@upperclassglass.com) | Date: 2003-08-13 15:01:57 UTC-04:00
Hi Narnian and Lawrence,

Sometimes boxes are so well hidden you miss them, sometimes they are missing
and sometimes they are damaged.. Keep with it and you are bound to find lots
of boxes in great shape with beautiful stamps!

My husband and I videotaped our first attempt at letterboxing over a year ago
now (new video camera, new letterboxing hobby - what a combo!) and we totally
missed where the box was at first.. the tape shows us scanning right past it
several times. We never video taped a hunt again - I got motion sick trying
to watch it!

Now I can usually spot the likely hiding spot from 30 feet away. Of course
I'm not always right.. :-D

Good luck on your next outing!

Tortoise (of the Tortoise & the Hare)
