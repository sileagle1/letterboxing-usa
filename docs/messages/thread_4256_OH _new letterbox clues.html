Great Serpent Mound

Ohio, Adams County ,the park is on State Route 73~ six miles north of
State Route 32~ 20 miles south of Bainbridge,Ohio.
Difficulty: moderate,trail walking
Placed : 22nd January, 2002 by franzsolo...

The most famous of all such (effigy) mounds is The Great Serpent Mound
in Adams County Ohio.
1,330 feet in length along its coils and averaging 3 feet in height.
Also the park has a great vantage point for photographers from the tower
overlooking the great Serpent.

The Ohio Historical Society will be collecting a vehicle parking fee
that varies according to your particular vehicle.
