From: schizokeet (Beckyrink@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-07-16 15:30:09 UTC
There is a 6$ fee for out of county residents to drive into the main
gate during the weekends in the summer. If you are out of county
like me and don't wish to pay there is a State run boat launch that
is the next exit over on 123. There is no charge to drive and park
in this lot as it is a state lot. To get to where the Scriminal
clues start follow the paved trail to the left the lake. You will
see the big parking lot on the laft. Old boots clues can be started
from here, just head right and you will hit the dam.
