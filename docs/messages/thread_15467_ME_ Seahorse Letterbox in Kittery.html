From: Laura (naturbuf@gwi.net) | Date: 2003-08-31 19:37:40 UTC
This box has been reported missing several times recently and the
owner was not replying to e-mails inquiring about its status. We
decided we'd try for it anyway and wanted to let folks know that the
box is indeed there. However, I worry that people have been trampling
around and digging up places in searching for it - in fact there is no
digging that is required. We are friends with someone whose father
manages that site and he is concerned about the impact it is having. I
don't want to give anything away, but out of concern for the habitat,
and to make sure the letterbox is allowed to stay there, please check
to make sure you have the right tree before taking your second
compass reading - it truly is a granite point as the clue says.
Thanks!
