Name:Indian Mound at East Fork Lake

Location: Batavia, Ohio

Address: Take SR 125 Amelia from I-275 about 11 miles, Bantam Road is Left at the
light and SR 222 is to the Right. Goto The Left, The park is about .5 Miles to the
Left. Once on the Park Road, Be on the lookout as after you pass Newman Trail
you will need to start looking for Indian Trail

Level: 1 (Easy)


Need: Stamp Pad, Pen

Clues: Look for a water Fountin with the yellow number 8. From here
take a reading of 280' and walk 65 steps. This should take you to the
corner of the woods. Now take a reading of 240' and walk 26 steps
to a row of small pine tree's. 7 steps behind the pine tree's at 340'
into the woods you will see a Triple Tree with one bad trunk. The
box is at the bottom in between the tree.
