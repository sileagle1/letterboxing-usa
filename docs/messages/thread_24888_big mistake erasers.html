NEWS FLASH!!!

For all you pink eraser carvers out there. Dollar Tree Stores are
getting in there back to school supplies! Look for the Big Mistake
erasers. They came in everyday pink and green. They measure in at
about 5 1/2 inches by 2 inches wide and 5/8 of an inch thick. These
are very big pink erasers. Cost? You guessed it a buck, buck, buck!
Some stores may also have the white erasers in 2 sizes. Last year
the small size came in 6 packs, the nice larger size came in 3 packs.
These are a great size for stamps. Have fun! May everyday be an
adventure.
