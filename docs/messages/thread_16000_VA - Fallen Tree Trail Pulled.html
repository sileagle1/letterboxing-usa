VA - Fallen Tree Trail Pulled

From: TownCrier74 (towncrier74@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-25 02:25:14 UTC-07:00
Thanks to Isabel and her daughter I pulled Fallen Tree Trail yesterday.

I visited the box on 22 Sep. and found it in excellent condition. However there were a number of freshly fallen trees across the trail although it was still usable at that time.

Visited again on the 24th to try a bit of trail maintenance. The post Isabel storm had dropped a few more trees. I bushwacked around two fresh groups and a new, but temporary, swamp. The box was still in great shape.

At this time I consider the trail unusable for Letterboxing purposes.

I will be scouting another trail as soon as possible for Fallen Tree Tail #2 or an alternate route to the first location. Then will make a decision on the first trail. After all we don't have a hurricane every year.
