NEW TRAVELER STS 107 COLUMBIA

From: defygravity2001 <defygravity@snet.net> (defygravity@snet.net) | Date: 2003-02-03 00:39:35 UTC
When the Challenger crashed I was a teen with no
way to express my grief, other than tears of grief and loss. I shed
the same tears as I watched the Columbia and her crew fall from the
sky, but this time I had an outlet to channel some of my feelings of
grief and loss. I made a stamp and launched the STS 107 Columbia from
CT as a Traveling Box. May she and her crew fly always in our hearts
and in the stars of heaven.

Bruce, Aili and Erin
