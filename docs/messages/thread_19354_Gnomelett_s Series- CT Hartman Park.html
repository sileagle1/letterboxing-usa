Hello all!

We went out to find the Gnomelett's Series today. The kids, myself
& some friends set out & easily found box number 1. It's had some
water damage & the log books are a bit soggy, but we sealed the bags
well before we left & hopefully they'll be fine.

Box number 2 was located, but the stamp is missing. Boo hoo. We
stamped in anyway & put the box back (I've contacted the keepers of
the series, fear not).

We could not locate box number 3, but I think that we stopped a
little too soon & were looking at the wrong boulders now that I re-
read the clue from the comfort of my couch. We'll have to go back &
search for that one again.

Just wanted to warn you that box number 2 is stampless & remind you
to seal the baggies when you finish please...especially if the clue
isn't in a sealable container.

In all a beautiful day for a little hike with the wee ones. We'll
be back at it again next weekend!
