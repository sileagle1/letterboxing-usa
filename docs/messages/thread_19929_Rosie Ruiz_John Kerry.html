Wow! We dropped off Rosie while heading out of town to go camping
and I was gonna post her whereabouts when we returned. Imagine my
surprize to see the John Kerry post. I thought it odd and went to
check on the box I left Rosie in and sure enough she was gone and in
her place a John Kerry stamp in the log book. Hand carved too. It is
a "negative image" stamp, but heck, pretty good for a first stamp!
Even odder was that I had left Rosie in a local word of mouth box,
knowing that a few folks were planning to try to nab it soon. Who
would have thought Kerry would stumble on a WOM clue? I guess folks
in high places have good resources!
