From: Janet (moonstone_baby@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-12 23:01:12 UTC

A find is a find. I count a HH that I found when checking one of my
letterboxes as a find. The "Should I move it along of leave it"
question has been discussed many times before on this website (as I
am sure it will discussed again.) Some people take the HH when they
find it in their own box. That is a fair thing to do. Others, like
me, do not take the HH out of their box. What I do is stamp into the
HH and leave it for the next person to find in my box. It is nice to
reward the people who find your box :) As with finding a HH in any
box (yours or someone elses) the decision is yours to take or leave.
I will tell you that I felt bad once when I missed getting a HH in a
box by a few hours because the box owners had come by eariler in the
day to check on the box and took the HH. I got over it LOL.

moonstone
