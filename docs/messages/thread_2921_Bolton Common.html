Judy and Elizabeth posted the webmasters reminding us that we've
never linked to their 15 fantastic letterboxes in Bolton, MA. We
Drews were lucky enough last fall to find a couple of these terrific
letterboxes and really enjoyed them. Bolton is one of those towns
that takes conservation seriously: beautiful New England scenes,
history, fun trails, inspired handcut stamps. Go if you get a chance:
