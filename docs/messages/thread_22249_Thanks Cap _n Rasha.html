Hi all,
If you do not have the privelage of knowing Cap'n Rasha then you are
missing truly great people. The other day I received a yellow mailer
and saw that it was from Cap 'n Rasha. I assumed it was a letterbox
and immediately opened it. Inside was a absolutely gorgeous lady bug
pin in the shape of a heart with hearts for the spots on her back.
This is almost identical to my signature stamp, Texasluvbug. Also a
note with their family of stamps was included that stated that she had
been out shopping and saw this pin and it reminded her of my stamp
that she had recently seen in a PLB logbook. I was actually kinda
having a bad day so this actually made my whole year. Thank you
Cap 'n Rasha for such an awesome, thoughtful gesture. I absolutely
love it. Ya'll are just super great folks.

Once again Thank You,
Texasluvbug
