From: Beth Houghtaling (JustBeth65@msn.com) | Date: 2003-05-16 23:55:44 UTC
Hi Christi,

I know you could reach the Presidential series #1 and Aarons B-day box also.
The Presidential series #2 is more uphill but I think it could be done
especially if it is a jogging type stroller. It may take you 1 1/2 or 2
hours to find the boxes. You would probably need someone to stand with the
stroller because some of the boxes are off the trail abit, but you can see
the trail easily from most hiding spots.

I hope that helps.

~Beth
