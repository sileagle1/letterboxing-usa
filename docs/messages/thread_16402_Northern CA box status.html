Well, it hasn't been a good week for my poor letterboxes! I have received reports that
several of them are missing - and I was on such a streak there for a while. Here's
what I've heard:

Every Flavor Beans in Fairfield could not be located - have not been up there to
confirm, which actually will be somewhat hard to do since A-Bear moved it for me
from its original spot and I'm not positive I know where to check!

Skywalker Ranch near Lagunitas in Marin County - all 3 reported missing! This was
one of the first series I ever planted and I have never had a chance to check them
since they are so far from my home. They are hidden in a state park, and it says so in
the clues. Has anyone else had any problems with California state parks? Maybe
some ranger pulled them all.... I will try to get to Marin sometime this month to
confirm that they are gone (including my Princess Leia stamp - boo hoo)

Beach Grover in Grover Beach - confirmed by Sir Balthazar. Lived only 8 days!

Well, they say bad things come in threes, so I am hoping that this will end the rash of
missing letterboxes for me. Naturally none of these boxes is near my home. Good
news is that I'm headed for Tuolumne County this weekend and will be planting out
there.
