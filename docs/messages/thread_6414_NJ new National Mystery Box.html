Hi,

It is not good letterboxing etiquette to publicly divulge any part of a solution to a Mystery Letterbox, since it can potentially spoil the fun for others. Letterboxers often put a great deal of thought and time into the creation of their Mystery Letterboxes. If you have questions or observations about solving one of these, you should direct them to the owner of the box only.
