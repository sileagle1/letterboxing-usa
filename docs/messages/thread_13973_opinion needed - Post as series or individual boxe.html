You're right, usually when you see series, they are all in one park.
But I have a series of 3 boxes in VA called In The Park that is in 3
neighborhood parks within about a 1 sq mile area. The clues have
directions on how to drive from one park to the next. Anyone reading
the clues can see that the boxes can be done in a different order. I
have a big series of 11 boxes coming up (if I ever get the initiative

different parks.

individual boxes.

I would concur with the opinion that if you have to drive from one
box to another, post them as seperate clue sheets. In some cases
they may be in different towns, counties, or states (for ambitious
box placers), and the clues are listed seperately by these facts.

If you can park in one location and walk to all the boxes, then they
can be all posted on one clue sheet.
