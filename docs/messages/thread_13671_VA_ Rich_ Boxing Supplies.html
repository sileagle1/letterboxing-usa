For Richmond, VA boxers or those boxing in Richmond in the near
future, FYI:

The Ben Franklin crafts store on Midlothian Tnpk. has lots of stamp
pads on clearance for $1.49 each. Lots of colors and both dye and
pigment inks. The other Ben Franklins in the Richmond area sent all of
these particular pads to this store, which I was told by the store
manager is the clearance store in the chain.

The Midlothian Tnpk. Ben Franklin also has several dozen types of
scrapbook papers on sale. They're discontinued styles, and they're
only a dime each. If you make your own logbooks or you scrapbook, you
might want to check out these sheets.
