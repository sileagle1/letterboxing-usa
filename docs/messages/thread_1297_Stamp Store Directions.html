Hi Melissa-
I am glad to give directions to my store-- I just hate to look like I
am trying to promote my business on the list. I normally carry 3
sizes of the Marscarve carving blocks. Unfortunately my supplier is
back ordered on some sizes currently and I only have a few at the
moment - but more should arrive soon.

Off Rte 9 take exit 3. If coming from the south (I-95) take a left
off the ramp then a right at the light.
If coming from the north (Middletown) take a left off the ramp then
another left at the light.

Pass a pond on your right, then the Essex Library and Town Hall on
your left.
Take a right at the stop sign then immediately bear left to the top
of Main Street. Continue down Main St. until the Griswold Inn is on
your right.

I am located in the same building as the coffee shop that fronts
directly on Main Street on the left - my store is at the rear of the
building. I will treat any Lb-ers to a great cup of coffee at the
coffee shop if you stop in. Should you not have already located the
Turtle Creek letterbox that would make a nice outing -- I believe the
other Essex box is currently missing, however.

If the Marscarve blocks are your main goal I suggest you call ahead
to make sure they have arrived --- toll free 877-662-7761

Hope to see - and meet you soon!
Kathleen
