Please note that the Rookery Letterbox in Clark County WA is back in action.
Also, please update the clue for Discovery Trail, Clark County WA as follows:

On March 8, 2002 Funhog placed the Discovery Trail Letterbox in Vancouver, Clark County, Washington. Take a
stroll along this greenspace trail or ride your bike to find this little letterbox.

to the Trailhead: From I-5 take exit 1D/Fourth Plain Blvd. west to the stoplight at Fruit Valley Road. Turn right
and go to the Stewart Glen parking area at the corner of Bernie Drive and Fruit Valley.

to the Letterbox: Go east on the Discovery Trail. Pass by the trappers' resting place. You will soon be walking
through an open meadow, a scattering of ancient fruit trees here and there. When you find yourself standing
between hollow trees right and left, the letterbox is eighteen steps away at 160 degrees.

Many thanks, Funhog
