MD insanity

From: (Fireflylight@aol.com) | Date: 2002-10-10 16:51:16 UTC-04:00
I'm convinced that Psychomommy wanted me to post first so that I can look like the completely insane one, but I'm dragging her down with me!  This was all her idea (although, I certainly didn't object!)

Today in the pouring Maryland rain (finally!) Psychomommy and I went sludging through Greenbriar State Park and found The ADG's latest series.  We stopped in the visitor center to get a map and the woman we spoke to there said "who would want to go out hiking in this mess?"  "Um... we do..."

Many thanks to The ADG for being kind to us Marylander boxers.  We loved all the stamps.  We had quite a system going.  We had to hang Karin's umbrella from tree branches so we could get underneath it and stamp away.  I laughed out loud at the note in the first box!

So, now I have gone boxing in complete darkness and pouring rain.  Now I just need a good snow storm.

Thanks again to Karin for the fun!  I had a great time.

Hey ADG - Karin says that we can have a party when I find my 100th box...
Of course, at this point, I'll be partying in the insane asylum!

Coleen
"Firefly"
P18 F77 X15 HH2
