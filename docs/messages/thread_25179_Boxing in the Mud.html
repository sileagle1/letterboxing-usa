Boxing in the Mud

From: ElizabethT (zorket@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-03-12 10:51:57 UTC
I decided I had to get out of the house for a short bit yesterday. So I went scouting, trying to hunt up some good clue ideas for a box I'm planning. However, it was so muddy, I didn't get too far. One of the roads was washed out, and my car bottomed out. I was afraid I was going to be stuck out there! But I pulled through it. One of the places I went to look at was completely closed. There was a sign up - trail closed, authorized personnel only - HARDHAT AREA!!! So I skipped that one. So, I took some pictures and called it a day. And I made it home in one piece.

zorket
