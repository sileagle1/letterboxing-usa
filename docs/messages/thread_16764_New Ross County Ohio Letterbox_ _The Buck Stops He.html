Title: The Buck Stops Here. Location: Chillicothe Ohio. Placed 11-08-
03 by Salamander. Difficulty: Easy. *** This box is located in St.
Margaret's Cemetery, on Worthington Avenue in Chillicothe, Ohio. For
driving directions, please see clues for "Handiwork", placed by
Salamander. CLUES: Upon entering the cemetery, you must go to the
left. Keep bearing left whenever you come to a crossroads. At
about .2 mile from the entrance, start looking for two monuments,
which will be on your left. One is a large white cross, while a bit
behind it and to the right is a black granite marker which is shaped
like a square balancing on one of its corners. Stand behind the
black monument and take a compass reading of 300 degrees. Ten paces
at this heading will bring you to a tree with a stump right up
against it. Here you will find the letterbox, under a pile of rocks
and leaves.
