Grist in the Mist, Mansfield , Ct.

From: Carolyn Stearns (cstearns@neca.com) | Date: 2000-09-10 19:11:27 UTC-04:00
Time: 15 minutes, on weekends plan to spend time for a little history lesson
 
Rating:  Easy with compass
 
Sponsor: Cock-A-Doodle-Moo 4-H Club
 
In Mansfiled , Ct. find out where there was grist. Not now but long ago.  Rivers powered old stones to grind local farmers grain. Dirt roads , not far from UCONN, now part of a special land trust who work hard to make Mansfiled a better place to live.  This location requires you to be VERY DISCREET  don't be seen.  It's a great place for a picnic which would make good cover for your activity.  When there follow these simple directions:
 
    from the center of the front step  go 20 degrees  , 13 paces.  next reading is 280 degrees 12 paces.  At 120 degrees you'll find your box. Remeber don't be seen getting it.                   Good Luck
