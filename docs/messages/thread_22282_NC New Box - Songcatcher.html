If youre between Leapin Lovers and Max Patch, this is a sweet little
"practically-a-drive-by box.

Three miles from the madding crowds of metro Hot Springs is my favorite
campground - so under-utilized by the camping public it has almost been
closed by the USFS. It is much quieter than its nearest cousins, and
charming in a CCC sort of way.

Very easy, very short hike with a beautiful long view and the perfect place
to stamp up.

Drive in and find what Spencer overlooked.. Several paths present
themselves; not to worry. Youll get there. Sit and enjoy the sounds and
sights and smells. Then pace left about six. Thigh-high geometrics make
the path interesting; above them a roundish form squats above a soft nest,
twigged and barked to camouflage a souvenir.

Shhhhh....did you catch the Songs?
