Title: Fox Trotting. Placed 4-01-03 by Salamander. Location:
Chillicothe, Ross County, Ohio. Difficulty: Easy. ***Please bring pen
and stamppad. This letterbox is located near the pedestrian/bikeway
corridor in Chillicothe. CLUES: Somewhere along the 3.1 mile long
pedestrian/bikeway corridor, find the memorial drinking fountain
which is dedicated to a Mr. White. Standing in the middle of the
walkway directly opposite the fountain, take a reading of 345
degrees. There is a tree not too far away at that heading. If you
look at the back side of the tree, you can find evidence of a fox
trotting.
