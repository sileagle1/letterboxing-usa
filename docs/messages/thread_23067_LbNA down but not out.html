From: (john@johnsblog.com) | Date: 2006-02-13 10:08:29 UTC-05:00
Our current web hosting service shut us off last Thursday
night due to what they felt was excessive load on their
database server. I've found several optimzations that can
be made (thanks to Ryan for his tips).

I spent most of the day yesterday loading LbNA on a new
web hosting service and experimenting with optimizations.
If we can't get the current hosting service to restore
service by the end of today, I'll provide a temporary link
to the new hosting service and start the process of
pointing letterboxing.org to it.

My apologies to everyone that missed out on some boxing
this weekend because of the problems.

one of the LbNA webmasters
