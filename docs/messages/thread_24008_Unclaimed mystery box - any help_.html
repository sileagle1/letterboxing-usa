But unfortunately she said she checked twice and couldn't find it. Very
interesting clues tho. I can see so many possibilities that the placer
might have used to confuse the actual location of the box. The path to that
box might not be as straightforward as it first seems.

And does the "to be continued....." mean that it was going to be a series
eventually or could it mean the clues were originally posted as a teaser and
there are actually more clues that should have come for that particular box
and they never got posted? For example, we have one box in our area that
was listed on LbNA for probably a year or more, but the placer only listed
the box name, state, and closest city/town. If one clicked on the name of
the box, there were no real clues -- they must have dropped out of
letterboxing before they got that far. The clues just said something like
"Stay tuned....."

I really like the Haunted Canal clues. Is there any way of knowing the
trailname of the original placer or is that lost forever once the box
tumbled down the stairs into the basement?
