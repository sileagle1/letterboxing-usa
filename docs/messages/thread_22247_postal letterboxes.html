Hi Bonedoc!

You have a good chance of catching an open PLB on the Postal Letterboxing Groups List. As soon you see a post "opening" a PLB that you are interested in, waste no time in Emailing the box maker with your address. Be sure, though, you can abide by any requirements set by the maker ex: Priority Mail, quick mail out, etc. Now, have a blast! MayEve
P.S. Word of caution... be careful of the # of PLB's you sign up for! You don't want to have to sell the antique vase on the mantle or eat beans for a week to cover your "habit" !
