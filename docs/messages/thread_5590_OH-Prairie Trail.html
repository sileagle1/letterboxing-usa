Name:Prairie Trail at East Fork Lake

Location: Batavia, Ohio

Address: Take SR 125 Amelia from I-275 about 11 miles, Bantam Road is Left at the
light and SR 222 is to the Right. Goto The Left, THe park is about .5 Miles to the
Left. Once on the Park Road, Be on the lookout as after you pass Newman Trail
you will need to start looking for The Prairie Trail

Level: 1 (Easy)


Need: Stamp Pad, Pen

Clues: From the Prairie Trail, Head off 200' You will come to a post with an
arrow on it. From that Post take a reading of 300'

Next you will come to a post that say's "Nature Station: Animal Signs" From
this station take a reading of 120' See the small tree, It's 18 Steps to the base
of the tree BUT watch out for the stickers :O)
