The Bald Eagle Letterbox
Placed 20 Sep 02 by Aryn from
Eagle(jedaryn02@yahoo.com)
Nampa, ID
Canyon County

Directions: Take Exit 35 of I84 and head north on
Northside Rd. Turn left at the Sugar Factory onto
Karcher Rd. Turn left onto Lake Ave and follow this
road until you see signs to turn right to the Deer
Flat National Wildlife Refuge Visitor Center where you
can park.

Unfortunately after I placed this awesome stamp and
box I found out the road to the Visitor's Center is
only open M-F from 8am-4pm so this limits your ability
to reach it. However, the opportunities to see an
assortment of birds is a must see on Lake Lowell.

Clues: Follow the 1/2 mile loop on the Nature Trail.
I'm hiding somewhere between the four and five. From
the 4 follow the narrow makeshift trail 42 steps at
170 degrees. Find me entangled at the southern base
of the multiheaded tree with its roots dancing in and
out of the ground (you must pass the trees with the 27
to find me).

Once you've stamped in please hide me well in and
under the tree root as I'm little, but easy to see if
the 2.5" diameter rocks don't cover me. Enjoy the
rest of the trail.
