David,

First of all my heart is breaking for you. This badness stuff tends
to come in waves but it will eventually get better.
Side note: as a pessimist I personally believe it generally gets
worse before it gets better (ain't I a fountain of hope...). Now,
having dropped that bomb: it makes it easier for me to handle the
tribulations just 'cuz I expect the worst. If the worst doesn't
happen that's a pleasant surprise... :rolleyes: Hey, it works for me!

In 2003 I lost my Alabama. She was "only" a dog, a first anniversary
gift (1991) from my cat-loving wife, and I miss her still. Don't kid
yourself, they become family. I started 'boxing in Nov. '05, In
Jan. '06 I planted my first letterbox: Alabama's Getaway. It means
a lot to me to remember my Al'ie in this way, and share a place with
others that we enjoyed together many, many times over the years.

My vote is to memorialise Rocky with his own letterbox; let the
healing begin.
Connfederate
