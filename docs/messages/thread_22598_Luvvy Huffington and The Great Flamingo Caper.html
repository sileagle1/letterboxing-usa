Hello Dear Friends:

It is with great sadness that I tell you that Sue & I had the
opportunity to visit with Luvvy Huffington this weekend. Suffice it to
say that she is half of her former self. When we entered her room,
Luvvy was in a stupor, folding napkins in the shape of little
flamingos, just as she was shown many years ago by Lady Prisspot at
LP's finishing school.

This note is to ask all of you to please help Luvvy find Percival on
October 1 at the Great Urban Plastic Pink Flamingo Caper in
Leominster, MA. Every letterboxing detective with a sharp eye and keen
wit is needed to ensure that we can finally hunt out Percival and
reunite him with his mistress, Luvvy.

For further details, please check Atlas Quest Events Page and our
website.

We emplory you to help - we don't know how long Luvvy can hang on! Her
little hands are getting chapped from the repetitive motion of the
napkin-folding!
