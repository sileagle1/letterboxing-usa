This box was planted on June 28, 2002 by Leader of the Pack, Cowboy
and Draft Dad on the last day of thier New Hampshire vacation. Please
consider this a thank - you to the planter of Seacoast 2 letterbox
and a added magical pleasure to those who come along next.
Follow Aisling's clues to the Urban Forestry Center in Rye, New
Hampshire. Follow clues to her wonderful box. We valiantly fought the
mosquitos and found our treasure happily. We were so happy we
decided to add to the fun! So continue on Goodwin happy with what
you found. Pass the ledge and woodpile. We came to a place where
we needed to make a decision . We stood and pondered a moment and the
Cowboy in his usual energy bounded to the right and said " I found
it!" Oh yes this was a suitable magical place. Look for a rotted
stump just off the trail. Be sure no others lurk nearby in the wood.
If strangers come upon a Golden Pegasus it may just disappear forever
from our midst. Magical creatures are so special so let Peg alight
in your journal before you tuck her back in her quiet home.
