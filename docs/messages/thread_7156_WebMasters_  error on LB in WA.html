Webmasters--I apologize for this but there is still an error with
the WA State portion of the website. Only one series of letterboxes
is in the town of Lakewood, WA--the 'Fort Steilacoom Letterboxes'
which contain the 'WA Territorial Insane Asylum LB' and the 'Heath
Farm LB'. The other two letterbox series-- 'Chambers Creek Park'
and 'Old Town Steilacoom'--are actually within the town limits of
Steilacoom. I'm sorry for the confusion. This clarification may
help letterbox seekers find these parks a bit easier since most area
maps don't necessarily highlight these small regional parks--they
do, however, highlight the towns/villages in this area.

Thanks again for all of your hard work in maintaining this awesome
website!!! See you on the trail...
