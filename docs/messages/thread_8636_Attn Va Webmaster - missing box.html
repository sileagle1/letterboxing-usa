Attn Va Webmaster - missing box

From: Mary from Virginia (daughteroftheolddominion@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-23 21:19:27 UTC
Today I checked my In the Park Series (Fairfax County) and am sad to
report that the second box, Blake Lane Park, is absolutely,
positively, undeniably GONE. Please don't remove the clues to that
box yet, just mark it as VERIFIED MISSING 10/23/02. Thanks.

I don't know how it went missing but since its hiding spot under a
heavy slab of concrete and not facing any trail, the most likely
possibility is that a letterboxer's activities were observed. This
reminds us that we need to be Invisible Letterboxers at these crowded
parks. I've been known to do the Happy Dance when I found a box.
I've got to cut that out.

Mary (moping in Virginia)
