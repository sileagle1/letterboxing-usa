ri: today's gathering (!!!)

From: Christopher Martin (cscm@edgenet.net) | Date: 2001-10-06 21:23:10 UTC-04:00
I've just returned from the Fall Gathering in Burlingame State Park in
Charlestown, RI, after a frenzied and chaotic day of stamping, stamping
and more stamping! Now with a little time to take stock I find that I
managed to track down 16 letterboxes, including four travellers and one
hitchhiker. I exchanged 32 or 33 stamps, depending upon whether Spirit
Kitty counts*. And I snagged one event stamp and one, um, game stamp
(for lack of a better category).

It was great to be able to put so many faces to so many stamps. Despite
the lousy weather during the first half of the day there was never a lack
of new people to meet. I'd like to extend a huge thank you to Trish for
organizing -- I had a fine time and you did a fantastic job!

Now back to my ordinary, hum-drum life. ;-)

CSCM
p2 f78 x35 e2

*If Spirit Kitty is imaginary, as I gathered s/he is, does he/she count
as an exchange? I counted Max, and he's a dog, AND I didn't even get to
actually meet him. But if I count Spirit Kitty, I should probably then
count Betz's Batik, which is a car and not even sentient. How many times
can you split a hair, anyway? Well, best to err on the side of
inclusion, I suppose. p2 f78 x37 e2.
