Hi everyone,

In 2000 a series of boxes was placed at Oxbow Park in Gresham, OR. There
placer has since fallen out of the letterboxing scene. Since I placed a
series of boxes out there as well, I adopted the box that remained. I had
printed the clues from the web site back in September. Since then the clues
had been pulled. I retyped the clues from what I had printed.

The set I had only listed 2 boxes.. and indicated that box 2 was missing.
Well, someone was out this last weekend and came upon a box that says box 4
in the Oxbow series. My question to the list is this, does anyone possibly
have the original clues for all 4 boxes? If so I'd like to go out and
confirm their existance and then add them to the clues I've adopted. It
would be a sad thing to have boxes out there that are not being visited for
lack of clues. Especially clues to carvings as wonderful as box 1 was.

And yes, I searched through the archives for 2 hours. I found the original 2
boxes posted but couldn't find boxes 3 and 4.
