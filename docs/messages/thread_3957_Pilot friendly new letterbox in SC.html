Wright Stuff Letterbox
Placed December 17, 2001
Commemorates man's first powered flight

Advisories:
- near airport - AVOID ALL RESTRICTED AREAS
- very easy access
- accessible by pilots (i.e. no car) as well as those driving to the
starting point.

Start:
Find FBO counter at MYR. Turn 180 degrees and pause at the "Nortons'
Memorial". Go to nearest water fountain and take the nearest exit
from the building. Go straight to Airdrome Avenue and follow it
to "Huger". Turn left on Huger and wander past the 'ultimate pizza'
(but don't expect to eat). Next you'll pass the biggest 'fox' you've
EVER seen until you start 'shining'. Turn right and "shine on".
Instead of the Harvest Moon, however, look for the three-posted
wooden fence. "Wright Stuff" is located at the farthest post on
the "Wright"!
Hope you all have fun finding this box and please pause to
contemplate what our world would be like without those like the
Wright Brothers AND the Nortons.
