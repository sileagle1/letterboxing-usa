New Member Intro MO

From: Malinda Welte (malinda@mweltephotoart.com) | Date: 2003-07-31 09:33:55 UTC-05:00
Hi All,
I'm Malinda (waves). I found about letterboxing from the Carving Consortium
Forum. I guess I did it backwards, sort of. I do mail art, artistamps,
artist trading cards and have started carving stamps recently, I've done 4
so far. My DH, Ron (Structural Designer), and our two kids, Rianna (3 1/2)
and Geordan (10 mos) and myself (SAHM/Photographer) all love to be outdoors
and have traveled around the midwest looking for cool parks and places to
visit and be out in nature. This seems like a perfect fit. I took the
kids out Tuesday and we found our first letterbox, Snoopy in Shawnee
Mission, KS, and a new playground to visit. We will be carving our family
stamp soon, I got everyone's approval for the design, and finding our log
book. I have lots of ideas for new letterboxes in the Kansas City Metro
area and hope to spend fun weekends searching new ones out! I'm glad there
are some letterboxers in the area and look forward to meeting them.

Malinda
