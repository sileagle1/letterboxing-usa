Wanda hits F8000 in Maine

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-28 19:15:25 UTC

Just heard from Wanda up in Maine where she found her 8000th letterbox
along the Appalachian Trail between the Crockers and the Bigelows.
Apparently, she got stuck in the "Back Kingdom" yesterday when a big tree
crashed, bringing down the electric wires and blocking the dead end road
during a torrential downpour.

Now I'm glad to hear that she's safely headed home, and will be planting a
few boxes in NH on her way back. Maybe she'll writeup something about her
trip on our web page next week.

Cheers,

Pete
http://alum.wpi.edu/~p_miner/Letterboxes.html
