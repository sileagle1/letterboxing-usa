Friends,
As you know, Thom and I each lost a box in the Rock Creek area recently.

I didn't think to notify you earlier, but I replaced my Coon's Cache Letterbox
this last weekend. It is exactly where it was before. The new stamp is
similar to the original, but is bigger and more detailed. I have included it
(as well as the Rock Creek Dragon stamp) at the bottom of my personal
webpage...

http://members.aol.com/letterboxr/stamps.html

Please note that the original Coon's Cache stamp was never published, and only
6 people collected its imprint. Also, please note that anytime I publish my
letterbox stamp images, they have been altered in regard to size. They are
accurate representations of the stamp image, but are not to scale, so that a
copy printed off the internet could not possibly be mistaken for an original
imprint of the stamp.

Thanks for your interest,
Der Mad Stamper
