Bassackwards Box Status and New MD box

From: bassackwardsfrommd (bassackwardsfrommd@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-02 22:25:03 UTC
I was completely convinced that our Bassackwards Letterbox must have
disappeared, because we got emails from some people right away last
year when they found it, then got an email from someone who couldn't
find it, and then nothing at all. So, I carved a new, different
stamp and made a new box, all ready to go out there and replace it.
We were very pleasantly surprised to find the Bassackwards Letterbox
still there and in great shape! Yay! It was so cool to look through
the book and see all the stamps that had found their way there.

Since we had another box with us, and it had a different stamp in it,
we decided to hide it as a continuation of Bassackwards the First.
So, also at Cunningham Falls, there is now The Bassackwards Box II!
