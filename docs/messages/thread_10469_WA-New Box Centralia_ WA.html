Find Fort Borst Park.
Find The Fort Borst Lake Sign at the SW corner of the Lake.
Find A Green Building to the West.

Find The Road past the Fir tree.
Find a Square piece of concrete poking out of the embankment.
Find a rounded rock under the northern end of the concrete.
Find a Letterbox behind this rock.
