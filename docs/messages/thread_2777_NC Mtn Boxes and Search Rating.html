I like your template idea, Mischief.

I think I'll give two sets of times - tortoise and hare.

For instance, our 10k loop on Mt. Mitchell (the highest point east of the
Rockies at 6,684') takes me 5 1/2 hours to do. I am no couch potato, but I
do admit to being a 48-year old overweight lover of cheeseburgers. My
husband and his grandsons (all of them jocks) can do it in 2 1/2 hours. The
letterbox will be planted next week. No one will have to do the whole 10k
to find it.

Two more boxes will be seeded in the next two weeks - one on the Blue Ridge
Parkway at Crabtree Meadows, and one near the Joyce Kilmer Memorial Forest
in the Nantahala Mountains.
