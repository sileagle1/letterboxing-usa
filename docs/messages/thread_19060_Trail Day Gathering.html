From: letterboxer2002 (letterboxer2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-04 19:05:05 UTC
Hello All-

The Trail Day Gathering is finally upon us. There was some
confusion over who and what will be happening. Here is the low down.

All the original activities are still going to happen. Any
letterboxers helping in trail maintenance will walk the trails with
garbage bags to clean litter from the trails. Lunch will NOT be
provided as it was unclear who would and would not be available to
help. Randy Hall will be presenting his Letterboxing discussion
with copies of the famous letterboxing book. Brian (of team green
dragon) was forced to cancel due to family commitments. However, I
will be teaching a similar class on Stamp Carving 101. Anyone who
has notified me of camping will meet at 3:00 with the park director
so that she can go over park rules as well as assign our camping
area. After this meeting everyone is invited to Rosie Tomorrow's
for grub. After dinner we will return to Tarrywile for some "Things
That Go Bump In The Night" Letterboxes, campfire, and restful sleep
in our tents. I hope this answers anyones questions. I understand
there are many people spreading information and much of it contrasts
what i have written. Believe me ... It's all set! If you have any
questions ... drop me an email at Letterboxer2002@yahoo.com

Safe Letterboxing- Alan (Letterboxer2002)
