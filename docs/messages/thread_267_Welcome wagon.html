This list has been recently updated. Just wanted to run it by you
again. Let me know if you are not part of this list if you would
like to be. It goes out to new members so they can find relevant
information about letterboxing stuff, how to get started, organize
and learn/accomplish specific things like how to make a stamp, use
a compass/GPS unit, etc. It can also be fun just as a way to meet
new people with like interests. As the list grows people will have
a greater chance of meeting somebody in their neck of the woods.
