Visited TN state stamp box in Scotland CT

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-11 18:49:18 UTC-04:00
Visited the Tennessee State coin letterbox in Scotland CT at Rock Spring
Wildlife Refuge today. Got to the box just as it started raining so please
excuse me that I only stamped in and left no note. I do want everyone to
know that the box is in fine condition, put together well, a nice stamp, and
good clues. Most of all, I was reminded of the excellent view. Molly and I
have been in this Nature Conservancy area many times, even before we started
letterboxing. We always enjoyed it here. Very nice trails, and from the area
of this Tennessee state coin box is the best scenic view in this refuge.
Excellent place to place a letterbox. Good job Needeeps!.....Chuck and Molly
