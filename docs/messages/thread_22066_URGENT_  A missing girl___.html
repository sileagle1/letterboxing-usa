Pat'n Mcrack (Pat Odel) has requested I put this out on as many
groups as possible.
If you see this please forward it to any groups you are a part of.

His daughter Lilly is missing. She is 23, petite dark hair.
She was staying at her fathers in Arkansas taking care of his
livestock and had a job.
She has not picked up her last paycheck. She was in the company of
her "boyfriend"
Christopher (Chris) Ballard, he is 27 and has jail time.

except for her guitar and a photo of her

following all the leads
they have.

This is totally out of character for Lilly and Pat is very worried.

Pat would like for feelers to be put out everywhere we can reach. He
wants to hear from Lilly in case she needs help or to know she is
ok. Just a phone call would help tremendously.

If you have heard from her or know anything about her whereabouts
please contact Pat.

713-516-6935.

Please help locate Lilly.
