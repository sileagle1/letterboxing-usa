Bunny Hop Hitchhiker

Placed August 31, 2002
By Magic Mike and Magic Scout

First Placed in Wallingford, CT

OKHeres a little background

We are magicians. And as a result, our assistants are bunnies. Recently,
we lost one of our best assistants, Fido the Bunny. Fido led a full and
happy life for a bunny. He is best known for being a ham on stage and for
making people laugh and smile.

We thought it would be a great idea if we sent the rubber stamp that we used
on our correspondence as Fidos likeness on a trip. Fido would have liked
that!

So, off he goes. He was placed today in one of the many letterboxes in his
hometown of Wallingford, CT.

It is hoped that the 20th person who finds Fido will return him home. A
gift awaits that person. The address is included in his logbook.

Heres wishing Fido good luck on his journey!

Magic Mike and Magic Scout.
