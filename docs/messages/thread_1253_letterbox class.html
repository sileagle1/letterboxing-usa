Hi Tom,

You don't need formal permission to refer to www.letterboxing.org
because it is, and was intended to be, public domain. HOWEVER, please
make sure your students understand that the boxes, contents, and clues
belong to their individual owners and are NOT group property. ALSO, it
is useful to point out the disclaimer.

It is always very nice to ask about using other peoples toys, land,
boxes, etc. I am confident that your class will stress respect for the
hard work of others and the natural environment in which the boxes are
found. Will you send us a follow-up on the class? Perhaps your
syllabus?

Good to hear from you!
