notification of found boxes

From: (imdurangogirl@aol.com) | Date: 2006-03-25 15:55:03 UTC-05:00


I have a question for the letterboxing community. Recently I have received
notification that two of my boxes have been found. They're both planted in
NE Ohio. The first one, I had planted December 31st 2005, and in February
received notification that someone had located it on January 2, 2006. I figured
the person must go in periodically and update their finds, thus the 1 month
delay in notification. However, when I checked the box there was no log of a
finder. The second occurrence of this just happened this week. It was for
a box I had planted October 25, 2005. When I received notification, it
shows the box was found in July. So I knew that wasn't possible. When I checked
the box there was no stamp from the person who hand sent the notification.

Has anyone else had this occur to them? I'm wondering if these folks
updated my boxes as a find, and updated the incorrect box? Not a big issue, just
one of those things that make you say hmmmm that's strange.
