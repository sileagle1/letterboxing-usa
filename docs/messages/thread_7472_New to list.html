RE: [LbNA] New to list

From: IronBear (ironbear@phlsystems.com) | Date: 2004-05-13 11:10:59 UTC-07:00
Good morning manda



Welcome to letterboxing, I hope that it will prove to be as enjoyable to you
as it has to so many others before you. Everyone was new to letterboxing at
one time or another, and I am sure that everyone has had some assistance at
the start, so it is good to see new ones ask for help, and be shown the
ropes as you say.



I am sure that there are letterboxers in your area, and hopefully some of
them will get a hold of you so that you may be able to make new friends in
your local letterboxing community. From time to time you will notice on this
list that there will be gathering announcements for fellow letterboxers. The
locations of these gatherings vary so I encourage you to keep your eyes open
and perhaps you will be able to find one close soon.



I know that pandora and I truly love to go letterboxing with other people,
it ingrains in us the spirit of why we are out in the mighty timber to begin
with. To enjoy the world around us, to value the simple things in life,
such as friends new and old friends alike, to accept the challenge whatever
that might be, and conquer it with what skills we have. And once we have
found our prize to leave it better than we had found it to begin with.





I hope that Saturday you are able to find some of your first letterboxes. I
also hope that you will be able to find some local boxers to go out with.
In the meantime you might find it helpful to ask questions about
letterboxing in general here on this list. I am sure it will prove helpful
to you.



I bid you well, and welcome once again.


Iron`Bear
