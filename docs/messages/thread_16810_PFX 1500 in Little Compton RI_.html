PFX 1500 in Little Compton RI!

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-11-11 16:06:05 UTC-05:00
Moonstone Baby and I went out for a wonderful day of boxing in Rhode Island
on Sunday.

We started out at Mothball Fleet by Jay Drew... which was one of the first
things to make my day! I got to see AIRCRAFT CARRIERS!!!!! Thanks Moonstone
for showing them to me! GO NAVY! (can you tell that I wanted to be an F-14
Pilot when I grew up???? Too bad I was too short!) BTW... just to let you know,
the box has now been pulled for retirement. Thanks Jay for a great box!

Then we headed out to Little Compton to find boxes there! During our outing,
I found my PFX 1500 box! Woo Hoo! And what made it super special, was that
the box was at my other favorite pastime! (For those who know me well.... you
KNOW where that was..... sip sip sip..... :-)

Thanks Laurette and Moonstone Baby for making my day!

Happy Hunting!
Music Woman
