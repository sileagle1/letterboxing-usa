"Enchanted Winds"
Placed by A-Bear on 8 Apr 03
Albuquerque, New Mexico
Bernalillo County

Directions: From I-25 take Exit 233, Alameda, and
head west until you reach the Balloon Fiesta Park on
your right a few miles down Alameda.

Easy and quick!

Clues: From the northeast corner of the the parking
lot walk north alongside the 10 wooden poles with
large rocks between them to the southeast corner of a
ball field. Trapped under the larger of the two rocks,
you will refresh "Enchanted Winds."
