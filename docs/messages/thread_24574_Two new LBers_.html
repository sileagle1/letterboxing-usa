Two new LBers!

From: Angela/Xeen (angela@resirem.com) | Date: 2007-09-16 20:59:04 UTC-07:00
Hiya all. Myself (Xeen) and my roomie Whitepaws (Whitey) have just
gotten hooked to letterboxing.

Whitey went on vacation on the 7th and I discovered letterboxing quite
by accident in the late afternoon of the 11th. I told him about and he
loved the idea. About 45 or less minutes later we were standing
excitedly with our first found letterbox in hand. It was a short drive
from where we live up on some trails next to a place we've been to many
times.

We had only run up there (right before it got dark) just to see what it
was all about. It was -very- exciting! We didn't have a stamp or
journal, so later that night we went and got our supplies. This is a
nice hobby that doesn't require much more than gas, but we wanted good,
sturdy journals that would take a lot of abuse. We went out the next
day and re-found that box, did our thing, and found 2 more on the same
trails.

So yeah, we've been boxing all day, everyday since then. Whitey goes
back to work tomorrow but we're practically foaming at the mouth to get
more boxes. Its like Pokemon ;-)

Its sooo disappointing when there is a missing box, and even moreso
because it was probably poorly planted. Sometimes I just want to hunt
down the Planter and slap 'em around a bit ;-)

We picked up supplies tonight to plant 2 boxes. We've got a ton of
places in mind :)

Oh yeah... I've started a website on letterboxing. It doesn't seem that
there's a whole -lot- of personal letterboxing sites. So I'm hoping
that someone will stumble upon it. Its not really for fellow LBers so
much as I'm sure you all already know what you need to know but here ya
go: http://resirem.com/letterboxing/ it'll be updated probably quite
a bit with other tips and tricks throughout this week.

Anyway. I've rambled a lot.

Shutting up now.

-Xeen
