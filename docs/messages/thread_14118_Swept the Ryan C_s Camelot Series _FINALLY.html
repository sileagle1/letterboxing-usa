The Don and Gwen flower stamp is almost ubiquitous with letterboxing in Southern and Central California. After our 10th or 15th box, my son commented that they were signed into every one we had found and this has, for the most part, continued to be the case.

I have only been to a small handful of boxes that Don and Gwen have not been to. Those boxes were all in Oregon. I guess there were also two in California that they hadn't been to that were also way north but they were also fairly new. I was the first in one and second in the other so maybe they just hadn't made it up there yet. : )
