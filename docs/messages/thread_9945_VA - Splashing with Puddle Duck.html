VA - Splashing with Puddle Duck

From: be ma (bema57@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-12-31 16:25:06 UTC


Team BeMa (at least the young and spry members -- Dad had to work...) got up
early to splash in the dawn dew with Puddle Duck (when you have little kids
they get up REALLLLLY early even if it is school vacation and, somehow,
especially if it is a weekend!...).

We got there before the library opened and were actually very excited to see
other cars there thinking they might be more boxers to meet (having overcome
our initial shyness we are now like grandma BeMa who talks to EVERYONE
[grocer, dry cleaner, person next in line...]) Unfortunately and
fortunately, they were just dog walkers and assorted joggers.
Unfortunately, because we are psyched to get more exchanges and fortunately
because we were able to be the first ones to log into Puddle Duck.

We had good luck with the clues (we tried to read them very carefully this
time and put up our "hand-L" to verify right and left and found the box all
snuggled in with its head under its wing or at least its Tupperware under a
nice pile of leaves, twigs and bark.

We took the opportunity to run over to the box office of the Alden Theater
at the community center next door. Even though it was closed (doesn't
everyone keep the same hours as Target, 7-11 and us?) we were able to get
the latest listing of shows. They have some great and very inexpensive
programs (good for younger kids because they are short yet the theater looks
like a real theater so they can learn proper "show behavior"....)

Now it's back to writing up the clues for our box which is already planted
and waiting to be discovered!

Thanks VA Waterbird! BeMa P4F28X2
