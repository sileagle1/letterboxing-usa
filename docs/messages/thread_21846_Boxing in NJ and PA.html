This weekend I had the opportunity to go down to visit Dave in NJ. We were able to visit the following boxes:
In the Pine Barrens:
Goth Sun Moon
Batona Trail Surprise
At the Palmyra Cove Nature Center:
Snail Mail
Palmyra What Exit
The Palmyra Cave Nature Center Series - Peregrine Falcon
- Red Fox

Over in PA at the Armentrout Preserve:
B&E' First Box
Jumpin' Logs

We also picked up a hitch hiker along the way - Grace's Hitchhiker. This HH was planted back on July 6th 2002 in honor of the planter's daughter Grace (born July 4, 2001)

All boxes were in fine shape. Our first Pennsylvania boxes brought us up and over the 300 mark for finds!
