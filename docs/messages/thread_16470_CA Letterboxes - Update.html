CA Letterboxes - Update

From: Pete (jiggs111@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-10-20 07:42:51 UTC
Hi all!

The Jiggs family took advantage of the gorgeous fall weather here in
northern CA to pick up a few "out of the way" boxes!

IDE ADOBE box in Red Bluff (lots of visitors!) and

HOG LAKE PLATEAU - found on our second attempt - the kids and I were
here a few weeks ago, without success. Brought Mrs Jiggs along, and
she promptly found the box (again, lots of visitors, for the "middle
of nowhere!!) within 20 minutes!

BRANDY CREEK - first finders, after an "easy" straight-up the side of
a mountain climb! Any of you North Coasters - this one's well worth
the trip!

I had all the bad luck on this trip - twisted ankle and badly barked
shins in the lava beds, then twisted the same ankle onb the Brandy
Creek trail, before slipping down an incline at the falls and
wrenching my shoulder and scraping my elbow!

Gosh, what a great pastime we have!!

Pete aka
