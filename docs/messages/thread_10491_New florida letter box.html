I have placed a new letterbox as follows:

Name: "Bitter Orange Cracker Shack"

State: Florida

County: Hillsborough

Nearby city; Tampa

Date placed: Jan. 25, 2003

My name: Craig Starns

Clues:

1 Enter the Flatwoods park off of Morris Bridge rd. east outside of
Tampa and NE of the main USF campus. The park is part of a large
Hillsborough county complex there.

2 Once inside the park drive to "The Y" and park.

3 Facing "The Y" (or WNW) bear right and follow the pavement.

4 At "26 Main" bear left off of the pavement onto the trail and follow
it.

5 At the intersection of the sand road and "27 main" immediately bear
left (west) down the old overgrown (not the sand) road.

6 When you get to the large (3' diameter) OaK on your left with the knob
at the base find the bitter orange tree nearby to the SSW.

7 Sight 316 degrees from the Bitter Orange tree trunk and walk 18 paces.

8 Sight 215 degrees from the palm tree trunk at the edge you see.

9 Walk to the edge and look behind it.
