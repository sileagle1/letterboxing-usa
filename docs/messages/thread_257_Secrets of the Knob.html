I just wanted to let everyone know that Rae Record has been working very
hard
on the above page and I think it has turned out very nice. Please have
a look
if you have not done so already.

Its a Massachusetts letterbox with a story theme. It is unlike anything
we
have seen to date. To find the letterbox you read the story about the
couple
who is visiting there. It IS a secret -- at least it was not
intuitively obvious
to me how to work the clues. (Mitch had to explain it to me.)

But I think it will be fun and I hope there will be more letterboxes
like
this in the future. I will be revising the Massachusetts map probably
in the next couple of days.
