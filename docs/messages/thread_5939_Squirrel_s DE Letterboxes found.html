The Itchy Butt Girls and family found Assawoman Tower and Ducky at
Assawoman letterboxes on Sunday. These letterboxes were planted
11/2000 by Squirrel. We were the first to find them! The clues were
excellent and the boxes are in good shape. They were in a gorgeous
location. From the tower we saw a bald eagle caughting his lunch.
Great day to be outside.

We also found Prince of Power in Pocomoke SP (Eastern Shore of
Maryland). The box was in very good condition. The box was placed
10/2000 and we were the third stamp. We will be going back to this
park with our kayaks to enjoy the water!


-Itchy Butt Girls
