Transient Letterboxing Nebraska rehidden in Nebraska

From: Scott Redd (redd@interbug.com) | Date: 2002-11-24 01:35:24 UTC
The letterbox known as "Letterboxing Nebraska", created by me, but
formerly placed in Oregon by the world-famous Funhog, has finally been
replaced in Nebraska where other Nebraska letterboxers can view all
the wonderful images stamped into the logbook by the Oregon letterboxers.

In order to find Letterboxing Nebraska, you must first visit (or
revisit) the Inferno letterbox. The clues exist only in the Inferno
logbook.

I can also report that I checked up on Inferno today, and it is doing
well. I tried to find the Troll in Ralston, Nebraska, placed by
Erich, but it seems to be gone, as initially reported by Silent Doug.
A couple of Omaha area geocachers have also reported finding a couple
of our letterboxes.

You can check out the Nebraska letterboxing scene, as well as get
access to clues to all the (known) Nebraska letterboxes at our web site:

http://interbug.com/letterboxing
