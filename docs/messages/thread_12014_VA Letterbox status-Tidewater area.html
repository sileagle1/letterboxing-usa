This past Monday myself and 3 of the six SEAL pups
checked the Mariners Museum Park in Newport News VA.
Cardweaver has four boxes in this park and they are
well worth the trip. The pups handled the 5 mile hike
like real troopers. The four boxes were in excellent
condition and in place. Except for box #2. The
container had some cracks in the bottom. Thanks to
second bagging everthing was dry, but maybe a new
container is called for. The stamps were great and the
history lesson of the pups even better. We finished
our day with a picnic at the museum. A
