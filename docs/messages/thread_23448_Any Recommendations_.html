I've only done a dozen or so in and around Los Angeles but I am really glad
I did "To the Batcave!" According to Letterboxer Q, it is "the actual
location where Batman and Robin sped out of the Batcave in their Batmobile". It is a
very short (maybe 5 minutes) walk along a closed road.

If you go, bring a camera. You will be rewarded with what I think is the
best view of the Hollywood sign there is.
