"IT'S GOOD FOR A DOG, WALK" LETTERBOX planted by catbead and fly doc

Difficulty rating: Moderate, not suitable for families with small
children. There are some difficult rocky areas.
It took us about 1 1/2 - 2 hours, we were not rushing, and were
familiar with the area.
The park has a carry in - carry out policy with garbage bags
available at the ticket booth. We picked up litter along the way when
planting this box and carried it out. Always a good idea on an outing.

Clark Reservation State Park
605 E. Seneca Turnpike
Jamesville, NY 13078-9516
#315-492-1590
Onondaga County

This is a 300 acre state park which includes a Meromictic lake. There
is a $6.00 fee to enter or use your Empire Pass. Dogs on a 6' leash
are allowed. Clark Reservation features 5 million year old exposed
bedrock, beautiful views, picnic areas, fishing, hiking, and is open
all year. **

Syracuse is centrally located in New York State, at the cross roads of
New York Thruway 90 (east & west) and Interstate 81 (north & south).
