Re: hitch hikers?

From: lb_isosceles (lb_isosceles@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-03-16 13:20:11 UTC
A good place to post about hitchhikers is:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox_travellers/

Opinions on posting about hitchhikers vary, but by posting there, you
can be more assured that you will not offend anyone, since most
members want to hear about them!

You may also want to check out Wanda and Pete's "Hitcher's Progress"
page:

http://alum.wpi.edu/~p_miner/travelers

A huge number of these little guys are tracked there.

Also a good rule of thumb is to only post about where it has already
been, and to not post about where you left it, potentially spoiling
the suprise.

-Isosceles
