Introduction: Valley Falls Park is in Vernon, CT. It has hiking and
railtrails. There is some logging going on the first part of trail but
view at the top is worth it. Round trip is only about 1 hour. Walking
is easy except for a few steep parts.

Directions: I 84S to exit 66, turn right onto Bolton RD, follow for 1
1/2 miles. Turn right onto Valley Falls RD, park is just on left.
