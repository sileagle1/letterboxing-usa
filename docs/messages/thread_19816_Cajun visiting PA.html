We live near route 309 in Quakertown and also not too far from the Quakertown exit of Rt. 476. Depending on the schedule, we might be able to exchange. Can be pretty muddy near the LB though. I guess the Lehigh boxes in Allentown would be possibilities for you too, but you'd need a little more time for them, maybe an hour or more. I think Lightnin' Bug said one is missing or vandalized? I forget. I've only searched for & found one of them-- the fish one. We may also be willing to look for them with you-- my kids weren't along that time either & could find them as well (they're older & won't slow anyone down; in fact they're usually out in front.) From: Lightnin Bug (rpboehme@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-09 19:40:17 UTC
For a quick list, go to the LBNA database and look under Eastern PA,
looking under Lehigh County, which straddles I-476. There is an exit
for Quakertown, about 15 miles south of Allentown and for Allentown.

I can give you approximate driving distances from the Lehigh Valley
Interchange:

Trexler Park - 2 miles
Rodale Fitness Park - 7 miles
Pass Set Spike - 3 miles
Allentown Rose Garden - 3 miles
Pool Wildlife Sanctuary - 5 miles
419 Scam - Mystery!!
Fish in Outer Space - 5 miles (not mine, though)

2 Lehigh County boxes are missing - Lock Ridge and Just Flying
Through.

Further up the Pike, you can take the Pocono Exit (I-80) to find the
two in Hickory Run park, though those WILL be a challenge compared to
my easy bags in Allentown.

Happy Hunting,

Lightnin' Bug of Allentown, PA
