Kentucky Sweetbird and our first day out

From: Celtic Relics (celticrelics@commandnet.net) | Date: 2002-11-11 21:11:21 UTC-06:00
We had a great first day out. There aren't many boxes in our area but we
got the Kentucky Sweetbird and four out of six geocaches.

For Deanne and Dave - The instructions were accurate until a week ago. They
have chopped down the tree and about half of the rockwall is gone. The box
is still there and in great shape. Email me at celticrelics@commandnet.net
and I can send you a photo of how it looks now so you can update the
directions.

We got there as the Chamber of Commerce was closing and Kathy was in the
parking lot getting ready to leave. She chatted with us for a while about
letterboxing and was as nice as you described. She seemed excited that
someone was out looking for the box.

We still haven't been able to come up with a team name. The only thing out
of the ordinary that happened today was how many times I fell and scraped my
hands. I don't want to be known as the clumsiest team in the south, so we
will keep searching for the perfect name.

For now we are
John, Helen, and Ian
