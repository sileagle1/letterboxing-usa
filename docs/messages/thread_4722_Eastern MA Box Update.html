From: REBECCA LAY (rlay@email.smith.edu) | Date: 2002-03-18 20:10:20 UTC-05:00
Hi Everyone--
Back from a wonderful little vacation on the Cape this weekend...JLR and
I managed to find quite a few boxes while we were there! We found: the
Nauset Light, Three Sisters Lighthouses, Highland (Cape Cod) Light,
Chatham Light, Reunion, and the Race Point Light. All were in good
condition. The Race Point lighthouse was quite a trek! But a beautiful
walk on the beach, and it sure wasn't crowded, being March and below 40
degrees! Be sure and make time for this one if you make it out to the
Cape anytime soon. It took us a little over two hours, but was
definitely worth it! On the way back, we also managed to swing by the
Birdhouse letterbox in Needham. Boy was this one a kick! Another one
to make time for if you're in the area.

The plan was to stop in Leominster on the way home (route 2 is soooo
much nicer than the Masspike!) but it was a bit too cold and snowy for
letterboxing. Next time!
