From: Brian, Ryan & Lori (bconnoll@marksonrosenthal.com) | Date: 2003-08-21 13:08:08 UTC
TeamGreenDragon recently broke the F500 barrier and to celebrate I
carved us a new Sig stamp . I just could not wait to get it into
action , so I headed for the Blue Hills . After checking on some of
our boxes there , ( where I learned that some people consider me to
be an " Evil Evil man " ) I went in search of " My Love " . Since we
had already found the " Blue Hills " box that this was replacing ,
this was fairly easy . Also I lightened my load a little while
there . After this I headed for one of my favorite trails in the
hills to search for The WindupHiker's elusive " Forest Hoops " box .
After some head scratching and fumbling around awhile I manage to
locate this one also . Both boxes are in great shape with some
interesting entries , I think concerning the upcoming King Richards
Fair. It will be interesting to check into this when we attend . All
in all , I had a great day off from work . We plan to join up with
Solar and Blinx this weekend to do battle with some pirates that have
been stirring up trouble in Hingham harbor . And probably will make a
trip to the end of the world .

Thanks All !!
