From: Randy Hall (randy@mapsurfer.com) | Date: 2008-11-30 15:32:16 UTC-05:00

Over the years I've thrown out various yes/no and other
question tokens -- either about specific boxes or generic
tokens -- either on this list or in boxes. Some of these
have been used, but most are just taking up space.

To facilitate new games, any such tokens out there with
no expiry date expire 12/31/08. Any such tokens related
to the CCC expire 12/31/09. The forgoing only applies
to tokens created with no expiry date. Now all game
tokens have an expiry date.

As for the other trivia, anyone who is interested in my
boxes and wants a BIG hint should be first finder of
Sheba'a latest wonderful carving.

Thanks
Randy
