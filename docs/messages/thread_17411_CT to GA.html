CT to GA

From: J. BRIDGMAN (jmbridgman@snet.net) | Date: 2004-01-01 20:01:53 UTC-08:00
Back from a road trip from CT to GA for a relative's
wedding. We managed to find 13 boxes on our travels.
Had a wonderful time and introduced to hobby to my
nephew, who helped us solve the GA National Mystery
Letterbox. Liked the Interstate stops with boxes.
Missed hunting for some near Richmond because of
missing a turn-off and it was getting too dark to be
fun. We did find Fredricksberg Trainstation (VA)
after dark. But we couldn't find Concord Lighthouse
box (MD) even looking under most every rock we could
move at the base of the tree. But we had a great
time!
