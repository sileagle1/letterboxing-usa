CT: National Trails Day Gathering

From: letterboxer2002 (letterboxer2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-05-17 14:41:46 UTC
It's that time of year again! The National Trails Day Gathering is
June 4th from 9-3 at Tarrywile Park in Danbury, CT. Eastern Mountain
Sports (Eric 'The Ram') will be hosting Trail Day events all over the
park including a Letterboxing area with information on letterboxing,
stamp carving classes and a letterboxing hike for a limited edition
letterbox. The Great American Stamp Store of Westport will supply
carving tools, pink stuff, and ink pads for sale at the event.

Please RSVP on Atlasquest at:
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=47

It should be a wonderful time and perfect day for a Letterboxing
Gathering. Bring your gear and a snack to share ... I'll bring The
Munchkins ;) There will NOT be camping this year but any interesting
ideas for fun events are welcome. Drop me a line off list.

Thanx and Safe Letterboxing-
