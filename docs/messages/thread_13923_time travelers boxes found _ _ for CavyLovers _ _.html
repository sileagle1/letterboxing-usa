From: cavy_lovers4 (adelcoll@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-02 00:45:50 UTC
Oh dear, I feel like we have been found out! ;-)
I confess we're very inefficient letterboxes. Especially
when it
comes to local parks like the Blue Hills then we are often one
box
a day people! We don't do them in order, either. [Good thing
there
are no rules ]

Yes, we have had the clues for E=MC2 for awhile. We had fun doing
our homework so we could interpret the clues! Now that we've been
nudged, maybe we'll try for it this week.

We do love the Blue Hills and are lucky to have it close by! Last
Sunday we stopped in to do a hike before going out to brunch. We
FINALLY found the Hancock Hills letterbox, thanks to a hint from
another boxer. Even then, we did check out a couple of extra
"Lefts"
to be sure we didn't miss it. But what a sweet find!

Of the Time Travelers series, we have found so far Weena, Fiendish
Things, Back to the Lab and Mr. Peabody, but not necessarily in that
order. We plan to meander back to the rest and other new boxes
placed in the Hills recently.

Is there a prize for taking the longest to get to 100 boxes? We are
contenders!

Happy trails!
Cavy Lovers
P13 F39 X5

P.S. We hear someone is developing a GTPS (Global Tupperware
Positioning System) which will locate all Tupperware containers
within a 10 acre radius. Look for it in your local stores soon!
