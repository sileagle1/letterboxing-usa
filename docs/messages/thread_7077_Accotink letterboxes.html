Thanks for the report. I got the double sided foam tape (Super Stick)  from a stamp site on the internet (Pigs Can Fly) and after 4-6 months, and at least 10 stamps, discovered that whatever is used in the glue it does not like the Speedball red stamp material. Hence the reaction you saw. The surface never sets and if the stamp is at an angle, will slide off the wooden back. The stuff works great on regular rubber used in stamps.  I have also used 3M paper thin, Dbl sided tape it works great. Not sure why. If you come across any more of my stamps like that just gently slide it back in place.
