From: sponghed (egda3432@netzero.net) | Date: 2003-03-31 13:25:08 UTC
Well it's been too long since I last posted here. But a long winter
kept us away.
On 3/16 a partial ESAK crew, with a few additions, headed out to
Cedar Hill Cemetery and found three out of the five boxes hidden
there. We knew going in that two were missing but that didn't stop us
from following the clues to their exact locations anyway. Sam Colt
and JP Morgan Letterboxes at Cedar Hill Cemetery have gone missing as
the clues stated. All other boxes are in fine shape.

But on a good note, we found the Julliette Hitchhiker and will be
back in the field soon.

On 3/29, Josina and I spent the day riding our mountain bikes from
Vernon all the way to the East Hop River Rail Trail letterbox. What a
great ride that was. Not for the casual biker I would say, especially
from Vernon. My guess is its close to a 30 mile round trip if the
East Hop River box is your target.

We picked up the Rockville Spur box which was right out in the open
(we replaced it at the spot intended) and then headed back out to bag
the Valley Falls Rail to Trail box along with the West Hop River Rail
to Trail box. All 4 boxes are in fine shape.

Time was short so we didn't pick up the three boxes at Valley Falls
but we'll be back for sure to get those.

Thank you all for the great box locations.
