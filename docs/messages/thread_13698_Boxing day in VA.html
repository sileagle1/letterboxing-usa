From: Raygan (dragonpearl@rcn.com) | Date: 2003-06-20 19:21:30 UTC-04:00
What a great day I had. first I introduced my friend midge to boxing and
then we went and found a bunch of boxes. fist we went to Northside Park to
find one of Spike's boxes. After circling the car two times and fending off
biting flies, we finally found the box.

Next was Kermit's Cousins. Easy find.

Then we went to Lakewood park for another of Spike's boxes. Midge was able
to find this one, I was way off the path.

Then we sung by St Paul's Cannonball and it is Still there! Wooo.

I dropped Midge off, hid a box (Elusive Red Wolf, clues coming soon) and
picked up the Wacky box at the Library.

All in all, a good day.
