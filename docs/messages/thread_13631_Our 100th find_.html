Our 100th find!

From: Steve (shol3039@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-17 18:45:30 UTC
I went out with the Drewclan, Butterfly, and Patrick with Ruth
(minus Amy), and we placed a few more boxes along a not-yet-released
series in Waterford, CT. Over the course of various suprises and
plants by the others, I managed to get # 100. Unfortunatley Heidi
was not able to make it out with us, but she was not to disappointed
once I showed her some of the beautiful creations of Mr. Drew that
just happened to find their way in the log!

We're just shy of 8 months into letterboxing, with our first stamps
dating back to Kids Day on October 20th, 2002. Glad we got into
the hobby!

By the way... in the neatest of happenings, the 100th box is
called "Friends". Carved by Butterfly. What better company to find
100 with than a group of friends, at the box called friends, in the
company of such legends as Jay and B'Fly....not to mention Patrick,
who got me hooked (and for the record, Patrick and Amy are in the
400's for finds...)!!!!

Thanks to all, it was a great morning!!!!

-Steve (with Heidi and Madras)
