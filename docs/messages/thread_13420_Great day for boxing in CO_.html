Great day for boxing in CO!

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2003-06-08 04:06:50 UTC
Today, Eric and I hiked up the Dakota Ridge Trail in search of
Funhog's "Rain Cloud Box". What a beautiful trail! It's rocky, with
some good inclines, but we encountered lots of serious mountain
bikers making a day of it, too. Great views all around.

We missed the box on the first pass, and found ourselves at another
great place for a plant! So, we sat down, carved a stamp, and made
this a two-box trail! Clues for this box will be up in a day or so.
On the way back, we resigned ourselves to the fact that we'd just
come back on another day and try for the Rain Cloud again, but we
found the landmark we'd missed earlier, and were rewarded with the
box!

Eric also came up with a great idea for a hitchhiker, so that will be
coming soon to a box near you!

Best wishes,
Autumn
