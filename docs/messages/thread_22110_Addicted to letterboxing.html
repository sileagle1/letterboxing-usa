My first time

Uhum..uhum.

My name is Heartland
..and er....and er I am a..er
A Letterboxer.

{pause for a chorus of Welcome Heartland}

It started just by chance you see!

{I catch a few soft baleful murmured whispers of we
know, we know}

I was working out of Boston and had to take a work
trip of two weeks up in Vermont and so I drove up and
stayed in a really nice little motel in such a lovely
town....

{uhumph}

Oh yes... well the weekend I was sorta at a lose end
and went for a stroll in a local park and saw two guys
and a woman and two kids taking something out from
behind a tree.

They looked kinda OK and they were obviously very
excited by their find, so I asked em what were they
doing.

They were kinda cagey and instead gave me a little
card with this LBNA web site URL.

{I am sure someone muttered Oh no Oh no Oh no}

I punched it into my lap top back at the motel and
found out about Letterboxing. It sounded sorta neat.

So when I was driving back to Boston I took a short
detour and followed these clues and I FOUND ME A
LETTERBOX

My FIRST Box.

You know how that feels???.

{Yeah we know. Go on}

I stamped up on the back of my motel bill and wrote a
howdy in the book and put the box back and hid it
and well it was way cool

I was a Letterboxer

Well actually I was a NEWBIE and ...er hooked.

So that night I fired off about 18 emails to the LBNA
main site asking for help.

{Groan}

I got this nice off-list email from a guy called Ryan
who asked me to post to the newbie site (whatever that
meant.)

So Monday in Boston in my lunchbreak I got me a blank
notebook and a store bought stamp as my trail stamp.

{more groans}

So that next weekend I went out hunting me Boston
Boxes !

I found three !

I actually found five, but two had odd things in. One
had some gum wrappers and a plastic frog and the other
some very old damp cookies and a Clinton campaign pin!

{some laughter}

I stamped a page from my book and put them in each.

{more laughter}

When I got home I went back in to the LBNA site to
tell folk about my great day and to write a long
SITREP only to find that I had committed two very
serious crimes ..I had used a store bought stamp and
had been Geocaching!

{There was a loud hissing noise and the controller
smiled at me and said We dont use THAT word in here
my dear!....but do go on; do go on }

The next Monday I was back at the art store asking for
Xacto and pink stuff..which they did sell me over the
counter in clear sight of regular customers, but yes
it was in a plain brown bag.

{Shame}

Well it just went on from there ... Ive been to group
meets and discuss the various types of adhesives and
blade types and ink and ....hitchikers

{an sharp intake of breath}

and Postal Boxes

{lets keep it clean! someone yelled}

and X counts and V counts and H and H+ counts and then
I started in with some..er some ..

WOM letterboxers.

{Loud gasps}

Im out every weekend, and in the evenings Im locked
in my room carving, I have Hitchiker records; I use
AtlasQuest and other even more unsavoury sites, even
one where a Sewer Rat runs it !

{Go for it someone called out}

I do cryptics and even carry letterboxes overseas in
my hand luggage..I even do cheeseracing..and have
actually seen The Maplines from a distance...from a
distance!

{universal Gasps!}

Is there any way out ?

Is there any hope?

An addict.
