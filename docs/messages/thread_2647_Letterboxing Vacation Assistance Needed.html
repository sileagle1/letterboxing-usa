Hi, Wingfoot! Oh, DO come to CT for the letterboxing! The area around
KJ's Best (Storrs/Mansfield Center) is just beautiful. You can easily
find cheap lodgings along US84 nearby: just use Yahoo or call your
favorite chain. Carol and crew (and many others) have been busy
there. There are lots of pretty boxes, and the hiking is perfect for
daytripping.

And do come down to the coast as Eric and Keith (thanks for the
compliment!) have mentioned. The New England shore is fabulous. Right
next door RI is also home to a bunch of sweet letterboxes. There are
probably 200 letterboxes within an hour of the ice cream at KJ's.

As Eric mentions, there are also "unpublished" letterbox clues at EMS
(and they really aren't hucking for business, but rather a real
resource that launched letterboxing in the area). My favorite of
those special letterboxes is "The Science of Letterboxing." Our next
series, half done so far, is one that that Eric graciously said we
post exclusively at EMS (hint, hint...).

Drop us a note off-list (drewclan at aol.com) if you'd like, but I'm
sure lots of our local friends will be answering your post with their
favorites.

Have a great time!!!

Jay in letterboxing heaven
