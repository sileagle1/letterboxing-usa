We want to thank Buzzard & Stamp Luis Obispo for sponsoring us.

We have about 19 boxes ready to hide at the site. Four specially
carved presidential stamps will be there from Just Beth (great work,
you will want to collect these!) Hitchhikers. A special event stamp
carved by Emily Rose. Other traveling stamps. Stamp Luis Obispo has a
stamp at their store, which will only be available on Saturday,
Sunday and Monday of this weekend.

We have at least 80 hot dogs (chicken based), catsup, mustard,
relish, buns, paper plates ready for you. There are drink machines in
the park. We will have two grills going just in case anyone brings
cheese. Food and exchanges before anything else (11-12:30) If you
wish to bring some food to share, please do.

We would like to do a group photo about 12:30. We will have the
drawing for prizes right after that. Then will pass out the clues to
the boxes hidden in the park. The order of the boxes on the sheets
will be randomized to ensure more even hunting.

JugglerMouse is releasing her latest Redwall stamp, Rollo the Bank
Vole. I am releasing three new in the Middle Earth series: Gollum's
Pool, Shadowfax, and Mirkwood. We have a new series starting:
Loteria.

Beer and liquor are not allowed at the park. Sorry. We could not
afford the insurance the park demanded.

Dogs are welcome on leash. Strollers, wheelchairs, and folks with
electric vehicles can easily navigate the park. Most boxes will be
close by the paths. Suggest you bring sunscreen and layered clothing.
If the fog comes in, it will be coolish.

Bring your compasses. JugglerMouse and I love to give compass
headings. All paces are single paces.

http://webpages.charter.net/astroweaver/letterboxing/meadow.html

I am sure I have forgotten something. If you are not on the list and
want to come, please do.

Write me for my cell phone number if you wish that link.

Tired and still carving,
Sir Balthazar (with Ash Raven and JugglerMouse)
'cause we are Googled and Feeling Lucky
