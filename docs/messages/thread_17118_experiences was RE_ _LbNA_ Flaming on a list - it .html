This is a great idea.

My favorite boxes are always the Russ Clan boxes. The newspaper down here (I
believe, it was a while ago)wrote of them and that's how we were introduced
to letterboxing. They are very inventive and talented people. Their stamps
are excellent, their hiding places ingenious. They have taken us on tours of
places we never even knew existed! They also include goodies for the kids in
most boxes. If you ever make it down to southern VA you should definitely
try their boxes. The spot Green Tea is in just made us ohh and ahh in wonder
at it's beauty, and astounded us that we had never been there before!
