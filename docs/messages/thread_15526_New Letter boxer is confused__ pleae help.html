Announcing your clues on this list won't get them listed on the site-- not
since earlier this year when the site was revamped.

Now you can enter your clues yourself on the site. Go to
http://www.letterboxing.org/MemberServices.asp and click "Sign up." Enter
your email address, choose a password and type it again to confirm it, then
enter your "trail name" in the space where it says "first name." You'll get
a confirmation email; click the link to activate your membership. Then
you'll be able to submit your clues to the site.
