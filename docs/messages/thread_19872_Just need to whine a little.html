From: Lisa (LisMax1128@aol.com) | Date: 2004-08-12 03:00:16 UTC
Hey Gang,

It has been too long since the family and I have really gotten out
there and did some serious boxing. We have hit a few drive by boxes
here and there, so I'm not going into major withdrawls... yet, but I
need to get out there.

I've carved 3 stamps and a HH and have the boxes in the car ready to
go at a moments notice, but we just can't seem to get to the parks,
ect. to plant them.

Anyone else have this happen where life just won't let you spend some
serious time letterboxing? All I can say is thank goodness for the
talk lists or I'd really be going crazy. Maybe someone should create
a 12 step program for letterboxers.

Twitching my way into serious withdrawl,
Lisa
The Family from Mass

PS: 12 steppers: 12 step comment was not meant to offend, but to
compliment strength of programs.
