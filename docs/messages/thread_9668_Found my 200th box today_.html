Found my 200th box today!

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2002-12-14 19:46:10 UTC-05:00
While out at Merrow Park in Mansfield, CT, I found my 200th box at the Merrow Park Letterbox!  Alan (Letterboxer2002) came prepared for the celebration by bringing two bottles of the best Root Beer to pop open at the box site!  I don't remember what brand it was off the top of my head, but the bottle is in the car....  All I know, it was awesome!

Another great treat of the day, was meeting up with Chuck, Molly and Chickabirdie at the Coney Rock Preserve to exchange and hand over to Chuck the Purple Heart stamp for getting injured in the line of letterboxing!  We (Alan and I) were excited when Chickabirdie handed us a couple of packages of Christmas cookies (They're the ones that look like snowflakes... I think they're italian ?????) and two hitchhiker exchanges!

After our lovely chat with the Straub family, we headed out to find the Singing Men, and I'm happy to report that we were victorious!!!  It was actually not very hard, once we found the correct parking lot :-)  So all in all, we found the following boxes in fairly good order.  If not in good order, I will state this.

Mother Daughter: GREAT STAMP AND COLOR!
The Princess Diaries Hitchhiker
The Duffer Hitchhiker
THe Singing Men
Barrows Pond
Squirrel's Treasure
The Three Letterboxes in the Pink Panther Series
The Three Wizards Letterbox Series
Merrow Pond (needs a new ziploc... we were unfortunately all out :-(    )
Dendrology 101  (needs a new ziploc....we were unfortunately still all out :-(  )
Totem Pole
John Storrs

Thanks Alan for a great day!

Music Woman
