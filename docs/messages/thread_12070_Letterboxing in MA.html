Letterboxing in MA

From: (ctferret@aol.com) | Date: 2003-04-18 09:55:49 UTC-04:00
We had a great few days in MA with some letterboxing in Gloucester. We found
the The Castle of Love LB in excellent condition. We also found Young's
Great Rock/Wigwam Point Light. This box took three tries and a trip to the
Gloucester Library at which point we realized we had been right by it the
first two times. Next was Kokopelli's Flute LB in Lynn. Found in good
shape. We then went on to find the Marblehead Rail Trail LB, which was
slightly damp, we dried it off and put it back. Everything was bagged so the
stamp and log book were not damaged. With the Marblehead Rail LB was the
Larry the Lobster HH. Next was Saugus and the Breakhart Reservation LB, it
was a beautiful walk. Finally we stopped in Lincoln on the way home and did
the Two Hearts LB. We had never been do DeCOrdova Sculpture Park, what a
wonderful place. I'm glad we stopped there on the way home.

Thanks to all the people who planted these boxes. We had a great time
finding them.
Christi
Wethersfield, CT
