OLD MAN RUFUS

Patapsco Valley State Park McKeldin Area
Marriotsville, MD
Carroll County
Placed September 24, 2002
By Psychomommy
Easy and short


From the Liberty Dam Overlook take the Switchback trail (white
blazes). Cross the creek. As you continue on the trail the rocky path
leads you to the lovely rock garden. At the top of the hill is a V
tree. Look at this tree with the trail in front, take a 45 degree
reading. There is the lovely rock garden. There you will find a tree
with a narly foot. Watch out for it's toenails.
