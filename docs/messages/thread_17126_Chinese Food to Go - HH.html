This HH has traveled far and almost full circle. Started September
2002 in Paris, it quickly flew to CT (Mecca to all well traveled
HH's), then a jaunt to lovely GA and now to my abode. It still has
it's original traveling baggie!
Princess Turtle and I were bummed when we went to Paris and
discovered we had just missed finding this little treasure. We had
just met Sarah of AppledumplingGang and marveled over her carvings.
So it was a "must find" to collect this carving. Now we are happy to
receive it after it's travels. First Find is good, but Last Find is
best. I love to read what everyone has written.
I will have to make this a special carry-out delivery.
"Be ready in 15 minute"
