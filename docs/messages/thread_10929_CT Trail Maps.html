From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2004-04-27 11:33:47 UTC
Just a quick note--
We were poking around on the CT DEP webpage the other day and
found that many of the trail maps for the state parks and forests are
being revised and/or updated. Many are now in nice colored versions
with hiking distances, etc noted. You may want to pick one up for
areas such as Talcott Mtn, Bigelow Hollow, Sleeping Giant, etc.

To access these try www.dep.state.ct.us then find
the "Recreation" link. Clicking on the "Outdoor rec" area brings you
into state parks / forests. Its a pretty useful tool!

-Steve, Heidi and Madras
