Mary and I got a chance to check on these boxes today.  What a beautiful day to hike!   If I do say so myself, the 3 hour circut is one of the better ones in the area.......  beautiful view points, 2 lakes, mountain laurel in bloom and 3 letterboxes........ what more could you ask for?  :)
