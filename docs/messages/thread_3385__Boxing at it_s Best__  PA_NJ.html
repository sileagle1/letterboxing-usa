'Boxing at it's Best!! PA/NJ

From: joie christensen (see_joy29@hotmail.com) | Date: 2001-09-29 20:17:38 UTC-04:00
Had a wonderful day with my best boxing friend "Floating Feather". We met at
Merrill Creek in Northwestern New Jersey and checked in on the letterbox we
placed there in July of this year.It's had one visitor and that lucky pair
picked up the hitchiker that was resting there and carried it along it's
way.A new "prize" was left in the place of the, "first visitor", bonus
prize.
We were happy to see it safe and sound. While hiking we planted another box
with it's own "treasure", and we both totally enjoyed the differing veiws
and woodland settings.
It's an outstanding place! I saw my first pair of Bald Eagles!!! This is
their third year at Merrill Creek,(who would think that they would choose to
live in N.J.? so glad they did!!) What a sight for a sore heart...a
remakable one for sure.
Watch for the clues for the letterbox we planted today to follow.
'Box On!! Live well.
See Joy
