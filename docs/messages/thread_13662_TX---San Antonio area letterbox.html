THIS IS A REST AREA LETTERBOX

DIRECTIONS::::

-----IH 10 EAST
-----EXIT 590 at REST AREA....
-----PARK AT FAR END BY THE LAST PICNIC TABLE
-----you will see a BUSH BY THE PICNIC TABLE AREA.
-----LOOK PAST THAT TO SMALL TREE--A CREPE MYTRLE BUSH ???
-----go to crepe myrtle bush and stand on its right side facing EAST....
-----BEGIN YOUR PACES (regular strolling size steps) APPROXIMATELY 105 PACES..
-----YOU WILL PASS UP A LIGHT POST ON ITS RIGHT SIDE...
-----YOU WILL REACH THE PAMPAS PLANT
-----STOP----TURN RIGHT APPROXIMATELY 5 PACES
-----STOP----TURN LEFT APPROXIMATELY 9 PACES
------STOP----TURN LEFT APPROXIMATELY 4 STEPS

-----POKE YOUR STICK IN
-----POKE YOUR STICK OUT
-----POKE YOUR STICK IN
-----AND POKE IT ALL ABOUT

----THATS HOW YOU DO THE HOKEY POKEY!!!!!

it is a small sandwich size clear plastic with white lid container....

please be sure to stick it back under the ""leaves""" well and fluff up the ""leaves"" to hide well.....they were alittle tough today :)
