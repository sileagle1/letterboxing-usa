Hi Deb,
I'll field this one for Autumn just so you have a timely response to

the postal lbox. A note also implores the finder to please post that

could quickly request to be added to the address list, but that would
probably be at the goodwill of the finder. Some folks might just want
to send it along and not fuss with that, others may gladly add new
addresses to the list.

Hey Debean,
Yep - the list of names/addresses went INSIDE the box. So the only
way to get your name on the list now, is to determine WHO has the
box, and get on the list before it gets mailed away again.

I guess the easiest thing to do would be to request that people post
here when they receive the box. That way, it gives folks who are not
yet on the list an opportunity to email the current holder before
they mail it on.

I still have the master list on my computer at home, so if you're
starting a new postal box, I can email it to you (I assume that
anyone who'd want my box, would probably want yours)
