Orange & LA County report

From: Doug Gerlach (silentdoug@douglasgerlach.com) | Date: 2003-07-01 11:23:35 UTC-04:00
I just finished a weeklong trip to Anaheim and was able to squeeze in a bit
of time to log a few finds with some letterboxing compatriots.

Turtle McQ and I found the Marina Pacifica Plumbing Problem and the Naples
Canal Conundrum boxes in Long Beach, and even met the planters, Chowder and
his humans, while we were stamping in at the fountain!

Then, RichBeau and I logged the Easter Bunny in Fullerton, the first of the
Strange Stamps Series in Santa Fe Springs (#2 was out for repairs and #3 is
missing, we believe), and all three in the Lizard Crossing series in Whittier.

All of the above stamps were particularly nice, I thought!

We weren't able to find the West Wetlands in Long Beach, but it was after
dark so we just may have missed it. We did enjoy our short visit to the
large craft that's in the neighborhood, too.
