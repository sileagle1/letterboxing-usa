I'm aghast and I intend to immediately STOP recommending the list to

FREE Dating Websites For Singles and Married.htm - that file's description

adult-only list, then it should be made clear SOMEWHERE. If it is for all

area?????? I intend to research the matter after I finish posting this
message to determine if this is a violation of federal law. I believe it is,
but I'm going to find out whom to contact and how to report that file
remaining on an all-ages area with NO warnings posted ANYWHERE. Since my own

file, that is AT LEAST ONE MINOR HAS BEEN EXPOSED TO PORNOGRAPHY AS A DIRECT
RESULT OF THE INACTIVITY OF THE MODERATOR and I'm sure he is not the only
one. Therefore, the moderator has already committed a crime against a minor
and leaving it there constitutes further crimes against children. Given that
it has been up there for 4 days, I'd think any moderator doing their job
would have had ample time to take it down. Unless the moderator wishes to
continue to distribute pornography to children? In which case, I'm sure the
FBI will be speaking to him/her very soon about their actions.

HELLO MODERATOR - there is one on this list right? Are you watching what is
going on? Do you feel that PORNOGRAPHY has something to do with letterboxing
and it is vital that those picture be distributed to MINORS? Is letterboxing
an adult-only activity and you just FORGOT to post that anywhere in any
information? Or is your goal to purposely lure in families just to
distribute porn?

I fully realize that file was posted by a member during the recent slew of
porno attacks on Yahoo! lists. Oddly enough though, of the 37 lists I'm a
member of, only this one has LEFT the porn file up for one and all to enjoy.
Every other moderator realized that DISTRIBUTING PORNOGRAPHY TO MINORS IS A
CRIME and immediately took it down. This list however, they appear to be
encouraging the crime to continue.

In my mind, continuing to have that file there, where any kid can view it,
is the moderator's purposeful distribution of pornography to minors and
he/she should be taken immediately to task if that file isn't off there
within two minutes of them reading this post. As a moderator of more than a
dozen Yahoo! groups myself, I know exactly how long AND how easy it is to
remove that file so choosing not to is choosing to continue to commit a
crime, one for which I intend to hold them accountable for.

Sex photos on an all ages site - how dare you leave those up there
moderator! How dare you knowingly expose children to pornography under the
guise of a "family" hobby.
