Status on "Critters of Ennisbrook" & other things in CA -6

From: Sir Balthazar (neovolatile@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-21 18:02:24 UTC
JugglerMouse and I did the "Critters of Ennisbrook" this weekend.
Found every one of the seven and they were all gems! We especially
loved the work that Legerdemain (sp?) put into his box: handmade
paper for the journal, beautiful stamp, and not a fake rock but an
altered one! You set the bar very high, sir! Very high, indeed.

All in all, JugglerMouse got nine finds; she got the Nojoqui Falls
and Solvang box, as well. I got eight finds. There is a new box,
which still has not had its clues published, placed by JugglerMouse
and EmilyRose and called "Scoobie Snacks." As soon as they tell me
to, I shall publish the clues.

Scoobie Snacks is a double stamp: you ink one side and stamp it, then
change to the other side and stamp over the first. Very smurfy!

On Friday, JugglerMouse and Ash found my "Blue Moon Hawk" and were
the first two to stamp in.

The Central California Coast (region 6) is becoming a great area to
come letterboxing. If anyone is coming here to visit, let us know.

Stamping and carving,
Sir Balthazar of 100**100D (Googled)
