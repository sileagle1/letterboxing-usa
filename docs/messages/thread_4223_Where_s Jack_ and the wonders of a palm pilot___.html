From: Caitlin Letts (windairen@softhome.net) | Date: 2002-01-18 18:59:52 UTC-08:00
Today, one half of the Traveling Circus (The Minstrel, The Plundering
Bunny and Windairen), went hunting for Jack. He eluded us at first, and at
second and at third.. but after running into many, many, many dead ends we
finally caught up with him, the Oreo cookie milkshake we ran into along the
way gave us the strength to carry on till the end.
After our spirits had been raised by the discovery of Jack, we decided to
seek out the Troll and Dragon of Rock Creek Park. Night had set on by now,
of course, leaving us somewhat in the dark. However, a small greenish
light shone through our despair when the Plundering Bunny produced her
trusty palm pilot. With the help of William (the palm pilot), we finally
tracked down both of Rock Creek Park's resident beasts, getting only
slighty damp in the process. Just another exciting day in the life of the
Traveling Circus. Stay tuned for more thrilling adventures (and possibly
another letterbox series) to come....
