From: solor8008 (sjgallant@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-05-05 11:35:30 UTC
Hey all,

Finally had a full decent-weather weekend (despite weather forecasts
for Saturday) and we were able to get out to the woods.

After a late start on Saturday, Binx and I finished up the Time
Travels Series in the Blue Hills and finally found Hancock Hill
Letterbox with a tip from russell.

On Sunday we found Marathon, Piece of Cake, Bonsai, Massachussetts
Wolf Mystery, Hunter's Falls (made us a little nervouse due to
private property postings), Tangled Woods (also a bit nervy being in
a rather public spot!), and Angel's Lookout at Peppercorn Point.

All boxes were in good condition. The Wolf Mystery had water in the
box but the contents were saved by the ziplocs.

Thanks to all who placed these boxes!

See you on the trails!

Solor
