From: letterboxing mobots (themobots@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-06 18:39:01 UTC-07:00
today the temperature was about a zillion degrees. we walked from navy memorial metro station to the inventor, practically back to the washington monument, to orion, and then to HARMONY CAFE in georgetown, the BEST chinese restaurant in the DC area.

anyway here are some updates:

-We think the inventor is missing. not sure.

-the starting point for skull and bones is currently closed to the public. the fence was passable but better judgement took over.

-orion is a cool stamp that we almost missed. the maps were gone, our tree identification skills are not great, and we almost missed it in the branches. the note from the stamp thief is interesting. has anyone considered that it might be a clue involving the old postage museum???
