Just posted clues to #7 in the off season VLb series for those who
are interested.

This one is themed as "a salute to Valentines day" ... it's
not "adult" in the naughty way, but it probably wouldn't be of much
interest to our younger members (who already have 3 VLb's sort of
aimed for them in this series)

This one is "stand alone" ... meaning you don't need to have solved
any of the other VLb's in the series to complete this one.


One additonal thing I need to note for those doing the series.

I'm sorry to change the rules in the middle of things, but there are
so many participants that I'm going to suspend sending the
individual 'virtual stamps' for logbooks. Instead, we're going
to "PLAN B".

I will upload all of the 'virtual stamps' to the Picture Trail site
in a locked album. When all 10 of the series has been posted, I
will unlock the album and you can download the 'virtual stamps' of
the VLb's in the series which you've completed.

I shall continue updating the Honor Roll pages for all of the boxes,
just as I have been doing. (Whatever you sign your email with, is
what I copy and paste to the Honor Roll)

Enjoy!
