Election Day had absolutely gorgeous weather out here, high 70s and
sunny-partly cloudy, peak leaves... My kids and I joined some other
folks on a family hike in Balls Bluff. We went the reverse way around
the loop from the clues, so I missed #3, but I found #s 2 and then 1
with no trouble. Well hidden; re-hid them even better. Box outsides
were dirty but insides were dry & basically clean. Very nice stamps.
Books were overfull, though - visitors had left extra pages. Please add
more books! The others had gone on ahead, so nobody but my 2-1/2 year
old saw their locations AFAIK. She screamed a lot because she wanted to
stamp everything, including her arms and the outside of my book.

Figured out in hindsight - once we passed #2 - where #3 was, but didn't
have time that day to go back to it, alas. Had to rush off for a ballet
class. Will go back some other time to finish the series.

Lovely location for a short walk with the kids! Thanks :->
