Hello all you l.b. monster hunters!! Believe it or not, a mere
month from tomorrow we will all be meeting, greeting, eating,
exchanging, and hunting down some creepy letterboxes.
I need to get a head count..no, not a SEVERED head, but an INTACT
head count. The Mash hosts/hostesses would appreciate having some
idea of how many plan to attend so they can have enough hotdogs,
papergoods, etc. at hand. Please bring a potluck dish to share if
you can. (No electricity available though).
Even if you have e-mailed the sponsers of this event, please
contact me. We are trying to come up with one all-inclusive list of
Mashers. Would you please list the first names of everyone in your
party, plus last initials (if you don't mind) and l.b. nicknames?
Bringing any 4-footed pals? Let me know that too.
I think it would be great to have a list which includes all of you
(no last names), plus the town (or at least) state you are from. If
you are o.k. with that, let me know and I'll send this list out to
attendees after the fact. (Or may be before).
Long live letterboxing!
