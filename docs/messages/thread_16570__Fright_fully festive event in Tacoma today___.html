'Fright'fully festive event in Tacoma today!!!

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-10-26 02:00:35 UTC
Just got home from today's big letterboxing get together in Tacoma.
Talk about a blast!!! Not sure of the final tally of attendees but
there were quite a lot of letterboxers present and even more
letterboxes. So many that I wasn't able to keep up with them. I'll
have to attempt to find the permanent boxes there in the park at a
future time.

Thanks Team Rayvenhaus for all of your hard work in coordinating &
outfitting such a great event and & myriad thanks to all the other
great folks out there who contributed so many neat and interesting
letterboxes to making today's event such a success! It was so great
getting to meet all of you and we can't wait for the next big
regional letterboxing event!

See you on the trails...

dvn2r ckr, rnrB & trkr
