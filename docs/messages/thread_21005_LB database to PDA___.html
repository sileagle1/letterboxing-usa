Re: [LbNA] LB database to PDA???

From: highlysung (highlysung@verizon.net) | Date: 2004-11-29 15:49:14 UTC

I have been syncing my pda with Avantgo (where I created custom
channels for the areas where I generally letterbox) since we started
letterboxing almost a year ago. It generally works beautifully. If
you have a pda and regularly hotsync, then you will have up-to-date
clues every time you are out on the road.

Look into Avantgo.com - register and play around with creating custom
channels - it's not hard at all. My recommendations: be sure to allow
your custom channel to follow off-site links (as many clues are
posted this way to LBNA), and be sure to allow your custom channel to
set at a link depth of at least 3. All you do is make sure the custom
channel links to the url that contains the clues you want. Each area
will be a separate custom channel.

Here is what I keep on my pda:
Southern NJ Letterboxes
Northern NJ Letterboxes
NJ Mystery Letterboxes
Eastern Pennsylvania Letterboxes
Pennsylvania Mystery Letterboxes

When we travel to another area, I simply create a new custom channel
for that area and remove it when I come home again.

A small warning about this method, though. Once when we were in the
middle of a series, my pda inexplicably reset and we were not able to
access the clues to the rest of the boxes in the series. Now, I
always read ahead!! You never know when your trusty gadget will throw
you a curve!

highlysung of Team SuddenTurkey
