What a fantastic thing I've found!

From: jlacoy82 (jlacoy82@attbi.com) | Date: 2004-09-19 23:56:57 UTC
I heard about this letterboxing on Discovery/TLC channel (I know, I
know, I read the post about the media thing :( ), and found the
website. This was probably 2 weeks ago, and I've probably snagged
about a dozen and a half letterboxes in my area thus far (Western
Mass). I've lived in this area for almost 20 years (nearly my whole
life, I'm 22) and I cant believe some of the areas I had no idea
existed. I'm now regularly making trips around the county to go
collect letterboxes :) Not only was I in need of the exercise, but
just the chance to have a reason to get outdoors and find some of
these incredible spots makes this really quite an activity. I want
to place a letter box, but I dont want to do it just for the sake of
placing one, I want to make it a truly interesting walk. I've made
sure to email every placer and let them know the condition of their
boxes, I know I'd like the same courtesy, and I always try to rehide
the box better than I found it (add a few rocks, maybe cover with a
little more debris :) ). I really wouldn't mind joining anyone for a
hike, if anyone feels like partnering up and finding something out
here in Western Mass, feel free to give me a shout, I'd love the
company.

Simply happy to be letterboxing. :)
-Jeff
jlacoy82@attbi.com
