Red Bluff Letterbox
Morgantown,MS
About 11 miles Northwest of Columbia,MS.

Take I-55 to Mc Comb, MS go East on US HWY 98 to Columbia. Just
before Columbia look for a town called Foxworth turn left (at the BP
gas station) on to HWY 586. Head north for about a mile till you
cross some rail road tracks. Take a right on to HWY 587 and follow
this road for seven miles to a town called Morgantown. After
crossing the tracks again the road will fork stay to the right and go
another 1.7 miles up the hill, the bluff will be on your right and
there is parking on the side of the road. I suggest to wear old
shoes because of all the red clay will stain anything. Take a
moment and look around and when you are ready to hunt for the
letterbox carefully walk down the road to the big cement barrier.
