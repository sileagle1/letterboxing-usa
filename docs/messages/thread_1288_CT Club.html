Dear Amy,

One of our founding tenets was to be inclusive and national,
international - one big club. Sometimes gatherings are announced and
anybody in the region who wishes to attend is welcome. There have been
gatherings in Seattle (3 attending, 1998); Vermont (15? attending, Nov
1999): CT, Rocky Point (May 2000, 10-15 attending) and some thoughts of
a Fall gathering. Your webmasters come from CT, PA, PA, MD,VT, VT,NC,
WA. I hope you think about this some more and happily join your
energies and efforts with the rest of us who are having such a good
time. Your contributions will be welcomed and appreciated all over the
country!

Regards,
Susan in we-just-got-a-frost Vermont
