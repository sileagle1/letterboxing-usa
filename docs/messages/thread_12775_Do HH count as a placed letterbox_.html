Isn't the whole, glorious, wonderful part of this sport that there are no hard and fast rules (other than leave no trace)? I can plant my boxes where I want (again, leave no trace), put them in whatever I want (Rubbermaid, Tupperware, soap dish, film canister, peanut butter jar) write whatever clues I want (complex or straightforward, riddles or pictures) and I can count whatever I want, however I want. I say, do it in whatever way makes YOU happy! If you want to count your hitchhiker as a plant - do it! If you want to make a new category - do it! Just have fun! I don't believe the LBNA police are going to come and fine you for a violation ;-)MemliliP.S. Happy Mother's Day to one and all!
