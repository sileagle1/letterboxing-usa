"Charge"
Placed on September 29, 2002 by Luluphi
Location: River Rock Park - Harrisonburg, Virginia
Region: Rockingham County
Terrain: Easy
Questions?: Luluphi@yahoo.com

Directions from Harrisonburg to the park:
Travel west on Route 33, towards West Virginia. Turn left onto Last
Left Lane. Turn right into Riven Rock Park. Drive to the river and
find a parking spot among the trees.

Crossing the river used to be much easier when the rope bridge was
open.
A cobblestone path will lead you northwest keep traveling along the
river.
In no time at all you will be in the proper place.
Rushing water fills your ears as the river pours over the dam.
Nadar? Se prohibe nadar!

Once you see "Se prohibe nadar", take a compass reading 330 degrees
and you will see two trees separated by a small rock wall the new
home of "Charge".
