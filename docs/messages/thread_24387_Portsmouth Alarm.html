I would like to take this oppurtunity to thank everyone who has come
out and found Portsmouth Alarm. I know that there are so many more of
you out there just dying to go looking for this box. I found out that
on Monday, June 11th, it was the 1 year anniversary of the boxes first
find. One the anniversary of the first find, I had to replace the
logbook due to it being full. What a year this box has had, both good
and bad. The bad being, I had to replace the container twice due to
vandals smashing it. Other than that, I have heard nothing but awesome
things about the condition of the box and the place I have taken people
to.

I would like to encourage everyone else to visit this box.
It is one of my favorite and know it will also be one of yours.
