Before posting this I went back and reread the Lemony Snicket post that Brian wrote and subsequently apologized for writing. Now I don't intend to rehash/reflame anything , but I would suggest to all that flamed about his post to reread it. Frankly I read very little to apologize for. Brian did not attack any one by name, at least that I could figure out, but spelled out some frustration about reading the same questions over and over and subsequent answers over and over. His post prompted some interchange that helped create something new; "Resources for New LBNA Members"(see post #40929 by Steve & Heidi).
