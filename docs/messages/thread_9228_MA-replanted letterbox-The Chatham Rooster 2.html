The Chatham Rooster 2 Letterbox

This replaces the original Chatham Rooster, the stamp is slightly different.

Planted by Deanne Bellefeuille-Besnia (the lazy letterboxer) and David Besnia

November 16, 2002

Frost Fish Creek Walking Trail

North Chatham, MA

Terrain: Easy

Clues: Easy

The Chatham Rooster crows once again! Take Route 28 South to Chatham, MA. Stay on 28 through the town of Chatham . Be on the lookout for the North Port Shopping Center on the left. Approximately .4 of a mile after that look sharp for a dirt trail on the left opposite a body of water (Frost Fish Creek). Pull onto the trail and park in the small lot at the trailhead. Follow the trail keeping the creek to your right. You will pass a yellow pipe sticking out of the ground on the left and a tree in the middle of the path with an arrow on it. Keep walking as you pass a large fallen tree on the right. When you come to the 2nd yellow pipe, walk 50 steps and stop. There will be another fallen tree on your right and fallen logs up the hill on the left. The Chatham Rooster 2 is hiding behind the fallen logs covered with bark.
