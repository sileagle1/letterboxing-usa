Directions: From Heritage Hill, turn left onto
Webster Ave. Turn Right at the Second Set of Stop
Lights. This is St. Joseph Street. Follow this to
the corner of East River Drive. It will be the First
Intersection after the Stop Sign. Park anywhere near
the lamp post. This will be your starting point for
the first of four boxes.

Clues:
Box A: When looking at lamp post on the corner of E.
River Drive and St. Joseph, in the distance there
should be two grouping of trees. Nestled amoungst
those is this box. Good Luck on your journey.

Directions: Now drive your car onto E. River Drive.
Follow it until Allouez Ave. (This will be a Stop
Sign.) Turn Left onto Allouez Ave. There on your
Right side you will see a car park, gravel, waiting
for you to take the "Riverwalk." Park there to find
the next three boxes.

Clues:
Box F: Walk over the bridge to the paved path on your
Right side. Walk path to the Wood Plank Path. There
are some rocks and wood pieces on right side. Under
the path is this box.
Box B: Follow the path furthur. You will cross a few
more wooden plank paths. When you are on the last
wooden plank path before the bridge to take you into
the park proper, on your Left there are some wonderful
trees. Just before this path ends you will find the
box underneath yourself.
Box G: Walk over the bridge. Continue on the paved
path to right. 20 paces from the grouping of three
spring bars on their Via Course, you will see a tree
at about 2 o'clock. Walk towards that tree and
underneath it, the box is stashed away.

This is the first four. You need to have a grid on
your paper that is two columns of four spaces. On the
sides of the paper they should be marked like this:
A B Each box has a letter that correspondes to a
C D place in the grid. The picture you make is
the
E F surprise. Enjoy.
G H Next four are continued....
