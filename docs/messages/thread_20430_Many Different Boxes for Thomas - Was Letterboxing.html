Perhaps I am the only party pooper here, BUT I see one potential
problem... not with this PARTICULAR box & event but with what could
happen.

I know, could haves are a waste of time BUT while this box &
certainly this cause are, I'm sure, genuine (& I'm not in any way
suggesting that they aren't) there is the potential here for a less
scrupulous person to start a "charity box" of their own... you see
where I'm going here.

I would caution you to have all of the checks made out to the school
or charity directly, to keep yourself above reproach & to keep cash
out of the mix entirely. I would also encourage you to keep pay pal
out of it unless you set up an account specifically earmarked for
the benefit of Thomas & his lessons & NOT in your name.

Just my two cents worth.

The Family
