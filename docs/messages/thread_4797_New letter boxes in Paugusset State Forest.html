> As far as attachements vs cut-and-pasting into an email, you can do
> either.

I would recommend plain text (e.g. text e-mail), unless you need the
formatting of a particular document type.

The negatives of sending a MS word file, IMO, outweigh the positives -

a) they are huge, when the amount of information they contain is
small. It is a bandwidth waste to send them around the 'net.

b) some people cannot read them

c) some people won't read them when received on the 'net (for fear of
viruses). (I'm in that category, so if you want me to read them,
send them as plain text)

The positives -

a) well, I'm sure they exist ...

Cheers
Randy
