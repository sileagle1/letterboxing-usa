The Easter Bunny Letterbox
Planted: 3/26/02
By:Amanda from Seattle

This walk can be as long or as short as you want it to be. The box
is hidden in Laguna Lake Park in Fullerton, CA.

The long way is only 1.8 miles. You take the Bud Turner Trail to the
Lake. This trail begins at the corner of Euclid St. and Valencia Mesa
Dr. More information on the Bud Turner trail can be found at
http://www.ci.fullerton.ca.us/comm_serv/parks/trailsp1.html

The short way is just a "drive-by". You can enter Laguna Lake Park
from the corner of Hermosa and Lakeside.

Clues to the box:

Walk around the lake counter-clockwise.

You will come to a small picnic area (only 3 tables). There will be
2 small wooden footbridges over man-made stonefilled streambeds and a
mile-marker that says 1/4. At the mile marker post, take a compass
heading of approx. 75 degrees. (My compass has a bubble in it, so
this is only approximate)

You should see a tree of 3 with a stumpy part too! Inside the stump
part behind a piece of wood is the Easter Bunny Letterbox.

Please use caution when poking into holes and reaching into
crevices. Read the waiver of Responsibility and Liability.

Also this is a busy little park. Be discreet when stamping in. You
can take the box over to one of the picnic tables and then find the
right moment to return it to it's hiding place! :-)

Happy Hunting

Amanda from Seattle
