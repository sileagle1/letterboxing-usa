boxing in CT and Honda Road Again HH

From: thelionandsanddollar (pmoriarty01@snet.net) | Date: 2003-03-13 03:05:11 UTC
We had a fruitful day boxing in SE CT today. We figured we had to
get in what boxing we could before MORE SNOW (big groan) hit again
tomorrow! Everything was still snowy and frozen, but better than
it's been for a while. We hit the new DEP box in Pauchaug State
Forest and met up with Wanda there. Found that in order and well.
Unfortunately we can't say the same for Misery Loves Company. From
there we found some roses which Wanda had thoughtfully dug out. Red
Roses for You and a Little Rose Too were in good shape, and we picked
up the Honda Road Again HH there! Then we went down to Mystery Falls
and found that one safe and dry. All in all, a great day. We really
enjoyed boxing with Wanda.

Patrick & Amy (& Ruth)

P.S. If anyone is planning on doing the DEP box, please be carefull.
There is lots of ice on the trail going up!!
