CT. going to UTAH

From: crownedcrone (jsperrazza@snet.net) | Date: 2003-11-20 23:00:27 UTC
This could be a really great happening. I will be in Salt Lake City
on Jan 4 th and 5th and have an idea that will give us boxers in CT a
chance to share with folks in Utah. Please email me off line if you
would like to make a box for me to plant while I am there. I thought
we could gather in CT. before I leave and if we all stamp into each
of the log books and write a line or two, then folks that box in Utah
will meet us all by way of our stamps. Also, you boxers in Utah, how
about a gather on one or both of those days, and we can exchange, and
maybe even go plant !! What say you ?
Happy Trails
Butterfly in CT.
