I-95 Letterboxes Page is up

From: Mark Pepe (mjpepe1@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-04-12 03:34:19 UTC
Hi boxers:

I had some time to get a preliminary version of the I-95 Letterboxes
page ready to go.

There are many missing states, as I-95 runs from Maine to Florida.
Please stop by, check it out and help me fill up this page with
letterboxes! We are looking for boxes that are quick, easy and within
15 miles or less from the highway.

Thanks for your help and to those letterboxers who have already
emailed us with their contributions. We hope that this page will help
all of those letterboxers as they hit the I-95 highway this coming
vacation season.


Have fun & just get out there & box!!!

Mark Pepe
http://pepeanddavidow.blogspot.com/
