Boxing Report

From: Cheryl (teammaddawg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-08 23:18:16 UTC

This past weekend we went to say goodbye to our Devil Dawg who is
leaving on his second Iraq deployment next week. Of course, this
means we went boxing on a beautiful Carolina Sunday.

We found:

Pine 4 You - Wilmington, NC, by kilroy. All 4 boxes doing great.
Devil Dawg's girlfriend's first found boxes. She's hooked of course!

Blockade Runner - Wilmington, NC, by Amanda from Seattle. Great
stamp! We saw beautiful blue herons by the lake.

Welcome to South Carolina - Little River, SC, by Green Tortuga.
Carving so precise it looks for all the world store-bought.

Wayward Viking - Little River, SC, by noydb. What a wild place, and
a very clever placement!

Thank you all for your artistry and for taking us to such great
places. It made a difficult weekend (for Mom) much easier.

Cheryl
Team Mad Dawg
