I am thrilled that "Letterbox USA" now has its own mailing list discussion
group!! I have Letterbox clues spread all over the Web, and subscribe to
other mailing lists. There's one that's supposed to be for Letterboxers from
around the world, but there's more talk albout recouping expenses,
memberships, and publishing rights, than there is about Letterboxing.

The "Letterbox USA" website is where the whole American Letterboxing
phenomenon began. It's where I, personally, have chosen to post nearly
all my clues. It is the only site that has always been directed by and devoted to
the people... the adventurers... the pioneers of American Letterboxing.

Thanks, and congratulations, "Letterbox USA"!!!!
