MISSING BOXES ON BIG PINE KEY

From: sniffypokey (sniffypokey@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-10 15:14:48 UTC

We decided to go box hunting Saturday 7th at Big Pine Key, which is
about 30 miles south of where we are in the keys.There were two boxes
here according to the maps.

One was in the deer key refuge at a location called the"Blue Hole". we
are familiar with this location because we have been there many times.
We followed the clues, which are a little vague, but we are reasonably
sure we looked in the right place. The box is missing. we did meet a
family from England though, and enjoyed talking to them.

The other box was located near the "Big Pine Flea Market". It was late
in the day when we looked for this one and the market was closed. if
you searched for this during market hours it would be very difficult to
be descreet as this is very crowded area. The clues were more detailed
for this one and we are sure the box is missing.

we have looked for eight boxes now and only found three. not a good
average. we are heading back to Ohio next week and we will resume our
hunting back in the Buckeye State.

sniffy and pokey
