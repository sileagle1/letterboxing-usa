Wonderful evening for letterboxing in S. OH

From: bigguy_45640 (bigguy_45640@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-09 01:52:45 UTC
We had a wonderful respite from the monsoons in southern Ohio this
evening so CardinalMom, the kids, and I took the opportunity to hunt
a few boxes in the Chillicothe area. We found the Great Seal
letterbox first - it's in good shape but needs aome new log sheets.
Then we found Monkey Shines after a fairly intensive search - good
hide Franzsolo! We looked again for Kick Up Your Heels but sadly it
was still not to be found. After that, we changed locations and
began looking for Eerie, Eerie, Eerie. Imagine our surprize at
finding Yoctangee Park which we didn't even have clues for! Almost
as good as finding a hitchhiker - maybe better! We finished up by
finally locating Eerie, Eerie, Eerie. All the boxes are in good
condition. Loads of fun! Keep up the good work folks!
