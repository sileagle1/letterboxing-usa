From: team_new_hampshire (gingerbreadjunk@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-17 13:26:33 UTC
this conversation was overheard during a search for Gingerbread Jim
through the New Hampshire woods this past weekend.

"Have you found anything?"
"The only thing if found were some foot prints"
<#1> "I'm surprized you can see anything after the ice storm we just
had!"
<#2> "Ya, your right."
<#1> "Where did you find them? They may be of some help!"
<#2> "Oh, they are over here."

::: they head over to the foot prints :::

<#1> "Wait, I have seen these foot prints before!"
<#2> "You have! Where?"
<#1> "These are your footprints you...."
"Sir, come quick! I think I found something!"

::: they all gather to that area :::

<#1> "What did you find?"
<#3> "I found a cookie crumb!"
<#1> "Then he must be in the area! We must act fast!"
<#2> "But sir, it is much to cold for him to survive! Lastnight it
almost reached 0 degrees. There is no way he survived!"
<#1> "Pull yourself together!"
<#3> "We should probably continue searching for Jim. He has not much
time."
<#1> "Your right. We must work through the day to find him. Time is
running out!"

::: the search continues :::
