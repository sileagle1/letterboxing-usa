Now that the letter has gone out, I'd like to say something else....

I actually know Pandora in real life, she's the one that made mention of Letterboxing to Kris and that's what got us started. I just wanted to show that it is so very easy for others to take what is said out of context others to react to them. Those types of words are read in the frame
of mind that the recipient is in, not in the frame of mind the sender is in as we are missing two very vital aspects of human communications, Tonal qualities and Body language. With those missing, perception is reality. In other words, no matter how I meant what I typed, you'll read it in your frame of reference.

It seems to me that ar to often, people are getting their (insert appropriate form of undergarment here) in a wad over things that really are not that important. I mean, come on, a war in Iraq still going on, troops perhaps heading to Liberia, The national Debt, the jobless rate, layoffs without warning, the crime rate climbing, near drought conditions in 3/4 of the world and water shortages only over the horizon, starving people all over the world, crude oil prices rising meaning higher pump prices, etc, etc, etc.

I do letterboxing because it's fun, I do it for relaxation, I do it because it allows me to complete with the one person I can compete with and not get angry if they win.... Myself. ;-)

My hope is that we'll all go back to what this once was, according to those that have been here the longest... A place where we can all have fun, and were we can exercise our right to delete that which we don't want to read. As simple as that. I know I do and I receive this list in individual emails. Not a digest. I'm a masochist guess.
