Hi Everybody,

Donna and I were cruising around the Branford area yesterday and
decided to grab a quick box. We found the Golden Thread letterbox at
the Branford Hall Career Institute. Unfortunately, the box was in
some serious need of TLC. The box was filled with water and the
logbook was soaked. We went Walmart, down the road, to pick up some
baggies and a new logbook. The box has been restored.

This box has been classified as abandoned on the new site. We
checked the old site and could not find an owner. If you happen to
know who placed this box, please contact off list (we do not read the
messages daily) and we will forward the logbook as soon as it dries
out. If no one claims this box, we would be more than happy to adopt
it. It seemed like such a popular box, it would be a shame to let it
go.

Donna & Darrin
