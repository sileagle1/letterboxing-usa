I just wanted to take the time to thank those that showed up for the
F100 gathering at Stanly Park in Westfield. Yes, some of us didn't
check our emails at 6AM and headed out only to learn once we got
there it was cancelled:) !!! Was so good to see and talk to Pine
Tree and Columbian Bugs and to do a few boxes together.
I personally had a GREAT time... I finally got to meet WANDA and
PETE together and I especially enjoyed the look on Tehutika and
Annetka's faces when we ALL showed up at their front door! ..Truely
they were "surprised" to see us all?? Phynstar kept me in stitches
all afternoon as she accompanied me to my 200th BOX.
(personal "yipee!!" here) And Team Green Dragon.. well what can I
say except, thanks for the "lesson" in clue deciphering skills, I
learned alot from you. ...(and, YES, I still feel guilty) you guys
are the BEST .
I think you've got me hooked because suddenly I'm hot on the
trail of several of "those" boxes. Can't wait to get out there next
weekend. I'm looking forward to the REAL f-100 gathering whenever
it does happen. CHEERS;) "kstar"
