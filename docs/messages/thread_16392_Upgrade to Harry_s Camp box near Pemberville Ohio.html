Hey all,

I am new as can be to this letterboxing sport, but my boy and I planted our
first box (listed in Ohio, nearest city - Pemberville) a while back and I
went by there Sunday to check on it. It was still in hiding, undisturbed,
and as yet unstamped, so I upgraded the notebook with a cool leather-cover
parchment notebook that I found at a historical reenactor's festival this
summer (it was cheaper than the small strathmore pads and looked perfect for
letterboxing). I plan to make up some more of my own - pretty simple
design - for future letterboxes. Happy hunting.

Koop
