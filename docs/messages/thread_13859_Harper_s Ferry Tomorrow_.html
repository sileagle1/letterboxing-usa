Okay 10am at the Park Center. The Triple V Gang (or at least those of us who don't have to work on Saturday, blech) will deliver Ryan from the front door of his hotel to the pavilion at the parking area. We are hoping to meet a bunch of you! Oh AND, in the past HF has had a huge beautiful fireworks display on the Saturday before the 4th so you may want to make a day of it, or maybe we should start later?

hummingphish wrote:Or, you could park at the park center. It's only $5 per carload and they run very nice shuttle buses into (and back from) Harper's Ferry every 15 minutes. These buses drop off at a nice covered pavillion with benches that could theoretically be used for the exchanges.
After a very recent trip there I really don't think there would be enough parking for everyone who says they want to attend and a $5 donation per family to the park service for 'hosting' this event would be a nice gesture. Just a thought.

TripleV Gang wrote:
The Triple V Gang met up with Ryan again last night at the Blackburn Trail Center. We met lots of cool hikerfolk and had a very enjoyable visit. Since no decision has been made as to when and where to meet I couldn't give Ryan a time or place but I am set to email him as soon as we decide. I know that parking is a premium in HF so I'm just not sure where the best place is. My personal vote is for the AT headquarters but again parking may be an issue. If we all park down at the river where the parking lots are we could just meet somewhere down there and walk up the hill to the icecream place for refreshments.

So what does everyone think? Who else is coming? 10am at the parking area by the river?

Sarah of the Triple V Gang
F19 P1 HH2 X4

Kittel Greg M wrote:
The Harper's Ferry Youth Hostel, located on the Appalachian Trail, It's
actually in Maryland in Sandy Hook. I was there in April with our scout
troop when we biked the C&O canal. Current proprietors are Loretta & Ray
Johnson (301) 834-7652 the address is 19123 Sandy Hook Rd Knoxville, Md
21758. They have a huge yard with barbecue pits, a full kitchen etc and
beds for those want to spend the night. $15.00 over 15 and $7.50 for those
under. Bring your own sheets or rent 'em for a buck. oh yeah they have
bathrooms and a shower for Ryan :). They would probably love to have ya'll
come but I'm not sure it's best to call 'em first.

Unfortunately I can't make it. But send my regards.
