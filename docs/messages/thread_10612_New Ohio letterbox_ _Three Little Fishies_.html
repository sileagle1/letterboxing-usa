Title: Three Little Fishies. Location: Chillicothe, Ross County,
Ohio.Difficulty: Easy. Placed 2-2-03 by Salamander. This letterbox is
located just off the Chillicothe Pedestrian/Bikeway Corridor. This is
a popular paved walkway stretching for 3.1 miles along the Scioto
River. You may access the corridor in many different places, but the
starting point for this letterbox quest is the parking area at the
end of Poplar Street. CLUES: Head west along the walkway from the
parking area access. You will soon come to a "3.0 mile" marker. Stop
at this marker. Go 17 paces at a heading of 292 degrees. (1 pace= 2
steps). You should arrive at a tree with several trunks, one of which
has been cut down leaving a somewhat rotted stump. The letterbox is
hidden within the stump, under a couple of rocks and a small chunk of
concrete.
