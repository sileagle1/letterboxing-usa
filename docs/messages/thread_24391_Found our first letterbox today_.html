Found our first letterbox today!

From: Amy (kevamy1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-06-14 05:16:18 UTC
I stumbled upon and article in Family Fun magazine while trying to come
up with things to do with my kids this summer. I almost didn't read
the article because it appeared to be part of a car ad, but am I glad I
read it anyway! My three kids and I went trapsing though a beautiful
park today and found 2 boxes. My 8 year old absolutely LOVED it! The
6 and 4 year old were happy to be in the woods at least. This is an
absolutely wonderful activity! We are looking forward to finding many
more!
Amy
