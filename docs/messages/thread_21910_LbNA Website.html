LbNA Website

From: Choi (john@johnsblog.com) | Date: 2005-08-22 19:14:39 UTC
There were a few minor changes made to the LbNA website today. Yell if
you spot any problems. Notable changes are 1) next/previous buttons at
the bottom of clue pages to make it easier to go through a list, and
2) check box in BoxAdd page to confirm you comply with LbNA Terms of Use.

One thing that might be a little strange with the next/previous links
is that when you use one and the letterbox that you go to has it's
clues linked outside of LbNA, you'll see a dummy page with the link
and have to click on the link to the off site clues.

Choi
one of the webmasters
