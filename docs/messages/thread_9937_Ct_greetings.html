Howdy all boxers.
I just want to give a hugh Thanks to all the folks that have planted
boxes. I have hiked the Blue Trails of Ct. for many years and since
beganning to letterboxing a little over a year ago, I have had such
pleasure in finding the lovely preserves and small parks that play host
to boxes. We live in a beautiful State and show off our treasures to all
who wish to search out a letterbox. So many new friends have enriched
my life and brought joy and laughter along as we hike and squeal when we
find our prize.
As Bluebird and I drove home from a day of boxing which ended with a
warm cup of cocoa at The Leader of the Pack's home, we figured we have
walked hundreds of miles together and are pushing a BIG number if we add
our finds, plants, HH and X's The numbers astound me, but the joy of
hiking and boxing with friends is the very best.
Thanks to all and Happy New Year.
Butterfly
