From: pell_lake_girl (mishiekins@prodigy.net) | Date: 2006-11-07 18:49:53 UTC
Just wanted to share a caution about these. There is a planter in my area who has used
these in 9-10 boxes thus far. After they get some use [from lots of finders stamping and
looking/reading] the pad of paper detaches from the backing. In addition, since these are
not actually "bound" like a book, the pages themselves easily come dis-attached after too
many people have looked at the book and turned the pages. So the pages for the first few
finders were coming out when I have found these books. And the pages themselves are
very thin paper and stamps show thru on the other side. I think these are intended as
notepads, not as books with pages to remain inside them.
