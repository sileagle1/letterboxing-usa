Sunset Rock Letterbox

From: (egd1125@aol.com) | Date: 2001-06-02 21:02:15 UTC-04:00
This is a single letterbox in the Northern section of Southington, CT. From I
84 East or West, take exit 34 off highway. If traveling East, turn right at
end and right again onto Woodford Ave. If traveling West, turn left at end of
exit and at second light turn right onto Woodford. Turn at next left onto
Ledge Road.
Parking will be on your left approx. 1 1/2 miles up.

This hike is moderately easy with one major climb and several smaller ones.
It takes approx. 45 min out and 45 min back.
