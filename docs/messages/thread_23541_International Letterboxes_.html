From: Pam (mindizney@cox.net) | Date: 2006-06-29 20:29:41 UTC
If you go to Atlasquest, there are currently five boxes in Spain on
that site. Click on the toolbar for Letterboxes, and do an advanced
search. Put ES in the country code box and press search. You will
find these:

1. Canary Island 1
Mystery, Canarias

2. Englishtown (this one is also on LBNA)
El Barco de Avila, Castilla y Leon

3. Es Cornador
Soller, Baleares

4. Jaume II Letterbox
Cala Ratjada, Baleares

5. Ole Letterbox
Madrid, Madrid

Have fun!
Music Woman
