New HH - SS LB Goddess

From: Fudrick (fudrick@attbi.com) | Date: 2002-12-29 12:55:25 UTC-08:00
A new Hitch Hiker was placed today, 12/29/2, the SS LB Goddess!
 
This is honor of the nice lady who, with her diminutive minions, has put South Sound Washington on the Letterboxing Map single-handedly.  In the short time they have been on the trails, they have found over 220 and placed over 50 Letterboxes!  There enthusiasm is contagious, others in the area have picked up the pace, providing more adventure for those with Letterbox Fever.  She wanted to attend a Gathering, so decided to make one happen.  Over 30 attended for a great day of fellowship and letterboxing.
 
This HH is sent forth in her honor.  It has been placed with a letterbox she has already snagged, probably the last place she would look!  When you log in, provide some kind words, and, please, keep it in the general area, so she may find it some day.
 
Fudrick
