New box planted in Nassau County, NY

From: Cindy DeRosa (bissy699@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-07-11 16:54:03 UTC-07:00
Hello, A new letterbox has been planted in Seaford,
NY.

This is the first box planted in Nassau County. Here
are the clues.
Planted by: Cindy DeRosa
Name of Box: The Bissy Box
Date Placed: July 11, 2002
Nassau County, NY
Nearest Town: Seaford
Moderate search.

Entering Cedar Creek Park, pull into the first parking
lot on the right. Take the sidewalk up to the main
path. On the right will be the entrance to the Bike
Path, you will see a large mermorial rock on the left
to the entrance of the path.
On this path you can use bikes, rollerblades,
scooters, or just walk.
Take this path until you go over the second bridge.
After you go over the second bridge, on the left you
will see a path going down to the beach landing. Take
the path to the bottom. On the left you will see a
group of Boulders and Rocks. Not to far up the rocks
you will find
your box.

Please e-mail me to let me know you found the box. bissy699@yahoo.com

=====
Cin-cerely,
Cindy DeRosa - Stamp till you cramp.
Happy Stamping.
