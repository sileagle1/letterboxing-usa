Sunvalleysu with Dragonjake and Soccerjessie (their names will
probably change everytime we go out!) and DH in tow (to read the
clues) took a detour to Vienna on our way home to the Shenandoah
Valley from McLean.

We decided to check out the Two-by-two series in Southside Park
placed by the Daughter of the Old Dominion. All three boxes were in
great shape and may I say the clues were really well written. There
were lots of trail checks to reassure you that you were on the right
track.

When hubby read the name of the third box as the "two left behind"
kiddies were so excited to see if they had guessed what the third
stamp would be...

Thanks for a super way to spend a pretty spring afternoon DOOD!
