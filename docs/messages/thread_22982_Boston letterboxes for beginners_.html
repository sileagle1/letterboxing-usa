From: Pam Wildeman (pwildeman@alum.dartmouth.org) | Date: 2006-01-04 13:38:08 UTC
I'm a first-time letterboxer and was hoping someone could recommend a
few good excursions in the Greater Boston area? We have a car and

Any suggestions would be appreciated!







Re: Boston letterboxes for beginners?

From: cpascott (seh-letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-01-04 19:24:34 UTC
Hi!

Always hard to suggest boxes and leave others out. Plus it depends on
what type of boxes you like. I'd check out all the boxes in the area
you'll be in and peruse the clues.

In Greater Boston, there are a bunch of boxes in Jamaica Plain. I
haven't got many of these boxes, but it's a good place to go to get
more boxes.

There are a few boxes on the Esplanade, and if you're in Boston during
the weekday, Little Prince is an interesting box.

Happy Hunting!
