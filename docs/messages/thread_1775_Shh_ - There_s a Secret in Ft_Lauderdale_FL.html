From: (SLOOPYTOM@AOL.COM) | Date: 2001-01-21 17:20:47 UTC-05:00
At the south side of Ft.Lauderdale from I-95 get off at SR 84 and head west
about 1/2 mile and watch closely for the Secret entrance on the north side of
the road.
Park inside and visit the park office and get a map.After entering Â the park,
you'll have to choose between the two paths. Take the one less traveled; you
can visit the river afterwards. This is a very short walk.
Take the first right after getting on the trail to the first semi-circle of
seats on your right. Go @ another 12 paces to the large strangler fig on the
left.Because your mission is a secret, make certain you are alone and go
around to the back of the tree and you'll find the box in crotch of the tree
about 3' off of the ground.
Take the box over to the benches to complete the logs and again wait until
you are alone before replacing the box.
Let me know how things are at SLOOPYTOM@AOL.COM
Thanks and enjoy,
