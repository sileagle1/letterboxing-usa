VA Letterboxing in Reston area 03/29/03

From: Silver SEAL (awktc@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-03 16:35:25 UTC-08:00
Seal pups 1 & 3 and I found the following boxes in the
Reston, VA. area, and had a great time doing so.

Frying Pan Park; Christmas Tree Graveyard; Furry
Woodland Creature and Tyson's Witch. Frying Pan Park
logbook was wet and we did not have a replacement with
us. We enjoyed the stamp. Seal pups 1&3 managed to
get their Jr. Ranger Badge at the Kidwell Farm, they
had a great time. The Graveyard and Furry Creature
were in good shape. I especially liked the Furry
Creature Stamp done by Firefly. Tyson's Witch was in
good shape. The slope going down to the trail was
fun, coming back was a different story. I'm glad we
put that box as our last find. I was ready to call it
quits after climbing back up that slope.

After running around all day Saturday in 70-75 degree
weather we drove back to Virginia Beach in the snow on
Sunday, but it was worth it.
