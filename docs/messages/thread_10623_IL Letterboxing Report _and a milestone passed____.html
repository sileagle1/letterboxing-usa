IL Letterboxing Report (and a milestone passed....)

From: Silent Doug (silentdoug@douglasgerlach.com) | Date: 2003-02-03 23:01:46 UTC-05:00
I finished my trip to the Midwest today with a letterboxing jaunt in
Southwestern Illinois. I was able to find 7 before a downpour ended my
boxing adventures:

* Watershed (Even though it's listed on the LBNA site as a
geocache/letterbox combination, it doesn't have a stamp, and I'm not sure
it ever did. It's currently a big mess with no journal and scraps of paper
stuffed in the box with logs jotted on them.)
* Valley of Coal (The journal was a little damp, but I left a dessicant
packet in the bag and that might help dry it out some.)
* Sitting Pretty Susie
* PokeyMan #43 -- Oddish
* PokeyMan #14 -- Kakuna (Needs some maintenance -- the outer ziplock bag
is torn, and it's in a very moist environment.)
* Fishers of Men
* CMPL (very nicely done, though some geocachers have evidently mistaken
this for a geocache -- there are some cache items in the box.)

U Island was inaccessible today, and I couldn't find PokeyMan #50, but I
think it may have been removed for maintenance?

For those who are keeping track, today's finds take me past 100, in 10
different states:
http://www.letterboxing.info/found_bystate.shtml

:-)
