I've already posted this on the Southern CA board and received some
good input from Q & Don. I thought I'd also post on the "big board"

We'll be in California from NOV 6-14 to visit some wineries, do some
hiking and look for letterboxes. We'll be flying into Los Angeles and
tentatively plan to head north along the Central Coast, including the
San Luis Obispo area and north to the Cambria and Paso Robles area.
Based on our preliminary look at LB clues, we'll probably also spend
some time in the Ventura area and Pasadena/Altadena area because they
have some "difficult" or "strenuous" hikes that also have a LB.
We're interested in any suggestions from locals re: good hikes,
interesting wineries and your favorite restaurants.

If anyone is interested in getting together for an informal exchange
over a cup of coffe or glass of wine, let us know.
