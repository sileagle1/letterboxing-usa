Ct. Gazebo

From: NLS (jsperrazza@snet.net) | Date: 2003-01-12 15:36:09 UTC-05:00
Yo all you boxers stuck in because of the snow. Start a Log Book
Improvement and build yourself a beautiful Gazebo. All you need is a
stamp pad of your color choosing and The Leader of the Pack has made all
the arrangements with the builders, lumberyards and delivery trucks. Go
to her Web Site and get your route planned. FYI there are many
letterboxes along the way to stretch your legs, as these pieces of the
Gazebo are all drive bys. It is a joy to 'build' as how many of us would
visit these charming gazebos in towns where we do not live ? Today the
Holiday decorations were being removed in the Andover Gazebo and we were
lucky to get the 'right' posts while the workers were on a break. My
Gazebo is all built and will be ready to view at the Ma Gather !! O.k.,
the roof leans a bit and the Copula is a tiny off center, but I love it
and you will to, so go.!!
Butterfly
