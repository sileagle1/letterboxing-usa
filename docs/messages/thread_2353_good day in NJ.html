good day in NJ

From: Noelle Bresser (avis4@voicenet.com) | Date: 2001-05-16 18:49:30 UTC-04:00
Perfect Circle LG,

I was in a boxing frenzy and decided to take the day off from work and
pick up some new stamps for my journal. I will be out of the country
(Holland) and wanted to get some boxing in before I left.

"Fisherman's Point" was very quiet today with no one to spy on me.
"Into the woods" was great. Lake Manny is very pretty, very scenic. I
have all 6 of your stamps, unless you put out more since then. Thanks
for the hunt.

SJinNJ,

"The Tourne" gave a beautiful view of the city. The stick I chose to
retrieve the box broke so I had to be a daredevil and just stick my hand
in. It was a success! I was growing weary but I stuck it out and
located "Mountainside". The second campfire gave me a tiny bit of
trouble, but I was soon presented with the clue. Yummmm!!! that ice
cream was good!!! The stamp wasn't the only prize I brought home with
me. A tiny silkworm decided to hitch a ride in my hair. Yikes! But
that is so much better than a giant snake. *s*

? for all,
Has anyone have any info on boxes in Europe, besides England? I was
wondering if while I'm in Holland I should plant a box. Thanks in
advance for your input.

Noelle
P4F34X2
