NY - Highland Forest

From: Ron Salladin (salladin@frontiernet.net) | Date: 2003-11-24 12:37:04 UTC-05:00
Yesterday was a great day to visit beautiful forest located in Central
NY, and to find "Flowering Fern", "Tall Pines" and "Tall Pines Under the
Moon" Lb's. All three are alive and well. Some water had gotten into the
T.P. M. box, but the stamp and log inside the baggy were dry. The
trails, which are also used by the horses, were wet and muddy, but the
weather was perfect and the stamps alone were worth the slipping and
sliding about. Thanks Libby and company!
