From: Gemini (bayou@mail.usa.com) | Date: 2003-03-14 16:05:26 UTC
After you get into NC, there are several that I know of that are
within a 10 or 15 minute drive on one side or another of I-85 in
Davidson County and in Rowan County. I have found all of these and
would recommend them to you!

We personally planted the Frog Prince letterbox in Salisbury, NC
(Rowan County) and it is very easy to get to, as is the Dan's Hikers
letterbox which was planted by our good friend TrailSeeker; both are
in parks and are easily accessable.

The letterboxes in Davidson County (Outside of Lexington, near
Spencer) are in a wooded area and will require an hour or so of
tramping around in the woods (there are THREE boxes there in the same
State Park!). Be aware that the Boone's Cave State Park has been
closed, but you can still park outside the gate and walk the 1/2 mile
down the drive to the park and still search for the boxes. In fact,
it's even better this way because it decreases the chance of the boxes
being accidentally found and gives you the park all to yourself! It's
a beautiful place!

Happy Hunting,
