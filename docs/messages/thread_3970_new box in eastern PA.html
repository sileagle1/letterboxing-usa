Tinicum Park-Delaware Canal
Erwinna, Bucks County,PA
planted Dec 21,2001-Winter Solstice
by Floating Feather
Easy
Directions: Route 32. a few miles south of the Frenchtown bridge. Right
turn into the park,drive as far back as you can to the canal and park.

INDIGO TURTLE
Head south on the tow path. Go past the not so golden "Golden Pheasant".
While you go under,the traffic goes over. Go past the BSA and then
"switch gears"-3 to be exact. After a short distance look down,around
and inside a "holy" tree for your grail.
Continue on for See-Joys Floating Feather box.
