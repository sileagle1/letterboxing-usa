I'd also like to point out that between Thanksgiving and New Years,
the garden here is decked out with over 200,000 christmas light--
similar to the Zoo Lights in Portland if you've ever seen them.
Unfortunately, I arrived a couple of days before the light
extravaganza began, but I could see the hundreds of thousands of
unlit Christmas lights and it looks like it'll be a pretty incredible
show! Definitely an ideal time to head out to this place, although
it might be a challenge finding this letterbox in the dark. (Or
maybe not. *shrug* This is one of my easier clues and the hike
isn't at all a challenge. Definitely doable, even at night!)

Enjoy!
