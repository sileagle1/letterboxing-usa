From: thelionandsanddollar (pmoriarty01@snet.net) | Date: 2003-04-17 12:18:36 UTC
Just a note to let the respective owners know that Patrick & I
exchanged HH with Steve & Heidi. We are now in possession of the
Vagabond Vulture, which will be flying off for parts unknown today.
Steve & Heidi have the Catbird. Also to update of the Vulture's
travels to date:
started 11/30/02 by Lobsta Lady at Milo Light
picked up by Josef on 11/30,
who passed it to the Hotties(Sue & Mike)on 12/8
left at the Nike Missle Mystery Box
Picked up by MusicWoman on 12/25
left at Avalonia on 12/27
picked up by Kurt on 2/26
taken to Gray Rock 2/27
picked up by Steve, Heidi, & Madras 3/20
passed to Patrick & Amy 4/11

All of these are CT boxes. We hope to have it back out sometime
today. Thanks for the great surprises! We love finding HH.

Patrick & Amy
