Ancestral Pueblo

Planted 14 March 2000

When asked who houses those were, the Dine replied, "The ancient ones."
The ancestors of the sky city lived here before the big drought drove them
east to better watered valleys. To find my imprint commemorating this antique
civilization, you might begin at the city recalling the name of the
Spanish Duke Francisco de la Cueva's (d. 1637) estates. Willa Cather's
bishop passed through it on the way to his see.
