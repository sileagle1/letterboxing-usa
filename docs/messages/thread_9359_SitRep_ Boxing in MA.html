TeamGreenDragon had a great ( although wet ) weekend of boxing and

the following boxes all in good shape : ) . " A Bridge in the Woods "

Wayland , " Heron on the Charles River " in Watertown ( our second
attempt
at this one ) , " Nauset Light " in Eastham " ( The waves where really

Wellfleet , "Cooks

Guests "
amongst others ) , " Chatham Rooster 2 " again in Chatham , " Oyster -

finally " Sagamore Hill " !! ( We had to take shelter in the Ammo

The snow may keep us from finding, but the rain won't stop us from
looking
!

We also completed our part in CSCM's mission to get a box in all 39
cities

I want to thank everyone for getting us up and out to see these great
locations.
