Opt Out Feature for Blue Diamonds

From: Lock Wench (lockwench@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-09-24 21:58:45 UTC
I am happy to report that Ryan has added an "Opt Out" checkbox for
Blue Diamond boxes. If you do not wish this designation on your own
boxes, you can now go to My Page on AQ and then Preferences. Scroll
down to the bottom and you can check the last box, if you wish. You
may have to log out and log back in after you have saved the changes.


Please take a moment to thank Ryan if you found this feature useful.
