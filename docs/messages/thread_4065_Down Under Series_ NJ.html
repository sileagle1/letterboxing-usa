Went for a walk in High Mountain Preserve today.  I started from WPU and did the red/yellow/red loop which took me by EMU and DINGO, so I checked in on them  both.  DINGO is just fine ... well hidden, warm and dry.  EMU, on the other hand, was a little damp.  I dried out the box and baggies as best I could and put everything back, but thought you should know that the notebook is pretty damp.
