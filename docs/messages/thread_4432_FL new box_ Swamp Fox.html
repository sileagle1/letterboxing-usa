FL new box: Swamp Fox

From: Mountain Scorpia (mountainscorpia@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-02-10 23:21:01 UTC-05:00
Volusia County
nearest city: DeLand

First find Swamp Owl, Clay Dog, and Clay Acorn, Nos. 1, 2, & 3 of Seven from
the Florida Ancients.

NO. 4 - Swamp Fox

There will be a bench on your left; stay on the north end of it. Behind it,
at about 112 degrees and 54 steps, is a straight-cut stump where the Swamp
Fox hides.

Reverse your bearing to return to the trail and turn left to continue.
Enjoy a rest at the riverview, and continue on Nature Trail.
