CT Successes Despite Ice and Snow

From: bluebird2boxes (debby_butler@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-03-15 21:14:06 UTC
Yesterday Butterfly and I had great fun at Hartman Park, getting
the wonderful (good job, Jay!) new Happiness stamp and
reaching my 1000 PFX! Happiness really led to more happiness,
if you know what I mean!

Today we went with Lobsta to Coventry and Mansfield, getting
almost 20 boxes, including the 3 hitchhikers we found: Winter
Wheat, Come Home Snoopy and It Only Takes a Spark - soon to
be re-released on a trail near you! Ice and snow-packed boxes
were not too much for us...we brought our handy dandy whammy
bar and chipped, pried and poked them loose. Won't Spring
boxing seem easy after these winter adventures?

Keep your eyes open; we are planting tomorrow!
