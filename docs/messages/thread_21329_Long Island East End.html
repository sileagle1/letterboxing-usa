Long Island in a desert as far as letterboxes. There are two in Manorville,
some in Brookhaven Park some in Blydenburgh Park, Smithtown (this is a
winter series) and one at Stony Brook & one at the Morton Wildlife preserve which
isn't far from the ferry in Sag Harbor. There are some at Orient but I just
didn't find that one yet.
This is a great place to start planting boxes. So get to it.
I have planted and got two people who are planting too.

I do most of my boxing in Ct when I visit my son.
