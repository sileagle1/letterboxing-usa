Message: 4
Date: Sun, 24 Oct 2004 23:30:30 -0000
From: "bassackwardsfrommd"
Subject: Great boxing day in MD!


Hello! The Basses had a very productive boxing day today! We found
the Box On and Zodiac series in Black Hill park. Except we
accidently skipped over the directions for Deanno's Capricorn, so
we'll have to go back sometime. Still have to get the Birder Series
anyway. We also found the Blair Letterboxing Project box. We got 8
stamps in all. While hiking back to the car, from finishing the
Zodiac series, a huge owl swooped out in front of us, landing in a
tree. You just never get to see (at least I don't!) owls out there,
so it was incredible to watch this thing in action! He kept flying
fron tree to tree so close to us. It was amazing that he never made
the smallest sound. They are so silent. It wasn't a horned owl, we
could see there were no "horns," but he was huge. His wing span had
to be at least 4 feet. It was very cool!
Keep an eye out for a new MD mystery box, coming soon. I am also
working on an idea for an Artemis Fowl series. Can't wait to get
started on it. (hands rubbing together, maniacal smile...)

Message: 9
Date: Mon, 25 Oct 2004 01:00:42 -0000
From: "Bigguy"
Subject: Tennessee boxing


The family and I enjoyed a too quick weekend trip down to eastern
Tennessee this weekend. We viewed the magnificent foliage and found
the Gatlinburg letterbox as a bonus. It was great to get to view so
many signature stamps from folks around the country that we usually
don't cross paths with. Very nice box - dry and in good condition -
and a very nice stamp.

Unfortunately, we didn't have as good a luck with the Smoky Fir box.
Despite very good clues and a thorough search, we were unable to find
any sign of it. We suspect the rangers may have got it. Too bad.
The Park Service needs a more enlightened policy about this. Hope you
all had a good weekend!

Bigguy

common to the group. The person who makes the request to complete the survey

get money for each name who completes the bogus survey and gets targeted
marketing. DON'T WASTE YOUR TIME.
