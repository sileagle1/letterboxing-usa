From: Gwen & Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2001-05-30 16:35:40 UTC-07:00
Todd,  we have only been letterboxing 6 months but have visited some great places thanks to this new activity.  Magu Peak was our 4th Box.  It was an exhilarating view  and we were stoked to be the first ones to the box when we visited on Jan.6th. The hike was long in terms of others we had done, BUT certainly not difficult.  We understood that the hike would be longish and planned accordingly.  Please reconsider putting Magu back on the Clue list,  It is too good to not be there for others.    We have recommended the hike to other "boxers" as the best in Pacific views in So. Cal.    We checked out clues to Superstition Box and decided that we would leave that one for the younger folks, as we felt the difficulty was over our heads.  That does not mean that others would find it so.  Letterboxing should not be all vanilla.   We are off to Los Osos and Vacaville to try for some boxes up north, but meanwhile thanks for Magu!      Gwen and Don from So. Cal.
