Just a note to let everyone know that people are still letterboxing
in the midwest...haven't seen any posts for the area in a while.
We went out Saturday and we were able to dodge the rain. Found seven
boxes in the area.
Updates:

Tyranena, Lake Mills, Wisconsin--found in good shape.

E-Way Trails, Madison, Wisconsin--found...a little wet in the
box...replaced ziplock bags that had holes in them.....Avoided the
rain by minutes at this point!

Hansen Golf Course-Milwaukee County Park Series--Found in good shape.

Dretzka Park-Milwaukee County Park Series--Found in good shape.

Brown Deer Park-Miwaukee County Park Series--Found in good shape.

Estabrook Park Boxes #2 and #3--Found in the dark...in good shape.
