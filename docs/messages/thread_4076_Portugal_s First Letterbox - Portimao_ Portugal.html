Portugal's First Letterbox
Portimao, Portugal
Placed December 25, 2001
by the Varrell family from Connecticut, USA

Park near the Portimao traffic circle with the fountain, between
Presidente and Edificio Three Castelos.
The beach is straight ahead, down many stairs.
At the bottom, you can see a double stone arch to the right.
Turn left and pass by the brown shack on your left.
Pass a stone arch on your right.
Pass a cave on your left.
The box awaits in a cubby straight ahead, above where "Xanghi 97" is
carved into the sandstone.

Please be discrete and rehide carefully.
