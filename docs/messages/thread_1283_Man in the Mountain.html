Here are the clues to our newest box, Man in the Mountain



Man
in the Mountain

This box is hidden on Vermont's highest peak, the chin of the sleeping giant
of the Mountains.

Take the Sunset Ridge Trail until the chin is in view. When the trail turns
sharply right (204 degrees) stop.
With the chin ahead at 140 degrees, stand at the West Chin Natural Area
sign. (*note- this sign did not look very permanent. If it is not there
look for the other 2 bearings.)
Set your compass to 94 degrees. The letterbox is hidden straight ahead
about 15 feet in a rock crevice.

Good Luck, and enjoy your day in the mountains Melissa & Dan
