Quick note: I (Steve) hunt waterfowl out of my kayak. As part of the
camouflage I buy out Wal-Mart's burlap supplies when the hunting
stuff goes on sale. Now's a good time to check as they convert the
sporting goods section to summer sports (i.e. fishing). You can get
a piece about 14' long by 10' wide (camo on one side, brown on the
other) for about $4. Don't buy it until they "red tag" it.....which
happens every spring like clockwork. 50% off is always better than
full price : )
