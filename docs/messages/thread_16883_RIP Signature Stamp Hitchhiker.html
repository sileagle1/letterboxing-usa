RIP Signature Stamp Hitchhiker

From: Laura/Mike B. (bedotcalm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-17 01:23:31 UTC
Hi,
Well we came across this little hitchhiker when we found Red Squirrel
yesterday afternoon. Our whole family (and some extended family) were
out hunting in Wapato Park (Tacoma, WA) and were treated to our first
hitchhiker. I'm claiming it since I was the one to open the box!!
WooHoo!! My daughter and I have a plan for dropping off this coming
weekend. We have a specific box we will be looking for to place it
in. Not sure how often the box is visited since it is off the beaten
path.

This was our second weekend of letterboxing and we were tickled pink
to find a hitchhiker. Especially not knowing what one was until we
found it! Nor knowing that one was placed earlier that morning!

Til later,
MiniBullyMom and Dolphin
