Rock Woods   Planted by SpaceDAW (with help).

As always be discrete when letterboxing. It is a good idea NOT to announce what you are doing to strangers. Some people get a kick out of tearing things up or keeping things for themselves. Be careful of the surrounding vegetation and be sure to put everything back...especially closing ziplocks carefully. For this box it wouldn't hurt to bring an extra freezer ziplock - just in case. For some reason the animals like to chew on notebook paper.

There is a lot of room there...why not take a box with you to "plant" after you stamp up "Rock Woods."
