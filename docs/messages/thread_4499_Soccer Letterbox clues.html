If you get a kick out of soccer, this box is for you!  In the upstate of South Carolina there are plenty of soccer fields to be found.  One such park, called "The Pavilion" is located on the eastside of Greenville.  When you turn into the complex a playground will be to your right, keep going through the parking lot and you'll see a train station on your right.  Go past the train station and turn to the left and park in the furthest northeast corner.  From where the curb intersects, go 88 feet east and there is where the letterbox will be.
