NY: Phantom Cat sighted in Buffalo!

From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2003-11-17 01:13:30 UTC
Hi all, I wanted to update everyone on Monsters of New York #5, the
Phantom Cat. Last I heard back in Aug, Delerium had found it but
noted some trail construction happening near the site. I think this
construction may have removed the signpost mentioned in the clues
and since then I have received a few reports this box couldn't be
found. Yesterday, Nov 15th, I got this note from Team Dakota:

"Phantom Cat is alive and well. I looked in the wrong trees for
quite some time. The construction clue made me think that the signs
had been taken down. But once on track again it was a great spot."

We're going to try to get the clues updated, which will be somewhat
of a challenge since we no longer live in NY, but hopefully we can
get the Cat back on track soon.

JeLyBean
