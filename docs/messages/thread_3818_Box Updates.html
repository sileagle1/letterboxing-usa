From: khatru (khatru24@yahoo.com) | Date: 2001-11-28 14:36:34 UTC-08:00
I have several items to report about boxes I've
visited within the last month:

The Fledglings Box on MT Pisgah in Northboro MA is
still there, however the trails have been re-marked
with new markers. The current instructions requiring
that you follow red dots will no longer work, the
red dots have been removed.

The box atop Mt Wachusett (central MA) was not sealed
properly and the log was soaked through and then
turned moldy. It is unusable in its current condition.

The Two Hearts Box at Purgatory Chasm in Sutton MA may
be missing. A thorough search of the area came up with
empty beer bottles in all hiding places close to the
area pointed to by the clue. The other three boxes in
that series are still there.

The Otis Redding and David Crosby boxes in Madison WI
are missing to the best of my knowledge. A thorough
serach of the area by a team of four people came up
with neither box. The woods area has been well turned
over, many trees were cut down.

The Majestic Theatre box in the center of Madison WI
is also missing. All of the trees and shrubs that were

on the State House lawn were removed for security
reasons.

The Aztalan Box in Aztalan WI is missing. The area
was cleaned of shrubs and many trees. There is nowhere
to hide a box anywhere near the coordinates given in
the clue.

K.
