Title: Fowl Play. Placed 11-16-03 by Salamander. Location:
Chillicothe, Ohio. Difficulty: Moderately Easy ?? CLUES: Go to the
section of the Chillicothe Bikeway/Pedestrian Corridor which runs
underneath the Bridge Street Bridge. Start at the east end of the
wrought-iron type fence which borders this part of the corridor.
Walk west, counting the larger, 3" by 3" posts in the fence. Stop at
the 6th post. Face south, away from the river. The box is hidden
among the rocks covering the slope ahead of you, about 2/3 of the
way up.
