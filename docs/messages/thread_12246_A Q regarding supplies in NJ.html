From: acahilly@prodigy.net (acahilly@prodigy.net) | Date: 2003-04-23 18:50:05 UTC
Are you near Paramus? You can get everything in one trip up Rt. 17.
Stop in Ramsey Outdoors for a compass; they have a nice selection.
Check out Michaels next door for stamping stuff. The selection's a
bit limited, but I know they carry the starter kit of carving tools
and a sheet of pink stuff. If you want more, continue on up 17 to
Pearl. They have a huge seletion of stamps and pads, and "the pink
stuff", too. In the linoleum block section you can get carving tools.
Oodles of artist books to choose from as a log.
Of course, Rubbermaid and baggies can come from anyplace.
