OR Did 2 boxes: Olive and Indian Creek

From: Susan Johnson (susan@kuku.org) | Date: 2004-07-31 23:19:57 UTC
We were staying at Edgefield in Troutdale, OR and bagged Olive, a
very easy box. I loved the stamp -- it was one of my favorites!

However, it was balanced by Indian Creek, a very stenuous but
rewarding box in Hood River.

We had the most trouble finding Union & 11th: "I've lived here 18
years and I don't know where Union is."

If we had looked at a map . . . When we found the streets, the clues
were wonderful, very accurate and clear.

The spur trail was extremely steep and slippery with dry dirt. But
going down was worth it: the box was fun, it was well hidden and the
stamp was great. This is definitely not a box that will be found by
a passer-by but only by boxers!

Kuku & Bisselator, Portland, OR
