Apples for the Pie Letterbox, Stow, MA

Many New England towns have a "first" of this kind, and Stow is no
exception. Find our "First", stand behind the middle section of the 3
paneled sign, and walk NW 24 steps to a small tree. Then head North 13
steps to the big oak and 4-5 steps to a small pile of large rocks near the
top of a wall where you will find your apple.

Bonus: If you go Oct 5th before 4:30pm, there is a bonus stamp for you in
the pie stand!
