Christi,
We have had reports back on Buffalo Jump and some of our others in
Montana and all are listed in place. Buffalo Jump keeps getting
found by non letterboxers that are Native Americans and I have had
some real interesting notes. All have been very supportive of our
hobby and this particular letterbox. We left a bunch in Cody, Wy.
and it seems as though a teacher in a third grade class has used
letterboxing in her class, so there is a bunch more there. Mtwalker
a new letterboxer in Mt. may be able to give you more precise info.
If you happen to hit any of ours please advise status.
