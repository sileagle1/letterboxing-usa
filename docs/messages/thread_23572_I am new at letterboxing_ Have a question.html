From: John Chapman (john@johnsblog.com) | Date: 2006-07-12 20:07:02 UTC-04:00
Many letterboxes have an inkpad but I think the majority do not. It's somewhat of a regional thing but overall it's best if you carry ink with you. A set of 24 Marvvy Brush Markers are wonderful. The ink stays wet longer than other watercolors so you have time to ink up a stamp with one or more colors before stamping your journal.
