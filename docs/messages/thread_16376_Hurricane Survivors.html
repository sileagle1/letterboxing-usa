Hurricane Survivors

From: warrioringilead (warrioringilead@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-10-14 11:19:12 UTC
just to let you know that Just Bass and Point Judith survived the
hurricane just fine.

The Music Man (one of the 4 Faces of Wickford) was totally destroyed
because the ink pad "exploded" and the last finder (letterboxer or
civilian) did not put the ziplocks on the logbook or ink pad.
Miraculously, the stamp was still there. I replaced the box, log
book, pen, ink pad, and put the original stamp back in there. The
original log book in on my kitchen table drying. I have contacted
Trish already. So if you go searching for "The Music Man", it is
still there, just repaired.
