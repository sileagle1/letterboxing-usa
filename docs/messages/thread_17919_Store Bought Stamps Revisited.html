Hi to all in LETTERBOX land,
My dog , Budster, was howling* when I read a [LBna] post on bought
stamps. Dogs have a special sense and I think he was sensing the way I
think about this, and maybe agreeing with me. Some bought stamps are
very nice and intricate enough to directly relate to the surroundings
where the letterbox is hidden . (the ones we have chosen) It is my
opinion that those who are creative and make their stamps are getting an
extra amount of satisfaction from this hobby. More power to them!
