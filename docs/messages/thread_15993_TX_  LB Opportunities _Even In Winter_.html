From: sileagle1 (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2003-09-24 18:13:35 UTC
Just wanted to entice Texas Letterboxers (and visitors) to
get out and find and/or plant boxes now that fall has arrived.
Since Texas is so big, most of us can't visit many of the
letterbox locations in a day, so I say plan a weekend campout
at one of the many nice State Parks. Central Texas is especially
loaded (Austin up through Waco) but Corpus Christi and East
Texas are also nice places to visit and have several boxes, too.
Fall and even Winter are great times to get outdoors in Texas
so I invite everyone to get out there!
