From: Dave Leach <dmleach@yahoo.com> (dmleach@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-01-27 14:28:32 UTC
My wife and I took a roadtrip this weekend to attempt the
Sewanee Domaine letterbox hidden near The University of the South in
central Tennessee. It is, by far, the most difficult and cryptic of
any box we've taken a stab at.
After a couple of hours of hunting, we had to abandon our search
to head back home. On the other hand, we loved exploring the
breathtakingly beautiful campus, and encourage anyone in the area
with the time to make the trek.
Has anyone been able to find this box? On the road back to
Atlanta we read through the rest of the clue and found there's some
cryptography to be done. In addition, we're not exactly sure what it
is we'll be doing with all the years we've been writing down. Any
nudges in the right direction would be greatly appreciated!
