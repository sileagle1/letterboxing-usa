Mardi Gras box in New Orleans

From: gryandrowansmum (gryandrowansmum@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-23 01:58:42 UTC
We took the kids to New Orleans today to see a parade, and on the way
down I carved a Mardi Gras stamp and made a little microbox. Clues
are on the main site, it was meant to be magnetic but I forgot glue
and had no way to attach the magnet.

Mardi Gras, by the way, is great!!!It was so warm, I have a little
sunburn, and the kids had a blast dancing for beads. DH and I shared
some chairs with some locals, who gave us beads, cups, beer and just
that amazing southern hospitality. Go,go,go if you have the chance.
