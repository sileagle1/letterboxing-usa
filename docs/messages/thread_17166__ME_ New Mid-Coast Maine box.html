(ME) New Mid-Coast Maine box

From: amymisha (amymisha@dialmaine.com) | Date: 2003-12-04 21:59:31 UTC
O, Christmas Tree! O, Christmas Tree! is a limited time only box.
I'll be keeping it out until we take our family tree down, on
January 15, 2004.

Take Route 27 off Route 1 toward Boothbay Harbor. Drive until
you get to the one and only stop light, and at that light take a left.
Follow Route 96 for a few miles, until you enter the tiny town of
East Boothbay, Maine.

On your right you will see a sign for "Mill Cove Overlook." Turn
right on that tiny turnaround and park there, just off Rt. 96.

Walk down to the crude wooden bench on the water's edge. Turn
right and walk about 20 steps. Turn right once more and walk 15
steps. You will find the box under low hanging tree branches,
near a white stone.

This is a very easy box, and great for kids. There is a winged
hitchhiker in the box for the first finder, if they should so want her

Dress warm! Enjoy! Email me at amymisha@dialmaine.com if
there are any problems.
