Name of the Letterbox: Julia's Monkey Box

Date Placed: August 24, 2002

State: Virginia

County: Nelson

Nearest Town: Wintergreen/Nellysford

Clues:

This letterbox can be found on the Upper Shamokin Falls Trail at Wintergreen Resort (www.wintergreenresort.com). Please see the friendly folks at the Trillium House Nature Center to get a trail map, which will direct you to the Upper Shamokin Falls Trail, which begins at the end of Laurel Ridge Road.

To find the letterbox, follow the yellow trail blazes to the Falls. Enjoy the Falls. With your back to the Falls, see the Trail sign that directs you to:

Loggers Alley Access
Loggers Alley Access to Upper Shamokin Gorge
To Chestnut Springs Trail.

Walk to the sign.
Once there, turn around 180 degrees, facing away from the sign.
Go 20 feet forward, to the big rock with a large split in it.
Stand on the big rock, facing the sign.
Look down and forward a bit, at about 1:00 on the clock.
