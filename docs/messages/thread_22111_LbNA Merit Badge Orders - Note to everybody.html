LbNA Merit Badge Orders

Just so you all know...the merit badge order form at the LbNA website is
wrong. It is the old form. Mohmers only takes orders for the logo patches ...

correct adddress.

To order merit badges
Please print & fill out the merit badge form located on the LBNA website
below BUT disregard all information that is different than what you read here:

LbNA Merit Badge Order Form
(no minimum order)

Size: ~1" diameter (Colors shown are approximate)

Available Letter Designations:
P = "Planted",
F = "Found",
X = "eXchanged",
E = Event stamps ,
S = # of States letterboxed.

Available Denominations:
10, 25, 50, 75, 100, 150, 200, 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900, 1000, 2000,
3000, 4000, 5000 .

Fill in circles on the form linked to below with Letter Designation and
Denomination.
