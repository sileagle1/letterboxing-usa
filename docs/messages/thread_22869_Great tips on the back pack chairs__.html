From: gallant_rogue (gallant_rogue@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-23 12:44:37 UTC
As the Gallant Rogue has a bad knee, he is unable to "squat" as he
stamps in to a letterbox. This put so much strain on my knees that
after one productive day of letterboxing I couldn't walk for a week!
So I investigated the BackPack/Stool combos like my friend Scarab
uses but instead I opted to buy a "mini stool" that clips on to my
present backpack.

I have purchase two types but the one I use all the time is called
"Slacker Stool" by XNation Sports which I bought thru Amazon.com for
$7.70 plus shipping... here are a couple of other online stores:


http://www.yuccadune.com/product1216.html

http://store.everestgear.com/123810.html

I have found that I don't have to detach it from my pack to set it
up. I just set my pack down and use the stool while it's still
tethered to my pack. It is very light and I quickly became
accustomed to having it attached to my pack.
