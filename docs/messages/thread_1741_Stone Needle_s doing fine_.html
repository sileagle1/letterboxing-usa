Stone Needle's doing fine.

From: John Rovetto (jrov@nji.com) | Date: 2001-01-16 01:35:16 UTC
After the 24 +/- inches of snow we had I took a trip up to the Stone
Needle letterbox. It's doing quite well under a lot of snow. The
gift certificate is doing alright too. It's just waiting for some
hungry soul to spirit it away to a local restaurant.

If you like winter sports, this is an ideal one to go for. There's a
cross-country ski and snowshoe rental facility right nearby and the
trails are maintained. I guess you could consider this a few more
hints. There's not too many places like this in NJ.

Don't forget, the 2nd to last instruction is 50 paces, not 60. I
used true north with a 11.5 degree west declination. When I was in
the US Forest Service I did some pacing on snowshoes. It works
pretty well. However, you can probably get away without them. It's
not too far from the trails head
Try giving me a call if you get it. My info is in the box. If I'm
available I'll join you for a drink.

Good luck,

John
