Just a word of caution to all future seekers. Be very wary of the Exeter Graves Superintendent. He is a very friendly man, yet very dedicated to this very historic cemetery. He came upon us as we were stamping in. We were able to convince him that we were avid history buffs and were researching the Mercy Brown story. His wealth of information is astounding. I do, however, believe that he would not receive the idea of a letterbox resting in "his" cemetery very well. Use caution and be alert when seeking this box.
