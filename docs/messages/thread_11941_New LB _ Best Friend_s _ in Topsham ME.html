BEST FRIEND'S LETTERBOX
TOPSHAM, MAINE

Clues and Terrain: Easy - drive by box

Directions to box:

Exit 24 from I-95 to Topsham.
Find the Dam.

Park in the lot for 19 Summer St.

Walk up the road, past the house.
See the fire hydrant on the right side of the street.
Turn left toward the water.
Look for a single white rock, on top of 2 black rocks.
The white rock has a tree behind it and 2 pines to the right of it.
Walk over 6 or 7 large rocks to the white rock.
Look low for the crevice which gently cradles "Best Friends"

Have Fun !
