> Two Birders Letterbox
> Placed September 8, 2002
> by L. and K. /twobirdrz
> Ellanor C. Lawrence Park
> Near Centreville, VA
> Fairfax County, VA
> Difficulty: Easy walk, easy directions
>
> Entering Ellanor C. Lawrence Park from Route 28 onto Walney Road, turn
left
> into Walney Pond parking lot. From the parking lot, follow the paved path
> to the right across a zig-zag walkway over the pond. Stay to the right on
> the mulch trail and turn left into the woods. Cross the wooden footbridge
> over the stream. At the Visitor's Center sign, take the path to the left
> instead of the steps leading up the hill. Bear left on the trail at the
> intersection of the trail and the service road to cross another wooden
> bridge. Starting at the two large logs lying perpendicular to one another
> at the right of the path, take another ten steps to stand by a large oak
> tree on the left. At its base, you will see some fallen limbs across a
> small footpath. Take the path about 25 yards up the hill until you reach
a
> slightly level space. You will see a large fallen tree about 10 paces
> directly to your left. The box is located in a cavity on top of the
fallen
> trunk and is covered with limbs and bark.
>
>
