New - OH - Engle Mill Bridge

From: geoflyfisher (geoflyfisher@yahoo.com) | Date: 2001-12-07 23:46:24 UTC
Name: Engle Mill Bridge
Difficulty: 1 (Drive by)
Near Spring Valley, Greene County, OH
Placed 7 December 2001 by
Flyfisher (geoflyfisher@yahoo.com)

This is the 5th and last covered bridge in Greene County. If you
have covered the boxes named "Covered Bridge," Massies Creek
Bridges," "Ballard Road Bridge" and this box you have the entire
set.

To get to the bridge, take 380 south out of Xenia for several miles
until it intersects with Spring Valley Paintersville Road. Turning
east, proceed 1.9 miles to Engle Mill Road. As you approach the
bridge, there is a turn off just before the bridge on the left side
of the road.

The box is on the other side of the bridge. Walk over the parallel
modern bridge and cross the grass to the south end of the covered
bridge. Turn to 140 degrees and take 4 paces. At the foot of the
tree, the box is under 2 rocks.
