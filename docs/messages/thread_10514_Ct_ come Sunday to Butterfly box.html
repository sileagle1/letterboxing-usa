Ct. come Sunday to Butterfly box

From: mothermoo2001 <cstearns07@hotmail.com> (cstearns07@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-01-30 02:39:48 UTC
I have a new box planted in honor /as a gift for Butterfly ( her
Birthday last week) only life gets complicated and she hasn't had a
chance to get there yet. So if anybody wants in on the initial find
with Butterfly meet us at the Diana's Pool parking area on Rte 198 in
Chaplin on Sunday at 12:30. This is not far from the new and
fun 'Melted" . We will come with the clues and we can all walk up
to the " Town Crier" letterbox. After Sunday's adventure we will
post the clues to the list for all to enjoy. Rte 198 is of Rte 6 at
Chapin pass the firehouse and Diana's Pool fishing area is on the
right. There is room for a few cars there. If you cross the river
you went to far. The day I planted this box I saw a whole flock of
Robins by the river along with a Bluebird! I don't know what they
could be surviving on in this long cold spell. The river ,the ice and
the Hemlocks make for some Stunning winter scenery. Come along for
just one more birthday blast!

If the weather is bad we will postpone as its a long drive in from
the shoreline.
Leader of the Pack -Carol
