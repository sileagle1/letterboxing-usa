RE: [LbNA] Las Vegas Strip LBs?

From: Melissa Barrick (mlbarrick@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-23 10:40:34 UTC-05:00
I just got home from Vegas last night!

The Triangle Building and Feeling Lucy are on the strip. No need for a car!
The Feeling Lucky stamp is crumbling and the log book was soaked. I took it
to my hotel room, dried it out and added another log book to the container.
But the stamp is still inkable and stampable so I say look for it!

Las Vegas Boneyard and Vegas Wedding are a $20 cab ride from the strip to
the park. You may want to have the driver wait as the boxes are fairly easy
to get - then it's another 20 min wait until they can get another cab back
to the park!
I couldn't find Vegas Wedding but I contacted the placer to let them know I
think it is missing. The hole was there - the box wasn't.
I would go to the park during the day. There seems to be a bunch of
transients that sleep in the park. But they left me alone.

Have a great time! Put $20 on Black for me!

From: Melissa Barrick (mlbarrick@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-23 18:37:51 UTC-05:00
Feeling Lucky is alive...barely but alive. I added a make shift log book
from some cash register tape the Venetian gladly gave me!
I dried out the old log book but it's in pretty sorry shape. It looks like
the last entry was on the 10th of August and I found it on the 21st, soaking
wet. Would love to see what the new stamp looks like! Maybe in a few
months after I recuperate from this trip.

Boneyard logbook was in fairly good shape. I think Vegas Wedding is missing
all together. The hole was completely empty, I saw the red rock but no
signs of the box, stamp, or log book!
