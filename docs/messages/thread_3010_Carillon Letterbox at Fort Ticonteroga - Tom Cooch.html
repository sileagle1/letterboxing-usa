From: Tom and Lisa Ouellette (tro4@earthlink.net) | Date: 2001-08-05 19:53:35 UTC-04:00
Dear Tom,

Thank you for your 7/15/01 reply concerning the Carillon letterbox at Fort
Ti. Sorry it has taken so long for us to reply. Tonight was the first
night since the beginning of July that we have searched for your message.
We look forward to returning to Fort Ti to try to find the letterbox again.

Next week our family heads up to Acadia National Park and we noticed that
you placed some letterboxes there, too! We plan to try to find at least
three of them. I (Lisa) grew up on Mt.Desert Island, so I know the area
well. However, I'm not sure I'll be able to figure out the Beehive! We
will let you know how successful we are at finding them.

Thanks again for your reply.

Lisa and Tom Ouellette

From: Tom Cooch (tcooch@sover.net) | Date: 2001-08-06 06:12:13 UTC-04:00
Dear Tom & Lisa,

Thanks for your message. Good luck with the Acadia boxes. Thunder Hole is
missing; Beehive is very hard. Cadillac and Sand Beach are not hard if you
work out the cryptic parts in advance. You may have noticed that there is a
way to check your trivia answers to Cadillac on-line; you can look at that
page without having answers given away, and I definitely recommend you do
this before you try for Cadillac.

Thanks again. Let me know how you fare.

Tom
