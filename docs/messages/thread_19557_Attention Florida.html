Attention Florida

From: devonpeter (devonpeter@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-23 18:10:22 UTC
OK---So I have just returned from a trip to the Pacific
Northwest....ahh what a place. We went sightseeing, hiked & went
letterboxing for 8 days. Needless to say being from flat Florida
those hills just about killed me! But none the less I had the best
time. Now that I am back in Tampa I want to continue. But there
are so few letterboxes here :( So I am sending out a challenge to
all of those who live here or are visiting. Get up, get out, & move
your butts! I am committing to placeing 10 letterboxes in the next
30 days! How about that. I am also interested in getting together
for a Florida swap! I know you guys are out there, ok so its hot &
it rains a little but our state has year round activities so there
is no reason not we can't compete with the rainy Northwest! Please
take this as a friendly challenge & let me know what you all think :)

Nicole-- devonpeter@yahoo.com
