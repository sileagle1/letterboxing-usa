New postal letterbox

From: Rayvenhaus (rayvenhaus@myndworx.com) | Date: 2003-11-15 13:28:20 UTC-08:00
To commutate the placement of the "Curse of the Black Pearl" series, we've made a special postal letterbox. This letterbox, called "Aztec Gold" contains the completed stamp that would be put together from the series here in Washington. If you live in this area, (Washington state, Pierce/King/Lewis counties) and want to get the stamps yourselves, then, please do not add you name to this list. If, however, the chance of you making it to Washington state in the near future is slim to none, then by all means, send me, offlist of course, your mailing address. Email rayvenhaus at myndworx dot com with this info.

We'll send this letterbox on it's way in 2 weeks, Dec 1st, 2003. One of the requirements of this postal box is that each person is to email me and let me know when it has arrived at each stop so we don't lose track of it. This is all that I ask.

We hope that you will enjoy this stamp and it's Treasure Box.
