Well...it's a postal letterbox....which means that it is a letterbox that comes to you in the mail rather than you having to go out in the wild and find it. This particular one is based on the books by Ann Brashares. Other people send out some based on other subjects.

This is a good group to check out if you have questions about postal letterboxes:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/postal_letterboxing/
