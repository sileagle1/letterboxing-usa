Marathon 4th of July weekend HH Wizard news

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2002-07-07 23:25:21 UTC
I had a marathon weekend with 24 boxes from 4th to 7th. I tackled
boxes in Tacoma and Seattle WA and made a return quest to Priest
Point in Olympia WA. I got skunked there last time. This time I did
find "Thanksgiving" box ( yummy pie in the rain) but still am a Loon
less Boxer. Thunder threatened and rain ate my clue sheet. Perhaps
a third time will be a charm. I did find a great "walking and poking
stick"

Totally missed the "Slug" box in Lincoln Park. This was a good thing
as I had found Modern Gypsy's "Wizard Hitchhiker" in the "Stump"
box.

The Wizard had been going round and round the park. Slug was going to
be my last box in the park and therefore Wizards new home. Because I
missed slug Wizard now resides north from Lincoln Park in another
city park.

Cheers
Hoopoe
Federal Way WA

From: (edugaytor@aol.com) | Date: 2002-07-07 22:26:12 UTC-04:00
I place my clue sheets in page protectors in a flexible soft plastic binder both to keep them safe from the elements and to keep records.  The binder is light and easy to carry, and I keep a pencil case inside with a couple pens, mechanical pencils and lead, eraser, my compass, a small ruler, and sticky notes -- everything you need for a successful hunt protected and at the ready.  And best of all, with the exception of the page protectors, everything came from the dollar store!

Rand
