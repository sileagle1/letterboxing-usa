Re: May 31 VA/MD/DC/DE/PA Gathering

From: Mary from Virginia (daughteroftheolddominion@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-08 12:43:31 UTC
There are 6 boxes at Riverbend now, I believe all planted by
Waterbird. I planted a new box but I'm waiting until we are closer to
the event to post the clues - keep your eyes open for this one. Be
sure to bring all the clues with you.

For those of you not familiar with Riverbend Park, here are the
directions to get there:

Riverbend Park may be reached by taking Beltway Exit 44 which is
Georgetown Pike west to a right on Riverbend Road (about 4.5 miles,
first street after the stoplight into Great Falls Park). From
Riverbend Road turn right on Jeffery Road and follow for approximately
1 mile to the park entrance.

We are all bringing food to share. We have had some promises: BeMa
is making her Trailer Trash macaroni salad. I've had the privilege of
testing it and it's very tasty. But when I had it she made it with
farafelle which is pretty upscale for Trailer Trash so she may need to
find a new name for that dish. :) Psychomommy says she will make her
famous lemon pound cake. I don't remember if this is what she brought
to the Richmond gathering so I can't give any review of this cake but
I can tell you that she makes a fabulous cheesecake so I have every
expectation that the pound cake will be great.

It promises to be a fun time - great boxes, great food, and most of
all the greatest people.

See everyone on the 31st.

Mary
