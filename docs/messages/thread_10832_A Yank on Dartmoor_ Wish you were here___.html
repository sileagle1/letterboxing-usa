Hi there,
just thought that some of you in the states would like to
know that a member of this group.. letterboxer2002... has arrived
from the states safe and well and is currently camping in Princetown,
Dartmoor.On Sunday we took him to Cranmere Pool. It was freezing
cold, the ground was frozen but alas no snow.
Today he has been out and about collecting large numbers of stamps
and having a ball!
The Dartmoor Letterboxing Club is currently taking good care of
Letterboxer2002 and I hope he returns home with a mass of stamps. I f
any of you have plans to come to Dartmoor, just let me know and we
will do the same for you. You never know a group of us limeys might
just visit the states one day to collect a few of your stamps.
Hope the big freeze soon thaws over there, we have the same problem
over here when it snows. Because of all the rocks on Dartmoor you
have to be so careful where you put your feet!. Usually the boxes are
weel frozen in under the rocks too.
I will put a photo of letterboxer2002 on the photo page for you all
to see. Hope he doesn't mind!
Cheers,
