We had a lovely evening in Providence on Saturday, finding the
H2OFire Letterbox before a wonderful dinner. The box was in good
condition, though surrounded by quite a bit of trash that makes me
think it might be discovered soon if groundskeepers take any pains at
all to clean up the area.

Very clever clues to this letterbox! It was actually our 2nd attempt
at finding it: We were cocky and figured that since we knew
Providence quite well we thought that we knew exactly where it was
when we read the clues, rather than actually following the clues as
written. Well, we were nowhere near the box on our first try before
it started getting dark, so we returned, humbled, followed the clues
as written, and found it easily.

Thanks, Chip!
