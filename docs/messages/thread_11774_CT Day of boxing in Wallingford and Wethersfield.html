We set out today in the mid afternoon when we realized that the snow
was going to hold off Spinglike conditions for at least another week.
We successfully located:

Wallingford, HOPE - what a tremendous Stamp. WOW. My daughters
enjoyed walking the labyrinth while I stamped in. (Through 7 & 4 year
old eyes - 100% of the fun is in the hunt.) Was well hidden, and we made
a point of hiding it well again. (Hopefully HOPE will not suffer the same
rock-disappearing issues that Double-Happiness faces from time to time.)
I was amazed at the number of people who stamped in given how young
this box is amount of time.

Wethersfield:
The George Washington and Butoff Williams House Boxes. Sadly,
the Cove park was closed - so we needed to save the Red Onion for
another day.

Peace to all. And to other Connecticut Yankees - dont put those snow
throwers away just yet!
