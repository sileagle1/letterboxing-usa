Great Seal Letterbox

From: lizard612002 (lizard612002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-09-01 01:44:55 UTC
The Great Seal Letterbox is located in the Great Seal State Park
near Chillicothe, Ohio in Ross County.It was placed on 8-20 -02 by
Salamander. CLUES: Take the Marietta Road entrance to the Ireland
Picnic Area. Find the southeast corner of the Ireland Picnic
Shelter.From here take a reading of 135 degrees and walk 25 paces (1
pace=2 steps) to the edge of the woods. You should see a small stump
immediately to your left. From this stump, go 11 paces at 150 degrees
to a large maple tree. If you look behind the tree, you will find
that the bottom of the trunk is hollow. Here you will find the
letterbox. NOTE: this microbox does not have a stamp pad.
DIFFICULTY: EASY.
