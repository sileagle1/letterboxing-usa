"It's a BOB thing" micro-letterbox
(Melbourne, Florida)
Rated: Easy (Drive up)
State: Florida
County: Brevard
Location: Melbourne, Florida
Planted By: Bob's Grand children 11/07/2002

The "It's a BOB thing" letterbox was created to mark my father's 75th
birthday. My brother's and our families had gather to celebrate this
milestone in Melbourne and after explaining Letterboxing and finding
no letterbox near by, we decided to create one. The art is from one
brother, the stamp carving is by another. The Grand children did the
hiding.

This is a MICRO letterbox so please bring your own stamp pad.

Directions:
The letterbox is located at the intersection of Rt. 192 and the
Indian River (intercostal), on the Western shore at the Claude Edge
Front Street Park. Beyond the boat launch you'll find a lovely garden
by the house where girls meet. Follow one of the paths to the waters
edge and look for a water fountain. It is there you will find what
you seek.
