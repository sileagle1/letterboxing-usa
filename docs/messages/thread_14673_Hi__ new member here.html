From: (cadenza74@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-07-30 02:56:54 UTC-06:00
Hi Kathie,

Welcome to a wonderful hobby! You are going to love it. I was hooked last
spring and have become a woman obsessed since :-) Some resources that I
found helpful are the getting started link on the LBNA website
(www.letterboxing.org) and a letterboxer named Ryan has a page that he did
that has some of the straight up as far as answering some of the questions
that some don't always dare ask as newbies. It is located at
http://www.geocities.com/rscarpen/Lb/Docs/Newbies/ Ryan is currently hiking
the Appalachian Trail, so if you see references to Ryan or "the Green
Turtle" that's who they are talking about.

Hope to see you on the trail. Oh, and while I'm here I'll put in a
shameless plug. If you ever make it out to Idaho, let me know. If you are
still interested in ghost towns, I am planting a series next week in a ghost
town here in ID.

Happy hunting!
Cadenza
