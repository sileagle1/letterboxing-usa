If any NoVA-area letterboxers happen to come to Worldgate Lights Up the
Holidays tonight in Herndon (5-8pm), look for us for a personal stamp
exchange! We're all wearing red/reddish tops and will have volunteer
sweatshirts, because we're lighting candles. I have my hair up in a bun
(sort of) and am wearing penguin earrings and black pants with a tan
geometric print.

If you weren't already planning to come, join us! It's free, there are
snacks, and there's music and lots of fun stuff to do, for kids in
particular. Lots of parking. And there's partial coverage in case it
rains. Near the intersection of 606/Elden/Centreville & 267/Dulles Toll
Road.

Was hoping to make a special stamp for the occasion, but didn't have
time :-< Maybe next year. Hope to see at least one of you!
