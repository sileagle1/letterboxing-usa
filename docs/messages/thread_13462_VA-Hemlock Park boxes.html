Only managed to get to the three boxes at Hemlock park over the
weekend, but that was such a beautiful hike I didn't mind. Saw quite
a few turtles, frogs, herons - it was wonderful! All three boxes in
good shape.

Bit of a warning though - with all this rain several sections of the
path are flooded. You can still get to the boxes, but you have to
leave the trail a few times for some high ground. We slipped and
slided quite a bit and almost landed in the water once, but had a
great time. : ) Thankfully I actually remembered dry socks and shoes
to change into this time!

One question - the clues say to turn right and go down the path...it
was a left. Did we read something wrong?
