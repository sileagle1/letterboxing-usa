Honor Liberty in OR

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-11-11 01:00:44 UTC

This afternoon Don and Gwen's well-traveled Hitch Hiker, Honor Liberty found it's
latest temporary home in Portland. It is residing quiety in the vacant home of Tribute.
Although the box by Wingnut appears to be MIA, I could think of no more appropriate
place to leave this HH. A gentle sweep of loose dirt will reveal the box.

For those who are not familiar with Honor Liberty, it is meant to be moved as quickly
as possible. To facilitate this, Don and Gwen have requested that it's hiding places be
posted on the chat lists. It is currently beginning it's third year of travels.

Since Tribute is missing, I was unable to stamp its image into the HH logbook. If the
person who picks up this hitcher has a copy of the stamp, it would be wonderful if
they scanned the image and pasted it into the book. If you need a copy, I can make a
scan at my end and email it to you. Wingnut could probably do the same. Pick up this
touching stamp! Funhog
