Pandamonium at Morgan Zoo


http://www.psychokiwi.org/letterboxing/Pandamonium.htm

(A mystery series - feel free to join in and add to the zoo, but please keep
in the mystery spirit.)

Newsflash! The bars are down, the walls have tumbled, and all the animals
have escaped their keepers at Morgan Zoo (Bates Woods Park, New London, CT).
Rumor has it, they have split into groups, spread out in all directions, and
have gone to ground hiding. Can you track them down and help them in their
bid for freedom???


Zoe Zebra


News just in from Zoe Zebra (who got a message out on the
catbird-underground-gossip-train) that the Polar Bears and she were seen by
the keepers making their way up Route 32 under cover of darkness. They took
fright, ran into the woods, and aren't sure where they are. They are all
quakering with fear! But then they found a message to back-track from A very
nice rock (luckily she found a Lantern to help her find the way in the
dark). Exhausted they fell asleep, and at daybreak saw they were under an
old beech tree. From there they followed a white trail through thick laurel,
skirted the bottom of a steep ledge, then trotted down hill. The trail
turned left at a triple-trunked oak tree. Immediately behind the tree, they
noticed a square glacial erratic. About 10 steps in from the trail Zoe found
a zebra-sized hiding spot in another smaller erratic behind the square
erratic. Thankful, she crawled in and hid.


Polar Bearing


There was no room for the polar bears so they went further a-field, leaving
the following incoherent message with Zoe (you know how polar bears get when
they are tired and scared!). "After Zoe hid, we met up with 9 laughing
penguins and 5 happy leprechauns and danced away. Then 14 angry tigers
chased 23 cheery unicorns down the path! We followed from a safe distance.
Then we chased 24 smelly mice and 4 nasty hob-goblins up a hill. Exhausted,
we spied a lovely big rock and found a hidey-hole in the base. I hope
someone finds us soon!"


Kuddly Koala


We also hear that the Kuddly Koala has hopped on a Qantas flight back to Oz
and is hiding out in a Queensland safe-house. We hope to make contact soon.


Dancing Dolphin


Also to hand, the Dancing Dolphin was smuggled into New Zealand where he is
hiding in a Wellington safe-house awaiting further instructions.

Stay tuned for further details - you'll read them here first!
