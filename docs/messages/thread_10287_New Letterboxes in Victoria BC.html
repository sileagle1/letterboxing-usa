The Beacon & Jester Micro Letterboxes, Victoria, BC Canada
Placed January 11, 2003 by the Secret Gardeners.
This letterbox follows the 20 Bucks Letterbox placed by the Canada Goose
last July.
After putting 20 Bucks safely back, circle round the bay and find your way
up to the memorial benches at Finlayson Point. Follow six " zigzags " from
the edge and poke below to find the Beacon. Note this is very busy spot and
you may need to be patient, also - we hope you are not afraid of dogs!!!!!!
Now look up and salute the flag, head that way and when you reach the pole,
search out Taylor Dawn's teddy bear. Sit with the teddy and appreciate the
view. In front and left you will spot a good size piece of granite framed by
broom. Check the feet and you will meet the Jester.
