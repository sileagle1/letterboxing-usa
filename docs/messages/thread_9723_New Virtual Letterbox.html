Re: New Virtual Letterbox

From: SpringChick <letterbox@attbi.com> (letterbox@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-01-03 23:18:08 UTC
Okay, a couple of things about the virtual box I posted yesterday...

First, there seems to be some confusion about the "national flower"
designation for this country (question for stamp #2). In rechecking
my original sources, one site indicates it is the national flower,
another that it is the "unofficial" national flower... I have added
this clarification to the clue and also added another hint that
should help to identify it.

Second, yes I realize this is not easy. It was my intent that there
would be some research and reasoning involved rather than just
hopping from one site to the next. It has been my frustration on the
virtual boxes that I have done, that not much deductive reasoning was
required. Just as there are clues to physical letterboxes that can
be at times obscure and leave you scratching your head while you read
between the lines trying to find your way, so is this. In that
regard, maybe it is not for everybody.

But... Don't despair, it is not impossible! I tested it last week
on two of my [adult] daughters who are not letterboxers and they both
were able to get through it with a bit of trial and error. There are
a few people from this list who have gotten the first stamp...

Hopefully you will find the stamps to be worth your effort once you
have completed it.

ENJOY!!!

SpringChick
