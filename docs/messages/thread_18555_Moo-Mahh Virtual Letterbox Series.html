Over the course of the past few weeks, there were several people who
got stuck trying to complete the task for stamp #3 in the Moo-Mahh
virtual series because the required search at WebShots no longer
yielded the indicated results. I tried uploading a few photos of my
own on the subject to my WebShots account, including the indicated
keywords, but they never got indexed (apparently they have too many
photos and are not able to index all of them).

Anyway while I was trying to figure out how to change the clues to
allow a person to get past this, the situation fixed itself and the
clues once again work as they were written.

So if you gave up because you weren't getting anywhere on #3, please
try again.
