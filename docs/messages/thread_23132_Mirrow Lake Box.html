From: pioneer.spirit (RMORGAN762@aol.com) | Date: 2006-03-08 04:26:03 UTC
Out of seven I've found this week, three were clearly visable peeking
out of rocks or bark. No doubt well hidden before the freezing and
Winter winds takes it's toll. Things well hidden by follage and grass
in the Summer become real obvious by now.

I've noticed many places were very busy with folks wondering about in
this un-Winter like weather we are having this year. Some could be
easily found when placed too near firepits at camp grounds where folks
search the woods and find a box hidden under tempting firewood. Too
much cover can also tip off folks, "I wonder why there is a pile of
rocks here alone?"
