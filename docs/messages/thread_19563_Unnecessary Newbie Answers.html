From: cpascott (seh-letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-07-26 15:57:50 UTC
Well, I was trying to be your guardian angel urging you over to the
newboxers board as I've witness the uh, bellicosity that is aimed
toward "newbie" questions around here.

Most (99% in fact) of the letterboxers here, new and old, are more
than willing to help new boxers and answer questions. It's just
that some are less willing to answer the same question multiple
times. That might seem unfair to a new person here who doesn't know
how else to get the answer, but on this board its the way of
things. On the other board, plenty of new and old boxers hang out
to provide answers and none mind repetitive questions. So come on
over, and ask away.

In the meantime, however, check out the following sources of
information ... you'll likely find most of what you seek there:

The LbNA FAQ -- http://www.letterboxing.org/faq/faq.html
Silent Doug's website -- http://www.letterboxing.info/
The "Folder of Useful Information" --
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-usa/files/A%20Folder%20of%
20Useful%20Information/
The Newboxers group -- http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/

Welcome to letterboxing (from all of us ... really!)

CPAScott
