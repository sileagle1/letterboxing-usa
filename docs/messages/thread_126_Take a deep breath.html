From: Daniel Servatius (elf@pclink.com) | Date: 1998-12-18 16:46:51 UTC-06:00
Take a deep breath everyone. There is tension in the air right now.
I'm at fault too. I've had a side-bar going with some of the technical people
in which I have been overly emotional lately. There's enough strife going on in
the world right now. Let's try our best to keep our little piece of peace
here. Its been really nice in that regard. I see nothing wrong with chastising
the guy who came in with the political statements recently. I took him off the
list as he requested.

Recently I have become disenchanted with Adrian also because I felt that we were
being blamed for some of the squabbles they are having over in Dartmoor. But I
want to remind everyone (myself included) that this is an open list. If people
are comfortable with Adrian or some anarchist (or yes, even a libertarian Mitch)
then let's keep the faith that whatever issues beset us will be ironed out. I
just want to say that I feel deeply comitted to the idea of the open forum in
which everyone is valued and can participate at the level they are most
comfortable.

So let's figure this out together. Let's keep it fun, and let's keep the
discussions healthy and robust without defaming anyone's character.
Personally, my main hope is that this not be a spring-board for whining and
complaining whether about political views or letterbox issues - that it would
mainly be a forum for constructive input to make letterboxing more fun, more
participative and more interesting.

Thanks for hanging in there with the group all you who are still hanging in
there with us.

- Dan
