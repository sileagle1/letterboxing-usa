"Leave a Moosage" Box
Central Maine
Nearest town:Dover-Foxcroft
Planted July 18, 2002
By Floating Feather

A very short walk for all ages.

Directions: Between Sangerville and Dover-Foxcroft.(the roads name is Pine
Rd., on the Dover side, and Douty Rd. on the Sanerville side.)Look for a
sign near a bridge that reads: "Forest of Dreams-Meadow of Enchanment"

Clues: Bring a picnic or have a make believe one at Lucy's Lunch stand.
Beware of the pirates! Be ready to play croquet or badminton. Behave, or
there is a "Time Out Tree". Brave the Kissing Bridge and "leave your
moosage" behind the first tree on the right.
