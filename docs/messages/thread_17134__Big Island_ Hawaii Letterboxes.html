our vacation!

Yes, unfortunately the mystery box did burn in a wildfire last July--
the fire occured about 5 days before we visited. All we found that
remained of the letterbox was the metal logbook spiral spine that
goes to a sketchpad logbook in the exact location as described in
the clues--it was in about 2' deep of ashes. The only thing left in
the area was a mostly charred skeleton of a tree, a bit of a 75%
burned Pepsi can and a singed vehicle hubcap. We attempted to
notify the placer with the sad news but never got a response. Glad
you enjoyed the other letterboxes. Oh, what we'd do to be able to
go back there for a bit... ;)
