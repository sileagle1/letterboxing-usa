HH Being launched at Molly Day Gathering

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-05 21:33:38 UTC-05:00
On Sunday April 18th at the Molly Day Gathering which is going to be held at
Diana's Pool in Chaplin CT, a hitchhiker called "Molly Looking for Ginger
will be launched. The object of this Hitchhiker is to reach Ginger the
Golden Retriever Letterboxer in Vernonia Oregon. To give it a proper
sendoff, Anyone at the Molly Gathering will be able to stamp in. we want to
get some good East Coast stamps in the log so when it gets to Oregon they
will be able to see what us East Coast people have for stamps. Much more
information about the hitchhiker, and Ginger will be available in my next
post, at the gathering and on my website next week. Hope you all can make it
Sunday. This is a very letterbox rich area. Should have a good day of
boxing.....Chuck and Molly
