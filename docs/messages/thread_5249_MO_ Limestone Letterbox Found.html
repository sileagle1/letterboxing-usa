HI Shorewood Folks!

We stopped at the Burr Oak area and found your limestone box on our way up
to Mount Rushmore. Thanks for a great hike...there are an abundance of
letterboxes and geocaches in this area and we wished we could have found them
all but it was getting dark and we had far to go. The Limestone box was the
only one we had time for with the kids. :o)

The ziplock had been chewed a bit by critters and the log book and tidy
little labels were soaked. We stamped in the best we could and added a
smaller ziplock to put the log and labels in. Hoping to keep them from
getting much wetter. You may want to dry it all out before it begins to mold.

Thanks again,

Mohmers and gang!!!!!
