September 19 every year is International Talk Like a Pirate Day.

I encourage all to plant on box on or near that day, and write the clues in
Pirate Talk.

An example may be found in the clues for Leapin' Lovers - western NC.

Or Google "talk like a pirate." You'll be amused, if not amazed....
