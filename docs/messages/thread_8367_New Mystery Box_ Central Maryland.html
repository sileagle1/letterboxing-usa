Name : LOST DIRECTIONS BOX

Planted : October 13, 2002
Planted by : Psychomommy and The Turtle
Location : Central Maryland

Michael woke up in a sweat. He was overcome with a terrible
feeling that his brother, Marc, was in distress. Michael wanted to
bring his dog, Target, but decided that bringing his pet wasn't a
smart idea.
Going south he saw Marc. Marc was racing east, then suddenly
Michael saw him running west. What was happening?
Obviously, Marc needed help. He said he had lost his box of
directions. They searched all day. Michael insisted the box was east.
Marc said no, it had to be west. Michael knew he was correct. Finally
Michael insisted he had to go home, take a bath and go to bed.
Marc is still racing around. Can you help him find his lost box?
There is a pile of rocks, please rehide carefully.


The best time to look for this box is on a Saturday or Sunday.
Don't even attempt this on a weekday.
