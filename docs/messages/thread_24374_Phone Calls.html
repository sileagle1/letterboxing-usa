You must not call my home during the dinner hour.

are not relevant to my decision in this matter.

etiquette.

garbled. The only word that I understood was something
about last spring. Perhaps finishing your dinner
before making phone calls would be advisable? Using
the correct names and titles of your suspects would
also be of much benefit.

Truly,
