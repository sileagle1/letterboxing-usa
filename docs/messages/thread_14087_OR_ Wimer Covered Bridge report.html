OK. That's just freaky. My son and I were under that bridge a little less than two weeks ago.

We had seen the box was missing but went to look for it anyway. There were a couple of cars that went over while we were under there and it was strange to hear them thumping and creaking overhead. I briefly thought about what would happen if it collapsed while a car was going over us but I shrugged it off as 'never gonna happen'. Now, 12 days later...

There are a couple of sites that have pictures of the collapsed bridge. It's pretty much gone. The whole span collapsed.
