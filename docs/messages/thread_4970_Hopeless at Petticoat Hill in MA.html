Hopeless at Petticoat Hill in MA

From: Bernie Mulligan (bfmulligan@mindspring.com) | Date: 2002-04-11 21:38:57 UTC-04:00
We could not possibly find either the lost hat or the lost mitten. Too many
places matched the clues. We counted two stone walls crossing the trail and
that was the least of the problems. Most of the clues had to do with
boulders. However, the number of boulders was mind-boggling. What is a
table rock anyway? And most trees situated on a hill do fall in the same
general direction when it's their time to go. It was a nice place though.
