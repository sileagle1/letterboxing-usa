Beware, beware in Exeter town
of the vampire we call Mercy Brown

She lies beneath the cold, hard ground
awaiting a boxer to move her around

(Are you scared yet?)You get to help me out by choosing your favorite
of the next two stanzas.

Not from your neck will she want to drink
but the boxes she'll visit to slurp up the ink
(Boop, Katybug, Trish and I like that one)

It's not your neck she'd like to seek
but the stamps of more boxes at which to peek
(That one,being more family friendly, is Ladybugs favorite)

One knows not where she's next to hide
so be on your guard as she looks for a ride.

This will be a unique set of boxes because there will be two, bearing
the same title, Mercy Brown, Exeter Vampire. They'll lie together,
awaiting the first boxer to take the hitchhiker for a ride.
For some background info on this Rhode Island Legend see;
http://members.tripod.com/~DominiquePerot/index.htm
http://www.endofline.com/Art/Exeter.html and,
http://www.velocitychannel.com/stories/vampire.html

Look for clues in a week or so, along with the list of credits for
this one.
Till then,
Bob,wondering where to find Buffy
