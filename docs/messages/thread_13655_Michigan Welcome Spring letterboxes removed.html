Michigan Welcome Spring letterboxes removed

From: clickermom3 (clickermom3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-18 16:38:43 UTC
I removed the Welcome Spring letterboxes from their hiding places at
Kensington Metropark today. They were placed as temporary boxes, and
I was concerned they'd be discovered and destroyed. I was pleased to
see how many 'boxers had signed into the logs. (thanks Springchick
for retrieving the remains of box #1 and placing them at the #2
location) The stamps may reappear next spring! In the meantime, I am
working on a "Notable Dogs" box series, the first of which will be
Greyfriars Bobby (to be placed in the next week or so). The Bobby
stamp is not carved, but home made with the rubber stamp making kit
from Artisan's choice.I will post clues when the box is placed.
