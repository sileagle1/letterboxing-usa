Rated easy , intended as an introduction for the kids at Southeast
school but because the park abuts the school it is open to all.

Planted 2-21-01 by Cock-A-Doodle-Moo 4-H vacation week

Take Rte 89 at Mansfield Center Drive past Southeast School and
Park at the ballfields.

Hut one , hut 2 , blitz ,and touchdown............ off the field
look between touchdown and the Golden Gate. About half way between is
a tree which is full of information. Behind it is what you seek.
Stamp privately and return as you found it for the next letterboxer.
