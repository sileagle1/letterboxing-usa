From: be ma (bema57@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-07-14 11:46:58 UTC
Hi Susan! Tell me the decision of which fast food to go to still isn't
determined by the Happy Meal toy... The more things change.... Your teens
sound like my little ones. I won't be able to break the news to Da that the
whining isn't going to stop as soon as we had hoped...

Sorry about the confusion with the two groups. We would look forward to
seeing all of you!

We're getting ready for a big BBQ at home so no boxing this past week. We
did check out a local park as a possible plant sight for "peace of DC".
Unfortunately, it looks like one of those weird it's on the map but you
can't quite get to it parks. We kept tryinlg all the different roads in the
subdivision but they would end at houses. I suppose the park is right
beyond.... You may only be able to access it from a bike path. The roads
are very odd too. You would enter from one side and then the road would
just end. Then you'd drive all around to the other side of town and the
same road would pick up again -- never to meet and never to intersect the
park! What a mystery box that would make!! We like to plant our boxes
pretty near by so we can check them often. Not only do we love seeing the
notes from people who have stamped in, but that's how we get most of our
HH's it seems.

Thanks for the note! BeMa
