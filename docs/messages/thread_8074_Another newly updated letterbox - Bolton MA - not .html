Whitcomb Garrison, Bolton, MA
Start at the sweetest road in Bolton, near 495. Drive a bit, and then a
sweet arrow to the right means stop and park for you. Look for the small
fenced area and then check out the garrison map. Stand in Josiah's living
room with your back to the sign. Four steps forward and some bricks lead
you to the Garrison for your book.
