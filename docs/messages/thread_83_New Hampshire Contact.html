Hi Danielle,

You happen to live in an area that is good for letterboxing.
Vermont has 8 or 10 letterboxes and New Hampshire has
at least one other letterbox enthusiast (Nisa). Nisa's e-mail
address is mailto:teapot@teapot.mv.com

If you want to see the spread for Vermont's letterboxes
go to the web page at http://www.pclink.com/elf/
and click on the State of Vermont on the right-hand U.S. map.
Vermont's contact person (Erik & Susan Davis) happen to be
cofounders with me of Letterbox USA. Their e-mail address
is mailto:davisarc@wcvt.com

Welcome aboard. Their is a lot of useful information for you
at the page mentioned above. My hope is that each person who
joins will submit one or two letterboxes and then eventually
we will all have something to search for. The long-term goal
of course is to have a variety of letterboxes to search for
all over the country. Realistically, it may be a few years
before we reach that point. We started this last April and
already there are abouit 45 letterboxes in 10 different states.

There is no shortage of enthusiasm and willingness to help out.
We have a lot of nice people in our group who contribute their
ideas and resources. If you have any questions don't hesitate
to ask any of us. We are all willing to help.

Hint: Try the Group Mail option at the page mentioned above.
