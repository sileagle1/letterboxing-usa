VA: The Heron and the Turtle LBs (Henrico County)

From: (clovering13@wmconnect.com) | Date: 2004-05-30 11:52:13 UTC-04:00
Schizokeet, Disclava, DMB and I were letterboxing in Henrico County yesterday
and our last LBs of the day were The Heron and the Turtle series in Three
Lakes Park.

Apparently the park ranger or someone had found the Turtle microbox on the
trail (tiny logbook not in good shape), went online to look up the series and
replaced it in The Heron box (which was very nice when they could have not cared
and thrown it away).

Schizokeet had an extra small notebook and ziploc baggy with her, and she and
Disclava moved the box to near its original location (too big for the small
cavity in the tree, its original location).

If anyone is planning on finding it this weekend, the New Clues for now are:
(Follow original clues until you reach the fishing pier). From the top of
fishing pier, take about 15 steps diagonally at 360 degrees. Go down into the
gully. Turn right. The Turtle box is at the base of a poplar tree (do not go
as far as the 3-in-1 tree).
