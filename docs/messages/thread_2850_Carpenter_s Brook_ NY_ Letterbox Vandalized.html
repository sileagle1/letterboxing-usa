Carpenter's Brook, NY, Letterbox Vandalized

From: stgeorge (stgeorge@gateway.net) | Date: 2001-07-16 18:08:09 UTC-04:00
    Last week I conducted a crash course on letterboxing for some teachers from the Baldwinsville area.  Today the group went in search of the Carpenter's Brook Letterbox.  'Kokopelli' informed me the box had been vandalized!
    The group found the letterbox intact, with all of its initial contents including the carved stamp, with the exception of the log-pages stamped by previous visitors to the site.  The pages had been torn from the log, and in place were found obscene comments. ( I'm so disappointed for my check of the box a  month ago showed visitors from Ontario, Canada).
    Having placed the letterbox and knowing of the 'traffic' through the area, I would advise a caution to all letterboxers:  whether retrieving a letterbox in order to stamp the log, or replacing it when done, be aware of observant others around you.  Above all else, we must be discrete!
    This is my first experience with letterbox vandalism, and it saddens me.
 
    Marilyn (St. George)
