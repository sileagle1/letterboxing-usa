From: cscm88 (cscm@toast.net) | Date: 2003-04-21 13:14:21 UTC
Rustypuff and I stayed close to home this Sunday, nabbing five boxes
in Dover and Milton, MA.

A Tree For All Seasons: Spring at Chase Woodlands in Dover is in
place, dry, and intact. While the log is by no means empty, we're
surprised more people haven't hunted up this sequential series.

We bit off a little more than we could chew with TeamGreenDragon's
Time Travels series in Milton's Blue Hill Reservation, but we feel
four out of the six is a respectable haul. The hand-carved stamps
are awesome, and if Brian doesn't submit some of his work for the
Smithsonian collection, we'll... well, we'll pout!

We got Weena, Time Traveler's Return, Into The Future, and Back To
The Lab, in that order. All were in place, dry, and intact.

We also set the Happy Trails Hitchhiker free... somewhere.
