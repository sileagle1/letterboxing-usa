From: Kathy Kalberer (kkalberer@cox.net) | Date: 2003-05-02 17:50:25 UTC-04:00
Hello,

Just returned from Yorktown. We introduced another 2 families to Lb (we
should be getting some more plants real soon!). They took us to the
Mariners Museum. We started with 3 families and 11 kids (ages 2-10), one
family dropped out after the first mile. The rest of us continued on, we
found boxes #1 and #2 then ran out of time. We were halfway around the 5
mile loop, and running out of daylight so we had to skip the last two boxes
(ohhhhh, how I hate not to complete something!!!) We're going back to that
area in a few weeks for another gym meet...and I'm determined to find the
last two.

We also went to York River State Park and found Cardinals Rule and Taskinas
Creek Letterboxes. I think Taskinas Creeks creative stamp has been my
favorite so far.

Happy boxing from the Happy Homeschoolers
