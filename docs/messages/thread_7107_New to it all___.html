From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-08-21 23:13:41 UTC

Finding a hiding place is just as challenging as creating your
letterbox. Look for hollow logs or crevices around the bases of
trees. You can use loose leaves, straw, twigs or rocks (whatever is
already laying around the area) to cover up any parts of the box that
might still be showing.

If you go hunting for a few boxes first, you can get an idea of what
other folks have been doing to hide their boxes :-)
--amanda from seattle
