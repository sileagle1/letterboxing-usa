I guess I went litterboxing today...

From: Q (puppygirl13@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2004-09-01 21:11:08 UTC
This morning I printed out the clues to about 10 or so different
letterboxes, grabbed my gear, my dog, and my dog's gear (tennis ball
& leash) and headed out. The first letterbox on my list was the
Repetto Ranch Raid letterbox in Buzzard's Tiburcio Vasquez series. I
found the box with no problem and sat under a shady tree with my dog
to stamp in. While stamping in I noticed a dirty box under another
tree about 50 feet away. It looked out of place since the park is
somewhat of a playground and very pristine. I decided to check it out
once I rehid the letterbox. As I got closer I noticed there were some
flies swarming around the top and I immediately thought there was a
dead animal inside. When my dog, who was about six feet ahead of me,
starting whining as she approached the box, I knew what was in there.
Kittens, four of them. They looked up at me with their blurry little
eyes. I love animals so there was no question in my mind what I would
do. I picked up the box and noticed that it was totally infested with
ants. Fortunately I had a clean box in my car to put them in, and
later today they will be checked out at the vet thanks to a great
rescue group called the Paw'd Squad here in Burbank, CA. The group is
already inundated with homeless cats (one of their members is
fostering over 90 cats in their home!) so it looks like these guys
will be staying with me for a while. Free kittens, anyone?

Please please please spay or neuter your pets. -Q
