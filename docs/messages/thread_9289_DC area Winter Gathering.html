DC area Winter Gathering

From: (Fireflylight@aol.com) | Date: 2002-11-22 01:26:08 UTC-05:00
Ok, it's official - I have set the date, time and place for all us wacky letterboxers to get together in the freezing winter to eat, drink, and stamp.  What more could one ask for??!

You are all invited to the DC area Winter Gathering.

When: Saturday, January 18, 2003  11:30 a.m. - 3:30 p.m.
Where:  Clyde's Restaurant of Columbia.  Wincopin Circle, Columbia, MD  in the "Library" part of the restaurant.

Because this is a restaurant, I'm going to need to get a firmer count on how many people are planning to attend.  But I really don't need to know this until we get closer to the date.  So don't feel the need to RSVP right now - I'll ask this question again in January!

Preliminary counts suggest that there will be over 30 people attending.  Because of this, Clyde's is going to give separate checks to each table.  Also, a 20% gratuity will be added to your check.  I hope this is ok!

So mark your calendars!  Clear your schedules!  Drive, fly, run to the wilds of Columbia, MD for the very exciting Winter Gathering!

Coleen
"Firefly"
P26 F103  HH7 TS1
