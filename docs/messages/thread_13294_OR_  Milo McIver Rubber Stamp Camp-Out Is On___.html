Hey All!

Thanks to a certain slab of bacon we all know and love we are set to
have our Portland area gathering at Milo McIver State Park in Estacada,
Oregon.

We've got Group Camp H2 and, per Funhog, the name it's under is "Rubber
Stamp Camp Out". We've got the group camp for Friday, July 11th from
4:00 pm that day to Sunday, July 13th at 1:00 pm, I believe. This is a
tent area that can take 25 campers. It ought to be lots of fun and even
though Ryan says that camping is overrated, I bet it will be just fine
and dandy for us all on that weekend.

The camping facility is paid for. Just bring yourr fine selves and all
the food and drink you can pack with you! For those not camping there
is a Day Use Fee of, I believe $3.00. Campers can just tell them at the
booth that they are part of the Rubber Stamp Camp Out at H2 and you
shouldn't have to pay to get in at all.

So there's the latest. Please use a search engine to look up Oregon
State Park's website which has details about the park itself and
directions that will take you straight there. This is going to be a
great time!!!
