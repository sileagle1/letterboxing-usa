Due to the conflicting reports, I haven't even gone to look for the
Phoenix box. There is a bike it - hike it at the same resevoir that my
sister in law found back in June. It's the box that I have in reserve
for when I can't get to an area with a couple boxes in proximity to each
other.

I don't know what your time frame on Sunday is like, but the Rocky Hill
based "Walk through geologic time" and Cherieph and my "native flower"
are at dinosaur state park. I would guess it's probably a 10 minute
drive down 91 from Cedar Hill. Native flower is a real quickie and the
trilobite box is at the farthest corner of the park. Bring bug spray,
too. I was told/read somewhere that the other geologic box is gone. A
heads up, Native flower is not hand carved, but trilobite is gorgeous
and is hand carved. Very cool stamp, I thought.

Cherieph is looking forward to meeting you at the gathering. It's funny
how we feel like we know each other from our boxes and the talk list.

I hope you have a great day.

r
