March 2nd is good for me too.
Raubsville Inn or the Ship Inn sounds great, I think Raubsville might have
more room for moving around, as in exchanging stamps and meeting new folks!
Food and service is good, bar is separate from the dining area.
Boxes galore for those who have not been over that way.There's "Fish In
Outer Space" in Allentown, PA. "Stone Faces of Haycock Mt.", and Ralph
Stover State Park with " High Rocks" and "Winter Night, Full Moon" less
than half an hour away. "Ringing Rocks" is 15 minutes down river with 2
boxes and there is a series of 7 letterboxes on the Deleware River from
Tinicum PA. to Easton Pa.,and another 3 along the Lehigh River/Canal near
and in Bethehem, Pa.
"Winahawcawchunk" and "Bowman's Hill" are a bit further down river from
Tinicum at Washington's Crossing. Tons more on top of those...
Then there are all those Jersey boxes! Wow! So many boxes, so little time!!
LOL!!
Hope to meet old friends and those, "know your stamp but not your face"
folks real soon.
Who is the black bird and black bat?????

Enjoy Life!! 'Box On!!
