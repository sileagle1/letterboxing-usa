From: (Fireflylight@aol.com) | Date: 2003-01-02 00:04:26 UTC-05:00
Hello all!

The time has come... I need to start getting an idea of how many people are planning to attend the DC Area Winter Gathering.

The gathering will be held Saturday, January 18, 2002 from 11:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. at Clyde's Restaurant in Columbia, MD.  We will be meeting in the "Library" section of the restaurant.

Just a reminder that Clyde's will be doing separate checks and since this is going to be a group of us - a 20% gratuity will be added to your check.  I hope this is ok with everyone.  Also, Clyde's opens at 11:30 - so if you are there early, you may end up standing outside!

So, please please please... If you are planning on attending this happy winter shindig, please let me know how many of you there will be so I can give the nice people at Clyde's a head count.

Directions to Clyde's can be found at:

Clyde's Restaurant Group
http://www.clydes.com/restaurants/cl-columbia-dir.html

Any questions?  Please feel free to contact me!

Come join the fun!   Can't wait to meet everyone!

Coleen
"Firefly"
