New Zealand Letterboxing yahoo group now active

From: Judi Lapsley Miller (judi@psychokiwi.org) | Date: 2004-07-11 17:32:37 UTC+12:00
Are you a letterboxer with a special interest in New Zealand letterboxing?
Do you have letterboxes placed here or perhaps intend to come boxing here
some day? Perhaps you live here already and are just waiting to find out
where all the clues are? If so, I have set up a yahoo group at
http://groups.yahoo.com/groups/nzlb for everything to do with letterboxing
in New Zealand. I must admit, it's pretty lonely over there at the moment,
but we have to start somewhere :-)

There are now 14 "public" boxes in NZ (ie boxes with clues publically
available) - 8 were placed by tourists from the USA and Canada. We've
placed 6 (and have 2 more with just clues to finalize - hopefully tonight!).
Most of our boxes were done in collaboration with our American friends. If
you like the idea of your boxes heading off to exotic locations, we would
love to do more collaborations and box-swaps. If you're coming for a visit -
do get in touch - we'd love to see you.

Wishing you all a boxious day,

Judi & Linton
Team Psychokiwi
Wellington, New Zealand
