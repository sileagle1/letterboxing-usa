To Jay, Thanks for telling Amy and I about the stamp store in Essex,
it's wonderful.

To Ruthann, Your sister says hello and that she's going to get back
to the talk list shortly, but as you know it's the busy season for
her.

To everyone else, We can't praise this shop high enough. It had the
carving matterial we were looking for. All kinds of premade stamps
and Miss Kathleen sponsers classes on various diffrent art forms. If
anyone is in the Essex, Ct. area it is well worth the side trip
however long to visit.

Thanks also to the Irish Tinker for the three new boxes in Coventry,
Amy really enjoyed the stories. All boxes found in execlent
condition.

Patrick and Amy
