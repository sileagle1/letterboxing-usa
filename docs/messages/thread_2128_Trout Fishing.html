The Cowboy and I will head out today to check the Drews trout fishing
in America. The lake was rising steadily yesterday and I fear it
will quickly be inundated if we get more rain this weekend. If it

purpose when you visit the area. I don't mind tending the box at all
as I work near there and keep an eye on the rising water on my daily
commute. I'll let you all know what we find. Carol - in haven't
