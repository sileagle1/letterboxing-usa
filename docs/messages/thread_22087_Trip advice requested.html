From: (StDebb@aol.com) | Date: 2005-06-05 16:28:57 UTC-04:00
Okay, it looks like we've finally decided on a vacation destination.
Niagara Falls. So I'm looking for advice on what boxes are good, both there and
along the way. The catch is that I'll be traveling with an impatient husband
who just loves to give me a hard time about stopping too much.

So, what I'm looking for are boxes that either are very fast--like at a rest
stop--or that have a really cool setting, so he won't mind going there. I've
found a couple like that, but was just wondering if anyone had any inside
knowledge of any more.

Once we're there, the restrictions can relax just a bit, but I still need to
make sure that I'm dragging him to fairly interesting places, not just a local
park that's very like most local parks, you know?

And if anyone cares to share any WOM clues, I promise to guard them with my
life, hunt discreetly, and rehide very carefully!

Here's our route:

I-95 Fort Lauderdale to Columbia, SC
I-77 to Beckley, WV
US-19 to I-79
I-79 to Erie, PA
I-90 to Niagara Falls

We'll be spending time on both sides of the border. I haven't been able to
find clues for any boxes in the area on the Canadian side, but I'd sure love
to find one. It'd be my first one outside the US.

Any suggestions would be appreciated.

DebBee
