Perfect Circle Letterboxing Guild

Mensajero de paz

anhela fructifiquen
tus altos ideales

The pillar marking the end of this messenger's voyage is in a desolate place.
But, every year, those who venture here to honor his memory bring nationalist
pride and a small piece of hope for the future.
