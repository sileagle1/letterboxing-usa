This trail is a two mile loop in the Whittier Hills. We planted three
boxes along the trail.
To get to the park take the 60 FWY to Hacienda Blvd. south to Colima
Road turn right you will go over the hill and start heading down into
Whittier. The park is on the right side of the road, and is well
marked with big black wrought iron gate with animals on it. If you
get to Mar Vista Rd you have gone too far.
