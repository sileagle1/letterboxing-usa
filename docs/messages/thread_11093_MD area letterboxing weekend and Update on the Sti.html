MD area letterboxing weekend and Update on the Stinkeye.

From: letterboxing mobots (themobots@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-03-09 19:10:07 UTC-08:00
Good weather this weekend. Admittedly, I spent most
of the time climbing, but we still managed to get some
letterboxing done.

1. Checked on the greenbelt coffeefish. It's still
there. So far only one stamp but from visitors from
very far away.

2. went out to rocky gorge and got dogpaddle beach
and the fishing one by the beaver dam. Realized after
we saw them that we probably missed the opportunity
for an exchange with the 4 people walking up the hill
as we were walking down. Which is unfortunate because
dead duck chuck has the best stamp we've ever seen (a
weehedgehog running close 2nd). We either misread the
directions or couldn't find the 3rd box for all the
snow. footprints would indicate that we were in a
similar predicament as others.

3. Replaced the Stinkeye. It was actually not
missing when it was reported missing, just under the
snow. We found it after the snow melted. We took it
away so we could replace it later after all the snow
was gone. It's now in the same place wedged under a
rock. Definitely a hard box to do at night when all
the college types are buying their potato chips at the
wa-wa. :)

Jen and Jim
