Store bought Sig stamps

From: Brian, Ryan & Lori (teamgreendragon2003@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-04 03:30:45 UTC

We used a store bought sig stamp for our first 500 finds.
Also anyone that exchanged with us in our first 10 months of 'boxing
has that store bought image (Yes it took us 10 months to find 500
boxes, whatever that means - to some it's a lot, to others it is not
but that's our personal experiences and wouldn't expect anyone else
to care one way or the other). We finally decided that we needed to
personalize our stamp after looking at our exchanges one day and
realized that there were at least 3 other "sig stamps" that were
identical to ours.

Moral of the story (if there is one) your sig stamp is your personal
mark in the letterboxing world. You need to decide when and if you
want your own unique image that cannot be imitated by anyone. If you
don't care, why should anyone else? (Here's to you, TeamRedDragon,
where ever you may be tonite)
