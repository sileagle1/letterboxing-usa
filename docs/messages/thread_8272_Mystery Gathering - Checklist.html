Checklist for Event

- Directions to location
- Letterboxing supplies
- Clues to existing boxes in the area
- Food to share
- Own cutlery, plate and drink
- Compass
- Tithe for the host (stamp exchanges accepted)
- New box to place for the event (optional)
- Homage for Alifair's event stamp
- Comfy walking shoes, moleskin, purple socks (IALC option)
- No visible alcohol

Available at the Location

- Lots of great boxes
- A covered picnic area (or "longhouse without sides") to meet at
- Lots of charcoal grills
- Opportunity to sample food from people whose hobby centers around
playing hide and seek with Rubbermaid
- Playground for the young letterboxers
- Alifair's unbelievable event stamp
- Opportunity to increase your E and X count (as well as F)
- Observatory (after dark)
- Free access

Questions?
Tom
