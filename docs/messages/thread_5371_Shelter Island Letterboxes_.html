Don't think so, Jocelyn...but there are two at Orient Point, not too
far from where you'll take the ferry to SI . The Turtle Crossing
letterbox in the State Park by Orient Point was reported missing.
It's a real easy walk once in the Park, so if you make it there, it
might be worth a try.

Enjoy Shelter Island. I've haven't been over there since I was a
kid, but I hear it's still pretty remote and beautiful. Protect
yourself from the deer ticks if it's the season because they're
pretty prevalent over there.
