The Fishing Hole

In Richmond, RI, on Route 91
Find a spot for fishing fun!

The fishing area at Meadowbrook,
Is where you'll want to start to look.

At the end of the lot at 42,
You find a path blazed in blue.

Over the log, trod the grass and pine,
Just a short walk to this box of mine.

When a dirt road comes in sight,
and double blazes in the distant right,

A faint, narrow path at 340,
Brings you to a fallen tree.
