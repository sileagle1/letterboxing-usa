Another thing I saw that I thought would be about perfect I found in the
Cabela's catalog under the fly fishing area. It's a fishing vest with lots
of pockets, so you can tuck in all your neccesities and it has a "desk" that
folds down in the front to stamp in on. I don't know about the rest of the
country, but boxing in Oregon from October on gets a bit muddy..so sitting
down to stamp in requires either a disregard for your pants or carrying a
tarp with you to sit upon. This gives you the option of stamping in on a
flat surface but not having to sit down.
