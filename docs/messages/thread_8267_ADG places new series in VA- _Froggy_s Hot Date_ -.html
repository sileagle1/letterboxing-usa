We really enjoyed Froggy's Hot Date. Where did you and Firefly learn
to make such beautiful books?!

Well we did get lost on the second clues. When you said he
remembered that was the wrong way we thought you meant don't go that
way and go the other way. Or something like that. Do you know how
many fallen trees there are pointing almost north up the wrong
trail? Oh well. Started over twice and finally figured it out.
Once we did the hidden trail was cool.

You need more rocks though I think. We were late and sorry we didn't
go find more. We finished as it got dark which was appropo to the
story.

I was fascinated by the old road. I imagined different peoples using
it over its many years. Found a garter snake crossing it. Doesn't
that mean something when a friendly snake crosses your path. Finding
new interesting places is what's it's all about for me.

We were the first to stamp in. Woohoo.
