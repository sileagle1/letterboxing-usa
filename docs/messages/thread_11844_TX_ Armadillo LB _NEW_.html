"Armadillo Letterbox"
Amarillo, Texas
Placed 7 Apr 03 by A-Bear from Star, Idaho

Directions: From I-40 take Exit 67 north on Western
St for about 1 mile. Sam Houston Park will be on your
right/east side of the road.

Easy and quick!!

Clues: So there I was, watching my buddies become
roadkill along the highway. I had to make a quick get
away before I, too, would join them. By the time I
reached Sam Houston Park, the sun was beating down on
me, depleting me from any energy I had. Exhausted as
I came to the amphitheater steps, I barely made it to
the base of the tree to the left underneath the bush
and leaves in front of the tree. I had survived!!!
