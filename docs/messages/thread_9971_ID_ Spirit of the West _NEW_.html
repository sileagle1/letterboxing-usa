Spirit of the West
Idaho Falls, Idaho
Bonneville County
Placed by J & A-Bear on 31 December 2002

Directions: From I-15 take exit 118 Broadway and head
east. At the intersection light of Broadway and
Memorial Dr. turn left and park immediately on your
right at the Melaleuca store and Grocery store. Avoid
parking at the Key Bank otherwise theyll make you pay
in one way or another, unless of course you need some
of their cash.

Level of Difficulty: Easy.

Spirit of the West Clues:
Safely cross the street towards the water tower on the
south side of Broadway. Go under the archway and
cross the narrow bridge to the three flags. Due west
is the sign for Taylors Toll Bridge. On the south
east side below eight bolts is the micromagnet box you
seek.

Please read the waiver of responsibility. Be careful
while reaching in for the microbox and replace it way
so it doesnt get washed away.
