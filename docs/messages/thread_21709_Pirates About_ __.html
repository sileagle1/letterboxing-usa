Hail all!

Just wanted you to know that I posted a picture of the Cap'n (me!)
on our ring's photo page and encourage others to do the same so we
can see who we are "talking" to. This way, you can get a look at who
you are replying to, sending packages to, or who is finding your
letterboxes!

The picture is of me in my "garb" at the Central Coast Renaissance
Festival held in San Luis Obispo, its our local faire. I am the
Captain of the imaginary ship, the "Galendere" which sails the high
seas looking for piratical adventure! However, when we are at the
Ojai Faires, our friend brings a 16 foot replica tall ship and we go
sailing and have ship to shore battles, I have posted that a picture
of that ship also. When we set sail to each faire, we go in our Ken
Kesey/Partridge Family style painted Gypsy Bus! You can see a picture
of that also.

I am also the Guildmistress for the crew, the "Gypsy Pyrates", a
pirate reenactment guild that travels to faires and SCA events
throughout California. We have a very large encampment with three
large period style pavilions and lots of props. We are famous for
our homemade Irish Cream which we call "The G-Creme"! We also make
another brew called "The Gypsy Jizz", sounds vulgar don't they? Well,
we arrrrre pyrates after all! Our website is:

www.GypsyPyrates.com

...but be forewarned that its my first website and I am just learning
HTML and how to "work it" so its under construction.

Do you have other hobbies besides letterboxing? "Yes,Virginia"
there are OTHER things besides letterboxing, despite the fact that it
has been in my dreams recently! Let us know about your hobbies...I
know that a while ago a "Hobby Ring" was going around, or may still
be...but at least we can discover more about one another through
emails.

I also write poetry and short stories, and you can check out my
website set up for others to post on as well at:

http://p218.ezboard.com/bmyriahswyndsongs

would love to see them, and others might as well!
