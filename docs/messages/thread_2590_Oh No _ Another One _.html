Well if anyone thought that having another website around related
to letterboxing was bad ---- I'm afraid it's going to get worse.
Let me announce to you all the imminent arrival of
LetterboxingCentral.Com. For those of you who do not immediately
throw rocks and bottles let me say that it is not my intent to
compete with anyone. Any and all information which is gathered by
this site is freely available and I invite you to either place it
on your own site or link to it as you see fit.

It is our belief that although letterboxing.org and others do an
admirable job at promotining letterboxing itself, there is a
lot of room to present many other aspects of this sport/hobby.
Our intent is to be a gateway to all of the elements that make up
this activity. I hope we do it well and that we offer something of
value to the letterboxing community.

We will be actively displaying links to sites which benefit the
sport and will undoubtedly link to yours. We hope you find
value in our site to warrant a link of your own. We will be On-Line
by June 24.
