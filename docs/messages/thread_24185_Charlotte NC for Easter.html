McDowell Nature Preserve, 15222 York Rd, was the site of a gathering
and has many letterboxes by different carvers and could keep you busy
for an entire day very easily. It is on the South End of Charlotte
near Carowinds Amusement Park. And don't forget to check out some
mystery boxes! Do your homework now, so you don't pass by a letterbox
unknowingly.
