WHITE SANDS - Unplugged

From: gingerbreadjunk (gingerbreadjunk@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-29 03:32:56 UTC
So you all had me go and do it! In hopes of drawing more people for
my White Sands clean-up, I went and Changed this box from a mystery
box to a regular. I have only a handful of people helping clean this
once beautiful location and I would love to have a few more. So if
anyone could lend a few hours of there time and help New Hampshire
stay beautiful, I would appreciate it a whole big bunch as I hope
others will in the community.
The Conservation Commision of Pembroke, knows about my effort
scheduled for May 7th, and are aware that I letterbox with best
intentions for the area. They know that many may come to the clean-
up as letterboxers, but many may come as concerned town residents.
Only the Conservation Commision knows that there is a letterbox
located in this location.
I plan on being down at White Sands at 10am and I don't plan on
leaving until the area is clean. I will have a special stamp for
this event for those that do make it and I'm working on having pizza
as well. Please consider leaning a hand for such a wonderful cause.

Livin Free and Keepin it Clean
Team New Hampshire
http://teamnewhampshire.blogspot.com
