WA: Must do area for visitors

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-04-20 22:24:35 UTC
For folks planning a visit to Washington State I must recommend the
wonderful collection of boxes in the Olympia ( Thurston County) area.

Today I visited McLean Creek yet again for Trial and Errors three new
boxes ( as well as a bonus box) What a wonderful venue. Swamp and
marsh deep woods and running creek. You will see some of the largest
tree stumps ever to nurse new growth. In the Fall the salmon run. I
guess 15 miutes from the state capitol.

Also further west is Kennedy Creek, to the north is Tolmie State park
and all around in Tumwater, Lacey and points west of the intersection
of I-5 and Hwy 101 are plenty of multibox venues.

It was such a perfect day that I have to encourage you to not
overlook our state capitol as a hot bed of boxing.

Hoopoe feels mighty happy to have done all three of Trial and Errors
boxes without a hitch. Things like that just don't happen to me.
