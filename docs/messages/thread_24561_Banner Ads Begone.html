From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2007-09-06 07:33:31 UTC
I'm probably WAY behind the curve on this one but I just made a
wonderful discovery. In case any of you don't already know about it, I
thought I'd share my discovery. I use Firefox and there's a free
add-on available for download for this browser called Adblock (or
Adblock Plus) that gets rid of that annoying advertising at the top of
Yahoo! pages. Yay!!! I feel so liberated. Funhog


Re: [LbNA] Banner Ads Begone

From: Gary & Elizabeth Anderson (garyliza@arkwest.com) | Date: 2007-09-06 04:09:30 UTC-05:00
Well, Funhog,,,

I don't have Firefox, but those ads popped up on Internet Explorer,
too... However, one with IE (and I thought any other browser) can go to:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-usa/join

Put in your Yahoo ID, if requested, and at the bottom of the box,
change your settings to "Traditional", from the "Fully Featured New &
Improved!" setting, which is all the advertisement banners.

Gary
