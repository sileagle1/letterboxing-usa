KINDNESS & TOLERANCE are desirable human traits and take less effort than packing out your trash on the trail. If you can "leave no trace" I believe you can also offer a hand over a rocky place to a trailmate, even if this effort costs you nothing more than letting a few things slide. Great and lasting friendships have been made on this Main list, people are lifeblood to letterboxing - we need to be kind to one another.

Peace,
