From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-05-29 21:58:49 UTC
Boxes in the area that I know are there (not missing) are the ones at
Byxbee Park in Palo Alto, and at least one of the boxes at Bay
Wildlife Refuge in Fremont. Also, Ryan Carpenter found the Loma
Prieta earthquake box in Aptos last month. The Presido boxes are
missing and so are the ones at Bol Park in Palo Alto.

For a complete list of boxes, go to www.letterboxing.org, click on
the big red book, this is where the clues are listed by State. Click
on your State, then your area, etc etc You can also keep up with
what is new, by clicking on the What's New section! This way, you
don't have to wade through tons of these messages to find boxes in
your area!

--Amanda from Seattle (who will be in SFO on Friday to nab the Byxbee
park boxes!--and maybe the new fishy's hybrid box too!!)
