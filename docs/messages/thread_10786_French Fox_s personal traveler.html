From: irishtinker (irishtinker@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-02-14 10:35:32 UTC-05:00
 I just carved (for the 1st time) and planted my very first boxes. I based them on the book Watership Downs.  It got me thinking of other books I have enjoyed that are simular. I had also enjoyed the book Long Patrol which is also about a warren. Anyway recently I have also noticed a rise in people having personal travelers. For example Butterfly has a riddle you need to answer and the Drews have a special gesture (I don't know how much I am allowed to say) I think this is a fun little "extra" and I now have my own personal traveler. If you see me on the trail or at a gathering just ask "Have you seen the Long Patrol?"
 
FRENCH FOX
