"Trailseeker" has planted a new box in North Carolina, and asked me to relay
the clues:

"Love Valley"
Love Valley, N.C. (Iredell county) placed 5-OCT-01

Walk under the Love Valley sign.
Continue north up the street to the 'Old Jail.'
Turn right - east - and go up trail behind the jail to the Twin Poplars.
Turn right - south - for 20 paces to forked dogwood.
Turn left - east - for 32 paces to an oak scarred on north & south sides.
Go 8 paces north/east to a rock pile.
There you will find a message in a box.
