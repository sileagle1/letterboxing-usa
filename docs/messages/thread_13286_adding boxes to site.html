RE: [LbNA] adding boxes to site

From: Wes Garrison (wes@wesgarrison.us) | Date: 2003-06-03 16:21:56 UTC-05:00
Put your link in the 'link' field, and check the box that says:
"Redirect to this link automatically"
You don't need to put anything at all in the clues page.

This will make the link on the state/region page point to your webpage,
not the LbNA page.

Just for y'alls reference, HTML (or XML) tags are automatically removed.
If you want to use HTML, get your own webpage.

Wes
