Hello everyone! I just wanted to thank everyone who put the Maine Gather
together (and for everyone who came!). We (True Soccer Fan and I) thoroughly
enjoyed ourselves, though he decided to go and read his new book and dive into
some quiet time while I boxed. It was so nice to meet so many great Maine-rs
(is that what people from Maine are called???) and to see so many familiar faces
from RI, MA and CT. Kudos to all of you who figured out my personal

;-) He made everyone work hard for the stamp :-)

THough I didn't really get to eat at the gather, I hear that the food was
scrumptious (I was lucky to get a half of a hamburger, a hotdog, and a small
piece of cake between the mad stamping!!!!)! BTW.... if any Maine-r picked up the
Order of the Red Hand HH, please place it in Maine for me :-) I totally
forgot that I brought it, and left it at the gather. I was going to place it at a
lighthouse today, but if someone else could place it in Maine, that would be
great!

Thanks again for a great time, and great exchanges and new friends! Hope to
see you all down in CT soon!
