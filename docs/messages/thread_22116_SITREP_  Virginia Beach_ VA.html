SITREP: Virginia Beach, VA

From: Janet (moonstone_baby@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-10 14:55:41 UTC
Yesterday afternoon before catching my flight home I found the
following boxes in Virginia Beach, VA:

Fort Story Lighthouse: What a great spot! I climbed up to the top
of the lighthouse and what a view! This box is alive and well but
log book is getting full.

Eggcellent Eggstravaganza: A fun series, but turned out to be a
little messy coloring all the Easter eggs! All 6 boxes are in fine
shape.

Wild Indoors: I wonderful place for a letterbox! If you haven't
been there yet you should. The store is great! (The box is too!)

I was disappointed that my time ran short and I had to head to the
airport before getting the box at Mt. Trashmore. Mt. Trashmore is
such a neat name I was looking forward to going there. Next time.

Thanks to all the box creators.
