The Stinkeye
placed 8 feb 2003 9PM
by themobots@yahoo.com
College Park MD
Prince Georges County

The stinkeye is a disapproving face. The residents of
College Park dedicate the stinkeye to the basketball
riots of 2002. From 95/495 take the rte. 1 exit into
College Park. At the intersection of Rte. 1 and
Knox road (the site of the riots) turn right. Park in
the lot on the left. start on the sidewalk
immediately to the right of the WaWa. Count 49
sidewalk blocks up the hill. On your left will be the
junction of a chain link fence and a wooden fence.
Reach between them; the stinkeye is in a film
cannister behind the 1st wooden post. As this is a
very public place, take the box to one of the many
nice restaurants, have a nice lunch, buy a book at
Vertigo books (the owners are very nice), stamp and
return. Have fun!
