"Root Cellar"
Wanlutport Towpath Series #2
Lehigh Canal Towpath at Walnutport, PA
Lehigh County, PA
Planted 10-20-02 By DiverDM
Clues: Easy
Hike: Easy 3 mile roundtrip

After "Indian Grinding Stone" The first in this series, continue
downstream. Soon you will cross over a wooden bridge with a right
railing. Pass one bench then at the second bench go 20 paces toward
the river to a small group of trees, descend the "root staircase" to
your right, Turn to your left 180 degrees, you will see a "shared
root system" of the small group of trees you just paced to. Inside
this "Root Cellar" you will find a box.
If you want to take longer walk continue downstream for about another
half mile, it is a very pleasant 4 mile total walk.
