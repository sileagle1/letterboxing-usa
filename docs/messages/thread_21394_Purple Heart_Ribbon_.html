Purple Heart/Ribbon?

From: Gwen and Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-02-16 08:35:51 UTC-08:00
I realize that I'm getting into this thread a little late, but figured that I would throw in my two cents. I too had a problem with the Purple Heart award, and even though I could have requested the award, I decided to pass.
The Purple Heart has an obvious special meaning and any use by us could be degrading for those that were awarded the medal. Purple, pink, or other colored ribbons and other possible "titled" stamps may too have other connotations that may be offensive?
Since the award that is in question, is for injuries occurring while letterboxing, why not just call it the "First Aid Box". Carve something similar to a Red Cross, and let it be simply an award that one receives when First aid was needed.

Don
