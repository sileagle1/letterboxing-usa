From: Francis Bacon (investigator_bacon@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-19 19:08:17 UTC-07:00
My Dear Der Mad Stamper,

You must have faith in the investigator that you,
yourself, have hired. It has taken many hours to
dumpster dive into your site. Just when I have a
handle on the suspect another one is thrown in. Was it
not you, Der Mad Stamper, who told me that this was a
close-knit community? I see an immense amount of mud
slinging here, a tremendous amount of name-calling.

I do believe my fee has just doubled.

As I am depriving myself of sleep with many hours
of research you make accusations of my snoozing on
I-5! Do you have any comprehension of how complicated
your hobby is? Is there a letterbox at the Applegate
Rest Stop?

You will hear more from me as my investigation
continues. Never fear I will solve this case.

Sincerely,
Investigator Francis Bacon
