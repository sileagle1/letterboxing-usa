From: rooindie (rooindie@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-30 18:03:28 UTC
Yeah! I finally made F100!! Once I realized I could possibly hit it
before my one year "anniversary", I made crazy boxing plans every
weekend. Have been out in some VERY cold temperatures, and digging
through the snow. When I found box 100, I could get my hands on it,
but the large rock blocking it into the tree was frozen in place, and
I couldn't get the box out!! What a way to find #100! Fortunately,
my boxing partner gave the rock a kick with his boot to break it
free. Yeah!! It was COLD, though, so no dawdling to enjoy the
moment.

Wonder what the next year will bring???

Thanks to everyone who places boxes - have seen some beautiful things
in several states this year I never would have seen if not for being
on the hunt!!!
