Queen of Hearts , Mansfield ,Ct.

From: Carolyn Stearns (cstearns@neca.com) | Date: 2000-10-10 16:17:56 UTC-04:00
Rated: Easy bring your little kids
 
Time 1/2  hour
 
Sponsor: Cock-A-Doodle-Moo 4-H Club
 
In Mansfield  take rte 195 to Rte 275 or rte 32 to 275 Owen's Mere is a tract of land owned by the Joshua's trust land preservation group on the side of 275 . This would combine nicely with Pine for You.  The sign is very obscure , you can park along the side of the dirt road, there's room for 1-2 cars.
   Pass around the gate  take the trail left along the edge of the pond. At the large gray birch cross the stone ford in the stream.
   Continue past the two benches where you can stop to enjoy the view or a moments peace. Going on around the end of the pond to a small wooden bridge.
  Cross over the bridge and turn left. Look for a tree with a gnarled base. A place where squirrels would tuck their acorns.  Here is where you will find the queens treasure. 
   Stamp your book secretly , replace the box where you found it. Return to the car by way of the  path ahead . Hope you enjoy.
