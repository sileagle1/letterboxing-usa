Time is quickly fleeting and Oct 6 will be here before any of us are
ready for it. The other date we had considered was Sep 15 and I have
to admit that with the tragic events of last week I am glad that the
Oct date was chosen.

Thanks to those that have volunteered their efforts for activities.
We should have lots of boxes in the area to keep busy that day but a
few quickies in the immediate area would be nice (hint, hint). Wanda
and Pete have several in Burlingame, there are some across Rt 1 in
Ninigret and I have yet to write up the clues for the series I
planted over a month ago in North Burlingame.

enough paper goods for dinner, etc. Let Shannon or I know what you
would like to do for dinner (potluck, pizza, ?). Now is the time to
speak up.
