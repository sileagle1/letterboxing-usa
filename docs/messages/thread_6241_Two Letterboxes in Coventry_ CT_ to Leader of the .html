I was just up in Coventry CT looking for two letterboxes which I
couldn't find. The first was at the Brick School House. The
outhouse (where the letterbox supposedly was hidden outside of) is
gone and there aren't any stones to be found except for the stone
wall. Is it in the stone wall, or was it part of the outhouse?

Also, I was next door at Laidlaw Park and could not find the "Mousing
at Laidlaw Park" letterbox. There was a huge hole in the stone wall,
and stones moved, where it looked like a letterbox could have been
placed, but no letterbox.

Please let me know if I am just mistaken, or if the letterboxes are
indeed missing.

Thanks!
Pam in Manchester, CT
