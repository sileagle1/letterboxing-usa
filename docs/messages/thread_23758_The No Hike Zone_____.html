Okay, I'm going to pipe in ever so quickly and make a
comment. I LOVE long hikes! I can rarely find
someone willing to go with me that is over the age of
18. Now that I'm in the letterbox ghostland I am in
the position of being the "placer" to jump start
things. Thus far, due to work schedules, I have been
unable to get out and do those long hikes (though the
boxes are ready) but I will say that there are a WHOLE
LOT of places that require minimal hiking/walking that
are FANTABULOUS for boxes! I found one today. The
best way to combat the No Hike Zone is to make the box
a BRAINER! You have to think and solve to find the
box. It's as simple as that.
