Letterboxing on FPDDC Property (IL)

From: Daniel Hebreard (dhebreard@dupageforest.com) | Date: 2007-07-18 19:02:54 UTC-05:00
The FPDDC is beginning a letterbox program and I have placed two boxes
to get things started. I recently placed the clues on the
letterboxing.org website, but would appreciate some assistance. We are
trying to catalog all of the current letterboxes on our property and
have their owners apply for approval. We are doing this to help ensure
they are following all of our rules and regulations, while not
disturbing any sensitive natural areas. Towards that I was hoping to
get our official Letterboxing Rules and Regulations information posted
on letterboxing.org and contact all owners who have placed boxes in
DuPage County, IL to ensure compliance. Please let me know if you can
help me with either of these matters. I hope that we can continue to
help letterboxing grow in DuPage County and give people another way to
enjoy visiting our beautiful forest preserves. Thanks for your time and
consideration, it is appreciated.
