From: Suzanne H. (realroadrunner@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-05-11 15:53:38 UTC
I believe this is northeastern Nevada's first letterbox.

Closest town: Elko, NV
County: Elko

Difficulty: Easy
Clues: Easy
Status: Awaiting its first finder

White King, the world's largest polar bear, is on display in the
coffee shop of Elko's Commercial Hotel and Casino. Standing 10 feet, 4
inches and weighing 2,200 pounds, White King's arrival here was the
result of a 1957 challenge to find the largest Polar Bear in the
Arctic Circle.

Big plaster replicas of the mighty ursine glower menacingly from above
the two main entrances to the casino.
