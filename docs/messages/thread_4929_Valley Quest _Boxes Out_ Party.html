Valley Quest "Boxes Out" Party

From: Steve Glazer (steve@vitalcommunities.org) | Date: 2002-04-09 09:47:08 UTC-04:00
We put out the Valley Quest boxes each spring with the help of many
volunteers.

67 folks came to our "Potluck" north last friday to help put out 45
"northern boxes." A "southern" box party will be held this friday
evening at Old South Congregational church in Windsor, VT. You are all
welcome to join us.

Vital Communities cordially invites you,
your family & friends to join us for our

Valley Quest Spring Potluck

featuring

Storytelling, a Treasure Hunt, Book & Stampmaking

What: A potluck supper to get ready for the new Valley Quest season
When: Friday, April 12th, 6 - 8 pm
Where: Old South Congregational Church, Route 5, Windsor, VT
RSVP appreciated but not required
More info: Steve @ 802-291-9100 or steve@vitalcommunities.org

Valley Quest gratefully acknowledges the support of the A.D. Henderson
Foundation, Walker Fund of New Hampshire Charitable Foundation, the
Robins Foundation, Windham Foundation, Wellborn Ecology Fund of the
Upper Valley Community Foundation, the Connecticut River Joint
Commissions Partner Program, Anne Slade Frey Charitable Trust-and the
support of many schools, teachers, students, individuals, organizations
and volunteers.

Happy Trails,
