Found our first box! Bay Area Wildlife Refuge, CA...

From: Jennifer Minnis (esmecat@earthlink.net) | Date: 2002-08-25 17:59:45 UTC-07:00
hi all,

well, my DH and I went boxing yesterday and found or first (and second) box.

we had a great time, and never knew that park was there before. we didn't
find the 1st of the 3 boxes (we even went back and rechecked after seeing
the small size of the other 2).

also, the log books of both #2 and #3 boxes are completely full. someone had
added a piece of paper to the #2 box to continue the log. we added a piece
to the #3 to continue it. also, no stamp pads in either box. I don't know if
they had them originally (small size and all) but wanted to let whoever know
just in case.

thank you for a great afternoon.

Jenni AKA Pandora Post

(and Javalord, too.)
