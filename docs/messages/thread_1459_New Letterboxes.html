Hi Jody - and welcome!

Just post your clues right here to the list and our
truely wonderful webmasters will take it from there.
They will place them on the What's new Page to start,
and them move them to the appropriate clue page.

Make sure to give them a title for your "personal"
webpage that they will setup with your clues. To be
helpful to them, indicate that it is a mystery box in
the subject line of the post.

If you care to reveal the state of the boxes, you can
put that in the subject line as well - but don't feel
you have to. The amount of info you choose to give
out to find the box is up to you.

Can't wait to check out the clues ;o)
