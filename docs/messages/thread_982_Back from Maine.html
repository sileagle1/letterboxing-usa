From: Tom Cooch (tcooch@sover.net) | Date: 2000-06-30 07:03:07 UTC
Greetings to all,

I'm just back from two days up in the Penobscot area of Maine. I am
glad to report that the Cadillac Mountain and Thunder Hole boxes are
just fine. Someone - I can't remeber who just now - reported the
latter missing a few months ago, but it was there! It had had quite a
few visitors too, including some in February, which must have been
brutal. Nice to see Kim's stamp, and Rae, Jerry & Mass Lone
Wolf's.

I put in another Acadia National Park box at Sand Beach.

http://www.sover.net/~tcooch/acadia.htm#sand

I got your clues, Warren, and will put them on the map. They won't be
posted, however, unitl Randy gets back the end of next week.

Randy, have a good time. I haven't had time to work on the Quatrains
for a month, but hope to solve it this summer. I'm currently stumped
on the first one ('lost but never found').

Best regards,

Tom
