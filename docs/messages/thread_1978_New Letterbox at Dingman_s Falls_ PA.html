Dingman's Falls, PA Letterboxes

Dingman's Falls is in the Delaware Water Gap area of the Poconos, and is
a nice falls that is accessible via a 1/2 mi long handi-cap accessible
ramp. It is run by the National Park Service and is open year round
(though you may need to hike a mile or so in if the access road has not
been plowed). There may be a small fee in the summer. When we were
there in the winter we ran into cross country skiers and some ice
climbers! The road to the falls is a stone throw south along Rt 209
from Rt 739.
