Black Coffee


Planted 10/28/02
Planted By: Leader of the Pack - en route to Louisville, Ky. for the
National FFA Convention.

Rated Drive - up : literally needed to pull over to stretch legs and
wake mind! Have Fun.

Connecticut to Kentucky is a long but beautiful drive. We were fortunate
to make it at the peak of fall color and enjoyed the trip. We knew we
would make many rest stops and coffee breaks thus the Black Coffee
letterbox. After a 3:30 a.m. start time we were in serious need of a
break as we drove along Rte 70 west and saw the sign welcoming us to
Wheeling, West Virginia. This piece of West Virginia is just a quick
pass through on the highway west. You will want to stop at the welcome
center / rest stop after crossing into the state.
