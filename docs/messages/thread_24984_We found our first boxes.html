We found our first boxes

From: HeatherS (hlynns@roadrunner.com) | Date: 2009-01-03 03:17:54 UTC
At Lake Perris on the Rocks. It was a great outing. My girls enjoyed
it very much and so did my best friends little girl. The great thing
about it is we all had our own stamp and they all fit on one page.
A couple were hand made and a couple were store bought. My reasoning
behind everyone having their own stamp is so that when my kids grow
they can continue Letterboxing and taking their own stamp when they
move out (who know's when that will be, they tell us now they will live
with us forever). So they all had their own 5x5 paper to make their
own books with.
Whoever stamped before us was pretty cool. They had gold ink! And it
looked terrific.
The other cool thing was we were the first one's to stamp the book in
the new year so my girls haven't stopped talking about it.
Punky did an excellent job at hiding these boxes and we made sure they
were sealed and back in their place, hidden. THANKS!
Heather
Craftlover10101
