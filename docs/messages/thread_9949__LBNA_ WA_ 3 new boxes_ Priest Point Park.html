Seasoned Greedings,

Got ourselves out to Priest Point Park yesterday during a lull in the rain.
Made a visit to the Nail's Game Letterboxes.
Glenn brought his son's Akita along for the hunt. Real nice dog around people,
but meeting other large dogs is a problem.

Found 42 and Farkle in great shape and marveled at the fine detail of the
carvings.
Nail 'em! gave us a problem, seems we made the wrong interpretation on one of
the clues and went off on a wild goose chase. When we got there, it was evident
that we weren't the only ones to make that mistake. After retracing our steps
back to a known landmark, we proceeded on another choice and were rewarded with
the Dog Tired Hitchhiker set to rest by Hoopoe in the Nail 'em! box.
Seemed fitting since we brought a dog!
She was tired too by this time, she'd had enough of this wandering around
aimlessly in the woods with two guys that can't read clues.

Since Dog Tired has no license, we decided to keep him until a suitable hiding
place can be found away from the authorities.

Later my wife and I went to Zoo lights at Pt. Defiance Park.
There are pictures in the pictures area.
http://folks.harbornet.com/bburk/pictures.htm

Thanks to the Nails for a good hunt!
Compliments to the carver too!
