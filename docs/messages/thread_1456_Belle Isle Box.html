Played hooky from work to add this stamp to my journal. Belle Isle
is a wonderful diversion surrounded by conflicting sights and
sounds.....the footbridge over the James with views of the skyline,
interstate overpasses, CSX freight trains thundering by, and the
serene monuments of Hollywood Cemetery off in the distance.
Following the clues to the Belle Isle box leads you through some
surprisingly interesting wooded trails....many more walks to explore
in the future. The intersections and forks are many, but if you pay
close attention to the clues, you'll have your reward. Allow enough
time to enjoy this one...and bring a buddy if at all possible...the
woods are a little spooky alone.

And, to the next "Voyager" who "treks" to Belle Isle, a surprise
waits docked behind the Belle Isle box.
