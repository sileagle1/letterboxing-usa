WY & MT: Road Trip LB's visited

From: tlrewok (teri@adminpowerinc.com) | Date: 2003-11-13 18:18:56 UTC
Well, I am finally back home after being gone for over 3 weeks!! I
was very busy and only got to visit 3 LB's all on the way up to MT.

WY Vedauwoo Found 10-15-03- This box is in a great little park!! Box
was in awesome condition eventhough one of the bags was not sealed
all the way. I was only the 2nd visitor (I think). First visitor
only left a stamp image of a small fish.

WY Happy Jack Found 10-15-03- This box was also in great condition as
I was the first finder!! I was so excited!! I did have to wander a
little bit, but only because I didn't read ALL of the clues!! This
is another great spot and a very easy find!!

MT Boulder River Secret Found 10-16-03- This box is in Natural Bridge
and Falls State Park and is absolutely breathtaking!! I was so
intrigued by the park that I locked my keys in my car!! Luckily
(literally) there was someone else at the park and we got in without
having to cause any damage!! The box was in great condition as I was
the first finder again!! Another easy side trip that was very
welcome after driving through the middle of nowhere!!

Well, that was all I did eventhough I figured I would be the first
finder of any of the other ones in my path!! I was tired and so
excited to spend time with the family. I will be going back to get
all those other boxes that were so close yet so far away!!
