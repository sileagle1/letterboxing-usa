MORE THAN TWO
More than two,
Not two to go -
You must go higher
For you started so low.

This trails not a loop
Look in the northwest
Under logs to the north
Uphill lies your quest.

Continue inland to
The dirty, steep bluff,
Now break out your compass
Youve followed trail enough.

Stand between the twins
And the last one on the right
Shoot an azimuth north
Three thirty is just right.

If it werent for the trees
You could see it from here -
A stone outcropping with a
Skull-like leer.

In the old trees dead roots
Youll find the golden goose
(But watch your footing
The soils kinda loose).
