RI - Relocation of Carolina South Box

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-06-13 21:15:03 UTC

Back from the Pacific Northwest doing our local box check, we noticed
that the access to the Carolina South box was becoming overgrown with
poison ivy. (Guess this is why it?s always a good thing to keep
rechecking boxes planted early in the season!) The box has been
relocated to a spot across from the faded white picket fence cemetery,
about 6 steps in along the east side of the south-tending stone wall,
as noted in our revised clues. We also wanted to point out that the
Grass Pond box clues have been posted on our web page for well over a
month now, and clues for other boxes already planted should be coming
out soon!
