Boxes found, Hitchhikers found

From: mepanj (mepanj@kynd.net) | Date: 2003-10-12 12:31:37 UTC
Had the opportunity to box in two states this weekend. Boxing wasn't
the reason for my travels, but you have to take the chance when you
get it! Found two boxes and one hitchhiker.

In PA: the Oak Leaf letterbox. Great location and interesting box.
Wanted to get to the Threefalls Series, but time just wouldn't
permit. Darn!

In ME: Just had to stop for the State of Maine box at the rest
station. Told the kids that if we could drive straight through from
PA without stopping at all then we would have time to stop. They
made! And so we got to stop. What a great way to be welcomed home!
While there we picked up the Butterfly Hitchhiker and will set it
free soon.

And since it is sooooo sunny out (and not yet below frezing) we will
head out again today to see what we can find!
