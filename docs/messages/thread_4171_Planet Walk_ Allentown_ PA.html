Well, we did the first two Planet Walk stamps this
afternoon (around 1:30), and must have missed See Joy
and friends by a narrow margin! Lots of winter
letterboxing going on there - I was there on 1-10, but

I'll head for the 3rd box later this week - we had a
young borzoi training with us and didn't want her out
for too long, so we didn't finish today :(
