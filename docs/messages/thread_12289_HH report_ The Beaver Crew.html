We found the Beaver Crew HH at the NL Harbor Light box in Groton at
Uconn / Avery Point. It was left there by someone who forgot to
add their personal stamp in it ( I think the name was Melissa? ) on
4/15/03 and they were awaiting the arrival of the USS Toledo into
Groton Sub-Base after the conflict in Iraq. Prior to that I see
Canopy Rock on 3/20/03 and Meshamosic SF on 3/11/03. Other visits
include the winter gathering, Butterfly and BlueBird (several
times), Pete and wanda and many many other personal stamps. We
visited NL Ledge Light and will probably put this guy back out in
the next week or so!
