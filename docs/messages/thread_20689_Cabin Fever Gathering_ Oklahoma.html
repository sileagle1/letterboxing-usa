From: jnm4scooter (jnm4scooter@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-12 17:58:47 UTC

It's happening! The first Letterboxing Event in Oklahoma (correct me
if I'm wrong). I'm trying to get a web page set up on Doug's site,
but in the meantime, here's a little info:

-Name- Cabin Fever Gathering
-Date- we're shooting for is April 2, 2005
-Time- as long as there's daylight . . .
-Where- Dolese Youth Park, Warr Acres, OK (Near OKC)
-RSVP at jnm4scooter at yahoo.com & you can let me know what
you'd like to contribute to the pot-luck.
-You can begin placing boxes in the area for the event if you
wish (although it could be a loooong winter) & e-mail
me the clues (so I can print out a booklet of the clues).
-ANYONE who would like to mail a HH to me for the HH Hostel is
absolutely encouraged to do so. E-mail me privately and I
can get you an address.

Feel free to contact me with any questions, comments, or whatever!
Hope to see lottttsssss of you soon!
