First off, many thanks to Der Mad Stamper for that great hike. I
just completed Act's I and II of the Tryon Creek Quatrain. These are
the first two stamps in my book, and I hope to have Act's III and IV
completed next week.

I implore any Oregon Letterboxers to make their way to Tryon Creek
Park as soon as they can. The park is just plain breathless (and I
don't mean the hike...) The outer trails are blanketed with bronze
and amber leaves and then you turn the corner and the leaves are
green and gold. Astonishing!

Decoding the clues last night built up great anticipation for todays
adventure. Thanks for all the fun,
