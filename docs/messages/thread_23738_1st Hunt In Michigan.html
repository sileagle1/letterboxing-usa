Hi Everyone,

Our first hunt for letterboxing was a success. Although, one of our
hunts we did not find the box. We still carried on, in hopes of
finding our third.. and we did! This is so addicting. I think "us"
parents are more addicted then the kids.. LOL
We did a family friendly one at Hertiage Park in Adrian Michigan
close to our home with our 3 kids. I bought notebooks with plastic
type covers for the kids and each of them have their very own stamp.
I made
sure to buy smaller type stamps, not to take up too much room on the
letterbox journals.
I really believe that the kids carrying their own journal is really
an awesome idea.. Our soon to be 4thgrader and 2and grader will be
able to work on those reading and writing skills with out much fuss
from them.
:)
Thank you all for all the great "hunts"
We can't wait to beable to hide one of our own
Peace,
Bren & Family
