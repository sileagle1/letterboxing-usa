Title: Light Wait. Placed 12-9-03 by Salamander. Difficulty: Easy.
Location: Bobblett Cemetery, Old Rt. 50, (Co. Rd. 13A), Vinton
County, Ohio, between Londonderry and Allensville Ohio. CLUES:
This cemetery is shaped basically like a large rectangle. You must
find a blue water pump which is near a white flag pole, located
approximately in the center of the cemetery. Standing just south of
the pump, take a compass reading of 183 degrees. Twelve paces at
this heading will take you to the letterbox, hidden under a rock at
the base of a stump.
