The Connecticut pages were getting a bit unwieldy for those of us
with slow old computers and for those high tech wunderkind who are

sections (east and west of the CT River) into five:

Southwest; Fairfield, New Haven, and Middlesex counties

Southeast; New London county

your "refresh" buttons and check to make sure I didn't accidently
lose any of your letterboxes.
