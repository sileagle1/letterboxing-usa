----->> South Sound Summer Letterbox Fest <<-----

Sat, 9 Aug 2003
1000-until you want to leave
Tumwater Historical Park
777 Simmons Rd SW
Tumwater, WA (Olympia area)

Meet other regional letterboxers, exchange signature stamps, get rid
of all of those 'unwanted' hitchhiker letterboxes, bring your house

likely ensue after all of the letterboxing festivities. Don't
forget to bring the munchkins as there is a GREAT playground
nearby! If you have a few folks who you've been dying to introduce
to letterboxing, bring them along, too! I'm sure we can get them
hooked...

POTLUCK--bring a dish & drinks to share that would feed the folks in
your party

> If you are NorthBound on I-5, you have the easy route. Take Exit

the entrance to the Thurston County CourthouZ. When you get to the
bottom of the hill, you are on Deschutes Parkway. Turn right, go
under the Freeway, don't turn left onto I-5 NB, but turn left at the
second road, which takes you into the Tumwater Historical Park.
>
> If all goes well, we will be loitering around the picnic tables by
