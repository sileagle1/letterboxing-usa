I am assuming you are referring to the clues posted in a message to
this list on Monday, May 5 (post #26106)?

Posting clues to this list is no longer the procedure to get them
listed on the Letterboxing North America (letterboxing.org) site.
The old site has transitioned to a new self-serve web site.
Therefore you are able to enter the clues directly to the site
yourself.

Go to:

http://letterboxing.wesgarrison.us/

First you will need create an account by clicking on "login" and then
the "sign up now" link on the bottom of the page that comes up.
Follow the directions to create an account.

Once you have a login name, log into the site and go to "member
services." Then click on "add boxes" and you can input your box
information and clues.

SpringChick
