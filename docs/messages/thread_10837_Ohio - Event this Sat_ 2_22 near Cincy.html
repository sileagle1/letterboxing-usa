Orienteering Cincinnati is holding a club event this Sat. 2/22 at
Mitchell Memorial Forest that will include (in addition to our
standard courses) a special course for GPS users that will end in a
unique area of the park where participants will search for three
small letterboxes. I have planned a course that will be approx. 2.6
miles that will include some of the best aspects of orienteering,
geocaching and letterboxing. Further details can be found at the
www.geocaching.com web page. I hope anyone interested, will be able
to jion us.

Kevin M. - Hard Shell Kamping Klub & OCIN
