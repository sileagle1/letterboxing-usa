From: Renee Hartsook (renee@ciliated-cell.com) | Date: 2007-03-20 22:05:45 UTC-07:00
Daniel,

I LOVE clipart.com

Not free, but some really nice designs. They charge per week, so I
browse the catalog and wait until I've found at least a half dozen or
so images I think I'll want. Then I pay the fee and download like a
maniac for the entire week. There are about 30 letterbox ideas I'm
mulling--of those I've created probably 10 and about 5 of those are
actually planted!! My point is, the clipart costs a little bit more,
but I've been really happy with the quality and variety.



Also, there is a random free site I stumbled across:

http://etc.usf.edu/clipart/

Depending upon what you are looking for, the free Educational
Technology Clearinghouse has some usable art.


For REALLY basic stuff, don't forget to check out the Microsoft Office
clipart collection. There are some good designs in there very amenable
to stamps (our signature stamp is based on clipart from Microsoft
Office!).

Hope that helps,
Renee
