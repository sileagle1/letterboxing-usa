Hey All!

My cousin and I went on a hike in the Columbia Gorge today. Horsetail,
Ponytail and Triple Falls were gorgeous! We checked out the Triple
Falls Letterbox placed by Der Mad Stamper. It is alive and well and
still more non-letterboxers find it than letterboxers do. But they are
respectful, leave notes and replace the box. The log book has a few
sheets left to stamp in, but it's getting very full.

Thanks to Lori "Lucky Charm" Lott, I was able to finally achieve the
Multnomah Falls letterbox image I've wanted to have in my logbook for so
long. Third time is a charm and my cousin is nothing but good luck!!!
What a great way to end a beautiful day in the Gorge. I can't believe
that I finally found this box. YIPPEE!!! I only wish Pony Tail was
still there, although, I've found the hiding place before and know it's
been missing for quite sometime.

This was a day of wonderful scenery and company complimented by
letterboxing that I didn't think I'd be able to do. Yowza!!!

Best regards,

Amanda Briles
