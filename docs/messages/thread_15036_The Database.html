From: Deb King (debean75@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-12 07:27:42 UTC-07:00
To me "Pretty sure we had the right spot-no sign of box" just means that the person looking had no luck. To me it means that maybe, MAYBE, the poster might want to check it if it is close (another point to posting boxes close to home) or at least keep an eye open for other people having problems. I usually say "MIA?". (note the question mark) As in, the box might be there and I just suck at reading the clues. This seems akin to having an A count for Attempted. If I see a "MIA?" on the database, I still will try for the box if I'm in the area. Just my take on the situation.
