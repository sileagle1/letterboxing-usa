Saluda Shoals Letterbox
Placed by Usagi-A on 9.22.2002
Saluda Shoals Park, 5605 Bush River Road, Columbia, SC
Admission: $4/car
http://www.saludashoals.com/
Easy, scenic walk.

Make a day of this. Take family, friends, Fido, and a picnic lunch. Park
in Observation Deck parking area.

After admiring the beautiful Saluda River, a designated State Scenic River,
from the Observation Deck, walk SE onto the unpaved Nature Trail. Pass a
Y-shaped tree on the left. Continue to a split trunked tree on the right
just before a gravel swale across the path. From that tree count 45 steps
to a small clearing on the left. Look in the cleft behind the largest tree
in the clearing. The letterbox is hidden under sticks and leaves.
