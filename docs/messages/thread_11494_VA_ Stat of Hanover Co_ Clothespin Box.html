I've gotten feedback from various boxers that they could not locate my
Clothespin Box in Hanover County, Virginia. I visited this box today
with the clues from letterboxing.org in hand and found it no problem
strictly by following the clues. I feared that I had written "right"
when it should have said "left" or something like that, but I hadn't.

I'm sorry that several of us have had trouble locating the box; take
heart, because two boxers have found the box since it was planted in
late January. The box is most definitely within public park boundaries
and NOT on private land, as some boxers have wondered.

I want my boxes found often and to have their logbooks filled to the
brim, and I was very concerned when I kept hearing that boxer after
boxer couldn't find it. Contact me if you have any other questions,
and good luck!
