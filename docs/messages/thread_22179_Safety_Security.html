I think most folks have probably figured out where I stand when it
comes to the Second Amendment, but I don't believe this is the proper
forum to criticize or discuss a person's civil rights.

If you would like to read an article concerning "Safety/Security" I
have posted at several web sites then I encourage you to do so. See AQ
under Code of Conduct, or Letterboxing 101 at Mark and Sue's site, or
under aticles at Silent Doug's site, and there is a link posted at the
newboxers site as well.

FYI, Pepper spray has a very short shelf life as does Bear Spray.
Regular Pepper spray can be seasoning for bears and vicious dogs i.e
PitBulls, and may not stop a cougar.

The bottom line in this discussion should be your safety and your
security and not everyone will arrive at the same conclusion on how to
remain safe, it should not be a discussion on how to repeal our civil
rights. Some folks don't like guns and some folks appreciate their
value, and neither side will change their opinion.
