From: John P. Sullivan (suljohn@cox.net) | Date: 2003-03-23 18:28:10 UTC-05:00
To all,
Well, it was a great boxing weekend on Block Island.
For those of you who've placed boxes there, we found them all. The Boy
Scout Troop 540 from Glastonbury (I have two sons in this troop) biked all
over the island hunting and seeking boxes and we have the following
report(s):

1. Gal Pals: Please write me directly - I had to move one of the boxes as
it was submerged. Stamp undamaged and in good shape, all else, well . . .
2. White Rock at Black Rock: Excellent shape. Not alot of visitors but
what a great spot.
3. North End: Lighthouse good shape (book practically full), treasure
chest excellent shape.
4. Indian Cemetery: Excellent shape, book full, working on extra blank
pages.
5. SE Lighthouse: Stamp pad needs to be replaced (missing).

Happy Hunting,

Great Bear
