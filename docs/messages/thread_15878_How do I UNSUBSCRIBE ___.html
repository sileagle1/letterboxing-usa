From: Molly Ives Brower (mibrower@eng.buffalo.edu) | Date: 2003-09-19 17:04:46 UTC-04:00
1. Go to http://www.yahoo.com
2. In the line that starts with Connect, click on the link for Groups
3. Click on the Sign In link and sign in with your Yahoo! username and
password
4. Under My Groups, click on the link for letterbox-usa
5. Under Membership, click on the link for Leave Group
6. Next time you join a list, file the unsubscribe instructions
somewhere. ;-)
