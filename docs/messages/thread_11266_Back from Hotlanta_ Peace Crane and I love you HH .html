From: letterboxing mobots (themobots@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-03-17 17:17:13 UTC-08:00
Whew, longer walk than we expected, but really cool.
Luckily the long hike to the peace crane Box
conveniently put us back near the B&B where we were
staying. We were surprised more people haven't done
this box. there were only a few stamps in it. I would
advise people to take MARTA rather than driving
because the start and finish are relatively close to
MARTA stations. You end up in a really cool
neighborhood at the end. The only problem we had was
that we used the wrong #'s for the math problem or the
compass direction was way off. We would advise
checking around for other trees that match the hiding
place description. We found it by accident after we
had given up. :)

Oh, the I Love you HH has also made it into the South
from its starting place in New England.
