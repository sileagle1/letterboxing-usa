This may be a cross-post, but I feel the need to be optomistic here

snowing for part of the morning, dumping 4 or 5 more inches, BUT

out trudging through the snow to check on some of my hibernating
boxes!
Spring is coming and the Snowdrops are peeking out of the mud and
snow in our garden! Life is good!
