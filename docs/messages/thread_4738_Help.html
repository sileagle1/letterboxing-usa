From: letterboxing mobots (themobots@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-29 10:07:46 UTC-07:00
Hi Macaco,

I don't know about letterboxes in Brasil, but if there were any (at least in parks in the Rio area), they would probably be in some of the larger parks (like Pico de Tijuca), and those parks tend to be on the sides of the morros (granite mountains), which is also where the majority of the favelas are. I don't want to be alarmist, but if you do find clues and/or go exploring in the parks, please don't go alone!

Sorry for the unsolicited advice--if you've been to Brasil before, this is all stuff you probably know, but I'd feel bad not sending the info.

Boa sorte, e espero que voce goste de sua viagem!
