Can you see the Date Line interview.

>>>>>So tell me again why are you in jail?

Well I hid a tupperware in the woods containing a log and hand

and enjoy the park it was in.

here?

No, just multitudes of people arriving at different times to find it
and enjoy the area.

>>>>>And they put you in jail?

Ya I'm bunking with Bubba who killed his whole family after smoking
crack. He wants me to carve him a P stamp so when he gets out on a

>>>>>Mr. Legislator why do you think Mr. Nut should be in jail?

won't have that.

>>>>>Well that doesn't sound so bad.

Oh but it is. These LB'rs walk off the trails and damage the
vegetation. The next thing they'll do is pick up trash along the
trails and then who knows what next.

>>>>>Isn't it park policy to walk off the trail 50' or more to
answer the call of nature and what's wrong with picking up trash?

Well when they pick up trash it's taking away jobs from our Rangers
and I don't think anyone pee's in the woods anymore.

-------------------------------------------------------

I can see hard headed bureaucracy is hard at work.
