WA: Early Birds and Road Trippin

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2002-12-01 23:49:18 UTC
The Early Bird almost gets the "Z" box. I suspect if I had NOT
stopped for my Americano ( 12 oz with 4 shots) OR driven the careful
speed limit thru the Tacoma to Oly speed trap, I would have been
firstie.

At least I stamped in correctly. :-p

Trail and Error kicked the tires on my new car and in turn showed me
a HUGE and Beautiful mushroom ( of which he took a picture) I am
surprised to see such a mushroom in the courthouse area as I believe
it is one that could land you in the clink ( or the morgue???)

I swung thru and collected the last of the Thurston county boxes
planted by DVN2R SKR and a lone animal. I then visited the new Elbe
Train LB.

We have been under a fog inversion here in Puget Sound Country and to
finally get out into dazzling sunshine ( and 60* and the burger stand)
was my main goal

Mt Rainier was breathtaking as always. I would encourage anyone
visiting this LB to take the time and go down to the sand bar and
look at the tracks in the sand, as suggested. The trail is short,
but that just gives you more time to enjoy your burger.
