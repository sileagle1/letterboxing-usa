Funhog and I were out searching today and decided to leave some things
behind for the next letterboxers that stop by. To find the Wild Rose
Letterbox you will have to find the Buxton Trestle, Horshoe Trestle and
Picnic Area letterboxes in the Banks-Vernonia network. The terrain and
clue are both pretty easy.

The Wild Rose:
Once you have found the Picnic Area box, continue down the trail. Walk
between the two gates and keep an eye out for a group of large cedar trees
on your left. Climb around behind the farthest tree and roll away the
miniature cairn that guards the box.

Enjoy!
