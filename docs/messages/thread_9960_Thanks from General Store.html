Thanks from General Store

From: mothermoo2001 <cstearns07@hotmail.com> (cstearns07@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-01-01 18:29:39 UTC
Went to visit the General Store in Mansfield Center,Ct. yesterday.
It has a indoor letterbox and we sat down over a sandwich and
counted stamps. In 18 months 227 stamps and many wonderful comments
were added to the log books. Yes it is on its second book already. It
is such a warm friendly location complete with coffee and hot soup.
The folks at the store are very happy hosting the letterbox and often
chuckle as people try to " sneak" it out of its spot.
Started the New Year right Party bear ( not seen frequently)
was willing to accompany me to the Pink Panther series. We had a nice
walk in the light rain. Great escape had aquired some water but
contents werte dry in bag. I dried the box out with my mittens and
returned it to its spot. Thanks for a fun series a perfect quick one
before the next storm. Leader of the Pack
