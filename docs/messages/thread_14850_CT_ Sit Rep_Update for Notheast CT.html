CT: Sit Rep/Update for Notheast CT

From: Odilio (odiliofinder@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-05 12:44:11 UTC-07:00
Irishtinker and I went out in the Northeast Corner before the rain today. We found the following boxes all in good shape:
1) Clark's Corner (almost earned the Purple Heart but made a quick save sliding down and embankment)
2) Rainy Day Box (right out in the open though)
3) Knight and Day Box (Really liked this one)
4) #1 Teddy (one of my favorite carvings by Irishtinker so far)
5) Test Tube (Very hard to open container) Neat container to hide it in though
6) Massachusetts State Stamp (Good thing Irishtinker was with me because I wasn't
going far enough on the trail)
7) Falling Stars (Also right out in the opening)(Irishtinker almost earned the Purple Heart here because I hit her with log book to kill a mosquito, Twice!!)
8) Otter See This (neat way the clues are written)
Great day. We had a lot of fun and enjoyed all the boxes. Thank you everyone!!!!!!!!!!!!
Odilio
