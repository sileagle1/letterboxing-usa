Glidden Park-Collinsville IL, Madison County
(Lebanon Road)
Follow the walking trail counter clockwise from the skating rink south. When
finally reaching the SE corner of the path - STOP.
Still to the SE should be a tree with a star shaped leaf - Take one home
...is it a sweet gum?
Place your back to the south side of the tree. Using your compass dial up
200degrees, put "Red Fred to Bed" and take your strides down the hill
--> 14 (Adult), 16 (8 year old) or 19 (6 year old).
STOP.
Turn east and look about 5' into the brush line. It may seem a little ODD -
but we recycled! Open up the old box...PLEASE DON'T PULL IT OUT OF THE
GROUND!!!
******STAMP UP*******
Be sure to recover it with lots of sticks ...add a few if you can.
