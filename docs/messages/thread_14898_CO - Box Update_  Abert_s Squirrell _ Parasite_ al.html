CO - Box Update: Abert's Squirrell & Parasite, also a HH

From: tlrewok (teri@adminpowerinc.com) | Date: 2003-08-07 15:54:41 UTC
Hello to the interested!! Yesterday, Aug. 6, Autumn, Fiona, Ariana,
Trinity Jah and I met up in Bailey to hunt the above mentioned box.
The spot was beautiful!! And, to our surprise a HH!!! The HH is
Pandy's Bear's Angel. We all stamped it and let Fiona take it to be
placed on one of her next adventures!! I never in a million years
thought that I would see the stamps of the like of Pandy, JuggleMouse
and some of the other "infamous" LBers. The Abert's Squirrel &
Parasite boxes were in great locations with really good clues. I
will update the placers. Should I post the HH info on that talk list
or is that someone elses job? I am sorry if this question is one of
those "newbie" questions that gets asked all of the time. This was a
first HH for all of us and I was delegated to do the updates. Thank
you to the placers and thank you in advance for answering my
question!!

Ewok & Trinity Jah (on behalf of Fiona & Autumn)

I have to tell you this was a great day! We found the box, found the
parasite box, and 'lo and behold a hitchhiker to boot (a rarity in

butt with her index fingers in the air imitating the "grown-ups"
doing a happy dance. I nearly peed myself.

Great boxes, great exchanges, lovely day!
Autumn
