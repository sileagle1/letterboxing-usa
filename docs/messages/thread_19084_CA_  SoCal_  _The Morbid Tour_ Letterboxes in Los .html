For anyone looking for a change of pace from Southern California
mountains and parks, I highly recommend "The Morbid Tour"
letterboxes, recently planted by Q. Along with jennby's "Rough Night
in WH," these are the first letterboxes I know of planted in urban
West Los Angeles; not in Griffith Park, not up in the Hollywood
Hills, but on Sunset and Hollywood Boulevards. The stamps are
terrific.
