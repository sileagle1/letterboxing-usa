CT Hunting Update

From: Steve (shol3039@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-03 03:37:43 UTC
Just a reminder to all boxers in CT that Spring Turkey season opens
Wed May 7th and lasts the rest of the month. State law limits the
time of day from right around sunrise to 12:00 noon. Please wear
your orange and remember that if you do hear a turkey don't
approach.... as spring turkey hunters are NOT required to wear
orange.

Also may want to stay away from shades of red and blue as these are
common colors found on the turkeys.

For more info see the CT DEP page at www.dep.state.ct.us
