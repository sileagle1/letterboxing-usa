Welcome SnowAngel & Waldenrun!

Hammonasset River & Chatfield Hollow (Witches Trail) are on the
Western CT list. The CT River is the dividing landmark.
However, Seven Falls, Bear Hill, Turtle Creek and Cheap Date, which
are on the Eastern list, are actually west of the river too, though
just barely.

Bruce and I were out today scouting for new hiding places, and should
have some clues to post tomorrow. We had a blast at Hartman Park
yesterday with the gang, and we can tell from Jay's "Happiness" that
he is truly a demented individual...but we won't elaborate about that
here...better for each to see for his/herself.

The polling for the Spring Gathering weekend closes tomorrow night. If
anyone has a preference who hasn't voted, better cast your vote now.
This is regarding the Gathering planned for Sleeping Giant in Hamden,
CT.

Happy Trails,
Aili
