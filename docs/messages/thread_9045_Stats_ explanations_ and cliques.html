F= find
T= traveller
V= vitual tours

C= confiscated
HH= hitchhiker
E= events

I think that is all the important abbreviations. Any other abbs. are

a code or clues to a future letterbox(thinking outside the box). For
more information you will need to go on a hunt. Have fun reading.

From: (Paddle2See@aol.com) | Date: 2002-11-12 08:32:56 UTC-05:00
I'm with mudbug on this one, I have no problem with people putting in stats,
I like seeing what people are up to. It does bother me a little bit when
people make up their own stat letters. Because I end up wasting time trying
to figure out what they mean (if only a few seconds before I determine that
it is bogus noise). Presumably, people are e-mailing to communicate. They
are using the list to communicate to a whole lot of people at once. And then
to withhold the meaning of certain "stats" except to a few close personal
friends is just rude to the rest of us.

However, I love the sport of letterboxing and I love reading about people's
adventures while letterboxing. So I'm willing to put up with the foolishness
and will just learn to ignore it. It certainly isn't worth getting worked up
over.
