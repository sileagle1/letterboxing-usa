From: Amanda Arkebauer (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2001-10-24 16:00:01 UTC
Hi there! I made it to F100 on October 23rd. My 100th stamp was the
Octopus placed by Funhog out here in the Pacific Northwest. It was a
very rainy weekend for letterboxing, but I was so close to the 100
goal, that I did not let a little bad weather deter me.

I also picked up Horsetail Falls, Who Let the Dogs Out, PCC Sylvania
#1, Rock Creek Dragon, Bronson Creek, Cedar Hills Twins #2 (Thanks
Ryan for the inspiration), the Tryon Creek Orphan, and Beaver Creek
Letterbox #1.

Funhog---I hunted for, but did not find the Oak Bottoms and the
Crystal Springs Rhododendron Garden boxes. Which I thought was odd,
since they were just placed! I'll try to contact you off the list, to
discuss the clues, maybe I am just dense. I also wimped out on the
PCC Sylvania boxes #2 and #3. It was just too rainy to balance the
compass and the umbrella for so many paces!! I also came up empty
handed at the Rock Creek Troll and the Blue Lake letterbox.

Whew! It was a busy 3 days in Portland!! But thanks to everyone down
there for placing such terrific stamp images.

Amanda from Seattle
