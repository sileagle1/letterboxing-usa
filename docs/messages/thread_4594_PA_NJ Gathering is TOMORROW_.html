March 2, 2002: The Pennsylvania/New Jersey Letterboxing Summit is
approaching! The good folks on the Yahoo LBNA Discussion Board have decided
to meet at 4:00pm at the "Fish Passage Way" at the Old Canal Museum off of
Route 611 in Easton, PA. If the weather is too sloppy, we'll meet at the lobby
of the Best Western (610-253-9131) just around the corner.

Then, afterwards, we'll all bounce over to the Raubsville Tavern (25 Canal
Road, off of Route 611, Raubsville, PA, 610-252-2165) for dinner.

How do you get there from New Jersey? Well, just take either Route 22 or
Route 78 west. As soon as you enter Pennsylvania, you're already there. Click
here for a map which shows the general area in relation to these highways..
