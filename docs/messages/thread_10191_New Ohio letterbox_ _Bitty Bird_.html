This letterbox was placed in Scioto Trails State Park,near
Chillicothe, Ohio (Ross County), on 1-11-03, by
Salamander.DIFFICULTY:EASY. **Please bring a stamppad and pen with
you. DRIVING DIRECTIONS: Scioto Trails is located approximately 11
miles south of Chillicothe Ohio, off of US-23.You will turn east from
23 onto SR-372. CLUES: Follow Route 372 (the park entrance)
approximately 0.6 mile to the fire tower. At the northwest leg of the
tower, take a reading of 285 degrees and go 21 paces into a grove of
tall pine trees. The Bitty Bird is under a pile of logs, hidden by
some dead leaves and pine needles.
