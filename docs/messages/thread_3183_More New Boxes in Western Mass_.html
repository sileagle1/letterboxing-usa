More New Boxes in Western Mass.

From: (bsennott@crocker.com) | Date: 2001-08-29 21:22:40 UTC
Hi everyone. Today I hid two new boxes on the scenic Loop Trail that
circles around Ruggles Pond at Wendell State Forest. I also checked
onmy three boxes on the Metacomet-Monadnock Trail, two of which are
now three years old. All were intact and have been getting visitors
steadily. I substituted newer containers for a couple of them that
had been leaking and rewrote the clues to make the Saint-Exupery
Letterbox and Letterbox Vincent less difficult to find. My apologies
to anyone who has looked for these two unsuccessfully! I've decided
the locations are just fine but the clues needed improvement.

This means there are now FIVE boxes at Wendell State Forest. It may
not be Dartmoor, but it's a start! The clues are here:

http://www.crocker.com/~bsennott/wendell.html

Thanks to all of you who have written such nice notes in the logbooks
and/or left little gifts. I was excited to see a couple of
hitchhikers have passed through the El Corazon box.

Happy letterboxing in Massachusetts!
