Responding to Spam and Adult Content Posts

From: pandora{HIB} (hispandora@phlsystems.com) | Date: 2003-11-23 17:39:46 UTC
This is not meant to be a slap on the hand to anyone, just an
explanation and request for the future. One of the big things that
the moderators on this board have been asked to do is be quick about
getting rid of spam messages sent to the board, especially ones of a
lewd nature or that reference adult content. I understand that such
things can be offensive to some, funny to others, and annoying to
many, and we do our best to get rid of them as soon as we see them.
However to keep such things off the list we also have to delete any
replies to the offending post. In the future I would ask that anyone
that sees such a post because we haven't gotten to it fast enough,
just ignore the thread please. I hate to delete posts from
legitimate letter boxers, and feel guilty doing it.

Thanks in advance!
pandora{HIB} "Pandy" of Team Chaos
