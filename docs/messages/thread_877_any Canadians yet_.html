From: Murray (murray@granite.mb.ca) | Date: 2000-04-30 14:14:36 UTC-05:00
Hi there,
    I read the Smithsonian article and was intrigued.  Love the Letterboxing N.A. site.  I have been carving rubber stamps, and tramping around, for years and this just puts it all together.  I'm also a minister and this Easter I prepared a letterboxing Easter pilgrimage along a beautiful trail that winds along the riverbank of our town.  It was very succesful, five letterboxes with an Easter theme, and twenty one people took part.  Now I want to get some letterboxes out on the trails that surround our town.  A nice bonus is that the soon to be complete trans-canada trail system will come right through here.  Any other Canadians along the trail interested in getting out some letterboxes to add to the experience?
Contact me, Robert Murray, at Box 222, Pinawa, Manitoba, R0E 1L0 - or e-mail murray@granite.mb.ca  Check out our town on the web, http://www.pinawa.com/ with a cool picture of our new pedestrian suspension bridge on a local trail.
