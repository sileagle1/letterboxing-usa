Hi Amanda! Sounds like the OR gathering was a rousing success! I'm glad; I
wish I could have come! Our mini-gathering here in ID was so nice. There
may have only been a handful of us, but that is still a handful more than I
have ever been able to see. When you are the only letterboxer in half of a
large state, an exchange becomes a whole different exciting event. It was
so fun to talk letterboxing with others that didn't just look at me with a
baffled look (those there are as addicted as I have become, if not more).

Anyway, I had a question for you. I remember you saying that you were
coming to Blackfoot around the end of July. I wanted to write and see if
you are still planning on that. If so, you will have to let me know. Don
and Gwen and A and J Bear just planted a couple of new boxes in Blackfoot
that aren't on the map yet, and I have several new ones in Idaho Falls.
It's wild, when I started this whole thing earlier this year, there were
only two boxes in all of Eastern ID. Now there's somewhere near 16 or 17!!
I just love it!

Also, if you have time and would like to meet for an exchange, I would be
happy to see you. If you are busy with family obligations, I understand.
Just thought I'd throw the thought out and see if it landed anywhere.

Happy hunting,
Cadenza
