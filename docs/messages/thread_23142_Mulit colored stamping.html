Mulit colored stamping

From: johnandkathy grandfield (goatkicks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-11 12:14:30 UTC-08:00
We have always used the Kaleidacolor brand stamp pads for multi color stamping. They are generally available at the bigger crafts type stores & can be a bit pricey. But they have five colors per stamp pad & come in at least a dozen combinations. They may be a bit big for some folks' preferences. What we like is that the blocks of color seperate & go together by pushing a little lever. Oh, & they are made by: TSUKINEKO, INC. Redmond, WA.
