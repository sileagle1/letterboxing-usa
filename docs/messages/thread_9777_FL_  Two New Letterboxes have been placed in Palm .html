FL: Two New Letterboxes have been placed in Palm Beach County

From: Terrapin Traveliers (terrapintraveliers@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-12-22 19:24:23 UTC-05:00
Location:
Palm Beach County
City of Boca Raton
Rating: Easy clues, and easy terrain
Placed 12/22/02


1. Tiki Idol Letterbox: Find George Snow Park in Boca Raton. Follow the
trail to the Serenoa Glade Preserve. Go to information board. Take the
concrete paved path to the left of the sign and follow in numerical order.
Between location 6 and 7 there is a tree on your left with 5 main trunks.
Look behind and under some debris and you will find that the "Gods Have
Spoken."

2. Florida Friends: This letterbox is dedicated to all of the great
friends we have met along the way during our tenure in Florida--as the
Terrapin Traveliers are currently planning to relocate back to New
England--our point of origin. Find Patch Reef Park in Boca Raton. Park in
the lot that is between the soccer fields and the Pirates Cove Playground.
Look for signs to the "Fit Trail." Walk along the Fit Trail
path--Performing each of the routines at each station is optional!! At
station number 9--(for the 9 years we have lived in Florida) face the sign
and look to the left and behind the sign. (220 degrees on compass) At the
base of a saw palmetto, under a piece of tree stump you will find our
tribute. Watch out for the thorn bushes!
