2 LBs found: Lewis and Clark Village

From: Scott Redd (redd@interbug.com) | Date: 2003-07-28 01:53:33 UTC
This weekend my family and I took a mini-vacation from Omaha, Nebraska
to Kansas City, Missouri. While site-seeing and visiting the Amelia
Earhart Birthplace Museum in Atchinson, Kansas (very cool: weekend
festival in honor of her 106th birthday), we stopped at Lewis and
Clark Village.

We seem the have been the first to find Silver Eagle's and Baby Bear's
"Lewis and Clark in MO" and "L/C Missouri River" letterboxes.

We also found and retrieved Silver Eagle's "Lone Star" hitchhiker,
which has really seen some miles!

Thanks for these fun, educatonal, and historical letterbox hides!
