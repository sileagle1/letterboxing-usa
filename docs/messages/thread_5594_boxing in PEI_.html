 Our family has visited PEI several times in the past years also, and, like Two Maine Cats, absolutely love it, tho we can't say we've ever been letterboxing there. Depending on your route, you may also want to meander along Route 1 through Maine and look for a few letterboxes.  It's about a seven hour trip from the Bangor area, depending on if the road conditions.
