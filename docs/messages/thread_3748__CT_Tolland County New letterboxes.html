This is are second set of letterboxes. It's a Halloween themed set, that
I didn't get a chance to plant until know.

The Pumpkin a Moon and the Cat

Planted by the Willington Gang on November 18, 2001.
1 mile loop its an easy hike along the Willimantic River taking about a
hour to do.

Location: Heron Cove Park Tolland, Connecticut in Tolland County. This
park has a tennis court and two ball fields and a River access for
Kayaks and Canoes.

Directions: Take Route 74 to South River Road. This is the border of
Willington and Tolland. Continue on South River Road for .4 miles and
you will see Heron Cove Park on your left. Go all the way into the park
and park at the very end of the lot.

Short note: When you stamp your journals Stamp Box 1,2 and 3 on top of
each other. To create one picture.
