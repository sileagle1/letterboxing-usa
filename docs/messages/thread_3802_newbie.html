Hi Goattrails, Welcome aboard. I'm sure letterboxers in Idaho are
happy to have a new member who hides as well as seeks. Just a
reminder to all newbies: Remember when posting your clues to the talk
list to put the state abbreviation in the subject line. "New ID
letterbox..." for example. This will greatly help people who are
searching for new letterbox clues. I live in Massachusetts, and with
the number of posts entered on this site, to save time I would just
pass by any new listings that are not in my geographical area.
Seeing the state abbreviation in the subject line is a great time
saver.

From: John Chapman (john@johnsblog.com) | Date: 2010-01-20 17:32:30 UTC-05:00
Hi Kim,

Both letterboxing.org and atlasquest.com allow you to do that. On letterboxing.org
, click on the green flag. After entering the date you found the
letterbox, you'll have an opportunity to send a private note to the
clue owner ("Contact the Placer"). On atlasquest.com, each clue page
has a Record Find button and there is a Record Find option under the
Letterbox menu. When recording a find on AQ, you have the option a
leaving a private or public message. The public message option is most
like the geocaching.com logging option.

BTW, you might want to join the Newboxers group: newboxers. http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/

Happy Hunting,
Choi
