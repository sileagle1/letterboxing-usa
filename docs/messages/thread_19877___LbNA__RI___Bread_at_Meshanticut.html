I have a solution! Hide all of your boxes inside the carcass of a
dead lemur. No one will think it is litter and only a diehard boxer
would be brave enough to stick an arm into a carcass.

So, you might ask, what happens when the carcass decomposes? That's
the fun part. You don't actually use dead lemurs, you manufacture a
facscimile of a dead lemur carcass out of papier mache. A little
stage blood, some fake fur, next thing you know you've got a
reasonably realistic looking lemur corpse.
