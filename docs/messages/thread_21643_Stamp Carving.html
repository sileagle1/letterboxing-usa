Carol:

Hobby Lobby - carving tools and pink stuff/mastercarve - try looking between the
canvasses/framing department and the model kits. Closed on Sundays.

Michaels - near the scrapbooking supplies and rubber stamps. Sunday coupons help.

bought" stamps. Maybe someone else can advise if they have carving tools and material.
Sunday coupons help.

I'm sure Houston also has it share of specialty art supplies stores.

Re: [LbNA] Stamp Carving

From: (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2005-04-03 14:24:52 UTC-04:00
Speedball carving kit, made by Speedball: It's got a carving tool (handle
and two different blades that fit into the handle), some pink stuff carving
medium, and a little booklet that explains how to use it. It's actually much
easier to use than I'd thought it would be, but you still need an x-acto knife
or art knife. You can also buy the carving tool, blades, carving stuff
separately. I found it in the art supplies dept. at Michael's.
