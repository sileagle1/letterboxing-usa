MUSKEGON BEACH BOX

West Michigan, Muskegon County, near Muskegon, MI, placed by Arbo on
7/30/02
A 1-hour easy hike located along the Lake Michigan shoreline in Lake
Harbor Park.
Getting there: From US31, Take the Sternberg Rd. exit west, at the
end of Sternberg Rd. turn right onto Martin Rd; On Martin take the
first road left (Porter). Continue to where Porter ends and turn
right onto Lake Harbor Road, also called The Shoreline Trail. About
mile down Lake Harbor road you will find the Lake Harbor Park, the
entrance being on the left side of the road after crossing the Mona
Lake channel bridge. From there the game's afoot.

Notes

If you're not from this area, be sure to enjoy the beach. The dunes
and woods hold their own charm, but don't miss out on the sun and
waves!

*I think these are oaks, but I could be wrong.
