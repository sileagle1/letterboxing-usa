I was in the stationary section of Staples the other day and a woman
and her husband who were looking for invitations asked the clerk if
they had sealing wax. I believe that in the old days the king wore a
ring with his seal on it and when he wrote an official letter he
melted some wax to seal it and pressed his ring into it to leave an
impression. That would be a unique way of stamping.
