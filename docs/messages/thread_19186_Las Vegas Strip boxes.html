Las Vegas Strip boxes

From: Linda Reedy (lreedy@gracebc.org) | Date: 2004-06-15 12:29:23 UTC-04:00


Just spent 24 hours in Las Vegas. I only had time to find two that were
on the strip (Triangle Building and Feeling Lucky). Triangle Building
needs more paper (unfortunately I had to pack lightly, so I didn't have
any of my supplies) and Feeling Lucky is missing the stamp (although I
used the M&M lid of the microbox as a stamp). I had two
non-letterboxers with me and I think they were more excited than I when
I found them. They made good cover for me as I was digging through the
brush for Feeling Lucky.

Woody's Mama

RE: [LbNA] Re: Las Vegas Strip boxes

From: Linda Reedy (lreedy@gracebc.org) | Date: 2004-06-16 08:20:10 UTC-04:00
Finding Feeling Lucky was pretty funny actually. We spent 20 minutes
looking through the top of the brush. It was already dark and there
were a lot of people standing around taking pictures. I asked one of my
cohorts to cover me as I sat on the ground and just reached under the
fence. I know reaching around on the ground in the dark in Las Vegas is
probably not the safest thing to do, but I was leaving for the airport
shortly and I wasn't going to give up. I must have felt around for
about a minute and felt something plastic. I pulled it out and there it
was! My friend said, "Oh, you're good!" Like I said before, I think
they were as excited as I about finding the boxes.

Woody's Mama
