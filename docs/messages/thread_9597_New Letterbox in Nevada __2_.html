Goldfish Letterbox
Red Rock Canyon
Las Vegas
Clark County
Planted 12/8/02
By Amanda from Seattle

There is a $5 fee to enter the Red Rock Canyon Scenic Loop.
Stop at the White Rock Springs Trailhead.(mile marker 6 on the scenic
loop) There is a lower parking area and then 1/2 mile down a very
bumpy gravel road is the upper parking area. From the SW corner of
the upper parking area take this trail. At the fork, go right.
Continue to the concrete pond. Locals will sometimes stock
this "pond" with goldfish. There were not any there the day I
visited, but you can collect a goldfish for your letterboxing
notebook!

Retrace your steps up the trail and at the point where the trail
curves, look to your left for a Large White Rock that looks like it
has acne! In the crack at the front of this rock is the Goldfish
Letterbox.

Please be discreet and read the waiver of liability and
responsibility!
Thank You
