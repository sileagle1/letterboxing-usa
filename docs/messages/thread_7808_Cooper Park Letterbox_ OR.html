Just placed "Cooper Park Letterbox"

State: Oregon

County: Washington

City: Beaverton

Difficulty: Easy

Enjoy an afternoon at Cooper Park!  Use the swings, and then go play some basketball. -From there you can spot an overgrown corner of the park. Walk down  and find a fallen log with one end peeking out of the brush. The letterbox is located under the log at the opposite end. Please be careful when reaching for it, as there are brambles and prickles around.
