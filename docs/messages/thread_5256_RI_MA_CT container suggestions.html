RI/MA/CT container suggestions

From: Trish Kurdziel (trishkri@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-05-06 13:21:28 UTC-07:00

Those in southern New England with a Christmas Tree Shop nearby may want to check out the selection of plastic containers.  They carry an inexpensive small container for tight areas for $.50 that could easily fit a 1/2 size pen, small-sized log, ink pad and stamp.  They also carry a box comparable to the new style clear rubbermaids with the blue seals for $.99.  The rubber on the seal doesn't seem quite as nice but as the new rubbermaid but still a far cry better than the old rubbermaids and at a very reasonable price.
