From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-02-11 17:08:30 UTC-06:00
I clicked on "View all boxes" at the bottom left portion of the "Letterbox
Search" page on letterboxing.org . It takes a while to load, but that is
the only way I know to find some of the International boxes. Here is what
was listed:

Finca Ecologica 2 Monteverde, CSR Costa Rica
Find Beto 1 Puerto Viejo de Telamanaca, CSR Costa Rica
Ramphastos Paradise Letterbox 1 Puetro Viejo de Sarapiqu, CSR
Costa Rica



Have fun!
