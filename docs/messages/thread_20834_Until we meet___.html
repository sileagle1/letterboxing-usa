Until we meet...

From: Tom Noone (mntnlover@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-10-29 09:44:10 UTC-04:00
I wait impatiently to be reunited with my people. We gather in the early
afternoon at Potowomut and are preparing for the event. Chief Taccomanan
cannot attend and the insect zoo has long since gone but we will find much
to share when we meet.

A gifted craftsman from Connecticut has agreed to etch rubber for all to
make an impression of the event. Travellers of a personal nature will find
welcome in the shelter but roving ones should be left in the wild, without a
hitch. The phantom phiddler has already provided direction for treasure
seeking. Look to the web and the event for more.

You are invited to bring the fruits of your harvest (and vegetables, too) to
share with others. Provide your own drink but, fear not, you will not have
to eat with your fingers as the white man's utensils will be provided.

I bid you a safe journey and wait anxiously until we can all be united.

Tom Noone
