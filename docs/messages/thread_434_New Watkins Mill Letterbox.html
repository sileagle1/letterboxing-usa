Thanks for the work on the form Dan. I like the system and it does make it
easy to submit and log.

Just sitting back watching, listening and watch history happening, I can
just see us getting together later in life just the men did in "Chariots of
Fire" and gray old men (of course there will be women there, more of them
than little old men).

Ginny and I are interested in ordering some of those neat containers you
offered as samples. Remember the one you sent to me? It is now doing time
in Arkansas as the first Arkansas letterbox (to my knowledge). As far as
quantity, we could use 10 to 15 for now as we plan our early summer travels.
We hope to also try to fill the void and pick up those states not 'boxed yet
to achieve Y2k. (Thanks for not calling it the millennium, by the way isn't
2001 and not 2000?) You will have to give credit to American Airlines, our
employer to get us there economically. Anyway, let us know how much and we
will send some dough.

FYI, the state of Iowa will fall Mother's Day weekend and probably Nebraska.
We want to salt and not orphan these boxes we set out this year. We could
always put them up for adoption as we grow for the states farther out there.
South Dakota will fall early summer and North Dakota, October (pheasant
hunting season).

Well I best get back to Microsoft flight simulator for my daily fix.

Again thank you for your dedication!
