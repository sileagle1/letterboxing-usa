Nursery Rhyme run in VA

From: be ma (bema57@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-11-07 18:14:15 UTC


What a great day (and it's just noon!). First wonderful teacher conferences
and then a fun jaunt to an adorable park we never knew was there! This is
one of my favorite things about letterboxing -- fun outdoors for the family
discovering all these great treasures!

The kids had a great time with the nursery rhymes and I'm glad they were
there to look under the little places they sometimes need to look! They
bend a lot more easily than the older members of BeMa do and their a lot
more discrete.

We had to keep good track of our little ones because it must have been
fieldtrip day for all the local elementary schools! Kids everywhere!! No
dogs, sorry.

I wish you all would have been there to see the "spectacular" show the
littlest BeMa put on in the Gazebo (Brittany Spears watch out!)

We didn't find Peter Peter until the very end because that back exit was
mobbed with field trippers and we couldn't go out that way. I'm glad we
found it though because it is an adorable spot!

We will definitely go back when the weather is a little nicer. I'm sure it
is beautiful with all the flowers in bloom!

Thank you for leading us to this wonderful place!

BeMa
P2F16
