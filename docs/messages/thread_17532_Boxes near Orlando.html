I think that's it. I was just in Orlando last week. I can tell you that
the Wilderness Lodge and Caribbean Beach boxes are both in
place. The Wilderness Lodge logbook could use a new Ziploc
bag, however, and the Caribbean Beach logbook is full.

I emailed the placer about these things, but if you are searching
for these boxes, pehaps you could be a good citizen and bring
along bags and a fresh logbook (the Caribbean box is big and
can easily hold a 4 x 6 book). I was traveling light and had no
extra supplies when I was there.

I did not search for the Celebration box.

wrote:
> I have a business trip the week of Jan 19 to Orlando.
> I only see three boxes near Disney and another near Cape Canaveral.
> Is that it?

There are some very nice letterboxes in the DeLand area, which is
just about an hour's drive north. Two are in town, and 5 are on an
island. We re-visited the two in town with our son just recently and
they are a hoot! There is also one in DeLeon springs, slightly
north of Deland. We just put one out in the Disney Wildnerness
Preserve just south of Kissimmee last week -- clues will be on the
website within a day or two. If you are traveling by car, we also
put one out at Guana River Wildlife Sanctuary just north of St
Augustine (near Ponte Vedra beach). Why not carve and leave a few
letterboxes while you are there? There are quite a few state and
county parks in the Orlando area that are crying out for letterboxes!
