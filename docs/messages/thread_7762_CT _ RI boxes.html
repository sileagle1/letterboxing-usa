From: mulbie (mulbie@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-09-17 17:50:02 UTC
Fort Adams was in good shape a few weeks ago. The relatively new
letterbox at Pt Judith is also in good shape. Two of the three boxes
(#1 and 2) of Fast Track to Your Degree were in good shape this past
weekend. In addition, there are a number of boxes on The East Bay
Bike Path (from Providence to Bristol, R.I.) that can quite easily be
reached by walking from the appropriate parking lot. If you venture
into Southeastern MA, you could look up Trees (in Fall River) or
either of the Seaside Series (I and II) in Marion, Mattapoisett and
Fairhaven. None require a lot of walking. Have fun!
