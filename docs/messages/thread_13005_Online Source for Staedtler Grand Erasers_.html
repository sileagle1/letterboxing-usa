From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2003-05-21 19:55:54 UTC
Does anyone have an online source for Staedtler Mars Grand Erasers?
I previously got them from April at Stampeaz, but she is out, as is
my secondary source, Du-All Art Supply. I called three other
art/office suppliers and none to be found. Checked Dick Blick and
they do not stock this size.

The story I got from two different places it that although the
company (in Germany) is still making them, the US supplier that all
these places use, has discontinued the product.

I don't generally use these as they are more costly than other
carving materials, but they are convenient size for doing a class.

If anyone is getting these online and would care to share your
supplier, I would be most appreciative. Thanks!

Don't have an online source, but an art supply store in Richmond, VA,
might be able to help. I discovered Plaza recently and was talking to
its inventory manager-type person. Plaza is only a couple of blocks
