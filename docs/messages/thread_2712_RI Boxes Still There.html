RI Boxes Still There

From: Trish Kurdziel (trishkri@yahoo.com) | Date: 2001-07-05 07:51:16 UTC-07:00
We had received reports that Viking Ships at Sunrise and Midnight on the Moon
were missing. We took advantage of the day off yesterday to go check up on
them. Both were still hidden, in fine shape and had been visited recently. At
Black Point the kids searched the tidal pools for crabs while I braved the
poison ivy to go check on the LB. It was a perfect day with the wild
blueberries in peak perfection and the wild roses spreading their heavy scent
on the trail. Fortunately I am not particularly sensitive to poison ivy...

Also, I updated the clues to Viking Ships slightly to accomodate changes in the
area.

Trish Kurdziel
