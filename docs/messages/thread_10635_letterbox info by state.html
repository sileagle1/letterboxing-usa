From: hoopoe4me <scribenbirder@msn.com> (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-02-04 15:15:31 UTC
Go here
http://www.letterboxing.org/index.html

Click on the book and go crazy. Enjoy!

Some people maintain lists for their own state or region privately.
You will find them with time. Sometimes, because they are less busy
than our kind Web Volunteers, the clues show up faster.

As I read the daily posts I copy and paste to WORD, clues for my
region. I then throw them into a folder on my desktop. That way I
have clues ready for that weekend, without having to search back.

Sometimes I miss one, but between our Web master and Mischief who
keeps a washington state page, we are well covered.

Welcome, it is a very addicting activity.
