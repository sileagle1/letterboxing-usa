Thank you Springchick for highlighting the difference between
WOM/offline/off-list. Offlist clues do not constitute off-line in any
way shape or form if they are posted on a blog. Offline means not
distributed electronically through means of internet/blog/email
posting. Off-list is merely diluted 3-fold as opposed to complete
watered down varieties. Those that are thirsty for more juicy options
better keep them offline completely.

You can wait for it to "grow up" or you can actively nurture it along. You could be the one with a few clues posted on a personal blog instead of LbNA or AQ -- there are many free blogging services that take very little time and technical know-how to get off the ground.
