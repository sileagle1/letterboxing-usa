Harris Hill Letterbox

Placed by: JeLyBean
22 September 2002
Chemung County, Big Flats, NY
Terrain: Easy
Clues: Easy

Between Corning and Elmira, NY, the town of Big Flats holds many
attractions for aviation enthusiasts. The National Warplane museum is
located near ELM airport, and in June there is an annual air show here.
South of the airport is Harris Hill, the "Soaring Capital of the World",
with a soaring museum and active gliderport, where you can sit and watch
the sailplanes take off and land, or perhaps buy a ride.
