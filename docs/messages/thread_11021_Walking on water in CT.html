Walking on water in CT

From: haalck (haalck@snet.net) | Date: 2003-03-04 23:32:48 UTC
Yes, you can walk on water. As long as it's frozen. We finally were
able to find the Cormorant letterbox today. Normally you need a boat,
as the box is on an island in Mansfield Hollow Dam (mansfield, CT),
but with the ice at least a foot thick, we were able to walk right
over to the island. Also found the Town Crier Letterbox in Chaplin,
CT. Worth the short hike the see the river exploding with ice and
water into Diana's Pool. Watch the trail though, as it is very icy.
Melted Letterbox in Chaplin is also in fine shape.
