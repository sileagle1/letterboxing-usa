RI - Hour of the Olympics update

From: Mary (RI) (rid25751@ride.ri.net) | Date: 2003-04-21 15:26:25 UTC-04:00
Dan the Safari Man and I stopped by Heritage Park late yesterday for this series.

All three boxes were somewhat exposed, but the first 2 were none the worse for it. We hid them back a little more discreetly. The last box, "Cool Runnings," was completely soaked through. The pen must have exploded, as it was broken. The stamp pad is ruined. There was a note in there from someone who found it that way and it said that he tried to dry it out and left a Happy Meal toy inside. Someone was there afterwards, apparently, as there was no geocache-type stuff inside. We took the box home to dry out the log book and to replace the bags and such. Trish, if you want to start a new log, just let me know. I can either put it back "as is" when dry or get another 3x5 book, or get the stuff to you. I think that the plastic container is no better or worse than it was when new, only stained red, so I'll just keep that the same unless I see a similar replacement in my travels. I have no idea how this box would have gotten so saturated if closed right and tucked under the ledge.

It should only take a few days to dry the book out and replace the ink. I'll post.

Mary (RI)
