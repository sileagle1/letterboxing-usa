From: Tracy Roberts (the_tailchaser@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-05-16 11:59:20 UTC-07:00

Hi everyone,

many thanks for checking out the website. I hope you can return to it again.

My stamps are not hand cut, I wish!!

Instead I use no copyright pictures and make the place names with the fonts on my computer. All the pictures are then placed on an a4 sheet of paper, as many as I can cram on. I then send this to a chap who makes a copy of the sheet into polymer rubber. He then sens them back to me and I cut them out and place them on some plastic . Hey presto one stamp!!

We do have alot of people here who hand cut and I envy them.

Hope that answers a few questions,

Regards The Tailchaser
