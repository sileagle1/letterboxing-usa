SNOWFLAKE Placed 10/04/01 By: Warren Remein, the Bayboxer
Location: Island Lake Recreation Area
12950 E. Grand River Rd. (@Kensington Road)
Brighton, MI 48116 810-229-7067
Difficulty: easy to moderate. Time: short

River Bend
Yellow / Blue
River to cross then 165 too
Split trails, split rails 320d
10 p 1/2 rock 3 hands x 2 hands x 1 hand
Stand on one foot
On rock (if you can do so without falling or hurting yourself)
Yell out loud the magic words
"Letterboxing Forever Please"
If you fail to say the magic word the letterbox
will disappear forever!
Back 3p
Flat post 40d .75 meter vert
Look up
look down
Rotting log
All around


Admission to the park $4.00. Be on guard for hunters,bikers, fishers,
canoers, swimmers, and picnickers. Watch for spiders and bees, poison ivy
(3 leafs, hairy rope), insects, and small animals in these woods. Be
cautious retrieving hidden objects. Be prepared for the weather
conditions, and take note of topography and landmarks, which will return
you to your starting point. The use of these clues is strictly at the
user's own personal risk. There is no express or implied warrantee or
guarantee of any kind with this letterbox clue. Take all trash with you,
whether brought or found, and dispose of it properly. Please do not harm
historical landmarks, wildlife or any living thing, or disturb local
vegetation, geology, geography or watershed. Please sign and stamp the
letterbox if you find it. If Contents are damaged or missing, or you are
unable to sign or stamp in please email me at bayletterbox@juno.com. This
letterbox is intended to be of minimal impact in the park. It is private
property left for public enjoyment. Please the respect my effort and
leave it as you found it, with contents intact and hidden from view.
Contact me if it is missing or damaged. I am committed to the enjoyment,
protection and stewardship of God's creation.
Warren Lee Remein, the Bayboxer bayletterbox@juno.com
