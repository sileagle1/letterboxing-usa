Letterbox Name: Area 51
Date Placed: 01/20/03
Sate: Nevada
Nearest Town: Rachel
 
You'll want to familiarize yourself with the Area 51 lore with a basic internet search, if you don't know about it already.  To find this hidden box, you'll first need to find the infamous 'Black Mailbox'.   This leads you down a dirt/gravel road which joins a road that officially does not exist.  Go as far as you can, you'll know you can't go any further when you see the signs forbidding you to proceed, plus the "Cammo Dudes" will be watching you at this point.  Look for them in their truck on top of the hill. 
 
Facing the continuing road, look to the right.  You should see a small hill with a trail heading to the top.  At the bottom  of this little hill, next to the trail, should be a large rock on the right side, with a smaller rock in front of it.  Move the smaller rock and beneath should be what you're looking for. 
 
If you make it into the little town of Rachel you've passed the 'Black Mailbox', but that's okay.  Stop at the Little Ale'Inn and have yourself some good food, and seek the help of the people there, they'll be more than happy to point you in the right direction.  Have fun and be careful...remember, they're watching you.  You'll be very happy that you made the trip!
