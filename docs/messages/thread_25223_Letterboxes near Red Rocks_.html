From: Stephanie Mustopich (stephmm20@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-07-28 10:35:29 UTC-05:00
Hello and Welcome!

Lighthouse Keeper here, my partner One Love Journeying and I made a trip out
to Colorado in January... and of course did our share of boxing. As far as I
know there are 3 stamps in Garden of the Gods, "Quilted Tree" "Siamese
Twins" and "Garden of the Gods Park".... it's a great place to place a box!
Hope that helps!

Also, seeing that you will be in Red Rocks, might I suggest checking out One
Love Journeying's box, "Box Heads Visit the Rockies," this is a series of
hers that she places all across the country, it's pretty cute! Also, the box
is a drive by so quite easy to pick up. Have a great trip!

Lighthouse Keeper
