UK Letterboxing.

From: Phil Drawbridge (phil@drawbridge.org.uk) | Date: 2003-09-13 12:50:31 UTC
I have read over the past months with great interest about the
exploits of all of you US letterboxers. Strangely enough, although
there are several websites, there isn't an obvious Yahoo group for
letterboxing in the UK. So, I started one.
http://uk.groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-uk/

I created it with the idea of working along side the letterbox-usa
group, (although I have no links with the moderators other than
their blessing). Like the US group, I would welcome people from
anywhere in the world, but if you are planning on visiting (or live
in) the UK, or have any stories or questions or clues (both on and
off Dartmoor) which would be more suited to be posted to a UK group,
please feel free to join and check it out.

I'm sure between the two groups there will be a place for a large
proportion of the worlds letterboxers.

Kindest regards
Phil Drawbridge
