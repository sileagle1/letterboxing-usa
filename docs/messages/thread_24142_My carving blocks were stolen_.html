From: xxxxxxxx (BrighidFarm@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-02-16 16:50:39 UTC-06:00
It could also be right number but the wrong street. Or possibly got
delivered to East instead of West, or North instead of South, etc, if the
same number exists on the other side of the dividing line in your town.

UPS often gets my address mixed up with the same number but one street to
the east of me. And vice versa. So we've come to know the other family a
bit over the years.

But it's a real mess when you're trying to track a package, and hoping that
if it was misdelivered, the other family will be honest and great about
getting it over to you.

Delivery people also often "hide" packages in some strange places,
especially since at Christmas, they caught folks following UPS trucks to
steal packages after they'd been delivered and after the delivery truck went
on aways. Do you have any shrubbery it could be hidden under possibly?
They sometimes leave packages on the doorstep and cover the package with the
doormat, which is more than kinda dumb. Not exactly difficult to see a
package sitting under a doormat, y'know? :-)

Carving Withdrawal can be rough.
