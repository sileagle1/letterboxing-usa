In spite of a very busy weekend I managed to fit in 2 quick
letterboxes, Polly's Rock Quest in South Kingstown and the 4th Rhody
Runaround box in Charlestown. Both boxes are where they belong & are
in good condition.

I also "found" the hitchhiker, Thumbs Up, in my friend Susan's living
room. She has had the hitchhiker for several months but has not been
able to get out anywhere to leave it. She felt bad about hanging on
to it for so long so I offered to help her out by taking it off her
hands & moving it along. I hope to get Thumbs Up back out on the
trail in the next few days.
