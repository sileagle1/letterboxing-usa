How to tell good camouflage

From: woodschuckstraub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-12-22 00:10:32 UTC
You know that a letterbox is camouflaged well if you put it down in
the open and can't find it. I camouflaged a letterbox today by
painting it before planting. I put it down to get other things ready
for my box planting trip. Next thing you know, I lost it. Didn't
remember where I put it and walked by it several times before
Chickabirdie found it for me. Guess the camouflage worked. I thought
it was pretty funny. Should have had Molly find it for me.
