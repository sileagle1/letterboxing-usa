va - an ode to bananaconda

From: be ma (bema57@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-12-24 21:02:22 UTC

We put on our shoes and socks
and went searching for a letter box

We were in no particular hurry,
but then it started to flurry.

Before the snow got any deepa,
we found where the box did sleepa.

We took a stamp of the bananaconda,
an image we're rather fond-a

Under a blanket of leaves and sticks
we bid good bye to find twenty-six.

On the trail we had felt rather hot,
'cause we'd even found a Jackpot!

As the snow turned into sleet
we headed home for something to eat.

As to this terrible poem I'd....
better stop before you head for the bromide!

We'll never replace Jack Prelutsky (sp?) that is for sure!!! Thanks for the
fun box in the cute little park. We can only imagine how nice it is in the
summer when the leaves hide the houses!

Enjoy the snow and happy holidays! BeMa
