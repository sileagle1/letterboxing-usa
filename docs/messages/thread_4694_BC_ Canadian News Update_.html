BC: Canadian News Update!

From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-03-15 05:37:54 UTC
Being in Canada and all, we HAD to get all the letterboxes out
there. =)

For whoever hunts for the Pacific Spirit letterbox, we found it in
sad condition with the box popped open, rain water all over it, and
the one zip-lock bag protecting the contents torn open.
(Surprisingly, the logbook was in remarkably good condition,
though!) We didn't have any zip-locks to replace it, so if you
happen to be in the area hunting for the box, be sure to bring a
freezer-size zip-lock bag. (We had a small one, but the logbook
wouldn't fit in it.)

We were also the first to find the Derby Reach series with wonderful
carvings! It's well worth a trip to Canada to nab them. =)

Amanda sent me on a wild goose chase after a Stanley Park box, but if
there ever was a box (it's not on the website!), it's missing now.
But somebody needs to hide a nice, big series in that park. With
stamps of the Lion's Gate Brige, woman in a wetsuit, totem poles,
miniture railway, racoons (and DON'T feed these things! *rolling
eyes*) and plenty of other great stamps! I would, but I'll be
spending my summer in Central America! =) So somebody else needs to
do it for me to find later. Yep. Definitely needs to be done. =)

-- Ryan
