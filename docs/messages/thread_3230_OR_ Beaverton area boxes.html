Speaking of difficult to find letterboxes.... I finally found
Possum's Penthouse of the Rock Creek Neighborhood Series today! *honk
honk* Didn't find it my first time around, but I found it this time,
darn it. I still didn't find the Coon's Cache letterbox, though, and
I'm SURE I'm looking in the right spot for it. Any reports on
whether it's missing or not?

And finally, the Dragon Box of the Rock Creek Neighborhood
Series.... I went with a friend who was looking for it today and she
got to the right spot, but neither of us were able to find it. I've
found it before, so I know it's the right location, but the "branch
pile" mentioned in the clue has become a "branch graveyard" several
inches thick over a very wide area. It looks like they've been
pruning the trees out there and left all the old branches behind.
(The needles on them were still green, in fact!) So I strongly
suspect the box is still there, but it's a LOT harder to find under
the tons of branches and needles recently showing up there. Bring
plenty of gloves and friends (to help!) when you go looking for this
box! =)

-- Ryan

PS. As a general rule of thumb, if you're certain you're looking in
the right place for a box and you don't find it within 5 minutes,
you're probably either looking in the wrong spot (despite how
absolutely certain you are you've got the right place) or the box is
missing. Nine times out of ten, one of those two things explains why
it took longer than 5 minutes for me to find the box.
