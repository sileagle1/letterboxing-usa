I would just like to add my thanks and congratulations for a
wonderful event. Sir B and JM were fantastic hosts and meeting fellow
letterboxers, both famous and infamous was a thrill. All the stamps
were inspiring and a pleasure to find and add to my book. I will
treasure each and every miniature masterpiece.
We had a very good camping trip and Lopex Lake was beautiful.
Unfortunately we were unable to do Pandora's Chaos letterboxes as
there was a cycling event going on and they were using the trails we
needed.
Sunday was a blast too as Simon was able to do his windsurfing and I
was able to spend some time exploring SLO. My non boxing son
persuaded me to buy Mossflower on tape for our return journey which
we all enjoyed.
Look forward to returning to SLO to do many more boxes!
