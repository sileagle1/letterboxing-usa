From: thedoubtfulguests (thedoubtfulguests@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-03-16 01:23:08 UTC
Sometimes it works to "Refresh" the page. If that does not work try
clearing your temporary internet files: Tools, Internet Options,
Temporary Internet Files, Delete Files. Your internet pages may load
a little slower for awhile after that but sometimes it fixes the LBNA
pages.

If the webmasters copy the files back in a way that the dates on them
are off it tricks your browser into thinking the old files still in
your temporary internet files on your hard drive are newer. It uses
them instead. That is why clearing your temporary internet files
might help. It forces your system to reload from the LBNA site. Of
course if there is really something wrong with the website this won't
help.

Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
