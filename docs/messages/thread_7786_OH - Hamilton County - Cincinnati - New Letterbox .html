Far Back Boxes #1 and #2

Location: Farbach-Werner Nature Preserve, Colerain Avenue,
Cincinnati, Ohio
Directions: From I-275 take the Colerain Avenue exit. Located one
mile south of Northgate Mall; Colerain Avenue to Poole Road to
entrance on left.
Level: Easy
Need: Stamp, stamp pad, pen.

**This series contains a micro-box and a mini-box.**

Clues:

Begin at the 2 benches near the gate to the nature barn.
Pass under the bamboo canopy.
Slippery When Wet follow the arrow.
Note the dyslexic sign.
At the fork take the easy way.
At the next fork change your mind.
Got ants in your pants?
Follow the curves then take a sit.
Variety is the spice of life but look past it to a holey place.
(Midnight Pines)

Now look ahead and cross that bridge when you come to it.
Do the right thing.
See the birdie?
Beware of the rocky road.
See the bench? Run away!
After the meadow box the holly tree points the way.
Look for the tower of power.
At the center of the many-tentacled tree is the end of your trail.
(Into the Sunset)

Continue to the fork left will take you home.

Once Upon A Pond

Location: Triple Creek Park, Buell Road, Cincinnati, Ohio
Directions: From I-275 exit south on Hamilton Avenue. Go right
on Civic Center Drive, right on Pippin Road, the left on Buell Road
to park entrance on left next to the pond.
Level: Easy (1 set of steps)
Need: Stamp, stamp pad, pen.

**This is a mini-box.**

Clues:

Once upon a pond there was a little duck longing for adventure. He
slipped away one day and down the trail he went. Help us to find the
little lost duck!

Follow the trail through the woods and past the clearing.
At the fork go 300 degrees. Follow the trail past the bird feeder.
Go around the bends and up the stairs.
Sit on the bench for a breather. See the tree directly in front of
you?
