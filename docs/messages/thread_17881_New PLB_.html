New PLB!

From: Leanne (ymgleanne@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-06 17:17:48 UTC-07:00
Hi all,

I just wanted to say that I have started my
first PLB! I have made it to be geared towards kids
and their families! I have three little ones ages 7yrs
and under and we love to get PLBoxes! So I thought I
would design one specifically for kids to do with
their parents! So I have made a letterbox that is
going to have a few coloring books and other little
games to play for kids or even the kids at heart!

We just had a big crisis here at my house my
littlest(20 months) fell out the window Tuesday night
the 4th, about a 10 foot drop! I was at Walmart when
it happened getting supplies for this PLB and her dad
was watching the kids! When I returned home I found
granddad here with our other two kids(7yrs and
3.5yrs)! :-( Aubrey is fine no injuries except bruises
they kept her overnight at the hospital! We are
soooooo lucky! So my point is, I want to name the box
after my Dare Devil jump out the window little girl!
So the name of the box is Kamikaze Aubrey!

It will have some activities in it that will
require mom or dad and will be a little messy! So keep
that in mind when you sign up! I do not mind if other
people are added to this list, what I mean by that is
if you are on the list and you have a friend that
lives near you that you can exchange the box with and
then get it back quickly then that is not a problem! I
would prefer not to add extra mailing addresses after
the box is closed though! I would also like this box
to travel with delivery confirmation! Any mail service
is fine with me as long as it has delivery
confirmation! I am going to have to restrict this box
to only 50 people or families! Also I need to know how
many kids or people will be participating per family
so I make sure I have enough coloring pages!:-)

I am rather new to letterboxing so my carving skills
are not wonderful so I hope my hand carved words are
somewhat easy to read! :-) LOL

Thanks so much! So please sign up if this
sounds like something you would like! ;-) My email is
ymgleanne at yahoo dot com

BigMommaOfThree
