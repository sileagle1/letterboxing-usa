Just wanted you to know about 2 weeks ago my, wheelchair bound, husband and
I found the potato creek letterbox in Indiana. It was a bit of a push over
a gravel path, but with assistance this box is wheelchair accessible. I had
to go off the path to actually find it, but I was within eyesight of my
husband so he could enjoy the actually "find"

This was our first attempt at finding a box and we were pleased that we
could do it together. On our next visit to Indiana we will go after the
other ones in the state.
