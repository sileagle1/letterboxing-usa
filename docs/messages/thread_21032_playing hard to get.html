aah, the box that plays hard to get. The Diva and I had one in my town that
we couldn't find one week while she was here. I promised I wouldn't look
for it until she came back. Months later we found that sucker and laughed our
heads off. You know, they all seem so obvious after you find them. It about
killed me to drive by it everyday without driving in to the park for a peek,
but I kept my word. We now have one in her home town eluding us, and we
have made the same promise. Frankly, I just want her to have that sweet
suffering I experienced. Hehehehehe. It really does make the find all that more
glorious. That's my story and I'm sticking to it! Obviously, she doesn't have
access to this list, so if she finds out...
