New RI letterboxes

From: Trish Kurdziel (trishkri@yahoo.com) | Date: 2001-12-30 21:18:43 UTC-08:00

After spending a fun morning boxing with everyone at Hartman Park we decided to check on our Minimalist boxes on the way home.  Our oldest daughter, Alicia, talked the whole way home and on the path through the Beaver River Preserve about carving her own series of boxes.  As soon as she got home she found erasers and carving tools and proceeded to carve the Four Seasons.  She insisted that we plant them today and chose the hiding spots for the boxes herself.  It had been a few years since we had been at Browning Mill Pond in Exeter and were happy to see that the trail has been well maintained and new bridges built. 

Hope you enjoy Alicia's version of the Four Seasons.  http://www.geocities.com/trishkri/4Seasons.html

Trish Kurdziel
