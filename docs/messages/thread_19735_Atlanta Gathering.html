Atlanta Gathering

From: red_bing (redbng@aol.com) | Date: 2004-08-03 13:23:48 UTC
Hi y'all,

Wanted to take one last opportunity to remind everyone that this
Saturday August 7 is the first ever SouthEast Gathering. If you have
the time and the means to come visit, we'd love to meet ya! It
promises to be a much larger event than we first envisioned; there's
a distinct possibility that attendance will exceed 50 people. It's an
all-day gathering, to allow people the freedom to come-and-go, and
the park has a *wide* variety of entertainments for the non-boxing
members of the family. (This last includes a laser-show and fireworks
display at the base of the 825-foot high granite face of Stone
Mountain.) For the avid letterboxer, there's the opportunity to
collect more than 30 hand-carved images, and that doesn't even
include exchanges!

Details and related links and info can be found at:
http://hometown.aol.com/redbng/2004SEG.html

If you want to contact some of the letterboxers who are attending
from at least nine southeastern states, please write to the letterbox-
southeast yahoo group and introduce yourself. We're going to have a
great time, and hope to see y'all there!

-Red
