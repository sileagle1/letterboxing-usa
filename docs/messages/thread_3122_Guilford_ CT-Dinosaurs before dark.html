Hi, I just started letterboxing. I live in northwest CT. I see that there
are many to search for. I spent last weekend in RI and went looking for
several that were close to where I was. I found only one of the three.

I could not locate the Beavertail Light box even though I'm sure I was at
the right spot ( and have the poison ivy to show for it). Next I looked for
the Point Judith Light box. I found the hole, but there was nothing in it.
The grass had been recently cut. Do you suppose the grounds crew removed it?
Next I went searching for the Galilee letterbox. And found it! Thank
heavens, because I was getting a little discouraged. I'm looking forward to
finding more.
