Let it snow.
Blackfoot, Idaho
Bingham County
Placed by A-Bear on 31 December 2002

Directions: From Idaho Falls travel south on I-15 and
stop at the rest area before Blackfoot. Enjoy the
.5/.75 mile trails of Hells Half Acre Lava Beds.
Unfortunately when we were there the trails were
closed to snow otherwise we would have hidden more
letterboxes here.

Level of Difficulty: Easy with a slight incline.

Let It Snow Clues:
Follow the short trail that goes around the main
circular building. Find the western most picnic table
without the half moon shaped protector directly behind
that building. From the table observe the lava due
west. On the northeastern side of the large mound,
remove the 6 diameter lava rock. Reach in for your
small box.

Please read the waiver of responsibility. Be careful
while climbing up to the lava rocks. Replace the lava
rock well to hide the box from those at the picnic
table.
