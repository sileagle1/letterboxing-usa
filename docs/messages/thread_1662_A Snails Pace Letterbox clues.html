A Snails Pace Letterbox clues
Frank G.Bonelli Regional Park in Southern California
Near Pamona,Los Angeles County, California
December 22, 2000

Enter the park off 210 Freeway and go to the West Picnic Valley area.From the
from the Clark Kent changing booth go east along the walkway (parallel to the
parking lot) toward the restrooms. Have a drink there and then proceed to the
bolder. From the bolder go 60 degrees toward a water source. From there go 30
degrees to the green hitching post. Then proceed 270 degrees west up a
dirtroad. Past the recumbent pine at the beginning of the road. Halfway up
the hill you will notice a small Euclyptus tree on the right. Look under the
brick behind the log to the right of that small tree.
