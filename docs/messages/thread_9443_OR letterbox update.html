OR letterbox update

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2002-11-30 02:19:32 UTC
Knowing full well that this is supposedly the year's busiest shopping day I ran screaming in the opposite direction. Instead of
heading for retail Hell, I hit the trails. I hit Ryan's new Crappy Stamp Letterbox and will have to admit that this is a REALLY crappy
stamp! In fact, I am almost embarrassed to have it in my logbook... you've just gotta see it to believe.

I then did maintenance on the Crystal Springs box whose logbook has been out for repairs. The poor thing got so waterlogged earlier
this year that the book was declared dead. However, all is well as of today. There were tons of migratory ducks on the pond, a great
day for twitchers. I next found that the Oaks Bottom box is indeed MIA. I will replace it shortly, perhaps this weekend. The Doyle
Owl is resting peacefully awaiting its next appearance.

Funhog
