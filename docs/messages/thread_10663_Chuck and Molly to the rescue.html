Chuck and Molly to the rescue

From: Carolyn Stearns (cstearns07@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-02-06 08:49:27 UTC-05:00
     Sunday's birthday hike to Town Criewr letterbox was such fun thanks to all who came then..... Butterfly arrived back at my house and went to go home.  NO CAR KEYS -THEY ARE LOST!  We returned to the Chaplin library and hunted Knight and Day we went to Melted and wished the snow would, we hunted there. We went back to Diana's pool and searched the parkng area. Light was fading to late to go up trail and we were pretty sure she had them after the walk. Oh well we returned to my house and called  her husband to bring another key. It was fun having Nancy/Butterfly help feed horses and do chores while we waited. Tuesday I had no work I returned and scoured the Chaplin cemetary of Knight and Day.  I returned to Diana's Pool and went all the way to the box I looked all around very carefully and resigned myself - no keys. back at the trail head I ran into DonnaMae and Corrina ( Thanks for the exchange) they to went along in search of the box and keys. Stamped in noting seeing me and still no keys.
    Then yesterday Chuck gets out of work early and he and Molly head off to Diana's Pool. ( Irishtinker and Chuck knew about the keys as they are so close by)  As Chuck reads the log book at the box he notes Molly her little nose pinned to the ground a few feet away.   THE KEYS!   Yippee!!!
   Returning home they called me and we will relay the keys back to the Butterfly. Molly got someMcDonalds french fries as a reward!
    Be looking for Molly's new box we will be putting out soon. Thanks Molly and Chuck!
     Molly our hero    From: Leader of the Pack - carol
