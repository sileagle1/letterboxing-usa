TeamGreenDragon's Time Traveler series is 5 years old!

From: dang4724 (dan.guilford@bt.com) | Date: 2008-05-02 12:45:13 UTC
Back in April 2003, TeamGreenDragon placed 6 letterboxes throughout
the Blue Hills Reservation in Milton, Mass. The stamps were all hand
carved and based on H. G. Wells' The Time Machine. The clues were the
best part, based on European history, you have to do research before
even getting into the woods.

Why did the clues come down off of the letterboxing.org website? The
boxes are still there, others should still get to enjoy them.

TeamGreenDragon, congrats on an excellent series, job well done.
Thank you for your hard work and a great time.

Dan

From: The Spooky Group (danelle_27@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-05-06 00:29:10 UTC

Wow!!!! This means that we have been letterboxing for five years!
Time sure does fly. (pun intended ;) We started boxing just after that
series came out and they were some of our first boxes found. They were
a wonderful adventure that we cherish. But just like in the clues,
maybe one needs to do a little work to find them. Letterboxing like
most things is what you make of it. For those that choose to dig
deeper, a wealth of riches is just waiting to be found.

Happy Boxing!
The Spooky Group
