A new June*Flowers Letterbox!

Name:  Squirrel Nutkin' Letterbox

Placed: December 26, 2002

Wake County, NC -- Raleigh

 

Between Oxford and Royster, you'll find Fallon Park,

It's a place where people love to walk, and dogs to play and bark.

Near a winding stream, with oak trees straight and tall,

You'll find an octagonal gazebo, it's size will be quite small.

Under the gazebo, along the front left side,

Behind the rock is a letterbox, with a frisky squirrel inside!

ENJOY!
