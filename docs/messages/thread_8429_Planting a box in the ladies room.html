Planting a box in the ladies room

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-16 00:16:06 UTC-04:00
A few months ago I was checking on one of of my letterboxes called Nipmuck and Natchaug Junction, in Ashford CT. The box was planted behind a huge oval rock. Great spot. You couldn't miss this rock. When checking up on it I noticed that on another flat rock on the trail in blue was written "Ladies Room Rock". There was also a notation in my book mentioning this.The box was also found by the Trail master who just wrote me a note in the log book and left my box where it was.  Molly Chickabirdie and I went to it yesterday with the intentions of moving it. I reached under the rock and found my letterbox and also a plastic bag. I said "look we have a hitchhiker". I pulled it out and found that the trail master put a plastic bag next to my box. The plastic bag I thought was a hitchhiker had a roll of toilet paper in it. I didn't find it so amusing but Chickabirdie sure did. Needless to say I have moved my letterbox and adjusted the clues......Chuck and Molly
