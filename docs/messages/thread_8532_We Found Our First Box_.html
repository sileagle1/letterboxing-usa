We Found Our First Box!

From: stampcamperchris (rubber_paper_scissors@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-10-20 17:42:40 UTC
My husband and I are new to letterboxing and wanted to announce that
we found out first box! We went to Baker's Meadow in Andover, MA
and found the Busy Beavers Box! We had tried it two weeks ago and
took a wrong turn on the trail and got totally discouraged. Today,
we are glowing from ear to ear! It was a beautiful hike and the
finding of the box was quite a challenge for us. What a feeling
when I caught a glimpse of it! I was in shock and just sorta stared
at it before calling my husband over. What a wonderful hobby.
Thanks to all who helped us when we were so bummed two weeks back.
We have learned alot from this board.
Christine
