Greetings,

My new company, Eons, has just launched a contest where we are giving
away $15,000but there is a twist.

The contest is limited to only people aged 50 and over. If you're over
50, great, if not, please do me a huge favor and forward this on to
everyone you know over the age of 50. Here's how our contest works:

1. Go to www.eons.com and enter in prize code 80412. This will get
you into the site
2. Enter in four or more of your goalsthe things you want to
accomplish between now and when you turn 100 , as well as your email
address so we can notify you if you win.
3. Forward this invitation to everyone else you know who is over
the age of 50.

Entering the list is a pretty cool self-learning task in and of
itself, but once you enter your list, on an ongoing basis you'll be
able to come back to Eons to edit your list of goals, gather
customized information that will help you achieve your goals, connect
with others who have the same goals or who have already completed the
goal, and access special deals to help you go out and actually
accomplish your goals. We're very excited about this!



We're having a contest internally to see who can get the most people
to enter their list, so thank you very much for your assistance in
forwarding this emal on. And if this was forwarded to you, please feel
free to re-forward this!

Best Wishes,
Nate Wolfson
VP Product, GM Fun
