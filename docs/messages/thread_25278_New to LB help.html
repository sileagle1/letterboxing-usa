You might be interested in joining the Newboxers yahoo group too:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/

There's lots of helpful information on the newboxers list, as well as on the 2 major sites, Atlas Quest and Letterboxing North America.

Here's a list of letterboxes listed on Atlas Quest for the Austin, Texas area (56 letterboxes):

http://www.atlasquest.com/results.html?gTypeId=2;gSort=5;gSubFlags=56;gCoord=30.266496,-97.74087;gRad=5;gLocation=austin%2C+texas;gTitle=Austin%2C+TX%2C+US

Remember to put the box back where you found it and cover it up well so that it won't be visible to non-boxers. 

Enjoy. :)
