From: Judi Lapsley Miller (judi@psychokiwi.org) | Date: 2003-07-11 11:54:09 UTC-05:00
Nothing ever truly disappears on the internet :-)
But please, if anyone does know of boxes that were missed, do let the
webmasters know (you can email me off-list at judi@psychokiwi.org and I'll do
what I can). If the owner cannot be contacted and no adoptive parents come
forward to look after the box then it will disappear from the main database.
I'm sincerely hoping that there will be no boxes that are ultimately abandoned
and unloved. I'm sure there is a fairy godmother or godfather out there for
them all :-)

This is also a great oppotunity to purge the database of all those missing
boxes with missing owners.

Now lets get out there and get our A-counts up! (A=adopted) :-))

Judi
