Visit to TX, OK and AR

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-07 21:27:49 UTC



Back from a great trip to Texas, Oklahoma and Arkansas. Wanda met me at the
Houston airport with a Brazos Bend T-shirt - a most useful present as it was quite a
bit warmer there than in New England! After she showed me a few of the dozens of
Houston area letterboxes that she had done earlier, we skipped on up to the Dallas
area where she got her F7000 at Crazy Christmas, and I reached F4500 at
Flamingos in Love. Then it was on to Oklahoma City for a very nice Cabin Fever
gathering and visit with Happydays. We love to see how the hobby is growing all
around the country, and contributed our wee little bit by adding on 2 boxes in TX, 2
in OK, and 2 in AR with stamps carved by RTRW of CT. Will get them listed soon.

Thanks for the fun,

Pete
