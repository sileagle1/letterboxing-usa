Come one, come all. There is a great void in the world of
letterboxing patches in the Southwest. Mark Pepe has offered to host a
contest (pictures, and poll so people can vote) for a Southwest
letterboxing patch design. Why post it here? Cuz people come to the

void. Send your design image in jpg format to Baqash at lliving51 at
gmail dot com. When the poll is open a notice will be posted.
