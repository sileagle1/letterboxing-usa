New Ross Co., Ohio Letterbox

From: lizard612002 (lizard612002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-19 23:57:22 UTC
Title: 'Shroom With a View. Difficulty: Easy. Location: Chillicothe,
Ross County, Ohio. Placed by Salamander on 6-16-03. **Please bring
pen and stamp pad. CLUES: This letterbox can be found in
Chillicothe's Grandview Cemetery. You will start at the gazebo.
Standing at the southeast side of the center post, take a reading of
127 degrees and go 64 paces at that heading. You should find yourself
standing in front of a reddish-orange man-made object. From here,
take a reading of 202 degrees. 34 paces at that heading will bring
you to a large Maple tree. The letterbox is hidden behind a rock in a
hollow at the bottom of the north side of the tree. Enjoy the view
during your visit! NOTE: Depending on the time of your visit, these
clues may bring you into close proximity to groundskeeping staff.
(Generally Mon-Fri. daytime hours).I have found that they are very
friendly, but I have never discussed letterboxing with them, so the
usual discretion is advised. And of course, please be respectful of
those resting here. Thanks!
