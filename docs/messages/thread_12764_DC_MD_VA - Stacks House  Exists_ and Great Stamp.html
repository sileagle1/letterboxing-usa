Yes it's true. The Stacks House letterbox really exists and the
stamp is Fabulous.

Attempt number 4. It's an event night in the area and parking is at
a premium. Maybe we should have taken the Metro since it's so close,
but our parking karma was with us and we lucked out as someone pulled
out right in front of us.

The place was crowded and fun last night. Night Owl and I
immediately guessed the red head must be ___. It took a while to get
her attention but she knew what we were talking about.

There it was. It really exists. Nice logbook. And we were first!
Had any of you stamped into the original misplaced box? We thought
it was a commercial stamp, it looked so intricate, before we took it
out of the ziplock. We liked the stamp so much we decided it just had
to have green ink so we donated a green marker, rubberbanded to the
box, to keep with it.

I don't think I'm giving much away to say that it's local, it's fun,
it's yummy and it's great to go for on a rainy weekend. And I
recommend the shephards pie.

Many thanks to Celtic Pride for the nice work on the stamp and for
replacing it.

Afterwards we were "fortified" to continue into the night ... and
dark. See next post. Letterboxing in the DARK.
