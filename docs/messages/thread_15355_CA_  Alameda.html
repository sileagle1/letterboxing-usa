The LB at Crab Cove is very wheelchair accessible. Jim didn't want
to go with me because he was watching a baseball game. This was
Sunday, 8/24. These LBs were right in our own backyard; I walked
over.

But, Jim would have been able to get all the way to the LB
location. He would't have been able to uncover the box & pick it
up; someone else would have to do that. But this was a very
accessible location. there is even a wheelchair accessible path of
travel through a tide pool area; accessible if the tide is out. :)

The other nearby LB location near a duck pond was more difficult. I
didn't want to try balancing to walk on a concrete wall extending
into the pond.

At the 1st site at Crab Cove, met a family who was LBing; 1st time
I've met other boxers. The mom & kids did go over the concret wall,
down, & up again, & onto a path. But, they couldn't find the
box. :(

This is a great hobby for families. Different members of a family
will have different interests & skills. They can each use their own
skills for an interesting family outing. In this case, the younger
kids is the rubber stamp maker in the family. Neat turtles, snails,
etc.
