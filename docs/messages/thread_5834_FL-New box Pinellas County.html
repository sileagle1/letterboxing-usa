From: Gary Watkins (scanogram@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-06-22 12:57:35 UTC-04:00

"Tanks a Lot!"

War Veterans Memorial Park

9600 Bay Pines Blvd.

St. Petersburg, FL

Pinellas County

Placed June 22, 2002 by Gary and Kayleigh of Largo, Florida

Clues:

Visit the tank and make note of the size of the gun.

Stop by the Sundial and remember those who gave their lives for our freedom.

West of the flagpole, off the paved road, is the southern entrance to the pedestrian trail. From the "Dogs Must Be On Leash" sign, go north along the trail the number of paces that match the gun size. To your right you will find 4 trees in a row. Behind the second tree you will find the treasure hidden in the brush.

What you will need: Your stamp, ink pad, log book, compass, and bug spray.
