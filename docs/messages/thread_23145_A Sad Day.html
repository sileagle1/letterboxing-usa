A Sad Day

From: Doc (coolwan@zoomtown.com) | Date: 2006-03-12 00:14:46 UTC-05:00
Tinker Tom has been a great letterboxer for some time. He has placed
alot of letterboxes as well as being one heck of a finder. He is one of the few in
our hobby that have letterboxed in just about every country that has
a letterbox.

The message below was sent out tonight. We thought that the more people
that kept him in their thoughts tonight would send out some nice vibes and would
help him make it thought the night. When we left the Hospital at 11pm he was still
in ICU and was not doing well.


Thanks Everyone!
