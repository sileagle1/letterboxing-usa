Leader of the Pack's Singin Men

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-11-16 11:10:38 UTC-05:00
        Got the clue sheets for Singin Men late yesterday and went over the clues last night. We left the house at first day light to beat the rain and started off in search of the Singin Men. Had to give some thought to a couple of the clues but we were able to find it. We were proud and happy to be the first to sign in, just beating the rain.
 
        The clues to Singin Men are not available on line . They can be given to you by a friend or you might find them in another letterbox.The clues travel like a hitch hiker. Use the clues when you get them. Put them back in their bag and deposit them at another letterbox when you are out again. Or pass them to a friend. The clues are not in words but in pictures with meanings. If read correctly they make perfect sense, if not, they can be a mind teaser. This was a thank you box from Leader of the Pack to Pete and Wanda. It was a nice inovation in writing clues. Clues are on their way to another letterboxer. Leader of the Pack did a wonderful hand carved stamp. Great job Carolyn! Thank you. We had a great time finding it.....Chuck, Molly, Chickabirdie
