Hello all.
 
Wow. I never knew that a fellow letterboxer was just a few minutes behind me on Saturday. I had a great day picking up most of the boxes in the area.
 
Harley's Hex - Great Mysterious Place. Letterbox is fine.
 
Grey Rock - Both boxes are intact.
 
Barking Eagle - Coast Guard Academy. What a place. Though I did have to patiently wait an hour for a wedding photo session to conclude. Seem like the gazebo is a favorite place for wedding photos.
 
Connecticut Arboretum - Great Walk. Keeps you on your Compass reading toes. Box is fine.
 
Forts Griswold and Trumbel. Neat places for a history buff. Both boxes are there. Fort Tremble does have two flat rocks with a hole in them. Can be misleading at first. Elizabeth, please do try again.
 
Elaine and Jerry's Bluff. Found the spot, but could not find the box. I know it is probably there and will return in the winter when the vegetation is much more sparse. Did find the Two Labs & A Box Mystery Location though.
 
All in all, a very good day.
