Betty,
Go to www.yahoogroups.com and click the link to sign in. After
signing in, look on the left hand side of the page where it shows the
link for the letterbox list. Once on the letterbox page, look about
2 inches down for the blue stripe that runs horizontally across the
page.
Click on "Edit My Membership" on the far right. On that page, look
for "Message Delivery" and select either Daily Digest or No Email(for
the time you're on vacation and then redo this process when you
return and select daily digest again).

I hope this helps!
Have a fantastic and safe vacation!
