From: Elizabeth (letterboxer@cox.net) | Date: 2005-03-09 13:44:46 UTC

Welcome to the group! Yes! Yes! Yes!Please remove your address! You
never know who is out there! You can remove your address by going
back to your posts and clicking delete message on the upper right
side of the page. Once you see an open box to sign up for at the
group below, you should email only the creator of the box with your
snail mail address and what other info they ask you for. Often, you
will have to agree to terms as well, like using delivery
confirmation, only holding the box x amount of days before
resending, including a story in the box, ect. The link for the PLB
group is here:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/postal_letterboxing/

I would read over their FAQ. It gives you some info on postal
letterboxing. You really need to be involved with this group if you
want to be part of this. This is the place to sign up for PLBs.

Liz
of Penguin Patrol
