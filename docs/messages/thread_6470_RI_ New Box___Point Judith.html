Placed by:  Mad Mary
Name of the Box:  Point Judith Letterbox
Placed on:  July 25, 2002
Located in:  Washington County, RI
Closest to:  Narragansett, RI
 
Difficulty:  Easy
Hazards:  Broken glass, ticks
Note:  Try not to let others see what you're up to, and re-hide carefully.  Also, please tread lightly on the grass.
 
Directions:  Take Rt. 108 South in Narragansett towards Point Judith.  Then...
 
 
Where Auntie's and Iggy's appear in the mist,
A road to the south will bring you to this.
 
Just past the refuge for folks from the city,
Turn right down the road whose with a surface that's gritty.
 
To stop or to swim is the choice at this place,
Hop out of the car, and turn about-face.
 
Head back up the road, past the hill that looks dented,
Once teaming with soldiers, most now long lamented.
 
Boulders stand watch 'cross a road just for ghosts,
Whose eyes ever sharp, safeguarded our coast.
 
Follow their lead, to the gun that did roar,
Now just a ring, set back from the shore.
 
Circled by concrete, in the center of grass,
'Neath a pile of rocks, is where lies the stash.
