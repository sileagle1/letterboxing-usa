what a day!(Here in Connecticut.........)

From: Chip Goss (kancamangusdabull@yahoo.com) | Date: 2001-09-01 21:34:20 UTC-07:00
HELLO Everyone,

You can't see it right now, but you will have to trust
me on this one, I have an extremely emphatic grin upon
my face as I type this message.

After sleeping late...the girlfriends away...Eric and
myself decided to go letterboxing...."let's..."eric
started... "go find Ray's" i finished.

For those of you unitiated with "Ray's" I am refering
to Ray's Dilema. Hidden somewhere in Connecticut..
that's all the help I am giving anyone with the desire
to find this box. It's taken me almost two years to
find this box, so if you haven't found this box you
can keep on looking. The clues to this box are either
too vague or too literal, you must choose. But gosh
dangit WE FOUND IT!!!! Mr. Hassen, you have done an
extraordinary job with this box!

After finding this box, we decided to make a real try
to get eric over the 100f mark... Before hunger more
than darkness set in we got him to within just 6 more
boxes of this small milestone. I know with a little
more effort we will get him there!

God, what a great day!

Chip
