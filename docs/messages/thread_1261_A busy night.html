Hello all,
We had a very busy letterboxing night tonight! We intended on going out
to find one box, The Ram's new one called The Books. We had many surprises.
We first stopped by to peak in the freestone box to see who had been
visiting since we last were there. That's where we got our first
surprise!!! We went on and found not one, but two hitchhikers. First we
found The Y2K bug at Reach Out Hatchet at it's mystery location!! Then at
the mysterious Books we discovered "Burr" the hitchhiker placed by the 4-H
Cock-A-Doodle-Moo Club! Lots of fun!! We enjoyed our surprises, Thanks
everyone!

Melissa & Dan
P4 F141 X4

P.S. Clues to our newest box will be coming soon!
