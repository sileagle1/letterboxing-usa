Majestic Mystery Box is located in Pierce County, Washington.
Placed by A & J Bear (Aryn & Jason from Star, ID with our new names)
on 29 November 2002.

After all the excellent turkey we had to get out so I carved this
stamp while enjoying a majestic view of the crown jewel of the
Northwest. Of course you cannot skimp with your pocket book or
hiking legs to head out for this small box. The box is easy to get
to, but from there HIKE, HIKE, and HIKE some more. Enjoy!



Find the parking area MLIORNEG (word scramble) off of 706 and stretch
your legs only for a minute. As you pull into the parking lot on the
right side 10-15 feet behind the first street light stands 4 trees
sharing one base. Under the west side of this base behind a rock is
the Majestic letterbox.

After you've stamped in continue up for a hike...take in the majestic
view. Awesome!!!
