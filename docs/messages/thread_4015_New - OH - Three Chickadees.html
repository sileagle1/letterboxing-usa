Name: Three Chickadees
Difficulty: 2 a short walk
Hueston Woods State Park, Butler County, OH
Placed 29 December, 2001 by
Flyfisher (geoflyfisher@yahoo.com)

Songbird Series II

First go to the Lodge at Hueston Woods State Park and find the
Thrasher letterbox. More directions for finding the park are listed
with that box.

Leaving the lodge by road, follow the signs toward the golf course.
If you get to the golf course you went too far, because you should
stop at the parking lot next to the bridge over the tail waters of
the lake. (Just look for a place to go fly fishing and stop there.)
