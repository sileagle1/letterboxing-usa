Announcing Winter Gathering

From: (HANNAHKAT@aol.com) | Date: 2002-12-13 12:57:15 UTC-05:00
Hi all! It's me Rustypuff!

It's been a long crazy year and I thought what better way to end it than with a winter gathering.

I would like to invite all 'boxers to join us on Saturday January 18th beginning at high noon (although ongoing after that for late comers) at the Moose Hill Wildlife Sanctuary in Sharon, MA in the Nature Center for a winter potluck.

I will provide soft drinks and juice and an appetizer. There will also be a hot drink table with tea, instant coffee and hot cocoa. Bring along your favorite dish (and recipes! since that seems so popular!)and your letterboxing gear. There are five boxes on the sanctuary here and I will probably be hiding a box for the gathering. I will also carve an event stamp, but would encourage others to do the same.The more the merrier!

The event will be hosted by me (Rustypuff)and CSCM, and though I work at the Sanctuary, please don't contact them for information. Just post to the list for either one of us and we will answer any questions.

Please RSVP to me and I will keep a count of who's coming (gotta know how much soda to buy!). Also please note, pets are not allowed at the Sanctuary(sorry! - not my rule)

I am excited to see you all (since I missed the Mystery gathering) and I hope you all have a wonderful holiday!! (directions are below)

Cheers!

Kim Calcagno
aka Rustypuff

Directions to Moose Hill

The Nature Center is the large white estate house located at the
intersection of Moose Hill Street
and Moose Hill Parkway. The parking lot is on Moose Hill Parkway, adjacent
to the Nature Center .

Follow signs with the Massachusetts Audubon Society logo (blue triangle with
a tern in the center) along the way.

Directions to Moose Hill

The Nature Center is the large white estate house located at the
intersection of Moose Hill Street
and Moose Hill Parkway.  The parking lot is on Moose Hill Parkway, adjacent
to the Nature Center .

Follow signs with the Massachusetts Audubon Society logo (blue triangle with
a tern in the center) along the way.

>From the NORTH:
>From Rt. 128 or I-93, take I-95 South (towards Providence) to Exit 10 (Coney
Street, Sharon, Walpole).  Go left off ramp.  About 1/4 mile up, turn right
onto Rt. 27 toward Walpole.  About 1/2 mile up, turn left onto Moose Hill
Street.  At the top of the hill, turn left onto Moose Hill Parkway.  The lot
will be on your left.

>From the SOUTH:
Take I-95 North to Exit 8 (Main Street, Sharon).  Go right off ramp, and
travel one mile past Shaw's Plaza and Ward's Berry Farm.  Take left onto
Moose Hill Street.  Go straight across Walpole Street.  Continue up the
hill.  Turn right onto Moose Hill Parkway. The lot will be on you left.

>From Stoughton/Canton/Sharon Center:
Take Rt. 27 into Sharon center.  At light, take a right into Rt. 27  Take
second left AFTER the overpass (train tracks) onto Moose Hill Parkway.
Continue up the hill.  Bear left at the junction of Upland Road.  The
parking lot will be on your right prior to the intersection of Moose Hill
Street.

>From Rt. 495:
Follow Rt. 495 to I-95 North towards Boston.  Follow the From the SOUTH
directions.
