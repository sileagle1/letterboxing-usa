Hahaha, this is so funny! I've been letterboxing for a little while now and
have been more or less "out of touch" with the egroups list. I look at the
LBNA website now and then to get more clues, see what's new, etc. I've found

affect our lives so much. Well, i'm off to find more clues, sniff the
flowers, watch the animals, and stamp up! Letterboxing hasn't changed for
me.
