Those Altoid Tins

From: JuneMcAllister (nfmoon@mindspring.com) | Date: 2004-11-30 20:39:16 UTC-05:00
If you like those little metal Altoid tins, which can be great containers, depending on the circumstances, but you wish they didn't look like, well Altoid containers, some Zip Strip, (the gloppy stuff) and some of that sandpaper stuff for metal and about two minutes will turn a mint container into a generic metal can. Who knew?
MissMoon
