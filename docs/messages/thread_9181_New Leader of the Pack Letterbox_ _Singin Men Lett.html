The clues to Singin Men are not available on line . They can
be
given to you by a friend or you might find them in another letterbox.
The
clues travel like a hitch hiker. Use the clues when you get them. Put
them
back in their bag and deposit them at another letterbox when you are out

again. Or pass them to a friend. If you find them in a box and have been

there already you may take them and pass them along to a friend or
leave
them for the next letterboxer.

Singin Men will take about 1 hour. Wet feet are possible.
Rated: Who knows - How clever are You?
