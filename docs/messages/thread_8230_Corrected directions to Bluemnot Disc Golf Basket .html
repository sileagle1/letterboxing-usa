DIRECTIONS TO BLUEMONT PARK:
From 495 exit Route 50 (Arlington Blvd) East. Follow route 50 past Seven Corners shopping mall. The light just after Seven Corners is Patric Henery. The 3rd light after Patric Henery is Manchaster Street.
At Manchaster Street turn left Drive until the street ends turn right into the parking lot of Bluemont.
If the gate is closed, park in the street.
Start where a row of tall pine trees makes a perpendicular row with a old wooden fence near the
fields. Follow the fence until you hit the bike trail.
Follow the bike trail right
Take a left over the first cement bridge crossing the creek
Continue on the gravel trail turn right and continue over a blue, white and green bridge
When you come to the AMOR & PSYCHE rocks continue left down the bike trail.
At the "Disc Golf Course first tee -->" This is the Disc golf Course a sport I enjoy. Next to the course is the Lifton Nature Area.
Continue on the bike trail you should see a small provision on your right with a bike rack under it.
continue on bike trail until you reach the next trash can. Next to the trash can is #8 tee sighn.
With your back to the sign, facing the bike path walk towards the creek until you see a small dirt trail to your right.
Step over the logs and follow the trail, this trail parallels the creek. WATCH OUT FOR POISON IVY I SAW LOTS OF IT
Follow the trail until you see two trees on the right, with fuzzy vines growing on them about 5-6 feet apart.
Stand between the trees and head due south About 3-4 steps.
Behind a log under sticks and leaves is the Bluemont Disc Golf letterbox
