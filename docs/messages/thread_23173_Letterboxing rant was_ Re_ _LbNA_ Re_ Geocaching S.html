From: Fisher & family (fisherandfam@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-03-25 01:42:40 UTC-05:00
I've been interested in lboxing for a couple of years. I, in the beginning, whole heartily wanted to be a letterboxer, wanted to be known as a letterboxer and be known by letterboxers. I have found out that I am too paranoid to really enjoy the hunt. I am also not creative enough (or cannot let myself make the time to be creative enough) to make a good hunt for others. I box and place as I wish, which this hobby allows but... I feel sympathy for the die hards. You don't come in and go crazy lboxing, then die out... you are still here creating and hunting at the same level of enthusiasm. I wish for you that you get to keep letterboxing as mysterious, challenging and creative as you want. You really deserve it. If that means I miss out on a whole bunch of really cool peoples letterboxes, oh well, you all deserve your private company.

Happy Trails to a truely cool treasure hunting hobby... Fisher
