From: lauralubelczyk (naturbuf@gwi.net) | Date: 2003-08-20 01:42:43 UTC
Just thought I would let you all know that I planted my first three
letterboxes in southern Maine. "Two-Faced Fox" is very simple to find,
the other is a mystery box which contains a clue to another nearby box
"The Green Fox". Both locations have annoyingly bad attempts at
rhyming for clues. However, it will definitely be worthwhile for the
person who finds these boxes first (i.e. you may find a few somethings
just passing through). Let me know if you have any trouble with the
clues.
I also know that two more letterboxes have been placed at the Wells
National Estuarine Research Reserve in Wells, Maine by Phyto but the
clues are not posted in the database yet because she is having trouble
with her e-mail. If anyone is taking a trip to the area anytime soon,
please get in touch with me and I would be happy to e-mail you the
clues (or perhaps Culex and I could even meet up with you to hit a few
of Phyto and Isocele's southern Maine clues we haven't gotten to yet
since we just started)
