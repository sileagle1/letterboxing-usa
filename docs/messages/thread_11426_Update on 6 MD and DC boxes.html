Update on 6 MD and DC boxes

From: tempusfugitmd (donnas28@juno.com) | Date: 2003-03-24 17:29:09 UTC
On Sunday we visited the new Meadowbrook Stables and Candy Cane City
boxes in MD (nice stamps and we were first finders, yea!). Very
nostalgic for me. I grew up nearby and went there often as a child.
This is a VERY busy park in nice weather. From there we went over the
DC line to Squirrel's 2 Rock Creek Park boxes at the nature center.
They are still there after almost 3 years! The logbooks are full and
we didn't have spares, so we added some pages. (If anyone is planning
to find these, maybe you could bring a couple extra logbooks.) We
drove back to MD with just enough daylight to visit the 2 Climber
boxes in Bethesda. Both are in good shape but very inky! (D and Shoo:
we'll be back to find your other 2 boxes another day.)

Donna (aka Tempus Fugit)
