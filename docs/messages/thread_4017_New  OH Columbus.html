A Rose Is a Rose
Columbus, OH Franklin Co.
Difficulty 1.5
Placed 12-29-01 by Floatflute & Blue1 emu

The park this letterbox sits is in a much-used park in the
Clintonville area of Columbus, Ohio.
The library is in constant use, and the park is a dog owner's
paradise. As you travel back through the park you will see all sorts
of activity. Head down to the area formed in 1951 as a special
garden.
If you are into GPS the parking area is N 40 02.592 W 83 01.619.

Head up the steps and turn left and stand in front of the second
bench to the left donated by Pete.
