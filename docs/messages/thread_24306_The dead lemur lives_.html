The dead lemur lives!

From: Flame Thrower (ADGLBNA@aol.com) | Date: 2007-05-19 23:07:18 UTC
Wow, reading some of these old posts has brought back some fantastic
memories, especially my over whelming fondness for dead lemurs and
all things lemuresque.

The ADG is not dead! We were merely in an extended hibernation and
have only recently awakened to find that letterboxes are still around
but somehow the bees have all gone. What's up with that?

Apologies to all who seek our old boxes to find them missing,
waterlogged, or just plain trashed. Feel free to adopt at will. If
we ever decide to get back in the game we'll track you down to check
up on our old babies. Or contact me at adglbna@aol.com if you have a
rogue book or stamp as we'd love to see them. I'm actually kind of
stunned that any have survived this long.

The ADG is headed for a 10 day Oregon jaunt in a week and a half and
I have been busily printing off clues to boxes on the Oregon Coast
between Coos Bay and Lincoln City. Sarah, ADG 3, grew up in Oregon
and is excited to show us her old haunts. Our trip starts at Crater
Lake which still has 40 inches of snow!

So the big question, does the list drama continue? And how is the
old crew doing? We still keep in touch with Poison Ivy and Lady
Prisspot but have lost touch with Squirrel, Firefly, Pyschomommy, and
the rest of the crew. What's up you freaky people?

A couple of ADG fun facts to catch you up on the last few years:

1. You take the blond and we'll take the one in the turban.
2. You can never kill a lemur enough, they're resourceful little
buggers.
3. It's really hard to make Poison Ivy cry. If you happen to be
extremely good at card games and live in the DC tri-state area please
contact me. I've been trying for the last few years to defeat Poison
Ivy at cards so I can dip my cookies in his tears but to date all
efforts have been soundly thrashed.
4. Lady Prisspot will eat gummy anything!

Dead lemur hugs and ADG kisses,

Tammy
ADG 1
