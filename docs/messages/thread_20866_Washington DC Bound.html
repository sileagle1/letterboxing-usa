From: Brenda Tassava (bktassava@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2004-11-25 23:51:33 UTC


I was in DC in late September and visited the Lion series. It's very
close to the Metro stop near the Zoo. Once you finish the series,
you will be in another part of town, but there is another Metro stop
pretty nearby. Be sure to give yourself plenty of time, as it got
dark on us and we had to miss one in the series because it was
further into the woods. I also visited the Teddy Microbox, but we
drove there on our way to the airport.
