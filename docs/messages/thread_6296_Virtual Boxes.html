Re: [LbNA] Virtual Boxes

From: A.D. (alwayschaos@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-07-17 04:36:31 UTC-07:00
Oh gee, open a can of virtual worms, will ya? Just
kidding (really I am!).

I print out the virtual box's 'stamp' image, paste it
in my book and count it as a find. You figured out
the clues and although the 'stamp' isn't in a
rubbermaid container it is a box, in my humble
opinion. And darn, some of those were harder than one
would first think!

I suppose, if one wanted to do so, a sig line could
now look something like this:
F30,P8,X7,HH4,V9=58

Now try to explain that to a newbie who is trying to
figure this whole thing out. LOL

I figured I worked hard enough solving the puzzles
that I'm going to count them. But then again, what
would I know, other than I had fun doing them? ;o)

Happy Boxing.

A.D.
