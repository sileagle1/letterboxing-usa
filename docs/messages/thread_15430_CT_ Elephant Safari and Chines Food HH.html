From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2003-08-30 02:01:45 UTC
Steve, Heidi and Madras ventured to Mansfield for the first time
yesterday. We had intended to check out some favorite musicals but
forgot the clues!!! We stopped at Ye Old Cemetary to find a lovely
stamp by Chuck and Molly that was unfortunately broken in half!

We then headed up for the Elephant Safari and found all boxes in the
north and south sections to be in awesome shape. Very nice job Chuck
and Molly!!!! We found the "Chinese Food to go" Hitch Hiker at the
Shep box in the north section and it will be on its way shortly.

The hike at the elephants was incredible - our new favorite second
only to Hartman. The trails were well marked and easy to follow. The
clues are perfect! Although we did see a brown recluse spider
sitting on the 2nd box ( the one up by the dam ) so be careful out
there and carry a walking stick! Its a great investment and/or find!
