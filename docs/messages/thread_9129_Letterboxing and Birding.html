If you ever make it to the S.F. Bay area, The Coyote Hills Regional
Park in Fremont, CA is an excellent area for bird watching. The park
which has a big marsh is located close to the Alameda Creek and the
bay, so natural it attracts many different types of beautiful
waterfowl. My Alameda Creek Trail series is located there. I am
planning on another series for that area sometime in the near future.
