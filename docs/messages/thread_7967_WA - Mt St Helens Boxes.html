I can understand why the webmasters don't want to post boxes as missing
unless confirmed by a placer, but this is a good example of where the policy
might be tweaked slightly. If someone who has been to the box before can
confirm that it is missing or moved, and the placers can't be contacted, it
would be nice if the clues could be annotated as "missing, but not confirmed
by planters". People will drop away from this hobby for a variety of
reasons, and it would be a shame if the site were littered with known wild
goose chases.
