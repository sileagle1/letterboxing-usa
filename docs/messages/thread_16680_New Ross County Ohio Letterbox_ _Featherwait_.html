Title: Featherwait. Placed 11-01-03 by Salamander. Difficulty: Easy.
Location: Near Bainbridge, Ohio. Driving directions: Going east on
Rt. 50 after passing Paint Creek State Park there will be a rest
area about a mile up the road and on your left. This is west of
Bainbridge Ohio. Go to this rest area. CLUES: Start at the brown,
sheltered sign which displays a map of Ohio. Take a compass reading
of 85 degrees and go 18 paces at that heading to a large pine tree.
Then go 16 paces at 52 degrees from this pine to a picnic table.
Finally, fron the northwest corner of the picnic table, go 6 paces
due north to a Maple tree with a small cave-like opening at the base
of its trunk. The box is resting in this little cave, covered by
some leaves.
