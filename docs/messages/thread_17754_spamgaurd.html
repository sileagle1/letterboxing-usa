From: Drew Family (drewclan@aol.com) | Date: 2004-02-06 13:21:33 UTC
Hi, Daniel! Unfortunately, SpamGuard is uneditable by the moderators
and is required across all the yahoo platforms. I think the level of
bulk mail and sexy ads has almost been elimitated on this list, but
if you're getting stuff marked as spam from this group in your aol
account, you might try stopping the individual mail and using either
the daily digest or "read on the web only." Go to

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-usa/join

to change your yahoogroup options.

I've noticed that aol itself has gone crazy with [spam] headings
since their own new upgrade. That's easy to fix if you're using v8
or 9: use keyword spam to go to your "custom word list" and
enter "[spam]" as one of the headings you hate. Poof, you'll never
get another mail aol has tagged as spam.
