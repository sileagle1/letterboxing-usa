Pink Ribbon Charity Hitch Hiker found and placed.

From: lb_seekyr <bglade@erols.com> (bglade@erols.com) | Date: 2003-01-19 18:59:37 UTC
Hi All,

Seekyr, Blaze, and Mobius found the Pink Ribbon Charity Hitch Hiker
at the Tree For All Seasons letterbox while we were collecting the
winter version of this stamp. We released the Lost Luggage Hitch
Hiker when we were there in the fall so it was fun to find a new one
when we returned yesterday.

The Pink Ribbon Hitch Hiker is a charity hitch hiker released by the
Green Dragon Team who ask that the journeys of this hitch hiker be
made public including where it is next released. Team Green Dragon
will be making a donation to the American Breast Cancer Foundation
as the hitch hiker makes its journey. Great idea, Team Dragon, for
a great cause.

We brought the Pink Ribbon HH with us to the MA Winter Gathering at
Moose Hill (Thanks Rustypuff and CSCM; we had a wonderful time!!)
where it gathered 22 more stamps. It's now hiding with the turkey
at the Turkey Trot LB in Havard, MA.


Cheers,
Seeky, Blaze, and Mobius
