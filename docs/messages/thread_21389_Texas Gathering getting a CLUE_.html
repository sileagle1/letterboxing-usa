From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-02-16 06:26:46 UTC-06:00


Howdy, y'all!!!



There's a whole lot of carvin' going on down here in the great state of
Texas - we're getting ready for the second annual gathering: The Texas
Letterbox Rodeo event, organized by Silver Eagle and to be held at Brazos
Bend State Park on March 19th!

Thanks to inspiration from wonderful Scarab of The Doubtful Guests, we are
creating a Wild, Wild West -themed CLUE game, complete with spine-tingling
locations such as the Saloon, the Jail and the Pullman Car; Weapons as
lethal as a tomahawk, rattlesnake and a wagon wheel chandelier; and
dastardly Suspects including Sheriff Mustard, Schoolmaster Plum and Madam
Meadowbrook the Dance Hall owner!



After our own Texas event, we're planning to offer to loan the set of Wild
West CLUE stamps to other gatherings around the country, with the request
that you add at least one new stamp to the collection! Any questions or
requests should be directed to celticlions at comcast dot net.



( Okay, back to trying to carve Miss Scarlett, the Wealthy Rancher's
daughter.!)
