From: mulbie (mulbie@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-24 19:06:44 UTC
We spent part of yesterday doing "box maintenance" on some of our
boxes that we hadn't been to in about a year. Happily, we can report
that all of the following boxes are in good shape and open for
business:

In Fairhaven:
Ft Phoenix
Toxic Tack
The Slugger

Along the Cape Cod Canal:
The Herring Run
Belmont Rock

We may relist these boxes separately, since it seems that some members
of the "Seaside Series" have many more visitors than others. Currently
each series is listed only under the name of the town in which the
first box is located.

We checked on boxes in Marion towards the end of summer, and Old
Landing and Island Wharf were in fine shape.

We also signed into the following boxes yesterday: Phoenix Bike Trail
and Cape Cod Canal Trail Letterbox. Thanks!
