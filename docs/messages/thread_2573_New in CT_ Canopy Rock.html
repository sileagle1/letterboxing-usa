Canopy Rock
We had so much fun at Karen's Haley's Farm letterboxes that we placed
a tag-along at the end of the trail. Follow Karen's clues from the
second Haley's Farm letterbox under the railroad tunnel and through
the wall to Canopy Rock. Continuing down the trail along the cove,
you'll cross through a major stone wall. Turn north along the stable
top of the wall to a section of smooth stone. Just beyond, where the
wall resumes at a sharp-angled corner, find the Canopy Rock Letterbox
tucked into the north side of the large flat rectangular rock on the
right.

If you're on the CT shore, be sure to enjoy this easy-lovely hike!
