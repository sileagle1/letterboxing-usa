Found Grey's Reservation LB!

From: (czf@mediaone.net) | Date: 2001-10-12 16:22:33 UTC
Got up this morning, checked the What's New (*thank you* for the
update!), and saw a new letterbox in Sudbury. Looked outside, a
beautiful warm sunny fall day and decided to go for it. Took the
longest car ride possible so as to enjoy all the foliage in the
area. Clues were very good, but the LB was *too* easy to find - the
last LBer left it quite exposed. Be assured that it is now safely
tucked away in its proper hiding spot but nestled in a bed of pine
needles which protects it from uneducated prying eyes. What a cool
spot! Very romantic. Looks like there was a party there recently,
so I took the liberty of removing the beer cans and throwing them
away. Thanks for this great LB!

Warrior Woman (who is now giving great thought about the Grist Mill
area as a possible next LB placement ....)
