Long Beach, CA

From: Wendy O'Donnell (wodonnell@socal.rr.com) | Date: 2001-03-25 16:18:07 UTC-08:00
Started out with lofty hopes of finding three letterboxes today:  White By Day Blue at Night, Signal Hill and El Dorado Nature Center.
 
No problem finding Signal Hill, but El Dorado eluded me and the kids ran out of steam at White by Day Blue by Night.  I spent about 45 minutes at the El Dorado site walking in circles.  I even made the kids go into the brush to look around, but no luck.  No box and luckily, no snakes.  Beautiful area.  Funny you can live so close and not know that it's there.
 
Oh well....guess I'll be going out again next weekend.....   I'll bring the hubby and let him walk around in the bushes.
 
Thanks again for your efforts Gwen and Don!
 
Wendy
