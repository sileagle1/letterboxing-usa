Cameras in Letterboxes

From: Tim Kuehl (tkuehl@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2003-09-06 20:31:07 UTC-05:00
There is a disposable camera in one of my Mathman boxes for letterboxers to
take their picture for me. I got the idea from the geocache/letterboxes
around St. Louis/Southern IL that have them. It also allowed me to verify
that the students who said they found the box were actually there. Got some
interesting pictures from them. Unfortunately, the letterboxers that came
didn't use the camera! : ( (Maybe I should've put one in the Buffy box too
- that one seems more popular.)
