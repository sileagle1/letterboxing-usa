Here is another Lewis and Clark Mystery Box. 2004 will mark the
Bicentennial of the Lewis and Clark expedition. They traveled from
St. Louis, MO to Astoria, OR and back again. I have placed several
Mystery Letterboxes along the Lewis and Clark Trail and I hope to
plant many more before 2004.


To find this box here are your clues:

Lewis and Clark camped here in 1805 and again in 1806. The letterbox
is in a park at the lowest elevation in what is now this state!
You can park at the playground. Walk toward the river. At picnic
shelter #2 go right along the path. In the grassy area, there are two
tall trees and a stump (you might not see the stump at first because
it is covered with vegetation). See the rock at the base of the
stump? The letterbox is behind it! Please when you rehide it, make
sure it cannot be seen by just looking into the stump. Thanks,

Amanda from Seattle
