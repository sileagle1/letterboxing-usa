I found my 100th letterbox over the weekend, one of the wonderful
Oriental Medicine Series in East Lyme, CT, in time for my one year
anniversary of letterboxing tomorrow. Now I know that in CT that is
the letterboxing equivalent of taking a tiny baby step, but when I
am in the woods I am also birding, botanizing, and just plain
savoring the moment so it takes me longer. Congrats to Music Woman
on 700 boxes. Maybe when I grow up........

Is there still a box that finds you when you reach 100? If so,
please put me on the list.
