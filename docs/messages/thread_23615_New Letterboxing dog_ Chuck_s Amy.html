New Letterboxing dog. Chuck's Amy

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-28 20:45:34 UTC-04:00
Almost all our letterboxing friends know of the loss of our dog and family member Molly a month and a half ago to cancer. I used to say that Molly was the brains of our letterboxing partnership. Well, I was wrong. She was the heart. With her gone, we were broken. We decided that we had to start all over again, raise a puppy, and have another dog in our lives. She won't replace Molly and can't fill her shoes but she will be "her own person". She is a 9 week old Chocolate Lab named Amy. We got her on the 21st and on the 22nd at 8 weeks old, she was at her first letterbox Chick in a Nest in Mansfield CT. Since then she has been to 4 more boxes (F5). To see pictures of Amy just go to the following link:

http://www.chuckstraub.com/amy.htm

Amy and I are hoping to see you all again on the trails soon......Chuck
