I'm sure I'm not the only person who receives crazy emails (usually straight
to my spam folder) with titles that seem like "mad libs" gone bad. A
noun, and perhaps a mismatched adjective or adverb usually make up the title
of the email. Some of them are truly funny. I have often thought that
some of the titles could make for some really funny names of letterboxes.
:P
