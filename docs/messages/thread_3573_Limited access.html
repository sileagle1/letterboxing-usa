From: Jay Chamberlain (ae4mk@1bigred.com) | Date: 2001-10-26 09:20:45 UTC-04:00
I confirmed this morning that my "Few Good Men" boxes located on the MCB at Quantico, VA have limited access to "civilians". Letterboxers will have to have a military, FBI or DEA ID cards or be a guest of someone who does, to enter the west side of the base to get to Lunga Reservoir.
 
I am guessing that the boxes at Accotink WR, in Fairfax  on the Fort Belvior Army base are also isolated.
 
What is the general consensuses? I am leaning toward removing them because of the limited access. Maybe this will change, but who knows when. Your thoughts please.
