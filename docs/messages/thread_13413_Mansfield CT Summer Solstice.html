Mansfield CT Summer Solstice

From: summermagic23 (summermagic23@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-07 17:24:57 UTC
I was looking around online the other day and found an event that
might be of interest. On June 21, all day long at Mansfield Hollow,
there will be a Day in the Park Summer Solstice event. It is
sponsered by the DEP and hosted by the Friends of Mansfield Hollow.
Highlights include hiking, biking, kayaking, and more, including
letterboxing at 10:15 am. There will be a special event stamp. For
more info:

http://www.mansfieldct.org/town/other/upcoming_events/2003_0621_FMH_s
olstice.htm

Unfortunately, I will not be able to attend since that day is
training for my summer job at Mansfield parks and rec day camp.
However, I thought I'd let others know since it seems like it will
be an excellent event.
Happy Trails!
