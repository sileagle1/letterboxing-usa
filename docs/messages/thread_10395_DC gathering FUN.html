Hello all!
I just wanted to thank everyone who came to the DC gathering this past weekend.  I had a blast.  The turnout was more than I could have ever hoped for.  It was so fun to FINALLY put faces with the names and stamps that I have become familiar with over the past few months.  The day was just one more reminder of why I love this quirky little hobby!

Many thanks to Squirrel for her excellent recap of the day!

Also, it was a treat to have our guest from CT - Darrin.  We all got to sneak a peek at his log book and see some of the famous CT stamps.  I am dying to take a trip up there someday and find some of these treasures for myself.

And yes, there was much discussion about having an East Coast gathering in the spring or summer.  All us DC folks would love to meet more of you northerners!
