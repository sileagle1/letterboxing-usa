I would just like to thank Rick in Boca and all the participants in
this survey. It reaffirmed for me why some of my boxes have gone
missing, and I learned a few things as well. Thank you, Rick..for
taking the time to make this survey.

One caveat I would like to include...make sure when you are planting
boxes further off trail that you don't create a "spur" trail...that is,
a new permanent trail made by heavy traffic. Pick a direction that
hides traffic better, such as over rocks or pine needles...not moss or
other low growing plants.

Thanks!
