VA - Vienna Report

From: thurston_1701 (jmatthew@thurston.org) | Date: 2002-11-10 22:20:54 UTC
Capped off a Letterboxing weekend by hunting through out the Vienna
area. Weather looked a little iffy, but we decided the worse that
could happen was we got a little wet. Successfully dodged the small
showers and picked up 8 boxes. We go In the Park #1 & #3 (didn't look
for #2 as it has been reported missing). Also found all five of the
Dead Authors. What a great narrative for the clue. I think we were
the first to find box #3. We finished the day by tracking down The
Mole. All the boxes are in great shape.

We also picked up another exchange (Hi Lauren). This has got to be
one of the best past times ever!!!!!

Plain & Peanut
