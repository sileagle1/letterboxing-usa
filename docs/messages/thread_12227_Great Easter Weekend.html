Great Easter Weekend

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-22 21:05:58 UTC


Back in RI again after a lovely little Easter trip down to the
mid-Atlantic! This time we didn't go for the "dozen-box days" that
we've done on other trips through states like VA, MD, PA, NJ, WA,
OR, etc.(just to let folks know that the potential for "big 'boxing
days" - for those few of us who really like doing them/not
recommended for others! :-) - is certainly not just limited to CT or
southern New England!) This time we decided to concentrate
mostly on mapsurfer boxes and enjoying the beautiful flowers
blooming serendipitously along the way! We're still amazed that
we managed to find some of those boxes and that our total
mapsurfer count(31) has doubled from what is was just last
month at the PA Mystery Dinner Gathering! What a couple of fine
and challenging weekends we've had recently, and what a truly
wonderful Easter to remember! Thanks,

Wanda and Pete
