From: Jay Chamberlain (ae4mk@1bigred.com) | Date: 2002-05-07 10:35:09 UTC-04:00
Ladybug of VA have hatched a massive Mystery Letterbox project and we (G-Man included) need your help.
 
Over the summer we need help in starting a series of Mystery Letterboxes, one in each state.
 
We will supply the stamp.
 
What we need is a boxer willing to take the stamp, build and place the box then write the mystery clues based on the subject of the stamp, and a little guidance from us..
 
It sounds mysterious because we want it that way.
 
If you are interested in taking part in this Mystery project,  please drop me an email off list  ae4mk@1bigred.com
and I will collect the names for each state. Don't be sad if I get multiple offers to help and select someone else based on a geographic preference. 
 
So, please send me your first name and or letterboxing alias, what ever your comfortable with, and home town in the email.
 
As a duly sworn federal G-Man, I will require you to sign a Non-disclosure statement and sacrifice you 37th unborn letterbox if you leak any information of this Mystery project to CNN or US Air.
 
Yours in Letterboxing
 
YILb
