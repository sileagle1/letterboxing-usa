Son of Flapjack

DeLeon Springs State Rec Area
nearest city DeLand
Planted 3-23-2

easy
fee area
leashed dogs ok
Great place to take kids - away from plastic, asphalt, and The Land of
Really Big Mice. Bring your swimsuits, cameras, and appetites. If your
table won't be ready for an hour, you have plenty of time to find Son of
Flapjack while you wait.

Take the trail that goes past the Big Cypress, where Son of Flapjack's
father disappeared as soon as he was planted. See the tree if you like, but
return to the trail, turn left, take either fork, and head out the Wild
Persimmon Trail. After number four, Son of Flapjack hides behind a
right-side root just before the footbridge.
