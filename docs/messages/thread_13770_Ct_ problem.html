From: Judi Lapsley Miller (judi@psychokiwi.org) | Date: 2003-06-23 14:26:49 UTC-04:00
You may have inadvertently clicked the "Redirect to this link
automatically?" box. Make sure you're signed into (logged into) the
letterboxing.org website, then go to page listing the clue. Click on the
"Edit" button underneath the clue and then see if the "Redirect" box has a
tick in it. If it does, click on the box again, save the change and cross
your fingers!

Hope that helps!

Cheers,

Judi
