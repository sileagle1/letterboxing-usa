Otis Redding Found in Madison, WI!!

From: cowpost (cowpost@email.msn.com) | Date: 2001-07-09 12:27:07 UTC-05:00
Hi! I just found my first box--Otis Redding--in Madison, WI. Unfortunately,
I think this is the ONLY box left in Madison. I will be carving some stamps
and hope to get at least 2 boxes planted soon.

This was a wonderful stamp, Deb! The only thing is, I would not recommend
hunting for it at the height of the biting insect season (now)--I think they
have made your hiding spot their headquarters and they were not pleased to
see me.

But--it was neat to see that there is a natural stamping shelf available
right there, too. I had to really hustle and did not get a chance to copy
down the names of folks who already visited the box, but there were several.
I also did not get a chance to appreciate the different stamps they used. In
fact, I did not even LOOK at the impression of Otis that I took until I got
home. I'm telling you the bugs were BAD!!

Cowpost

trails. If you can get there alive with all the bugs you shouldn't
