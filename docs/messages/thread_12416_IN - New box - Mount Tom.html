Mount Tom Letterbox
Near Chesterton, Indiana, Porter county

Placed 4/27/03 on a letterboxing foray into Indiana by Matt the Rat &
Jeni B

Clues:
You might think its only a hill of beans.
But around here we make mountains out of these!

Mount Tom is the tallest peak in the area, with a great view of the
forests to the south from its summit.

At the top of the mountain, look for a horizontal log. Behind this is
a tree with a hole at its base. Be sure to completely cover the box
with pieces of bark and hide it well when you leave.
