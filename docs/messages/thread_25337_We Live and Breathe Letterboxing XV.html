We Live and Breathe Letterboxing XV

From: Choi (john@johnsblog.com) | Date: 2012-07-18 18:28:21 UTC
Der Mad Stamper and Choi would like to cordially invite you to a weekend
jam-packed full of letterboxing madness and mayhem to help celebrate the fifteen
year anniversary of our beloved hobby!

We will be frolicking in the forest along the beach of Grotonwood Christian Camp
in Groton, MA. This is your chance to experience summer camp for letterboxers!

All ages are welcome, and we're planning to organize everything... food, cabins,
entertainment, and an amazing variety of letterboxes! All you need to bring is
your sleeping bag, your letterboxing gear and your sense of adventure as we
revel in the past, look forward to the future, and party 'til the needles fall
off our compasses!

Mark your calendars now for Labor Day weekend, August 30 through September 2,
2013. Stay tuned to our event listing on AtlasQuest
(http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1970) for costs, reservation
information and additional details as they develop.

We promise this is a celebration you won't want to miss!
