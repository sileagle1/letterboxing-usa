Hi there,
I was out and about the Columbia River Gorge just yesterday and was
able to pick up Ponytail Falls and Wahkeena Falls. It was great to
be able to get these stamps. These copies by Ryan look just like the
originals!! Although Ryan's clues for Ponytail were a little off.
It is at the same tree as in the Original Clues, but not at the base
of the tree--rather the letterbox is about 4 or 5 steps away to the
SE underneath a few large stones.

The Wahkeena box was exactly where he said it would be--no problem.

I also went on out to Triple Falls and snagged that box, since I had
not gotten it before. It is amazing that so many Non-letterboxers
have logged into that notebook. It is almost full. I think there
were only about 6 or 7 pages left at the end there.


I dropped off a couple of hitchhikers during my time in the Gorge as
well!!
:-)

--Amanda from Seattle
