Apparently my earlier missive was unclear and for that I wish to apologize.

What we want to do is to carve stamps to be placed in each of the 50 states of the union. And, since we are, after all, Team Rayvenhaus, we're going to carve the state bird for each state. Rayvenhaus = State birds....... Get the connection? (grin)

Anyways, what we want to do is to get one person from each of the 50 states to volunteer to place and maintain a box for us in that state. We'll do the carving and recarve as needed, providing the entire contents of the boxes, we'll maintain the clues, we'll be listed as the placer and you as the maintainer.
