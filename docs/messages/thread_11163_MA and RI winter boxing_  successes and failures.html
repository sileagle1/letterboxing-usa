MA and RI winter boxing: successes and failures

From: mulbie (mulbie@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-03-12 18:24:58 UTC
We haven't done too much letterboxing since he Winter Gathering at
Moose Hill, but can make the following report:

We found Sister Road, Sister Town Letterbox in MA in late January.
Also found Queen of a River, the Little Bonus Basket, and the 3
Pasquiset Pond boxes in RI in January. We also found The Great Blue
Letterbox, the Sakonnet Pt letterbox and two of the Rhode Island
Compass Mystery letterboxes in February.

Last weekend encouraged by warm weather, we found the following boxes
in MA: The Tree for all Seasons (to pick up the "Winter" stamp before
it is retired) and the Noanet Woodlands box (which was buried in snow
and ice). Unfortunately, ice at the summit prevented us from
uncovering the 3rd Noanet letterbox --- will return after snowmelt!

Then on Sunday in glorious weather, we set out to plant some new
letterboxes in Massachusetts. The clues for these boxes will be
forthcoming soon!

Mary Manatee and The Yachtsman
