These 3 boxes were placed by my friend and I. We are new to
letterboxing and hope that you will enjoy them. The letterboxing
website stated that it would be posted on the site if we posted on
the group. If anyone knows more information on this, please let me
know.

Creator: Caroline Bader-Hepting
Date Placed: September 8, 2002
All letterboxes are in Alum Rock Regional Park in San Jose.

Letterboxes for:
Alum Rock Regional Park
16240 Alum Rock Ave.
San Jose, CA 95127-1307
(408) 259-5477
www.ci.san-jose.ca.us/cae/parks/arp

Mineral Baths Letter Box --
Follow the path along the sulfur hill.
Pass by the bridge, rotten-egg smell in the nostrils fill.
First "shrine" on your left, at the base on the right.
Move the stones, you'll find the box wedged in tight.

"Ohlone Glade" Letter Box --
Follow the path along the sulfur hill.
Cross over the bridge, rotten smell in the nostrils fill.
Pass the Grove of Sycamore and Living Oak,
On to the look-out point where Ohlone once spoke.
Rest here and enjoy the view,
For a little more is all you do.
A few more steps to complete this test,
You'll find the box where trolls lie to rest.

Eagle Rock Letter Box --
Find your way to the eagle's peak.
The most difficult to place, the most difficult to seek.
Face the trees and shrubs and rock.
On the right grows a tree of good stock.
To it's left the Rock with first crevice not too deep,
At it's base, under rock piled `round, a letterbox at the Eagle's
feet.
