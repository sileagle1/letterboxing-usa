Denise,

After discussions with the person who had tried to find my "edge of
the swamp" box, I believe it is _very_ likely gone missing. I suspect
that the NPS has removed it as they appear to be doing in other
places. I don't know if they have removed the "deep in the swamp"
box, that would require a little more motivation (and a lot more
blood loss this time of year). I will hopefully be back down there in
October on a fishing trip and will check the situation at that time.
I would still suggest you check out the area where the edge was
placed. Lots of interesting things to see there. If anyone out there
likes backcountry salt water fishing, IMHO there is no better place
than Everglades National Park. Ever hooked a 100+ pound tarpon in 8
inches of water? They say well over 90% of repeat visitors to the
park are fishermen. There is good reason. I might even be willing to
send some cryptic clues about fishing spots if someone asks about it
;-)
