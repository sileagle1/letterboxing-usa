in a somewhat salubrious sounding place.

As I described recently on the traveler's list "Letterboxing is TRICKY in
Australia. We did try placing one last time we visited, but had to abandon
the attempt (partly lack of time, but partly we just couldn't find a safe
place). The thing is, Australia is filled with poisonous snakes, nasty
spiders, and all sorts of other critters that you just don't want to come to
close to (unless you're Steve Irwin!). And all these critters love little
hidey holes under rocks and in old trees. And if, like us, you're not from
those parts, it is hard to know who is friend and who is foe. If nothing
else, take a hiking pole or something you can use to poke into those dark
spots! Then stand well back!"
