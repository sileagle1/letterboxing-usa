I've just hidden my first box, and the first box in Pitt Co., NC. I've
made the clue pretty explicit, but its a little bit of a hassle to find
nonetheless.
Here's the clue:

At the end of Beech St., park your car behind the apartment complex.
Walk around the yellow and red gate and down the sandy trail/road.
Go a couple hundred yards past a lake on your left.
When the trail hits a T, go right.
Between the 2nd and 3rd concrete round structures (on the left), look
for a small pile of bricks in the brush to the right side of the path.
Hidden under the bricks is the Floyd's Eye box.

Wear bug spray, and if there has been a recent rain, wear boots. This
area looks like a scene out of Scooby Doo. Its really creepy.
