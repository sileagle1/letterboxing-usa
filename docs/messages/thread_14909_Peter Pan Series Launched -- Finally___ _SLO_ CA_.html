Dear Folk,

Pandora carved a series of boxes devoted to Peter Pan. The first one
of the series was Tink's Wand. It was quickly lost (sigh). Pandy left
the area and left me in charge of the rest of the plants.

Now, I am placing the boxes with the aid of other Googled members.
The first one of the new plantings is Peter's Shadow.

These are very high quality carves and I think well worth hunting. My
goal in planting them is to 1) associate the stamp with some physical
location around the Central California area, and 2) make the boxes
accessible for folks with smallish children or trouble walking. I am
not encrypting the first clues but may make some of them harder as
the mood strikes me. *GRIN*

I hope to have the whole series out and ready for our October 4th
Letterboxing Get-together.

Would appreciate feedback as you look for them,
Sir Balthazar
