Hello Ohioians!

Wild Child and I have recently left 3 new boxes in your state--two in Stow
(NE) and one in Van Wert (NW)--the first in Van Wert County.

We introduced friends from Danville to LBing by hunting WilloWander's Dawes
Arboretum LBs. GREAT clues--we especially enjoyed those to "Commanding View."
Alas, the MIA status of the first box appears to be correct--we found a
glued-on lid on the underside of the bench, but no box attached. I'm happy to
report that "Wood, Water, Stone" IS there, tho I had given up after what I thought
was a thorough search of the site. Then one of the children we were
introducing to LBing found it by looking from another angle--hooray for the newbie
9-year-old! According to reports in the other log books, this box has thwarted
several searchers (and apparently it really was missing for a while & has since
been replaced). Too bad we'd run out of time when we got to "Trees
Knees"--we'd have loved to attempt +.

Many thanks to WilloWanders for our first four Ohio finds and helping to
introduce a local family to our hidden hobby. Our Danville friends are very busy
folks, but I'm hoping they will find time to plant boxes of their own for WW
and others to find!

--Wild Woman
