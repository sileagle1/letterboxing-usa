I would like to place The Confederate Widow in a Confederate Cemetery
here in Huntsville. Of course, I would need permission from the
cemetery. Wanted to know if any of ya'll would find that interesting.
Also, there are no rocks or downed trees under which to hide it. Can
it be hidden IN a tree instead of UNDER a tree?

Huntsville really is a neat little city in Alabama. We are called the
Rocket City because of the Space Flight Program here. I would like to
write clues that would take you by some interesting areas. What do
you think? This would be my first placed box.
