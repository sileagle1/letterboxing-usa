Name: Sharon Woods

Location: Mason Exit, Springdale, Ohio

Address: Take OHIO US 42 South from I-275 about 4 miles, Park is on the Left.

Level: 4 (Not REAL hard)


Need: Stamp Pad, Pen

Clues: The Clues to this box are very small as Sharon Woods is a good place
to get lost in. This trail is out of this world as far as looking down over a very quick running creak and some very nice falls. (You'll like it)

You will find the Richard H Durrell Gorge trail. The trail is Left of the Dam. (If you never been here, It's not like a BIG dam) the road goes over it and you can't miss it.

You will walk along this trail for about 15 Mins UNLESS you stop off to check out the so cool views.

You will walk till you come upon a Green on your left hand side, Humm, maybe get a time????? (that's why they made cell phones)

Not far after that you will cross the creek. BUT as Obi-Wan Kenobi would say (YYYOOOOOU Don't WANT to cross the creek), At the first post where the railing starts for the bridge, You want to take a reading of 320' (I want to take reading of 320') You will see a fallin tree about 6 paces (pace=2 Steps) into the side brush. The letter box is in the middle and UNDER the tree. (Just use those Jedi powers and LIFT that tree up)

May the force be with you
