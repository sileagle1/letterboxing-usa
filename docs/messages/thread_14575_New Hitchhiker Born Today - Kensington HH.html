From: markpepe (mjpepe1@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-07-27 13:32:24 UTC
Hi:

This new hitchhiker was a result of an email I sent to Delirium
after reading on the LBNA Talk List that she would be putting out a
new series called Kensington to be placed in Michigan. The reason
behind the series is that her husband, Ken, sings alot . . . Ken +
sings + tons = Kensington. Get it?

Since we live in Kensington, CT I though and emailed Delirium
such, that she might want to carve a Kensington Hitchhiker that I
could place out here in Kensington, CT. She immediately responded
that she would and thus was born the Kensington Hitchhiker. Where
else but in LettboxingLand could 2 complete strangers work together
via email to collaborate on a HH? Look for the beautifully carved
stamp with a wonderful logbook! [Yes Ken, he is a little large but
what a piece great piece of carving!!] ;)
