Veterans' Pride

Beyond the iron gates you passed,
Head northwest to a flag full mast.

Turn sharply left, avoid The Meal,
But Cloverleaf your

Remember the Children but walk on past,
By 2 and 7 (but not TOO fast!)

Continue on and soon you'll find
Headstones alike (but they don't mind!)

A stand of maple trees surround
The souls of soldiers in the ground.

Lawrence Bower is the best.
Now look above and find the rest.

Be careful as you look inside,
The box you seek was placed with pride.
