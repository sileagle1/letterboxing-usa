From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-04-28 17:19:54 UTC-04:00
X's mean Exchanges. These are stamps of other letterboxers that you exchange
with. Lets say I meet you at an event or on the trail. If I stamp into your
book and you stamp into mine, its an exchange :-)

Hope this helps!
