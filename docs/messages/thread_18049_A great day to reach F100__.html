A great day to reach F100!!

From: mepanj (mepanj@kynd.net) | Date: 2004-03-07 18:36:23 UTC
Finally reached that seemingly ellusive goal of 100 finds! Spent the
day in Portland, ME and found some really nice boxes (as usual).

Riverton trolley boxes: only able to find the trolley. The other box
was no where to be seen, dispite all of our efforts. Slippery and
wet, will try in the dryer weather.

Munjoy Hill Observatory: Safe and sound. Great spot both for the
observatory and the box.

St Lawrence Church - Starry Night: Yeah! 100 boxes found. They were
getting ready for a concert when we were there.

ME Valentine: Just for insurance went for 101. Found it after a
short miscalculattion by fellow boxers (you know who you are!). Neat
box, but I just wanted to warn Casco Bay Painter, that spot has a
hole through it and the box fell through on me. I shored it the best
I could without changing the location. It was dark when we found it.

Now that spring is here (well almost) it is time to plan on some
placements and to get some boxers up to our area!

On to the nextt 100!!

Mepanj
