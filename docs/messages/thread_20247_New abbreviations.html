I want WWSONTWFLB for wearing the wrong shoes on the wrong feet
letterbox count for the time I had to go to a funeral and there was a
particularly enticing letterbox nearby. I threw a pair of sneakers in
the car to change into. When we got to the trailhead I started to change
into my sneakers, put on the left one and picked up the other to find it
was also a left one! Well I put it on and looked like the fellow in
"Best in Show" who had two left feet. I couldn't stop laughing and
walking around in circles to show that's what one would do who had two
left feet. Got the box, though.
