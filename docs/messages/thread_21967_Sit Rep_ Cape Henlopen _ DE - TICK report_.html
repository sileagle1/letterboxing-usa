Right, Cutter or Deep Woods Off works OK. You want at least 25% DEET.
More is better as far as ticks are concerned and some of
the 'outdoorsman' brands have as much as 50%, but some people have a
skin sensitivity at that level. Best to keep it on clothing.

Pyrethrum sprays work the best. This is for clothing only and should
not be applied to skin. WalMart Outdoors Dept. sells one under the
Permanone(sp) brand.
