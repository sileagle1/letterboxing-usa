Hi again everyone.   sorry about the patch confusion.

I just spoke with Andrea at Town Hall Embroidery (really the embroidery shop in Town Hall Archery).

She was very nice.  She said she would NOT like to take individual orders for the LBNA patches anymore.  The one time was way too hectic and too much trouble to keep track of.

The merit badge orders and the decal orders should be sent directly to the distributors. 

Please go to this site before making any orders:   LbNA regalia updates

Hope this helps.
