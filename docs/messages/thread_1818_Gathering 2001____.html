really loved meeting all of you and exchanging stamps. It's great to know
that all the other letterboxers out there are such nice, friendly people.
We hope to see some more of you out on the trails, expecially those of you
who had to leave before we had the chance to talk (Drew Clan & Ram) I can't
wait to get together again soon. Carolyn once again, thanks for a great
day, we hope this becomes an annual event!
