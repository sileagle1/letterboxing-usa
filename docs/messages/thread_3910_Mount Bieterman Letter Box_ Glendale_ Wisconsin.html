Mount Bieterman Letter Box in Glendale, Wisconsin has been found by
two letter boxers so far. I regret to record that the first was
apparently a letter boxer of the rodent variety whose stamp involved
teeth and claws. The second letter boxer, a two legged one, very
kindly informed me of the demise of the contents. I will be repairing
and replacing it in a few weeks. It should be considered to be MIA
until further notice. I hope noone tried to find it and wasted a trip.
I'll get it back in action in a more robust and less vulnerable manner
asap.
