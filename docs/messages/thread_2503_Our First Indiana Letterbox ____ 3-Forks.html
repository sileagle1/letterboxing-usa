3-Forks
Cumberland, Indiana
Lions Park

One of 3 letterbox/geocaches we hid along I-70 on a road trip between
Collinsville IL and Waynesville Ohio.

We set out to find April Fools but weren't wearing the proper wading attire.
We hid this as an easier alternative.

Planted by:
MjLikePink, SpaceDAW & Mohmers
06/04/01

Cross the Foot Bridge near the parking lot.
Follow the brush line North East along the creek
(44 degrees)
59 strides ( 8 yr old)
67 strides ( 6 yr old)
54 strides (5'-8" Adult)
Turn 335 degrees.
You'll find 3-Forks at the base of a tree.
39degrees 47.01 N
85degress 56.66 W
Please be sure to place the log and brush back over the box to prevent it
from being washed away by rain or accidentally found.
