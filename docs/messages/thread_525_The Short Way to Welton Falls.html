Dear Danielle and all,

After last month's enjoyable but unsuccessful trip to Welton Falls, I
was eager to pay another visit to that lovely site. Instead of hiking
over Mt. Cardigan, I drove the extra 30 miles around and followed
your directions. They were very clear, and I could see that I'd come
out on the river too far upstream last time, mistaking the access
trail in your clues for another.

This time I found the box immediately and stamped up at 8:15 this
morning.

Your box was in fine shape, Danielle. Mine was the second stamp to go
in. No surprise whose was the first! (Trilliums have gone by, but the
stamp is still lovely)

Best,
