Hi Gang,

EMS as a chain carries the Kelty Kids Child carriers.
These are an excellent investment meant at keeping you
AND your child safe. Many of the packs come with
sun/bug screens, pockets for kids
essentials...diapers, etc. and some have small bags
for holding the parents stuff to (like letterboxing
supplies)... they come on a rigid metal frame that
stands up when you take it off... NOT meant to support
child in it when off of your back!!!

I do not work for EMS, but I know these are a really
good product as I would just about stand behind
anything Kelty makes! Well worth your money!
