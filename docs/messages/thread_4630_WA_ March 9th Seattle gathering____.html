From: BB (bburk@harbornet.com) | Date: 2002-03-06 12:07:14 UTC-08:00
Red Robin is technically in Tukwila.
Sometimes referred to as South Center.

I-5 Northbound
Take exit #153 to South Center Parkway.
Turn right (no choice) onto South Center Pkwy and continue to Minkler Blvd.
Red Robin is on the corner of So.Cntr. Pkwy. and Minkler.

I-5 Southbound
Take exit #154B and turn right at 61st Ave.
Turn right again onto South Center Parkway
Continue to Minkler Blvd.
Red Robin is on the corner of So.Cntr. Pkwy. and Minkler.
