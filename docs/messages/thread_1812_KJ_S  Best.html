KJ'S  Best is located inside Kathy John's Ice Cream Shop, junction of Rte 44&195 in Storrs. Right on the UCONN campus.
 
Rated :Easy
 
Time: how long does it take to eat ice cream?
 
Also:  The best little rubber stamp shop going is just inside the door!
 
Arrive at kathy John's hungry for great ice cream and a usual line of lunches and suppers. Plan enough time to go through the millions of runbber stamps.  They are located at the junction of 44&195 in Storrs behind the Texaco.
 
When you enter the shop keep to the right. Flags, candy, sumptuous cookies, decorations and nick-knacks are everywhere. When you are ready to seek the box remeber to do so discreetly as if you were shopping.  Be prepared to take it to a booth to sit down and stamp in.  I was attracted to  a great display of candy in old fashioned jars. Penny candy now a little more but just like grandpa used to buy it. Jars in a row. My eyes are distracted by displays of decorative flags for the home. So many designs they are stacked in boxes on the floor!  Pondewr which would look good at your house. As  you look through them you should comea across the reward you seek.  Stamp in and eplace well hidden in its spot for others an a quest. Through the calorie laden shop. Have fun most of all!
