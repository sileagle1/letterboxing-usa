Hi Scarab,

Some people also post new boxes to this list (I do at least), but they
are listed on the below talk list...

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/lbox-announce/

This list is automatically updated when new boxes are entered into the
site, but only if the person entering the box has the "automatically
post to list" box clicked. So there still are boxes that sneak onto
the site without it being announced. It happened to me this weekend. I
climbed to the top of Stone Mtn. just for kicks and later checked the
Georgia section of the site to find that a new box had been planted at
the top of the mountain. D'oh!!! Well, more exercise for me I guess.

Hope this helps,
