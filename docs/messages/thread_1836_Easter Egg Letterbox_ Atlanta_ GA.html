Just got back from Atlanta. Met a guy from Sacramento at a business technical session. Got to talking about hiking and before you know it, the subject of letterboxing came up. : - }
Making a long story short, he asked to go with me to find the Easter Egg Letterbox on the Emory University Campus in Atlanta. He also called up the webpage, found 3 boxes near his home, plans to get those when he gets back, plans to hide at least on in Hawaii (Oahu) this summer, and several along the coast of California in coves that he will sail into. Looks like we have found another avid letterboxer.
Oh, by the way, the Easter Egg Letterbox, is fine. It now has four visitors [1- Atlanta, 2- New England, and 1-California].
Glad to hear that the gathering was a success, this makes both winter gatherings I have missed due to business trips. : - {
