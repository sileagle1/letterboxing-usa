The stamp blanks come out to about 50 cents apiece plus whatever it will
cost for me to ship to you. Depending on how many you order, I may have to
buy a padded envelope for them, plus I guess the weight and your zip code
will determine the postage. In the end, I would guess that the cost will be
somewhere between 84 cents and $1 apiece.

weigh them in their envelopes at the post office, then email you to tell you
your total. Then you can back out or send a check.
