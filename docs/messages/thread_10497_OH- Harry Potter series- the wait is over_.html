From: Dog Scouts Troop (DogScouts@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-01-28 22:02:04 UTC-05:00

Be careful of leaving tracks in the snow that others could follow. This is a busy trail!

 

Placed on: 01-26-03

Placed by: A true blood witch (aka Scoutdogs)

Location: only found by exceptional Muggles

Closest city: Harrison

County: Hamilton

Leashed Nifflers welcome

This series contains a total of 4 boxes and 12 stamps. How you count them (if you survive) is up to you.

 

Although you are a muggle, you have been chosen from a select group to participate in an experiment. I, a secret true blood, have been authorized by the Ministry of Magic to conduct an experiment to see if muggles could possibly survive and learn (doubtful) the ways of the wizards. For a limited time, a few muggles will be allowed to experience the journey taken by true bloods and Squibs to Hogwarts. If you are able to survive the journey, you will be given limited access to Diagon Alley, Hogsmeade & Knockturn Alley so that you can equip yourselves with the tools of the trade needed to attend. Then you will seek the Sorting Hat that will assign you to your proper house for your brief stay at Hogwarts. Finally, you will journey to Kings Cross Station, platform 9 to get to Hogwarts. If you feel you are prepared for this test, you will need to find the treasures listed below in the Forbidden Forest. By making it past Boggert, Bane, Hinkypunks, Kappa & Ronin, thus surviving your trip through the forest, you will be rewarded with portkeys to various locations. So load your bag with Bertie Botts Every Flavor Beans and chocolate frogs, leash your Niffler and begin your journey if you dare!

 

Dissendium

You will be starting your journey at Owl Wood Hollow. Your first port key is located in the Badlands. This port key will take you to Hogsmeade, Diagon Alley & Knockturn. I recommend you visit Flourish & Blotts to peek at the books and learn a few needed spells (if you can even make them work). If the shop owner takes pity on you, he may even be willing to give you a Kwikspell course. Once you enter the trail, you will have to decide which way to go. Although left or right may seem the logical choices, you will go straight; for ahead of you lies what you seek. It is hidden well in the split between the large fallen tree. As you seek, watch for muggles that might interfere & once you have completed your task, re-hide it well (just in case another muggle is as talented as you). Along with the spell that opens your passage to the shops you will need to visit, you will find your magic wand from Ollivanders and 3 sets of plain work robes from Madam Malkins. Be sure to use them wisely, or you could turn yourself into a beast, or worse!

 

Now, youll need to use the spell to open the passage to visit Borgin and Burkes, Madam Malkins & The Magical Menagerie because before you locate the sorting hat, you will need the following items:

one plain pointed hat for day wear

one standard (size 2) cauldron

an enchanted broomstick (choose a Firebolt  if you dare)

optional: you can bring an owl, cat or toad

 

Be sure to don your invisibility cloak to hide your activities from muggles that have not been chosen!

 

The Sorting Hat:

Once you have the items you need, your journey is not over and is still threatened by the evil forces of the forbidden forest. To continue your journey, you will need to use the four points spell on your wand to assist you. Finding a friend who has done some arithmancy study may help as well. Since you do not have the training or the license from the Department of Magical Transportation (and you would splinch for sure), you will not be able to apparate from one place to the next, so youll need the clues youll find after getting the Dissendium Spell.  You may want to bring along a writing instrument to copy the spells youll be using.

 

If you survive your whole journey and are admitted to Hogwarts (very doubtful), you will be able to use the Tarantallegra spell (dancing spell) to celebrate as well as visiting Hogsmeade for some well deserved butterbeer (adults only). 

 

If any of you survive, please contact me so that I may amend my report that I have written on this study, which says muggles cannot possibly survive this experiment.

Good Luck!
