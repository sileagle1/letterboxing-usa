Under the Overpass - UWGB #4.
After finding the Bay Shoreline box, continue on the path you were on,
following the 90 degree turn to the left towards the road. Follow it
until the overpass, which is the second path on the right. There you
will find the box held by a stone that resembles a table on the left
side. Walking over the rocks is required and is always possible.
