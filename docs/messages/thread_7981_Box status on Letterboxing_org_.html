Box status on Letterboxing.org?

From: Mike Rego (michaeltrego@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-09-26 07:21:15 UTC-07:00
Having lead a disappointed group of letterboxers to a missing (unbeknownst to me at the time)
letterbox last weekend, I was wondering if it would be possible to indicate the box's last known
status and date on letterboxing.org. Something as simple as:

Name, #Boxes, Nearest Town, County, Established, Status

Where Status could be "Reported missing 9/26/02", "Last found 9/26/02"

And folks could report the status by sending an email to the list with the subject containing
"MISSING:" or "FOUND:", similar to the way NEW boxes are reported.

Anyway, just a thought!
