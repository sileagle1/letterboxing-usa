DONNER LAKE OVERLOOK
Rating
Clues: easy
Distance: Easy, short walk from your car on dirt.
Caution: There's snow here in winter months which will probably render this
box unreachable.

This box #4 should be #3 but it didn't turn out that way. Trust us: do this
one first, THEN do #3.

Take the "Vista Point" exit overlooking Donner Lake I-80. From the pointed
monument, take a bearing of 165 degrees for 60 paces, to a tree among several
large rocks that just peak above the ground. From the tree, take a bearing of
275 degrees for 15 paces to a boulder that stands higher than the rest. Just
past the boulder on the north side, a few feet away is your prize under a flat
rock.
