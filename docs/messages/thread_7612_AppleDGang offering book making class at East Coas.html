AppleDGang offering book making class at East Coast gathering

From: adglbna (ADGLBNA@aol.com) | Date: 2002-09-11 01:16:49 UTC
The Apple Dumpling Gang is pleased to announce that Sarah, our artist
in residence, will be offering a book making class at the East Coast
gathering. I doubt that we will make it by 8:00 AM so plan on an
afternoon lesson.

If you are interested in participating, please e-mail us at
ADGLBNA@aol.com and indicate if you would prefer to bring supplies or
chip in a little to defer the costs. ($3.00 should cover it)

Bring any papers that you would like to use for your cover including
handmade papers, wrapping paper scraps, color copies, photographs,
ticket stubs, or anything else meaningful to you. Sarah likes to
bind her books with ribbon so bring some if you like this method.
She generally uses unwired ribbon about an inch or thinner.

To letterboxing dreams and hitchhiker wishes!

The Gang
