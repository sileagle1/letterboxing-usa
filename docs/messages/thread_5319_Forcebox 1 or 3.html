ForceBox #1
(Area  Cincinnati, Ohio)
 
(Forcebox #1 can be found at Groh Park in Amelia)
 
(Trail, flat black top walk, Very Easy about 1/16 Mile Round Trip)
 
  From I-275 take the SR 125 Exit (East to Amelia)
 
 At the Traffic light in Front of Quail Creek, Turn right onto Huntsman Tr.
Go about 1/10th of a mile, The park is on the left.
 
 From the Parking lot the trail is a loop. Start on the trail by going to the left
and in the direction of the Shelter. After you app the shelter, The trail makes a
90' left turn. Walk about 12 paces and on your left you will see a Dead Tree trunk
sticking up about 10 feet.  At the base of the tree trunk is the tree that snapped
and fell. inside the part of tree that is laying on the ground is (Forcebox #1)
 
 
 (This is Box 1 of 3 of our May 2002 Theme  (Attack of the Clones) Each month for the rest of the
year we will hide at least 3 boxes with different theme for each month).
