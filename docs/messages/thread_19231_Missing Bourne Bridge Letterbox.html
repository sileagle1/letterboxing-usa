From: Christopher Martin (cscm@toast.net) | Date: 2004-06-24 14:28:51 UTC
It means that boxes that are reported as missing are very often not.
Unless you are the placer of the box, and know for sure it's missing,
you're safest saying you "couldn't find" it. False reports of missing
boxes, however well-intentioned, may keep others for looking for boxes
that are merely well-hidden.

Hancock Hill is a notable example. Reported as "missing" by several
boxers over a period of more than a year, it was found exactly where
the clues said it would be.
