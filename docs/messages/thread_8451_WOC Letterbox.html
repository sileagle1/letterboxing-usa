This WOC letterbox was placed 10/15/02 by fIsh and day Z. 
Shenandoah National Park
White Oak Canyon Trail
Rappahannock County, VA

 
This journey begins on Skyline Drive at mile 42.6 near Skyland.  This is the parks most popular hike, boasting 6 waterfalls, a must-do for first time visitors.  The trail is 5.3 miles one way (the letterbox is approximately half the way down) but can be done as a 7.3 mile circuit when joined with the Cedar Run trail. 
 
FIsh and I come to the park every year on the second weekend in October to celebrate fIsh's B'Day.  It's one of our favorite hiking spots (100's to choose from).  We hope you enjoy it as much as we do.
