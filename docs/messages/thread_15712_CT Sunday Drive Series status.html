CT Sunday Drive Series status

From: birder579 (birder579@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-10 22:03:16 UTC
I went out and did the Sunday Drive series today. All the boxes
were found and in good shape.

There is a road closed on the route. It is from mileage 13.5 to
14.7 in the clues. I was able to take a detour around the area and
come back in to get the third box. The signs say that the road will
be closed at various times for reconstruction during this fall. It
looks like there will be times that access to the parking lot at
mileage 13.8 will be closed.

There was a couple and their dog having lunch at the picnic table
just 50 feet from the box. I sat down over the box to pick it up,
stamped it in the car, and sat down over the spot to rehide it. I
don't think they were suspicious. This is a nefarious hobby at
times.
