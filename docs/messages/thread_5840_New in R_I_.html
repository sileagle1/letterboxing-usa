Bobbin' Bobber at Carolina Trout Pond

Route 112 to the Carolina Trout Pond Fishing Area
Drive down the dirt road until you reach the pond.
Park and head for the small monument remembering Samuel Stillman Cudworth.
Go down the steps, then walk clockwise around the pond.
Enter the woods on a narrow path along the pond's edge. When you are across
from the sandy shore, look left for a large chunk of ledge sticking high
above the ground. Go to the southern corner and look between a broken off
shard and the ledge under leaves to find the Bobbin' Bobber Letterbox.
