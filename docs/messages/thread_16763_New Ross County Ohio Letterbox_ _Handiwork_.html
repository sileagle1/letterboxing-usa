Title: Handiwork. Placed 11-08-03 by Salamander. Location:
Chillicothe, Ohio. Difficulty: Easy. This letterbox is located in
St. Margaret's Cemetery, Chillicothe Ohio. Driving directions: From
Rt. 50 (Western Ave.) turn north onto Delano Ave.( Next to Central
Center shopping center). Follow Delano north until it dead ends at
Allen Ave. Turn left onto Allen Ave., and go 2 blocks to Worthington
Ave. Turn right onto Worthington; the cemetery entrance will be
about one block away, on your left. CLUES: Upon entering
the cemetery, bear towards the right. Continue until you come to
a "hairpin" turn. Stop before entering the turn and look for a stump
near the fence. From this stump, you will need to take 12 paces at
18 degrees to a large pine tree. Then go 13 paces at 335 degrees
from the pine to another tree. The box is at the base of this tree
under some bark and a rock.
