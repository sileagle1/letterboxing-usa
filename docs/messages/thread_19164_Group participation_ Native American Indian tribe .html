Hello to all,
First thanks for all the responses from everyone. Most have been
asking questions. Hope this helps a bit.
The series is open to any Native American Indian tribe of North
America. I am hoping for representative stamps from various tribes
around the country. There will likely be a bonus stamp for those that
complete the entire series.
Those that submit stamps for this series will receive index cards
with the imprints of all the stamps of the series as exchanges. The
option is to decline this if you are able to search for the stamps.
All carvers will be given credit on the clues for each stamp.
Stamps are not due until August 27th. Remember to contact me off
list with questions.
I am looking forward to this series. Thanks to all in advance who
help make this possible. Safe trails!
Koyote
