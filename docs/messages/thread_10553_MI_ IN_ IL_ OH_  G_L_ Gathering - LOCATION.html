MI, IN, IL, OH: G.L. Gathering - LOCATION

From: SpringChick <letterbox@attbi.com> (letterbox@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-02-01 20:06:57 UTC
Just an FYI... I closed out the location poll since today. The
votes are spread, but it does seem that most people would prefer a
central location to alternating regions from year-to-year. On the
other hand, a lot of people said they would try to make it either
way... Considering the areas that have expressed interest in coming
(Chicago, Cleveland, all areas of MI), Ann Arbor/Jackson is centrally
located to all, and would be about 3.5 hours from the outer edges.

I liked Ruthann's suggestion about Hudson Mills MetroPark as a
central location, but am wondering about the size of the park? I was
there last fall, looking for the April Fool's box and it didn't
appear the park was very large. Of course, I stopped only to find
the box and made a beeline in the direction of the clues from the
parking area, so I could have missed a lot... Just wanting to make
sure there will be ample space for hiding boxes. Also... was there
a shelter of any kind there?

I am not real familiar with the Ann Arbor/Jackson area, so any other
suggestions as to location would be welcome.

And just to reiterate, or in case you missed the earlier message --
the date is Saturday, May 31, 2003. Mark it on your calendars!

Within the next week or so I will be creating a web page for details
and information about the gathering as we go forward... If you have
any ideas or suggestions about activities, etc., please don't
hesitate to speak up!

Deb (SpringChick)
