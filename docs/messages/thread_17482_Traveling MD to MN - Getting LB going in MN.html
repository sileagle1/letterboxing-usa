If you plant them, they will come. When I started here in Idaho
August 2002, there were only 2 boxes in this state. After a while I
was bored of planting and would travel FAR to find some, but look at
our state now. We're even having a gathering this weekend with
several folks from Idaho and it's going to grow even more. Thanks to
all those who have travelled through our state, left some for us to
find even mystery boxes, and stamped into our boxes. We're so
excited. So I encourage you to keep planting them and slowly others
will too. =-) We have cousins there in Minnesota and one day
they'll be getting married so you can count on some more boxes placed
by us in MN whenever that day comes. =-)

A-Bear from Star, ID
