A couple of people contacted me to let me know that the book barn LB was
missing, thank you. Today I finally was able to replace it. I carved the same
image but it is in a new place. These are the clues that I posted on the LBNA
website.

As you enter the grounds of Book Barn go up the stairs on your left into the
main building. Enter the building, head up the stairs, watch out for sleeping
dogs and cats. At the top of the stairs look right toward the children's
section. Notice the corner in front of you. Reach up at the top of it to find
the newly carved book barn letterbox. (It is the same design as the one that
went missing but in a new location)
