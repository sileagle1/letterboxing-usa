East of the Fork Event (July 27-29th) Update

From: covertcachers (covertcachers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-06-19 23:53:45 UTC
I will try and post an update ever week or so ssing the event is
only 6 weeks away.

7 more letterbox stamps have been added. We now have a total
of 110 stamps for the event plus however many out of town stamps
have been sent in. We also need everyone's help in getting the word
out about this event. Next year we are going to have a Midwest
letterboxing event and we will need a lot of help to do it. This
event will help us see what will be needed to have an event of
grand scale. The Date and and place of the event will be coming
at the end of June.









East of the Fork Event link
http://swogo.org/cachebb/viewtopic.php?t=793


If you can not make this event but like to be here anyway, Just
carve a stamp and send it to us. We will have a table set up
where people can stamp up your stamp. SWOGO will give you a
log ook where letterboxers can place their personal stamps in
and will sent it all back to you after the event. All you need
to do is send your stamp!

Here is the address to send your stamp to. PLEASE make sure you
send in all the information about your stamp.(I E your letterboxing
team name and you address to send it back to)

========================
East of the Fork Event
c/o Dave Kilgore
19 Hopkins Ave
Amelia, Ohio 45102
========================
