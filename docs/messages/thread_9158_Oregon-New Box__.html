Hot Spring Box
Bigelow/Deer Creek Hot Spring
Eugene, Oregon, Lane County
Planted by: Floating Feather & Sprout
November 2002


Directions: 61 miles NE of Eugene, Oregon. From Eugene take Rt. 126, 57
miles East to Belknap Springs. Continue 4 miles North (midway between
milepost 15 anf 14) and turn left onto Deer Creek Road. Cross the river and
park just before the bridge.

You can hike the upper trail and/or indulge in the natural hot spring along
the lower trail. Either way, as you enter the paths with the Deer Creek sign
on your left, sign in under the "rootwad" on your right!
