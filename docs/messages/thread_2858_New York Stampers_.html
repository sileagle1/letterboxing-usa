There have 2 stamp conventions at the South Jersey Expo Center in Pennsauken
every year. Both are put on by Heirloom productions.   The expo center
address is:

2323 Route 73
Pennsauken NJ
(856) 661-0200

The next one is August 4th and 5th.  

They have them every april and august.  The april one is much better and
bigger but both are still really great.  Most of the vendors are from private
stamp companies.  Suze Weinburg has a booth in April. She does free demos all
weekend. There are free classes and demos going on all over the place.  
Several booths also offer free make and takes.  You can stock up on supplies
and most companies sell both Mounted and Unmounted stamps.  I love it and go
to both every year.
