A misspelled Bush,
an asphalt Brooke.
Near both of those
you start your look.

Not far from a Duke
or big gray state.
Noon is too busy,
nite is too late.

Lilypads and fish
await you there.
A 2 story circle
with Asian flair.

Beside the pond
green frog spits.
Box is hidden
where he sits.


* Please make sure to cover the box back up with the felt.
