Got home today a little early today and took Molly for a walk down the old Air Line RR Trail. We walked (and Molly played chase the stick) up to my "Ghost Train" letterbox. I thought I would check it and head back before it got too dark. It was about 5 degrees out there without the wind chill factor. To my surprise we found the "Underground Railroad" hitchhiker. It's been sitting there since Nov 19 so I took it home and will relocate it soon. Here is the history of the HH as I understand it from the log book.
 
Launched By: Funkybluesmama  4-17-02 at "Pine Plantation" Scotland CT
Found by Patrick and Amy and moved to "Little River" Scotland CT
Found by Ruth & Company and moved 4-29-02 to "Arboretum Adventure" New London, CT
Found by Rubaduc, Joann & Barry and replanted 5-9-02 at "Compounce Ridge" Southington, CT
Found by Pete and Wanda and placed at "Clarks Corner" Hampton CT
Found by Leader of the Pack, Linten, Judi, The Bunny and replanted 11-19-02 at "Ghost Train" Chaplin, CT
Found by Chuck and Molly 1-23-03 at Ghost Train and replanted????
 
Both the "Clarks Corner" and "Ghost Train" are located on the old Air Line RR bed in Windham County CT Kind of appropriate for "Underground Railroad"
 
Still love walking on the old "Air Line" RR.....Chuck and Molly
