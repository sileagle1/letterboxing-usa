Does anyone know if the Pt Judith Light and High Liner boxes are
missing? (maybe they were washed out to sea?) I was out there today
and could not find either one. I think I was looking in the correct
places. There were the tell-tale signs of a missing letterbox -- a
scattered pile of small rocks where you expect the letterbox to be.

Also, there was a note at the Black Pt box that the Salty Brine and
Jerusalem boxes are missing. Does anyone know the status of these 2
boxes?

Janet
