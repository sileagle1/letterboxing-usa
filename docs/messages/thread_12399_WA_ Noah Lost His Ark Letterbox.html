Noah Lost The Ark Letterbox
Placed by: Little Moose, Jag and Rosebud
Date: April 27, 2003
County/State: Cowlitz, Wa (Region 5)
Town: Longview
Current # of Boxes: 1


Noah has lost his ark. Can you help him find it?

Clues: As the water was drying up, Noah fell out of the ark. Noah
looked around and to his surprise he was at Lake Sacajawea. It is
said that Noah built the ark or did he play on it? But, wait this
can't be it, the ark is caged in. It can't float. Noah wasn't sure
what to do, so he will go and ask God under the arch entrance. God
tells Noah to go 250 degrees and he will find the path to lead him
to the ark. At the beginning of the path go 67 steps. It was once
said that things can always be found in the center of one of three
trees.
