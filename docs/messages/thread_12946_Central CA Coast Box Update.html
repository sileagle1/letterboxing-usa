From: Sir Balthazar (neovolatile@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-18 23:35:05 UTC
Folks,

Ash Raven, JugglerMouse, and I went out yesterday along the coast. It
was a P1 F6 day for Ms. Mouse and myself. Raven got a P1 F1, since
she had already gotten most of the lot.

Boxes we checked that were still there:

Citizen Kane series (all three happy and healthy)
Moonstone Castle (good stamp, pretty location)
Salmon Creek Falls (great stamp & planting site and lovely view)
Potty Break (still very much there in Gorda)

Missing in action:

Missing Map (we found the stones but they had been long disturbed)
Tink's Wand (a fountain plant in too easily seen location)

We also did a first for us: we cut in a store owners on the plant and
have him and her hand you the box if you say the right thing.
*Teehee* They are stoked about it. Will post this Gandalf stamp clues
soon. Read about something similar in England where you go to a pub
and ask. Anybody else doing this?

We are proud that the Central California Coast has more boxes than
any other region of California. Okay, we are none Connecticut yet,
but we are trying.

I'm Feeling Lucky!
