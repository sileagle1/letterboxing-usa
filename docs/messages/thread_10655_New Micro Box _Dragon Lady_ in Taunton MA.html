New Micro Box "Dragon Lady" in Taunton MA

From: Brian, Ryan & Lori <bconnoll@marksonrosenthal.com> (bconnoll@marksonrosenthal.com) | Date: 2003-02-05 21:27:56 UTC
The Dragon Lady restaurant is located at 17 School Street . It is
just off Main street in Taunton Center.
After you enter search for the large golden Buddha by the fountain.
Look low and left of the fountain.
This is a Micro Box so please bring a stamp pad and pen. The
restaurant has great food (Approved by Wanda & Pete!!)
and a delightful wait staff. The owner knows of the box but please
do be discreet.

Have Fun
