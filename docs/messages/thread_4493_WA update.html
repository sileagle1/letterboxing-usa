WA update

From: dvn2rckr <dvn2rckr@yahoo.com> (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-01-08 18:10:17 UTC
Located a few LBs and checked on several others the past few days.

Found Farrell's Marsh in Steilacoom in great shape.

Checked on Saltar's Point LB in Steilacoom. Doing well with several
visitors.

Found Little Happiness LB in Kelsey Creek Park with a few friends
yesterday. It's doing well. Checked on 2 of the 3 other boxes in
that park and they're doing well. Couldn't find the 3rd one in the
series (did find Mischief's and the Horse one--but didn't have the
clues on me and wasn't able to 're-find' the other one without the
clues). For some reason now, I really want to go out for some great
Dim Sum... ;)

Found the Bond, James Bond mystery LB. What an incredible letterbox
with great clues, nice hike and a great stamp, to boot!!!

Found the Table Mystery with no problem; instantly knew which table
was referenced in the clues while reading it. It's such a lonely
little table these days... Loved the box--wish I had a great
workshop... ;)

Checked on Eliza B's Heart of Lake WA (Mercer Island) a few weeks
ago and it's doing well. Several visitors with a few HHs, too!

Found Cougar Mountain LB and it's doing quite well with a few
visitors well. If you're looking for a good, hearty workout--that's
a great hike! Absolutely gorgeous when the weather's good (which it
has been lately).

Placed a new LB yesterday but the clues will have to wait until I
can return home to my own computer.

Hope this update helps some of you with your LB maintenance! Have a
great week & keep finding (& placing) those boxes!
