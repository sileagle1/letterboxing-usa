Last week we visited the Schroon Lake area and located the Scaroon Manor
Letterbox. It was well worth the time we spent looking for it - the
majestic white pines and the stone work were magnificant.
This was our first letterbox find and we are hooked. Unbelievable how
someone my age could get so excited over such a simple thing but my
daughter and I both giggled with glee when we finally figured out the
directions and uncovered the box.
