From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2002-06-05 05:28:55 UTC
On June 4,2002 Funhog (with the aid of the ever-creative Windairen)
placed the Fallen Feather Letterbox along the Banks-Vernonia Trail
near Banks, Washington County, Oregon. This is an addition to the
newly initiated Banks-Vernonia Trail Network. Please refer to the
Buxton Trestle Clue for further details on this endeavor.

to the letterbox: From the Buxton Trestle Letterbox continue south on
the trail. Thirty six yards beyond the final plank of a wooden span
you will spot an avian abode east of the trail. The Fallen Feather
rests in its front yard screened by bark. Do not disturb the
neighbors. There may be babies on board.
