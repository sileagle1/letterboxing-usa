From: Lynda (neverenuff1969@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-31 18:28:48 UTC
We had a long day of hunting yesterday and are proud to say that we
found 3 boxes! :)

We started with Wolf Box, and ended up walking in circles for about
an hour. The first few clues are a little confusing once you're out
there. The box is in great shape though, and has two log books. A
clue in the second book leads to a beautiful spot. :)

Dance of Joy was next, and very easy to find. It's in great shape,
and has the cutest stamp. ThunderWing (our 12 yr. old) really liked
that one.

Next up was Brandywine Box. The clues are pretty easy to follow and
we found it with no problem. We did have to cross an overgrown field
with stickers in it though. (which we didn't know where there until
we where halfway through the field) SecretAgent (my partner) also had
a close run in with a frisbee! The box is in good shape, but there's
no stamp in it. The box is abandoned (I think) and if I had a spare
stamp with me, I would have put it in there. I may go back sometime
and leave one. There are also a few not so nice notes scribbled in
the log book by what I think were some kids out playing frisbee golf
that happened upon the box. *shakes head in disgust at how
disrespectful some people can be* It was a long hike back to the car
also, but it may just have seemed like it since we were so tired
already. LOL

All in all, it was a nice day though. We're hoping to get up to PA on
Sunday and then possibly down to the shore on Wednesday. I wish there
were more boxes in DE though. Maybe I'll have to plant a few. ;)
