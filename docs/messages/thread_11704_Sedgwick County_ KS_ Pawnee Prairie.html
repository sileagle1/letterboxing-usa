"Pawnee Prairie"
Placed by Jay & Kendra 4/3/03
Sedgwick County, KS
In/near Wichita, KS

From Hwy K-42 (Southwest Blvd) in Wichita, turn north on Tyler Rd.
Pawnee Prairie Nature Center is at 2625 S. Tyler. Take the trailhead
of the "Nature Trail" by the nature center.

Pace calibration: the bridge from the parking lot is 46 paces

CLUES:
1) From the sign of the beaver and deer, walk 1/3 mile without
disturbing a single blade of grass
2) Follow the horses' lead and cross the "Big R"
3) Follow the trail along the cowskin toward the sunset
4) At the meeting of the ways, descend 20 paces and look left
5) The box is southwest of the woodpeckers' friend about 10 feet, in
the fork of a fallen tree

Watch out for briars, ticks, and snakes!
