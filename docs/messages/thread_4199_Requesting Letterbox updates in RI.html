From: riwizard (Hopkinsr1@aol.com) | Date: 2002-01-18 15:01:39 UTC
Visiting Mercy in her new home would be fine. Also, finding the
Kurdziel's 2 boxes in the Crossing Over series would be fun. I highly
recommend both, since Mercy is mine, and the other two were planted
by letterboxing friends. But don't give up on Jamestown just yet. I
just got back from the Island, and Ft. Getty is right where the
Drewclan put it a few years back. It's in need of a new box, but all
else is well with her. As it is too, with Ft. Wetherill. I placed a
few more rocks over that one to hide it a little better from those
who aren't out there letterboxing. As for the Island Sanctuary, even
if you didn't find the box, the views across the wetlands are truly
awesome. Take the hike. I'm not absolutely sure, but my guess is the
box is still out there. It did turn up once missing it's journal,
which I've replaced, but it's not a box likely to be found by
accident, so I'm guessing you're gonna find it. And even if you don't
find them, like Jay said, the Island and the views are super this
time of year. You won't come back empty handed. Let me leave you with
a few words from a man who spoke volumes during his to short time
here with us, "It's got to be the going, not the getting there that's
good". Harry Chapin
