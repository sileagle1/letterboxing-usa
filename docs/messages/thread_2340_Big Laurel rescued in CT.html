Big Laurel rescued in CT

From: Miner Peter R NPRI (MinerPR@npt.nuwc.navy.mil) | Date: 2001-05-14 16:46:08 UTC-04:00


Wanda recently went looking for the Wadsworth Falls Park
boxes and found Big Laurel missing. After hiking the loop
to the falls (a great photo site) and back she went to use the
telephone and there on a nearby picnic table was an unattended
tupperware box - Big Laurel, intact and ready to go home!

We have located dozens of other boxes in CT, RI and nearby MA over
the last three months with great enthusiasm and only occasional difficulty
and have come up empty handed only 3 times:

Avery Memorial in CT and Stepping Stone in RI are missing; they
are no longer at home where we found them earlier.

Devil's Hopyard #2 in CT also seems missing as all we found under
a likely 4x6 rock were leaves and acorn shucks.

Has anyone seen these 3 boxes in the last few weeks?

Moose on the Loose was found Friday hitching a ride at Wilcox Park
in RI. It has joined us for awhile in search of greener pastures!

Palantir was found at Bear Cave on April 27th and is now residing at a
new lookout spot in another state.

Pete and Wanda
