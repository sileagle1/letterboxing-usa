'Tis a bitterly sad day, indeed. Please do not read any further. The next
paragraph is not suitable for young children or older persons. In fact, the

destructive power--a phrase meaning billions of dollars in damage and even
more dreadful loss of life--of Hurricane Charlie first hand. The pictures I
have seen on my small black-and-white television flickering in the back of
my dreary cave have convinced me that Count Olaf was in Florida at this time
gleefully enjoying the hurricane.

We can hope and pray he did not survive in such a tempestuous storm, but
