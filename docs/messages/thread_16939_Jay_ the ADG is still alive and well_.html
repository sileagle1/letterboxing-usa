I just wanted to post a little something to thank Jay for his
encouraging comments in his interview.

I honestly stopped posting on this site because I got bitter due to
all of the sniping. (uh, oh, I smell a flamewar!)

Plus we were totally obsessed for the first year but then our lives
started creeping back in and we just didn't make time to box a lot.
We are still in contact with the local boxers and hope to make a
comeback next year.

Sarah, our master carver, also got involved in lots of other crafty
projects but I am sure if I ask and inspire her with new ideas that
she will carve some new marvels for the ADG.

So thanks for the reference. It is nice to know that someone out
there enjoyed the stamps, the clues, the humor and the slightly off
kilter remarks.
