New Massachusetts Letterbox

From: Fred Lorch (lonemasswolf@earthlink.net) | Date: 2002-09-18 19:47:35 UTC-04:00
There is an interesting letterbox being prepared. It shall consist of four individual stamps that will be available for a limited time only.
 
The name of the letterbox is "A Tree For All Seasons"
 
The first stamp "Fall" will be placed on 21 September 2002, and removed on 20 December 2002.
The second stamp "Winter" will be placed on 21 December 2002, and removed on 20 March 2003.
The third stamp "Spring" will be placed on 21 March 2003 and remover on 20 June 2003.
The forth stamp "Summer" will be placed on 21 June 2003 and removed on 20 September 2003.
On 21 September 2003 the letterbox will be retired.
 
The clues to the letterbox location will be posted this Friday, 20 September.
