Hi Ann,

I started letterboxing in May with my 5 year old twins and a now 7 month
old. We've now found over 50, and I've lost lots of weight! My 7 month old
usually travels in a Gerry backpack which has a simple aluminum frame. It's
got a pouch with 2 leg holes for the baby and a strap that goes around his
middle. I started with him in it when he was 4 month old. They do need to
be able to hold up their head to ride in it. He has always loved it and
falls asleep in it frequently. I also have an LL Bean backpack that is more
substantial. I've tried it once but didn't have it adjusted correctly. The
people I have talked to have used this for older "babies" and have
recommended it. Please feel free to e-mail me directly if you have more
questions.

Megan in CT
