Hi there--

Fun trying to letterbox in Asia, eh? To date the only stashing games
we've successfully hunted for in Asia are geocaching & terracaching--
so if you've got the bug to look for stuff you might want to go that
route. We ended up planting letterboxes in the various places we
visited in Asia (Korea, China, Thailand, Cambodia) just so there'd be
some boxes there to seek out. Unfortunately, we haven't yet visited
the Philippines. :(

We also did a bunch of geocaching & terracaching there just so we'd
get to explore more of those little known secret treasures scattered
about the region--that letterboxes typically lead us to. There are
probably a few geocaches scattered about the Philippines--just
knowing how more internationally popular that hobby is!

However, when I look for 'little-known' international letterbox
clues, I usually go to this site:

www.letterboxing-germany.de/

Occasionally, you'll see clues to letterboxes around Asia. Be sure
to click on the British flag to view clues in English. Otherwise,
hone your German skills. ;)

Unfortunately, there aren't any listed there for the Philippines yet--
although you could change that. ;) Oh, sometimes you might find a
hybrid letterbox/geocache on the geocaching site so you could look
for those, too.

Enjoy!

daelphinus
