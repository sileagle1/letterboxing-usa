From: (see_joy29@hotmail.com) | Date: 2001-01-30 00:37:00 UTC
Thank you Cock a Doodle Moo crew, Carol, Beth and everyone who made
the gathering at the Ag. center in Vernon possible!! It was well
worth the long drive to meet so many great friendly folks and their
great friendly children!! Ct.is a beautiful state indeed.Our
contingent of four representing PA. and N.J. letterboxers enjoyed the
Drew Clan's "Trout fishing in America" boxes,event boxes,ice cream
shop box and the "gold rush" stamp exchanges!! I was excited to match
faces with on-line names and messages but will admitt to being
greatly disappointed to have missed the Drew Clan..next time..we also
missed the first two boxes at Lake Compounce which we hunted on our
way up to Vernon.If you could have seen us on our hands and knees
digging up the snow for 30min. on the top of the "fish head" you'd
have had a good laugh!!! We are a determined bunch but snow does make
a difference! Carol, thanks for the fun games!! The adults were
alittle slow "coming round" but we all had a blast!! My thanks to one
and all!! joie and austin christenten aka "see joy" and "lockmaster"
