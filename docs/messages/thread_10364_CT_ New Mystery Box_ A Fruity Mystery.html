A Fruity Mystery
Somewhere in CT
Placed by Music Woman in honor of Letterboxer2002's first year anniversary of letterboxing.
Placed: January 2003

Please note that this box can only be accessed
Wednesday through Sunday
11:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
Closed Major Holidays


Find this person for you to bother,
Who shares his name with some others.
Neither pastor, athlete nor musician,
Not philosopher or metaphysician.

No "Sunshine" or "Honky Tonk,"
Do not worry, this is not bunk.
Go Two where they say Wyassup!
Then to Maine and then go up!

Cross the Wood,
To where a Dairy Barn stood.
Up the stairs,
And right for your affairs.

Although a pastor may use
Hopefully you can't refuse.
After you take a sip,
Ask for a "special" tip.

For near the sacrament divine,
Is a letterbox for you to find.
Stamp in and have fun
In the summer sun!
