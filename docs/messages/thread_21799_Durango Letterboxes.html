There are actually a fair number (at least for Colorado) of
letterboxes near the Durango area! On the campus, in town, Animas
Overlook (which has something like 5) and in the mountains and into
Silverton. Easy ones and some that need you to strap on your hiking
boots.
And I for one am tickled pink to hear there are a few more in the
works.. woot!

Es, waiting for the sign to GO!
