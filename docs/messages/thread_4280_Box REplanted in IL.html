Stand at the Corner.

What a Mistake! Check your Compass.

Us too!

This box was the 2nd letterbox planted in the Illinois St.Louis Metro East
area. Due to "unforeseen" circumstance it was removed from circulation in
11/2000 and has just been REplanted. . It is now known as:
U ISLAND: the letterbox formerly known as Across the Board. .
When you visit the area, the reasons will become obvious (at least for a
while). .
Sorry ... no access on Sundays or some evenings after 5:30pm.

Happy Hunting

new find .. if they find it again!
