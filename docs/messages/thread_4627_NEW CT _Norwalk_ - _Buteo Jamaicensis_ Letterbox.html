I placed a new letterbox in Norwalk, CT. It is at a nature preserve
that is in danger of being turned into a driving range and restraunt.
It happens to be a point of debate in the city of Norwalk.

I hope you will all get to do this very easy letterbox. If you enjoy
the area ... and would not like to see it detroyed ... I would ask that
you write to the address included on the web page to voice your
opinion.

NOTE: Birdwatchers ... BRING BINOCULARS!!!! There is a family of Red-
Tailed Hawks who call this preserve their home. Though the box will
take 10 minutes to do. I spent 1.5 hours sitting and observing the
hawks, woodpeckers, mallards, etc.
