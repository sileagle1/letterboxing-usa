From: (HANNAHKAT@aol.com) | Date: 2002-12-27 19:05:01 UTC-05:00
Hi folks!

Just to let you know...
So far we have about 20 people that have RSVP'd. Yay!

I haven't heard whether Deanne B.B. (aka the lazy letterboxer) has found a ride up from the Cape yet. I think Warriorwoman was also looking for a lift from the Metrowest area. Post to them if you can give them a hand...er... I mean...ride.

I have carved an event stamp and am going to try to place a new box just for the occasion. I am very excited to see you all.

I also want to reiterate the invitation to anyone that may not yet have heard. We will be gathering for a potluck at 12 noon on Saturday January 18th at the Moose Hill Wildlife Sanctuary in Sharon, MA. We will be gathering inside the Nature Center. (Sorry, no pets)

I will be providing the hot and cold beverages and an appetizer. So bring a favorite dish to share (and the recipe) and your favorite letterboxing buddies.

If you are planning on coming, please RSVP to the list or to me privately (or to cscm@toast.net) so we will know how many to plan for (tables and chairs and soda etc).

See you soon!
-Kim (aka Rustypuff)
