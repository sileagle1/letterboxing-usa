From: Steve (shol3039@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-24 20:54:09 UTC
We started our new, improved logbook (Second book) today and got out
for a short trip this afternoon and found Fort Griswold, NL Harbor
and NL Ledge lights. Fort Griswold was pretty damp and the baggie in
side was in poor shape. I only had 1 spare bag so I put the logbook
in it, resealed tightly and hid it well. The logbook, by the way,
is filled to the brim and has been since last fall. We added a few
blank pages but they are not in the book securely, so I think a book
2 is definately in order. I may try to put a #2 book in it this
weekend if we have time.

Found the NL Harbor and NL Ledge boxes in fine condition and got a
bonus at NL Harbor (The Beaver Crew HH, which has a LOT of visits in
it!)

We were headed to Bluff point and Haley Farm but it was VERY windy
out and we got sick of running after the clues when the wind yanked
them from our hands!

Steve, Heidi and Madras
