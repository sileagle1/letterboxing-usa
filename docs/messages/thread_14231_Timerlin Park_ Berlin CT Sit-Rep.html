Just hiked the trail for the 3 boxes
1. Pinecone-needs new ink pad-lid was broken
2. Timberland Park-it is still the old misspelled stamp so I was
very excited to have it since the clues said it would be replaced
with the correctly spelled Timberlin park stamp--and by the way it
is a BEAUTIFUL Stamp!!!!!
3. Ball and Tee-sadly it was missing. I could see where it had

We also found, purely by mistake, a geocache-in a very cool

It was a GREAT Hike full of rocky ledges, hilly terrain, scary drop
offs and gorgeous overlooks, creaky old bridges etc. I enjoyed
the hike considerably and recommend it to any one in who hasn't
gone yet. I'm tempted to leave a new box or 2 on this cool trail.

One word of caution- not good for small children, since path
follows rocky ledge for quite a while.
