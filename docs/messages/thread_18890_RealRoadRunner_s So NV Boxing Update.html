From: Suzanne H. (realroadrunner@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-05-23 15:38:28 UTC
Kerblammo, Sammybird, Dad and I went out to the Valle del Fuego yesterday before it gets
too hot here in Southern Nevada to even think about going hiking.

We found Ryan's Petroglyph Series #1 with no problem. The log book is full up. We have
to remember to start packing extra logs with us so we can help out in this situation.

Petroglyph #2 is verified missing. Don and Gwen's Rock Art aka Petroglyph Series #3, is in
great shape.

Finally, we went to the Cabins and picked up Pony Express. Again, the log book for this
microbox is at capacity.

I haven't been out to this state park in a long, long time so it was wonderful to visit. I was
glad to see new letterboxers Gronilot and crew had already visited. There's a couple new
reasons to visit this site...hope top hear from you when you do.

I will be planting more of the Johnny West Series in the next couple of weeks...stay tuned.
