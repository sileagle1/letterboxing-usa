"There & Back Again" Hitchhicker found

From: markpepe (mjpepe1@attbi.com) | Date: 2002-08-22 03:00:25 UTC
Hi:

On our way to Gatlinburg, TN, we found the "There & Back Again"
Hitchhiker planted by the Drew Clan with the Lord of the Rings
series of boxes in Carlisle, PA. The Lord of the Rings Series was
great. Our nieces were so excited. This was our first hitchhiker and
we were all thrilled to find it on August 10th.

The Hitchhiker now resides with the Davy Crockett letterbox in a
park that shares his name in Limestone, TN. It is a great stamp and
we thoroughly enjoyed finding and having it with us on our vacation
to Gatlinburg!


The Pepe's & Davidow's
Kensington, CT
