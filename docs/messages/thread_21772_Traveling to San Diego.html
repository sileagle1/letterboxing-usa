RE: [LbNA] Traveling to San Diego

From: Curt Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-04-15 15:51:46 UTC-04:00
I was just in San Diego last month and had the opportunity to do several
LBs. A couple that I recommend (if you have transportation are just north
of San Diego in La Jolla. "La Jolla" placed by Cadenza is an amazingly
beautiful walk along the cliffs overlooking the Pacific Ocean with lots of
brown pelicans, wildflowers, and an incredible view. It is also in the
heart of La Jolla shopping district with wonderful restaurants if you are
so inclined. I left a HH in this box, but haven't heard anything "from it",
so if you happen to pick it up, let me know. Another beautiful walk is just
a few miles up the coast at the Scripps Coastal Reserve in La Jolla - the
letterbox called "Tea Time", again with gorgeous views. Then about 5 or 6
miles north of that is the San Diego Sails Letterbox which you can combine
with a walk on the beach and a short hike through the hills above the
ocean. I also tried to find the Pacific Crest Trail LB to no avail. I'm
pretty sure I located the spot where it should have been but after
relentless searching, came up dry. The Pacific Crest Trail is about an hour
east of San Diego off Interstate 8. It's a beautiful hike, but I reported
that the LB is apparently missing. I had printed out clues to several other
LBs, but there just wasn't enough time! Enjoy your trip.
