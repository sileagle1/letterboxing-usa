From: oceanwytch (oceanwytch@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-18 16:26:32 UTC
There has been a slight change in location for the gathering. It
will now be held at the Fort McClarey picnic area, which is right
down the street from Fort Foster on Route 103 in Kittery. Same
day....October 22nd....new times...9-4. We're going to have many
boxes, so we thought we'd start a little earlier.

Come one come all to Frightnight at the Fort. It's a day sure to be
filled with fun for all ages with special games, crafts and prizes.
This will be a potluck event, so please bring something to share.
There is a covered picnic area and bathrooms nearby. There are
plenty of boxes in the area, so plan to spend the day. We are going
to have quite a few event only boxes, so plan your day accordingly.

There will be 2 costume contests at 2pm...one for kids and one for
adults with prizes awarded at 3pm.

Please RSVP if possible either on Atlasquest or by e-mailing me at
oceanwytch at yahoo dot com.

Hope to see you there!
Oceanwytch
