CT - hard to find trail maps

From: defygravity (defygravity@snet.net) | Date: 2002-09-03 20:14:22 UTC-04:00

Hi all, today I scanned and downloaded (see files link) a few trail maps which aren't necessarily always available on site.
If you like to have map-in-hand when leaving the house, or to plan out a route with more than one set of clues, here are maps for:
Canfield Woods/Meadow Woods in Essex & Deep River
Chatfield Hollow in Killingworth
Gillette Castle Park in Hadlyme
Timberland Trails in Guilford (not to be confused with Timberlin Park in Berlin)

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-usa/files/

They are all in .pdf (Adobe Acrobat) format. If you don't already have Acrobat Reader, it's FREE from the www.adobe.com website.

Sleeping Giant also has their map available in .pdf on their website
http://www.sgpa.org
