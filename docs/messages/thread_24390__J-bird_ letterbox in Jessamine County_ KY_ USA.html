"J-bird" Letterbox by tsjwheeler (aka the Wheeler Family!)

Heading south on Main Street (27 business route) in Nicholasville, turn
left onto Longview Drive across from Kroger. Follow Longview until it
turns into Park Drive which leads into the park.

Enter INTO the City/County park property. Immediately on the right
hand side you will see a sign "JCYSA" and some baseball/softball
fields. Turn RIGHT into the parking lot. The sign will be on your
right. See Cassity Park, turn right. At break in fence turn left onto
gravel road.

At the dead end, walk up hill on left. Walk carefully between
chainlink fence and scoreboard/wooden fence.

Corner of chainlink fence walk straight 25 paces to treeline. Find the
large tree with the hollow in it.


(Central KY, USA; Nicholasville, KY. Jessamine City/County Park)
