I'm posting notice of this mostly on the Southeast boards and list, but in
case there's anyone planning to be in the Fort Lauderdale area on February 5,
we're having in picnic/exchange/meet&greet that day.

Come join us for Florida's Fabulous Flamingo Fiesta, on February 5, 2005.
We'd love to have you come. If you can let me know at StDebb at aol.com,
that'd be great, but if not, and your plans change at the last minute, y'all come
anyway!

It'll be a Tree Tops Park in Davie, FL (which is basically southwest Fort
Lauderdale). There'll be boxes in the park--some are already there, some will
be planted for the event--which everyone will be free to go after on their own
schedule. We'll picnic (bring your own food, and a dish to share if you
wish), we'll exchange, we'll talk boxing. A good time will be had by all!

Hope to see you there!
