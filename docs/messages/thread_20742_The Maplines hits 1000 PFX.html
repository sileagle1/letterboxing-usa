The Maplines hits 1000 PFX

From: Mary & Paul The Map Lines (themaplines@snet.net) | Date: 2004-10-18 03:43:13 UTC-07:00
Hi All

We are sorry for the cross posting but we are so happy
we had to tell everyone. Paul ( Doing the HAPPY DANCE
) & Mary ( I little tired )have hit 1007 for our PFX.
Yes with all the Monster hunting and exchanges while
Monster hunting we have hit 1007 PFX.

First we want to thank MommaFox and Bald Eagle for
kidnapping us and forcing us to letterbox with them.
No we are only kidding. We love you guys and miss our
time together. Ever since that week-end on 8/29/2003
we have become addicted to this wonderful hobby. They
are really our best friends.

We would like to thank everyone that has exchanged
with us or planted a letterbox for us to find. Without
all of you this never would have happened.

THANK YOU !!!!!
THANK YOU !!!!!
THANK YOU !!!!!
