Title: Lion in Wait. Placed 8-30-03 by Salamander. Location:
Chillicothe, Ross County, Ohio. Difficulty: Easy. This letterbox is
located in Grandview Cemetery in Chillicothe, Ohio. CLUES: Enter
the cemetery via the entrance road off Belleview Ave. Shortly after
driving through the gate, you will take the first turn to the left.
This road will soon fork---you must turn onto the right fork. Upon
turning onto this right fork, if you look straight ahead up the
slope,you should be able to see a polished red granite globe, about
2 feet in diameter, atop a monument. Find a safe parking spot and
then walk to this monument. Standing at the monument's west
side,take a compass reading of 345 degrees. 46 paces at this heading
will take you to a lone tree. The lion awaits in a hollow space in
the base of the trunk.
