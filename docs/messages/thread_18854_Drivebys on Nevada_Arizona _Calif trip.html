When heading south on I-15 from Salt Lake to Las Vegas you go through a very small stretch of Arizona in the Virgin River gorge, just before Mesquite, Nevada. check out "Shotgun Shorty" a fairly short hike.

In Bullhead City, Arizona, on the Arizona part of 95 is a driveby"No Bull".


Amanda's "Snoopy's Brother Spike" resides in Needles on old route 66.


On your way back up north "Tule Elk" is just off I-5 near Bakersfield, Calif.


Just before Willows, Ca."Wild Goose" can be accessed even if the area is closed, and it is right off I-5.
