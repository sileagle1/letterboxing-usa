New Letterboxers!

From: (ddport@ucdavis.edu) | Date: 2000-07-20 21:33:44 UTC
Hi everybody out there in the letterboxing community!
I'm a student at the University of California at Davis.
My roommate recently came across the letterboxing website from
yahoo's outdoor sports listings and we found the idea interesting.
So we decided to give letterboxing a try. We created some stamps out
of erasers from OfficeMax, and we found the letterbox in Auburn. Our
first find, and it seems that we were the first to find it!

We also created our own letterbox and hid it on campus. Placing the
box and writting the clues was a lot fun, and we put a lot of time
into it. There will probably be more coming in the future.

Does anybody know if this is the fist letterbox to be on a University
campus? Also, are we the only college students participating in
letterboxing?

I will post the clues.

Thanks,
Derek Deavenport
