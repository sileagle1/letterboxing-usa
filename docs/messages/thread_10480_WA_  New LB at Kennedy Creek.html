'Scout' Letterbox
Placed by: dvn2r ckr, rnrB & trkr
Placed on: 15 January 2003

Salmon Trail
Kennedy Creek
1/2 way between Shelton & Olympia
Mason County
Washington

Directions: From Highway 101 in south Mason County, approximately
halfway between Olympia & Shelton, turn West at milepost 356 onto
Old Olympic Highway. Go 3/4 mile to the turn-off to a gravel road
signed "Kennedy Creek." Go 1/2 mile on the gravel road to the
Salmon Trail parking area. Remain vigilant as lumber trucks use this
road quite frequently. Start at the trail that heads North from the
parking area.

Clues: From the metal salmon, head West through the teepee. From
the Western edge of the teepee, take 35 steps. Head South for 10
steps. Scout is hiding out in a cubby hole, behind and within a
moss covered tree on the left.
