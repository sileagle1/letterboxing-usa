In Suffield Connecticut we placed a box to find but not a hike. It once lived a day at a winter gathering and  found many a friend there. But a gathering is no place to live so it crept along til it found a suitable place to stay forever.  From Gathering to Gathering you  should begin your quest by  going to the place in Suffield where the town comes together. We were there for a swim meet at the Academy, others come for church or to commemorate the past. When you find this place  where bands could play on a sunny day.
  Where speeches are made following a hallowed parade
  Where our flag flies in pride under a gold ball spied 
Here we chose to hide
Winter gathering Aside
Where all  the important people step
Look under to get!
 
 
Short and sweet can't be  beat! Hope you enjoy Winter Gathering at Suffield.
