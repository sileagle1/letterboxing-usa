From: Michelle Lee (misch@pobox.com) | Date: 2005-09-25 22:08:53 UTC-05:00
I would be interested in hearing about letterboxing and the Girl Scouts.
There was a great workshop on letterboxing, orienteering and geocaching at
my leaders weekend two weekends ago. I was inspired to find out more about
it! There are several letterboxing sites in my local area that I think my
Junior scouts would like to visit.

Perhaps if there are several Girl Scout leaders out there we can develop a
"councils own" badge. This is definitely a Junior level or beyond badge,
with making a stamp, finding a box, and hopefully making one of our own.

If you are Girl Scout troop leader, I'd love to hear from you about your
experiences with letterboxing with your troop!
