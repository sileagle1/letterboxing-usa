Camp Six
Pt Defiance Park
Tacoma, Washington
Placed December 2001
Easy and fairly level
Be discrete as this park can be busy.
Find some litter to take out with you!



Clues:

Find Camp Six.

From the patriotic stick the rifle will point your way.

10/20 paces and on your way at 260 degrees you go.

From under the trestle the path you seek is at 5 degrees.

When you see the skidder and go to its farthest corner.

Follow the trail at 330 degrees.

At the end to the trail go to tallest pole of three.

Look at the other two and go to the farthest one to the side you can't see.

Five paces more to a fern, I am on the side you can't see, reach in and
find me.

Continue down the gravel path to parking lot.
