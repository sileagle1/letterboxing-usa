From: Deb King (debean75@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-28 05:52:38 UTC-07:00
I played hooky from work on Friday to metro down to DC and see the Stinky Flower that was blooming at the Botanical Gardens. Unfortunately I was a day late and it was already starting to die. So I spent the rest of the afternoon looking for boxes.

The Robert Taft Memorial is missing, (well, not the memorial, but the box that is planted there. ;) ) It looks like the area has just been mulched, so the box probably was trashed.
strike one

Skull and Bones at the WWI memorial also seems to be missing as I think some other people have posted.
strike two

The Inventor seems to be MIA, unless I just can't read my compass.
strike three

but I'm not out yet, one more try on the way back to metro

Orion the Hunter is right where he should be! YAY The black marble at Einstein's feet needs to be cleaned, the stars are not very visible, but the micro box is right where it belongs. It will need a new log book very soon. I did not have any supplies or I would have changed it then.

I'll try for others next time I get down there.

Debean - MD
