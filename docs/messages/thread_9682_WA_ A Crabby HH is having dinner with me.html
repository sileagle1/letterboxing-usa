Carpe Dium. Around here that means if it stops raining run like heck
to a LB venue.

I made a return attack to Pt Defiance in Tacoma to tackle Trial and
Errors Gnome series. After a little consult with DVN2R SKR I decided
to use a marker ( pen on the back of my hand)to help with counting.
With so many things to see ( mushrooms are popping everywhere and the
birds were mighty happy to have a dry day) I keep loosing count.

It worked! I found the Crabby Hitchhiker still with Gwendolyn. She
still has her happy smile and is prim and neat in her skirt. All of
the Gnomes have neat tidy homes and all are snug and dry.

I love when Amanda brings us new HH's from the far flung places. It
is great to see new stamps for people and places you have only heard
of; the Psychokiwis, Chickabirdie, Chuck and Molly, The Haunted Trail
and of course Pete and Wanda.

Crabby HH came home with me. I am treating him to Dungeness Crab (
first of the season)Dinner before sending him on his way.
