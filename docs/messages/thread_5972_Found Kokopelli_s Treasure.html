Found Kokopelli's Treasure

From: Gary Watkins (scanogram@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-07-04 07:50:08 UTC-04:00
On Saturday June 2, My wife, daughter and I set out to find Kokopelli's Treasure.  It was a very enjoyable day.  We visited 3 beautiful parks, and had no trouble collecting the clues.  Spotted along the way was a nude sunbather.  Thankfully, He covered up befor we got too close.  We then made our way to Honeymoon Island.  We hiked the trail and found the last clue and were on to the box right away.  On the way back we enjoyed the view of the water and the calls of the osprey.  We saw a gopher tortoise and a baby armadillo.
