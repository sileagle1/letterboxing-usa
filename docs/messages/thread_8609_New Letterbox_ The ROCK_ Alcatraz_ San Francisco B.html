The residents came from many walks
Some were smart but all were caught.
One way in and two ways out.
One's alive the other's naught.
Over the days and years I'm told
Some grew weary some grew bold
But never the less they all grew old
Some were strong and some were ill
Some lived long and some were killed
Those that couldn't walk out the gate
Were lain to rest but not in state.
Visit this place of silent din
where mortals past are interred in.
Look to your left and see what may rise
They don't go to heaven in anyone's eyes
Look beneath, for that's where it lies
Make sure you're not seen by any spies
There are some boards there is no ledge
It's behind these that The ROCK is wedged
