From: lord_ladybug (Lord.Ladybug@adelphia.net) | Date: 2006-04-06 18:44:23 UTC
We did not find that Wanda and Pete had visited any of the boxes we
found in New Zealand. But we have found them everywhere we have
boxed in the US: all along the eastern coast, and in Arizona and New
Mexico. A couple of years ago, we sought out a box in Ft. Myers, FL
and discovered Wanda and Pete had stamped in to that box three days
before! And in Virginia, we had fun finding a box (placed for the
Northern VA Mystery Gathering) called, "Where's Wanda," which had as
its stamp a nicely carved image of the lady herself.

My favorite experience of finding a box that they had NOT visited
was in New Hampshire. About halfway into the logbook, the placer
stamped in when doing maintenance on the box. At the bottom of the
page was a P.S.:

"Pete and Wanda,where ARE you???"

We did run into Pete on the trail in Mansfield, CT a couple of years
ago. But we have not had the pleasure of meeting Wanda yet,
something we look for to!

Patti, the "Lady" of Lord & Ladybug
