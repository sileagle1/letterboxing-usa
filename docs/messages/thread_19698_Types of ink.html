Types of ink

From: Susan Johnson (susan@kuku.org) | Date: 2004-07-31 23:12:36 UTC
I know the pigment & dye based ink thread has been quiet for a while,
but I wanted to mention another option.

The brand is "Ancient Page" and is "acid free archival waterproof dye
inkpad". They come in lots of fun colors and, best of all, they come
in a mini size, 2-1/4 X 1-1/2 inches.

Also the lid snaps into the bottom of the inkpad -- one less thing to
drop in the woods.

If you can't find them in a store around you, you can get at
Clearsnap, www.clearsnap.com.

Anyone else use these and like these? We do!
