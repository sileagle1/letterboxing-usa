VA 5/31 Riverbend Gathering

From: (gbecket@aol.com) | Date: 2003-05-25 09:10:38 UTC-04:00
REMINDER - the Mid-Atlantic (and anywhere else anyone's willing to travel
from) gathering will be at Riverbend Park in Virginia on Saturday, May 31,
beginning approximately 10am. Meet at the picnic area near the boat dock, near the
visitor's center.

The website for the park is: http://www.co.fairfax.va.us/parks/nature.htm

Directions: Riverbend Park may be reached by taking Beltway Exit 44 which is
Georgetown Pike west to a right on Riverbend Road. From Riverbend Road turn
right on Jeffery Road and follow for approximately 1 mile to the park entrance.


Sam-I-am has done an event stamp and anyone else is encouraged to create
event stamps as well and as many other boxes as we all can. I am slipping out
this morning to go a'planting there....

Can't wait to see and meet and exchange and have fun with you all!

Mother of Crickets
