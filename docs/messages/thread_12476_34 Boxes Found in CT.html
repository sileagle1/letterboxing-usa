34 Boxes Found in CT

From: (Mae62202@AOL.com) | Date: 2003-04-30 10:55:23 UTC-04:00
Hello Everyone, My DD (Corrina) & I (The Engraver) spent 9 hours in
Mansfield area on Monday and found 34 boxes. The BEST part was that DD
arranged for my 200th box to be "Mother/Daughter." I Love You Corrina. She
got me hooked last May 8th and I wanted to try to hit the 200th box before my
1 year ann., WOOOO HOOOO I made it with time to spare. I was so excited that
I carved 6 stamps (1 for each of my grand children) and will be planting them
next Monday. Thank you everyone for your time & effort in Letterboxing, we
love you for them.
The Engraver
&
Corrina
PS We will be attending our 1st "Gathering" on the May 17th, See you then
and PLEASE be safe out there!
