From: trekkiegal1701d (kjnohr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-13 22:22:57 UTC
Hitchhikers are meant to be taken with you and planted in a different
box. When you do plant it in a different box, you should stamp the
HH stamp into the letterbox's logbook and the letterbox stamp into
the HH logbook.

You can only log unlisted boxes on Atlas Quest if you are a premium
member. Premium membership costs $30 per year right now, but will be
going up to $35 soon.

TG
