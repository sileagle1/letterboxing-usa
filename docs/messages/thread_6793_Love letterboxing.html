I'm new to this activity, and I love it. I've been reading the posts
(I receive the digest), and there have been lots of interesting stuff
over the past few days. It's too bad that some folks have been
insulted/hurt/offended by certain topics.

The many recent news articles on Lb have increased the number of
folks who want to join the Lb ranks, and there are bound to be some
growing pains. As a newbie, I will continue to focus on the positive
aspects of Lb, and I can weed out the rest as no-never-minds. It is
understandable that some of the veteran Lb'ers would feel upset at
some of the newer, odd ideas.

I hope that the kind webmasters and the Lb community will not stop
supporting this activity because of the complaints or rudeness of a
few. Overall, feedback is a good thing. My family and I hope to
enjoy letterboxing for many years to come.
