Abandoned boxes simply mean that the individual who planted the letterbox
has not yet "claimed" it on the revamped letterboxing.org web site. The
person could be an avid letterboxer who doesn't follow this list, but who
may maintain their own boxes quite diligently.

A better term to describe it would be "unclaimed," in fact. I wouldn't
necessarily avoid any box listed as "abandoned" on the web site.

Hi Ellen! Concerning the abandoned box, that simply means that the box has
not been picked up by the placer on the website. Many of the placers have
found out about the new site and have signed up, whereas others may not have, or
may not be boxing anymore. Don't let that discourage you from looking! We
have a famous abandoned box here in CT (Ray's Dilemma), which is still alive and
well, even though it is technically abandoned.

Don't let an "abandoned" box discourage you from looking! Go out and have
some fun!
