Hey Canada Goose...

Don't feel bad. I have yet to obtain this series, too. My mate and I
walked and searched high and low and came away with nothing. However, I
know they must be there because lots of local letterboxers have nabbed
this bunch of stamps. One of these days we will have to go back and try
for this series again. Ryan can be very clever at hiding his
letterboxes, so when you do find them, it is a source of real
accomplishment. Hang in there, go for some easier letterboxes to keep
your letterboxing mood bouyant then try for the Murder boxes some other
time.

It seems like, with many letterboxes around here, it takes me three
times till I'm successful. This was true of Der Mad Stamper's Mt. Tabor
and Multnomah Falls letterboxes. Tough to figure some of these things
out.

I try to make my letterboxes easy to find, so that people won't be
disappointed or won't have to think too much to obtain them. Sometimes
they come up missing, though, but most are usually still in tact, I
believe.
