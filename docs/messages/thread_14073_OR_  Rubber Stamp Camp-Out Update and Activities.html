OK, Folks! Following is an update on activities for the Rubber Stamp
Camp-Out this coming weekend.

Also...If you have letterboxes that you have planted at Milo McIver Park
prior to the gathering, please print off lots of copies of the clues
and bring them along. Thanks.

A. Rose has bestowed upon me some fine gathering items that will be
making an appearance!

First off, her roving event stamp that was debuted last year at the
August 2002 Pacific NW Gathering will join us. You may use this event
stamp to stamp into your logbook. The logbook for this stamp, however,
only allows the event stamp in it.

A little birdie told me we'll be having a gathering stamp from
SpringChick in Michigan gracing us with its presence. Once the event
stamp is stamped in, attendees may also stamp into its logbook.

The Hammerin' Hank hitchhiker is rumored to be set free in a letterbox
within the gathering vicinity so be on the lookout for him and his
mechanical bull this weekend.

And...Just when you thought she was jailed and it was safe to attend a
letterboxing gathering again you find it just ain't so. That's right,
folks...The infamous K-Martha has made her way West once again. Be sure
to get her personal stamp in your letterboxing journal this weekend!

Any other bright ideas, feel free to advise and we shall do our best to
accommodate!
