Ambiguity revealed

From: Iron`Bear Taleon (IronBear@greathallofar.org) | Date: 2003-10-22 07:59:00 UTC
Allow me to introduce my ambiguous-self, perhaps many of you have
heard of me, if perhaps you have read any of pandora's comments to
the board I may have been mentioned. Some of you may still believe me
to be an apparition of pandora's creative and astounding imagination.
Nonetheless I am here to set the record of that ambiguous tale
straight. I am for real, I do exist, and yes love it or hate it,
pandora and I are a pair. Some say made in heaven and others say
made in hell but you will have to decide that for yourself.
Furthermore my name is Iron`Bear for those of you who are not aware
of that trivial fact yet.

I have been compelled for sometime now to acquiesce myself to the
letterboxing group, and felt that now would be as good of a time as
any to become more involved vocally on the boards. I have been
involved in letterboxing with pandora for some time now, and it has
truly been a liberating experience when I have been able to take her
letterboxing in the Portland area. She tells me every detail in
regards to the conversations that occur on the boards, and I must say
that I am looking forward to getting to know many of you much better
in the projected future. A fair warning beforehand I do tend to
remain silent overall, and will more than likely say very little, but
I do read the vast majority of what comes across the board. However
when I am so inclined to comment it usually can be consider a
diatribe, but usually with a point, at least that is what it is an
attempt at anyways.

With all of that said I bid you all well.

Iron`Bear

Our life is a journey get out and hike it.
