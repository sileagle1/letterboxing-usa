Barton Creek Greenbelt Found!

From: Scott Hall (skoothor@mac.com) | Date: 2003-10-05 20:36:07 UTC-05:00
Alrighty! This was our first find! Long story, but we tried last
Saturday and couldn't find it. Of course, we ended up starting at the
wrong trailhead and hiked about 10 miles! But, it was fun anyway.

So, today, we tried again, starting from the correct trailhead, and
found it just as the directions stated! We stamped in and left with
our books containing their first stamps. Both our 6-year-old and our
4-year-old loved it and the weather was great.

The only note about the box, is that the logbook is basically full. We
were stamping the backs of pages today. It may need to be replaced
soon.

Looking forward to more finds in the coming weeks!
