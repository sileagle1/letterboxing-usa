Mansfield Centre General Store Letterbox
Rte 195 Mansfield Center , Tolland County , Ct.
Planted on June 10,2001
Planted By: Leader of the Pack, Cowboy, Wizkid, Fishboy & Little Chick


After several boxes in Mansfield you work up quite a thirst.

Remember to stop at a quaint little store

I wonder who will be first?

At the junction of 195 and 89 it stands

You can't miss it by the light

A place on historic Mansfield Lands

It's renovated clean and bright.

Friendly service and hometown news

A neighbor to greet allright

You stand in the door

There are choices galore

A snack and cold drink of your choice

Then you're reminded Letterbox ,by a little voice

And to your quest , you've added more.

Pass by the sodas, and even the juice

Frozens will do you no good

What you need you will find

if by ice cream you stood

Mountain Dairy milk ,on your right you should find

Then look up and you'll see

A box planted by me ( and trail friends)

Ask for help if you can't reach please.

In the store

There's a table by the door

You could stamp here

Or on the porch without fear.

Enjoy your seat

A rest for your feet

Then out to the trails once more.
