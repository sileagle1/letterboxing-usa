I spent the last 4 days and nights touring Texas boxes in the
Austin, San Antonio and Corpus Christi area. Here is a listing of
finds, placements, or problems. Thanks to all for your boxes!!!

1) Lost Loblolly - Found - Good shape
2) Trail Rider - Placed - Bastrop State Park - clues posted soon
3) 360 Bridge - Found - Good shape
4) Park and Hide - Found - Good shape, but hard to follow clues.
5) Mayfield Park - Found - Good shape
6) Gordian Knot - Could not park in area due to Tow signs now posted.
7) San Marcos Salamander - Found - Good shape
8) Bobcats - Placed - San Marcos - clues posted soon
9) Emily Ann - Found - Good shape
10) Madrone Tree - Found - Good shape
11) Dino Tracks - Placed - Natural Bridge Caverns - Clues posted soon
12) Paddle Pair - Placed - Guadelupe State Park - Clues posted soon.
13) Bookmarks the Spot - Found - Good shape, but weeds very high.
14) Friedrich Warbler - Found - Good shape
15) Texas Armadillo - Found - good shape
16) Raymond Russell - Found - Good shape, but took awhile to find
correct rock pile.
17) Peanuts - Placed - McAllister Park, San Antonio - Clues soon.
18) Cottonwood - Could not find. If near large statue, then beds
have been re-mulched and box is either buried or missing.
19) Hokey Pokey - Found - Good shape
20) Mexico Flag - Found - Good shape
21) Newbie's Flower Hitch Hiker - Found - good shape - moved on
22) Echinacea and Twin Dragonflies - Found both - good shape, but
hurrican caused bridge to be closed so must hike around.
23) Lost and Found Leaf - Found - good shape
24) Ragged Sun, Rest in Peace #1 and Watchful Birds - Found all 3 -
all in good shape.
25) Looping lassos series - Found all 5 - all in good shape
26) Hiker's Maze - Placed - Bob and Bessie Welder Park, Sinton -
Clues soon.
27) Pencil me in - Could not find. Found cement item, but ants had
built up around. I dug in ants, but finally gave up after much pain.
28) Music Man - Placed - Corpus Chisti SRA - clues soon.
29) Light Catcher - Placed - City of Corpus Christi Wildlife and
Bird Sanctuary (near Corpus Christie SRA) - Clues soon.
30) P-3 - Found - Good shape, but weeds very high and had to wade
through.
31) Super Frogger - Placed - Lighthouse trails park - Aransas Pass -
clues soon.
32) Texas Bonefish - Placed - Goose Island SP - Clues soon.
33) Big Tree - Found - Good shape
34) Mighty oaks from tiny acorns grow Hitch Hiker - Found - Good
shape and moved on
35) Rest in Peace #3 - Found - Good shape
36) Hurricane Claudette - Found - Good shape
37) Karankawa - Found - Good shape
38) Hog Lake Whooper - Found - good shape, but trail very wet and
grass very overgrown.
39) Mustang - Placed - Aransas Wildlife Refuge - Clues soon
40) Aransas Whooper - Found - Good shape
41) French Flag - Found - good shape
42) Go Texan - Placed - Lake Texana S.P. - Clues soon
43) Hogg Pecans - Varner Hogg SHP - Clues soon

I need a vacation
