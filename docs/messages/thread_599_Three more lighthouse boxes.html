Sturgeon Bay Shipping Canal Lighthouse:
Take the first right turn off the 42/57 bridge in
Sturgeon Bay and follow the pointing ships to the
Coast Guard Station. There is visitor parking, and
the public is able to access the north breakwater, the
rest of the station is off limits.

Clue:
If you wanted to get to the bright red lighthouse on
the end of the breakwater, you'd need to climb up on
the steel walkway, but since you can't do that (unless
you're USCG personnel) why don't you poke around in
the rocks near the base of the ladder and find the
letterbox.

Bonus stamp image of the breakwater light can be found
by following the clue enclosed in the main letterbox
above.
