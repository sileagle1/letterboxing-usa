in my Totem Pole Box, where you had not been, and I placed it, (as I
was allowed and encouraged to do by the instructions given,) in
another of my boxes that you had already visited. The next day,

additions to the Critters of Ennisbrook series.

Also, if you purchased any books during your afternoon visit to the
bookstore, you very likely purchased them from me.
