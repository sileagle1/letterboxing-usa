Withlacoochee Letter Box

Placed by: Chan Bliss and Troop 53 of Tampa Florida

Date placed: 2/15/03

The box is located in the Withlacoochee State Forest near
Inverness,Florida

This letterbox is on the Citrus Hiking Trail in the Withlacoochee
State Forest. You can find it at the northern intersection of the "B"
and "C" loops. Once you get there look due east and you will see a
crooked pine tree. The box is at the base of the tree. The round trip
walk to this box is between 6.5 to 12 miles. Be prepared to bring
water as there is no place in the forest to refill.
If you like the walk tell me 300dpi@gte.net
