Letterbox goes to school

From: Thom Cheney (tcgrafx@imagina.com) | Date: 2000-06-02 15:55:09 UTC-07:00
I just returned from an interview with our local Parks & Rec district.
They were advertising for someone to teach art classes at the Nature
Park Interp. Center. I took in samples of some of the "nature" type art
I would like to teach to adults and children (building bird and bat
houses, leaf prints, fish prints, etc).

I included some of my carved stamps, my letterbox journal, and a sample
box with letterboxing onformation. The director and I got talking about
letterboxing and how it relates to being outdoors and enjoying nature
along with the art aspect. She was very enthusiastic and so.......

starting this fall, I am teaching letterboxing!! woohoo!!!!!!
