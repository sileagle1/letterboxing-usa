Placed by Lenny/Kathy---THE NEEDEEPS placed 10/5/02
This is our first Letterbox. we chose the Frog Rock because when we
started letterboxing we bought a big stamp with two frogs on it.then we
came up with the name the needeeps so it only seems right that we make
frog rock our mascot.ok coming from the east on RT 44 from Pomfret the
parking area will be 0.4 of a mile from the Eastford town line sign on
the right. coming from te west on RT 44 from Ashford the parking area
will be 2.0 miles past the Eastford sign on the left. from west use the
second parking area you see.park your car and step over the guard rail
and walk down the paved path until you get to the giant frog rock.go
directly behind the frog and take a reading of 360" degrees due north to
a cement picnic table.from the table take 11 paces over a little log to
a evergreen tree.from that tree take a reading of 270" degrees to a
bigger evergreen tree which has six visible legs between the fourth and
fifth leg is your prize.just beyond that down the path a little more is
a small stream where all the little needeeps like to spend there time on
hot summer days.hope you enjoy the rock.Lenny/Kathy
