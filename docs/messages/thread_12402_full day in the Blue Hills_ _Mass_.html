full day in the Blue Hills! (Mass)

From: solor8008 (sjgallant@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-04-28 11:33:56 UTC
A glorious spring day in eastern MA yesterday led us on a meandering
romp through the Blue Hills. Though we're both from this state, it
was our first trip to this park and certainly not our last! We were
amazed at the lack of people on the trails but we're assuming that
it's only because it is so early in the season. Regardless..it was
great to have some peace and quiet while enjoying the views of the
surrounding areas and the Boston skyline.

We based our trip around TeamGreenDragon's Time Travels series and
picked up:
-Into the Future
-Time Traveller's Return
-Morlock Holes
-Fiendish Things

...in that order (and all in great condition). This is a great series
of stamps and it was fun to research the historical data beforehand.
It looks like we had the wrong date for "Weena" so we'll pick up that
one and the sixth box on our next trip. Along the way, we also
nabbed "Blue Hills Birthday" and "City Lights", both in fine
condition.

Looks like "Hancock Hill" is still missing as the clue suggests.
Thanks to all posters for a great day!
