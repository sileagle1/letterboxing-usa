"1 fish, 2 fish, red fish, blue fish", Mansfield ,Ct.

From: Carolyn Stearns (cstearns@neca.com) | Date: 2000-09-10 21:29:17 UTC-04:00
Mansfield Hollow State Park, Mansfield Ct.
 
Rating: Easy
 
Time: 1 1/2  hours
 
Sponsored by: Cock-A-Doodle-Moo 4-H Club
 
    Rte 195 to Bassetts Bridge road at Mansfield Center. The parking lot is where you begin. ( Not at the boat launch)  Follow the Paved road into the park this is easy walking stay with the road 20 minutes.  One small grade down a hill then along until you first glimpse the ball fields and Stop!  take the ski trail to the right. Past a tree with arms  outstretched and onward a couple minutes. Abandon the  ski trail for Nipmuck trail on your left. Folow blue dots until you pass a steep drop at the right edge of trail. Pass by here carefully, then look for a tree lightning has struck.You'll find your quarry behind it. Stamp in secretly , replace and go on. Folow the blue dots of the Nipmuck. Up and Down  through Pine woods deep ,all round about, to the beginning you meet.
