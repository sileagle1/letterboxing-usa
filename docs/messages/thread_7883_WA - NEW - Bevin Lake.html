Bevin Lake Safety Rest Area
Nearest Town: Packwood, WA
County: Lewis
Placed: 9-17-02
Placed By: Fudrick
Clues: Trivial
Trek: Easy
The Bevin Lake Safety Rest Area is in the perfect location for a
break when I go over White Pass. Now that I am in the habit of
looking for letterbox sites, I happend to find one. Although named
for Bevin Lake, the lake can not be seen anywhere around. The lake
is probably somewhere across the hignway up Bevin Lake Road.
To The Trailhead: As you drive along US 12, look for mile marker
126. You can't miss it, it is right across from the Bevin Lake
Safety Rest Area!
To The Letterbox: After you safely rest, look to the west to the Pet
Area. You will note that the entire area is fenced. At the far side
of the Pet Area, you will see the fence comes to a point, with a
grove of trees in the corner. Inside the grove of trees there is a
stump, under which your prize is hiding.

Before you set out read the waiver of responsibility and disclaimer.
