From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2002-10-29 16:34:58 UTC-08:00
Hey Folks!

Barring vehicle problems, John and I ARE planning to attend the South
Sound Letterboxing Fest on Saturday. The expected relatives flaked out
till a future time.

Question I have...Is there a shelter with a plug-in for a crockpot we'll
be using, or should I go to plan B for food options? Am hoping to bring
something hot and meaty (sorry...not Trish's great Mushroom Broccoli
Chowder this time around, just my potluck special, which I'm ashamed to
say is NOT vegetarian).

If there is no plug-in, I have some other cold food options for ideas,
but if the weather will be chilly up in Steilacoom like it is in
Portland this week, then I think a hot dish will be more appreciated.
Can someone enlighten me on the status of electricity at this park?
Nails Family? Ryan? I know you've all been there, so no pleading the
fifth. I just want to help make it a really yummy experience if I can.

Any chance Legerdemaine will grace us with those absolutely excellent
cookies again? If so, man, are we in for a treat! If not...well I was
lucky to bite them when I had the chance! Will K-Martha appear or will
she be arraigned in court about that time, unable to appear unless she
escapes wrapped in an orange jumpsuit. HA! HA!

I'm so happy that we get to attend!!! Unless my old bucket of bolts of
a truck can't make it up there, it is almost a certainty that we will
attend!!!! YIPPEE!!!

Best regards,

Amanda Briles
The Paisley Orca
