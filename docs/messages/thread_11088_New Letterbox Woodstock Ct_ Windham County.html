The Gone Fishing Letterbox

Placed: 3-8-03
Placed by: Teach & Preach
Location: Woodstock Ct. Windham County
Rated: Easy 5-10mins.

It's on the Woodstock/Putnam Town Line.. Go 171 west. Go left onto
Harrisville Rd. The road is the first left after a big sign that
says-"Welcome to Woodstock Home of the Woodstock Fair" At the Y go
right unto Peake Brook Rd. The first bridge you go over is were you
want to park. (Your best bet is to turn around at the Putnam Water
Supply Building or Lane Rd then you'll park just before the bridge
and your on the correct side of the road) Walk the path towards the
stream. Just over a small bump on the path; you'll see 4 black
chunks of tar. In between the 4 chunks and the path will be a good
size rock/boulder. Under the rock hidden by sticks is the Gone
Fishing Letterbox. Please rehide well people fish here, including me.
Thanks
Teach & Preach
