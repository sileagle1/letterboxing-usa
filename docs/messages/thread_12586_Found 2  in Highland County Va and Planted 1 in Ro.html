Just a note to let everyone now that I planted my first box today in
VA and it is on the web. I'm so excited to be on the web and that I
am finding boxes. The walk in Highland County was a bit wet but I had
an umbrella and my hubby had on a water proof jacket. I loved the
signs that he had written to his wife. He was so understanding and
helped me to find the box in excellent condition. Actually, I found
two because there was a bonus stamp in the container. I thought that
somehow it was accidentally left there but my hubby knew about the
moving boxes. I gues there is still alot I don't know. The moving box
was placed by the Hitchhiker so it is again on the move. I don't know
when or where I will replant it but hopefully it won't be too long.
I guess one of these days when we go out it won't be raining or wet
but it is kind of hard to turn around when you are already on the
road. I guess I'm kind of addicted.
