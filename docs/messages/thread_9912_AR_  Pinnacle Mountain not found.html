AR: Pinnacle Mountain not found

From: Celtic Relics (celticrelics@commandnet.net) | Date: 2002-12-29 21:25:00 UTC-06:00
We just got back from Christmas vacation and tried to hit a few boxes along
the way. We tried Pinnacle Mountain in Jasper, Arkansas on Dec 20th. It
was no where to be found. We spent about an hour and a half on top of the
mountain inspecting every clump of trees there was. For the next intrepid
soul who attempts this one, the East trail is very difficult and you will
need pretty good shoes for either one. We went up the East and decided to
go down the West side and walk around the mountain back to our car. I was
foolish enough to think State park, handicapped accessible and wore my
sandals which turned out to have very slippery bottoms. Huge mistake. My
knees hurt for a week from all the times I landed on them. The box owner
may want to go check on this one to see if I was just unlucky or if the box
is really gone.

The Stantons in KY
