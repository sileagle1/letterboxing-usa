CA Shore Bird Letterbox

From: Jane Reynolds (jr8276@cafecrochet.com) | Date: 2001-07-02 14:02:47 UTC-07:00
Hi All
Found Shore Bird Letterbox at the Bolsa Chica Ecological Reserve.
Perfect length walk for a hot CA day. Saw many birds, stingrays and sea snails. Beaches were packed but we felt we had the Reserve to our selves, so few people made the effort to walk beyond the bridge!
Thanks to Gwen & Don.
We saved the other Long Beach boxes for another day but did find the "Lions guarding the cul-de-sac" and drove past Signal Hill on our way to the 405! It adds to a journey when you know there's a letterbox still waiting to be found.

Jane
San Diego
