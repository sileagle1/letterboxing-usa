From: CompassPoints (ltrboxingrichters@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-09-03 02:49:11 UTC
We had a gathering last spring with about 30 or so letterboxers.

This year we discussed another, possibly with Okies and some from
Kansas in the norther part of the state, but at that time there were
only about 6 active boxers. My family ended up going to a gathering in
Texas which we enjoyed.

I've seen some other stamps in local logbooks but haven't really been
contacted by the boxers themselves, so I'm not sure how many boxers
are in the state.

You can check the Midwest yahoo group mentioned. We are attempting to
get some discussion started there.

Where are you boxing? Are you interested in hosting or helping with a
gathering? What time of year would you be interested in?

We really enjoyed the gathering hosted by Mandy last year. She worked
very hard and did a great job. I believe I found about 50 boxes that day.
