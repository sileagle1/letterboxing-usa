A Better Subject to talk about

From: ruhlette (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-11-26 19:13:24 UTC-08:00
For the past 3 days we've gone day-hiking and letterboxing while the
weather was unseasonably warm. Yesterday we had just finished up a series
of four letterboxes and a single letterbox off the same trail. As we
headed back to our car, I saw a family lingering near a spot where we were
earlier in the morning. I saw "Dad" with a piece of paper. My better
half did not want to stop, but I did, and I met a new letterboxing family.
They had just found their very first letterbox!!! I'm sure they were
surprised to find someone else out doing the very same thing.
