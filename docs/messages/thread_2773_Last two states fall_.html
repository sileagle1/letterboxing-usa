Last two states fall.

From: Todd Lane (tlane@princeton.edu) | Date: 2001-07-10 16:19:57 UTC-04:00
There are now letterboxes to be found in North and South Dakota.
Knowing that there would be a "gold rush" mentality about being the
first to find the NoDak box ;-) I have posted the clues on my website

http://members.tripod.com/lane.t/grid1.html

At the bottom of the clue page is a link leading to a USGS website
containing pictures and a description of the area around the box.
This box requires a short hike after what will likely be a long
drive. The last part of the road in is dirt but fairly well maintained.

This part of N.D. is actually rather beautiful. To the north is the
town of Medora (touristy) and the Theodore Roosevelt National
Park which we also recommend.


The clues for the S.D. letterbox will follow soon .

We have now placed boxes in 6 states, Probably not a record but I think
you will have to travel quite a distance to collect them all.

Good Hunting!

Todd and Pam
