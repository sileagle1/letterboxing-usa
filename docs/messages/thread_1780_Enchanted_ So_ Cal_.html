Hi, Yours was our first box so is very special to us.  We revisited it  this weekend  based on a note from Lone Wolf.  Nothing additional there, but we were the 5th visitors to the site so whatever he left is having fun somewhere else.  We were amazed upon driving to parking area, there must have been 20 cars and parking difficult yet we could see no one around.  On our way down the hill  we saw 40 folks hiking up.  We then had the area to ourselves and proceeded to enjoy the view and checked out the box again.

I'm so glad that my box was your first.  I should probably add to the clues that a second box is hidden and the clues are in the 1st box.  The trick is to very carefully inspect everything in the box and you'll find the clue for the second box.  I checked on both boxes a few months ago and was please to find that they were both still there.  The second box was found by a passerby and they wrote a nice message in the book and replaced the box.
 
As far as the popularity of the area... Yep that is a favorite hiking, biking, and riding area.
