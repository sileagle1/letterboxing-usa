times and have the Letterboxing gods smile on us. We hit our 100th
box with "The Big House"! A little cool by the lake for most of the
weekend, a little rain at the beginning, but very nice at the end.
We didn't make it to Grand Traverse, Grand Rapids, or Kalamazoo as
planned, but we had a great time and will be back again.

Wow! That is quite the letterboxing marathon, and some long days! I
was looking at a couple of those days, thinking I would have to push

park. I stopped there a few days ago and the box was safe and sound,
but I certainly see where that one could get you twisted around!

Congrats on your 100th find!
