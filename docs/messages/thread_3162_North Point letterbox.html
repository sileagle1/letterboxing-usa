The nearest geographic feature is North-South Lakes (North Lake Campground).
At North Point, stand with your back against the North Point sign pole.
*Face north, turn towards 10 o'clock, and walk toward the rock ledge. The
rock ledge is about 3 feet high. Small trees/bushes may be in your path.
Walk directly to the ledge where you will see a right angle 'cut out' (a
hole) in the ledge face. The letter box is in the hole under a flat rock.
*If the North Point sign pole is not present, stand in the approximate center
of the large bedrock terrace at North Point. Follow the directions starting
at the asterisk above although you will have to step up on the next level of
bedrock terrace.
