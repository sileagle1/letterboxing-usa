From: SpringChick (letterbox@attbi.com) | Date: 2002-12-06 03:29:01 UTC
I and a few others have been trying unsuccessfully to get in touch
with a letterboxer named Arbo, who planted a couple of boxes in MI
earlier this year, although I don't think he is from here.

The Muskegon Beach Box is still in place but has been missing its
stamp for a couple of months now. The Pines Point box is believed to
be missing.

The Muskegon Beach Box is in a great location at a nice park along
Lake Michigan. As it is only a couple of miles from my house, I
would be happy to adopt this box and replace the stamp, but don't
want to step on anyone's toes if the owner is still interested in
maintaining the box. If so, if you would like to send me a new
stamp, I can run out there and put it in the box.

Not sure what to do about Pines Point... it is kind of remote and I
hate to see people drive all the way out there only to find the box
missing, but it is my understanding that boxes are only marked
as "missing" on the clues site if the owner reports them missing.

Arbo, if you are reading this talk list, please contact me offline
about the Muskegon Beach Box. Thanks!

Deb (SpringChick)
