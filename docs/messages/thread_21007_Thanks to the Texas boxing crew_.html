Thanks to the Texas boxing crew!

From: dasprite222 (dasprite222@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-27 22:51:04 UTC

Took a few days to visit the Sprite's family just outside of Dallas
and was delighted to find boxing action in the most unlikely of
places. Upstate NY lends itself to boxing because of the woods and
other unspoiled areas, but we were impressed with how easily the
Celtic Lions, Ruby Tuesday and Cactus Licker were able to turn an
urban sprawl into prime boxing territory! We got to see a BEAUTIFUL
park with fountains behind the Mary Kay headquarters, saw an old
homestead not far from my family and other great locations in the
area. We even got two couples involved with us for the day who're
going to LOVE joining the North Texas boxing community.

Anyway, Thanks for Giving...boxes, that is! We loved it!
