There have been carvers who use the iron-on transfer method who have
been experiencing problems lately due to the reformulation of the
toners in newer cartridges. Apparently, some of the newer toners do
not transfer well if at all. Yet another sort of "treasure hunt"--
hunting for the laser copier with the right toner.

Inkjet printers do not make image that can be heat transferred, as
stated. Man, I wish they did...
