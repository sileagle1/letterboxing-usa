River Watch Letterbox in Fort Atkinson WI

From: Alison Dunkelberger (dunkproductions@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-07-15 18:53:24 UTC-07:00


You can find the River Watch Letterbox in Fort
Atkinson WI.
Find Lorman Bicentennial Park downtown. There is an
old railroad bridge across the
Rock River. Look for the box under the second
railroad tie tucked in the I beam.
This letterbox was placed July 15th 2002 by
Dunkproductions, et al.
This is our first letterbox. Let us know how it's
doing!
