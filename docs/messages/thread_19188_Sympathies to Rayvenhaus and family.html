As a humble Dartmoor Letterboxer and LBNA follower may I extend my
sincerest sympathies to the Rayvenhaus family.

Even though I am a stranger to almost everyone on this list and thousands
of miles away and have even been forced off the moor for some considerable
time, I have nevertheless enjoyed my Letterboxing thru reading the
postings on LBNA and have especially of course enjoyed following the
adventures of the famous Rayvenhaus family. So it is as if we come to

curious e-medium of ours.

If I may quote from the Queen of my small nation , " The pain and grief
that we feel when we lose a friend or relation, is the inevitable price we
must pay for the gift of love ".

Cherish that love always and celebrate the best of memories.

My prayers are with you.....I know that they really do work.

Sincerely
