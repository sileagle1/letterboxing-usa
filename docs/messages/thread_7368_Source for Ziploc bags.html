>Where did you get the Ziploc's? I could really use them and getting them

American Science & Surplus sells a 1 lb assortment, 7 sizes ranging from
18" x 13" to 3" x 2", for $3.50. They also have an assortment of 50 Ziploc
bags in assorted sizes & shapes for $2.50, and 100 Itty Bitty Ziplocks in
various sizes for $1.75 to $2.25. Shipping might be expensive for smaller
orders, but you can check out their site for info:

http://www.sciplus.com/category.cfm?subsection=7&category=82
http://www.sciplus.com/category.cfm?subsection=7&category=85

It's definitely a fun site to browse. I keep looking for
letterbox-appropriate watertight containers, but haven't found any that are
suitable for anything other than microletterboxes.
