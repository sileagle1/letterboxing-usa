From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-19 22:53:40 UTC
Seattle Walking Tour Box is downtown, but it takes a lot of walking!
The REI Flagship Letterbox and the Klondike Goldrush box are also
within walking distance of downtown Seattle.

These would be the only boxes you could get without a car in the
downtown area.

If you take the Elliott Bay Water Taxi
http://transit.metrokc.gov/tops/oto/water_taxi.html

to West Seattle, you can walk along Alki Beach and pick up

Mee-Kwa-Mooks Low Tide
Weatherwatch Park
Constellation Park Marine Reserve
Cormorant Cove Park
Birthplace of Seattle
and
Bread Batter Hat, Outlaw Tracy
AND The Schmitz Park Letterboxes! without too much trouble (however
it would be a long walk!)

You could email me off list and I could give you more detailed info.
The Water Taxi stops running Oct. 1st.

-Amanda from Seattle
