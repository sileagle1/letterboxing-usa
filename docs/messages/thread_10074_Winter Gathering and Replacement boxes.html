Hi,
I am looking forward to meeting a lot of you at the Winter
Gathering in DC. Until then I will be making replacement boxes for my missing boxes. I am hoping some of you will volunteer to take these boxes and replace them or parts of them. It would count as a find for you, a sure find at that.
I am trying to gather up a list of what is missing from all the past emails to me so hopefully I will have it covered. I have gotten better boxes, now that I know that the gladware ones won't last last for 2 years. Who knew at the time. So if you would like to replace one, come up to me at the gathering and we can talk. I am the one with gray hair (almost all white).
Even if you don't want to hid a box please introduce yourself,I am looking forward to meeting everyone and of course exchanging stamps.

May there be lots of nuts in your life,
Squirrel
