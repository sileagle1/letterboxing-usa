Pisgah Covered Bridge, in Randolph county, nearest city Asheboro.
Placed February 8, 2003 by Trailseeker, Hunter, and Lil Davey.

To reach the covered bridge follow signs from either highway 49 or
220.

Clue-
Walk through the Pisgah Covered Bridge, continue along the trail (a
very short trail. Pass over a creek on an arched bridge. From the
end of this bridge count 49 paces (good size steps)and pause. At
245 degrees a WSW direction stands an old snagged tree on the bank
of the creek. In it's base you will find our LB.


Happy trails w/ many boxes
