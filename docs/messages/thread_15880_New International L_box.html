Samasati letterbox

Samasati Nature Retreat
Costa Rica
Placed sometime in winter of 2002
by Bill Bullock for franzsolo ...
(motofranz@webtv.net)
Difficulty: easy

Samasati Nature Retreat is located 130 miles South of San Jose ,the
capital of Costa Rica, and 30 miles South of Puerto Limon on the
Caribbean coast.

You will cross the Braulio Carrillo National Park,the Santa Clara flat
lands .Then the road unwind along the beach.
Right after Cahuita National Park ,four miles before the village of
Puerto Viejo,you will find a large sign
"Samasati Nature Retreat"

Letterbox clues:
At the top of the steps,along the path leading to the Group Meditation
Hall ,there is a stump of a large winged base tree. The box is hidden
behind a rock in the crease of two wings,on the (road side) of the
stump.
This winged base stump is on the left side of the road leading to the
"Group Meditation Hall " at the top of the hill just before the hall.

Please email me if you find it
(motofranz@webtv.net)
