Finally back from our 2-day turned 13-day trip to CA. We managed to
snag some more letterboxes along the way (up & down I-5).

Question--"why is it that I seem to attract poison oak like Amanda
from Seattle attracts hitchhikers???." Ugh!!! I'm sick of fighting
this stuff.


Anyway, here goes for the box updates from yesterday & today:

Covered Bridge LB Series #2 & #3--alive & well (ran out of time for
#1)
Horsetrap Trail (Grants Pass, OR)---alive & well
Schroeder Park (Grants Pass, OR)--alive & well
Cedar Hills Twins (Beaverton, OR)--#1 is alive & well, we fizzled
out on #2
Old Growth LB (Portland, OR)--we bombed out on it
Willard S Stump, Willard B Stump (Portland, OR)--both alive & well
(Willard S was our 300th find!!!)
What's For Dinner Series (Portland, OR)--alive & well
Inuit Sun (Portland, OR)--we bombed out on--not sure if it's there
or not as recent trail maintenance had occurred (obvious pile of
pulled ivy & blackberry brambles in the vicinity)

Another great letterboxing trek throughout the NW! What a gorgeous
time of the year to be boxing, too. We had nothing but blue skies &
a whole bunch of sun on our trip! We even wore shorts along the way-
-sure missed all the snow out East... ;) Maybe next year...

Thanks, folks, for several more excellent letterboxes around these
parts! See you on the trails...
