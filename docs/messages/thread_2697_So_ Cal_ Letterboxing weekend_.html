So. Cal. Letterboxing weekend!

From: (Nuthatch@PaperDance.com) | Date: 2001-07-04 02:42:50 UTC
We hit 3 letterboxes this last weekend, 2 in Long Beach and 1 near
San Diego.

The first was El Dorado on our way down -- what a pleasant little
respite from the urban surroundings (and the relentless LA traffic!).
Saw lots of turtles as well as a green backed heron, among others.

We hit Painted Rocks on Sunday early afternoon on our return home. A
word of warning to those planning on going to this box -- there is
poison oak all over the place. It grows right into the trail where
the trail passes behind the houses, as well as around the letterbox
location! After returning the box we washed up just to make sure. All
is well.

Next on our return home was Shore Bird in Long Beach. We hit this one
at sunset for a delightful time. It was a little cooler out and very
pleasant. (Don & Gwen, the book for this one is just about half
filled up, so you know!).

Karen (Nuthatch) & Rich (Silver Raven) Saul
Team Saulsa
