We placed our first letterbox yesterday! It's the Lakeside Eagle at the
Lakeside Nature Center in Swope Park. It's on an easy trail, 1/3 mile long
and a pretty cool nature center with a Bald eagle, a coyote and a bunch of
snakes and raptors to check out. The new Nature Center building is also
quite an architectural sight as well. It's about a half mile from the Zoo.

I also carved the stamp for one at Loose Park coming soon!
