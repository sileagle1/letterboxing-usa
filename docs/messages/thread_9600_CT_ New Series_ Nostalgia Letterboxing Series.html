Nostalgia Letterboxing Series
Meshomasic State Forest

Town: Glastonbury
County: Hartford
No. of Boxes: 3
Difficulty: Moderate due to the terrain.
Planted in: November 2002 by Pam

Directions from North (Rtes 84, 91, 2):
Take Route 2 to exit 10. At end of ramp, turn right. At next stop sign, turn
left onto New London Tpke. Bear right at the fork onto Wassuc Road. At next
stop sign, turn right onto Country Club Road. When you come to the next
major intersection (you do not have a stop sign, but the other three streets
do) turn left onto Mott Hill Road. Take a left onto Dickenson Road.
Dickenson Road will turn to dirt. You can park on the left hand side in a
little parking area just before the road turns to dirt (there is some kind of
business just after the parking area on the left). When it turns to dirt,
you will notice that you are on the white trail.
