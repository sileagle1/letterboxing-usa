My Grandson Graceson [the right way to spell it] carved his first
stamp as his signature stamp this morning and off we went as Music Woman
reported, to "A Drive in the Park" series on West Rock. He is on his
way to Brazil on a school project so we only have a short six day visit.

a nice touch to Bluebirds ,Music Woman's and my butterfly stamps. There

the boxes, you will know that we did not have much time to write
either.!! It is a well planned series, covering the great trails of
West Rock all the way down to the lake. The stamps are beautiful in
every way. You really must try this out.!! I agree with Music Woman
that we think it is about 10 miles [ only kidding] But some great
climbs and views.
thanks Sadie and Russ.
