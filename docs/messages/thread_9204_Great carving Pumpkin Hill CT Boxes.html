Great carving Pumpkin Hill CT Boxes

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-11-16 11:32:28 UTC-05:00
Yesterday we went to Christmas Around the World boxes at Pumpkin Hill, Chaplin CT. I think the 1 1/2 hours that is the estimated time to do the 6 boxes is actually even a bit less. Irish Tinker and her little elf Rosie Bunny planted some nice boxes with great carvings. I was impressed. with the stamps and there were several nice views. Molly enjoyed running around and going in the water.  The tree on box #1 is very unusual. I like that kind of stuff. It really stands out. While there we were also able to do an exchange with Pixie Pan, The Egg Man, and Peter Rabbit. Lil Pup was there also, being carried. Lil Pup dropped his stamp out there. If  anyone finds it, please post it on LBNA for them. Had a nice time there. Thank you Irish Tinker and Rosie Bunny for planting them....Chuck, Molly, Chickabirdie
