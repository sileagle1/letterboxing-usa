More folks reaching high PFX counts

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-02 20:31:03 UTC

Just wanted to note a few other folks we've heard from recently hitting their
new PFX milestones: Lightnin' Bug of PA reached PFX500 on June 19 and
has been quite busy lately planting boxes and solving mapsurfer mysteries,
Bird Stamper of CT reached F500 on July 16 and tells us he is working on
some new plants; Patrick and Amy of CT reached PFX1000 on May 2 and
that must have been quite a challenge with their new baby, Thomas, who just
has his first birthday a couple of weeks ago! (Imagine what kind of PFX count
Thomas could have, starting out so young, if he keeps it up - and we've
already seen him out letterboxing, too!) Then, Wisconsin Hiker and Martini
Man reported having already reached PFX1000 on July 4 with letterboxing
trips planned for this summer from the east coast to the west coast and in
between! Finally, we heard that that quiet, but extremely active CT couple,
Sadie and Russ, reached F2500 way back in January and have most likely
already passed F3000 by now! We hope those good folks and many others
keep up their high levels of enjoyment and achievement in this fun little hobby
that is so much benefited by their hard work and dedication!

Cheers,

Wanda and Pete
