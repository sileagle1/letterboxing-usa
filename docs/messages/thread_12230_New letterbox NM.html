0n April 20, 2003 Funhog placed El Corredor Letterbox near Cochiti Pueblo, Sandoval County, New Mexico. The
2.6 mile roundtrip hike to this letterbox is hot and dry, through amazing scenery. Don't forget sunscreen and
water. The compass heading is magnetic.

To the trailhead: from I-25 between Albuquerque and Santa Fe take either exit 264 or 259 to Cochiti Pueblo.
