From: connfederate (connfederate@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-31 01:38:59 UTC
David:

I would "google" these two terms for comparative photos: "em rash"
and "poison ivy rash". Click on the top link for each search; great
pix. :p
If what you have is not poison ivy, then it may be Erythema Migrans
(EM) which is indicative of Lyme Disease (LD). Note Well: EM
doesn't always show up as a nice, neat, classic "bullseye rash", it
can resemble poison ivy. And it may not be at the bite site IF it
even shows up, some folx with LD don't get the rash. Don't mess with
LD it can be some serious shi-ite. A simple blood test can detect LD
but the Western Blot is more reliable.

Connfederate
(living in Connecticut, ground-zero for LD...)
