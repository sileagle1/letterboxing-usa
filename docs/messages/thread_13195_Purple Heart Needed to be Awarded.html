I was wondering if this was the same injury we were told about being on the

so sorry to hear that it was (well, I guess I'm sorry it was anyone, not
just letterboxers). I'm glad that it sounds as if Pandora will be okay.
Best wishes for a speedy recovery. You'll be in our thoughts, and lets all
remember to think of the safety of our locations when we are making plants.

I checked out the page for the purple heart. Chuck's story made me smile.
Only a letterboxer would fall about 25 feet down a cliff, have to go to a
hospital, then insist on their party looking for the box still before they
even get off the mountainside. I mean, Psychomommy's story always made me
giggle (you should have included the part about wearing the jacket home and
getting stopped in the story-it's okay to laugh when it's over isn't it?),
but this one really hit the mark.

With all the recent talk over it being a "sickness," I think you just took
the cake. You fell off a cliff, climbed out yourself, still looked for a
bit for the box, talked to all the hospital personnell about boxing and
spread the word, planned on going back for that same box for your next one,
and then last, but not least, used the whole nightmare experience as an
excuse for a new stamp! Yeah, I think you've got it bad. If there were
another award. I nominate Chuck and Molly for it. Somehow the "most sick"
of us all, just doesn't have the right ring. I'm glad you weren't seriously
hurt, and can be here to help keep us smiling.

Cadenza
