First carved stamps - file posted

From: enders_angel_80 (enders_angel_80@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-02 11:35:44 UTC
I spent the night at my Moms (Bindle Babe) while the guys were out
of town and we stayed up all night fooling around with letterboxng
stuff.

I finally got up the courage to carve my first stamps. I used the
pink stuff and an exacto.

I'm giving them to Mom to take when she goes on her trip to Portland
in a couple of weeks. The Film Fest is going on while she'll be
there so the stamps are themed for that.

I made a folder in the files area and uploaded them. Here's the link
if you want to see them. http://tinyurl.com/36xrw

I posted them for two reasons. (1) to show all those who sent me
encouraging email when I was nervous about trying it myself and (2)
to show anyone else who is new to this that being WORRIED about
carving is way worse than actually doing it.

Enders Angel
