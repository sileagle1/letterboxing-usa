From: Lisa (limurme@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-11 18:32:03 UTC

I am pregnant, so I can be excused for having crazy dreams....but
lately I have been having some funny ones concerning letterboxing. One
dreams involves a mysterious stranger calling me on the phone and
telling me to go search the Acadia National Park Visitor's Center. I
do and there, along the steps leading to the building, I spy a small
pile of stones and sticks. I wipe them away and find the box! I never
open it up to see the stamp or logbook (I don't know if I have reached
that level of obsession yet...it will come soon I am sure!)

My question is: do I add this to my F count? Maybe I should have a
logbook in my dream and have subconscious dream stats too!

Cheers,

Limur

This is very timely! I am not pregnant so I cannot be excused for my crazy letterboxing
dream earlier this week. "I hopped out of my car when I spied a letterbox. As I approached
the hiding spot, I stopped and could not go any further. There was someone in the
distance rummaging through the reeds getting something. I said, 'drats, I can't go for this
one, because he will see me and come and take away the letterbox.' The urge to pull the
box was soo strong, but I didn't want to commit the letterboxing no-no of being found
out. I resigned myself to the fact that I could not have this box at the cost of losing it
forever to other visitors."

Now, if that isn't the most vivid dream I've had AND remembered the next day ... I am
already going crazy for warmer and drier weather. Hello, my name is speedsquare. I have
been letterboxing for 8 months and I have a problem.
