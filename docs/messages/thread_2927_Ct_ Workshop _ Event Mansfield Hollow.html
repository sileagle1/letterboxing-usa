The Friends of Mansfield Hollow Group has asked me to do a
letterboxing introductory workshop . It will be held Tues July 31 at
7 p.m. in the Mansfield Hollow State Park Picnic grove off Bassttes
Bridge Rd. Mansfield Center. ( off Rte 195) The rain date is Aug 3,
7 p.m. This is a workshop on how to get started , BUT if you want to
come we would love to have you. It would be a good time to boost a X

it will be available in original form only at the workshop/ event.

water), Kayaking Box(water), Grumpy, Opening Day, Friends of
Mansfield Hollow, Troutfishing in America, Watermelon Sugar, One FIsh
Two Fish Red Fish..., Paddle to the Sea, You Otter See This,and the
new event box Sunset Trails ( forthcoming clues). Hope to See some of
you there! Leader of the Pack
