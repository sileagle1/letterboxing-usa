about badge orders - to everyone

From: (mohmers@aol.com) | Date: 2006-07-07 08:10:44 UTC-04:00
Just a note to everyone.

I want to remind or let you know that our badge orders are filled by a very
considerate non letterboxer who does this for us on the side.

She charges very little for her time (compared to other embroidery shops) and
does great work.

Just like the rest of us, she makes an occasional mistake.

Please show her what a great bunch of people we Letterboxers are and be
patient in the face of one of those mistakes. If you are kind and polite, I'm sure
she will make it right - even if you are rude and nasty, I'm sure she'll make
it right.

:oP

We actually lost our first embroiderer because of two very mean spirited LBNA
patch orderers. I'm sure they weren't actually letterboxers just grandparents
ordering a patch for their letterboxing grandkids. :o)

ANYway - follow the Golden Rule when ordering badges please.

If you've never ordered a badge before there is a link on the LBNA main
page.

THANKS & HAPPY HUNTING,

Mohmers
