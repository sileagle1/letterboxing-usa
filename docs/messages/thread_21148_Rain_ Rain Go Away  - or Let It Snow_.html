From: grumpygrinchy (ffuselier@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-12-28 22:01:11 UTC

I am finally recovering from my cold and am eager to get out and
box! The rain has put a definite damper in that activity - so I
console myself by carving instead. :-)

Grumpy and I are about to embark on another cross-country road trip
with lots of letterboxing thrown in for good measure. Crossing I-10
to Florida then up to New Hampshire where we will linger for
awhile. It should be interesting to search for boxes in the snow.
I can't believe that we lived there for over 11 years and didn't
know about letterboxing. So many boxes that might have been found...

We will maintain our contact through this group and look forward to
the late March (or early April) gathering. In the meantime, happy
letterboxing New Year to all of you.

The Grinch half of Grumpy Grinch
