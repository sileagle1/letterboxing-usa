IN - Letterboxing Day

From: barbw427 (barbw427@insightbb.com) | Date: 2003-10-12 00:33:04 UTC
We experienced our first real letterboxing adventure today. Since
boxes are few and far between in central Indiana, we decided to
venture down to south east Indiana to hunt down a few of ScoutDog's
and Franzola's boxes. We found all 7 of the ones that we looked
for! Below is a box by box report:

Tow Path Park: Good condition!
Old Metamora Mill: The log book is quite wet.
Old Post Office: It took us awhile to hunt this one down, but we
finally found it and it is in good condition.
Hilltop Overlook: Good condition!
snakes ... : Good condition and we were first finders!
Historic Little Cedar Grove Baptist Church: Good condition!
Cedar Grove Church: Good condition!

That brings our P count to a whopping 9. Too bad we missed the turn
for the Waterfall box or we could have made it a nice round 10.

We are looking forward to our big trip in 2 weeks to John Bryan
State Park. 30 boxes in one park is overwhelming! That is more
than are in the entire state of Indiana!

Barb of The Weaver Family (Weaver Mom)
