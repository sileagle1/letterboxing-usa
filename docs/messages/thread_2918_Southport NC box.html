Ok this is my first box I hope that some of you find yourselfinthearea and find it.Let's Visit Southport NCTake a nice drive downhowe Street tward the AtlanticOcean.Beforemaking it to the Waterstop at the light at theintersection and takea left on to MooreSt.Take some time ans seewhat's in the antiqueshops.When you havefound some good bargins head back down Moore streetwiththe ocean toyour right.You'll arrive at the main entrance of Old Smithville. Thefirst homeis dated back to 1804 however earlier onesare assumed.Thisis a peacful community so lets be kind and respectfuland not"Step onannyone toes". Walk tward the Fake tree trunk. Before you reach ityou'll pass thehome sweet homeof "PETER SCALES". He's an old man soif he doesn'tsayhello don't be hurt. Ater passing his home take aleft with the oceanto your back.Please be quite passing "MARYAMANDA" home. She is ussally restingquietly. Isn't her cradle sonaturaly lovely? Now lets go on.Take a moment to visit the "BROWN"family. "CAPT BROWN" is veryproudof haveing the tallest iron fencein the nieghborhood.From the frount door of the Browns continue onwith your back stilltothe ocean toward the large oak tree in thecenter of the nieghborhood. Pass between the homes of "JAMES ANDLAURA CHURCH" and"JOSEPHSKERRITT" Now remember this is a quiteneighborhood lets respect thierhomes. "KEEP OFF" signs are notalowed just commonknowledge.Contiue on past "MOTHER ELLA GILBERT"Tward the gate opeing ofthecommunity at the en of Old Smithville.Where "MELVIN AND MARYLEWIS" home lies. And there you will find the letterbox in thierlovely foilage.Please becraful and notdistrubthem.Ihope you ahve a good time looking for this letterbox. Ihad agoodtime planting it.Happy hunting and Lovely DaysMysticPrincess
