First 2 days of letterboxing

From: Julie (oceanwytch@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-30 15:01:43 UTC
Gryphon and I just spent our first two days letterboxing! We went
out on Sunday and found: the OM box, Trolley Days, Fort Allen Park
Letterbox, ME Valentine, and Canine Letterboxes #1 and 2. We
attempted to find Casco Bay Letterbox and Starry Night, but were
unsuccessful. I think it was because we were getting tired. We had
such a good time that we planned to do it again this week.

We went out again yesterday and found: The Key to the City, Starry
Night, Munjoy Hill Observatory, Pizza Pie, and much to our surprize,
Casco Bay! By the time we found Casco Bay, it was getting really
late so we had to call it a night, but we are going out again this
weekend! This hobby sure is addicting!

~Julie~
