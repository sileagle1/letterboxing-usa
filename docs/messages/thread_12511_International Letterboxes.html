From: Gwen and Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-07-03 16:26:04 UTC-07:00
The link to International letterboxes on the map page seems to be broken. We recently posted 3 letterboxes in Sweden and used "Sweden" in the titles. This allows them to be pulled up by going to LBNA Search for boxes and typing in "Sweden" in the Word/phrase in box name. Google has them listed also, but that link goes to What's New, but hopefully that may change. If you are planning on planting a letterbox out of the country you might want to use the country's name in the title.

Don
