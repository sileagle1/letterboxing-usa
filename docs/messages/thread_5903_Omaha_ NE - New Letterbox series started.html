Omaha, NE - New Letterbox series started

From: Erich Sawyer (riposte9@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-06-27 21:38:34 UTC-05:00

A new letterbox series was started yesterday. The first box in the "Ghost Town Series" was planted in Sarpy County, Nebraska. This box could easily be considered a 'drive by' box.

The "Ghost Town Series" is a series of boxes planted on or by the locations of early settlements in Sarpy County that may or may not still be there. Have Fun. Each boxes clues will be preceded by a short history describing the settlement and what mat have led to its demise. the clues can be found at  http://letterboxnebraska.8m.com/clues.htm
