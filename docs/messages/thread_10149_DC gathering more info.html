At the request of a few people, I am posting this list of letterboxes that are in or nearby to Columbia.  That way, you all can go boxing after the gathering!  Please feel free to contact me off list if you need any help.  Please print up the clues and bring them with you to the gathering.  I can probably help you get from Clyde's to the various boxes, but once there, you are on your own!  Also, if I have missed any boxes - please let me know!

Right now the head count is 37!  Should be a lot of fun, looking forward to meeting everyone!
