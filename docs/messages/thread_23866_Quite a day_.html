Quite a day!

From: baliddle (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-10-14 21:22:08 UTC-04:00
WOW! We went on a marathon tour today. Didn't really intend for it
to be this long of a day! Very eventful.

We started at this one bizarre park with all these concrete bridges,
benches and sculptures that looked like they were made from
trees. There were 7 boxes in that park, with one confirmed missing
and one we didn't find/possibly missing... so we found a total of 5
there... plus a hitchhiker!

Then we headed to another park, but couldn't find the box. Not sure
if we looked in the right place, but the last seeker who posted on
lbna couldn't find it either, so, it may be missing.

Then we headed off to a huge plant conservatory/garden that was the
most gorgeous place! Will have to go back to spend more time.

After this, we headed to three other parks and found three more
boxes. For two of them, we were the first finders! We also found an
unexpected geocache box!

So total stats:
9 letter boxes found
2 first finds!
1 hitchhiker!
2 unfound boxes
1 geocache box found
2 sore feet
1 sore butt
1 sopping wet muddy foot
and 1 fabulous day spent outside!



BNL Bali Grl
