The majority of the WNY gathering boxes are still down (and
permanent), although changes have been made in order to return boxes
to owners. There's about 2 less boxes at Royalton Ravine, but that
just shortens the trip a little instead of throwing off the whole

to check under the owner's logbook instead of ours.
