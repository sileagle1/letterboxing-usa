Pacific Northwest letterboxing update

From: Amanda Arkebauer (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-03 17:33:59 UTC
Hey there,
Princess Lea and I just got back from Vancouver, Canada where we were
able to find some great letterboxes! The Aldergrove Lake Regional
Park series of 3 were alive and well. Cute little microboxes. We also
got the Gardener's Box in Abbotsford, BC, but the Mill Lake box
eluded us--could be missing. And at Derby Reach, we got the
Explorers, Fort to Fort and the Gillnetter, before we had to leave
(as it was getting dark)--We searched in vain for the Wood Box and
believe it to be missing.

On the drive, we picked up the Padden Puppy Trove which is a
letterbox/geocache hybrid (very well executed with an
entire "letterbox" within the geocache so folks cannot mistake it for
just another toy to exchange)

The Starfish and the Clam at Larrabee State park were alive and well
and we picked up the Tacobet Mystery box too!

--Amanda from Seattle
