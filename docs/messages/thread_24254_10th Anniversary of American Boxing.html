10th Anniversary of American Boxing

From: Mountain Scorpia (mountainscorpia@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-25 17:33:26 UTC-07:00
Location plans are now solidified for the B.A.B.E. (Birth of American Boxing Event) to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the seeding of the Max Patch letterbox on April 26, 1998.

Event Headquarters:
Hot Springs Spa and Campground
315 Bridge Street
Hot Springs, NC 28743
Reservations: 828.622.7267 or 828.622.7698. On weekends, these numbers are answered longer into the evening.

VISA, MasterCard, and Discover accepted.

Cabin prices per night including tax:
No. 1 up to 8 people; no bath $67.20
Nos. 2-4 up to 5 people; no bath $50.40
Nos. 5-8 up to 5 people; 1/2 bath $72.80

Tent sites per night:
Deluxe on river 15 amp & water $40
Primitive on river $30
Primitive regular (not on river) $20

RV sites per nite:
On river, 30 amp full hookup $40
Not on river, 30 amp full hookup $30
RV pull-through sites 30 & 50 amp, $35

SPA RATES
Hot Tubs (these are natural hot-spring fed!)
2 people before 6pm $25/hour
2 people after 6pm $30/hour
Massages range from 1/2 to 1 1/2 hours, rates vary depending on type of massage - Swedish, hot stones, etc.

There is a nice primitive USFS campground called Rocky Bluff about 3 miles away, which is smaller, cheaper, and quieter, but with a shared restroom. Don't recall they have showers, but they might. First come, first serve.

This and other info will be posted also on Atlas Quest.

If you want to be on my email distribution list for this event, please email me at mountainscorpia@yahoo.com off-list and put BABE in the subject line.

Lucy
