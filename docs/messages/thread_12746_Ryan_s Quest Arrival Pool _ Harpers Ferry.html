The Triple V Gang met Ryan and Amanda in Waynesboro today!!! Ryan was quite surprised as he knew nothing of the plan that Amanda and I had concocted. We went into Afton Mt. information center to ask where the trail actually was as we saw no signs, and saw 2 thru hikers. I offered them treats from our cooler in exchange for them showing us the way to the trail so we could be trail angels until Ryan and Amanda showed up, (we thought we were early!) and then I happened to look down at the register and "Green Turtle" had signed in. I was excitely telling Tony that he was already there and the guy that we had been talking too raised his hand and said "I'm Green Turtle". He had no idea that we were coming so he was struck speechless when he realized that we were there looking for HIM!

We took him back out to our truck and fed him Hawiian Punch and bananas and twinkies until Amanda arrived. We all sat and talked for awhile and then headed out in search of lunch. When last we saw them, they were headed to a hotel so that Ryan could get a hot shower and then off to the library to get online and update us all.

Sarah aka V-Mama of the Triple V Gang
