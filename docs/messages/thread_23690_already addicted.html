I just have to say that yesterday was my first day of letterboxing,
yes, I was a virgin to the whole thing. I am already completely
addicted. I just did the easy ones with my two kids, they are 4 and
2. I have to say my 2 year old kept up amazingly well. My four year
old thought we were treasure hunting.
I ended up talking about it a lot at work today, apparently too much.
My co-workers all think I may have gone a little looney. One actually
told me I really have to start dating again.
The whole point of this was that I am totally addicted and am even
trying to figure out times I can get my mother to watch my kids and
just go off ny myself for the harder ones. What fun! Thanks to all
the planters out there! Adria
