Don't worry, Wanda--when I started this hobby a little over a year
ago, there weren't even 100 boxes in my state!!! We had to travel
to several other states to reach 100. Now, in just over a year

you place them, they will come' has certainly come true in this
area. Don't worry, give it time, plant some boxes here, there &
everywhere & you will eventually reap the benefits of other local
letterboxers/letterboxes in your region, too.
