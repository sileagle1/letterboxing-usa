Mansfield Hollow Event

From: bubbatroysondae (fourdesign@snet.net) | Date: 2003-06-22 02:47:31 UTC
It was great fun to meet for the first time many of the letterboxers
in this area, and to finally put a face to the stamp. Exchanges
were loads of fun and of course I nearly filled my book with new
hitchhikers, exchanges, and Mansfield stamps. It was also a
blast to hike with such a large group of people with such orderly
stamping skills -lol! We had a system going by the 2nd stamp!
Thanks to Leader of the Pack for organizing and to all those who
hiked the loop-PineTree, FjordhorseLynn, Metoui's Chicken,
Painterly and 46R, Needeeps, Hands and Feets from VA,
Preach, The Columbian Bugs, Emma the Old Bird, Cat Lover
and hopefully I haven't forgotten anyone. It was great meeting you
all and hope to see ya soon on the trails.
BubbaTroySondae
