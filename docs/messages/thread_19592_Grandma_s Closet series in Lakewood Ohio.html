From: theseeker_22 (theseeker_22@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-25 19:48:26 UTC
Has anyone found these since the floods this Spring? Saturday I took
my 2 grandsons and their Mom out looking for "Grandma's Closet
Monday" and we didn't find any of them...I know I went on the wrong
day and they are invisible every day except the one they are designed
for right? *SMILE* #1 is noted as missing on the clues. I have
never seen a micro-box so wasn't really sure what we were looking
for, but assumed something like a film canister because that is what
I heard lots of folks use for micro-boxes. There was lots of washed
up debris so we thought maybe the floods took them all. Please let
me know if anyone has seen them since the flood as I will be back in
Lakewood in September and would like to take the boys out and try
again if they are still around.

Any information would be appreciated. TIA

Hi.

Found Grandma's Closet Monday 1 and 5 on 03-Oct-2003.
Both were easy finds.

Found Grandma's Closet Monday 4 on 09-May-2004 after a
hard search. Could not locate 2 or 3!

Found Grandma's Closet Tuesday 1-5 on 03-Oct-2003 - all easy.

Found Grandma's Closet Wednesday Morning 1,2,4 on 09-May-2004
3 appears to be missing.

Found Grandma's Closet Wednesday Afternoon 1,2,4 on 09-May-2004.
3 is missing. Both these sites are across the ROAD from Tuesday (not
across the bridge per se). Wed. morning was easy. Wed Afternoon was
an exhausting climb.
