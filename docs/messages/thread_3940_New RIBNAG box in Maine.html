RIBNAG has planted another letterbox in Bangor, Maine.
This one bears the name "Prentiss Apprentice", and
we have written the clue as a very easy mystery box:

http://users.tmok.com/~ribnag/lb-prentiss.shtml

For those who have not yet read or seen Harry Potter,
the clue makes vague references to it, but does not
bluntly give anything away. For those who have written
Harry Potter based clues, read this clue before you
suspect us of copying your style, you'll get a kick
out of it. ;-)


Also, we mention this with every clue post, but we
still get box status reports for boxes that our main
page very clearly indicates as "missing". *PLEASE*
check our main page for the status of any box you
plan to go after, *before* looking for it. We don't
really care about getting redundant reports, deleting
them takes very little effort. By not checking,
however, you waste quite a lot more of your own
ime than ours. Go here:

http://users.tmok.com/~ribnag

Thank you.
