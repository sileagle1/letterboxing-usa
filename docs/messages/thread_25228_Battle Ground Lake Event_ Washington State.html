Battle Ground Lake Event, Washington State

From: mitchklink (Letterboxr@aol.com) | Date: 2010-08-22 06:47:01 UTC
For those who plan to attend the "Gather Around the Campfire Stamp Camp" event at Battle Ground Lake State Park in Washington, this is just a note to inform you that I checked on both the "Marshall Lookout" (circa 2000) and "Battle Ground Lake #2" (circa 2002) boxes today. Both have received necessary maintanence and are currently active. The clues can be found on the LbNA website. (www.letterboxing.org) RaqsEnigma and I have previous obligations, so we won't be able to attend the event. But I wanted you to know that the first two boxes in the park are alive and well.
