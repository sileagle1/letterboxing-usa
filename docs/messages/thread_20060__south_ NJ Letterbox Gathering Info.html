From: letterboxingbee (letterboxingbee@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-24 12:58:48 UTC


2nd Annual South Jersey Letterbox Gathering

DATE:
October 16, 2004

PLACE:
Palmyra Cove Nature Park, Palmyra, NJ at the foot of the Tacony-
Palmyra Bridge

WEBSITE:
http://www.palmyracove.org/

THEME:
Nature and wildlife

TIME:
10am - 4pm approx. Park closes at dusk. Classroom closes at 4pm

FOOD:
Please bring a dish or something to share to eat. Hoagies,
sandwiches, chips, dips, brownies,
cakes, pies, fruit, water, soda etc.

PLANS:
We will have use of the classroom if the weather does not cooperate.
What is the consensus of a
carving class here?? I would be willing to teach a 1 hour class
after lunch if there is a request for
one.

BOXES:
If you plan on planting your own boxes and wish to have these
included in the clue sheets I plan to
hand out please email me at: letterboxingbee at yahoo dot com and I
will include them.

NOTES:
NO pets of any kind as this is a nature reserve
NO BBQ'ing or fires of any type
They have picinic tables and benches outside for us to use
Classroom closes at 4pm.
