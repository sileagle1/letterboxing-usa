Our first box!

From: Danielle (firstimemom2003@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-09 05:21:32 UTC
My husband and I found our first box today! We live in the Central
Valley near Modesto CA. We found FLIP- The missing Tiki man!
We are so excited about this. We love to be outdoors and are going
to Lake Tahoe for a week long vacation next week. We are planning on
hunting for as many boxes as we can find!
We found this hobby/sport in Time magazine and although it looks as
if newbies who found out about this in Time are being disrespectful-
that is NOT us! We love that we found something that we can do as a
family with our dog and our 8 month old daughter outdoors and for
FREE! We are planning on hiding a box in the Lake Tahoe area and
will soon be posting clues :) We LOVE this.

Danielle & Rob
