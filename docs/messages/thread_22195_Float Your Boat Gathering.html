Hi Everyone,
Just found out some more info about Liberty Lake. The pricing is as
follows. RV sites (one rv, one small tent, four people) is $24. Each
additional person over 5 is $2. Tent sites (Two tents, four people)
is $18. Each additional person is $2. If the people who have signed
up would like to save some money, we could place more than one
family on a tent site. For example if two families come and there a
two adults in each family and the family one has three children
under five and family two has one child over five, the site will
cost $20 for both families. Then both families can split the cost.
If the people who have signed up can e-mail me with the day they
plan on coming and if they mind sharing a site or not. Then I can e-
mail each with the e-mail of the family you will share with, so you
can coordinate when coming in the gate. Hope this isn't too
comfusing. The prices are pre night. Hope to see everyone there.

For those of you that are just coming in.
The float your boat gathering is a gathering on Labor Day weekend.
Sign ups are sill open. It is in Eastern Washington at Liberty Lake
County Park, near Spokane. There will be a stamp carving clinic, a
logbook making clinic, a hunt, potluck, exchange and a bonus box.
Please e-mail me if you would like to come. RV's a encouraged to
come on Friday, due to lack of RV space, but tents can come either
Friday or Saturday, dur to they can stick us anywhere there is open
space. Howerver if coming on Saturday you may not get a site with a
firepit or grill. Please keep this in mind when planning.

Looking forward to seeing everyone.
