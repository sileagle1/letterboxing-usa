Mr. Marconi's Wonderful Wireless
Location: Marconi Station Site, South Wellfleet, MA
Difficulty: Easy
Planted by Deanne & David (WR1T) Besnia 02/09/02
 
This is basically a drive up letterbox. Take Route 6 to South Wellfleet and turn at the sign indicating the Marconi Station Site and Marconi Beach.  Follow the signs to the Station Site.  Park in the lot and walk to the boardwalk on the right. Walk up the boardwalk and when you get to the viewing platform step off onto the sand.  Walk around the platform until you get to the point where the water tower is at your back and the pavilion is in front of you. Reach under the platform and find Mr. Marconi's Wonderful Wireless letterbox honoring Guglielmo Marconi, wireless wizard and winner of the 1909 Nobel Prize in Physics.
