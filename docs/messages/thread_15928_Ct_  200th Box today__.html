Gathering (My Dogs are Barking trail and Chase the Tail trail).

cooperative/collaborative efforts of a number of letterboxers
creating stamps for the series.
Back to the topic of 200. It had to be one of Chuck and Molly's

visited and read from A to Z. I frequently saw the unmistakable
vehicle Chuck drove, often in the Schoolhouse Brook parking lot.

letterbox placers. For instance, lately, I've been hunting down
Irish Tinker l.b.s
Here's to much more fall hiking and box finding before the snow
flies (and even after) for all of us. I have orange plastic garden
spade..will dig for treasure.
