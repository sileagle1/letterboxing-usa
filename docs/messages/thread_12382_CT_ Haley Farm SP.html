CT: Haley Farm SP

From: Steve (shol3039@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-27 22:22:12 UTC
We got a late start this afternoon and hit Haley Farm SP in Groton.
Did the first box by the green bar gate and decided we didn't have
enough time before I had to go to work to traverse Bluff Point.

Started on Karen's clues for Haley Farm and got the Chester Cemetary
box. The log book for this box if FILLED to the brimn, we actually
had to back track a few pages and stamp in out-of-order. It appears
that several non-boxers have signed into this log, which is neat. I'm
impressed that the box has not been vandalized with the amount of
finds, this spot appears to be a popular one to...uhh.... reflect?
With one "group" having a particular fondness for botany... namely
the Canabis genus according to the drawing (shaking head). Great
stamp though!!!!

I think we'll try to get back and grab the picnic spot Lb and the
canopy rock ones this week, might drop off a new log-book for Chester
Cemetary if either thats satisfactory to the owner or if the box
owner can't get there soon.

Up to PFX 98, was actually disappointed we didn't have time to hit
the 100 mark today as it was a BEAUTIFUL warm sunny, breezy, perfect
day!

Also made a stop in that general area and dropped the Beaver Crew HH
off for a continued voyage across Groton. We don't plan on being out
of the area in the next few days so we decided to let someone else
have the fun of the find rather than it taking up space in our pack!

-Steve, Heidi, Madras
