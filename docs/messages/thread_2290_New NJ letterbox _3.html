Box #3: Panic/Attack
Location: Somewhere in New Jersey
Placed 04/03/2001
Clue page: http://members.aol.com/pcletterboxing/clue3.htm

On Halloween night, 1938, there was a radio drama that caused
a nationwide panic. The town where this drama unfolded is the
town in which you find this letterbox.

At a certain public park in this New Jersey town, there is a
monument commemorating this phenomenon. Once you are
there, look for the small boardwalk. When facing the water, go to
the left side and look under the side edge of the boardwalk; the
box is resting there.

While this letterbox is hidden in an interesting and historically
significant area, it is neither remote nor secluded. Please try to
be discreet when you find the letterbox.
