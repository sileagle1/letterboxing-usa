I zipped up to Purgatory Chasm Friday night to get the Proposal
series, but only snagged Intimacy and Feelings. Both boxes are
showing their age with plastic cracked by exposure to the weather.
The Feelings log is very damp. The other two boxes eluded me as I
was being chased by a swarm of ravenous mosquitoes and the sun was
going down. I don't think they are missing, I just need to go back
armed with a can of Deep Woods Off.

On Sunday, Rustypuff and I spent a few hours in the Blue Hills before
attending an Indian pow wow. We finished off the Time Travelers
series with Morlock Holes, and then found the Peabody bonus box
(Rustypuff's 200th!). We were a little put out (in a good-natured
way) over the clue for the E=MC2 bonus box, as we did not have the
required information with us. Oh well, I guess we'll never be done
with the Blue Hills. I see that kingdh22 has placed yet another one
there. We'll be back!
