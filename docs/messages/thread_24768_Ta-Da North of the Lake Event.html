Huzzah! Huzzah!

Her Royal Majesty Queen Beeloved and His Gentle Lordship Beneficus Rex
hereby extend to all within hearing an invitation for a gathering of
fun, food, and frolicking (for letterboxes)! Those who care, bring
potluck fare for friends to share. For artisans who would place a
treasure there, a Camelot theme would seem most fair.

Journey a short distance north of Lake Lewisville and join us at South
Lakes Park in Denton, Texas. The park is located on Hobson Road, about
one mile south of the corner of I-35E and Teasley lane.

June 7, 2008 from 10:00a.m. to 6:00p.m. Details can be found at
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=662
or email
kevan.beijan@verizon.net .

Until then, Fare Thee Well!
