A Single Rose Letterbox

Placed by J&A-bear (jedaryn02@yahoo.com) on 22 March
2003
Boise, ID (near the Boise Depot)
Ada County

Directions: Take Exit 53, Vista Ave, from I84 and head
north. Turn left on Rose Hill and then turn right on
Opal St. and park as it dead ends. Cross the railroad
tracks safely and start this search at the flag pole
in front of the Boise Depot.

Description: A short letterbox find which includes
ascending and descending a few stairs and paths. Enjoy
a stroll through a pretty garden with fountains.

Clues: Start at the Flag pole. Platt Gardens sign.
Descend those stairs which lead you to cross the stone
bridge. Smell the roses on the left and glance at the
small pond behind. Continue down this path in a
backwards J direction. Follow the rock trail up
toward the water fall. Pass the bench bearing the
name of a month. Cross the smaller bridge that
admires a smaller water fall. At the T hang a left
and climb up eight times. A Single Rose letterbox
awaits you at the right rock bench past the trash
container.
