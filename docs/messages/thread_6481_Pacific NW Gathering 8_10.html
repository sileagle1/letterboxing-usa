From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2002-07-26 17:46:19 UTC
With our get together but a week away, I want to make sure that all
the PNW boxers know the details. We will be meeting at Upper Macleay
Park on NW Cornell Road aroung noon on Saturday the 10th. We'll have
lunch (bring your own,) swap stamps, share clues to those special
boxes placed for the event, and then hit the many nearby trails. A
free trail map of Forest Park is available at Audubon House, just west
of our meeting place or at the Forestry Center beside the Zoo. Hide a
box for the event, bring your clues and bring yourselves. See you there!
