Honk! Honk! Mystery Box

From: Ken Jones (KenJones@sc.rr.com) | Date: 2002-08-20 22:07:04 UTC-04:00
Our name: Sissy and CR

Box name: Honk! Honk! Mystery Box

Place: Secret

Placed: 8-18-02

Clues:
- This place is billed as "the only place on earth that you will find all
eight species of swans living together."
- You must find a particular bench on which to sit yourself.
- This bench is on a secluded grassy trail.
- You will have enjoyed the sights and crossed a great bridge.
- You will have then walked along a raised wooden trail.
- You will have then come to the end and circled around again.
- As you come to the end of the clearings you either must climb slighty to a
gravely trail or choose two grassy ones further to your left.
- You will also see a bench with it's back to the short rise--walk to it,
but rest not.
- Continue past the bench into the woods--woods to your left and the rise to
your right--onto the narrow grassy trail.
- The bench you seek will be the next one. Rest there.
- Directly in front of you is "The Tree of Three," the largest tree you can
see.
- The Tree guards the box.

Take care when approaching "The Tree of Three" as people may be able to
observe you above on the rise--you MUST look all around you before entering
the woods.

Hope you enjoy the area and the hunt.

Regards,

CR

PS:  Is the clues too hard?  Please send feedback.  I've tried to write the
clues so that when you are there, they are fairly obvious.  ~CR
