Wow... NBC did a nice job with their program last night at 7pm. The
program was geared towards educating kids and had features on sailing
in Mystic, lobstering in Groton, and letterboxing in the CT State
forests. Grant Stinchfield hosted the bit on LB and he was walking
through the forest with the Moshier family (sp?). Not sure which
forest he was at, but they walked by Nathan Hale's historic
home...and I think that's in the greater Coventry area?

Anyways, they kept it simple and focused on the State DEP's series of

the idea of spending quality family time together!

Another positive promotion! Yay!
