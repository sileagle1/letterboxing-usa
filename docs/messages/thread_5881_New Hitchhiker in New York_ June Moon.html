From: The McQuades (mcquades@twcny.rr.com) | Date: 2002-06-25 20:35:54 UTC-04:00
Turtle McQ, her McQ-lets Lone Wolf and Strawberry Girl plus first-time
letterboxer Mrs. T placed the new hitchhiker June Moon in the Taughannock
Falls box in Ulysses, NY today.

June Moon is a companion hitchhiker to June Bloom and June Gloom, which
found new homes in Louisiana just days ago.

We were most excited to be the first to find the Taughannock letterbox -
beautiful stamp and handmade logbook. Thanks to The Chicken-counters for a
welcome excuse to tromp through the water on this hot and steamy day.

TurtleMcQ and the rest of the gang
