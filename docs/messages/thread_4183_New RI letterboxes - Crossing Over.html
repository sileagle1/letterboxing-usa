From: Trish Kurdziel (trishkri@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-01-15 08:47:18 UTC-08:00

The Fisherville Brook Wildlife Refuge in Exeter has some wonderful hiking trails. Most who hike there only do the trail around the pond. There are two nice loops on the other side of the road that it is worth "Crossing Over" for hiking (and now letterboxing).

Bob Hopkins (the RI wiz) just relocated the Mercy Brown LB and we just placed two new LB's on the less visited side - Hedgehog's Hidey Hole and Twister on Tuesday (another Magic Treehouse box).

Enjoy!
