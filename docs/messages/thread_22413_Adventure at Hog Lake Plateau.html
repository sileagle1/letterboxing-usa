Adventure at Hog Lake Plateau

From: boxfunretired (boxfunretired@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-10 02:07:45 UTC
We're very happy hawgs after visitng the Hog Theme Park in two visits
and finding all the hogs! These stamps, logs, and boxes are some of
the most outstanding and creative we have seen! Each new discovery
brought a smile and real appreciation for the talents involved.

Something eerie is happening on the Plateau, though! There is a
Phantom Hawg that appears in the dark night shadows! This Phantom
Hawg helps a little HH pig trot from the box where he is placed!
(This Phantom will soon have his own box!) Oooooeeeeeee!

Also, beware of a stinging rock! Mistaking it for a nice place to sit
and stamp, one of us ended up with "ants in the pants"!

Now, as we pass the Plateau, we reverently raise our trotters and
oink, "Yo...to The Hog!"

We wish you great letterboxing! ...RetiredHavinFun (Boatman and
Mystery Gal)
