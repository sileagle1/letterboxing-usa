I took a few minutes on the way to the grocery store and stopped at
some quick boxes. Fort Trumbull is in good condition and well into
the second book. MY was it cold out! Very windy spot but a nice
view.

Also stopped at the Jordan Mill Site and got that box. I think the
directions may need to be corrected. It was listed as Rt 215, it
actually is Rt 213. Also you go South from 213/156 to get to
Trumbull Road. I found the box on the North side of the rock, so
either it was moved or maybe the clues need to be altered a touch.
All in all, took me about 10 minutes of checking the larger rocks in
the S.E. corner to figure out "Maybe I should look on the other
side!". Anyways, I found it so no biggie! The stamp is
INCREDIBLE, I like it a lot... what good detail!

Ran out of time after grocery shopping,I was going to do the other
Fort (Griswold) but I unfortunately need to sleep a bit means i got
called in to work for Midnight (Yuck!). Looks like no boxing
tomorrow means its supposed to snow a bit down here.
