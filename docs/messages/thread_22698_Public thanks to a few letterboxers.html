Public thanks to a few letterboxers

From: cpascott (seh-letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-10-11 17:58:18 UTC
I wanted to publicly thank a few letterboxers who have agreed to carve
stamps for my upcoming boxes.

Great thanks to Choi, Scarab, Brandy You're a Fine Girl, Team
Randalstik, and Talking Turtle.

A number of boxes are planned and these wonderful carvers are will
provide fantastic images worth seeking. Choi and Scarab have carved
for me before and their work is superb -- and I'm sure most of you
know that the work of the other three are top notch as well.

Look for the boxes to be announced upon their placement, from later
this autumn through next spring, or catch the preview on my website:
http://home.comcast.net/~scott.e.hall/Placed.html

Although I seek to improve my own ability to carve, I enjoy using the
fine work of others. If anyone else out there is willing to carve for
me, please let me know.

Thanks again to those carvers listed above! I'm excited to place the
boxes with your fine work!


Best,
CPAScott
