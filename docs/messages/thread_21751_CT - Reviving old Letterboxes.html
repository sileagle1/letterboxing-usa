As long as there's no objection, I'd like to bring some Letterbox
clues out of the dark and dusty "basement of unclaimed Letterboxes"
to
allow more folks to visit and enjoy them. This would mean that I
would become caretaker of them since the whereabouts of their owners
is unknown. My name would become the "contact placer", but full
credit still goes to the original placers.

The boxes that I'm referring to are:
- "The Alchemy Letterbox Series-Mercury" by Cobalt Nine in New
Haven,
CT.
- "Lewis and Clark in CT" (3 boxes) by Winternuts, in Woodbridge, CT.

I found all 4 boxes intact and dry, but they haven't been visited in
about a year since the clues aren't readily available.
It would be a shame not to share these older gems with everyone.
