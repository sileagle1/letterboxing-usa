From: (mporter@pobox.com) | Date: 2001-07-22 20:44:21 UTC
Hi Mona,

Yes, my e-mail set up is on the fritz - Hopefully that e-mail address
will be working by tomorrow, Monday.

It's a pretty easy up and back hike, 2 and a half miles round-trip
from the parking area to the falls and back. There's about a 450
foot elevation gain on the way up to the falls, so take your time
getting there; but you can fairly dance down on the way back.

"Scrambling over rocks" is just walking over them, really. I usually
carry a walking stick, but you'll just be walking up a 4-5 foot
incline that has some rocks on it. On a 1 to 5 difficulty rating for
the hike, I'd give it a 1.5.

If you do the hike in December, let me know if that little ballpark
guess-timate gauge was accurate. It's not that difficult at all...

Mick
