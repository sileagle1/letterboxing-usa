The first time I went with someone else (when my ex and I broke up) was when
I hooked up with Letterboxer2002 for boxing in Mansfield. I don't exactly
remember who initiated the conversation and the idea on meeting up to box,
however, I had never met L2002 before and we decided to go out to Mansfield to box
one Saturday morning. A week later, I was talking with Butterfly and Bluebird
and asked if I could tag along when they went out to do the Christmas in
Bethlehem series (or was it the other way around??? I remember really wanting to
do them too). And that is when I friendship was born! I had met Butterfly and
Bluebird previously at an event, and we had been exchanging e-mails for a
month or so, before we were able to hook up to box.

I also box a lot by myself, and there have been some places that have totally
freaked me out (not the conventional places however, like the Nike Missile
Site). That's when I usually ask someone to tag along. Most letterboxers would
be happy to tag along with you, especially if they haven't done that box.

BTW... I would be more than happy to go letterboxing with you! I'm currently
on summer vacation and there are weeks where can go almost any time. All you
have to do is ask :-)
