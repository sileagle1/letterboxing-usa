Y2K hitchhiker moves on.

From: graybeardr (rll1924@linkny.com) | Date: 2001-12-15 14:56:27 UTC
After a couple of stops in New York State (lastly at the Sugar
Hill Lb) back in August, this - which I believe to have been the
first hitchhiker - finally got moved on to Maryland (it had been
waiting for a ride farther west). I forgot to record who it was
that originated it; I hope that letterboxer will notice this post.
It's unique in more ways than having been first . . . go find it!
