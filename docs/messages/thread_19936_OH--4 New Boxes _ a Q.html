[LbNA] Re: OH--4 New Boxes & a Q

From: catbead1 (libby@twcny.rr.com) | Date: 2004-08-17 04:18:19 UTC
Yes, that is to redirect to the letterbox clues on your own webpage.
For example I have all my boxes on my own website, so when you click
on my boxes you are automatically taken there.

If you were to put the park link in the "link" box (without the
http://) then anyone wanting the clues for your box will end up at the
park's website.


If you want to put a link to the state park, then the best place to do
it would be in your clues. I think if the http:// part _is_ included
then the link will be active...but I could be wrong.

catbead
