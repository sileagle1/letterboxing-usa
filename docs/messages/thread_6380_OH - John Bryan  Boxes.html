From: geoflyfisher (geoflyfisher@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-07-22 13:56:44 UTC
Whew!

I have been very busy with kayaking for a couple months now. See my
homepage at www.qsl.net/ws8g for details.

I made a sweep of the Valley of the Frogs boxes yesterday. (94
degrees and high humidity) I checked on all 30 boxes in the park and
two nearby.

3 are missing... 27 still in good shape.

Of the 3 Rediscovery boxes, the first has gone camping. I will
likely put a replacement out when it gets a little cooler.

Of the 3 ProtoTree frog boxes, the second one is out swimming down
the river somewhere. I will find a cousin and put him back somewhere
nearby by the fall.

Unfortunately, franzsolo's Ohio Century box is missing. It was a
beautiful stamp and I found no trace of it. I had visited before, so
I know I was checking the right place.

There are still 3 boxes which have not yet been found by anyone. 3
have only been found by franzsolo. Most boxes had between 5-10
visitors, and I believe pete and wanda have not been by yet.

I replaced the logs of some of the early microboxes with new improved
logs, so there is now plenty of room for fall visitors.
