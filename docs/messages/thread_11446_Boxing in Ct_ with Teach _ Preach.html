Boxing in Ct. with Teach & Preach

From: Dan (danzad_99@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-03-25 02:11:42 UTC
We had a great 4 days of letterboxing(Fri.,Sat.,Sun.(skipped
church,thought we would go to the night service), and Mon. Friday we
started with The Picture This Letterbox(picked up the Singin Men
clues here) and The Nipmunk Trail Letterbox. Then we did the
Nipmuck/Natchaug Junction Letterbox. All 3 boxes were in great shape
Chuck. Sat. we did the Presidential Animals(nice carvings),
Watership Downs(again nice carvings) & the Ben E Letterbox. The Ben
E box was a little wet but everything inside o.k. & last we did the
Erin Go Braugh. Very nice series, very well done. Sun. we started
at the Mainsfield Depot area with Train Trestle, Great Grape and Rub
a Dub Dub(that was funny Chuck) all was great but right across from
the Train Trestle Letterbox was a skelaton of a deer. Not for
someone with a week stomach. We did the Feed Store and picked up the
Swift Passage Hitchhicker(must have just missed TeamGreenDragon), The
Schoolhouse Letterbox, The Boo Two You Letterbox(The best puzzle
series), Merrow Park Letterbox, and the 3 Wizards Letterbox(best
carvings I have seen). All boxes were in great shape. Wish we meet
Leader of the Pack & crew at Merrow Park. Mon. we finished with
Inherit The Earth, The Bed Rug, Plane Spotter,Paw Print,Singing Men,&
The Village(Oxen series)all boxes were great. We also bought all the
supplies need for a series that will be at Wolf Den in Pomfret. We
have been going up there to get ready for it. It should be a 22box
series coming within the next month or so. Everybody be safe hiking,
God Bless Teach & Preach
