LBNA Message Board

From: nseftor (neil@seftor.com) | Date: 2003-09-03 13:28:31 UTC
Due to the overwhelming traffic on the Yahoo group list, I have
started a message board devoted to letterboxing:

http://pub188.ezboard.com/bletterboxingnorthamerica

On this board are forums for various areas of interest, including
general letterboxing information, new boxes, box reports by region,
and events, among others. Box reports and letterboxing stories are
welcome. The forums allow you to read only areas that are interesting
to you, without having to wade through hundreds of messages. Also, the
message board format will allow for threaded topics, keeping related
messages together.

Getting the board going is a slow process. While a number of people
have visited, a small (but growing) subset have posted. I hope you
will try the board and make suggestions. I believe this could be a
major improvement in communication for the letterboxing community.
Thanks.

- Neil (Frog Dad of the Forest Frog Family)
