I found my first!!!!!!!

From: Kerrygirl (kerrygirl@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-08-21 12:58:13 UTC-04:00
This is a real red letter day. A friend and I went out this morning and found Lieutenant Lisa in Bushnell, FL. The clues were great and we found the right area without any trouble. Had a refresher course in compass reading last night as when we used to go hiking my husband always did the navigating. I was glad I thought to bring a walking stick as it was very useful for prodding around ground cover, leaves and branches without reaching in. Now I really can't wait for my trip to Connecticut and Maine next month. I've told my friends that I'll be visiting about it and they're interested in having a go, as well. What a great way to spend the day!! Can't wait to go again.


Kerrygirl
