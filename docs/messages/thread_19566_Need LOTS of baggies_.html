Need LOTS of baggies?

From: Debbi Scott (dscott5377@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-07-23 16:31:20 UTC-06:00
If you live in an area with lots of boxers (or just plant lots of boxes
yourself). Associated Bag Company
(http://www.associatedbag.com/default.asp) has great prices. But, you
do have to buy in large quantities. They have every size baggie you can
think of... name brand and their brand. They even have the silica gel
packets.

I buy through this company for baggies and boxes for the dog cookies I
sell... they are great. Very quick shipping, and very customer
oriented.

Debbi

Re: Need LOTS of baggies?

From: catbead1 (libby@twcny.rr.com) | Date: 2004-07-23 23:32:32 UTC
Another good place to find small ziploc baggies is J.S.Ritter, who has
a $15.00 minimum order. You can find the baggies here:
http://www.jsritter.com/boxes.htm#zip
They have 3" x 3" zip baggies 100/$1.93 and
4 x 4 zip baggies 100/$2.65 both are perfect sizes for stamps.
4 x 6 100/$3.02 and 5 x 8 100/$4.53 work great for logbooks. These
are not thick like freezer baggies, but since they are smaller they
slide nicely into the freezer baggies adding an extra layer of
protection without bulk.

catbead
