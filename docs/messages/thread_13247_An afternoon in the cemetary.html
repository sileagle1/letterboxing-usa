An afternoon in the cemetary

From: BigGuy_45640 (bigguy_45640@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-01 03:19:04 UTC
Despite temperatures in the 50,s and what seemed like a 100 mph wind
up on that hill, CardinalMom, the kids, and I ventured up to
Grandview Cemetary in Chillicothe, OH and located 4 of Salamander's
boxes. We started with "Mistletoe & Holly", then "Governor's Rest"
(Adina tried really hard to be inconspicuous to the walkers in the
area as she retrieved this one from a rather exposed position.),
then "Luna Verde" (where we were rewarded with the "Legends Live
Forever" hitchhiker), and finally we were privileged to be First
Finders of "Lonesome Pine". All boxes were dry and in good shape and
Salamander's stamps are great as always. An added bonus was the
spectacular view from the ridge. I'll bet many a developer has shed
bitter tears that the best views in the city belong to those who
don't get much benefit from them.
