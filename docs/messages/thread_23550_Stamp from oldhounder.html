If you were at any of the following gatherings and acquired a stamp of
a pony-tailed woman riding a galloping horse, the stamp now has a name
and is listed as an event stamp on Atlasquest so that you can score
keep, should you wish to do so. It is Tallahassee Lassie carved by
Kirbert and is in process of being planted as the box stamp within a
hitchhiker hostel by the same name in Littleton, MA.

Right Under Your Nose (Grayson, GA)
Wilmington Film Fest (Wilmington, NC)
Solstice Boxing Brunch (Biddeford Pool, ME)
Greek Myth-Story Gather (Wendell, MA)
