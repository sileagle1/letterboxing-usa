Hello.

I will be travelling to LA, CA for a wedding at the
end of December and plan on doing some letterboxing
while I'm there. I'll be taking a letterboxing
neophyte with me in hopes of getting her hooked, too.


Has anyone visited any of the following boxes lately:

CalState U, Long Beach
Enchanted Palos Verdes Box
Signal Hill
2 Grey Squirrell's CA boxes

It's our first trip to CA and we're looking forward to
exploring. We plan on placing a box in Topanga State
Park while we're out there. It doesn't look like
there are any boxes there yet. Does anyone familiar
with the area have a "don't miss" box we should try
for?

Thanks for your help.

From: Gwen & Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2001-12-04 12:06:07 UTC-08:00
Donna If you want to spend the day boxing you might want to try this
schedule. Bolsa Chica "Shore Bird" along with Wendy Gault's "Pelican" ,"El
Dorado Nature Center". "Rose at the Ranch" between 1 pm& 5pm. The boxes at
CSULB,Signal Hill, "White by day" ,West wetlands can all be found at night
in fact they might be better at night. We were at the Squirrels about a year
ago. Make sure you include Enchanted Palos Verdes as it has a super stamp.
