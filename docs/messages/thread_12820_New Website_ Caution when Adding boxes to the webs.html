New Website: Caution when Adding boxes to the website

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-05-12 14:28:26 UTC-04:00
Just an FYI to all those who are putting their boxes on the new site
(especially those in CT since I'm starting to plan my boxing summer!).

When you add a box under "Add Box", make sure you change the number by the
heading of "Number of Boxes" to reflect how many boxes you are placing
(especially in a series). Many of the boxes in CT say that they are one box,
however, when you read the clues, there are numerous boxes in the series.
