Pink Ribbon HH Update

From: Mike Schneider (tehutika@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-20 16:35:11 UTC-07:00
Greetings,

As requested by TeamGreenDragon, we're updating folks
about the Pink Ribbon HH.

Two letterboxers found it on 4/14/03 at Turkey Trot,
but they did not leave their names in the book, only
their stamps. They also did not write down or stamp in
where they left it.

Pink Ribbon was then found and dropped off somewhere
on 4/23/03 by Susan A. from Leominster. She also did
not say where she found it, so it's location between
Turkey Trot and Pig Pen is unknown.

On 4/25/03, Trickster, Thunderbird, E-man, and The
Dawn left it at Pig Pen Ledges. They also did not
write down where they found it.

On 5/17/03, Fuzzy's Friend found it at Pig Pen Ledges,
and moved it to the Northampton Bike Path. True to the
spirit of the HH, Fuzzy told us where he put it, so it
could continue it's travels.

TehutiKa, AsetKa and the kids picked it up on 5/20/03,
and we will let everyone know where we bring it next
once we do.

So, since it's last update, 12 people have found this
HH, and it's been to at least 3 boxes.

Mike S.
