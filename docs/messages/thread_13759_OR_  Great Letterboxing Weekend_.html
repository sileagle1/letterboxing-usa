From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2003-06-22 21:18:51 UTC-07:00
WOW!

This has been a great weekend for letterboxing here in Oregon. Friday I
kidnapped John and we were able to scout out some new spots for stamps I
want to place (stamps are now carved, just have to get the LB's together
and then place them...) probably sometime in August when every weekend
on my calendar is not booked solid.

We were able to nab a couple of dvn trckr boxes at the coast: Sitka
Spruce just outside of Seaside and Ecola Letterbox right in the heart of
Cannon Beach! At both those we came across geoboxes first, but
eventually found the letterboxes we were seeking! The Ecola Letterbox
is totally adorable. It really got a smile out of us. What a sweet
little stamp! We also managed to score a couple mystery boxes this
weekend, which was a great bonus!

Thanks to all who place letterboxes in the Pacific NW. We are very
grateful for the wonderful art we come across in our travels. What a
blast!!!

Best regards,
