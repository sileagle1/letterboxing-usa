Script Ohio

Difficulty: easy
Terrain: easy
Placed by: Bigguy
Date placed: 6/28/03

Go to the Grandview Cemetery in Chillicothe, Ohio. The entrance is
on Bellview Ave. near the bottom of the hill. Once in the cemetery,
locate the four sided stone enclosure. From the Northeast corner of
the enclosure, take a bearing of 98 and walk about 13 paces (1 pace
= 2 steps) to a large maple tree with a hollow near the bottom.
You'll find Script Ohio hiding in the hollow under some rocks and
bark. When you find it, email us and let us know how it's doing at:
bigguy_45640@yahoo.com While you're there, take time to locate some
of the many other boxes in the cemetery and admire the very
impressive views from the ridge.
