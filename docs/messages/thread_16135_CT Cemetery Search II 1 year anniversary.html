From: LoneLetterboxer (JustBeth65@msn.com) | Date: 2003-10-01 21:58:09 UTC
Tomarrow will mark the 1 year aniversary of my discovering the time
consuming sport of Letterboxing. In honor of this day I have taken it
upon myself to plant Cemetery Search II. Many of you may remember
Cemetery Search I as the letterbox which spawned the Great Gerbil
Gathering, and remember plans are underway for the Great Gerbil
Reunion in May of 2004.

So without further adu...Happy Anniversary to me!!!!
Oh yeah I finally did the mundane task of counting and I surprised
even myself F101 x105 P22

Thanks to all the great friends I've made, boxes I've found and for
wonderful things to come!!

JustBeth the LoneLetterboxer!
