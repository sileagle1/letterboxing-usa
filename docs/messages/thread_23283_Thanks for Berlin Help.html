OK, I did manage to find *one* rock in all the parkland of Berlin and
was able to plant a box I'd prepared while still in the states. I'll
do micros next time, most definitely!

I had thought about ditching the box I'd brought with me and going
with something more easily hidden in an urban setting, but I had
sticker shock after pricing little lock tites in the shopping center --
five euros for one container! No wonder letterboxing isn't widespread
here! ;O)

Anyhoo, "In Celebration" was successfully planted in the Rudow section
of Berlin. If you plan to be in Berlin, I hope you'll look for it and
drop me a line to let me know what shape it's in. I should be back at
the end of summer and can maintain it then.
