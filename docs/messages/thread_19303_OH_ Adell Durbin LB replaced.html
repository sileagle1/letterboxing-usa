Hello Ohioians!

Wild Child and I were in Ohio a couple of weeks back and replaced the missing
Acorn box @ Adell Durbin in Stow. Wow--we were amazed by how many people
had stamped into Squirrely's box--and how many said it was their first box. We
had no idea that there would be that many boxers in the area/passing thru.

If you go after Acorn in the near future, take bug spray with you--the
skeeters were out in force.

--Wild Woman
P.S. Sorry that we left a bit too soon to nab the new series in Kent! We'll
be looking forward to it next summer during our annual visit to Stow!
