There is now a letterbox in Idaho!!

After placing the Golden Spike box in Utah on Sunday, Oct. 1, Der Mad Stamper
wandered on to Boise, Idaho.

On Monday, Oct. 2, 2000, The Raptor Letterbox became the first letterbox in
Idaho. It is located along the walking trail at Dedication Point, overlooking
the Snake River Canyon.
