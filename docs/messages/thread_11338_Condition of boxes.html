From: roberta a stillson (Rubaduc@juno.com) | Date: 2003-03-21 18:06:05 UTC-05:00
I went to Timberland North today, found all 6 boxes which were in good
shape except Kissing Rock. It was wet inside but everything was o.k. so
after drying it out, I put in all new baggies and moved it a few inches
closer to one of the boulders on a little higher ground. It had been
sitting in a puddle of water. After that I went to Bittner Park. The West
River was raging, in fact I had to wade to get to one of the bridges. The
Playground box was very wet and the log book cover was saturated. I dried
everything, put in new baggies and wrapped Kleenex around the log book
cover hoping to absorb some of it.
