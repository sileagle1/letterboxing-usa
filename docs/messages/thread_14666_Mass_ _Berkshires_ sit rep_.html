Yesterday we took the less direct route home from Falcon Ridge Folk
Festival.

#1) Monument Mt. box (Great Barrington) is in fine shape. I
replaced torn plastic bag log book was in). NEXT time we'll climb to
the peak!!

#2) Norman Rockwell box (Lenox) is also fine. Sap from that big old
pine dripped onto my logbook, so 2 pages keep sticking together.:-/

#3) Eagle Ledge box (Lee - Oct. Mt. State Forest).The box is
fine. It does require exact compass readings to find it. The
logbook is hand made and really unique. I love the stamp and it is
now one of my favorites. Only a handful of people have been here,
which is a shame. There is a nice network of trails to hike through
and the walk to the box is pleasant (bring skeeter repellant).
