Trout Brook Letterbox
Holden MA
Placed 1 January 2002

Trail Advisories:
- 2 miles round trip (probably not even quite that much)
- Areas of the trail may be muddy.
- Bring your skates if it's cold. Bring a picnic lunch if it's warm.

Map & Information:
http://www.wachusettgreenways.org/wgghold.html

Drive:
- From Rt 31 in Holden turn onto Manning Street,
go 0.9 miles and look for Trout Brook Parking on your left.
- From Rt 140 in Oakdale (near Wachusett Reservoir) turn
onto Laurel St and go 2.4 miles. Trout Brook Parking will
be on your right.
- Or from Interstate 190 Exit 5 go north on Rt 140, take your
first left onto Legg Road. Follow it to the end and turn right.
Trout Brook Parking will be on your right about a mile along.
