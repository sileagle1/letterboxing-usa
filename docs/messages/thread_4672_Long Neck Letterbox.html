LONG NECK LETTERBOX
Clepper Park
Summerside, OH  Clermont County
Difficulty: easy
Placed March 10, 2002 by Team Boji (ZAX31@AOL)

Driving Directions: From I-275 take State Route 32 west.  First traffic signal turn right on Old State Route 74.  First stop sign turn left on Summerside Road.  Clepper Park is about a mile on the right.

Clues:  Find a building containing restrooms and a flag pole out front.  Walk a heading of approx. 300 degrees to the "Enter Here" sign.  From here walk in a counterclockwise direction around the lake.  You will pass three benches and come to a boulder.  From the boulder walk south on the asphalt path to the "Y." From the "Y" walk the asphalt path, approx. 215 degrees, to the first bench on your right.  From the bench walk north to the fence line.  Under a rock at the base of a small tree you may find LONG NECK LETTERBOX.
