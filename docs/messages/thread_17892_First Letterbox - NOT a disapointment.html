First Letterbox - NOT a disapointment

From: quarksmom (quarksmom@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-02-21 03:45:32 UTC
I took advantage of a break in winter weather here in Montana to
hunt my first "real" letterbox today. It was definitely not a
disappointment. Although the description says short hike I knew
that would not be the case as the state park where the box is
located is closed for the winter so it was a three mile uphill hike
to the start of the clues. But it was a gorgeous sunny day & the
views were outstanding.

The box itself was buried under the snow & also took a bit of
coaxing to separate it from the frozen ground. But once that was
done the box was in great shape & the hand carved stamp was
beautiful. I was happy to make it the first of what I hope will be
many stamps in my log.

mt walker
