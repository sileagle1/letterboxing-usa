From: mepanj (mepanj@kynd.net) | Date: 2003-10-19 15:13:17 UTC
Can't seem to let a weekend go without doing SOME letterboxing! Had
great luck this time finding all four letterboxes that we searched
for and even a hitchhiker.

Rest and Relaxation letterbox: Unfortunately not the first (so we
missed the special surprie) but well worrth the effort anyway.

Lucia Beach Letterbox: What a beautiful beach. Great for the kids
to climb around the rocks awhile.

Owl's Head Light Letterbox: found it easily and then enjoyed the
lighthouse and the views for a while.

Lobster Letterbox: If you are hungry this is the box to look for!
Did it in the dark (usually end up that way). Box needs some minor
maintenance but other wise in good shape.

Surfin' Santa Hitchhiker: In great shape. Found him enjoying the
Lucia Beach and will take him to new sights soon.

Also made my 20th find this weekend! Seems like a long way to 100,
but have no doubt that we will make it there.
