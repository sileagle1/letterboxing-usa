Well 2/3 of the Apple Dumpling Gang had a pretty successful day.  Cat was feeling so sick and stir crazy that she really wanted to go out but didn't want to leave the house.  (how's that for an oxymoron?)  We dodged a couple of bullets, got lost, called home for clues to new locations, hiked, sweated, and after all was said and done, snared 4 boxes.  

During our travels we found a lovely, lakeside home for "The Tramp," our latest hitchhiker.  

We want to take a moment to thank Cat's hubby, Conan the Grammarian for looking up the clues while we patiently waited in a parking lot.

Box on!
