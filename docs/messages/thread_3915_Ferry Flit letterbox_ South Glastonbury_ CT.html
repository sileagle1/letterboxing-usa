Ferry Flit
South Glastonbury Ferry
South Glastonbury, CT
placed December 4, 2001
by the Varrell family

Easy, <15 minutes

Directions: Route 2 East to exit 7 (left exit) onto Route 17 South. At
4th light, right onto Water Street. 2nd right onto Ferry Lane. Parking
area on left near end of the road.

Tilted tree
Furthest from
Parking place.
Six strides
Toward tides.
Look left,
Bottom, back,
Cozy cubby.

Bonus points if you see the sneakers! :-)
