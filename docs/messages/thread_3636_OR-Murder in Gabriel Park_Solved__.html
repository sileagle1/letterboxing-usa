For those of you who haven't seen my signature stamp in a letterbox
near you, It is the silhouette of Nancy Drew! And being such a big
mystery fan, I could not let the Murder in Gabriel Park go unsolved
for long!

Ryan only gives you 2 clues to get you started, and then it is up to
you to find the possible 6 letterboxes in the area which contain
pieces of evidence to convict the murderer!

Yesterday, I found all 6 pieces of evidence. So it can be done!

It was a lot of fun trying to figure out where the boxes might be and
what they might contain! The park itself is a small little park in
Portland and a mecca for dogs and their owners. I highly recommend
this letterbox hunt. Especially to those who enjoy a good mystery.

Amanda from Seattle
