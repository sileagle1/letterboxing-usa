I plan to hunt for boxes in the Storrs/Tolland area in Connecticut this
weekend, including the New England Authors series, Penguin Puzzle,

(vegetarian) or other area attractions that should not be missed, let
me know. I'll be staying at the HoJo in Vernon off I-84 and starting
out early Sat. morning. Thanks!
