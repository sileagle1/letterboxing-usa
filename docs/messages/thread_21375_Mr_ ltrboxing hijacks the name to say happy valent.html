From: ltrboxingrichters (ltrboxingrichters@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-14 16:09:51 UTC

My wife is the one who posts under this name, but I wanted to come
onto this board and publicly wish a very Happy Valentines Day to the
love of my life.

There are so many ways she loves and cares for our family.
Letterboxing has admittedly been something I've had to warm up to.
I've learned to enjoy the hikes and enjoying being outside as a big
part of letterboxing.

This is yet another way that our family spends time together. It is
no surprise at all that she has a hobby and passion for something
that brings our family together more.

Happy Valentines day, Kim. The greatest quest I've ever known is the
lifetime I'll spend getting closer to your heart. Keep giving me the
clues and directions, and I'll pursue it the rest of my life.
