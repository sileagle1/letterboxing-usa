WA NEW BOX (Maywood Park)

From: Canada Goose (canadiangirl@email.com) | Date: 2002-10-07 19:09:57 UTC-05:00
Well we spent some time on the weekend exploring Lewis county and found Fudricks two boxes along Highway 12. We wanted to add to the area so more folks will venture out. Great locations Fudrick and what a lovely area!
Here is our addition.

Near: City of Packwood
Where: Junction of Highway 12 and 123
County: Lewis
Very close to Fudrick's Letterboxes

Clues:
Don't bring your commercial vehicles here....stand near the left leg of this warning and take a reading of 140 degrees. From the landmark you see at this angle take another reading of 135 degrees while standing even between the pillars.
The tree you see at 135 is your last marker to finding the box. Stand on the north side of the tree and count 3 boulders over. Search beneath its mighty stature and you will find the treasure.

Placed by The Secret Gardners and the Canada Goose Oct 6th 2002
