Last month, Box 2 of the In the Park series went missing. It's been
replaced. It's in the same park, but with a new location, new clues,
and a variation of the original stamp. I'm counting it as a new plant
so those of you who found the original box, can count this
replacement as a new find.

Shell from Knights of Columbo made a beautiful website for me. The
clues for the entire In The Park series are at:
http://wildwinds.tripod.com/dod.html#park

My other boxes, Dead Authors, are at:
