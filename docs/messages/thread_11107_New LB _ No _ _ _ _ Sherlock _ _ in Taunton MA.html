Watson Pond State Park is a small , well maintained , and very picnic
friendly park located just under a mile East on Bay Street from Exit
9 off of 495 in Taunton MA . Once at the park head for the extreme
North West corner where the fence meets the water and you will see a
chest high stump . Look at the base under rocks to find
" No _ _ _ _ Sherlock ! "
