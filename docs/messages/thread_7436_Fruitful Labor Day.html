Fruitful Labor Day

From: wanderingsole143 (breedenbunch@insight.rr.com) | Date: 2002-09-04 02:50:04 UTC
The Breeden Bunch (Wandering Sole and Sole Searching) had a fruitful
day in Cincinnati yesterday :) Went to Woodland Mound Park and found
a bunch of boxes.
We found Franz's Spring Fever and Pond Life and 7 of 8 of Team
Jedi's Jurassic Workout. It was great fun. Got a little confused on
number 7 of the Jedi's.....could not find that box for the life of us.

The trails were absolutely packed yesterday, but we were as discreet
as we could be....though one little girl told her daddy we were lost
in the woods when she saw us off of the trail when we were returning
after replacing a box.

I was cracking up reading the letter from the letterboxing
Dad....hysterical! I do hope he is recovering :)
Sue
