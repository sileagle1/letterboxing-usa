Cheyenne Bottoms Letterbox
Planted by rtrw on October 4th ,2002

Difficulty - fairly simple
Time - almost a drive up once you arrive at the parking area.
Directions to starting place - K-156 to K-4 west. Look for a sign
for Cheyenne Bottoms on your left. If you come in from any other
side of the reserve, make your way to the parking area north of pool
3A.


Details you should know - This is an incredible bird and wildlife
preserve. You could spend a long time here hiking or bird watching
from your vehicle. The web address is
http://www.cheyennebottoms.net/. It's well worth checking out before
you visit.

Details you don't need to know, but might enjoy - The day I was there
I saw 15-20 different kinds of birds, some I knew, some I'd never
seen before.
