Hello all~
Took our first ever excursion to the letterbox on the middle fork river.  Awesome hike and great fun for 2 mom's with kids!  The only real disappointment was we never could find the letterbox!!!  (Does anyone have any idea of how many "grey rocks" there are by a river?)
 
Wondering if anybody out there knows if the box is still there?  My oldest was quite disappointed that we didn't find out "hidden treasure".  Letterboxes in Idaho are few and far between and we were so excited there was one within a couple hours drive of us. 
 
I'm thinking if we ever FIND one... we could learn how to plant them and get a few more in our state!!
 
Thanks for any ideas if the box is still there.... We looked and looked... despite it being rattlesnake season...
 
The Wildest Hare and family
