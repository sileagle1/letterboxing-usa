Cross Country Road Trip - Thanks

From: grumpygrinchy (ggboxer@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-07-08 05:38:36 UTC
We are nearly finished with our month long road trip from California to
New York and back again, with the letterboxing cruise to Bermuda in the
middle. We have found numerous letterboxes and have left a few along
the way. Thank you to everyone who placed letterboxes in fast, fun, or
unique locations all across the US. You have made our trip so much FUN!

The letterboxes that we have left along our route will be on their own
for quite awhile (like teenagers leaving the comforts of home.) Thanks
in advance to any boxers who report finding them or provide any first
aid if/when needed.

Grumpy Grinch
