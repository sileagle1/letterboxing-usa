From: twobentriders (marisafink@bsu.edu) | Date: 2005-02-08 22:26:44 UTC


Thank you to all of you who partipated in the survey I posted to test
its validity for use in my doctoral research on letterboxing. I
received a tremendous and speedy response to my request for survey
participants. Now I must review the survey and determine if it
obtains the information I hoped it would. I will also look at how
long it took people to complete and the rate of those who began but
did not complete it for some reason. This fall I will be launching
the updated version as I begin to collect data for the research. As
far as I can tell this is the first research study being conducted in
North America on letterboxing. Louise Donovan is doing a bachelor's
research project in Wales on letterboxing. Again, thanks for your
help. Happy trails to you all - watch this space for further updates.
