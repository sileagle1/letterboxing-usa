WA: Suggestion to Webmasters

From: Nail Family <c.nail@attbi.com> (c.nail@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-02-02 19:13:48 UTC
Looking over the LBNA website and trying to pull clues for a
friend I noticed a bit of a problem with the Washington State
listings. There are so many new boxes in the Pierce and
Thurston County areas (due mostly to DVN2RCKR and friends)
that is quite confusing trying to locate boxes in a specific area.

I propose a re-organization of the listings for WA State. Make a
new group for the listings called South Puget Sound. It should
be Pierce County/Thurston County/Mason County.

This would give the needed room for all the S.Sound boxes
cropping up and also give Seattle/King County its needed room
too. Also, this would keep boxes in Olympia from being lumped
together with boxes in Vancouver, WA. Geographically speaking,
this would be much more consistent.

Comments?

P.S. I know you webmasters and moderators are very busy, but
there are a lot of boxes still listed in the archives of the whats
new section that need to be added to the main list.
