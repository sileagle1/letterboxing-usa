C'mon, I at least narrow it down to the planet :-) Anyone who thinks this
box is easy might look into the hobby of Armchair Treasure Hunting, where
years are spent on a single hunt. I imagine the two hobbies will overlap
in some form someday (or at least appeal to some of the same people) -- in
fact, a couple of my boxes and one in progress sort of almost could pass
for the very low end of what goes on in that hobby.

I hope to see more hard boxes of this nature over time, while keeping the
mix of easy and medium ones too. There definitely is a gap in the hard end
of letterboxing and the easy end of armchair treasure hunting that I think
would be fun to fill and fun to participate in. We'll see ...
