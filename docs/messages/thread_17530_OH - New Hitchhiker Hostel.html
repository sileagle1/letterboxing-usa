Buckeye Hills Hitchhiker Hostel

*** Alive and well 1/11/2004 ***

In the Buckeye county seat named for Old Hickory, there is a
necropolis on a beautiful mountain. In the extreme northwest of this
necropolis is a large tomb for a King. Stand at the east end of this
tomb and take a bearing of 35 degrees. Approximately 24 paces away
(1 pace = 2 steps) is a tall tree. At the base of this tree on the
northeast side you should find what you seek under some fallen
limbs. Take care not to be seen and rehide carefully several boxes
have disappeared from this necropolis.

This is a hitchhiker hostel not a conventional letterbox.
The hostel has no stamp of it's own. There should always be at least
3 hitchhikers in residence here. Feel free to exchange them on a one-
for-one basis or leave an extra one if there's room but make sure
there are at least three still there when you leave. The hostel does
have it's own log. Please stamp in any hitchhikers you place in
residence here and note what it was exchanged for. Happy hunting!
