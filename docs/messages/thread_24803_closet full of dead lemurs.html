interacting with you strange people.

overfloweth I'm considering jumping back into the LB fray. Any
recommendations for Frederick or Baltimore area boxes that are just
screaming for a visit from an old letterboxer? (or 2 or 3 if I can
rope the rest of the ADG back onto the trail again)

And a shout out to Squirrel, Firefly, and Psychomommy! Any of you
wacky ladies still hanging about this list? If so we would love to
catch up.

From: Mary Erickson (tworstaggering@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-04-08 11:25:27 UTC-07:00
Box #2 of South Mountain Trinity is still in place. I
took the sodden logbook home one week and dried it
out. The landmarks getting to the box are a bit
different, but the final resting spot is the same.

I've looked for several other ADG boxes, but had only
one other success.

Let us know if you find others of yours still out
there!

I recommend Wood Thrush's Serpentine Barrens in the
greater Baltimore area. A good hike!

Mommo
