WVA: Seneca Rocks and Dolly Sods Letterboxes

From: groupfink (groupfink@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-11 04:12:02 UTC
We visited West Virginia at the end of June and searched for these two
letterboxes. We quickly found the Seneca Rocks letterbox, although it
was very wet ("a swamp," as a recent LBer wrote). So - we hope we did
not violate LBing ettiquette here - we took the stamp book back to our
cabin, dried it out and peeled the pages apart again. The next day we
went back, dried out the box, swapped the old ziplocs for new ones,
and added a new stamp book (the old one we also returned to the
letterbox, but left a note that folks should stamp in the new book -
the old one is just too fragile now). Hope we have improved the
situation without doing anything wrong!

As for the Dolly Sods letterbox, despite an hour of diligent searching
we never found it. And I checked the WV list tonight and notice that
it is no longer listed. Anybody know what happened to it?
