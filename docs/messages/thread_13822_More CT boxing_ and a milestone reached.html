More CT boxing, and a milestone reached

From: Mike Schneider (tehutika@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-25 15:59:20 UTC-07:00
Greetings,

Summer finally decided to show up! Taking advantage of
the wonderful weather the last few days, we took a
break from planting to go hunting!

Stratton Brook Spur Trail: Another nice box from the
Hike it or Bike it guys.

Avon: The directions to the trail head are written
like you are coming from Rt. 10, but the landmarks are
written as if you're coming from the opposite
direction. Once we got that sorted out, we had fun on
the trail and found our quarry. :-)

Spring Has Sprung: The huge trees were really cool.
The unbelievable amount of bugs was not!

Runaway Soccer Ball: Took a bit, but we found it. The
bike trails back there are numerous and somewhat
confusing.

East Vile Nirus: It's fun to be first!!

And, after all of the finding and planting of the last
few days, we have broken the PFX 200 barrier! On to
500!

Mike S.
P20 F178 X6 V6
