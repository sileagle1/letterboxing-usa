Title: "Fred: A Rose By Any Other Name". Placed 6-24-03 by
Salamander. Location: Ross County, Ohio. Difficulty: Moderate.
CLUES: This letterbox is located in Grandview Cemetery, Chillicothe,
Ohio. You must first find the "Patriot Square" Revolutionary War
Memorial. Stand in front of the marker and take a reading of 285
degrees. Go 23 paces at that heading to a smooth-trunked tree which
has seven lines of words carved into the trunk. Standing at the
west/northwest side of this tree, take a reading of 300 degrees. 37
paces at this heading will bring you to a large tree. Check around
the nooks and crannies at the base of the tree--- you should find
Fred! NOTE: Microbox--please bring pen and stamp pad.

Title: Taking 'Stock #1. Placed by Salamander on 6-24-03. Location:
Ross County, Ohio. Difficulty: Moderate. **This box is located in
Grandview Cemetery, Chillicothe, Ohio. CLUES: Enter the cemetery at
the Brookside Drive/Bellview Ave. entrance. Shortly after driving
through the gates, you will come to a road which turns off to the
left. Take this turn. Immediately after making the turn, look to the
right. You should see 2 large pine trees which would be about 25
paces away from the road if on foot. These 2 trees lie roughly on a
southwest/northeast line. Find a safe place to park and walk to the
northeasternmost tree. Standing at its south side take a reading of
187 degrees. Go 39 paces at this heading to an old splintered stump.
The box is hidden within the stump, under some leaves and pieces of
wood. ( And coming soon to a hiding place near you---well, within
driving distance, anyway--" Taking 'Stock #2"!! Watch for it!).
