CT: News from Butterfly in UT

From: lobstact (lobstact@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-06 05:55:22 UTC
Butterfly snail mailed a note from Utah that arrived today, Jan 4th.
She wishes everyone a Happy New Year & said they planted one box on a
bike trail along the Jordon River. Lots of snow there in Utah.
On her lay over in Baltimore? they had a Great time. 3 boxers showed
up, & Sam I Am actually made an Event stamp! She is bringing it back
to CT & making it a HH. I am sure she'll write more on her return.
Lobsta Lady

From: cadenza74 (cadenza74@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-01-07 02:44:27 UTC
Can I just say how much I enjoyed this get together! It made me
so happy to see the efforts of all you letterboxers out there for
those of us who don't have as many boxes to search for
ourselves.

Everyone was delightful! It was so fun to get together with
everyone and we got to top it off with a free carriage ride through
Salt Lake given by a friend of mine. For those who weren't able
to make it, we missed you. Hopefully the weather will be better
next time. For those of you who took the time to carve for us-
thank you soooo much! We loved the stamps and can't wait to
get the rest planted (the snow is really deep right now).

happy hunting,
Cadenza
