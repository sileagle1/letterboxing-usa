OH - New Letterbox - St. Benedict Box

From: teammaddawg (teammaddawg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-07-14 01:51:34 UTC
Location: Glenwood Gardens

Address: 10405 Springfield Pike located on Route 4 at the
intersection of Glendale-Milford Road in Woodlawn

Level: Easy

Need: Stamp, stamp pad, compass

Clues: Follow the Garden Trail to the 0.4 mile marker. Standing on
the marker take a compass heading of 300 degrees. Follow trail to
the bridge bordered by the split rail fence. From the beginning of
the split rail fence at the left side of the bridge take a heading of
220 degrees. See the 3 sisters at the stream's edge. At the base of
and behind the Siamese Twin will be the prize you seek.
