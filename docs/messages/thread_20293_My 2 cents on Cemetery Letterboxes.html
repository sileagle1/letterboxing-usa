My 2 cents on Cemetery Letterboxes

From: Lisa (LisMax1128@aol.com) | Date: 2004-09-13 00:36:57 UTC
They say if someone remembers a person who has passed they'll live on
forever. For me this was the whole idea behind planting my cemetery
box (Catch a Falling Star). I feel the idea behind the gravestones
is to memorialize a person so they can in some way live on forever.
If no one shares the beauty of these stones with their precious names
and dates, won't these monuments to our loved ones be for nothing?

Share the opportunity provided you by a person's loved ones to feel
the peaceful solitude of the cemetery. Try to feel the memories long
since forgotten. Respect them, cherish them, and bring others there
to share in what you felt.


Enjoy life,
Lisa
