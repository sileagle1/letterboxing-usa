Letterboxing in South Texas is improving, but very slowly. I think
there is just one new letterboxer besides me, and she lives several
hours away. Well, actually, two of my grown sons are interested, but
they have only talked about placing boxes, they aren't really
getting out there and doing it. And they don't make it a big
priority to go letterboxing, although when they do go, they love it
and 'plan' to go again soon, each time. :s

The biggest boost we get down here as far as letterboxing activity,
is when a few avid and generous letterboxers from other parts of TX
make trips down here and both look for and place boxes.

We have 'Snowbirds' here too, only they are called Winter Texans. I
don't know if there are any letterboxers among them or not....

Enjoy your winter boxing!
