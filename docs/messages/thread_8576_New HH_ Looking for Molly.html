From: bcostley (bobbyeubanks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-21 21:32:22 UTC
The "Looking for Molly" hitchhiker was dropped into a mysterious
letterbox on 10-21-02 by Brett. This hitchhiker features a hand
carved stamp of our dog Ginger. Ginger would like to play with the
famous Molly of Chuck and Molly. Hence finders are requested to move
the HH towards Connecticut to increase the change that Molly finds
it. If you can't move it closer to Connecticut, then please just
place into another letterbox.

This HH will have a web page soon (in the next couple of days) at the
following URL:

http://home.agalis.net/bcostley/Letterbox/LFMolly.htm

Re: [LbNA] New HH: Looking for Molly

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-21 20:26:53 UTC-07:00

I am continually amazed how well known "Chuck and Molly" have become. We don't do anything special. Just a couple of hikers and fellow letterboxers. Now with me, I might be remembered as being a bit odd. I'd just as soon be hiking with Molly than any human and I treat her as I would a person. I may even argue with her in the middle of a swamp or something. Molly, she's the friendly more social one. Not a mean bone in her body and a bit of a coward at times. But I still don't know why we are so well known. We do want to thank you for naming a hitchhiker after Molly. I will post if we ever do get it. Molly will most likely be there if I do. She goes letterboxing about 98% of the time that I go. Ginger looks like a great dog. Pretty well trained to sit with that biscuit on ther nose. Golden retreivers are excellent dogs. Usually smart too. Wish they could meet someday. Molly and I thank you......Chuck and Molly
