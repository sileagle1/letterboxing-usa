Don't give up on your hitchhiker. I recently picked up a hitchhiker and contacted the owner with an update on it and where I had found it. It had been all over and had only two pages left in the log before it was to be sent home. It turns out I was the first person to contact them with an update since it had been released three years ago!. They had considered it long gone. I hope you find that your H.H. is still truckin' and if anyone out there runs across the I'd Rather be Mad H.H. please keep it moving to fill in those last two pages and send it home!

Happy trails all you little H.H.'s out there!
