From: Dierdra Baumgart (fencing_lady@yahoo.com) | Date: 1999-09-19 19:02:15 UTC-07:00
Dearest Letterboxers,
This wanna be letterboxer in Wisconsin, just finished twelve, yes
12!, new letterboxes. I shall be placing them over the next week. Der
Madstamper will have some more work cut out for him. Remember,
Madstamper: I send hugs and kisses for a job well done. xoxoxo.
I have also counted the boxes that I have placed. 21 is the total
when I get these in their spots. I have found about 11 or so. Not bad
stats. Where will this put Wisconsin in the number of letterboxes?
Ahead of any of the New England or an out west state?
Well, that is all for now. The clues will come with pictures and
stamp images. I may just send them snail mail for there will be a lot.
Love you all. Good luck to the new addicts of our hobby. Addicts
love company. Die xoxoxo
