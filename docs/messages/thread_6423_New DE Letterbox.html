Brandywine Box

Northern Delaware, New Castle County, near Rockland, DE , placed by
Arbo on 7/12/02
A 30-45 minute easy hike located in the northern Delaware countryside
in scenic Brandywine Creek State Park.
Getting there: From I-95 South, Take exit 8 (Rt.202 North). Turn left
onto Rockland Road. Pass by the Rockland, DE Post Office. Follow the
road left-becomes Adams Dam road. Across the bridge and up the hill
you will find the park entrance. Start your hike at the park office.
