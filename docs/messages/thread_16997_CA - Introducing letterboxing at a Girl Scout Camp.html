CA - Introducing letterboxing at a Girl Scout Camp

From: Bob Prager (pragerr@alumni.princeton.edu) | Date: 2003-11-25 17:31:13 UTC
Had the opportunity to expose about 50 adult Girl Scout Volunteers
to letterboxing over the November 14-16 weekend. Unfortunately, the
boxes are on private property, so I don't think they count for PFX,
but we'll get more letterboxers out of this one! Everyone seemed to
have a great time. They were all surprised at the number of boxes
already in place "down the hill" in the Long Beach area (thanks to
Don and Gwen -- and Chowder -- and...)

If you'd like to see what the event looked like, I put the
information up at

http://www.geocities.com/pragerr/SkylandLetterboxing/letterboxskyland
.html
(the letterboxing part, including clues which require some knowledge
of the camp)

and

http://www.geocities.com/pragerr/SkylandLetterboxing/stampmaking.html
(making really simple stamps with "fun foam", including images of
some of the stamps the group came up with).

"Bob-baloo"
Volunteer, Girl Scout Council of Greater Long Beach
