Rosiebunny is a trickster

From: irishtinker (irishtinker@earthlink.net) | Date: 2002-11-10 14:48:54 UTC-05:00
Today my two and a half year old and I hid a new series in Chaplin, CT.
(Clues to be posted later) I was letting her hold one of the boxes while I
counted off paces to the hiding place. I turned around to get the box and
there is Rosiebunny smiling at me but she doesn't have the box.
"Where's the box?"
"I hid it!" RosieBunny answered proudly
"Where?"
"Over there. Under leaves" she replies pointing to the left side of the
trail.
With all the leaves off the trees I could not see anything odd and let's
face it a two year old can cover a lot of ground in just two seconds.
Finally after quite some time I did find it!!! I think it is my favorite
find!!!
