From: (Letterboxr@aol.com) | Date: 2001-02-27 16:04:34 UTC-05:00
Der Mad Stamper is on the loose again!!!

On Sunday, I planted der Lacamas Creek Letterbox in Camas, WA (just 12 miles
east of Vancouver, WA). Expect more of der boxes to appear in this area soon.
Meanwhile, clues for this one can be found at this URL....

http://members.aol.com/letterboxr/usa/lacamas.htm

Monday, I climbed to Council Crest, der highest perch in Portland. It was a
mild, clear, sunny day, and I could see everything from Mt. Ranier to Mt.
Jefferson. I planted two new boxes: der Marquam Trail Letterbox and der
Council Crest Letterbox. Clues for both are at this URL....

http://members.aol.com/letterboxr/usa/marquam.htm

The latter of these two was one of those where der planter of der box ALMOST
deserves credit for a F(ind), as well as a P :-) It's as if our friend
upstairs has already provided der ideal spot to hide der blasted thing, but
we gotta go out and find it.... and we don't even have any clues ta go by!!!
I wandered around for over an hour looking for the perfect spot to hide der
box at Council Crest. Wouldn't I be surprised if one of these days I reached
my hand under a rock and discovered that God had already left a letterbox
waiting there for me :-)

Peace,
Der Mad Stamper
