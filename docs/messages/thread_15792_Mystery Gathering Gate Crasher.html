Mystery Gathering Gate Crasher

From: Tom Noone (mntnlover@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-09-15 10:02:03 UTC-04:00
We are expecting many great visitors to our Mystery Gathering - flying
insects, orange bombshells, departing Kiwis and more. My medicine man has
had visions of an unwelcome guest who may be making her presence felt at our
Gathering. It is foreseen that the demon Isabelle will make Brother Wind
howl and the heavens weep unceasingly. Though she will probably be staying
far south of us we will wait and see if we need to hide from our unwelcome
visitor until she is long gone.

Should she visit to the lands south of us we will continue our celebration
in defiance of her just after her departure. Even if the heavens are
disturbed by her we can remain dry and sheltered in the white man's lodge
where we shall meet. As mentioned before the lodge contains strange, large
white metal boxes to keep your offerings cold as the White Bear's lair or
hot as the Sun Dog's tracks. If the Sun Dog resumes his unfettered trip
across the sky you may bring food to cook on the charcoal grills outside the
lodge also.
