OH-Gathering (COOL)

From: coolwan (coolwan@zoomtown.com) | Date: 2002-10-06 05:23:07 UTC-04:00
Wow it was such a nice day, Boy did we luck out.

After only getting to plant 3 boxes because of the Monsoon we had on Friday and the fact that they were talking about rain on Saturday, It turned out
perfect!!!!!

Clear Blue Skys a bit nippy at first but warmed up nice. We had a very nice turn out that lasted about 6 hours with a few adding on time as they were off to another park to look for more boxes.


For the First Gathering I would say you could not have asked for a better time. It makes me feel good that another Gathering next year will be
very very possible.

The only thing that went wrong for me was my Battery Died in my Camera so I did not get any pictures of the party, SO i would be so happy if someone E-Mailed me a few pictures so I can post them on my Web-Site.

Thanks Everyone for coming and look forward to next year! :O)
