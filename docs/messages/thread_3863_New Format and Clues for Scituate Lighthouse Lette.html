New Format and Clues for Scituate Lighthouse Letterbox

From: fred (lonemasswolf@covad.net) | Date: 2001-12-03 21:28:57 UTC-05:00

Letterbox Name: Scituate Lighthouse

Location: Cedar Point, Scituate Harbor, Scituate, Plymouth County, Massachusetts

Accessibility: The grounds around the lighthouse are open all year. The tower is open only during occasional open houses.

Placed:  03 August 2001 by the Lonemasswolf and You Inspire My Spirit.

All questions and comments should be directed to lonemasswolf@covad.net

Difficulty:  Easy, this is a drive by box.

 

For Lighthouse History and Photographs please visit www.lighthouse.cc/scituate/index.html

 

Driving Directions:

            SR 3A to First Parish Road in Scituate.

            East on First Parish Road for 0.7 miles.

            Continue East on Beaver Dam Road for 1.5 miles, to Jericho Road.

            North on Jericho Road for 0.8 miles to Lighthouse Road.

            Southeast on Lighthouse Road for 0.5 miles to parking area.

           

Letterbox Clues: 

Forty paces from the lighthouse door on a heading of 160O you will find a flat tan colored rock (part of the stone jetty). From here, the lighthouse door is at a heading of 345O, the beacon at the end of the stone jetty is at a heading of 140O and the point of the jetty across the inlet is at a heading of 195O. Sit, face east looking out to sea. The letterbox is at your feet hidden below many stones. 

When you return the box, bury it beneath many stones so that it doesnt wash out to sea. 

BE OF GREAT CARE THIS IS A HEAVILY VISITED AREA. BE DISCRETE.
