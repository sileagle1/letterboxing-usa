What we do is we print out our clues on nice paper-kind of watercolered
tannish-to make it look like a pirate map. We take pictures along the way...if a
clue leads us to something cool or we pass by a spot along the way we like.
We stamp the cluesheet, date and comment. When we get the photos developed
we put it all nicely in a photo album. Might not be the best way to do it,
but we like it.
