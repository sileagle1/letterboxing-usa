Baby Bear and I took advantage of another nice, cool day
to letterbox in the Tomball/Montgomery area. There are
several boxes in the area (Bear Contry, The Hunter,
Dragonfly, & Star Buck) and we placed two more on the
Lone Star Hiking Trail (Lone Star Hiker & Blue Norther).
We also found the Fluer-De-Lis HH and will release it soon.
I highly recommend this area for a day outing, or even a
weekend of backpacking on the LSHT, especially now that
the weather is cooler.
