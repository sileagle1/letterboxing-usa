OH> mudstumping in Southern Ohio

From: mudstumpers (mudstumpers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-24 22:54:15 UTC
We had a fabulous time in Cincy. Short as it was, we were able to
find 7 out of 14 boxes that we went after. Several were definately
missing others may have misled us due to the amount of orienteering
involved and time we did not have. We had lots of fun and Sunday was
a beautiful day.

Our successes included:
Lake Isabella
Sleepy Hollow
Coffee Break
IScream
Somethin' Fishy
Mirror Lake
and
Union Terminal

Thanks to you all for the fun and adventurous way to go site-seeing!
I went to lots of places that I haven't seen since living there! When
visiting family in the past, it was just visiting, eating and riding
roller coasters!

We were able to leave 2 goodies behind. And will be releasing a
hitchhiker that we were unable to place.

Clues will be posed soon.

mudstumpers
