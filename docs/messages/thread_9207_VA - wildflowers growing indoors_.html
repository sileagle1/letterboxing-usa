VA - wildflowers growing indoors!

From: be ma (bema57@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-11-16 20:58:44 UTC


What a perfect treat for a rainy day. Having had to delay our search for
the Scooby boxes and having broken a nail bowling with the kids... BeMa was
in need of some fun. So, off we went to the library in search of a
letterbox book! What a cool concept (and a nice library). It was hard
keeping the Ma part of BeMa library quiet. At one point she saw a platform
that looked like a stage....

We found the right spot for the box but it was gone! Daddy and Be sat down
bummed but Mommy and Ma went on a spying expedition! We found the boxers
logging in the box. We were too shy to say hello and when we looked in the
book later we saw stamps but no name! Oh well! Serves us right for not
stepping up and introducing ourselves (and getting our first X -- second
missed opportunity for that). We saw Mary's stamp "daughter of the old
dominion" who we email to but have not yet met. I didn't realize you were
there this morning. We missed you too! Anyway, we were trying so hard to
be secretive (and distract Be away from all the Pokemon magazines -- our tax
dollars at work...) that we might have forgotten to write our names too!

The box is very ingenious and we really enjoyed be able to keep boxing on
such a gloomy day -- Thanks!

BeMa
P2 (still have to plant that third one -- it's been waiting for 3 weeks
now) F17 firsts 2
