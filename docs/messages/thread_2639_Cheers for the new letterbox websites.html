Well, it seems that all that recent "flaming" on the LbNA site has
left not ashes and ruins, but creative fires burning. I think the
LetterboxingCentral.com and the Letterboxing.com sites are both
looking pretty good. Eric Mings and the and Coxes are coming up with
some interesting concepts and configurations on their sites and I
support them both in their efforts.

It's no secret that I, along with MANY others have an affinity for
letterboxing "regalia". It looks like Bill and Val Cox are going to
be coming up with some great letterboxing design merchandise. It's
their living, they're good at it, and we're lucky to have fellow
letterboxers "in the business"!

Keep up the good work, guys. This is getting exciting!

-Valerie
