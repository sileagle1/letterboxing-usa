"The Rings" Letterbox
Ogden, Utah
Placed on 11 April 2003 by A-Bear (jedaryn02@y...)

Directions: From Ogden travel north on I-15/I-84 and take exit 360,
Willard Bay. Turn left. When you come to the Willard Bay sign
above some rocks that's where you need to be. Do not turn left and
enter the Willard Bay fee area.

Trail: Easy-total drive by. The stamp commemorates what took place
in Utah during the winter of 2002.

Clues: Up at the right base of the Willard Bay sign lingers The
Rings. Please cover well with rocks when you are done as I'm not
sure how well this area is manicured.
