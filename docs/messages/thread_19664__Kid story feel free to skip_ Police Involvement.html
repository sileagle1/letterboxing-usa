Just a funny little anecdote along these lines. We were out boxing, with our
new tee shirts on, my way of trying to find an exchange,lol. When along the
path comes a father and son riding bikes. My littlest one jumped and looked
with horror at her new shirt and slaps her hand across the Lbma insignia on
the chest, realized she looked suspicious and so starts bellowing out the
National Anthem! Like that won't attract attention! Well, after she started
singing I realized what she was doing. (Picture the three of us standing
there dumbfounded, while the bicycle people go by giving us VERY strange
looks.) It took me 15 minutes to recover from the laughing attack I was
suffering from, I'm chortling now just remembering. So what do you think?
Did I stress the point that it supposed to be a secret well enough with my 5
yr old? Ahhh, kids are always good for a laugh, especially just when you
start to take yourself to seriously.
Kerri,
