I went up to the Thorton Burgess series in Hampden, MA to check on my boxes.
The second box (Mother West Wind) was infact missing. I have replaced it to

Diner in Stafford Springs. It is a good burger joint with a great atmosphere
and they have a juke box filled with 50's and early 60's music. Fun!
