From: Poison Ivy Hedge (poizniv@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-03-11 20:37:04 UTC

Some of you are going to hate this....

Here is a type of letterbox.

I'll call it a Trailer Hitch.

It does not get put into a box and hidden away with clues to find it.

It is handed from boxer to boxer who meet by chance or design.

It happens all the time with regular hitchhikers....but these will be
intentionally homeless.

There were a type of these in the PNW, called Cooties. They had to be hidden
in another letterboxers possessions or on their body. They were designed my
Camp Fire Lady and pandora for our Battleground gathering last summer. The
kids had a BLAST sneaking around trying nto slip them into people's things,
and the adults had a blast tyring to look "innocent" so the kids would slip
them to us.
