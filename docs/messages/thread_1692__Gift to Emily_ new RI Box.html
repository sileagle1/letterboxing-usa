"Gift to Emily" new RI Box

From: Bob Hopkins (Hopkinsr1@aol.com) | Date: 2001-01-06 15:24:39 UTC
The gift giver(s) write of Emily;

A smile so big, with giggles that grow.
A sad little pout and big tears that so easily flow.
Some hugs and some kisses always bring her around
The gift of her laughter the most wonderful sound.

That's our little Emily, she is so cute.
Stamp her box and send her a message.
She'll be happy to see them.
And that's why we all love her.

An easy mile, or just a little better, around picturesque Belleville
Pond in Ryan Park, North Kingstown, R.I.

To find this box, first find the Kurdziel's "Magic Tree House Series"
on the What's New page and grab a copy of "Afternoon on the Amazon".
Follow the directions to Ryan Park, and the boat launch area, and
from there, proceed to find "Afternoon on the Amazon". After stamping
in at that box, continue on to find the steps, and take a few minutes
to explore the old camping area. After exploring the camp, continue
heading southeasterly, turn left with the trail, and at the "Y"
intersection, continue right for about 25 paces, to a rock on the
right, and just slightly off the path. From that rock, take a bearing
of 120. You'll find the "Gift to Emily" box tucked away in that group
of rocks. You'll have to bushwhack through some briars, but after
meeting Emily, I think you'll agree, it was worth it. And she'll be
glad you came. After stamping in, continue on the trail. and when the
pond comes into view, keep it on your right all the way back to your
car. After crossing two wooden bridges you'll walk a dirt road back
to the boat launch ramp. Hope you enjoy our Park.

Bob, the "Wiz", Renee and Emily
