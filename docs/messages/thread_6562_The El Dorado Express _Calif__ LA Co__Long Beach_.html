The El Dorado Express ,Calif., LA Co.,Long Beach.

From: Gwen & Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2002-07-30 20:35:37 UTC-07:00
Long Beach,Ca.,placed 7/28/02 ,by Don & Gwen
 El Dorado East Regional Park entrance is from Spring St.near the El Dorado Nature Center . Parking fees are $ 3.00 during the week ,$ 5.00 on the weekend.  The Express runs Friday ,Saturday ,and Sunday. The park is open 7 am to sunset daily .Obtain a map at the entrance and proceed to area III . Follow the train signs . From the North end of the train loop walk 105 paces North to a small pepper tree.The letterbox ( a black cylinder ) is located at the base in a small hollow . If you have trouble locating the tree go to the"Lakeview Grove - Reserved Site " sign and walk 70 paces due East to the tree . The box is covered with dry leaves . The parking fee will allow you to check for the box at the Nature Center also . If you are looking for this letterbox after Easter let us know if you can't find it , there is a large egg hunt in El Dorado each year and that could place it in jeopardy. Happy Hunting ! Don & Gwen
