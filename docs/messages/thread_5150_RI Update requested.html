From: moonstone_baby (moonstone_baby@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-04-26 17:21:16 UTC
I did Great Swamp Monument this past Sunday (April 21) and it was
alive & well. I did the Asa Pond boxes & the Four Seasons boxes
about 4 weeks ago and they were in good shape then.

If you are looking for other places to go in RI I also did the Black
Farm boxes & Carolina South this past Sunday. Black Farm is a pretty
nice place to walk (My first time there) I did the Carolina North
boxes on Easter sunday and they were ok then.

If you have not done the new Hills Are Alive letterboxes on the South
County bike path you should. I did these this past Sunday too.
There are 7 boxes in 4 miles. On a bike it's a breeze. The last box
ends by the Asa Pond boxes so you could combine those into one
hike/bike.

I hope this helps.
