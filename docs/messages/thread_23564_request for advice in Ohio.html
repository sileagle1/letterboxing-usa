From: Warren (bayboxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-20 22:40:12 UTC
there are a couple of boxes in Huntington Park directions see clues
for NE Ohio I-90, SR 252 north to Lake Rd, west to park 2-3
miles,there is a beach, picnic area, interpretive center,
planetarium, ice cream, or try the Cleveland Metro Park, I-71 exit
Columbia Rd SR 252, e on Country Club to old Columbia RD, south/right
to Lewis Road, left on Lewis (east), left on Valley Parkway, to Rocky
River interpretive center, there are a few nearby boxes, or Cuyhoga
Valley National Recreation Area= I 480 to I-77 south, exit route 21
or brecksville rd, better google or check map after that, there are
boxes at Canal Park, but there has been recent flooding, should be ok
by now. happy trails bayboxer
