Box #2: "Toe" Path
Location: Griggstown, Somerset County, New Jersey
Placed 04/10/2001
Clue page: http://members.aol.com/pcletterboxing/clue2.htm

Begin your journey at the Griggstown Mill, located at the
intersection of Griggstown Causeway (Route 632) and Canal
Road, Griggstown. There is a parking lot on the south side of
Griggstown Causeway.

Start at the two millstones on the tow path along the canal. Walk
south-southwest down the path until you see the stone mile
marker (you'll see the numbers 20 & 24 etched into the top,
albeit weatherworn).
