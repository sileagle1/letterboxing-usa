In honor of his birthday buddy, Tempus Fugit, our tadpole Owen
decided on Sunday to find the van Gogh letterbox in Seneca Creek
State Park in Gaithersburg, MD. True to form, he slept through the
entire hike while Mama and Dad Frog mucked their way through a whole
lot of mud (figures he'd pick a trail adjacent to a stream after all
the rain - the kid has a sense of humor, I tell you.) But despite
the slick and gooey trail, the Forest Frog parents were really
excited to get out again after so many months of waiting. Big
Brother Frog was at a picnic with grandpa that day, but that's just
as well, since he might not have done so well with the mud. Thanks
to Donna and Chuck for a fun hike!

Mama Frog
