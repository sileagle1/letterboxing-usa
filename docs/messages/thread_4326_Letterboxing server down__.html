Ahhh! This happened to me a few months ago after the
hubby downloaded something from Address.com. I was
trying to get to the letterboxing.org site and was
constantly rerouted to an x rated site and/or a
"calendar girl" site.

I finally received and email from the company whose
site I was directed to telling me that there was
something at this Address.com site that infected my
computer so that instead of getting error messages
when I couldn't connect to a site it would reroute my
computer to the site chosen by whomever placed this
bug at Address.com.

'things' (for lack of a more specific term) relating
back to Address.com (which I thought I had completely
deleted from my system using the uninstall function).
After that I've had no problems whatsoever.
