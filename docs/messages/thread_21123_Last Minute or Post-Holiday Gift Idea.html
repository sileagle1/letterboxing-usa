From: Brenda Tassava (bktassava@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2004-12-24 15:17:01 UTC

I was at Prizm Art Supplies yesterday, picking up some last minute
gifts when I spotted the ultimate carving lamp. It was actually
called the "Ultimate Lamp" or something to that effect. It has a
lamp, on an adjustable arm that has a daylight bulb in it. There's
another adjustable arm with a large magnifying glass, and a third
arm with a clip for keeping your original drawing handy. The base is
compact and takes up little space, yet has little "caddies" in the
base for storing small supplies (blades, etc.). I believe the cost
was around $90.
