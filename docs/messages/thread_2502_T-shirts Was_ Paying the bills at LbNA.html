T-shirts Was: Paying the bills at LbNA

From: Jeremy Disch (jdisch@emerald.tufts.edu) | Date: 2001-06-05 11:07:59 UTC-04:00
Yet another idea, although I think it could be shunned. But I will put it
out there anyways...

There is a website out there (www.cafepress.com) which allows you to
create a storefront for logos on T-shirts, mugs, mousepads, etc... They
have a set amount of prices for printing (you can order one at a time).
They do all the order processing, charging, printing and shipping.

All you do is supply the artwork.

They allow you to increase the price to whatever you want and they will
cut a check for the difference. Or if you want you can just print
t-shirts, mugs as their base cost.

You could picture possible tacking on an extra $1 which will be used to
support the cost of running the website.

Just an idea, although I understand many will shun the idea of supporting
coorporate greed...

Jeremy
