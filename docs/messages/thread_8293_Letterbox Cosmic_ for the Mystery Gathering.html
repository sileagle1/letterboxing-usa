Pole Shift

There are those among us with knowledge of upcoming events cosmic,
prophets seen by so-called science and by temporary authority as
unreliable, and therefore harried and persecuted, harried and
persecuted as were the now-respected seers and saints of old, but who
know, who really KNOW and who understand, who truly UNDERSTAND the
nature of the upcoming disaster which will overwhelm the Earth and
all who call her home, the clear and unavoidable disaster which we
are powerless to avoid and which is perhaps retribution for our
mistakes, our great and unchangeable mistakes. Secrets are kept by
the governments of the United States, Russia, and Japan, secrets kept
which if revealed to the public would cause widespread terror and
panic, which if revealed would release a terror and a panic such as
has never been contemplated. There are those, however, those who know
the truth, who understand the foreshadowing, and who are not afraid
to speak in the face of overwhelming and insurmountable forces, speak
loudly and forcefully, speak to explain the coming events, the events
of destruction and to explain, to explain clearly and for all to hear
who would listen, the single option, the only possible option for our
survival, the survival of the people of this wonderful, this generous
planet.

There is a heavenly body previously unknown in our Solar System, a
planet large and gaseous, a planet on a steeply elliptical orbit,
perhaps a loose galactic body snared by the sun's gravity, and this
planet is now on a collision course with earth. Sweeping in silently
and unseen by all but the most sophisticated radars and telescopes,
this object was code named "Planet X" by NASA and the FPCS when
detected in 1967 but has been known for millennia by prophets and
bears the name Nibiru and an ancient and mystic culture of creatures
of foresight and holiness, the Nibiruiata.

The Nibiru Letterbox
Through the gate in the Northern fence, find a line of standing pipes
that serve as mounts for amateur telescopes on clear nights. Line
them up and follow that bearing (200*) down south to the edge of the
grass and look a few feet into the bushes. The answers you seek are
under a flat rock.

When a tidal wave is forecasted, we evacuate the beach. Foreknowledge
of Nibiru leaves us just one option.
