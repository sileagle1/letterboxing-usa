I went to Side-By-Side a few days ago and it was very difficult to stay on
the path at the beginning. Not to mention that I forgot my compass (again).
But I did make it to the box after a lot of uncertainty. I almost gave up a
number of times. And I also realized that the reason the path is so hard to
follow over the boulders is that the box planters wanted to guide finders
along the safest path. Did you find "BL?" I didn't, but I did find the
correct "JL" tree.

I had a similar experience with the new Maydale box. Although the bamboo
forest is small, it has a number of paths, any of which could or could not be
the correct path to the box.

My advice is to keep pushing your way forward through the parks and look for
landmarks. You'll definitly notice the two logs, the "pregnant" tree and the
flat sandy area for Side-by-Side, and you'll see the footbridge for Maydale.

Good luck.
