From: Pete (jiggs111@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-11-27 20:47:22 UTC
Hi all!

The Jiggs clan had the opportunity to spend a few days in the Lake
Tahoe area, and were able to check out a few boxes at the same time!
Here's the latest...

SACRAMENTO GOETHE PARK - Alive, well and very well-visited (we "LET"
doublesAJ and Ol Blue find this onew first!!)

SPENCER's BONE (Ripon, CA) - another great box - first finders (Ha -
take that, 2AJ!!)

BRIDGEPORT - alive, well and STILL awaiting its first visitor!

BEAR CUB (Donner Pass) - hibernating. Trail was accessible, but he's
buried under abour 2 feet of snow!

EMERALD BAY - WOW! Super location on east side of the Lake! Lots of
visitors! Found the RRR Hitchhiker too (bonus day!)

EAGLE ROCK - We're sure the box is probably fine, but the approach
paths were snow-covered, so will wait until spring.

LAKE TAHOE NINI BOX - alive and well, in an absolutely gorgeous
setting on the North Shore of Lake Tahoe! Well worth the "easy" 15
min hike straight up!!!

There you have it!
