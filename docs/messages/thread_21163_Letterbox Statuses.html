Louisiana just had tweenty-five boxes added to the LbNA site, more
to come. The clues have been there the whole time if you knew where
to look. Thanks to Jay doing alittle checking we now can get them
to link propperly. If you have boxes planted in Louisiana please go
to the LbNA site click on member services then edit boxes. Once on
the boxes edit page delete the word Parish in the county box. This
will allow your boxes to show up when someone uses the LbNA map to
search. Thanks Jay.

*****Why yes! We've got a gathering planned for March
19th at Brazos Bend State Park. It's a great
opportunity to meet area letterboxers and find some
boxes and make some exchanges. We had our first one
in Texas last year in Bastrop, with somewhere around
35 attending! This year I would imagine there will be
a lot more people since the number of boxers in Texas
has roughly doubled in the last year, so that probably
means double the fun too!

Here's the link:

http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/Events/TLRE/

If you want to check out what we did last year, here's
the link for that:

http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/Events/FTLE/


Silver Eagle and Baby Bear have taken on the task of
pulling it together - and they are awesome!

Lucy
