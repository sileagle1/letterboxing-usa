John and I had a lovely time in the McMinnville area the past couple
days and we found three letterboxes while out that way!

The Spruce Goose, recently placed by Annette is alive and well at Galen
McBee Airport Park. This is a swell little park, kind of reminds me of
Riccarton Bush in New Zealand somewhat. The clues to this box were IDEAL
and the stamp is adorable!

Bald Peak State Park placed by Funhog was a great find. Clever, clever
girl with those clues, Karen! And the stamp is quite humorous!

Bald Peak State Park placed by the Bendicksons was VERY REWARDING. We
were on the wrong trail at first, but eventually backtracked and found
the "cornerstone" and then were on the right path. This stamp(s) is
absolutely beautiful and the hiding place is fantastic.

Thanks to all for a wonderful Saturday of letterboxing. Anyone who
visits Oregon's wine country should definitely add finding these
letterboxes to their list of adventures.

Best regards,

Amanda Briles
