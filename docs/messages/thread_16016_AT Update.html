AT Update

From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@pocketmail.com) | Date: 2003-09-26 03:12:26 UTC
Just thought I'd let everyone know--I have finished the Appalachian
Trail! Yesterday afternoon, September 24th, I stood at the top of
Katahdin, pulled out Amanda's cell phone, held it up to my ear, and
said, "Can you hear me now?" Even got a picture to prove it... ;o)

Anyhow, I'm now visiting with my cousin in the Boston area for the
next week or so and might have a day or two available if anyone from
this area wants to meet up with me. I'm not sure what days I'll be
available, but I'm sure something can be worked out.

And I'm also looking into hiding a REALLY cool letterbox out here,
unlike the relatively crappy ones I've been hiding on the Appalachian
Trail where the stamps were pre-carved and excessively small since
I'd have to carry it on my back and space and weight were limited. I
can't let you all think those ones I've left on the AT are the best I
can do, because you'd all shoot me if you thought that. =)

Carpe diem!

-- Ryan

PS. Why yes, I *have* hiked the entire Appalachian Trail. =)
