Jacksons Square
Jackson, Wyoming
Teton County
Placed by A&J-Bear on 28 December 2002

Directions: Go to the Town Square at the intersection
of Broadway Ave and Cache St in Jackson, Wyoming.
There is 2 hour free parking around the Town Square.

Level of Difficulty: Easy and flat.

Jacksons Square Clues:
Begin at any of the Elk Antlers Arches and head to
the riding cowboy in the center of the square. 240
degrees from Teton County Veterans Memorial Sign find
the large standing rock. Mr. Colter directs you to
the post at 330 degrees. Under the boardwalk is
Jacksons Square.

Please read the waiver of responsibility. Ensure you
hide the box well again so its not easily seen by
non-letterboxers. Continue onto the Elk Letterbox.
