Welcome, Lynne, from another HSer. My ds is 10 and my dd is 7. Have I
seen your name on some HS boards? You can answer off list if you like.

My motivation was similar to yours. We have gone out 4 times now and found
boxes on all 4 occasions. All 4 were places we have never visited before -
and I thought we got around. *wink* I will let older and wiser voices
answer most of your questions, but I will tell you how we came up with our
name. We sat around the table and offered suggestions. We came up with 9
possibilities. Then we let everyone vote for their top 3 choices. This
narrowed it down to 3. One of the choices was on everyone's list, so we
went with that - God'screationchasers. A little long, but it works for us!

Shalom,
Kaycee
