Well I found the Hancock Hill Letterbox today! What a beautiful day to be
letterboxing!

I thought I knew right where I was going and I forgot my map at home so I
decided to try it without it. I had the directions though. As it turned out
I was wrong where I was going. But after much persistence (just plain
pigheadedness if you really want to know!) I finally found the correct
marker to proceed from on the directions. I learned my lesson though...bring
the map! A friend to box with would be good too since I did this one on my
own since my letterboxing buddies (my sons) were at school. If I had my son
with me he probably would be lecturing me about forgetting the map. ;)

All in all a great day. I felt victorious after finding it! :)
