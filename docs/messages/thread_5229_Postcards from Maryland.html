Planting out of state boxes can be iffy. I never know when I will get back
to check on them. So getting news from their visitors is wonderful! In the
past two weeks I have recieved three postcards from boxes in Maryland that I
planted on past trips down. It is such a pleasure to meet new 'boxers and to
see new personal stamps. Putting self adressed stamped post cards in my
boxes has been a real plus! Thanks to all those who take the time to write a
note, stamp their "stamp" on the card and mail it off!!
Enjoy Life! 'Box On!
