From: Brian, Ryan & Lori (bconnoll@marksonrosenthal.com) | Date: 2003-11-10 15:27:07 UTC
We had been hearing a lot of good things about the Headless Hunters
boxes on the Cape and decided to check them out for ourselves . We
started at his new Whydah box at the Marconi site in Wellfleet .
Excellent work ! We knew at this point that we were in for a
treat . Oh ! Will , you might want to check on this box for reasons
I won't divulge here . We spent the rest of the day traveling about
the Cape finding all of his boxes , plus a few more .

For a young man who relies on his bicycle to get around,the
Headless Hunter has done a remarkable job on these boxes . Great
stamps , ingenious hiding spots and some great historical info (
who knew you could take an airplane to the drive - in ? ) ! We are
anticipating the day he will obtain the means of spreading his boxes
over the whole of New England . If this is the future of
letterboxing , then I would say things are in good hands .

Thanks Will !!

Brian
