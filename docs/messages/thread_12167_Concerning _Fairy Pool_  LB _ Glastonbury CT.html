The status of Fairy Pool Letterbox:

I searched for the box on Saturday and found it after a bit of brookside hiking! It is a neat location once you determine the correct stream. Unfortunately, the weather was not very kind to the box. It was completely filled up with blue tinted water. Everything was completely soaked. The book and accompanying info was in bad, really bad shape. I dried things off as well as I could and made a temporary book. It looked like it had been a long time since anyone had been there. Could the owner stop by and check on it sometime?

Happy trails,

Josef Carlson
