Welcome Susan ! The NPS will confiscate any boxes inside a National Park
that it can find on our web site. If you are planning to place one there
make sure the box isn't called " Shenandoah Valley " and you had better make
the clues some what difficult to figure out. NPS workers are even logging
some boxes in BLM areas here in Calif. I guess it is for future confiscation
