Day of the Dragon King will be on our "to do" list.

And just yesterday, Laurette, Dan and I biked the Kingston bike path and
found every note in the Hills are Alive series. Great directions! Who knew
there we hiking trails there? Not us! Some of the paths were rough on my
street bike, even walking it. We finished at dusk and the woods were alive
with fireflies. One drawback: finishing late will require one to hike or
bike back amidst clouds of gnats. All boxes were in place and in good
condition except for the one "down the steps" (Stairway to Heaven? ),
which was a little wet inside. I felt that the series would be more fun on
foot, simply because there was very little biking between boxes, and some of
the path boxes required leaving the bikes behind. The ride back was fast
and fun as the light disappeared, if you don't mind gnats up your nose.
BTW, we found a couple of hikers, too. Too bad we didn't have a real Time
Machine that would turn the clock back a couple of hours for us, but the
Shore Birds flew away with us.

Thanks!

Mary
