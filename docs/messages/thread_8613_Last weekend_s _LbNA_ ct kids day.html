Last weekend's [LbNA] ct kids day

From: Josef Carlson (jose_ole1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-22 13:34:02 UTC-07:00
It had a wonderful, fun-filled (FUNDERFUL?) time
meeting so many of you at the CT Kids Day Gathering in
Rocky Neck Park. It was a unique chance to meet face
to face with those whose letterboxes and notes I've
come across in the past year. I've thoroughly enjoyed
this hobby and look forward to future hunts. I am SO
impressed with the quality of clues and stamps around
here! I really admire the creativity and talent that
went into them! I can't wait for the next chance to
meet/gather/socialize/compare notes/share war stories
with you all! If anyone wants to meet up for a hike,
just say the word...my bag is packed and the "FALL
orange fashions" are in the car.
Peace! Happy trails!

Josef "Jose" ("Hello...from Joe") Carlson
