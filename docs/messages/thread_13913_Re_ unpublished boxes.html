From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-30 19:00:41 UTC

As far as unpublished boxes goes, there are alot of them out there.
It is all about WHO YOU KNOW! If you go to gatherings, sometimes
there are clues passed around that never make it to the LBNA Map. If
you are friendly with another letterboxer, they may let you in on
some unpublished boxes that they know about. Some boxes are in spots
where a high volume of traffic is not wanted...so the clues for them
are spread around to a select few people.

Some boxes are placed for personal purposes. A birthday present to a
dear friend or an aniversary of an important family event. These are
private letterboxes that are only shared within a family or among a
close group of friends.

--Amanda from Seattle

If you're ever boxing in Richmond, VA, I have copies of a cuckoo clue
floating around in a few boxes to an unpublished cuckoo box I did. The
instructions on the little laminated cards that are the cuckoo clues
ask boxers to kindly leave the clue they've just found in the box that
they found it in or to move the clue to another Richmond-area box, but
I would rather not have the clues being planted in boxes outside the
Richmond area. It just seems easier on all involved.
