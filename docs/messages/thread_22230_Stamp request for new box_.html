From: cpascott (seh-letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-07-07 16:24:37 UTC
Hi all!

The success of partnering with some of you great carvers is exciting,
and I am eagerly developing new boxes to place!

So far two of my four boxes contain stamps carved by others -- Willard
Clock (placed) and The Lady in Black (scheduled be placed in late
August). Many thanks to Choi and Scarab, respectively, for their
magnificent artwork!

I have another box I'd like to place, and thus another call for
someone willing to carve.

The box is entitled "Walden" and will be placed at Walden Pond State
Reservation in Concord. My research on LbNA and AQ has not turned up
a box in this area, so I'm claiming it! (If someone knows of a box
there, please let me know as I wish not to duplicate).

Any stamp dealing with serentity, contemplation, nature,
transcendentalism or, of course, Henry David Thoreau are welcome and
appreciated. As always, carvers are fully credited on both the box's
clues published on my website and listed in LbNA and AQ as well as on
the box and journal itself.

If you're interested, e-mail me -- and thanks in advance!
