FOUND Letterbox, Southington CT

From: Jeff P. (signal73@cox.net) | Date: 2007-11-14 17:46:42 UTC
Hi all,

My name is Jeff, I am a geocacher. I have found a few letterboxes also. I was recently out
Geocaching in Panthorn Park in Southington CT and my search brought me into and area that
I found a Letterbox in. The container was opened and all the contents where spread around
the area. I did not find the stamp that was in the container, but I was able to recover the log
book (although damaged severely from being in the weather) and the container and a few
items that were on the ground in the area.

The name of the Letter box had worn off and I could not figure out whose it was by searching
all the clues to all the Southington Letterboxes in the area.

I was just wondering if anyone here has a Letterbox in that park or knows anyone that does.
If no one claims it in a couple of weeks I am just going to get rid of the stuff.
