From: midlandtrailblazer (shadowrose@minister.com) | Date: 2007-07-19 12:44:54 UTC
Hello Tammy! I am in Charleston WV. We have a cabin at Minnehaha
Springs and go to Watoga often. In fact, I've already hit all the
boxes there and left a little something on our last trip, that as far
as I know no one has found yet. I do have a goal of planting a box on
each trail of the Arboretum associated with the flora that is
labelled. But I plan on walking them all first. I think that a bunch
of boxes in one locale like that makes a nicer trip -- you can just
plan on going to one park and spend all day walking the trails, maybe
even camp out. I also want to go to Ann Bailey's lookout, because
I've never been. We do have pic in the cabin supposed to be a view
from the lookout that was in a newspaper or magazine....

midlandtrailblazer
