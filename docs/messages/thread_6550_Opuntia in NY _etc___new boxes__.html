Finally getting around to announcing that a few of our boxes that
have been out there for a while have now been posted on our webpage:

http://alum.wpi.edu/~p_miner/Letterboxes.html

across the river from West Point, and #20 is Part 2 of the Polish

Men" did last October, but meanwhile please don't go looking for Part
1 unless we report that it has been replaced. In the meantime we
wonder who will find Part 2! Have fun - baw sie dobrze!
