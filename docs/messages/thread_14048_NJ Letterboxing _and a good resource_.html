Hey,

I'm fairly new to this letterboxing thing, but have fallen in love
with it already. Rather unfortunately, I have so far been limited to
South Jersey. Letterboxes aren't as sparse here as out in the
midwest, but they aren't as thick as in Connecticut, either. Also, a
lot of them are mystery boxes...and not easy ones.

I did find an excellent resource for finding some of the more obscure
places in South Jersey, and thought I'd pass it along. I also added
it to the links page: It's a group called "Ghost Towns of Southern
NJ - South Jersey and Pine Barrens history, ghost towns" and the url
is http://groups.yahoo.com/group/ghosttownsofsouthernnj/

I was also curious about any other NJ letterboxers out there. I'm
not comfortable tramping through woods alone, and my boyfriend will
doubtless eventually get tired of chasing tupperware boxes. He's
not a LBer. Anybody from NJ, especially the Cherry Hill area?
