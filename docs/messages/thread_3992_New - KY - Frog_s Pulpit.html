Name: Frog's Pulpit
Difficulty: Easy mile walk on paths
Near Ft. Wright, Kenton County, KY
Placed 25 December 2001 by
Flyfisher (geoflyfisher@yahoo.com)

This letterbox is a modified microletterbox with stamp, stamp pad,
and log book contained in two 35mm film containers. The log is only
1.5 inches wide, so don't expect a large stamp to fit. Bring a small
cousin.

With the stream gurgling or babbling (depending on one's proficiency
with ancient tongues) nearby, the frog's bully pulpit is filled on
quiet spring evenings with either local talent or with the bass notes
of circuit riding bullfrogs. The choir of cicadas breaks in to
enthrall the congregation, and the message is deeply infused in the
listener.

Far from the Valley of Frogs, pioneers sent out by the ancient order
have settled this little glade. Their northern cousins would be
happy to know their Kentucky cousins are prospering, growing,
learning, and making lots of little froggies!
