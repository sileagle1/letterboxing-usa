Box #5: Sneak Attack
Location: Somewhere in New Jersey
Placed 04/27/2001
Clue page: http://members.aol.com/pcletterboxing/clue5.htm

January 3, 1777...

Stand in Tom's backyard. With your back to the carriage house,
go straight down the hill and enter the trail.

Follow the path Washington took that led him to victory. At the
intersection of many paths, take the path at 125 degrees.
Eventually, it will bend to the left; find the tree where Boris is
immortalized. From that point, continue along the trail 35 paces.
Then, start walking at 155 degrees until you find the tree with a
hollow at its base.
