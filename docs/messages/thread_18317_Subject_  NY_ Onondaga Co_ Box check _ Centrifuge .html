From: catbead1 (libby@twcny.rr.com) | Date: 2004-04-04 03:41:37 UTC
I went out and checked on our boxes in Green Lakes SP this
week and am happy to say that they all survived the winter.
Even the one that I thought was lost last fall. Turns out
it was tucked away in a spot not quite where it should have
been, and was well hidden by last fall's thick layer of
leaves.

I was expecting to find the Crystal Dye inkpad I left in
one box to be useless after the long cold winter, but
other than the pad starting to separate from the box, it
still had ink and made a good impression.

We'll be getting out to check our other boxes soon and will
have some new additions to announce before long.

The little 50mm centrifuge tube micro-boxes came thru with
flying colors. I placed my order for a case and expect to
see them in about 10 days. If anyone wants to see what they
look like I have some pictures with more information here:
http://www.catbeadmischief.com/microbox.html
Some quick information: I ordered the ones with a place to
write on, so the cost will be higher at 2 for 29
plus shipping from me to you. Since this is my cost, there
will not be any sales tax for NY. If anyone wants to give
them a try, contact me off list or follow the email link on
the above page on my website.


Cheers,
catbead
