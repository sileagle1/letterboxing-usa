I reconn'd this site recently and there are quite a few picnic
tables scattered around this park. Major renovations are underway
so the running water/bathroom situation isn't the most pleasant
(doubt they'll be finished by the time this event occurs) but the
views at the park are still incredible. There are quite a few small
regional parks not very far from the Beach Park so creating
interesting and fairly local letterboxes won't be that difficult
either. The playground is decent and is quite a distance from the
water so keeping young kids from water's edge won't be as difficult
as some other venues. The most picnic tables in the park are
nearest the playground so that would work well for 'self-contained'
babysitting!

For those staying overnight in the area, nearby
hotels include a Ramada Limited about a half mile away
from the park (I actually stayed at it in back in 1997--not 4 stars
but provides a bed, shower & cable) and there are a bunch of nice
little restaurants in the area, too--including one of our family
favorite fish n chips type locales with outstanding blackberry pie
(Wally's Chowder House). If folks are coming from a ways and need a
free place to stay for the night, we also have a few spare rooms in
our house which is about 25 miles away from the venue but very near
to MAJOR letterboxing locales (40 boxes within a 5 mile radius I
think?). Just let us know sometime before Friday.

Hope to see you there!!! At last count, I had about 35 folks say
they plan on attending!!!
