CT: Ledyard Oaks @ Night!!!

From: Steve (shol3039@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-17 01:49:27 UTC
We went out after dinner and some errands and did the Ledyard Oak
trail. Probably should have started a little earlier too! Did well
for the first half but where the blue trail runs by the road is
TOTALLY flooded for a looooong stretch. Back tracked a bit and came
upon a guy running the trail with his dog and he showed us the way
to hop on the road and then jump back in the woods and on the trail
again. It got a weeeeee bit dark and we needed the flashlight at
the second box and all the way down the steep hill and back to the
big oak. Got the 3rd one with no problem and then got the heck out
of the woods! Finally out of there at 8:50 and it was VERY dark by
then. Good thing I thought to bring that darned flashlight!!!

All boxes in good condition, dry, logs have room. Last activity was
early may in #1 and #2. June 2nd for #3 box.

-Steve and Heidi
