This Yahoo Group is for the purpose of sharing information and discussion concerning Smallpox vaccination. Members are free to discuss their experiences with Smallpox vaccination and opinions concerning it. All those who read and share the information need to remember that this is not a medical clinic. The information is meant to be factual but is neither medical advice nor prescription. Any opinions shared by members are not to be taken as medical advice. All those who read and share the information need to consult their own healthcare providers for advice on their own vaccination status. As always, members are responsible for their own statements and should remain civil with others. Please do not offer or recommend anything for sale to the group or you may be deleted. Kids should make sure they ask their parents' permission before they join the group or view images. Conspiracy theorists need not apply.
