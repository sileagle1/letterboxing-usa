Started of my day with finding the Psychobunny HH at the Bear Mt Reservation
in Danbury! I was thrilled !! Will be happy to move it along! All three
boxes at Bear Mt are in good shape. There is no longer a stump at box 2,
however.

I then headed to Putnam Memorial State Park in Redding where I found all
three boxes. The first box is in poor shape - if I return to that area next
weekend I will replace it.

Next I tackled the Three Boxes in the Danbury Area with no problem. These
had eluded me in the past so I was really happy to find these.

I ended my day finding the four boxes at Tarrywile Park in Danbury. The
third box was quite the hike but enjoyable. All boxes there are fine as
well.

Thirteen boxes in total today plus the HH - I'm really liking this
letterboxing!
