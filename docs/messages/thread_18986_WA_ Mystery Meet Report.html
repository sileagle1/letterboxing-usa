I know I'm omitting an entire family but, unfortunately, I never got
their signature stamps. I think the final count was mid-low 40s.

Meeting all the area letterboxers from near & far was awesome! It's
so cool to finally get to put faces with signature stamps and email
exchanges. The food was great and the weather held up perfectly.
Just threatening enough to discourage all the locals away from the
park but dry enough to be able to enjoy fellowship, stamp exchanges
and to seek out a few boxes in the area. Also, one of the traveling
letterboxes that Team Rayvenhaus brought was ABSOLUTELY AWESOME!!!
Not sure if they want me to reveal the details of the box on the
board but, hands down, this letterbox was one of the best I've ever
seen! The message, concept, stamp, container, craftsmanship,
artistic effort--you name it, was simply impressive and the final
product was stunning! Definitely above & beyond the call of duty!!!
A great tribute to some true heroes in our lives!!!

Thanks, folks, for joining us to heartily welcome Pete & Wanda to
the region. We look forward to seeing you at future gatherings and
for getting an opportunity to possibly look for a letterbox or two
you might place in the future!
