From: Tracy Pruett (blackmtnranch@frontiernet.net) | Date: 2005-12-28 17:06:46 UTC
We're the Black Mountains 4-H club, and we're combining the
letterboxing activity (it falls under our natural resources project)
with our photography and goat packing projects. I'm the adult leader
for the club and the three afore mentioned projects and I'm looking
forward to sharing ideas and gleaning experience and information from
you all for our newbie group. I'd like to see 4-H make this a
legitimized activity for all 4-H'ers across the U.S. within the
natural resources or orienteering project fields complete with project
standards to go by and display entries at the fair levels. Our club
will be placing it's first box in the Mount Nutt Wilderness Area of
Arizona this weekend (weather permitting). The young member that
places it will be posting clues at the letterboxing usa site. Is
he/she supposed to do that here, too? Thanks again!
