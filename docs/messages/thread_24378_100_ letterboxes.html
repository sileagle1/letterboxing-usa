http://swogo.org/cachebb/viewtopic.php?t=793

The link above talks about an event July 27,28 and 29th 2007
They have place over 100 letterboxes so far. It's listed as
both a Letterboxing and Geocaching event. The letterboxes will
be in both formats (Compass and GPS).

Also, if you can not be at the event. They will have a table
set up. If you want, you can carve a stamp for this even and
send it to them in the mail. They will set your stamp(s) out
on that table for guest to sign, stamp and place their personal
stamp in a log book that will be sent back to you in the mail.
SWOGO will pick up the postage for the log book and your stamp
to send it back to you. Her eis the address to send your stamp
to. PLEASE make sure you send in all the information about
your stamp. (I E your letterboxing team name and you address to
send it to)
