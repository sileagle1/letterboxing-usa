Bonnie:
Hojos is walking distance to Reins deli. People travelling from NY-
Boston, seem to all stop there, great quality, fast service, Reubens,
bagels, etc.

The Penguin, Orange Parrot, and Rotten Egg are in the same park. I
would suggest comparing clues before setting out to avoid back
tracking. (2hours?)

New England authors is a 5 minute drive from Crandall park. (the
logbook for the first box is separate from the box. I went to replace
it and it wouldnt fit in the box) It is temporarily inside a
ziplock. (1hour)

Shenipsit lake and Daisies in the Wood are fairly near to each other.
Ten minute drive from Crandall Park.
(1.45 hrs combined?)

Mansfield, CT is loaded with great boxes. Do stop by kathy-Johns ice
cream shop. Junction route 194 and 44.

Wishing you cool breezes. It is a scorcher here at the moment!!!
Pinecone
