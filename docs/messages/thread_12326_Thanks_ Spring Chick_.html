Have you tried the Stampeaz PZ Kut product? It is white vinyl, 1/4"
thick, 10" x 4 3/4" sheets. Carves wonderful and can't beat the
price! I usually purchase the A grade, but have purchased the B
grade in a pinch and only once was even able to notice a small
imperfection, which I easily worked around. It is only available
online from April at:

http://www.investorsnet.com/stampeaz/
