That clue is in the Pagan Pride Box. And I'm going to
take this opportunity to announce the immienent change
of this box.

On Oct. 5, 2003, at Strathmore Park in Russel MA, the
previous Pagan Pride 2002 Box will be retired, and
replaced by the Pagan Pride 2003 Box. The box will
have a new stamp, and a new hiding place. The logbook
will not be changed. For those who want to get the old
stamp before it is retired, consider yourself warned.
Also, for those of you that want the new stamp, and
might not have the old one yet, I am going to make
both the new stamp and the old stamp available during
Pagan Pride Day, in the Community Center. Entrance to
the event is a canned or boxed good for donation to
the Western Mass Food Bank. The grounds open at 11,
and events are planned all day through 8PM. For more
information on the event itself, please go to
http://www.sismoon.com/paganpride.htm

Additionally, I will be carrying my personal traveller
with me that day, so come and find me on the grounds.
Hope y'all will come. :-)
