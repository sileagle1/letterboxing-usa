GNIS found Lothian, MD at 384954N, 0763642W. GNIS is yet another
wonderful American taxpayer financed tool that can locate just about
anything if its on a map. It never ceases to amaze me that you can
enter the name of some gulch that some prospector named 150 years ago,
and if its on a map, GNIS has a chance of finding it for you. Then
you're a few clicks away from a contour map or terraserver. Us clue
writers always have to stay one step ahead of the technology :-)

GNIS is at http://mapping.usgs.gov/www/gnis/gnisform.html if anyone is
interested.
