Directions from North (Rtes 84, 91, 2):
Take Route 2 to exit 10. At end of ramp, turn right. At next stop sign,
turn left onto New London Tpke. At next stop sign, turn right onto ???.
When you come to the next major intersection (you do not have a stop sign,
but the other three streets do) turn left onto Mott Hill Road. Take a left
onto Dickenson Road. Dickenson Road will turn to dirt. You can either park
at the fork in the dirt road or drive up the left hand fork (if you have an
SUV, or are brave like I was!) and park at the top of the hill, just before
the large descent. DO NOT GO DOWN THE DESCENT! As you can see, you are on
the White Trail.
