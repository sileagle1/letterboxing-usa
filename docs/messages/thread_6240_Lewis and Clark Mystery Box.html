The Jefferson Peace Medal

2004 will mark the Bicentennial of the Lewis and Clark expedition.
They traveled from St. Louis, MO to Astoria, OR and back again. I
have placed a second Mystery Letterbox and I hope to plant many more
before 2004.

To find the letterbox, you must go to where Meriweather Lewis was on
December 25, 1806.

To find the letterbox:
Once you reach the park, pick up a trail map from the kiosk. Follow
the Red Trail down to Martin's Branch. Cross the stream and where
the Brown Trail intersects with the Red Trail is a V shaped tree
leaning over the path. At the base of this tree is the Lewis and
Clark Mystery Box. The Jefferson Peace Medal.
