Journey of The Saints Box 2

Planted on 4/18/03 by Mama Bunny of 7 for Irish Bunny's 9th B'day

This saint was a slave in Ireland. After escaping he became a priest & bishop. He returned to Ireland to convert the Irish. This stamp is the symbol of what he used to explain the Holy Trinity. Do you know what saint it is?

Clues: Go to Bethany Park. Start @ playground. Walk toward Vine Street 'til you come to sidewalk. Make a right and follow the side walk to the bridge. Sit down on the embankment on the right side of the bridge. Reach under the base for a green box.

Enjoy and remember to be discrete! Rehide well. Please let us know if you find our box!
