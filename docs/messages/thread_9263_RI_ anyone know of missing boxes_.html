Monument, Viking Ships at Sunrise, Queen of a River, and maybe the
Wickaboxet group. We've already found the boxes in Narragansett, and
some of the Charlestown group. Any help or suggestions will be
greatly appreciated.

From: Janet (moonstone_baby@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-11-22 22:46:24 UTC
Hi mulbie,

it just so happens I compiled a list of missing letterboxes in RI.
Here is what I know.

RI Missing Letterboxes, , , 11/17/02
Fort Getty, Jamestown, Believed missing but unconfirmed, 1
Purgatory Point, Newport, Second box missing, 1
Fond Farewell, Newport, 3rd box stamp missing & 4th box missing, 2
Land Sailing, Newport, Missing, 1
Frosty Drew Quest, Charlestown, Missing, 1
Polly's Rock Quest, Charlestown, Believed missing but unconfirmed, 1
Kimball Arbutus, Charlestown, Removed by Refuge Manager, 2
Perfect Summer Day, Charlestown, Missing, 1
Trustom Pond, South Kingstown, 1st box missing, 1
Stepping Stone, Exeter, Missing, 1
Fast Track To Your Degree, Kingston, 3rd box missing, 1
Porgy & Bass, Narragansett, Porgy missing (now called Just Bass), 1
Viking Ships At Sunrise, South Kingstown, Believed missing but
unconfirmed, 1
Canonchet's Waters, Ashaway, Believe one of the two is missing, 1

As far as I know the Hills are alive, great swamp monument, queen's
river, and wickaboxet are all there. The road to great swamp
monument was closed a few weeks ago, I do not know if it is open
now.

happy hunting,

moonstone_baby
