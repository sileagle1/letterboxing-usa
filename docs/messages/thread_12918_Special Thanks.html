Special Thanks

From: Dan (danzad_99@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-17 13:46:34 UTC
I would like to give a special thanks to all that have stamped the
JJZSr. Letterbox in Putnam, Ct. so far. Today I'm walking in a
Relay for Life event in Woodstock. For every stamp that is in the
box for my Dad's birthday(a week before) to his birthday, we said we
would donate a $1 per stamp in the log book. I told my family about
this and they said they would also. With 20 stamps so far, this year
we were able to donate $230 from the letterbox(another $92 from co-
workers) for a total of $322 to help fight cancer. I hope next years
total will be great. Thank you again to everyone that has stamped
the log book so far.

Teach & Preach (aka Dan & Robin)
