What you can do is check out Atlasquest.com. They have a trip planner
that you can use that will show all of the boxes along your route. You
can even get specific and have it show you all of the boxes within a
certain distance from the highway.

Many of the boxes listed there have the clues posted on
letterboxing.org. Fortunatly, they have many of the clues cached, so
you can still get the clues. Just click on the view cache button.
