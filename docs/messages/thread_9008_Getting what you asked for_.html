Getting what you asked for.

From: (twograysquirrels@aol.com) | Date: 2002-11-08 11:10:40 UTC-05:00
Hi,
Last year I felt all alone in Maryland hiding boxes and begging for more to find. Along came an article in the Post and with it a flood of nuts. As most of you know I usually sign off with "May there be lots of nuts in your life." Well I think that has come true for all of us in the Mid-Atlantic Area. Enter Squirrels Girls ( the collective name) consisting of Psychomommy, Turtle, Apple Dumpling Gang, Fireflylight and Mudmommy. Now if that isn't a bunch of nuts I don't know what is. They have come into our lives with sillyness and lots of boxes to find and for that I am truly grateful. I got what I wished for and I hope the rest of you are enjoying this silly hobby.

May you be a nut in someone's life,
Squirrel
P30-25=5 left but wet F? X marks the spot
M25 missing and gaining (I think I hold the record on missing or wet boxes)
