For those that don't like lengthy letterbox reports please excuse
this post. We just received Honor Liberty from Singing Wolf and we
are posting this on 9-11. Normally a Hitch Hiker report is found on
the HH chat list, but with other different aspects of this HH, the
report is being filed on this list. A short explanation for those
newer letterboxers is probably in order, so here goes; After
9/11/2001, I wanted never to see the Towers coming down as was shown
over and over on TV. As more time passed I changed my mind and
believe that we should probably have that scene replayed several
times each year to make sure we never forget the horror. With that
sentiment Honor Liberty HH was launched on 9/11/2002 with specific
instructions to post on the chat list it's current location, and
write in the logbook any sentiments the finder wished. In some cases
the race was on when ever the location was posted, and in other cases
the new finder was reluctant to post the new location due to the
procedure being contrary to normal procedures for Hitch Hikers. The
first year's travels can be found on the main LbNA yahoo chatlist in
message # 34174, dated October 03, 2003, the subsequent years travels
can be found in message # 46814 and #57329. The following is a list
of those locations and letterboxers that commented during her travels
this past year. (Please excuse any omissions or errors).
Some folks have had more than one visit with her, and I think Silent
Doug has the record for most visits at 3.
