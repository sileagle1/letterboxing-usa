From: (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2003-05-31 20:33:15 UTC-04:00
Ewok:

I too am not very artistic and was so impressed by the stamps carved by the
talented Lb'ers (I truly believed some were drawings--which I couldn't do
either!) I thought I'd never even try to carve one. Then, as I started being
impressed by the great hand-carved stamps in the letterboxes I found plus the
Smithsonian suggesting this is a true American art form, I began to think about an
attempt myself. Know what? It's FUN! The feeling of the carver slicing through
"the pink stuff" is amazing! I tried just a design the first time instead of a
'still life' or a portrait or some 'thing'. A mistake on a design is just
another design!

My latest is a Zodiac series so I carved a Ram (Aries), then a Bull (Taurus)
and although they may look like a 3rd grader's drawing, you can still tell
what they are and I've even gotten compliments! (Thanks H&S!)

Really--I encourage you to try it--you'll like it!
