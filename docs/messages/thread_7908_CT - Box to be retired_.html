CT - Box to be retired.

From: lisa sanderson (bikenutct@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-09-23 16:17:31 UTC-07:00
Sadly, the building that housed the "Third Rock From
The Sun" Letterbox in Old Saybrook, CT has been sold,
leaving this special little cafe with no where to go.
Unable to find a new, suitable location, the staff is
going off in different directions, and the cafe will
close on Friday, October 18th, 2002.

The last day "Third Rock" will be available is Monday,
October 14th, the Columbus Day holiday.

I, for one, will miss this terrific eatery and its
talented and friendly staff. Thanks to all who
visited.

Lisa
