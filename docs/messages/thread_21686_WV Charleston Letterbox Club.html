Hi! My wife and I are new to letterboxing and we are trying to form a
club in Charleston WV to meet up with local letterboxers. We'd like to
do things such as day excursions where we all go out to a location and
hunt for boxes, have a picnic, plant boxes as a group, have events at a
local mall in partnership with a bookstore to help raise awareness of
letterboxing, etc. To read more about the group, please visit:

http://letterboxing.meetup.com/32/

You can also RSVP through this site.

We also go out to Marlinton, WV monthly during the summer and wouldn't
mind trying to set up a group there too. We noticed that there are
several boxes located in Hillsboro, Elkins, Davis and Spruce Knob that
are all near to Marlinton.

We are interested to hear from any locals!
