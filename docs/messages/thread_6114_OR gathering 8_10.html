Sifting through the ballots, no hanging chads have been found. The date 8/10 seems to be the clear
winner. The rough plan is as follows: Let's get together for a picnic lunch around noon at Balch Creek/
Macleay Park in Portland, OR. There are only about eight parking spaces so it could be a challenge to find
a spot. I know letterboxers are creative thinkers and there are lots of trailheads that connect to the
trails in this park. We can take a couple of hours to meet and greet as well as swapping clues for all
those new boxes that many of us intend to hide for this event. After lunch, those who want, can hit the
trails and seek out the hidden treasures. Sound OK? Any further ideas are welcome. Yahoo!!!
