From: Mark Pepe (mjpepe1@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-08-17 17:30:25 UTC
Hi boxers:

John Kerry said he was slowing down his campaign schedule the next
few weeks and I guess he meant what he said. He had time for this
interview with us this afternoon.

In an effort for fairness in campaigning, we encourage President Bush
to contact us also, send his signature stamp and do an interview with
us. This will enable the reader to experience balanced reporting and
the ability to see both sides of letteboxing issues.

If you, the reader, are interested, the interview is ready. Just
click on Senator Kerry's picture in the first story on our website
and you will be magically transported to Kerryland.

And always . . .

Have fun and just get out there & box!!!

Mark Pepe
http://markandsuepepe.blogspot.com/
