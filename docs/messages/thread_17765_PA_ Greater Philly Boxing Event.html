PA: Greater Philly Boxing Event

From: Rainear, Tim TQO (trainear@tqs.com) | Date: 2004-02-08 13:47:23 UTC-08:00
Hi all,
I am still planning to hold this event.
I have had great support from Silent Doug and good advice from several
others.
We do expect this to be a pot luck day, so send Doug or me a note on what
you plan to bring.

With all the bad weather we have been having in the area, I was concerned I
would even get out to place the event boxes.
Fear not. I made it today. I did fall flat on my back within the first 10
feet of hiking. But I was much more careful after that.
So, there will be plenty of boxing available from this starting point.
I will have clue sets printed for these four event boxes as well as the
existing Green Lane Park boxes (3) and Perkiomen Trail set (6).

Hope the weather improves. But we'll have fun no matter what.
See you soon.

Raindear.
