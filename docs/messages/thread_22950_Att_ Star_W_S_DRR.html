You might have missed my post about deleting messages off of the list,
but here it is again.
Any one that posts a message can delete that message(only their own)
from the list. If you go to the message list click on that message,
then up in the right hand corner you will find 3 buttons;
Reply,Forward, and Delete. Just hit delete and then hit ok when
prompted. Several of my posts end up coming in duplicated so I use
this function occasionally.
The other alternative is to request that your message and a subsequent
message that contained the same info be deleted by a moderator.
Unfortunately somebody left the info in when they posted a request for
more info about PLBs and that one you do not have any control over.
Sometimes it is just too easy to hit reply and leave the previous post
attached to your reply.
