What a great two days of boxing!

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-06-08 19:00:48 UTC-04:00
The weather somewhat cooperated for boxing these last two days. Butterfly,
Bluebird, Letterboxer2002, and I ventured out at 7:30 a.m. on Saturday to find
Dragon's Fire Letterbox in fine form, with a hitchhiker to boot! Happily, we
found the Shooting Star Hitchhiker (? I forgot to write it down), which
Butterfly will send on its way soon. We then ventured to find Otewah Campground,
Fritz' Letterbox, and Granny Austin's Tavern all in fine form.

Then we ventured out to the Relay for Life, where I was happy to finally meet
Steve, Heidi and Madras! The rain started pouring as soon as the Relay for
Life started, however it didn't dampen any spirits! Teach and Preach blessed
us with the Joe Schmoe's Geography Hitchhiker! Unfortunately, I had to leave
to go to Jonathan Edwards Winery for a wine party, so that is all that happened
on Saturday.

Sunday proved to be much more fruitful for boxing! I found all of the
following boxes in good shape:

Bee Happy
Go Batty
Big Rock (yes, I forgot to do the new boxes on the pink loop.... that's what
happens
when I'm not paying close enough attention!)
Synderella
Dragon of Krakow
2 years-2000 boxes
Teapot
Teacup
Cream and Sugar
Sassafras (spelling?????)
Rogers Lake
St. Matthias
Milo Light
Milo Light II
Butterfly's Anniversary
Lakeshore

If you recognize some of the above boxes and wonder why I didn't get the
whole series, there were too many people at most of the spots, and Grassy Hill was
missing.

Anyway, it was a great day! Hope I can go out tomorrow after school and find
more!

Happy Hunting!
