What a weekend - The best birthday gifts were found on the trail!!!

From: IrishTinker (irishtinker@earthlink.net) | Date: 2002-10-06 19:23:42 UTC-04:00
Hello all!
Saturday we flew off to RI and  5 found wonderful boxes! Such fun! 
Today we hung around CT and visited Lebanon and Windham and came back with 6 boxes. History stamps tend to be a favorite of mine. Sad to say that we couldn't get the 1st box on the loop because they are rebuilding the walk-way right at the box!
French Fox triumphed over his 100th find today leaving me (Irishtinker) at 98 finds. Maybe tomorrow if it doesn't rain too much I will reach 100 on my actual birthday! 
Other exciting news from this weekend was getting the wonderfully romantic traveler/HH the Clandestine Lovers.
We planted 6 boxes today (clues to come) 
and hmm there is something else....what is it....hmm...oh yes now I remember. After hours and hours of going buggy eyed, scratching our heads and occasionally getting snippy with frustration we solved the clues and found the DANCING MEN MYSTERY!!!!!! 
Thanks to everyone who made this weekend so much fun! Loved the boxes!
Irishtinker
