Hey letterboxers all!!
Got a problem with relentless deer flies?
Cutter and Off aint workin?
Try this little gem - FLYPAPER!
Yes! Flypaper!

You will look like an A- #1 dork... BUT....
Pin a strip around the brim of your hat!
Pin another across your back!
Bugs check in... but they don't check out!

Stamp up with the lovely death-nell buzz of blood
sucking parasites in your ears! Revell in the
happy realization that they will never again drink
your vital juices! yee ha!
