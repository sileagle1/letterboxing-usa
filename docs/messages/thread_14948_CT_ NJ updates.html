Have tried to get out and box when it hasn't been raining these last
copule of weeks, which is NOT offen (ugh!!).
Here is a short update....
Connecticut
Captain's Walk , Olde Mystick Village boxes are great. Thanks for
the captain's walk it was beautiful. Even got to see some artist
painting along the road side.
Barking Up The Right Tree - Our first "first finder" box! If your
passing thru Ct. check this one out.
New Jersey
Round Valley great walk box is in good shape. Is this box a geocache
also? It had many little trinkets in the box along with the stamp
and notepad.
Edward Stratemeyer used ghostwriter's and this box is now a ghost -
MIA - ( we looked high in every direction!)
Turkey Mountain what a great hike! The boxes were in good condition
but have not seen a vistor in a year. (We started this hike and 1/2
way thru a thunderstorm and downpour got us! But our log book was
spared!!) Did Pyramid Mountain a while back that too is a great
hike! Thanks Wingfoot!!
We have the clues for "Huts" worked out and are planning on heading
there and to the Great Swamp to look for Lord Sterling when the rain
lets up.

All hiking in old socks,
C&B in NJ and Frogga
