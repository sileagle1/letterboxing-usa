A Not so Perfect Adventure

From: letterboxalex (letterboxalex@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-14 22:05:09 UTC
We had quite an adventure today. Went to Sugarloaf mountain in
search of three boxes and found only one in the middle of a
thunderstorm. We had to hide under the roof of the "Snack
Shack" until the rain passed. Then we went and found the
"Ultimate Walker" at Black Rock Mill in the rain. The microbox
was dry and clean but by the end we weren't. Ended up 2 for 4. It
was kind of fun anyway and isn't that what letterboxingg is all
about?
