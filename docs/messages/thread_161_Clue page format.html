I would have to say that I am also in favor of a default setting for
color, background, etc. The color scheme is a low priority as far as
that goes.

It would be useful to have a link to Tiger maps while creating our
clue pages, in order to include a map of the area right away, along
with means of posting stamp images and photographs and such. I may be
just dreaming here, but is it possible to set up this system for
creating our clue pages in an easy-to-understand manner and thus save
the more computer literate letterboxers all this homework of creating
pages for us? I'm just looking out for your long-term leisure here
folks...
