Castelo de Pombal letterbox
Pombal, Portugal
placed December 27, 2001
by the Varrell family from Connecticut, USA

Traveling clockwise around the castle, drive through the stone arch and
park at the back side of the castle. Near the three-sided building
(chapel?), take the footpath leading you up nearer to the castle.
Continuing clockwise, pass one entrance. There is a big pink rock
straight ahead, down low, where the castle wall is thicker than
elsewhere. At the left end of this section (opposite end from pink
rock), look behind the triangle rock at the base of the back inner corner
for Portugal's second letterbox. Please notice how the triangle rock is
positioned so you can replace it in the same way.
