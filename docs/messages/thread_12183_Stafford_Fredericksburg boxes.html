Stafford/Fredericksburg boxes

From: justplaingeorge (justplaingeorge@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-21 13:51:41 UTC
Hi all,

Managed to get down to Stafford on Sunday and collect some boxes.
Beautiful day to be out!

Fredericksburg Confederate Cemetary - Box is great but even the
second logbook is now full! There are some spaces on the back of
some earlier pages though.

Blue Heron - Despite having to do math we found this one safe and
sound. Had to wait around awhile for all the families to finally
leave so that we could return it! There was one group who seemed
determined to just hang out for the day!

Akoben - It takes you through a disc golf course so we had to avoid
the golfers, but the ravine was really pretty! Box and book are
doing well!

Antietam Bridge - Box was open and full of water, but thankfully it
hadnt made it through to the logbook yet! We dried it all out and
put some extra ziploc baggies around everything!

Somewhere along the way we picked up and dropped off the Matchbook HH
as well. : )

Dead Snellings and Weeds - Pretty confident we found the spot, but no
box. Anyone found this one recently?

Shell
