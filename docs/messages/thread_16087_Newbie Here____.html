Welcome to this addicting hobby. I hope you will enjoy it as much
as the rest of us.
In Connecticut, most people just stamp into the log book. I have
adopted a few boxes, and I go look at the log to see who has
visited. I haven't seen return postcards in boxes in this area. I
do put my e-mail address in the log book, and the web site has a
link from the clue page to send an email to the placer (that's me).
I haven't got any email back, but I only adopted the boxes in the
last few months.
Happy boxing.
