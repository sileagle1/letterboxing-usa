Great 4th of July weekend

From: Mark Pepe (mjpepe1@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-07-06 12:43:18 UTC
Hi boxers:

Spent a great day Saturday in VT with TeamGreenDragon, finding a few
mystery letterboxes and paying homage to Prayer Rock. We were
surprised how few people have logged into this wonderful piece of
letterboxing history. Prayer Rock is at the very least the first hand
carved letterbox in the country if not the very first overall. We
encourage all New Englanders to make that trip to Bristol, VT.

Received an email from the new owner of the house where our Mystery
Inventor box is located. It is out of commission for now, but he has
the box and has given me permission to rehide it on the property.
I'll keep you all informed when it's back.

Hope your 4th weekend was the best,

Have fun and just get out there & box!!!


Mark Pepe
