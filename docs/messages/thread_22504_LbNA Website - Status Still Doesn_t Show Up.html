No, you're right - it doesn't show by clicking on the flag. The way I got
it to show up was to go to a list of boxes in a region, click on the box
title for a box just prior to a box that has non-LBNA clues, then go to the
bottom of these clues and click on the next box title that shows up in the
lower right corner. This will advance you to the non-LBNA box, but instead
of showing the clues, you'll see a page w/basic header info INCLUDING the
last posted status.
