OR: Rood Bridge Park Letterbox is "missing" ! (webmasters note!)

From: Mark Bendickson (nomad_pnw@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-10-19 06:59:23 UTC
Upon checking recently on our "Rood Bridge Park Letterbox", we have
sadly confirmed the stamp has been apparently abducted and entered
the Land Of No Return. Thus, webmasters, please remove the box clues
from the Letterboxing.org clue sheet.

For those of you who had the chance to claim this stamp before its
disappearance, you can now flag it as a collectors item, since it
will not be reproduced. We have chosen this option due to the
ongoing changes in the park. Since its opening, what was once a
secluded side trail area perfect for housing the clues and stamp, has
now been converted to a high traffic area with most of the
undergrowth cleaned up and out, thus exposing letterbox type
activities.

You can, however, expect to see a birth of more boxes in the near
future, which will be strategically located in more remote areas.
