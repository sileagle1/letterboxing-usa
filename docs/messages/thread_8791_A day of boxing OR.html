A day of boxing OR

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2002-10-31 01:19:37 UTC
On this perfectly glorious autumn day, Ryan C and I headed south to find Salem's first letterbox. The clue
is really fun and a great way to meet cute coeds, Frat Boys and bespectacled profs. This is not one for
the shy. Heading home after lunch with Windairen we passed the 45th parallel and relived the Mystery
Movie clue for Brett's latest creation. We did drive a wee bit slower than the breakneck speed shown in
the movie. The box has a wonderful stamp in it and the hitch hiking puppydog that was inside was grateful
for a breath of fresh air. I also got a good laugh watching Ryan as he attempted to find the Little People's
Letterbox. It was in plain sight but the fellow just couldn't see it. And I thought I had bad eyesight!?
Thanks for the good time, Funhog
