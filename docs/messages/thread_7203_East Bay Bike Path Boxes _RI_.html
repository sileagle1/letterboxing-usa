How nice to discover this path, less than an hour from our house!
On 8/25, we did a portion of this 14 mile trail, starting in
Providence. We located, from Series II, the Sightseer and the
Fisherman. We also found the "East Bay Bike Path" box -- this
provided a shady respite as well as a pretty wooded walk. Gor to try
out our new personal stamp too.

The "Stroller" eluded us. Perhaps we were too hot to scrabble long
enough in the gravel? Or we may have been in the wrong spot. No
matter, the view to the island with the gazebo was reward enough!
