Hi,

I just wanted to let people know that I am currently taking
responsibility (as a volunteer) for the Sudbury Valley Trustees
(headquartered in Sudbury MA)
boxes.

I have created a blog as a central repository for clues boxes placed
on SVT land. Then the clues can be placed on LBNA or Atlasquest, but
be available as a single entity on the blog
http://svtletterboxing.blogspot.com/

I would like to do this with boxes not owned by SVT as well.

There are still many properties that don't have letterboxes on them.
I think they would like to keep them limited to the "20 frequent walks
locations"

http://www.sudburyvalleytrustees.org/page.php?id=96.

There are trail maps to link to.

At this point the only requirements are that we be notified about the
placement, that not too much of the habitat is disturbed in placing
boxes, and it would be nice if a reference to SVT be in the clue, or
on the stamp and logbook.

i am also in the process of replacing the store-bought stamps with
carved ones, each of which will have SVT on them.

So anyone with an SVT land box, can you contact me?

Diana
