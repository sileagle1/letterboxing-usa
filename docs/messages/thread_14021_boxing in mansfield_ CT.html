boxing in mansfield, CT

From: Odilio (odiliofinder@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-05 06:30:20 UTC-07:00
Hello,
I went boxing in Mansfield CT yesterday with Freedom Fox and Irishtinker and newbie "Licks"
We did the Pink Panther Series, Totem Pole, Air Mail (A new favorite!!), The Wizard Series, The Circle of Life Series, Merrow Park Box, and Friendly Skies. We missed one box in Merrow Park because the heat was getting to us.

Irish Mum your Totem Pole box is broken but the contents are okay.

We loved all the boxes and had a great time. We definately have a new convert to letterboxing with Licks. Irishtinker carved a very cool personal stamp for him.

This was Irishtinker and Freedom Fox 1 year anniversary!! Freedom Fox hit a milestone but I'll let him tell you about it!!

Extra nice bonus of meeting Teach and Preach for an exchange!!

See you on the trail!!
Odilio
Thank you to all the planters for a great day!!!!
