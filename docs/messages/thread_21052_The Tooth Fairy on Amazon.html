I posted this to the Great Lakes Fourm, but thought others might
appreciate it, too. If you are not a regular to Amazon, during the
holiday season they are doing a new mini movie each week. This weeks
is the Tooth Fairy and it is delightful and has distinct letterbox
overtones. If you go to www.amazon.com it is on the first page.

Enjoy - I do most my letterboxing with very young children (I'm the
crazy Aunt that every family has one of) and this really made me
think about some of the clues my buddies come up with for me to
decipher! I hope you all enjoy it as much as I did.
