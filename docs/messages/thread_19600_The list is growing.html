From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2004-07-26 11:31:06 UTC
In case no one's been keeping tabs, this morning we're well over 1800
members on this list. Thats up from about 1640-1650 prior to a
certain magazine article!

Please remain positive with your responses and try to help out some
of the newer members as they get up the courage to post to our group.

For the newer members, take note of the FAQ's and other helpful areas
and resources. Also note that there are regional talk lists, a new
members talk list and a travellers list (for hitch-hiker reports).

Thanks and have a sparkling day!

-Steve, Heidi and Madras in CT
