Challenger 7 park in Webster has these boxes: Space City Shuttle,
Neptune, STS 51-L, and Aduceus-Cay. Neptune has one of my favorite
stamps in this area. That is a nice outing (sweaty and mosquito-y in
June, I'm sure).

We haven't gotten to as many in that area as we would like, but one on
our to-do list is Haak Wine (in Santa Fe), because it seems like a fun
outing. Lots of drive-bys in that general vicinity. If you need help
figuring where things are, feel free to email me off-list.
