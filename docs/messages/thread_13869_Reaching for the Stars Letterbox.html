found it last year, I believe it was late summer (I don't have my log

to find, and I actually had trouble even finding the park itself. But

what the title referred to. It really touched my heart ( I'm afraid
to be more specific because I might unintentionally be posting a

same as when I visited, you will not find the stump itself without a
thorough search--don't give up. Happy boxing, Salamander.
