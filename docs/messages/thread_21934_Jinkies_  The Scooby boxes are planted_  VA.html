The Scooby Doo series - Creature of Fountainhead park - has just had
it's final clues posted. All located in the area of Fountainhead
Park, VA.

Six parts, 18 boxes and alot of walking on some beautiful trails!

I just wanted to thank everyone who sent in stamps for the series.
They were all wonderful and I had a great time writing up the clues.
