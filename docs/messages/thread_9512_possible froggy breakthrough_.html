From: adglbna (ADGLBNA@aol.com) | Date: 2002-12-02 22:59:08 UTC
Before you head back out there to kill yourselves again, I wanted to
let you know that the Frogshackle Diner reference is actually talking
about a real buidling in the park. Since I don't have a map in front
of me right now I can't tell you the exact name of the building but
it is called the "Frogshackle something." Make sure that you pass
the building before you cross the dam or you will not find the box.

If you want to go back and try to find box number three, I suggest
you walk straight down Vestals Gap Road past all of the buildings and
work the clues backwards. Although that may sound confusing, it is a
much shorter walk. Once you reach the house (it will be on your
right if you go in backwards) that is to the right of the fence, turn
around and then find the big tree. Pay careful attention to the name
of Robin's housing development and you should have a better idea of
what area to search for the big tree.

Break a leg!

Tammy
