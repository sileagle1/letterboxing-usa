Now that my clues have been published in an international magazine and
are jetting around world, I've decided to cash in on this good fortune.

Hmmm ... banner ads, pay to play, clothing, widgits, gidgits ...

As I stand here starving between the proverbial cash cow and golden
goose, it occurs to me that there is a way to cash in, and keep my
clue site clutter free ... endorsements! I mean, if Michael Jordan
can make more money selling shoes than using them, why can't I?

My accountants tell me someone has already trademarked the mapsurfer
letterboxing boot (and it is quite nice, they tell me, because it is
designed to walk along those stone walls, and each one has a personalized
tread in case you forget your stamp), I will have to settle for
the more mundane sort of endorsements ...

http://www.mapsurfer.com/boxes/box43.html

I hope no one minds a "commercial" clue ...
