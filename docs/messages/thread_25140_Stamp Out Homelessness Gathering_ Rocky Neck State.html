Stamp Out Homelessness Gathering, Rocky Neck State Park, Niantic, CT 9/26/09

From: maire_young (maire_young@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-09-16 04:17:54 UTC

Please come and join us for a letterboxing gathering on Sept 26th from
10am-5pm at Rocky Neck State Park in Niantic, CT. This gathering is
also a benefit for Malta, Inc. Malta is a 501c3 that reaches out to the
homeless by providing socks, underwear, jeans, jackets, toiletries and
hot soup and sandwiches. Donations of the above will be collected. All
who come will receive stamps depicting 16 different items as well as the
event stamp and the Malta stamp. There is also a new series planted
entitled "The Twilight Series". These stamps are amazing and were
carved by my daughter, a gifted artist.

Fun and prizes for all. We will have a drawing for tickets to the
Basketball Hall of Fame, and also a gift card to Flander Fish House.
Pot luck too, of course.

Please come and enjoy the fun. There is camping on site. The park has
multiple trails and letterboxes and a beautiful beach as well.
