Hey y'all

I tried to post to group using the webpage form last week but it doesn't
appear to have made it to the list. I haven't been around for a while,
but I see that it appears I still have the only letterboxes hidden in
Florida. I went out this past weekend to verify they still exist. Two out
of three survived hurricanes, fires, and vandalism this past year. The
only one that didn't was vandalized, the squirrelfish box. Strangely, it
was opened and the log book stolen leaving the box and the stamp behind.
Fortunately, this makes it easy for me to relocate it. One surviving box
had been visited once, the other twice. I sure wish there were some other
boxes in Florida for me to find. I need to know how to modify my record
for the relocated box since I haven't been on the list for a while. I
have also been doing more off road bicycling lately and likely will hide
other boxes if someone is there to find them. I would appreciate anyone
on this list that lives in Florida, or is likely to visit in the near
future, contacting me directly. Thanks.
