To add to my recent message:

Almost every attachment that's appeared on this list over the past
few days IS infected--and they're still coming (one since my last
post alone). **Don't** download/open the attachments.


Play it safe! On this list (or *any* list):

1. Be suspicious of messages that (a) don't have directly to do with
letterboxing and (b) have attachments to them.
2. If there is no text to a message, just an attachment, or it has
text that is vaguely worded, and you still wish to download the
attachment, be ***sure*** your virus software is up to update and set
on real-time status, so that it will catch an infected download
before it has a chance to damage your computer.

Knowledge is power. We *can* do something about people who get their
kicks out of hurting others in this way. Pass the knowledge on!!
