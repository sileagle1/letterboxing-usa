From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-13 02:22:45 UTC

My original Kent Trails box (GR, MI) had a visitor from Brazil stamp
in. This was the first box I ever placed, BTW ... almost exactly one
year ago.

I knew NOTHING of carving stamps! I used an old eraser I had on hand
and hacked out the stamp with a kitchen knife after an insomnia-
induced night of internet surfing when I first discovered
letterboxing. LOL ... placed this, my first box, the next afternoon.

This box lasted from October to March, was replaced about a month
later and is still going strong. Funny thing is, this summer while
doing a maintenance check, I found the original stamp and logbook
stuck back into the container with the new box and stamp. Vandals
remorse? Who knows ... there was no note or explanation.

Enjoy!
---Bindle Babe
