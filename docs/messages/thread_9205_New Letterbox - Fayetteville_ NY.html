Tom's Letterbox

Tom's letterbox is hidden in Green Lakes State Park, 7900 Green Lakes
Road, Fayetteville, NY (Onondaga County). There is an entrance fee
from May 1st through Columbus Day (weekend of May 1st through
Memorial Day weekend [9am-6pm]: $5; Memorial Day weekend through
Labor Day [8am-7pm]: $6; weekends and holidays after Labor Day
through Columbus Day [9am-6pm]: $5). The park is open from daylight
to dusk. You can pick up maps at the Ranger Station by the park's
entrance. Park at the parking lot near the beach area.
