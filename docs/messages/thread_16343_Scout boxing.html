From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-10-13 00:33:05 UTC
The Kennedy Creek Series in Washington State was put together last
year at this time for a Pack. Sponsored by the Nail family boxes
were carved and hidden by dvn2r skr, Fudrick, Trial and Error, Glenn
and myself. Ms Alafair sent a lovely event stamp with Patricus, who
was visiting the area on business.

As it turns out the boxes ranged from basic skills to tougher. We
did not agree to it, but skills in compass use, tree and plant id
came into play. Themes were salmon and scouting, again not pre-
planned, we each did our own thing.

Glenn's clues are just all compass bearings, mine tried to introduce
natural history and woodlore. Held in a very small area it was
tricky trying to find hiding spots. I found 2 boxes placed by the
others while trying to place mine.

Now is the best time for anyone planning to visit. The Salmon are
running thick and heavy.

Hoopoe
Redmond WA
