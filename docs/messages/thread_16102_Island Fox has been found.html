From: Maddog (mcjones@layc.org) | Date: 2003-09-30 04:56:51 UTC
All the Island Fox saga continues...

A geocacher contacted me and states that they have found the fox!

She will be sent to me, if she is in good repair, I will replace her
in a more hidden place with some geocachers leave me alone notes.
Perhaps I can obtain a fake rock at the SLO gathering but the rocks
on the island are very strange and shale like, not granite like at
all. Perhaps I can spay adhesive some matching dirt on the hidden
box when we get to the place.

Replacement Question? Should I keep the old stamp log with all the
Geocachers notes or start a new one There was only one stamper in
there the Leach family.. and the stamp had already gone away by
thier visit.

Here is my geocachers correspondance!
----------------------------------------------------------------
this is gripingrany iam so sorry for takein so long to you i have
been out of town for two weeks i have good news i have found your
stamp i can't understand why someone did what theydid but belive me
i was no part of it . anyway i serched for the stamp after you wrote
the details i realy didn't rember it please forgive my spelling
and send me your address as soon as possible and i will mail it to
you iam so glad you contacted me and every thing worked out i will
be waiting for your answear gripingrany
----------------------------------------------------------------

I am now waiting for little fox to come back...COOL!!!
