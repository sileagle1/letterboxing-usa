As someone who has NOT been around this list since the beginning of time, I
can only give that viewpoint. Can everyone please stop the bickering? It
seems like a few people have personal problems with each other that go way
back. Believe it or not, everyone does NOT want to know the sordid details
of who tried to get what going in letterboxing and how the chips fell or who
wishes they were a webmaster but isn't. And I couldn't care less if the
secret "archives" are opened or not. (Side note: Remember when they opened
Capone's vault or whatever that was on TV and it was empty? That was pretty
funny...) If there is hidden "evidence" that will expose the "truth," I
suspect there is only one person, two tops, who cares at all about it.

Anyway, letterboxing is a simple hobby for me and many others, and it is a
shame to see all this. I urge you to be dignified and stop.

Carpe diem...
Carrie
