Aroostook Sleuth went to the New Brunswick letterboxes, which reminded me of checking up on the weather. I thought I'd offer anyone traveling a chance to glimpse what a region looks like nowadays (like if the snows have melted). I go to Tommy's List of Live Cams. It offers links to all sorts of civic and (family-friendly) livecams overlooking towns and natural sites all over the world.

http://www.rt66.com/ozone/cam.htm

I can check up on the family's weather in Fort Kent, Maine, or just go look at Rothenburg, Germany or Hawaii if the weather here is dreary and I need a pick-me-up. Before a trip to another state or province, I'll look around the streets to see how people are dressed lately - sweaters, raincoats, etc. Enjoy!
