From: Dog Scouts Troop (DogScouts@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-11-11 16:05:15 UTC-05:00
Thank-you to Seeker of Flags and Reporter at Large for delivering the Dancing at Bougival HH to me from Mary of Va! It's a touching poem and very nice stamp Nails & I love the 'box'! I couldn't bring myself to stamp it with black ink and cover all the color that had been used to stamp it previously...So, I made a special trip to the store for some markers. It was the first time I had used markers to color a stamp and was very impressed with the color saturation (I hadn't expected it to come out so crisp & dark). I still used colored pencils to enhance the nice image a bit further (colored in her dress) :-)-old habits are hard to break. I will be taking it to our F-100 gathering this weekend and it will go home with the letterboxer that is most likely to meet with another letterboxer soon (or it will have to go in the mail). The box is too nice to leave it out in the woods. I made an additional log book for it too, since the first will be nearly full after the gather (both fit easily in the box with the stamp).

Thanks again!
