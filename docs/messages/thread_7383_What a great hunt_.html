What a great hunt!

From: Kristoffer J Buquet (KBuquet@ApertureFoto.com) | Date: 2002-09-02 19:25:32 UTC-04:00
Wow! 2 Gray Squirrels boxes are great. My wife and I headed over to the
Wheaton Park in MD in search of more stamps for our log.

Being my wife's first hunt, she was really excited. It was a little hot and
humid, but no complaints here.

All three of the boxes were in great shape and had excellent clues. The were
all "very well visited" by other LetterBoxers.

As fate would have it we ran into another family "on the hunt" and had the
chance to exchange stamps with two members from their family. They didn't
know about the list here, so I told them to check us out. So hopefully we
will be interacting with yet another family of Letterboxers on the list real
soon.
