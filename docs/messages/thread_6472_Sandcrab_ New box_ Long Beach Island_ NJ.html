Clues:

The Sandcrab Letterbox

Head south on Long Beach Island. Continue until you
find an orange water tower. Pass a fire station on
your right. Two blocks later turn east just
before the tennis courts. Continue east until you can
drive no further. Walk east to a covered structure
containing four benches. Walk around the far side of
the structure and face it. Looking at the base of the
structure, you will see that it is divided into
thirds. What you seek is in the right divider behind
some sand. Be discrete, this site is
crowded in the summer but early morning or late
evening will give you a chance to make your discovery
undisturbed and return it safely.
