What the consensus about changing your trail name? After a year of
letterboxing, I've been considering changing mine, although at the
moment I have no idea to what.

Basically, I don't like that the name I chose is four syllables, nor
do I like the inclusion of "CPA" in the front. Yes, I am a CPA, but
it's not really what I currently do. While I'm at it, I'd like to
pick something more anonymous anyway (eliminate my name from my trail
name).

So, CPAScott must retire ...

What do you think?
