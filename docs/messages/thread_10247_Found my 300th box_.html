Found my 300th box!

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-01-14 05:54:22 UTC-05:00
It is amazing what one can do in a month! ;-)  I found my 200th box (including HH's) on Dec. 14th!  Then found my 300th box (NOT including HH's) yesterday at an undisclosed bonux box in CT.  I can't tell you where, since if you haven't done the series, I don't want to give up the surprise, but I can tell you that it was a morbid experience finding your 300th box in a cemetery!  Actually, it wasn't that morbid, but I wonder what finding your 300th box in a cemetery means????  LOL

Anyway, I gave myself until the MA Gathering to get to 300, and now I'm victorious, so the pressure's off!  Since I have a concert tonight, I'm going to start building a gazebo on Wednesday!

Happy Trails everyone!
Music Woman
