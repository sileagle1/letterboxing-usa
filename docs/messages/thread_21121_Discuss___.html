And in south Louisiana, the large sandwiches are called Po-boys.
The best are Shrimp po-boys, oyster po-boys, and (my favorite)
crawfish po-boys.

Instead of an oyster po-boy, I prefer an oyster loaf - fried oysters
in a hollowed out loaf of good crusty french bread with a good spicy
cocktail sauce poured over it, then reheated in the oven wrapped in
foil.

Enjoy them all with Abita Turbo Dog.

Happy hunting in Baton Rouge,
