From: Jess <jmwestin@yahoo.com> (jmwestin@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-02-25 08:17:48 UTC
Hello,

It was a windy weekend in the good ol Columbia River Gorge, but I
managed to fight off the wind to go letterboxing, however, I wasn't
very successful in my searches...

The first box, "Sandy Delta" placed by Funhog was a bit tricky. I
wasn't able to find it on my first try, but I plan on going back in
the future...I've heard it's a tricky one. I just couldn't seem to
find the yellow warning post at the end of the clue.

The second box was a great find though! I headed up the Wahkeena
Falls and found the box that Der Mad Stamper had originally placed,
but that Ryan has replaced.

The third box, again, stumped me. I've heard this one is especially
tricky as well, but I could not find the Multnomah Falls box. Of
course, my day for looking wasn't the greatest--it was Sunday and I
think there was some kind of tourist group of about 100 people
travelling up and down the falls, so I didn't want to make
spectacle of myself looking for the box. Again, this is another box
that I will return to, probably next month.

I was happy to find at least one stamp, but was let down that I
wasn't crafty enough to find the other two yet :) Alas, I will try
again.

--Jess
