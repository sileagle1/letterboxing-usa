HH rec'd : Dancing @ Bougival

From: psycomommy2003 (ktborrelli@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-10-09 22:10:37 UTC
I had a wonderful surprise waiting for me this afternoon. I came
home to find that "Dancing at Bougival" had arrived via post.
It is a beautiful little carving with an equally attractive logbook.
Renior is my favorite Impressionist painter, so the little container
it is in, brightened my day.
I hate to put such a lovely HH in any box around here for various
reasons. The boxes here have either been trashed, gone missing, or
mildewed quickly. This lovely dancing couple should be handled with
care.
Thanks Mark in GA for sending me this lovely little treasure
created by Chuck Nail and dedicated to his bride.
Psychomommy and Princess Turtle
