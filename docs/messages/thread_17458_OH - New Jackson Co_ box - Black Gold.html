Black Gold

Difficulty: moderate
Terrain: easy
Placed by: Bigguy
Date placed: 1/3/04

***Alive and well on 1/3/2004***

***Note: This is a microbox please bring stamp pad and pencil!***

This is the 9th box in our `Legends of Jackson County' series.

Among the rich natural resources of Jackson County is the one honored
by this letterbox. If you look across the valley a half-mile or so,
you can plainly see that black gold remains important to Jackson
County in the 21st century.

It will help you solve this cipher if you remember the name of the
bucket you sometimes put this `black gold' in.

YQNHM SIUNU RVPQW VYKRU YKVIY YDXSV

YKHFX WPCGX ECLJL XXLFG WNYHF VEKFX

LYSJV BHYZE CJCEE WSGMZ HKLPS TAXMC

IWUIN MSSXV BXWCM NGQTR MSPKM BGLLG

NUMMS ITCMX HYXZG YTLEW AFY

As always, spacing is for ease of transcription only. When you find
it, email us at bigguy9210@yahoo.com and tell us how it's doing.
Have fun!
