I've been giving some thought lately to a possible late spring
Michigan letterbox gathering -- or perhaps a "Great Lakes" gathering -
- located centrally to include nearer areas of Illinois, Indiana and
northern Ohio.

Wondering a couple of things:

- First, is there interest? From MI? From surrounding states?
- Where exactly are letterboxers in MI located? I have a pretty
good handle on those in West MI, but not in the east or north.
- How far would people be willing to drive to attend? 1 hr? 2
hrs? 4 hrs? What is too far?

I know there was some talk of a Michigan gathering last summer, right
about the time I began letterboxing, but that kind of fell apart. I
have exchanged e-mail with the person who was trying to organize that
and hopefully we can avoid some of the pitfalls on this try.
