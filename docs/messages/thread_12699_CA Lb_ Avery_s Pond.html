This morning I checked up on this box and found that it is alive and
well. However, some pinheads toppled the stump that had been its home,
found the box and made an X-rated entry in the log. The only nice
thing I can say about them is that they didn't trash the box.

The log has been censored, the clue updated (dontcha just love that
Wes for setting up the new user friendly database?) and is once again
suitable for a PG rating. Kudos to those persistent letterboxers who
managed to find the box in spite of the altered hiding place. It even
took me a while to find it.

Anyone who has a yen to see the California foothills at their glorious
spring best must head for this box, NOW! The wildflowers will take
your breath away... Funhog
