Cute Cluckers Letterbox
 
Rated : Drive by
 
Planted By: Cock-A-Doodle-Moo 4-H  during the Wapping Fair  at Rye Street Park Sept. 8,9, 2001  This box was one of the Tolland County Fair boxes which now makes a permanent home at the site of yet another fair.
 
Street directions:  Go to Wapping near South Windsor Ct.  From Sullivan Ave. ( Rte 194)  Take Troy St. ( Battisons Cleaners on the corner) follow Troy St. to the end. This  T  intersection take a right on  Rye Street. The park is just a little  bit up the street on the right. 
 
Box directions:  Drive into this lovely park  where the option to turn left comes stay straight and count 6 single Maple trees on the left. Note the spot and park the car. From the sixth Maple  cross the driveway  enter the woods, a few feet in a tree was cut and under a chnk of its wood you will find a cute clucker.  The box has many stamps from  Tolland County Fair a great collection, now there are more pages to fill at this the Cute Cluckers permanent home.
    Beyond the tennis courts and to the left is where the fair is held. A small country fair with  a few rides , great french fries. 23 cows and quite a few  goats we brought 3 sheep and they were the show. One team of oxen walked to the fair from their home 1 1/2 miles away. The fire and police dept. had nice displays and 4-H kids brought home ribbons for the work of cleaning the animals and showing them. A  great weekend topped off by leaving the box .  Hope you all enjoy!  Be safe and have Fun!  Leader of the Pack & Cowboy with   5 more club friends.
