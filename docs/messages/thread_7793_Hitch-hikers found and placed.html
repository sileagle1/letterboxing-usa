We were getting pretty desperate that we were nearing 100 boxes and still no hitch-hiker so we planted another one:
 
The Answer to Life, the Universe, and Everything hitch-hiker set out to explore the galaxy on September 11, 2002  and  find happiness.   
 
Then we stumbled across one (what joy!): the Travelling Valentine, which was hiding out at the Valentine Mystery Box. It was so well camoflagued that at least one set of boxers thought it was the mystery box and didn't take it with them! It took us a few minutes to realize what it was.
 
One more box to go now to get the 100 (great excitement!)
 
Judi (Psychokiwi #1)
 
P.S. I've been following the discussion about whether a HH should have its own box or not. I've put mine in smallish rubbermaid boxes (which is not ideal), but  the Travelling Valentine is a huge box, which will make it very difficult to re-hide. Just something to keep in mind when designing HH.
