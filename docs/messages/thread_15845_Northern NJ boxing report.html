Northern NJ boxing report

From: Silent Doug (silentdoug@douglasgerlach.com) | Date: 2003-09-17 21:11:05 UTC-04:00
I logged the "Charmed for Sure" and "Merrill Creek" letterboxes (in
Washington, NJ) today. There was standing water in the Charmed for Sure
box, but the contents were safe and dry inside their baggies.

I also tried to find the Point Mountain box in Penwell, but the clues broke
down at the end. The clues are fine up to the point where you reach the
stone wall, but there is no longer any tree with 4 orange markers, which
makes it practically impossible to find the box. This box was planted in
June 1999, and it looks like new blaze markers have been put up since that
time. The box is unclaimed -- I don't know who "Floater" is and there's no
contact info on the clue, so this one may be a hopeless cause.
