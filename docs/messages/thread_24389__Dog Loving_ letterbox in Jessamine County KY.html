"Dog Loving" Letterbox by tsjwheeler (aka the Wheeler Family!)

Heading south on Main Street (27 business route) in Nicholasville, turn
left onto Longview Drive across from Kroger. Follow Longview until it
turns into Park Drive which leads into the park.

Enter INTO the City/County park property. Go straight past
the "island" on the left. Jessamine County Dog Park on the RIGHT hand
side.

BIG DOGS love letterboxes! Walk the boundary between big and small
puppy parks. At the end take a left. Walk 25 paces to large tree.

Dog park website: http://njcpr.org/Dog%20Park.htm

(Central KY, USA; Nicholasville, KY. Jessamine City/County Park)
