Here is a new Lewis and Clark Mystery Box!

Planted 9/20/02
By: Amanda from Seattle

Lewis and Clark explored the West,
Their Corps of Discovery was the Best of the Best!

One man, John Colter, did not want to go home,
He stayed in the west and continued to roam.

In the Summer of 1807, he saw amazing things,
Herds of Buffalo and geysers and hot springs.

When he tried to tell the folks back East about this place,
They didn't believe him, they laughed in his face!

So in this valley that they called "Colter's Hell",
when you are here, you'll take the Lone Star trail.

Once you are in front of the main attraction,
continuing onward across a footbridge is your next action.

After the second footbridge you'll enter a clearing,
continue onward and downed trees you'll be nearing!

Past the downed trees, a root crosses the path,
to your right 3 plus 3 steps, you do the math.

On your right is a large tree down on the ground,
behind a rock shaped like a triangle a letterbox is found!

should look up the word DISCREET before you go!
