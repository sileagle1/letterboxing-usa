#1 Taco Bell all items checked & noted  Consumption: 2 Tostados,1 Chicken Burrito Supreme, 1 Chicken Quesadilla, 1 Taco and 2 colas. Tallied all required items excluding food.
 # 2 Taco Bell - Popped 2 Tums prior to entering. All items counted and tallied.  Consumption: 2 small colas and two more Tums.
#3 Taco Bell - Two more Tums prior to entering.  All items checked and noted. Tallied figures over a shared cola.
We screwed up on the route number but using good investigative techniques we guessed at the most obvious possibility . As luck would have it we ended up across the street from a city dept.that had all of the addresses and locations on an accessible computer. We were delighted that sitting in their front garden was this historic bell .The city employee directed us to the other side of the street where we checked " Let the garden be your guide " but this box was empty. No letter box or guide was to be found .We were tempted to leave our Tums package but decided that we probably would need them later.We found no stamp, but realized that Ryan needs to put in a couple of disclaimers if he replaces the stamp . First- Bring plenty of antacids,  second do not search for this stamp on the narrow streets of San Luis Obispo driving a first time rental of a 26 ft motorhome.   Ryan, it was a burping great  3  hour adventure!  Don & Gwen
