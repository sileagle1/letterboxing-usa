From: Steve (shol3039@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-08 23:32:46 UTC
Wow! What a busy weekend!!!

Our apologies to those who could not find us... the opening
ceremonies at the Relay went a little long and when we got back we
had so many boxers at the RV that we did not make it to the grassy
island to pick up a few that were waiting. We're sorry about that and
If you made it to the relay and did not find us, email off list and
we'll make sure you get the event stamp. If anyone has a Relay coming
up and would like to borrow the stamp, let us know. It is headed to
Maine to Sealights but we can arange something!

That said, thanks to all who showed up Saturday to support us! At
the risk of missing someone, we visited with Butterfly, Bluebird,
Music Woman, Team Green Dragon, Patrick and Amy, Wanda and Pete,
Letterboxer 2002, DragonHeart, Sealights, and Teach and Preach! Wow!
Also visits from Ghandi, Stalin, and Joe Shmoe the HH. Madras found
lots of love from everyone and rewarded them with some Mystery
Munchies!

Butterfly's clues to the Syt-u-ation at Walden Preserve were released
and are now available online, check the Salem, CT listings. Also I
mentioned a new series on new land trust property in Waterford that
is partially planted and will be released in a few weeks! What a
great weekend!!!!!!

We thank everyone for the support.... we walked about 15.6 miles and
Steve has blistered heels from wet sneakers! Our team raised $3142
and actually beat the WCTY/Country Radio team's totals, and they had
months of air time to push for donations! Thanks to everyone,
especially Butterfly for her encouragement and her hard work putting
out the Walden Boxes.

-Steve, Heidi , Madras
