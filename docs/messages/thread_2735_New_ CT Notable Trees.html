"Double Happiness" at the 2nd Largest Red Oak in Connecticut
Quinnipiac Linear Trail, Wallingford, CT (New Haven County)
Placed by Aili, Bruce & Erin
July 7, 2001

Very Easy, 1/2 hour on foot, less on wheels

Proceed down trail until you reach the Oak, there's a plaque so it's easy.
Box stashed carefully in front of the oak, under rocks and wood and bushes and stuff. Carry box to bench a
little ways farther down to stamp in. Be discreet when retrieving and replacing box.

Getting there:
Easiest from Merritt Parkway Route 15.
