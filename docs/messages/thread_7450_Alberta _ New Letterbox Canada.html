Hello all,
Here is a new letter box with only the following clues and photos to assist you in your hunt.
City: Calgary, AB
Park: Prince's Island Park (Down town)

Find the attached landmarks to piece your puzzle together. A reading of 295 from the NW corner of the building in the photo will lead you right to the other two photos and the location of the box. Good luck!
