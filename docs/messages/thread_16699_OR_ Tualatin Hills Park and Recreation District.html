If you've placed boxes in this jurisdiction, please contact the land manager.
They have questions about the placements. Remember that under the LbNA Terms
of Use (http://www.letterboxing.org/lbna/tou.html), you warrant that you have
permission to place boxes where you do. I've asked the land manager to send
a list of boxes that violate their land use rules -- these boxes will be
removed from the site unless the placer works out something with them. So
if you care, go directly to the park people.

Cheers
Randy
