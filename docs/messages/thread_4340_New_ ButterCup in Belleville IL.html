ButterCup

Planted by MjLikePink & Mohmers


ButterCup is on a mission!
Help Her save the World before Bedtime!!

You can help by meeting her at Rotary Park on 6th street in Belleville IL.
[ N 38 30.910 W 089 59.440 ]


What is IT ?
!!!!
A star?
I don't think so.
But stand there anyway.
Head SE for 20 steps.
Then due East for 14 strides.
Turn south, take 9 steps to a little trail.
Be careful of the wicked looking tree on your right!
Head west on trail until the Star is 320 degrees NW.
ButterCup is under the broken concrete.
The cinder block is the only chunk you will need to move.
Put on your gloves and reach into the hole for ButterCup.
There will be two identical containers.


As long as you have the clues this will be an easy find. The hard part will
be in trying to be inconspicuous. Bring along a plastic bag to pick up trash
and no one will ask questions. Mj spent a lot of time finding snail shells.
:o) . Be sure to completely close ALL ziplocks.
Please carefully replace all the rocks so that the boxes are not washed away
in a heavy storm AND are hidden from prying eyes!
Bring gloves for reaching into dark, wet, buggy holes.
