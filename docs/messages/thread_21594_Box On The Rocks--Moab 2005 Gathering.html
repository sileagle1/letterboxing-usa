Box On The Rocks--Moab 2005 Gathering

From: cadenza74 (jjcadenza@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-03-22 00:29:13 UTC

We have been having some discussion about a couple of letterboxers
getting together in Moab and next thing we knew, it had blossomed
into a full-blown gathering with people coming from at least CO, UT,
ID, and MT. We are gettting excited, and I thought it might be nice
to let others know about the gathering in case they want to make a
trek out to some of the most fun country in the, well, country. We'd
love to have anyone join us, just let the organizers know who's
coming. It should be a blast. It looks as if it is shaping up to be
one of the largest gatherings yet in the Rocky Mountain area.

Here's the info from Kat and Y-Nought:

OK, here it is. With lot's of help from the kindly Y-nought, we have
got it organized!!!!!!

Box On The Rocks - 2005, Moab, Utah

Date: Sunday, April 10, 2005
Location: Rotary Park, Downtown Moab
Time: 2:00 - till whenever, maybe early evening?
What: Exchanges, maybe a little box hunting, lot's of laughing and
chatting, and did I mention pie???

Bring a picnic lunch and beverage and join us! I think everyone is
excited to finally meet each other and it is an awesome place to
visit! I will organize some desserts for everyone after their
lunches and we'll go from there!

Rotary Park: Unfortunately, no dogs are allowed in this park. It is
a nice green oasis with lots of trees and grass to relax in. Lot's
of playground equipment and the kids can just run!!!
