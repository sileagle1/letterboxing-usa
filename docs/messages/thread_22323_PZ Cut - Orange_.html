I asked Stampeaze about various colors (maybe you COULD get lavender!)
and whether this was just a temporary situation. Here's the reply:

Yes there have always been multiple colors available to me, but I have
always sold only white until now. Unfortunately due to part & chemical
changes at the manufacturing plant, over which I have no control, the
white no longer has the smooth surface that we need. The quality of
the
orange has remained unaffected, and is thus at this point the best
option available for us.

I will be able to continue to get white in a Grade B quality, but for
the moment I cannot say when we will be able to get Grade A quality in
white again.

--

Justin Pease
