Keeping to my promise of planting 10 letterboxes in 30 days (7/23-

below is the new Florida mystery box.



Created by: Tony & Nikki (Devonpeter@yahoo.com)

Clue Difficulty: Easy

Terrain Difficulty: Easy-Drive by


Description: On a Mystery University Campus


Clue:

There is a bull on the loose- last seen on a University campus in
Florida. The students have chased him on his stampeed across campus
through the engineering & past the medical area. He even eluded the
greeks before turning south toward the sun! He thought he had lost
them by heading down the dead end lollipop lane- but alas they
spotted him at the 10th iron lightpost. Fearing this was the end the
turned around and at 140 degrees he spotted the perfect hiding spot.
He crouched low between the three & sure enough the students gave
up. But not you! You know exactly where he is hiding don't you??


Happy Hunting!
