Happy Birthday
Rated: Easy (10 minutes or less)
Location: Southeast Park Mansfield Center, Connecticut
Planted By: Pink Trotters (Leader of the Pack is my Mom)

The Happy birthday box is a special box to celebrate Leader of the
Pack's birthday (10/7/02).

Take Route 89 to Warrenville Road. After the southeast school you
will see group of sports fields. This is Southeast Park; take the
dirt driveway to the back of the park. There is a trail opening
marked with a rock. Enter the trail and follow it to the left. You
will eventually be walking in an area with pines on both sides of the
trail. Toward the end of the pine section there is a very narrow path
down the hill toward the lake, follow that. You will see a tree with
three trunks, behind it is another tree with two trunks one is alive
and tall, the other is dead and short. The Happy Birthday box is
behind that tree under some sticks and leaves.

As always be discrete, close the lid tight and rehide the box WELL.
