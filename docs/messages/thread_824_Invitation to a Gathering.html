Springtime greeting to the group!

Please join us in southeastern Connecticut for a weekend get together
to do some letterboxing. After polling the list, we found the best
weekend for everyone will be May 13-14th. We have both camping and
lodging options for your stay:

Camping at Rocky Neck State Park in East Lyme. No reservations
necessary: there are lots of sites available on a first come/first
served basis. My family and I will go over Friday evening and/or early
Saturday morning and stake out our favorite sites ahead of time for the
group. Rocky Neck is a beach campground with some beautiful wetlands
and upland hiking. Directions: from I-95, take exit 72, the Rocky Neck
Connector. At the end of the road, turn left and find the park on your
right. For info, the telephone is (860)739-5471, and check online at
http://dep.state.ct.us/rec/parks/rnsppav.htm and
http://dep.state.ct.us/rec/parks.htm#Parks

We've got two recommendations for lodging: across the street from Rocky
Neck is a funky little 1950s era roadside motel, the Rocky Neck Motor
Inn (tel: 860-739-6268). A couple of miles down the road is a wonderful
B&B, the Inn at Harbor Hill Marina (tel: 860-739-0331, and online at
http://www.innharborhill.com

All the sites are surrounded by a multitude of fabulous letterboxes,
and although we can't duplicate last year's hot-tub bonding, there is a
terrific little blues club/brew pub in Niantic. Looking forward to
seeing you!

Jay & family (with help and encouragement from Sarah and Bob)
drewclan @ aol . com
