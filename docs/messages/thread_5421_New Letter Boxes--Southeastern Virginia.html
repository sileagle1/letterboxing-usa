We are so very addicted!  What a day!
Today we added two new letterboxes to the world...found two, picked up a hitchhiker  and discovered one missing....(we think).

Box 1.

Location:  Southeastern, Virginia -- Norfolk, VA
Name:  "Hermitage's Hermit"
e-mail: loveletterboxing@aol.com
dropped by "The Russ Clan" 5/19/02
Hermitage Foundation Museum grounds

Off of Hampton Blvd. in Norfolk, and down Northshore Road, you'll find the Hermitage Foundation Museum...one of the prettiest places in Norfolk.  As you are driving along Northshore Rd. you will skirt the water lined with rocks...look closely into the woods...there's a special "lighthouse" looking building nestled in the trees...it's a lovely site to see before you get to the grounds.  On the grounds somewhere--don't worry it's not a lot of ground to cover--you'll find a cobblestone path...along this path you will find a backwards brick bench...it looks like a little brick wall from the path...but closer inspection shows you it is something that can be sat upon.  Here as you sit...you will find seven little rounded bushes.  Hermitage's Hermit...dwells inside of one of these bushes.  Even if you don't make it to the inside of this unusual Museum the grounds are remarkable.  We especially love the mill stone pathways, lovely landscaping and views along the water.  Enjoy...you'll want to go back again!

Box 2

Location:  Southeastern, Virginia -- Virginia Beach, VA
Name:  "Meditation Garden"
e-mail: loveletterboxing@aol.com
dropped by "The Russ Clan" 5/19/02
Edgar Cayce Foundation grounds off of Atlantic Avenue

This is a tiny little plot of earth...but soooo special.

Say a prayer and meditate
cross the bridge--walk on slate

Have a seat, gaze at the wall
listen to the water fall.*

In a stack of rocks, above the pond
lies a box, and a pad to stamp upon.

It's near the ground and crystal blue,
it sits there waiting just for you.

It's on the right and towards the rear
you'll find the box that's to us so dear.

Take good care and leave your mark,
we're glad you've found this special park.


The Russ Clan
*today it seems the little water fall was disabled but you'll know it when you see it.

Stamped into the Meditation Garden box today. I never realized what
a bustling place that was! If you do go for this box try not to go at
lunchtime- very busy!

had the top of it pulled away but was no where near the bottom. Got
the box when he left for lunch. I debated not putting it back but it
didn't look as though he was going to find it where he is working.
You may want to go and see it's status and perhaps pull it until he
has it fixed?
