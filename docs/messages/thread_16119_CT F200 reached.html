From: birder579 (birder579@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-30 23:54:33 UTC
I went out boxing today in Killingly and I reached F200! The 200 th
box was the New Jersy state stamp on the Quinnibaug River Trail.

I have been boxing about a year now and I want to thank all you
wonderful people for carving such beautiful stamps and hiding them
in such nice out of the way parks and fun places to visit. I would
not know of these nice places otherwise. I hope to see many of you
at the DEP gathering Saturday and get more X's.

Happy boxing to all and to all a good F, P, A, X, HH, D, Z, W, L,
and all the letters you use. ;-)

The Bird Stamper
F203 P1 A(dopted)6 X39
