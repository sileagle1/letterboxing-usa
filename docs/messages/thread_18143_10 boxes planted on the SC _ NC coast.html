From: Roadsideguide (roadsideguide@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-03-16 21:16:27 UTC-08:00

Just got back from a 10 day tour of the South Carolina and North Carolina coast where we planted 10 new boxes. Spent most of the day cleaning up shells. The only one that isn't on the coast is at South of the Border--the south's favorite tourist trap. Clues for the following boxes can be found on the map:

Hilton Head, SC--Tower Beach Sand Dollar

Charleston, SC--White Post Garden Scallop Shell

Charleston, SC--Hampton Park Seagulls

Myrtle Beach, SC--Futrell Park Shell Collection

Carolina Beach, NC--Lake Park Sea Shells

Caswell Beach, NC--Oak Island Lighthouse

Bald Head Island--Bald Head Lighthouse

Corolla, NC--Currituck Beach Lighthouse

Nags Head, NC--The Curious Dunes of Jockey's Ridge

Dillon, SC--Pedro Post--South of the Border
