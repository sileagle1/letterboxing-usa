Since I can't remember which list was recently talking about these
two things, I thought I'd just post it to all 3 as others might be

I was looking at their magnifying lamp ($19.19) and ran across these
two things on clearance:

Archival Photo Corners - 240 corners for $1.67.
Portable Chair/Stool - 16" for $11.37 or 19" for $12.07

supplies and cutters, etc.

used to frequent when I lived in Illinois. Unfortunately, the closest
one to me now is a state away in Georgia. :-( Luckily, they have
online ordering.

I know it's not "kosher" to post about products, but thought some
might be interested.
