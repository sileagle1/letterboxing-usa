I'm not going to take it ...

From: Kim Stone (phoenix@phoenixascending.net) | Date: 2003-12-02 15:36:38 UTC
Hi all,

As many other non aggressive letterboxers have recently, I too am
leaving the list. After 4+ Digests a day of constant name calling,
complaining and down right nastiness, I've decided that I can do
something about it and leave. While I have silently read each and
every post, taken many letterboxing suggestions to heart, and hiked
in my own "pagan/hippie" way, I've gotten sadder and sadder about the
mutual disrespect many people have displayed.

This activity/hobby/fun loving lifestyle is marred by those who are
competitive in such a nasty way. At one time I looked forward to
reading people's adventures and excitement about new boxes. Now
reading the list is just a drag on my day. So ... in the spirit of
Yule and being thankful for what I've got ... I'm not going to take
it any more. I'll be in the RMLetterboxing list. It's much more fun
and friendly.

Ciao
Phoenix
