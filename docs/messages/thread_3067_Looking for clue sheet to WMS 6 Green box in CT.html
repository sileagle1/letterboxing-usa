Val and Bill, I don't mean to sound like an old curmudgeon, but I'm
not sure about posting other people's clues without permission. There
are a bunch of letterbox cluesheets that are not "on line" for the
whole world to see, and I assume that most of them are that way on
purpose. It could be that the WMS 6 Green boxes are different, since
they were a Middle School expository writing project. They may even
be orphans...but still, I don't think they should be scanned and
posted without permission.

Now why photocopying and sharing the old fashioned way would be any
different, I can't say, but I'm more comfortable with that. I also
have a complete cluesheet and think it's "legal" to share by sending
postal. If anyone would like one, just drop me your address off-line.

Jay in CT
