A grateful return home...

From: Eric Eurto (intentsone@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-03-08 19:56:14 UTC-08:00

I am happy to let everyone know (or y'all who know me)
that I have returned to the Waterford, CT Eastern
Mountain Sports as the Store Manager. I am very happy
to be back in the store that had a huge part in
sparking the interest of Letterboxing in CT and am
planning having a number of hikes and clinics this
year. I will also be posting the Sleeping Giant
gathering on our store website (www.ems.com) and will
keep you all updated and or posted on any upcoming
store-led letterboxing outings. For a final thought, I
will also be updating our letterboxing clue binders in
the store and will be including many of the eastern CT
clues in those binders. Also, please note that we do
have some clues in those binders sponsored by our
store that do not exist on the LBNA website.... gotta
come in to get those! Happy hunting all and I hope to
see you soon!

Cheers!
