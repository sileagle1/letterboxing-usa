Froggy Series - VA

From: (kauket@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-05 23:23:27 UTC
I went out on a hunt for more letterboxes today, since I had today
off work. I found the Balls Bluff series in Leesburg, VA. All in great
condition. Then I drove to Sterling, VA to look for the Froggy Goes a
Courtin' series. I found box #1 with no trouble, but could not find
box #2 no matter how hard I looked. I did find a tree and some
unnaturally placed rocks that matched the clues, but no box. Judging
by the number of prophylactics littering the clearing, I suspect the
box has been stolen. Can anyone tell me whether I just was looking in
the wrong spot or the box is really gone? The last two in the series
were found and in great condition.

- Briar
