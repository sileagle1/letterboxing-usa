WA: 3 new boxes in Chuck's Tacklebox Series, Pierce Cty, WA

From: Nail Family (c.nail@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-06-30 02:33:55 UTC
Placed 3 more boxes in the Chuck's Tacklebox Series. These
new boxes are located in Pierce County at Rapjohn Lake (1 box
so far, another to go there soon) and Tanawax Lake (2 boxes)
both near Graham, WA. Clues on the new lbna site.

I have 7 more stamps/boxes ready for launch in this series.
Have to wait for a clear weekend away from work to get out there
and place them.


PS. A reminder, in this series one box in each pair is placed at
the public launch area that is accessable to non-boaters while
the other box in each set is located on the lake and does require
a boat to find. It is my goal to give the fishing boxers out there a
good opportunity to enjoy both sports simultaneously. For those
non boaters who would like to find the lake boxes, Tanawax has
3 different lake resorts that rent boats. Other boxes to be placed
soon may have other rental boat opportunities but that is not my
main concern in placing the boxes. My main concern is to place
boxes at the lakes that I enjoy fishing the most.

Have fun and let me know how you like them.
