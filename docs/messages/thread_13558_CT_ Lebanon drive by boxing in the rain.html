CT: Lebanon drive by boxing in the rain

From: (RNSaunders@aol.com) | Date: 2003-06-14 00:11:42 UTC
Friday is our only day to box together now a days so to we planed to
get a couple drive by's in the rain. First stop was Brandi's
memorial box, not really a drive by, but the rain let up and we
lucked out. Then to the blue goose of Lebanon. We found the first
box of the Lebanon Loop then got the Capt. Gray box. Stopped for
lunch at Uncle D's Italian restaurant for a couple sandwiches. Very
good, quiet atmosphere. Then found the rest of the Lebanon loop
boxes. All boxes were found in very good condition. Next time it's
raining we're heading for Mansfield!!! All in all, the area is full
of history and good boxing! The two person, one holding an umbrella
over the one doing the stamping method works quite well-- everything
was dry except our legs from the knees down Oh Well!! Better than
staying home any day-- Box On people!!!!!! Sunshine+Swamp Yankee
