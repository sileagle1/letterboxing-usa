Speaking for myself, here, but I think I can also promise help from other
South Florida boxers, not that there are that many of us . . . yet.

I think I can guarantee that if anyone plants a box in Miami-Dade, Broward,
or Palm Beach counties, and you get a report of a problem with your box, we can
get someone out there to check on it for you. So if anyone's thinking of
planting one as they pass through our area, PLEASE DO!

If you're going to be farther away than that, well, we'll try, but it may
take longer before someone gets out that way. Still, we'd be thrilled if you
plant one, and we'll "do our best" as we say in Cub Scouts.

Well, it's in Bayside park in Miami. I didn't have any transportation,
so I had to just do what I could. It's a very easy location.

have to climb a bit. I hope you find it....let me know if you do!
