Procedure For Placing Hitchhikers

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-11-05 02:47:43 UTC

Lately I have noticed many of the HH I find have not
been stamped into the logbook of the box they currently
reside in, and sometimes the stamp of the hostel has
not even been put into the logbook of the HH. This is
just a reminder that you should do both when placing HH
in a new box, along with stamping in with your own stamp.
The owners of both will thank you.
