Moonstone Baby,
I have planted 6 letterboxes in Leominster, which is the next town over from
Fitchburg. I am sorry I will not be in town this weekend as I would have
loved to be your guide to our area. 2 of the boxes are extremely easy to find
(Native Son Bernard Doyle, basically a drive-up and Native Son Lou Little just
a short hike around a pond)the other four are longer hikes, each about an hour
to an hour and a half). I hope you enjoy your visit. We met at the gathering
at Sleeping Giant, my stamp is the 3 Wise Men.
