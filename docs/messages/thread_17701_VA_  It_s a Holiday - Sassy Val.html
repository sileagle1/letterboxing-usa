It's a Holiday - Val

And you thought "It's a Holiday" meant a crudely carved eraser. Meet
Sassy Val. She may not be appropriate for the kiddies. She is the
direct descendent of last year's valentine. Very few saw that stamp
before it went missing. I doubt if anyone got the reference to the
valentine's heart's origin.

I just had to make Sassy Val tall. Originally she was meant to bend
over to fit into the microbox (like the Queen Mary II). But Night
Owl just couldn't bare to watch me saw the lady in half. "Just make
the box bigger." Hence tall Sassy Val now lays in the
first "extended" microbox. I recommend bringing red and black
markers. Please be extra careful replacing her, centered on the
velcro so she won't fall.

Scarab of the Doubtful Guests

three parts I had there for the Icebreaker.
