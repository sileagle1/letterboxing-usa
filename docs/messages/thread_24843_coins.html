I've never bought a geocoin so I can't help you with that part of the question, but to make my answer letterboxing-related, there's a cool letterboxing geocoin available. Here's a photo: http://www.geocoindesign.com/images/lbgold.jpg

A good size for a stamp -- I'd say under 2 inches. I carry two sig stamps - a larger one, 2" square for the average logbook and a smaller one, 1" square for small logbooks. If the logbook is smaller than 1", I'll leave a partial stamp image.

I own a couple of that letterboxing geocoin :-) It's very nice and
was designed to take ink. I've been looking for a nice place to hide
a box that would use that as a stamp. I've been trying to think of a
good way to hide a cache that would also be a letterbox, or
vice-versa, if you prefer ;-) This geocoin would be the stamp, and
cachers could "Find" the geocoin. It would be a way of helping each
group learn a little more about the other.
