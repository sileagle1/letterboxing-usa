I'm biased, perhaps, but may I suggest either College Park or Greenbelt, MD?

from the College Park metro, and 94th Aerosquadron Restaurant would be a

have a certain Woody-Guthrie-power-to-the-people-esque charm that all DC
Metro residents should experience at least once in their lifetime.
