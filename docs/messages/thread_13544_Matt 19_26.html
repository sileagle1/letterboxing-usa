Matt 19:26 is my life verse. It is from the Bible, and its how I sign my
name -- on just about everything. It is that one verse that has special meaning
to me. I had panic attacks that began in 1990 and I wasn't able to get out of
the house at all. Not to go to the mailbox, no where! I prayed and medicated
and prayed and I was finally "well" enough to go out of the house some. In
1994 I went to a church (my current church) where I heard the Pastor preaching
and using Matt 19:26 in his sermon. "But Jesus beheld them, and said unto
them, with men this is impossible; but with God all things are possible." My
Pastor actually used a reference to medicine here. He said that the Drs can give
you medicine and you can still be sick, but that God can heal you completely.
God made you and God can do the impossible. So, I took this verse and
prayed over it and believed it. When I truly believed it, and in His time, God
healed me. This year I might have had one panic attack (from a bad experience). I
am now married and have three small children and we go everywhere, often.
LOL God was too good to me to not put that verse after my name. This was
probably more than you asked for but thanks for reminding me how good God is!

Detra
Matt 19:26
