From: sillly_ol_bear (redsmon@aol.com) | Date: 2004-09-07 01:19:09 UTC
I agreee we should be careful with the media. Recently, we had
reporters emailing group members and posting on our southeast list. I
guess what we also need to consider is do we comply and let more
experienced boxers do the talking or chance them getting their hands
on a newbie that doesn't have a full understanding of the "boxing
experience". Call it damage control I guess but it may be one way for
us to control what is shared about letterboxing.

Growth is nice, but a slow progression is going to be better in the
long run. Boxing has exploded here in Georgia. We are concerned but
we also recognize that with some it will blow over when the next fad
shows up in a magazine or tv program. For the others, the quality
boxes will last.

All we can do is prepare for the worst and hope for the best.
