For Whom the Bell Tolls
Placed: 9/22/02
By: Amanda from Seattle

FOR WHOM THE BELL TOLLS

IN OUR TIME a letterbox was hidden. No need to travel to THE GREEN
HILLS OF AFRICA, it is right here in the United States. TO HAVE AND
HAVE NOT a compass is no big deal. It was THE DANGEROUS SUMMER and I
think there was a DEATH IN THE AFTERNOON. But that is what you can
expect when you have MEN WITHOUT WOMEN. I traveled to THE GARDEN OF
EDEN where THE SUN ALSO RISES. I visited the gravesite and a
monument to THE OLD MAN AND THE SEA. From the monument, it was 20
steps to the right along THE TORRENTS OF SPRING. There were no
ISLANDS IN THE STREAM and no need to go ACROSS THE RIVER AND INTO THE
WOODS. Beside a concrete slab and a square green box is a clump of
trees and in the middle of the trees is a letterbox. WINNER TAKE
NOTHING, just an imprint of the stamp to add to your book. A
FAREWELL TO ARMS until the next letterbox hunt!
