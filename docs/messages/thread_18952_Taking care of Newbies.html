Taking care of Newbies

From: (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-27 22:26:35 UTC
Here's a suggestion for further thought:

Now that there are letterboxers in each state, some states with many
letterboxers and some wishing for more, and each with thier own
regional nuances, I would suggest that when the new site goes up, we
have a page that lists "state letterboxing ambassadors" or
a "contact a local letterboxer" page that newbies in a particular
state could/should use for more information on letterboxing in
general and in the particular area/state. That way their questions
would be directed to someone with an inherent interest in answering
them, and the ambassador/contact could also help them get introduced
to the local and national letterboxing community.

This idea came to mind because I personally seem to have become the
WI letterboxing ambassador without much effort. I have had a number
of recent WI newbies contact me personally for information about
letterboxing in WI and in general, just from them finding my email
address on my clue pages. (I'm sure this has happened to other
letterboxers as well, and I'm not alone.)

Sometimes when starting something new all one needs is a new friend
or a mentor to see them through.

If this come to be, I volunteer to be the WI contact/ambassador.

Dragon
