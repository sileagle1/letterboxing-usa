Chincoteague Letterbox Clues
Chincoteague WMA, Virginia
Placed November 24, 2000 by Squirrel

Take the Scenic Road and enjoy all the abundant waterfowl. When you have
almost completed the loop you will come to a service road. Take that road to
your right and go down it for about 4.5 miles until you come to Area 7. Stop
there and look to the left side of the road for a tree by a stream. The tree
is on the right side of the stream. Look at the base of the tree under a log
for the letterbox. Cover it back well so it does not get washed away.
