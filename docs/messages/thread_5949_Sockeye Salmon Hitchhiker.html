From: (rmlay@mindspring.com) | Date: 2002-07-02 10:54:47 UTC-04:00
Hey Everyone--
I have a request:
Somehow, my Sockeye Salmon Hitchhiker has been separated into two pieces--the
logbook went one place, and the stamp went another. I heard that someone
created a makeshift logbook for the stamp, but I'd really like to have the
originals back together! Amanda (The Paisley Orca) was nice enough to send
the original logbook to my father on the west coast because we thought the
stamp was hiding in Bridle Trails, Washington. But we don't think it's there
anymore because the box has reportedly been visited a few times since the
stamp was seen there. SO, if anyone knows where the Sockeye stamp is right
now, please let me know so I can put my Hithchiker back together!
Thanks :)
--Buckaroo
