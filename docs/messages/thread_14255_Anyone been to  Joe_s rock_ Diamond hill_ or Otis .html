Thanks for wanting to visit my three letterboxes. At Otis Farm Trail you will need Bug Repellent. Diamond Hill isn't all that hard to hike up if you stay on the cut trail. After visiting the letterbox follow the trail heading in a southern direction for some great views from ledges. Also after your visits, stop at the Ice Cream Machine across from Diamond Hill Park. THe long lines are worth the wait. I think it's one of the best Homemade ice cream stops in the state.
