Appeal to Western Mass Boxers

From: monotropa (bsennott@crocker.com) | Date: 2003-10-07 13:33:33 UTC
Hey folks! Letterboxers in eastern Mass. are doing an amazing
job of planting boxes, but what about beautiful western Mass?
We've got some great ones out here, but we could use more. So
this is an appeal to all western Mass. letterboxers: Hide at least
one box in October! Let's give everybody a reason to come out
here this fall (in addition to the gorgeous foliage).

And thanks again to Deanne and Ginger for organizing the
five-year anniversary gathering at Wendell in August. It
re-energized my letterboxing spirit.

Bonnie
