Gilroy Hitchhiker Hostel Planted

From: Aahz (mraahz@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-05-02 02:39:35 UTC
Zairabear and I planted the Gilroy Hitchhiker Hostel over the
weekend. It is currently seeded with 5 HHs, but has room for PLENTY
more.

Located just off the junction of highways 101 & 152 we hope it sees
plenty of use during the summer travel season.

Unlike many HHHs there is no "take one/leave one" requirement for
this Hostel. We wanted to encourage our visiting HHs to travel, so
feel free to pick up one or more even if you don't have any to leave
behind. Or if you've picked up a few on your journey, feel free to
leave them all here in confidence that they will not rest too long!
All we ask is that there always be at least one HH left in the box
for the next visitor.

The Hostel is located behind the counter in the You Got Memories
scrapbooking store, which is a place we visitat least once a week,
so it should be well maintained. We also intend to keep it well
stocked with hitchhikers ourselves as we are both starting HH series.

You can see the clues and log your visits to this hostel at
http://www.atlasquest.com/lboxes/showboxinfo.html?gBoxId=14625

-Aahz
