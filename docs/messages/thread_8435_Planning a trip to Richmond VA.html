With the exception of Hollywood Cemetary, all the Richmond boxes are
in dogs-on-leash-friendly parks and locations. I can't recall a "No
Dogs Allowed" posting at Hollywood, but I would guess that if your
dog was on leash and held close, it would go unnoticed as there are
rarely too many people around, unless you catch the weekend walking
tours.

Maymont Park, although lovely with many great box hiding spots, does
not allow dogs. Been there to plant one with Maggie and had to
leave. As far as I know, there are no letterboxes there.

Have fun and good luck!
