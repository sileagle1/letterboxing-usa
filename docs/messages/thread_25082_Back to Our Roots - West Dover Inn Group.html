It was great to meet all of you letterboxers staying at West Dover Inn, and also letterboxing with some of you (even with the extra hike at the end). Thanks for being so nice to my non-letterboxing wife, too. She had fun hiking and talking with all of you.

I'm still laughing about that biker looking for nude letterboxers on the trail and I have a related story for you. After we left the Inn that morning, I tried for one more box that required parking at an Inn and walking across a meadow. I was gone a while trying to find the box, but my wife was not bored. It seems a neighbor next door walked out to get his paper and he was completely naked! I thought I had seen it all during this Event, but my wife saw more :)

Silver Eagle
