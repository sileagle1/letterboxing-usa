I've made some great friends through letterboxing.

in Boise, ID on the Rocky Mountain letterboxing site, which I
believe allows public access to archives. This was such a fun
day for us. A few came from WA, but other than them, this picture
shows you every single letterboxer in the state of Idaho!! We've
more than doubled our numbers in the last six months, but when
you consider that there were only two of us before, it isn't quite
such a feat!

I will also do one of the Salt Lake gathering as soon as I get my
film back. We held both inside due to very cold temps and snow,
but somehow A-Bear and I ended up tromping out in the snow
looking for boxes after both gatherings. I guess we're gluttons
for punishment!

anyway, here's the reference for the Rocky Mountain group:

have a great day boxing!
