For anyone heading to RI,
Day of the Dragon King is a great hike with a very cool stamp. David and I
did this hike today (sans kids) and it took us 2 1/2 hours. We saw all kinds
of great things. Beautiful woodland plants in bloom, a million baby toads
and even 2 great horned owls. Bring bathing suits if the weather is right
because right when you are longing for it, the river rambles by. Thanks
Trish for a great series.
Laurette and David
