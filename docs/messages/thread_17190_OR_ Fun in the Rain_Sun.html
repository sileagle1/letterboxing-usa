OR: Fun in the Rain&Sun

From: jedaryn02 (jedaryn02@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-12-06 22:26:42 UTC
Thank you all for planting such great boxes in and around the
Portland area. I had so much fun trying to find boxes.
I was blessed to meet Sleepy Whippet, Bookworm and Funhog. Thanks
for taking such good care of me ladies and linking up with me.

I wish I were there today for your gathering, and I hope it goes well
for you. =-)

I'm back in Idaho and want to report on the following boxes that I
found while in Oregon:

Canine Series/Playful Whippet in Gresham are alive and well. Cute
series.

Circus Memories are doing fine in Gresham too. So fun.

Birthday Box is doing well and under cover. I was wondering what in
the world is a huge green birthday cake-perfect!!

Lewis & Clark Microbox is looking good. What a great idea for a mini
logbook. =-)

Oregon Zoo boxes were fun. Thank you Funhog for your patience with
me and not laughing too hard. Bethany was laughing at me too as far
as the Wapiti as I've used that in clues before she so nicely pointed
out to me. Oops! I do have an awful memory. =-) I especially
loved Packy's Place-perfect spot and great stamp. I need one of
those handy dictionary.coms too. =-) I've used it and the plastic
bag lots these past few days. =-)

Department of Reduncancy Department Box in Oregon City-what great
clues that were great and that tree is an amazingly amazing tree!!

End of the Oregon Trail box is well, but the stamp seems to be
falling apart. I was so impressed with the mammoth wagon. Awesome!

Silver Falls State Park-I was running out of daylight since I got a
late start so I want to return for the rest. What a neat park. I
found francais falls =-), South Falls, Winter Falls and Buddah. They
were doing well and what a great day to go with sun and few visitors
there.

Whoozy Goldfish II in West Salem is as whoozy as ever and waiting for
visitors.

Spruce Goose is fine in McMinnville...I was a little spooked when I
walked by the GIANT shroom and it was set up for a homeless person,
but the stamp was so cute and the walkd fun.

CT/OR Exchange is well and what fun. I hope to return and enjoy more
of those great trails. =-)

Ace of Fun-thank God I figured out at least one. =-) Thanks Funhog
for more Fun!! Spirit of Dartmoor is killing me...but I'll figure it
out one of these days.

Hidden Palms in the rain is doing well too. We had to rehide
Multnommah Falls as it wasn't in its original spot. Fun in the rain.

Road Trip 2003-we searched, but couldn't find it so I'll email
Heather off line to see if she knows more on it.

Thank you again to all for all your boxes. I'm not sure when I'll be
back, but I look forward to it. =-)

A-Bear back in Star, Idaho
