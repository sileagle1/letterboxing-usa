Back in 2001, I created a letterbox in St Paul, MN. Alas, this box,
the "Battle Creek Regional Park" box got repeatedly vandalized, and
after I moved from the area, I could no longer repair/replace it. But
I still had the stamp that got rescued from it, so I've recycled this
box as the "Misplaced Minnesotan" hitchhiker.

The "Misplaced Minnesotan" HH has started it's travels near my home.
I planted it today at the Footloose Caboose letterbox in Grantham,
NH. I hope that eventually the "Misplaced Minnesotan" will eventually
be transported back to Minnesota.
