Snail Mail Camera Letterbox Sign Up

From: Laura (naturbuf@gwi.net) | Date: 2003-09-08 22:09:15 UTC
Last call to sign up for the snail mail camera letterbox! I floated
the idea about a hitchhiking camera letterbox on the list a bit ago
and got lots of responses. Based on suggestions and concerns I've
decided to try it as a snail mail letterbox first. Each person who
receives the stamp will also have to take a picture of themselves and
I have someone who has volunteered to replace the camera part way
through its travels so it can go to more people before returning to me
(thanks Lone Pony!) I still have space left on the list. If you're
interested, please send me your letterboxing name, real name, and
mailing address and I will put you on the list. FYI- I ended up
carving a different image than the one I originally intended, so I'm
going to call this box "Sam the Camera Man" instead of "Candid
Camera". I guess if you sign up to get the box you'll see why. Please
send me this info ASAP so I can get this thing moving. Thanks!
Nautilus
