From: Bonnie Sennott (bsennott@crocker.com) | Date: 2002-08-09 19:08:28 UTC-04:00
Today I checked on the two boxes (Moon & Stars and Salamander) that I hid
at the Quabbin Reservoir in western Mass. three years ago. They're both in
good shape and have had plenty of visitors. I left souvenirs in one of the
boxes for the next five finders and put a new hitchiker, the Massachusetts
Armadillo, in the other one. Both boxes got new logbooks as well. I also
added some hints to the clue to make the Salamander Letterbox a little less
tricky to find (at least I hope so!).
