From: mothermoo2001 (cstearns07@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-11-12 04:12:22 UTC
Thanks to all who have been giving me heads up on my boxes in need of
repair. I carved a new Ship to Neverland tonight as well as a roof

to verify and replace. It is on the same trail as the new Watchful
Eye. I was out of boxing for awhile and have a lot of catching up to
do. Thank you to all of you who have fixed a box yourselves with
paper or ink or whatever it really helps! And when will I get the
whole bag of boxes in my car planted? Bet you can't wait! Carol -
