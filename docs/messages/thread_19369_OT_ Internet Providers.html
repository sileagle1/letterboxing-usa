connect, I am lucky if it is at 28kbps. There is major lag time where

anything on their side could be wrong.

I don't want to go through the whole process again.. And that means my
phone line is tied up all day.

From: Tiger Moses (tigermoses@houston.rr.com) | Date: 2004-07-06 12:23:49 UTC-05:00
To rule out your phone lines 100%, move you computer to a friends house,
neighbor what, and have it connect via thier phone line
its its flawless, it is your line.
If it the same problem, then the problem could be your modem or it could be
ISP related.

When they give you a DSL line down here, they give you a filter for your
regular phones to cut of
noise the human ear doesn't generally hear, but just in case (but a modem
would hear it).

If you can get a hold of one of these, that could make a difference.
