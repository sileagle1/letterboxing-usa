IT GENERALLY GOES AGAINST MY BETTER JUDGEMENT TO PUT OUT BOXES IN OR AT AREAS THAT REQUIRE AN ENTRANCE FEE.  BUT THIS PLACE MAY BE THE EXCEPTION. BUSHKILL FALLS IN THE POCONOS IS  VERY AWE-INSPIRING PLACE. PLAN TO TAKE A HIKE YOU CAN SPEND ABOUT THREE HOURS HERE HIKING A SLOW HIKE. THERE ARE NUMEROUS TRAILS THAT ARE WELL MARKED. THE RED TRAIL HAS YOU TRAVERSING OVER 1200 STEPS. DON'T GO TO THIS AREA DURING A DROUGHT.
 
CLUES
 
BUSHKILL FALLS
 
STANDING ON A TRAIL WITH A STAIRWAY BEHIND ME I SEE THE MAIN FALLS @ 20 DEGREES.
I SEE A STRAIGHT PINE TREE @ 350 DEGREES.
AT YOUR BACK BEHIND  A ROCK WEDGED INTO A TREE.
 
THIS IS VERY BUSY PLACE AND LOTS OF PEOPLE AROUND BE CAREFUL TO HIDE WHAT YOU ARE DOING
