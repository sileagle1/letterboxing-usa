"`ZOO Two"
Placed by Azobox
October 13, 2002
Easy urban park walking
.
"'Zoo Two" is hidden in Milham Park, in the southeast corner of
Kalamazoo, Michigan. (The park is adjacent to Milham Golf Course).
The main entrance to the park is located at the corner of Kilgore
Road and Lovers Lane. (Easily accessible from I-94: use the Portage
Rd exit then Kilgore west to Lovers Lane and the park). Check a city
map for details. Mapquest or links from City of Kalamazoo website
Parks Division should help.
Note: This is a popular and well-used park. Be discreet when
searching/discovering the box to avoid revealing its location.
Further caveat: if the ground is snow covered you may experience
difficulty with this box.

Park in the main lot at the Kilgore/Lovers Lane corner.
Now the fun startsand always remember to look high and low as you go!
Standing in the stone shelter let the red arrow point the way.
Follow the paved walkway heading north. At the cross go right for
100 paces. Cross & cross then step up 15 and on to 139 below. Sight
340 degrees to the talkingtree. Follow the line two more. Though it
splits, it makes no trouble. Now look 20 degrees and go on to find a
lonely blockhead with hair.
