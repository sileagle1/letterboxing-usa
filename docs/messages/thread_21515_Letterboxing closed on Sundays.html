Fridays leading into long weekends.

days recovering from the past weekend adventures of letterbox finds.
What do you think?

Re: [LBNA] Letterboxing closed on Sundays

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-03-09 17:00:37 UTC

Heck, closed on Sunday seems a minor impediment
to me! I have letterboxes hidden on land that is closed
months at a time. My Oak Island boxes in OR are
inaccessible during the winter waterfowl migration
and my new Ae'o box on Maui is in an area closed
during nesting season, behind locked gates.
There are many boxes hidden up in the Cascade
range that are under several feet of snow for much
of the year. As I see it, this is just one of the fun
challenges of letterboxing. Get 'em while you can!
