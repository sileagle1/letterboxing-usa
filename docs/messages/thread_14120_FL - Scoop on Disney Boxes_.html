Re: [LbNA] FL - Scoop on Disney Boxes?

From: Lynne Dinger (lynne@lynnedinger.net) | Date: 2003-07-08 11:34:25 UTC-04:00
My husband and I found the Wilderness Lodge box last year when we went down
for vacation. It was fairly simple to find. We had to do it dead reckoning
though, as we forgot the compass. We didn't have time in our schedule to
look for the other two. It was one of our first boxes. And it had a
hitchhiker in it! Our first! The Wilderness Lodge is a beautiful hotel. It
has a geyser that erupts every so often. Also, tell your friend to have
lunch in the restaurant there. I forget the name but it is the less
expensive of the two in the hotel and is directly off the lobby. Excellent
nachos and, if he's got little kids, they have hobby horses for the kids to
ride. Hope this helps!
Lynne

Re: FL - Scoop on Disney Boxes?

From: devonpeter (devonpeter@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-09 00:41:15 UTC
We searched a month or so ago. Here's what we found. The
Celebration is still here & a must see town, we love it there. I
believe "The mouse" has confiscated the two at the Disney hotels.
There was a mouse trap (no I'm not kidding) exactly were the
carribean beach was supposed to be. So either we were off or they
are gone. There is one that is not to far called Sebastian. The
hardest part is getting through the gate guard. Try saying that you
are interested in the property and want to drive by some houses.
There are tons for sale in the neighborhood (I'm in Real Estate-
definitely not a good sign). Watch for ticks at this one. I think
that celebration would hook them for sure

Nikki
