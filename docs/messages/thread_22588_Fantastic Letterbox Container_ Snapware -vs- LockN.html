Hello again...

The "Snap 'N Lock" by SNAPWARE that I bought does look like the Lock N
Lock that some of you have mentioned. It looks like the Lock N Lock has
the side flaps that flare out a little bit and are more rounded...
other than that, I don't see a big difference. Both claim to be air and
water-tight.

The one I mentioned was $2.49 where I purchased it (Kroger,) which is a
pretty reasonable price -- especially for boxers like me who don't
place many boxes each year(!) But I did find a link easily to these
boxes online right from the Snapware company. Just Google the word
SNAPWARE and you'll find all kinds of hits. You can buy the little
sizes individually direct from Snapware, without having to buy a whole
huge set... but it looks like the price is about double what I found it
for. I think it was $4.99. Again, still not a bad price if you only do
this hobby occasionally.

I think I'm off to see if I can scarf up a few more of these at my
Kroger before they disappear!!!
