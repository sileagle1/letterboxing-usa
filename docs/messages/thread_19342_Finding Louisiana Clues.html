Search for clues using the LbNA 'offical' site. I don't know of any
other site that have postings. ATTENTION: There is a trick to
finding them on the site. Here are the clues to our LA clues. At
the home page of www.letterboxing.org click 'search for boxes'at the
bottom of the page. Then under 'pick a state' select 'Louisiana'
then click 'search' Bamm there they are. Do not try clicking on
the map page because you'll only get three clues. Louisiana is the
only state to have Parishes instead of Counties therefore the Map
doesn't work. No telling how many boxers pass us by do to that
clich.
Tiger please check the clues just before coming down I think I may
place some this July 4th weekend. I'll be typing clues with one
hand and lighting fireworks with the other.
