From: Jay Drew (drewclan@aol.com) | Date: 2000-11-05 15:37:02 UTC
With the addition this week of our Village of Hogsmead letterbox,
we've completed our Harry Potter series that began with Gryffindor
Tower ( http://members.aol.com/_ht_a/drewclan/tower.htm ). With the
schools, the village, and the Quidditch balls, there are a total of 9
letterboxes to be discovered.

On the way back from Gryffindor the other day we ran across a group
of three children noisily scampering along the trail above us. Soon
after their Mom, with a 4th Weasley-looking child, hiked up. Hoping
the kids would wait up, she was calling "Harry? HARRY!!!"

Jay (in honestly I don't make this stuff up) Connecticut
