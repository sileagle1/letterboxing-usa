Book Keeper Letterbox
Placed by: Lottabit & Lillbit
Date: March 23, 2003
County/State: Cowlitz, WA (Region 5)
Town: Longview
Current # of Boxes in Series: 3

The Book Keeper Letterbox will house clues to other boxes based on, what we
consider, classic novels. Right now, the Book Keeper box has clues to two
other boxes (The Count of Monte Cristo & Treasure Island). If you have
letterboxes that fit the basic theme and hide them in this general area,
feel free to hide clues to them in the Book Keeper box. Just email us
off-list and let us know!

Clue: In the side yard of the Longview Public Library, you'll find many
trees. From the tree giving shelter to the Book Keeper, you can see brown
mountains, a musical child's toy, a very still girl reading to a cat, a
president's white head and a woodsy squirrel. Once you've found this tree,
you'll find the Book Keeper is the 'root' of this series.

NOTE: We suggest bringing along a variety of colored ink pens to do this
series. The stamps look best when stamped in color!
