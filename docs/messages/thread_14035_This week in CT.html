Just thought I'd report the status of boxes we found this week. We started the week at Odilio's new

John Wayne Series - Very enjoyable. Love the trivia. I now need to rent JW movies as I have only seen 50% of the movies mentioned on the trail. I love the bridge on this trail!
Elephant series (South) Found 5 of the 6 but that was my fault. I missed a paragraph and when I realized it we were too far away for the little feet in our group to back track. I am still catching grief on this one from Freedom Fox! Anyway the Horton Box & Jumbo are my favorites!
RTRW the kids & I went to Beaver Pond, Windham Trust Duck Blind (the kids LOVED this one), New York SQ, JJZR, Gone Fishing, Troop 25, and Putnam Airline Bridge. Then we ate at a restaurant not far from this trail called Nikki's that is train themed. Tied right in!
Yesterday we (F.Fox, Odillio, Licks & myself) found Pink Panther, Totem Pole, Merrow Park, 3 Wizards, Circle of Life, & Friendly Skies. ( All but 1 in good shape as Odillio mentioned in an earlier post)
Today we found our final piece of the gazebo (we have had all but 1 piece since January), Ghoste Riders in the Sky, Rhode Island SQ, Bats in My Belfry, Litter LB (We had an X here with Von der Insel) St Louis, Bridge Over Troubled Water, and Life in the Fast Lane. We watched some go-cart racing from here! Fun spots, fun week!!! Thanks to all the planters!
