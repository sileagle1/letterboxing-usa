Salt Spring State Park, Franklin Forks PA

From: The McQuades (mcquades@twcny.rr.com) | Date: 2002-06-09 23:51:22 UTC-04:00
The first of our family's two finds today, also shared with our favorite
letterboxing friend (who really should post for himself one of these
days...). This was a more difficult trek for the three year old in our
party, with lots of off-trail walking. The ground was quite wet and
slippery in places with fallen trees over the path as well, but still
doable for little legs.

There was a bit of water in the box when we found it, but the book and
stamp were safe and dry in their bags. The park itself was a little tricky
to find (at least for a driver distracted by chattering junior letterboxers
in the back seat) but was definitely worth the two hour drive for us.
