If anyone is interested in some additional walking during the weekend
of the RI "get-together", You might want to try the "Walking Weekend"
to be held in the NorthEast "quiet corner" of CT. There are over 70
free guided hikes planned for the three day weekend during "foliage"

or phone 860-963-7226 to have a brochure mailed to you.
