Harry's Secret Wizard Cache
by Jared's Seekers

N 38 47.844 W 090 42.552
State: O'Fallon, Missouri , St. Louis County
Hidden on: 2/3/2002

(ratings out of 5 stars)
Difficulty: * Terrain: *

The cache is a (roughly) Harry Potter themed cache. The Fort ZumWalt Park
(just off of Veterans' Memorial Parkway) and the cache are great for the
kids! So bring the kids and after you find the cache, go explore the
"castle". As of 3/7/02 this cache is also a letterboxing cache! Enjoy! Thanks
go out to MjLikePink!


One day, Jared was visiting with Harry before a Quidditch match. "Oh no!"
said Harry, in the middle of the conversation, "I forgot to retrieve my lucky
magic ring!" He looked at Jared, "Could you go and find it for me if I give
you the clues?" Jared replied, "Certainly, Harry!" Harry sighed in relief,
"Thank you, Jared, this very scroll you are reading provides you with enough
clues to find my secret wizard cache. Just take your wand and say the magic
words -Jeepy-ess- and your wand will lead you to my hidden wizard cache. Once
you are there, you may need a spell or the help of a friendly giant to move
the stone that marks the hiding spot."
So, Jared set out on his adventure. His wand led him to the enchanted forest
near the Lake of Whetsel. Inside the forest, he followed his wand. Along the
way, he saw the towers of a nearby castle, "Hmmm" Jared thought, "I'll have
to go explore the castle, it's just over the creek and across the roadway at
the top of that hill (NE). But first, I'll have to find Harry's secret wizard
cache." Lo and behold it was not long before he found the spot. Fortunately,
Jared asked a giant friend of his to accompany him, and the stone was easily
moved, revealing the hidden cache. "Hooray!" shouted Jared as he recovered
Harry's magic ring. Before he left, Jared decided that he would pass Harry's
clues to others and leave many prizes for others to find. So, now the clues
are yours; will you find Harry's Secret Wizard Cache?
