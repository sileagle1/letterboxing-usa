I have several "sticker boxes" with handles that came
from teh clearance bin of a craft store.... they hold
a 5 by 9 strathmore hardbacked journal/sketchbook,
personal stamp, and whatever else fairly well. Looks
like a skinny lunchbox. And the sappy kitten wasnt too
hard to peel off. THOSE i throw into the "joe camel"
backpack and they just stay there- im not taking th
bag anywhere else in public because i quit smoking,
but its a nice leather bag. The compas goes in a
little pocket, the baggies on the bottom, and the days
instructions stuffed in the top. The hardest part is
opening and lining up everyones books for an "assembly
line" stamp, but isnt too bad when i remember to mark
teh empty spots with postits hanging off the page
edge. I cover the new stamp with a postit so it doesnt
smear.
