From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2003-03-24 16:53:01 UTC-08:00
Hey All!

John and I headed West this past weekend and collected six of the
letterboxes hiding on the Banks-Vernonia Rails to Trails park. We
forgot to carry-on to Horseshoe after Fallen Feather and The Minstrel's
letterbox in the Travelling Circus' New Year 2003 series eluded us. We
had a great time and added 6 to our
"F" count.

This trail has always been considered the backYARDS of Oregon, more than
the backWOODS of Oregon as far as I was concerned, but with letterboxes
on it -- WHAT FUN!!! We are hoping to go back again and ride our bikes
to find the two we missed out on sometime in the future.

Thanks so much to Brett, Penny and Grace and the Travelling Circus for
planting such wonderful letterboxes!

Best regards,

Amanda Briles
The Paisley Orca
