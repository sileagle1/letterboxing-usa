HITCHHIKER ALERT:
Virginia is for Lovers has been set free on 8-4-02 by the Ultimate
Syndicate somewhere in northern Virginia...

The World's Smallest Hitchhiker was found in Stafford, Va., and was
dropped off later that day... but no where near where we found it.

Blue Heron and the Kenmore Confederate Cemetary boxes, both in great
locations. And as the sun baked us in our car, we hunted down and
found the Scrabble Mystery Boxes (well, most of them) and G-man's Be
Prepared box. It was a fun-filled, well spent day with the whole
family.
