Sapsucker Woods, Ithaca NY

From: The McQuades (mcquades@twcny.rr.com) | Date: 2002-06-04 16:36:10 UTC-04:00
Our family found our first letterbox last week. A beautiful walk, plus
quite the thrill for the kids when they finally spotted the box.

Everything was in good condition but the logbook is completely filled. We
may have also accidentally put our new purple inkpad in the box when we
repacked it - enjoy it if you find it! :-)

Be sure to wear insect repellant if you go. Ithaca's not usually buggy but
the bogs in Sapsucker Woods are apparently mighty attractive to a large
number of ravenous mosquitoes. We're still scratching.
