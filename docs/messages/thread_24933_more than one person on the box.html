There can only be one entry in the "Placed By" field at the top of the page. That will be automatically filled in withthe trail name of the person/group who is logged into letterboxing.org.
But in the text of your clues, you canadd as much information as youlike about everyone who was involved in the box creation, logbook making, stamp carving, location selection, planting,writing of the clues, or anything else of interest.
