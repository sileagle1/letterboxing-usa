Re: [LbNA] Letterboxing near Lake Arrowhead, PA - Any ideas?

From: Anna Lisa Yoder (annalisa@fast.net) | Date: 2004-05-25 10:11:12 UTC-04:00
Hikers & Hounds, I haven't actually checked a map to see where Lake Arrowhead is, but I live about 1 hour & 15 min. South of the Poconos (at least, that is driving time to Canadensis or Mt. Pocono). So, I can think of a few boxes that should be within your scope. The new Rt. 33 has shortened driving time. Any boxes in Allentown, Bethlehem, Easton, Phillipsburg, etc. should be about 45 min. from Mt. Pocono. Nazareth would be even closer, but don't know if there are boxes there. The South Mountain ones would be a bit further. You could check the distance to Hawk Mt., for the Pulpit Rock and Pinnacle boxes. I believe Silent Doug's website has a few intriguing sounding ones in the Poconos too, one near Canadensis & I'm not sure where the rest are. At least one may be a mystery. Also look for Minsi Trail boxes. Sorry I have no personal experiences w/ these except for a couple Allentown ones. West Park is under construction, so "Just Flyin' Through" was not available a week ago. Good luck! -lunaryakketyact
