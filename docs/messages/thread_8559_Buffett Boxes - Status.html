From: Mary from Virginia (daughteroftheolddominion@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-21 12:23:21 UTC
After sitting quietly in the woods for a month, patiently waiting for
visitors, the Jimmy Buffett boxes saw a lot of activity this weekend.
Celtic Pride stamped in first on Saturday and picked up the
hitchhiker. Then I showed up on Sunday afternoon. And minutes behind
me were the Knights of Columbo. The boxes are in fine shape and are
still water tight. The palm tree surprise is a nice touch!

I'd never been to Mason Neck before, in spite of living in Fairfax
County for a LONG TIME. Thanks Quilter and Parrothead.
