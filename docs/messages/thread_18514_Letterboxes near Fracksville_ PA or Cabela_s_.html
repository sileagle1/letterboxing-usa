Pottsville is about 20 miles north of Cabelas. There are several boxes close
by though, including Pinnacle and Pulpit Rock. I don't know the status of
these boxes though, I have not searched for them yet.

About ten miles south is Blue Marsh Lake, where you will find the 4
letterboxes in Stamp Collection. I found the first two of these, in good condition,
last week. A heads up, they are long, and it was about five miles of hilly
hiking to get those two.

Good luck.
