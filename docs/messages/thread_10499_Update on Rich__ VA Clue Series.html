Thanks to all of you who are participating in the Richmond, Virginia
Clue series! A list of who signed up for what can be found below. All
items have been taken except the secret passage. C'mon, fellow boxers,
who can come up with a creative take on this rather abstract concept?
We'd love to see your stamp!

Miss Scarlett - mlgainey

Col. Mustard - The Bobcat Crew

Lead pipe - The Jolly G-Man
Wrench - The Jolly G-Man
Rope - Daughter of the Old Dominion
Secret passage - ?????

If you're able to attend Hiding Day on Saturday, March 15, we'd love
to see you! If you won't be able to make it on March 15 and haven't
mailed your stamp and logbook in their container already, please
contact Ladybug at btrplc2b@aol.com for her mailing address. If
possible, please get all out-of-towners' stamps to her by March 11,
but if that's not possible, definitely by March 14.

Contact mlgainey at mlgainey@yahoo.com or Ladybug with any questions.
Happy trails!
