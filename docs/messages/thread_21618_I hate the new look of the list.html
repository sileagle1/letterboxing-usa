I know that new things on my computer are sometimes an improvement so

usually.) And sometimes once I get used to it I am happier. But I

way only 5 of my groups are listed on my list. I don't like how I feel

let us have the old version available?

Grumble, grumble.....

Re: I hate the new look of the list

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-03-27 22:07:38 UTC

Yeh, what is the problem with that first/last/previous/next stuff
anyway? It is completely backward -- must have a left-handed dyslexic
doing their programming (now don't flame me on that -- it is meant
sarcastically, I have nothing against left-hand people or dyslexics).

I also don't like the way when I am typing a message, the last few
letters of a line disappear off the edge of the editing window until I
do a hard return.

I sent several feedback messages both about the list appearance and
the administrative features. I am certainly not against change, but
this does seem like a change for the worse.

Somebody mentioned e-mail and I do have some of the regional LB groups
on e-mail, but there are just too many messages coming from this list -
- even to direct them into their own folder in my inbox, it would just
be a lot of clutter. Also it is much harder to follow a thread up the
line using e-mail.

On the positive side.. I do like the "clean" look it has by using a
leaner, non-serif type face for the sidebar and adding more white
space. Just don't like the additional advertising on the home pages
and the extra fluff you get in the message list and on the My Groups
page. Now if they really wanted to add something useful to the My
Groups page, they could take away the picture and add "last message"
time.

SpringChick
