Midcoast Maine Brunswick/Harpswell-Boothbay Harbor are wonderful places to explore. Popham Beach, at the mouth of the Kennebec River is great for body surfing, sunbathing, hiking; Morse Mountain is a quiet hike with lots of birding; Fort Baldwin & Fort Popham are great places to explore all within 3 miles of each other. The Maine Festival featuring Maine only artists takes place at Thomas Point Beach August 2-4

Boothbay is a wonderful town with whale watching tours and lots of neat little shops and outdoor cafes. Although Bailey Island in Harpswell is quite touristy, the Giant Staircase is a unique natural Maine phenomen. Freeport/Pownal has Bradbury Mtn and Wolf Neck State Park, Falmouth has Mackworth Island, and Audubon Sanctuary. Cundy's Harbor in Harpswell has a nice working fishing community with Estees III to eat right on the dock.Letterboxers and geocachers will be kept quite busy between Falmouth-Boothbay. Enjoy your trip.
