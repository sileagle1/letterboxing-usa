From: Randy Hall (randy@mapsurfer.com) | Date: 2000-10-30 19:36:04 UTC-04:00

> 1. How can we remove or note on the letterboxing.org web site that
> they aren't there....

Only the author can confirm the non-existance of a box. Only
the author may initiate a change to clues.

If the author goes missing, does not check on their boxes, etc, all
that can be done is to rely on the list archives or other word of mouth;
webmasters other than the author should not make change the clues (IMO).
Perhaps other people have other opinions.

This is treasure hunting -- hunter beware.

FWIW, I found those boxes in March of '99, and did not think
they were particularily well hidden, so there is more word of mouth :-)
