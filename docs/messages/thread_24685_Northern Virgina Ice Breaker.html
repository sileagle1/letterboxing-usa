I figured I would take a moment from carving (yes, I am actually almost
done the event stamp) to send out an announcement to let everyone know
that all is good for the Ice Breaker Gathering in Herndon, VA.

Everything is a go and it looks good for Sunday as far as the restaurant
goes. They know we are coming and should be ready for us. Remember,
this is The Breeze in Herndon, not the one in Ashburn.

When you come into the restaurant, just come up stairs and go to your
left. You can sign in on the event board and get the official event
stamp. There will be lots of new faces, don't be afraid to say hi to
folks, get exchanges, have fun with the other event stamps, personal
travelers and the like. This is very casual. Come when you want and
stay as long as you like.

The forecast right now is showing rain but no cold weather, so a good
day for an indoor gathering.

So I hope to see a lot of folks out that day!

I case you didn't hear the first time, here are the vitals:

December 23, 2007 from 5PM to 9PM

The Breeze

Herndon, VA
