Ducky Letterbox at Assawoman
Assawoman WMA
Near Ocean City, Maryland
Placed November 25, 2000 by Squirrel
Easy

When you see the first big sign for Assawoman WMA you should stop and pick up
a brochure. Return to your car and drive a very short distance to some red
barns on a curve in the road. Pull in there and park and get out and begin
your search for the letterbox. You should see a small white building close to
the road. Look between the that building and a fence behind some concrete
blocks. There you will find the letterbox. Isn't is ducky! Return to your
car and continue on down to road to the entrance to the WMA. Hope you
remembered to bring you binoculars, there is good birding here.
