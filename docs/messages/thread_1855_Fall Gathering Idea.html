Fall Gathering Idea

From: Mark S. Fitton (mfitton@snet.net) | Date: 2001-02-04 08:59:33 UTC-05:00
I would like to give my idea or suggestion for the next gathering. I think
it would be nice if we held it at one of the State Parks. The location
could be announced well ahead of time. Then various letterboxers could
place letterboxes at the Park in advance of the Gathering. If enough people
were willing to plant them, they could each plant one. It would be great to
get 6 to 10 new letterboxes planted in advance. That way everybody,
including the planters would have boxes to seek out.

Where and when would we have it? I have a suggestion for that too, although
I certainly understand the need to diversify our locations. I would
recommend Chatfield Hollow State Park. It's in Killingworth Connecticut. It
is a wonderful park and is especially nice for picnics. It can easily
accommodate the number of letterboxers who would descend upon the park. It
has trails and, I believe, it doesn't currently have any letterboxes.

Thoughts?


Mark
