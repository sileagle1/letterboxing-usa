Name of the Box: Lime Kiln
Updated on: September 29, 2002
Located in: Bolton, Massachusetts.(Middlesex County)
Difficulty: Easy ? requires compass

Find Bolton Quarry, east of junction of 495/117, on the left before a red
wooden fence, before Bolton Springs Apple Farm.

Go to the quarry hot spot, very near the trail marker number of someone old
enough to go to a real "hot spot". (Note ? if children are with you, don't
let them run ahead beyond this marker ? there is a dangerous drop off.)

Walk further and find a small (pathetic) cedar tree, and go about 112
degrees to the edge. Your quarry will be there!

Be sure to replace the trail guide before you leave.
