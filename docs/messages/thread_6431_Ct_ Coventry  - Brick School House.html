The Brick School House

In Coventry turn from Rte 44 to North River Rd. this turn is at the
Glass Muesam site. 44 Bisects N. River so take the north side with
the colonial house not the brick house. When the road comes to a Y
bear right to the stop sign. Then turn right again and follow this
until you come to the small Brick School House. Park out of the
neighbors way. This is a historic muesam owned by Coventry
Historical Society and a very photogenic place. A Storm came
whistlin through and tore down the large tree that formerly hid our
treasure. It crushed the old two room outhouse that was part of the
first set of clues. So here we go another try. Stand behind the
school see where the tree came down and the few stones remaining of
the outhouse founation. Now let your eye travel to the left . There
is a large Maple which has stood here long enough to remember the
laughter of children at recess. In front of the tree in the fern
garden your quest is hidden under rock carefully placed. Please
remove and stamp at your car then seal tightly and rehide exactly.
Do not disturb the garden. As always Have Fun and be safe.
