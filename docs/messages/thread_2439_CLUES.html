STONE MOUNTAIN
LETTERBOX CLUES

WATERFALL LETTERBOX:

At Blue Ridge Parkway mile marker # 233 look 250 degrees west
to waterfall. From fir tree at top of waterfall go 230 degrees for
48 paces to fir above the pool. Then go 130 degrees for 10 paces to Beech
tree.

Placed 5/28/01


HIGHEST POINT LETTERBOX:

Go to the highest point in Wilkes County. From first step go
220 degrees and 20 paces to slashed pine. From pine go 130 degrees
for 18 paces.

Placed 5/28/01

TRAMWAY LETTERBOX:

From "V" of the tramway support go 72 degrees for 60 paces to
water. Go 105 degrees for 12 paces to game trail. Then go 335 degrees
for 58 paces to hollow tree.

Placed 5/20/01

These letter boxes are all in Wilkes county, North Carolina, in or around
the Stone Mountain State Park. Placed by R. Hood
