Hi Laura

Sorry you are having a bit of trouble. From what you are posting it
sounds more like a link problem (from LbNA to AQ) with those clues.

If you are going directly to AQ there should be no access problem as
even folks who do not register at AQ can access clues. The only clues
you can not access are those with restrictions (F-count, Premium
Member, etc.), but those should not be linked to LbNA as that creates
problems.(Maybe the problem?)
If you just go to AQ then use the Pulldown function under Home, and
use the "Contact Us" function you can send a message to Ryan.You do
not have to register or log in to accomplish this.If you explain your
problem including the letterbox name then Ryan can look into it.

Don
