Doubled our 'F' Count

From: barbw427 (barbw427@insightbb.com) | Date: 2003-10-26 20:48:43 UTC
During a very pleasant afternoon of letterboxing in John Bryan Park
in Ohio, we managed to double our 'F' count (from 11 to 23). Now I
was hoping to triple or quadruple it, but I had to settle for merely
doubling it. My family elected to investigate a corn maze on our
second day that was closer to our hotel rather than return to the
park. I must admit that we did have a wonderful time exploring the
maze. As my kids say, this just gives a reason to return to the
park in the future.

Just before our trip, the kids decided that they wanted their own
stamps. So, each of them drew up their designs. My son started to
carve his on his own, but decided to let me finish it up for him.
Our stamping in takes a bit longer these days, but I will go along
with anything that keeps up their enthusiasm for this hobby!

Barb of The Weaver Family (Weaver Mom)
