I am thrilled with my afternoon - my mother, her friend and I all went letterboxing in Savage, MD today (beautiful weather finally - in the 50s and sunny!)

The subject line is not a typo - we went armed with the clues for two boxes (and found them.) In the second box was a clue for a "bonus" box in the area. The bonus box also contained a hitchhiker!

I should box with my mom and her friend more often - they are good luck! This is the first HH we've ever found.

The Spider Hitchhiker is in my posession now, and will find a new place to spin it's web soon.

Hope everyone else had great weather and good luck too,
