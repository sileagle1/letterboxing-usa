From: Amanda Arkebauer (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-05 21:21:44 UTC

Just a note to let everyone know that 3 out of 4 apples are alive and
well in New York. It took hours of computer research, long distance
phone calls and brainstorming sessions into the wee hours of the
morning, but Princess Lea and Amanda from Seattle were able to figure
out the letterbox clues for this series (with help from Funhog and
Legerdemaine himself)!!

We went a hunting for the boxes and sadly, Liberty Apple was the one
missing. We were also victorious in translating the ghoti letterbox
clue (Funhog take a bow). It is alive and well.

Gorgeous Stamps from Legerdemaine, as usual. Well worth the workout
our grey cells endured!

Thanks so much for the challenge.

--Amanda from Seattle
