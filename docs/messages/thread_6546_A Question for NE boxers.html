From: Erich Sawyer (riposte9@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-07-30 13:36:25 UTC-05:00



Irishtinker,

     As far as I know all boxes listed on the site are still active with two exceptions. 1- Jewell Park is no longer out, I pulled that one earlier this spring. 2- Cornhusker box in Lincoln has been hunted unsuccessfully and is presumed missing. It is great to hear about more boxers moving to our state. Letterboxing is really starting to take off here in eastern Nebraska.

For a very up to date list visit Scott Redd's Wiki Site. I don't have the address handy at the moment, but if you go to my site shown below and go to the links page there is a link to his site there. It doesn't get more up to date than that, trust me.

Happy Boxing,

As Erich posted earlier, letterboxing is starting to take off in
Nebraska. It's great to know we may have added a couple more.
