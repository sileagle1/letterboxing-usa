Hey All-

I have wanted to place a letterbox in a historical cemetery for quite
some time. However, I did not feel right leaving a letterbox in a
rock wall that pre-dates the Revolutionary War. I threw the idea
around in my head for a while until I discovered 'Ecoscavenging'.
Created by a fellow Letterboxer/Geocacher, the concept is to give
clues to a place ... rather than a box. At this location you will
find the answer to a question of some sort. By inserting the answer
into a web address, you will find an additional page with an image to
print out for your journal. This idea is similar to some virtual
letterboxes. For more information visit their web address:
http://www.ecoscavenger.com/ Up until now there were no Ecoscavenges
located in Connecticut. I have decided to create the first. You can
find the clues at the following web address.

http://Letterboxer2002.tripod.com/Brookside.htm

Please consider this a valid find for all of you who keep count. The
work needed to find such a box is the same. I hope you all enjoy
this type of letterbox as much as the ussual.

Safe Letterboxing- Letterboxer2002 (Alan)
