Re: [LbNA] Lord of the Rings

From: Rayvenhaus (rayvenhaus@myndworx.com) | Date: 2004-03-27 08:22:02 UTC-08:00
If I remember correctly, Sir Balthazar of San Luis Obispo (Sp?) did
something along those lines, though I'm not sure if there are still in
place. I believe they are and you'd have to contact him directly concerning
them, however, just because a 'series' or 'subject' as been done in one part
of the country, doesn't mean you can't do it somewhere else. A Lord of the
Rings series in each state shouldn't be a problem, since the chances of me,
for example, getting the stamps in, say, Rhode Island of the LoR stamps,
when I live in Washington state, are slim to none since I don't travel
anymore.

But that's only my opinion, I am sure someone else will feel different. I
think that every person is an individual (obviously! grin!) and their
talents are completely different when it comes to carving stamps and how
they turn out. Lord knows my feeble attempts at stamp carving are mediocre
at best and that some one could interpret the Curse of the Black Pearl way
better than me.

Just my two cents worth.
