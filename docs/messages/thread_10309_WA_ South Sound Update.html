WA: South Sound Update

From: BB (bburk@harbornet.com) | Date: 2003-01-16 19:22:25 UTC-08:00
Visited Burfoot park to day.
Had the clues for both Fudrick and dvn2r ckr boxes.

The first hitch was that the gate was closed and locked.
We parked, blocking the exit gate and walked in.

Then we see the guys all wearing the same kind of clothes and realize it's a
penal work party in cleaning up the place.
They were picking up debris, actually sweeping some places, and hauling it off
into the woods and discarding it.
We saw only one guard, and he didn't seem too concerned about us being there,
but kept an eye.
We decided to venture (sneak) in and grab a couple boxes anyway.

Got to Fudrick's Burfoot boxes, but not to dvn2r ckr's.
Decided not to push our luck with the man.
Both Burfoot boxes are alive and well and yielded 4 HH each - it was crowded,
but we managed to find a place for all the HHs in our pack.

Think I'll start a collection bag for Amanda to take back East her next trip.
;-)

We have to catch the Adventure Seeker's clues next trip.
