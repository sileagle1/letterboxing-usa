TeamGreenDragon had an awesome weekend of boxing ! We got a late
start Saturday ( Ryan had a birthday party to attend ) but made it to
Woodshole about 3 O'clock to find " Secrets of the Knob " ( I believe
this to be the oldest box we have found : ) We found it sitting in
the open : ( but have returned it to its original hiding place : )
also " Nobsque Light " ( This was tricky Chris ) we then headed for
Pocasset to grab the " Four Ponds Conservation Area " ( Very nice
stamp ).
After dinner at Shaws ( the place with the landlocked Tug ) we
decided to throw caution to the wind , unpacked our flashlights and
headed out into the dark and very foggy night to find " Gateway to
the Cape Cod Canal " ( Ryan somehow found the right tree , it was
incredibly dark out here ) and the " Belmont Rock " Letterboxes .
Sunday we headed for RI and found " Blue Jay at Blue Pond " then
headed for the Long and Ell Pond Natural Area ( this place is great )
to find " Dragon in the Rock " ( a bat was keeping this box company ,
I don't know who was startled more him or me ) and Laurette's " To be
precise... ". Next up was the " Dog Days of Summer " series . We
found all three of these ( great stamps ! Moonstone Baby ) and I
remembered something about " Tin Tin " and airplanes and managed to
find " Adventures of Tin Tin " with out any clues : )

As I said it was awesome to be out in such great weather .

Thanks All
