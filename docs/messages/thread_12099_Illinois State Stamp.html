Illinois State Stamp

From: (needeeps@webtv.net) | Date: 2003-04-18 22:13:56 UTC-04:00
Hebron CT. Rated Easy
Planted By The NeeDeeps 4-18-03
Directions: From the intersection of RT66 and RT.85,Take 85 north for
0.7 of a mile and turn down the road with the boat launch sign,this is
the entrance to Holbrook Pond.Drive down a little ways and park in the
parking area on the right before the pond.(This is the same starting
point as Red Men at Holbrook Pond.) Enter the trail and walk over the
wooden bridge you will see the dam on your left.Continue down the trail
( some muddy areas but you can walk around them) soon you will see a
trail going off to the right but you keep going straight.You will pass
by a stone wall off to your right and you will come to a small stream
cross over and keep on the main trail.Then you will come to another
stream that feeds into the pond (DO NOT CROSS) follow up stream a little
bit and you will see a big rotted tree that has fallen,in the end of
this tree opposite side of the stump under some dead pieces of wood is
the letterbox.return the way you came.The NeeDeeps.
