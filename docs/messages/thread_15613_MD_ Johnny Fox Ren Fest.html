This is probably a temporary microbox for the duration of the
remaining Maryland Renaissance season. Check local listings or Google
their website for opening hours and fees.

Johnny Fox, sword swallower extraordinaire, has been entertaining at
the MD Ren Fest for over 20 years. The first time I saw his show I
was so shocked, I laughed so hard I cried and I hurt. "Water from
India," protects him from harm. I'll let you figure out whether he
really swallows the swords or not.

After the show buy some snacks from Johnny Fox's Almonds booth. Then
turn left past the School of Juggling and find an interesting triple
tree with a trunk smaller than the other two. Behind it is a zig zag
path going down the hill. Halfway down find a long curved bench.
Count 4 support posts from right to left and sit down next to the 4th
one. Reach under the bench and between the supports for what you seek.
