To all

First I would like to apologize for the mistake I made
on my new The Maplines Air Line Railroad Series on Box
#7. I am usually very careful about these sort of
things. But I am human and I can make one mistake a
year. Thats it for this year. Here is the way it is
supposed to read.



OK you can use a new page for the next 2 stamps.

Ok walk back towards your car. Go over the old bridge
again. Take 72 steps RIGHT FOOT ONLY. Stop and you
look for a path on the right. BEFORE THE POWER LINES.
Now with the path behind you and the rail bed on your
right and left. Now look for a large Maple tree in the
woods. It will have 3 rocks in front of it. Look
inside the 3 rocks and under a small rock you will
find Box # 7.


Sorry about that I hope you had fun anyway.

Paul
