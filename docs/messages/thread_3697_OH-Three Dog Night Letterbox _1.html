Three Dog Night Letterbox #1

November 13, 2001

Ohio is the Buckeye State
All of us Buckeyes think our state is great!

At the top of Ohio is Williams County
This is where you will find the bounty.

George Bible Park is where it is at
Never heard of it? Imagine that!

Look between Montpelier and Bryan
If you cant find it, just keep tryin.

At the corner of Williams County Roads 13 and J
When you get here, you are on your way!

The park entrance is out of sight
If you go too fast, miss it you might!

Park your car and travel on foot
Youre getting closer to the loot!

Walk around the chrome guardrail
Now you are almost on the trail!

Walk to the sign that says Bible Park
You might miss it after dark.

Turn to your left and walk 28 paces directly east
Youre getting very near the feast.

Turn right at the flower bed
The prize you are seeking is straight ahead.

30 paces to the south is a gnarled old tree
With gnarled roots; you are now home free!

Carefully reach up inside
That is where the treasure will hide!

Randy and Becky Adams
3dognight@cityofbryan.net
