Just wanted to say we're back from our annual CDT volunteer work trip in
New Mexico (just in time for the Mansfield Monster Mash in CT!). We built
several new miles of hiking trail, with only a little time left over for letterboxing.
But, we did manage to find a couple of hitchers ("The Hunter" and "The Jolly
Roger") and plant a couple of boxes of Laurette's ("Simple Pleasures" and
"Sandia Picnic") in some pretty special places while we were out there, too!
Clues will be coming out soon. Meanwhile, hope to see a lot of folks
tomorrow at the MMM!
