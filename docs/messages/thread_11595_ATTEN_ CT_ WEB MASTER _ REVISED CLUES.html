The Vermont State Stamp Letterbox
Mansfield Center Conn. Rated Moderate 3/4 of
a mile Planted By The NeeDeeps 3-23-03
Directions: On RT.195 in Mansfield pull into the old Town Hall &
Historcal Society across from Spring Hill RD. Go to the back of the
building and park,then enter the blue blaze trail.(Don't forget the
clues for other great boxes like The Plane spotter,the bed rug,Inherit
the earth and the rock climber.)
Walk all the way down and in awhile you will come to a wooden
bridge,cross over and you will come to a T. You will turn right here
keep going and you will come to a place with a beautiful view.Go to the
clearing that has the best view,go over and take a moment to enjoy the
scenery.Now from where you are standing facing the view, off to your far
left aways is a stone wall.Go over to the stone wall and walk down the
back side of it,you will come to a stump with a medium size tree next to
it.On the other side of the tree opposite of the stump you will find
your Vermont LB. in the wall behind a stone door.The NeeDeeps
