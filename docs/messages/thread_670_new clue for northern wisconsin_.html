From: Dierdra Baumgart (fencing_lady@yahoo.com) | Date: 1999-09-27 05:38:42 UTC-07:00
Dear Letterboxers,
A surprising phone call lead me to Northern Wisconsin
this Saturday. A friends that go to school at
Northland Baptist Bible College needed a ride down. I
had finished my box that I wanted to plant up there.
They took me to a wonderful park/camp ground called:
Twelve Foot Falls. This may be a new series for me in
the spring. It is so gorgeous! The scenery is breath
taking. I have pictures still in the camera. I'll
send the image and the pic along with my Green Isle
Series when it is placed. Maybe this weekend.

Directions:
Take 41/141 N. Follow 141 N to Crivitz. You are an
hour away when you reach Coleman. You have fifteen or
so minutes when you reach Wausaukee. After Wausaukee,
there will be a sign for Northland on your right. It
is the next left after that sign. After taking that
left, follow this new road to a stop sign. There you
will see a wooden arrow sign for Twelve Foot Falls.
Follow that until you reach it. Once you enter, park
at the parking lot after the Camp ground entrace (left
side, you'll be parking on the right side down the
hill a bit.)
Clue: Take the path to the right. It is also for
vehicles. Take the left path to the Rocks by the
falls. You will know them for they are situated like
a conversational nook. Follow the lower path towards
the left. Once a tree blocks your progress you've
found the local of the box. Look in and around the
tree. It is there. "Wow," is all one can say.
Die xo
