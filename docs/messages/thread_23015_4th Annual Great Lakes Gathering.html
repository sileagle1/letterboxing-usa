4th Annual Great Lakes Gathering

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-01-21 02:34:10 UTC
After all the poll results were in, we announced the theme for the 4th
Annual Great Lakes Gathering on the regional board on Monday. The
theme this year will be "Wandering in Wonderland", based on Alice's
Adventures in Wonderland and Through the Looking Glass. We've had a
great response on our regional board but I figured I'd also post the
info on the national board in case there are some local boxers who
only read "the big board". (If you'd like to join the regional board,
here's a link: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/ )

I'd also like to extend an invitation to anyone who will be traveling
to or through the midwest in early June to join us for the event. It
will be held on Saturday June 3 at Lapham Peak State Park, 25 miles
west of Milwaukee, WI.

For more details, please visit the event website:

http://2006greatlakesgathering.homestead.com/
