From: parrotheaddg (parrotheaddg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-05 13:15:50 UTC
I spent a fabulous Sunday boxing in CT! Irishtinker, your carvings
are amazing. Thanks also to the other "placers" for some wonderful
additions to my collection!

The following boxes are all in great shape:
CT State Stamp
BenE
Glass Factory
Mousing in Laidlaw Park
Red Bird and Skungamaug River
Presidential Animals (all 3)
Mary Lemist Titcomb
Uncle Sam's Nephew
The Great Charter Oak's Great-Grandson
Pumped Up
A Trip Through Town
The May Basket

Also, all the stamps for Women of the Revolutionary War were in great
shape (and very well done!), however, poor Deborah Samson's logbook
is a sponge and ought to be replaced before all the ink inside runs
together.

It could be me, but I couldn't find:
Hazel of Watership Downs (but the other 2 were fine)
Lake Wangumbaug
Stars and Stripes Forever

If anybody knows about these 3 boxes and want to toss me a clue, I'd
be good with that! Of course, it'll be a while before I'm back to
Coventry.

Thanks everybody!
