"nope.......its national park, but id do weekend only ones, then pic
them up"

One of the things that my sons and I were struck with this last summer
in Yellowstone is that the Park Rangers really do not like it when we
leave anything in the park. Letterboxing in National Parks they say
leads to the destruction of the natural environment and flies in the
face of 'Leave No Trace Principals' they support.

What they did enjoy doing with us were 'virtual letterboxes' - where
you find the clues and they lead you to a website to get your stamp
and leave a message.

Just a thought.
