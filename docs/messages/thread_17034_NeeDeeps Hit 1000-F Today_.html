NeeDeeps Hit 1000-F Today!

From: Lenny/Kathy (needeeps@webtv.net) | Date: 2003-11-28 21:34:27 UTC
Today Kathy and I went out and did a few boxes to hit a 1000-F even though the weather was not the greatest we had a good time anyway.We did the Eagle mystery box then the Christmas mystery box,I thought we were all done for the day there,1 hour and 75 dollars later I finally got Kathy out of there.Then we did boxes 2 & 3 of the Cranked Lebanon series,hopped down the road and did the Firebird at Gay City park, then seeing how we were in search of 1000,we hit 1000 on in search of Chuck Straub.Both stamps were very nicely done,we even picked up a HH today, Betsey Beagle while stamping the new stamp at the Hidden Cemetery Series.Not a bad day,Happy Trails.The NeeDeeps
