A New Years Milestone!

From: Bigguy (bigguy9210@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-02 00:51:05 UTC
CardinalMom, the kids, and I got out today and managed to find 6 and
plant 1 to bring our PFX count to an even 200. We were pleased to be
first finders of Light Wait (very nice certificate!), Beacon Hill,
and Fowl Play (another nice certificate but sadly soaked because the
lid had come loose on the box) as well as finding Impatiens,
Impatience, Untie the Ribbons, and the Gull-ible Duo hitchhiker in
the Fowl Play box. CardinalMom also planted the "Bee"gin Again box
in honor of the holiday.

We were also pleased to verify that Patterson's Pride is very much
alive and where it's supposed to be. We'd had a couple of folks
suspect it was missing.

Hope you all have a wonderful 2004!

Bigguy
