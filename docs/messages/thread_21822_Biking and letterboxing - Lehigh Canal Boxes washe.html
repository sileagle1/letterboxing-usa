In the past I may have recommeded the three from Space Traveler
along the Lehigh Canal Towpath in the Bethlehem, PA Area. However,
the floods from the past couple major storms (Ivan last September
and the floods we had on April 2-4 this year) absolutely washed them
away. Both hiding places were there, but definately below the
waterline and eroded. Alas, perhaps somebody downriver from there
may be more lucky than me and my mountain bike!
