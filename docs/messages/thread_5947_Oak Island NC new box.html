Welcome to Oak Island North Carolina.

THE MARSH
CLUES:
Find the Oak Island Parks and Rec. Center.

Park your car next to the Building and Head behind the swings to
the "Overlook Trail"

Follow the trail and Keep your eyes out for wild creatures.

Pass "Mr. Black". He loves his "Gum", but doesn't blow bubbles.
Look to your right at the wetlands. Perhaps you'll see a marsh
rabbit, or a Skink.

When you reach the bottom of the trail find the Bridge ahead and to
your right.

Cross it.

Continue to your right and before the next bridge in the clump of
trees to your left you'll find your box held between two twin pines.

This trail is busy and popular. So please make sure you are not seen
taking it out or replacing it.

Enjoy your search and enjoy Oak Island.
