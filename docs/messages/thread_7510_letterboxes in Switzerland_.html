I grew up in Germany and if I had known about letterboxing back
then, you better believe that I would have placed boxes along the
way. I also spent time in Asia a few years ago and I would have
done the same there. There may be a possibility that we will move
to Europe or Asia in the future (next couple of years) and I
definitely will place letterboxes when I go. I just hope, that
before that time--if it ever were to come, that others who also
share such a similar passion for the sport will start placing boxes
in their own areas which might, in turn, encourage others to get
involved and place boxes in their neat and interesting locations.
What a fantastic sport!!!
