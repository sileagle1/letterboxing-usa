Prospect, Maine update on Box - and funny story

From: Two Maine Cats (TwoMaineCats@msn.com) | Date: 2002-07-05 22:01:00 UTC-04:00

The Prospect, Maine British are Coming Letterbox  (First part of the clue) will be undergoing repairs.  The second letterbox in the British are Coming Series is still in action.

 

It seems that someone found my carefully hidden box.  I have learned a lesson!  The box was located in steady stream of traffic at the park.  When we arrived there this evening to check on the box – the box was out in the open- sitting on a step for all to see!  We have a lot of tourist who signed in the log book.  There is also an inscription “I am a single mother…” giving name, address, and phone for all to see – she wants a date. We had just one letterboxer and the others who logged in were hard up on  love.  It seems that I have started the first dating letterbox!  How bizarre! Alas, my stamp was taken by a klepto so I must recarve and will change the clues so only LETTERBOXES will find it.
