"EWIS Letterbox"
East Windsor Intermediate School
(Broad Brook, CT)

Placed by: Rebecca
Date: June 19, 2002 (last day of school)

A 3rd grader's self-initiated project to introduce fellow
classmates and local families to letterboxing.
Park in front parking lot of EWIS (off Main Street in Broad
Brook). Start on the front main sidewalk, below East Windsor
Intermediate School sign, facing the parking lot. Walk to your right
along the building's brick wall. Pass three large trees (on your
left). Soon you will see a short bushy shrub (before the fence).
Walk to the right by brick wall and look under the shrub. There you
will find the treasure among the leaves!
Log in the journal book and securely replace all items for
others to find. Place it carefully back, out of sight with a few
leaves scattered on top.

Happy Letterboxing*.. Enjoy!

*Please not during school hours when classes are in session, Thank
You.
