From: Kim Calcagno (hannahkat@cox.net) | Date: 2006-08-24 23:22:14 UTC-04:00
Welcome to our addiction.

You are very fortunate to live in New England. We have some of the biggest
gatherings going...often 100 or more people. CSCM and I have been unable to
attend most of them this year, but we have no fear, as there are a lot of
them....it just depends on how far you are willing to travel.

The next MA event that I am aware of is the Great Cape Escape on Cape Cod on
Sept 30.

You can check for events on Atlasquest (
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/).... Or you could host one of your
own..but I'd recommend attending one first as the exchange feeding frenzy is
a thing to behold.

Cheers!
Kim (Rustypuff)
