It is an MSN chat room. Go to the main MSN Chat page
(http://chat.msn.com/), then under "more categories" click
on "interests" and when you get a list of active chat rooms look for
the one called "Dartmoor Letterboxing". It is at 3:00 pm Eastern
Time on Sunday afternoon (which is 8:00 in the evening in England) --
the host says she starts it promptly at the start of the hour, but
it seems to take 10 or 15 minutes for it to show up in the list, so
you have to keep looking around for it.
