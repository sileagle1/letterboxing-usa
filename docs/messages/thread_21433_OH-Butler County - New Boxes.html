Winter Boxing With The Kids

Placed: February 24 , 2005
By: Team Mad Dawg
State: 0hio
County : Butler
Town : West Chester
Number of Boxes: 3

Location: Brookside Cemetery, West Chester Road, West Chester,
Butler County, OH

Please bring pen and ink pad

This is a series of 3 boxes just for those car-boxing kids with
cabin fever and a very short attention span. Store-bought stamp
warning!

These have a winter theme and will be in place until the first day
of spring.


Frosty

Enter the cemetery and go up the hill to the white house. Go right
at the fork past the McKinney angel. Turn left at the next road.
Continue to the Denman stone and park. Right ahead will be a tree
with a very large open knothole. Behind the tree at the base Frosty
is waiting.


Chilly

Turn right and continue along the road following the curve around to
the north. This is the older part of the cemetery. You will pass
the Muchmore family. Continue around the curve past the 2 buildings
to your left. Park near the white building to your right. Behind
the building by the step under the plantings will be Chilly.


Flurry

Now turn right at the next road to the back fence and turn left.
Continue until you see the triangular island with very large
evergreens. Park by the Keehner stone on your left. Behind you
next to the fence will be another large tree with an open space at
the base. To the left inside the hole under a large piece of bark
will be Flurry.
