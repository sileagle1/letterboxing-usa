> Are you leaving letterboxing or just going more underground?

I'm not underground -- the rest of the world is overground. I
was here first, and that's the way I look at it ;-)

But, to answer your question, I've retired, but those who are
keenly interested in my ideas on outdoor puzzles hopefully will
not be disappointed going forward. We'll see, I guess.

That said, this thread is unrelated to all that. I just don't want
someone e-mailing me five years from now regarding some box I've
never heard of, and it turns out to be mine :) I've actually had
that happen once, and its pretty spooky, but it least it tells me
my ideas on simulated treasure hunting have been internalized ...
