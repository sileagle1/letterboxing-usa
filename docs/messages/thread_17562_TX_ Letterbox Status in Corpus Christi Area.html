My son, Liberty Eagle, and I were able to find a few boxes around making a trip to Corpus Christi for the Stay Board program on the USS Lexington.

We found the following boxes in good condition:
Funhog's "P-3" in Portland, Tx on 1/10/2003
Silver Eagle's "Corpus Porpoise" and "Texas Turtle" on 1/11/2003
Baby Bear's "Coral Snake" and "Super Frogger" on 1/11/2003. We were the first ones to find "Coral Snake".


Also found the "Biltmore Fleur de Lis" Hitchhiker with the "Corpus Porpoise" letterbox. We have moved it on.
