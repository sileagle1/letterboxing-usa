The JJZSr. Letterbox

Placed: 3-8-03
Placed by: Teach & Preach
Location: Putnam Ct, Windham County
Rated: Easy 5-10mins.

I saw people posting about charity boxes and thought it was a great
idea. So I made this box up in memory of my Dad who died of cancer
almost 2 yrs ago. For each stamp that is in the log book on his
birthday of each year, myself and Teach would donate $1 to the
American Cancer Society. This will not cost anybody anything, please
don't leave money in the box. All it will cost our fellow
letterboxers is the time to come out a stamp the box for a great
cause.
Directions:
Go Rt. 171 west in Putnam. You will turn into the St. Mary's
Cementary on your right(It's across from a Cumberland Farms) The
cementary has two orange angels at the enterance. Go straight when
first entering. At the Y go left, the second Y go left. Make your
way to the building in back. Park anywhere by the building. In the
back of the building is a water spout. From there walk straight
ahead to the 7th row. Down that row you will see the Book of Life.
