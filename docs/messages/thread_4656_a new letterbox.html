North of New Haven, in the town of Branford, off of I-95 snuggled in an old building that has watched the century pass by on North Main Street is a real treasure, The Shoreline Stamp Shoppe. 

Hidden within its walls are hundreds of treasures but only one will be the one that you seek  
For directions to this magical place go to the website and take a peek   www.shorelinestampshoppe.com

Come into the Shoppe and keep your eyes wide
Look up and look down, be sure to check every side

When you find the letter-boxing sign
Turn halfway around and walk a straight line

12 paces will bring you near to your quest
6 paces to the right will do the rest

When you find the stacks look down toward your socks
And nearby you will find the hidden letter box

If you are among the first to find it, there is a treasure for you inside it.
