Hitchhiker/traveler STS 107 Columbia was found in DC at the Year of
the Ram letterbox by the Chinatown gate, 2/21/3. (Those who know
this microbox will find this very puzzling.) Subsequently it stamped
in to The Inventor, DC and It's a Holiday, VA.

Today it visited and stamped into The Year of Enlightenment series in
the Bull Run Library in Manassas, VA. These are indoor letterboxes
hidden in books on the shelves. Very cool.

There was no place to leave the traveler so it will continue with the
Doubtful Guests until a suitable letterbox is found to leave it with.
