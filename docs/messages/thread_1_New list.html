From: Daniel Servatius (elf@pclink.com) | Date: 1998-10-11 02:42:42 UTC-05:00
To all concerned:

This is an experiment with a free talk list that
Mitch Klink of Oregon told me about.
Here is the e-mail address of the newly
created talk list:
mailto:letterbox-usa@egroups.com

More free lists can be created.

It works the same as other talk lists
according to what I've been told.
We'll see how this one goes in the next few days.
According to Mitch, it will be virtually
the same as other exclusive lists, except free.

I had the option of leaving it unmoderated, which
I did, and of leaving it open to anyone which I
also did. The list of members is open to all
members also, and not just me.

See you!
Dan'
