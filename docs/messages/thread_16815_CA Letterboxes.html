Hi all.

We took advantage of the absolutely gorgeous fall weather out here in
Northern CA to hit the trails once again.

Found the Buckhorn box in Black Butte park - first finders (!). We
saw our first in-the-wild roadrunner in this neck of the state! For
those of you close to here, this is a must-get!! Gorgeous, nearly
empty park, fairly easy 1 1/2 mile hike, and wonderful views, with
another of Funhog's excellent stamps!

Syopped off at the Wild Goose box in Willows. surprised that there
haven't been more visitors to this box, given the easy access from I5
and its proximity to about a zillion migratory birds! Again, another
neat stamp!

Cheers, Pete aka
