WI-Castle Rock LB Warning!

From: Tim Kuehl (tkuehl@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2003-03-23 23:53:45 UTC-06:00
A warning to those letterboxers thinking about searching for the Castle Rock
(Camp Douglas) letterbox. First, the box IS there and my friend and I and
his kids were the first to find it! However, due to its proximity to a
military base, the MPs we encountered (although pleasant) made it clear that
there better not be any more letterboxers searching for the box until the
area it is in is opened for the summer on June 2nd, and then only during the
daytime. During this time of crisis, I'd suggest obeying this request.
Otherwise you might find yourself surrounded by some of America's finest
without warning like we were!

Note: Matt the Rat's other "mound" boxes were all in their places on
Saturday and in good condition too!
