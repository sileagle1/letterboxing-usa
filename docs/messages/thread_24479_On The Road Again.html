On The Road Again

From: grumpygrinchy (ggboxer@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-07-28 22:39:39 UTC
We will be hitting the road in a couple of days for an extended driving
and letterboxing trip through the northern states, southern Canada,
down the U.S. East Coast, and back north and west again to California.
We plan to leave a number of letterboxes along our path, as a thank you
to the local letterboxers who provide us all with so much enjoyment.
If you find one of our boxes outside of California, please consider it
an orphan. Treat it kindly, enjoy it, and let us know if it goes
missing.

Thanks, Grumpy Grinch
