Cougar Mountain Letterbox
Planted 12/22/01
By Amanda from Seattle

Start at the Wilderness Creek Trailhead.

To reach the trailhead From I-90
Take Exit 15 and drive south on Highway 900 (17th Avenue NW and then
Renton-Issaquah Road SE) for 3.3 miles. Look for the trailhead sign
and an asphalt driveway that goes uphill to the right. You can park
here, there is a portapotty and trail maps are also available here.

Follow the Wilderness Creek Trail to the Long View Peak viewpoint.
(approx. 2 miles) At the viewpoint there is a hitching post for
Horses. To the left of the hitching post and just behind it is a
multi-trunked tree. Look behind this tree and under a log for the
letterbox.

You can also combine this letterbox with the Halloween Letterboxes.
