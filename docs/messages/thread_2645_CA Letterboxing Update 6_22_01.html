CA Letterboxing Update 6/22/01

From: Wendy Gault (wendygault@earthlink.net) | Date: 2001-06-25 12:50:17 UTC-07:00
Hi All,
 
It was a Letterboxing bonanza!  Teri and I went to Bolsa Chica Friday to find the Shore Bird Letterbox, leave behind our newly made Pelican Letterbox and were stoked to find our first Hitch Hiker - Home is Where the Heart is And Heart is on the Road.
 
We went in the the late afternoon so the birds were quite active and entertaining while we were on the trail.  Thanks to Gwen and Don for introducing us to the Bolsa Chica Reserve (both Teri and I have driven up and down Pacific Coast Highway a zillion times but haven't ever stopped to check out the Wetlands.  The Shore Bird letterbox is in very good shape.
 
Clues for the new Bolsa Chica letterbox, Pelican Letterbox, will be coming soon on a web page that we are developing and will be providing the address to the Webmasters at that time.
 
Life is Good,  Wendy
