Title: Lonesome Pine. Location: Chillicothe, Ross County, Ohio.
Placed 5-27-03 by Salamander. Difficulty: Easy. CLUES: Go to
Grandview Cemetery (entrance at Brookside Drive and Bellview Ave.).
Find a long stone wall which faces north. From the northeast corner
of the wall, go 3 paces west(along the wall).The lonesome pine is
hiding in a small niche in the wall, about 18 inches up from the
ground and behind a couple of small stones.
