> Rush Gatherer
> aka: Tara

Gun shops
Army navy surplus stores

Rules and regulations vary from state to state, so make sure you are
not caught with it by an officer of the law unless you have a Federal
Identification Card that says that you are legal to carry it. You
can obtain the license for just chemical propellant from your local
police department. You get 10 carded and a lifetime background
check, but you don't have to do anything special. Getting a Class A
LTC (which is what I have) involves a lot more training and
certifications.
