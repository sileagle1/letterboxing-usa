I've also planted or in the process of planting two mystery boxes, and have also planted one hitchhiker: Traveling Troubadour Hitchhiker.  BTW.... if anyone finds my hitchhiker, please let me know before sending it on its way, if possible.  I think I'm going to need to mount the stamp because I'm afraid it may get broken. Maybe there can be a way I can get a block and glue to you :-)

Anyway, if you are in CT or RI, go to Eastern Coastal CT!  There's very little snow in Mystic, Groton etc!
