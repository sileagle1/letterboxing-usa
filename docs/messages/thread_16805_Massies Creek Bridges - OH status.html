Massies Creek Bridges - OH status

From: cavecricket312 (tfearday@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-11-11 17:17:37 UTC
Found these boxes yesterday.

The first was completely waterlogged. Have taken it to do some first-
aid. I have an email out to Flyfisher, but haven't heard back. The
logbook is almost completely illegible (although I could read
something from a CT letterboxer and a few others), so it will be
replaced. I will also put a new inkpad in it. I hope to have it
back in its hiding spot sometime this week.

The second is in good shape. There was no rock covering it, so I
lugged one up from the creek.

FYI, the clues for these boxes take you to the first bridge then
proceed across the bridge from there. When I got to the road leading
to the first bridge, I encountered a "Road Closed" sign. You can get
to the parking spot at the bridge just fine, but the bridge itself is
closed. So, if you want to do these, bring a map to find a detour to
the second box!
