"Amy's Sweet Valentine's Day Mystery" box
Placed by Patrick for Amy on Valentine's Day

-1659: The town was first settled on land acquired from the leaders
of the Mohegan Indians.
-1786: The town this place is in was established.
-1873: The ground was broken for this place, and was named after the
man who donated the land for it.
-1964: The ground was broken for this place in "addition."
-2000: Amy and I had a very important date there.
-Stand in the western most corner.
-Find the tree at 260 degrees.
-Behind this tree, behind some rocks is the Valentine's Day prize.

-Please be very DISCRETE, as this is a very public place.
-Please rehide this box carefully.
-Please, due to the amount of people on Sunday mornings, try to avoid
this box at that time.
