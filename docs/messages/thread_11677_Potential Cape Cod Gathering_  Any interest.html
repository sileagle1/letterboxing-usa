Potential Cape Cod Gathering: Any interest

From: mulbie (mulbie@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-02 18:36:43 UTC
In response to requests from others at the Winter MA Gathering, The
Yachtsman and I are offering to sponsor a spring gathering on Cape Cod
and are interested in floating a potential date out there to see what
the response might be. Currently we are looking at Saturday, May 3rd
as a potential date, and the event would probably be at Nickerson
State Park in Brewster, MA. The Yachtsman is currently checking the
availability of a picnic pavilion (in case of rain). The park opens
up to camping on Friday, April 11th, so folks could camp there if they
want to. There are a fair number of boxes on Cape Cod now, and of
course some more could be planted before then. An alternate site is
Shawme Crowell State Forest in Sandwich, but there is no covered
pavilion there. What do you all think?

Mary Manatee
