Down Under Series doing fine

From: Patrick Donnelly (smilnoncloudyday@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-06-26 10:53:46 UTC-04:00
Found the Down Under Series yesterday in Wayne, NJ [my hometown]. Very nice
hike, some beautiful views of the putrid brown New Jerey air from the summit
of High Mountain [which isn't high at all at a walloping 850']. Some of the
worst bugs I've ever seen in my entire life on the "white trail" section.
It's in a valley, we've been receiving lots and lots [too much] of rain and
I was killing about 10 misquitos per minute! Anyway all of the boxes are
intact and doing fine, except I couldn't find the Emu box. Hell short of
that, I couldn't even find the tree that has "Art is Beautiful 4Ever" carved
into it! So I'm gonna have to go back and try to find it again. Oh well.
