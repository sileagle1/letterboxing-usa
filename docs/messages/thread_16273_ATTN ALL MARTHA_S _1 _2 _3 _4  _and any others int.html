Introducing the "DAYS OF HER LIFE HH" Postal LB

Now that Martha has little to do, she is a bit confused about time.

I have carved 7 stamps in Martha's honor. She has been counting the
days and I thought it might be nice to give her something to count
them by! Should anyone wish to join this HH postal box please email
me your name and address to:

phynstarrataoldotcom


I shall send this HH first to Busy Bee with respect to her fantastic
job on the FREE MARTHA Postal LB!!! (Thanks BB!) She will then move
it from there. Each person will be given one week (if they choose) to
make a small carving or do something of their own choosing. The
stamps are small and should not cost more then 2$ to send. Please
feel free to add something special to the HH as time marcheth on for
dear Martha! Stamps of the craftiest kind are most encouraged!!!

Thanks for the interest!!

phynstar
