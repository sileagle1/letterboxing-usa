I have two boxes that would be considered "I like harder hikes, but
straight forward clues", and the rest are defiantly easier, but even
though the votes are almost even, the harder one don't see any
visitors. One has only had one, and it really is a nice place.

Just from my observations, I think that only a very few like to
extreme puzzles with the challenging hike, while most like the grab
and go's and the puzzles. I think that if a larger sample was taken
that you would see the bottom two choices pull away

I personally am not ging to place anymore g&g's unless the location
really warrants it. It is nice to get the increased action, but I
have been having people not even having stamps signing in and then
not re-hiding very well. Twice my one g&g has been soaked.
