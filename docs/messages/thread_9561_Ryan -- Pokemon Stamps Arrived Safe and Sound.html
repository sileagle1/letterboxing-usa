Ryan -- Pokemon Stamps Arrived Safe and Sound

From: be ma (bema57@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-12-05 15:41:01 UTC


Hi Ryan! We wanted to let you know that the Pokemon stamps arrived safe and
sound and in time for the very first snow of the winter. Although they were
not allowed to go out sledding with the little BeMas (one mitten is already
a MIA) they did get to sit at the table for hot cocoa!

Identity update: The Pokemon we thought might be Goldbat is actually
Nidoran male (there is a female one too). It is a pink (don't ask why -- at
least they're not sexist) poison needle Pokemon. It can be nice or mean
depending on who the trainer is (a lot like Pit Bulls....) The cat is
Meowth. The turtle is Squirtle. The sea horse is Horsea and the purple
thing is Tangela.

The imprints of the other stamps were pretty cool! You definitely surpass
us in carving!!! We are sorry that you are on the other coast so we won't
be able to search for your boxes!

Once the snow melts, we'll start doing some research for a good park. We
have actually pulled some site plans off the internet (Fairfax County Park
Authority has a great website with maps). But the actual walk throughs will
have to wait.

Thanks again! Team BeMa is psyched and we will try and do the stamps proud!

P.S. You should try and come over to Northern Virginia/DC. We've got the
Nation's Capital, the Smithsonian (not to be put in the subject line), lots
of great restaurants featuring cuisine from EVERYWHERE and a ton of great
boxes to find! (And if you do find Froggy -- let us know!)
