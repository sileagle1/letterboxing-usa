From: bcostley (bobbyeubanks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-09-29 01:17:13 UTC
On Sept. 28, 2002 Brett, Penny, and Grace placed the Bugle Mystery
box somewhere in Oregon.

Clues and further information can be found here:

http://home.agalis.net/bcostley/Letterbox/bugle.htm

and the clues are also reproduced below:

Find a gemm of a location in Oregon where a mighty band gathers. See
the band almost any day November through April and occasionally the
rest of the year. If you go early mornings in mid-September to mid-
October you may be lucky enough so hear the band of brothers blowing
their bugles. Once you've found this location, head west a few miles
to a wooden abode where finned and feathered friends fall. Once
there, head towards the top. You will find what you seek stashed in
the south and shorter Siamese snag.
