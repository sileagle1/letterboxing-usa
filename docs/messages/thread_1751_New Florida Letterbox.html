The Christmas Letterbox

JB Starkey Wilderness Park
New Port Richey, Florida

Rated: Easy Walking

Time: 1 Hour or less

Start at the sign for the Green Nature Trail.  The trial is a long winding
trail.  After post #17 look for the sign for the Swamp Red Bay Tree.  As you
pass the tree look for the sign with the arrow.  As you stand in front of the
arrow walk about 20 paces into the woods until you see 2 palm trees growing
together.  Look down between the two trees to find what you are looking for.  
Good Luck!
