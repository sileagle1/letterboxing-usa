Box # 2, The Point.



Follow the directions to the Scott's Cove box. When you have finished with
that box face the water and look to your left where you see the next point of
land. Make you way back to the trail and head for that point. When you arrive
at the end of the trail facing the open water turn around 180 degrees and find
a leaning oak tree between two pines. At the base of the tree you find what
you seek under a rock.

After you have enjoyed the scenery and maybe caught sight of a Heron or an
American Bittern, head back down the trail to find box #3.



Box #3. The Stump

Make your way back on the same trail, cross the creek and follow the trail to
the left until you see a skinny tree with a white blaze on the right. Take the
way less travelled up the hill and at the wide trail at the top,turn right.
Continue on past the crooked tree with the grey rings until you come to a metal
gate. At the gate turn left onto the small trail and go 10 paces. On the left

of the trail you will see a tall tree stump with lichen on its bark. At the
foot of the tree is a fallen tree (probably the top half of the stump), Where
the fallen tree breaks you discover the box.

Back on the trail, follow it down the hill where it will intersect with the
trail that leads back to the parking lot.
