From: psycomommy2003 (ktborrelli@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-10-01 20:48:21 UTC
I was a bit bummed when I found out that the HH found in WA State
was the other Poison Ivy. That got me thinking about my 2
lonely "children", Haircut Rabbit and Poison Ivy. Whoever finds them
could they please email me offlist.
OR
I have found that Defygravity has the site for our travellers. You
go to Groups Home at the top right of this page. Then join, a group
letterbox_travellers. Go to links and click on Hitcher Tracking Page
by Wanda and Pete. Wanda and Pete have a wonderful HH page. All HH's
out there are already listed. All you have to do when you have found
a HH, is to post where you found it, when, and who found it. NOT
where you plan on placing it. Post in the traveller site or to
wandaandpete.
I know that many boxers would like to know where their HH's have
travelled. I see posts just about everyday where the question is "Has
anybody seen?" or "Does anyone know the whereabouts of?"
Posting on this site will help diminish the same old questions
and provide a place for those of us that would like to know.
Thanks to Defygravity and Wanda and Pete,
Psychomommy

I was aware of Pete and Wanda's HH tracking page, and have reported
HH info to them when it didn't get picked up off the letterbox-usa
list.

I was not aware of the other Yahoo list, letterbox_travellers, until
psycomommy's post. If I may ask, why two separate groups? Wouldn't
it be easier just to post HH reports to the letterbox-usa list,
perhaps with specific verbage in the subject (i.e. "HH Status")?
This is after all where we report box conditions and boxes that are
missing, etc. Why wouldn't we also report HH finds here?

Having to read and post to separate lists just complicates things and
seems to be a bit redundant. Just my two cents...
