From: jjp (exposto1@airmail.net) | Date: 1999-01-07 18:55:39 UTC-05:00
Hi Letterboxing friends---

I went to check boxes #3 & #4 in the Duck Creek series and both were GONE.
I found bits and pieces of #3 including (eventually) the plastic box, stamp
pad and some info sheets. Sadly the most important contents (to me) were
gone--the Log Book and the stamp I carved for that box. #4 is completely
gone. Totally missing. Vanished.

I'm not as upset about the stamps going missing as I am about the Log Books
which actually have cool stuff in them. A couple of pages from the #3 box
book turned up in my search of the area, but they are badly damaged and
barely readable.

Deepest of sighs.

I can re-carve the stamps so I may...but I'm thinking I might want all my
clues off the web. People who are interested in letterboxing can contact
me via e-mail for clues. If #1 & #2 are gone when I go check on them
tomorrow that's a definite.

Sadly yours,
Julie
