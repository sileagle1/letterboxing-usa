Hi all,

I finally posted the clues to the Eklutna Lake Letterbox that my husband and I planted on our vacation last month. For all those residents who wish there were more boxes to find and for those who might be vacationing or planning a vacation in the last frontier, here's a little taste of Connecticut in the largest state of the USA. Many thanks to Rush Gatherer and PowWow Dancer for carving this wonderful stamp, which is one in a 2004 series of five CT State Symbols: Seal, Flower, Insect, Bird and Tree. Their object is to have the boxes placed as far from their home as possible! I'm am honored I could help in that endeavor!
