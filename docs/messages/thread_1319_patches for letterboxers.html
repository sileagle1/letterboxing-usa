From: (mperry03@snet.net) | Date: 2000-10-06 13:47:39 UTC
I would like lots of feedback please. Both positive and negative.
My mom, mom's friend and I have had large patches made they are about
4*8 oval and say 25 letterboxes on them with a picture of a mail box.
they cost about $10.00 to have made.
I would like to have smaller maybe 2" round patches made. They would
be cheaper and many could fit onto an orange blazer. The problem
would be that I have to order them by 50's or 100's. There are only
four of us who go hiking together. This is where you come in. Would
you like to be able to purchse a patch for a reasonable cost to
display for others to see? I was thinking of having a basic patch and
then patches for finding 25, 50, 75, 100 ect... I would post my
address where people could get them on the web, at EMS, and in
letterboxes. I know that there are some areas that don't even have 25
letterboxes yet, which is why I was thinking of a general patch.
Also people have been interested in window clings. What's your view?
I would like to have a cling in my window so that other letterboxers
could see we were out in the woods. I don't have an estimate for
these. What size would you like to see?

Please reply.
Debbie
