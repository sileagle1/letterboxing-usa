Yes! We're excited to announce the:

"Ice Scream Sunday Letterboxing Event"

When: Sunday, April 23rd, 2006
Time: 11 am to 5 pm
Where: Great Brook Farm State Park in Carlisle, MA
(http://www.mass.gov/dcr/parks/northeast/gbfm.htm)
Hosted by: oldpatterns, Team Randalstik, Eyze on the Prize

Great Brook Farm is a wonderful state park located ~25 miles northwest
of Boston. The 1000-acres of open fields and diversified woodlands
support over 20 miles of trails used by walkers, hikers, mountain
bikers, horseback riders, and of course, Letterboxers. One big
highlight of the park is the on-site working diary farm and the cool
treat made from milk!
