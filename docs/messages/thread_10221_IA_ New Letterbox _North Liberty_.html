Turkey Creek
Location: Coralville Lake
Nearest Town: North Liberty, IA
County: Johnson

Turkey Creek is a recreation area located in Coralville Lake. It is
located just past the Coralville Dam. While driving through North
Liberty there are signs pointing you to the dam, and also Turkey
Creek.


Directions:

You might want to bring a frisbee. Play a round of disc golf. From
the pad of the seventh hole, there is a path that runs through the
woods. Halfway down this short path is a bridge. From the bridge on
one side there is a large fallen tree. The letterbox is underneath
this log. Be careful when retrieving and replacing this box, as this
is a popular place to play disc golf.

A shortcut to the 17th: Drive your car to the last shelter. The
17th hole's pad is nearby.
