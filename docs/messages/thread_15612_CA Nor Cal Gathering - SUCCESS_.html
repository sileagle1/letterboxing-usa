CA Nor Cal Gathering - SUCCESS!

From: (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2003-09-07 01:12:21 UTC-04:00



What a wonderful day we had! There were 44 people--including 2 babies and 3
dogs! We had 15 boxes to find (in one park!) tons of food to eat and great new
friends to make. We had Amanda from Seattle, A-Bear from Idaho and Silent Doug
from PA to make it a 4-state triumph and Sir Balthazar and Jugglermouse from
way down in San Luis Obispo. Lizzardbutt, Hungry Eye and their family,
PrincessLea, Snail Lady, Turtle Leader and their crew and a bunch of enthusiastic
newbies too!

Thanks to all who attended and made the day exceed our expectations--and
believe me, our expectations are always high!!

There are photos and I'll get to posting them soon!

~~Doublesaj & Old Blue~~
