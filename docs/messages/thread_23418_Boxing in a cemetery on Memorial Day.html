Boxing in a cemetery on Memorial Day

From: Rose Halter (rosmarinus@nrcengineering.com) | Date: 2006-05-30 01:46:08 UTC
We thought that Memorial Day would be good to look for letterboxes in
a cemetery, since there would already be visitors and no one would
notice two more. Well, while we were there, people started pouring in
to form a parade. Very quickly the small cemetery was full of
Veterans, Boy Scouts, policemen, sheriff's deputies, and politicians.
Finally, when two school busses loaded with marching bands pulled in,
we decided to bolt.
