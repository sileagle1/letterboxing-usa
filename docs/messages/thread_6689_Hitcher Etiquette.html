Re: Hitcher Etiquette

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-08-06 19:14:02 UTC
Yes, please please please stamp both the hitcher into the host box
logbook and the host box stamp into the hitcher logbook, as well as
your own personal stamps into both boxes! That way there is a record
of not only who has found a particular hitcher, but also where each
hitcher has managed to get to in its travels, which is often very
interesting both to its owner and to any others who enjoy following
hitchers past travels. We try to keep up on this on our hitcher
tracking list at the bottom of our webpage, but it is very difficult
to maintain with any accuracy, what with the limited info we get from
postings,etc.,so we appreciate any additions, corrections, or
whatever that folks send us! Thanks,

Wanda and Pete
