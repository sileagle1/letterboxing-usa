From: Volks Boxer (volksboxer@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-04-03 20:17:18 UTC-04:00
A seventh box - George - was planted today at the Biltmore Estate.

I have rearranged the clue pages so that the boxes are "clumped" together
based on where you park your vehicle - most of them (except Cornelia)
involve a bit of hiking.

I met Llama Mama while she was stamping up at Cornelia, and we hiked
together to Nos. 3, 4, and 5. 3 and 4 were in place, but I spotted 5 (Toe
Cooler) high and dry up on a creek bank a couple of hundred yards downstream
of where it was supposed to be. The stamp was missing (even though the lid
was still on!), but the log book was there - damp, but there. And the last
people who stamped in before the September '04 floods were Llama Mama's
daughter and son-in-law!

Look for more new boxes later this spring!
