This one can be picked up with modest effort on your way to the Bar Harbor area, and contains links to many other unpublished boxes thereabouts:
 
 
The Early Bird
 
a sanctuary right on the miracle mile
a haven to roost for a night or a while
choose from the trails marked in various hues
her majesty's patiently waiting for you
there in the shade of her stately old crest
a pile of stones ensconces your quest
 
 
 
legerdemaine
