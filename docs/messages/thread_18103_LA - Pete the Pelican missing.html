From: zed_boxing (szorzi_1999@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-03-13 20:21:08 UTC
Sorry to report that Pete the Pelican is missing from his nest. The
dock he was hiding under has been torn down by the park and a new
one built. No trace of Pete, his box, or the really special journal
and pencil that were with him.

I would have hoped that BREC (Baton Rouge Recreation and Parks
Commission) would have more respect for the welfare of wildlife than
to just pitch Pete out with the trash, but...

I will re-carve and re-plant Pete at first opportunity. I hope
those of you who had already visited will come back and restamp when
I get him comfortably placed in his new habitat. Stay tuned.
