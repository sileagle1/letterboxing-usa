Letterboxing Article in New Haven (CT) Advocate

From: (axtowner@aol.com) | Date: 2002-07-07 20:58:25 UTC-04:00
Did anyone else happen to see the very nice article in the June 6th issue of
the New Haven Advocate entitled, "Tupperware Party in the Woods"? It was
written by Hank Hoffman, who chronicles his letterboxing experience at the
Letterboxing Gathering at Sleeping Giant State Park in Hamden, CT, in May and
explains some of the in's and out's of letterboxing in general. The article
mentions and/or quotes our fellow 'boxers Butterfly, Amanda from Seattle,
Wanda and Pete, Aili, and Jay Drew. It's accompanied by two photos: one, of a
"headless" Jay Drew displaying a notebook page of some of his hand-carved
stamps; the other, of Caitlin and Ariel Drew - two of Jay's daughters -
finding a letterbox at Hammonassett Beach.

If you'd like a photocopy of the article, please contact me off-list
and I'll make arrangements with you to put one in your
hands.
