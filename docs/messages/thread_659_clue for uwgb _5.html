Der Mad Stamper,
Heir ist mein Heimlich Satz. Or in English, Here is my clue. Roughly
translated.

UWGB #5
Consult the map at the beginning of Steve's letterbox at the Arboretum.
When one goes clockwise, there is another observation spot for a
lovely view. Once there face the north most post on the deck. At 20
degrees to your right, in front of you, you shall see a group of trees
and a few bushes. Nestled amoung them shall be the letterbox.
Warning: There are many small bunnies, mice, and live creatures
around. My friend and I spied
a few of them while finding a local for it. Enjoy. The view is truly
picturesque. (sp?) Die xo
