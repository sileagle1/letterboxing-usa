From: Kendra Perry (bibliophiles@mac.com) | Date: 2003-06-02 22:26:29 UTC-05:00
Had a lovely letterboxing weekend in Kansas and Nebraska!

Saturday, took a trip to the Prairie Peace Park near Lincoln, NE. Who
knew walking through grass could be so challenging? Great hunt, and
lovely stamp! Introduced 3 newbies to the "sport," including one who
was skeptical to begin with but is now a convert!

Sunday, stopped at the Wakefield Arboretum for the Conestoga stamp...
alas, could not locate it. Has anyone seen this one, or is it AWOL?
The last direction was a little fuzzy, so it may just have been be.
Beautiful walk through the woods, in any case.
