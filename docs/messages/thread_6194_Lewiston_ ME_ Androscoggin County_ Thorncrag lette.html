We planted our first letterbox in the Thorncrag Bird
Sanctuary, Lewiston, ME.

Enter the Sanctuary at the Montello Street gate. Pick
up a brochure on your way in. Follow the yellow trail
past the Anthony Fireplace. Just past the Anthony
Fireplace, take the first trail on your left. Walk
approximately 38 paces down this trail. To your right
you will see a rock wall. Look on the back side of
the wall to find your treasure. Contained in a clear,
square plastic container. Have fun!
