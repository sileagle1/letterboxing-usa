ME boxing on Sunday

From: amymisha (amymisha@dialmaine.com) | Date: 2003-06-09 15:47:16 UTC
I visited the really lovely spot of Mackworth Island this weekend on
my way home from Boston and found three great boxes planted by phyto.
The stamps were just gorgeous and the clues were spot on. I suggest
bringing markers and or an ink pad with you, beacuse two of the boxes
don't have ink pads. Wish I'd had markers in a variety of colors for
one of the stamps... it would have been beautiful! It was a great walk
and as the first finder there was an extra treat in the fairy houses
box... pretty shells etc. with glitter for the first finder! Thank you
phyto and isoceles for more nice boxes relatively local to me! Can't
wait to find some more this weekend.
Amymisha
