Greetings fellow Boxers! I would think that a lot of us truly enjoy
The Great Outdoors, and what it brings to our sport. So here is an
idea for this Earth Day:
Take a sturdy garbage bag with you on you next search. Pick up as much
litter from the trail and surrounding area as possible, and then
dispose of it properly when you leave. It's a wonderful way to keep
our trails and parks clean. Also, it's a good excuse to give any
Muggles that meet you on the way. (I know it's not original, but it's
worth saying.)

Take care, and tread lightly.
We only have one Mother!
