Name: Lunken Field Box 1

Location: Lunken Airport Walk/Bike Track

Address: Wilmer Road, Between SR125 and Kellogg Ave in Mt. Washington

Level: 1 Easy

Need: Stamp Pad, Pen

Clues: Well, You use to feel like a MillionAIR when going to the trail BUT because of 9-11, You get to park in a
gravel lot that looks like a 5 year old built it with his tonka truck.

Once you park your car, You head up the hill to the trail. You will want to go right on to the trail.

As you walk you will notice that you are on top of a Earth Flood wall. You will walk till you come to a tree stump on your right side, You will note it's the only one that is about 5 feet tall.

This Stump is 11 Paces (pace=2 steps) behind a white 270 marked on the black top on the left hand side of the trail.

From that stump you will want to walk Dead North 0' About 11 paces. You will see a tree in the shape of a V, The Lunken Letterbox is under the tree.
