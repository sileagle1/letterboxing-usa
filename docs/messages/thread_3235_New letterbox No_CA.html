New letterbox No.CA

From: K.L.Sinizer (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2001-09-03 20:13:09 UTC-07:00

On August 30th Funhog placed the Route 36 letterbox in Tehama County near the town of Red Bluff, California. This is almost a drive-up letterbox. It marks a spot, where Nature, both Mother and Human, have collaborated to create something incredible.
To the letterbox:
Go east from I-5 in Red Bluff to the junction of Highways 36 and 99. Go exactly 10 miles on Route 36 to a generous parking area on the right side of the road. You will know the spot. Go uphill along the fenceline to a large pine tree. Sixteen paces beyond this tree is a small thicket with a large boulder on the left side. The letterbox is hidden beneath this boulder marked with a stick. Happy Trails, Funhog
