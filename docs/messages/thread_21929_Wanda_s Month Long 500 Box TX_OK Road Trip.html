Wanda's Month Long 500 Box TX/OK Road Trip

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-05-12 22:14:41 UTC


I'm finally getting a chance to post Wanda's most recent report on our web
page. Between getting sidetracked by other projects, like gardening, and
then being slowed down by some health problems, it took her awhile to get
around to writing it up. It's still amazing to me, though, that she was able to
find so many boxes while on the road from mid-March to mid-April, driving
through parts of the country that until recently typically had very few
letterboxes! Anyone interested in rejoicing along with us about this new
growth in letterboxing can read Wanda's story at

http://alum.wpi.edu/~p_miner/ourtrip.html

(It's a long one, though, so some of you may want to make a rest stop along
the way , or just take an exit now!)

Pete
