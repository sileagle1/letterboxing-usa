The Squirrel Girls place a trilogy of 5 boxes at Greenbrier State Park

From: (ADGLBNA@aol.com) | Date: 2002-10-19 20:54:57 UTC-04:00
Well the Squirrel Girls sure had a lot of fun today!  A group of us decided to meet at Greenbrier today a small, unofficial meet and greet.  Among the list were: Squirrel, Psychomommy and the Turtle, Firefly, and the ADG.  After chatting for a while and exchanging a few stamps, we headed off into the woods to plant some boxes.  Since we were all along for the ride, it seemed only appropriate that we form a new, mini-gang.  So please welcome the Squirrel Girls and enjoy our first (and hopefully not our last) series of boxes.

To all the Squirrel Girls:  Enjoy! I hope this bit of strangeness meets with your approval.  You all owe me a chocolate milk shake.
