New Letterboxer in Alabama

From: jrrflr (jrrflr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-17 15:35:36 UTC
My brother has been telling me about Letterboxing for a couple of
years now, but I had never tried it for myself. This weekend, he
visited Alabama from his home in Nebraska. I asked him if we could
go out together in search of a letterbox. We chose a box that was
located at an old mine ruin near Birmingham, but we could not find
the box. Based on the clues, we are confident that we found the spot
where the box was placed, but it was missing. All was not lost
though, we had a great hike in an interesting forest. Later that
day, I set out on a search of my own without my brother and I FOUND
MY FIRST LETTERBOX. I am now hooked. The LBNA page shows only about
a dozen boxes in Alabama, but I would like to know if there are
others that anyone knows of. Also, if there are any letterboxers in
the Birmingham/Alabama area I would like to hear from you.
