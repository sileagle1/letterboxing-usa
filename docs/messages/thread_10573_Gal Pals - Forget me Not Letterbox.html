Hey Gal Pals,

We were up to Mt. Pistapaug yesterday to look for the Forget me Not Letterbox and believe it may have been taken.  We saw an obvious pile of rocks (it sort of looked like a little well!) next to a four branched tree, but when we looked inside we only saw a frozen glass jug and bottle!  We hesitated to try to pry the bottles loose to look under them for obvious reasons, but suspect there was nothing there anyways.  If there was another pile of rocks we didn't see it under the snow, but will get back out there sometimes after snow melt.  However, if that was the right pile, we, naturally won't bother--unless, of course, you replace it!  It was a really nice spot in either case, very pretty--

Take care.

LB
