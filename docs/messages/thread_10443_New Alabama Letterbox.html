The Yellowhammer Box

Nearest City: Birmingham

Drive up Double Oak Mountain to get to the trail to Peavine Falls. Follow
the trail and cross the bridge. Standing at the bridge, to the right is the
falls (don't lean over) and to the left at approximately 70 degrees is a
blue trail blaze. From the blazed tree, look for a fallen tree at 290
degrees. Reach between the branches for the Yellowhammer.

TLC, we'd appreciate the help.
