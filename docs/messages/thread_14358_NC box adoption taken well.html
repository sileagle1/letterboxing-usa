From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-16 16:20:16 UTC
I just want to say that the folks in NC were terrific about
my "adopting" of their boxes. I have not had a single negative
comment about my adopting of all the abandoned NC boxes. I have been
trying to contact all the original placers and have gotten great
feedback. Many are taking over their own boxes and were unaware of
the changeover at LBNA.
And I even had one email from someone who does not plan to be active
in letterboxing in the near future and wants me to keep on
maintaining their clues!
Thanks so much to the Laid Back and Easy-Going letterboxers of NC!!
