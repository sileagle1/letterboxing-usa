Yes Dan,
I started counting attempts, because I found they usually took more of my
time than finds (because "it's gotta be around here somewhere!" or "Maybe if
I try the clues THIS way?" LOL)
Lucky you having an artist friend willing to carve for you and your friends.
Even so, I'd try your hand at carving. I had my doubts about my abilities as
a carver and my first few stamps kind of reinforced my doubts :-) But with
practice, I've gotten much better & found that I really enjoy the carving.
It's a great artistic outlet, even though I'm using clip art :-)
