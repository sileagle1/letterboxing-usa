Portland Trip

From: fred (lonemasswolf@covad.net) | Date: 2001-10-23 18:58:57 UTC-04:00
Hi all
 
I have visited the site and am particularly interested in the following boxes.
Tryon Creek State Park
Portland Community College
Tualatin Hills Nature Park and Rick's Farm
Rock Creek Neighborhood
 
If I still have time I also would like to capture
Pooch Place
Hyland Forest
Fanno Creek Greenway
The Lost Letterbox
Who Let The Dogs Out
Japanese Garden
Mt Tabor
 
I am also interested in placing two of my Wolf Mystery Letterboxes. I have located a promising site in Washington, but my search in Oregon has not produced any leads as of yet [any assistance would be appreciated].
 
Another of my big goals is to meet and exchange stamps.
 
I was in the Portland area about 4 years age. At that time I did hike in the Gorge area and also climbed Mt St Helen (before anyone put a box up there).
 
I plan to concentrate on getting the most boxes I can to see if I can get close to that 300 number.
 
hope to see you all.
 
lonemasswolf
