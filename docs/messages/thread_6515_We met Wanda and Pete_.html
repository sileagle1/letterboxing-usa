We met Wanda and Pete!

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2002-07-28 21:07:36 UTC-04:00
As you may know, Darrin and I are fairly new to letterboxing, getting involved during this past Memorial Day weekend.  Anyway, we were letterboxing in the Mansfield area looking for the Mansfield 300 stamps which Leader of the Pack wonderfully hid in the area.  We drove up to the site of the Peep Letterbox and noticed a car with Rhode Island LP on it!  We immediately thought of Wanda and Pete and watched them enter the woods.  We traveled in behind them and introduced ourselves!  We had a lovely chat about letterboxes in the area, and are still dumbfounded that they are close to finding their 1300th letterbox!  We also exchanged stamps and stamped into the Peep Letterbox at the same time.

What a great surprise!  Thanks Wanda and Pete for making our day extra special!

Pam and Darrin
from CT
