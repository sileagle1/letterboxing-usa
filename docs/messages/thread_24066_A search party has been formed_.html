dear letterboxing community,

as some of you may know, gingerbread jim has been kidnapped. it has
been decided by local officials, to organize a search party to comb the
state for him. with winter lacking its luster in the northeast, there
is concern that winter will not hold off much longer. if the winter

also been warned that the locals only have enough funding for a month

he didn't sound well at all. i can only hope that everyone will try to
find time in your busy schedules to help find jim. his life depends on
it.

a copy of his letters are posted on a special website dedicated to
saving jim. http://savingjim.blogspot.com

may your search be successful.
