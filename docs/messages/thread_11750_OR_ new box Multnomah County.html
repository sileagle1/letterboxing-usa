I’ll get to the clues in a minute, but first a brief rant about the value of
independent bookstores, like Wallace Books: One of the many reasons not to
buy your books from a chain bookstore (Barnes & Noble, Waldenbooks, etc.) is
that publishers pay the chains to promote their books. This means that a
great book by a little publisher can languish on a bottom shelf somewhere in
a chain store (if they include it in their inventory at all), while a lousy
book gets top shelf or center-table billing because it was published by a big
house able to buy that promotion.

Independent booksellers, on the other hand, promote the books they love and
think that you might love, too. Supporting independents = a greater variety
of books and a greater diversity of authors.

So look up the answers to the clues for this box on a chain’s website if you
want—but buy independent! Okay *hopping down from my soapbox* here’s what
you need to know to find the box:
