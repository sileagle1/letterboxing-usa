Hi All,

We found "Elaine & Jerry's Bluff" today. The box was
laying out in the open next to it's hidding spot. The
log book was inside, but the stamp and stamp pad were
missing. We rehid the box, but don't think it's safe
there any longer as there was a note that appeared to
be from the person that took the stamp.

We also found "Two Labs and a Box" intact, but the
stamp pad was dry.

Also, to Heather, it appears "Turtle Creek" is
missing. We came up empty handed on that one also.

Hope other people had better luck in their searches.
