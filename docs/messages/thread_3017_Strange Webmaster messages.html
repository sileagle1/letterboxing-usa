Hi folks,

This is Susan Davis, one of the Webmasters. I wanted to reassure
you that the Webmasters DO NOT advertise offers of any kind. I suggest
that you immediately delete anything that says reply to:
letterboxing-usa@yahoogroups.com if it contains anything that isn't
obviously relevant to letterboxing. We all need to be watchful
regarding worms and unsolicited email (we all get enough of the
"questionable" type already). I always empty my recycle bin right away
as well.

in a poll while we are on the subject. I personally see no problem
