Yippee-I found the rodeo. =-) Funhog you did awesome carving the
Let 'er Buck stamp. I was the first one to find it yesterday.

Unfortunately I picked the worst possible time to seek it out as
kids were breaking for lunch and that is their local smoke spot. So
for over a half hour I sat on a rock across the way and wrote all
over my clues as if I were writing a letter. I was determined to
stay there until they lift. One of the kids finally came over to
ask what in the world I was doing. =-) I guess they were going to
be up to even more no good had I left. I persevered (barely) and
got it once they left. Of course I was dodging finding and hiding
it from when passerbys came. Well worth the wait.

Thanks again!!! Again great stamp.
