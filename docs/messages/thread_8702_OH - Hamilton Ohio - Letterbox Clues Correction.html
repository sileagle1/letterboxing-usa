Wings of Peace

Location: Winton Woods, Winton Road, Cincinnati, Ohio
Directions: Take I-275 to Winton Road-Forest Park exit, south on
Winton Road to park entrances on left and right. Also accessible via
Ronald Reagan-Cross County Highway to Winton Road exit, north on
Winton Road to park entrances.
Level: Easy find, easy trail
Need: Stamp, stamp pad, pen.

Clues: Begin at the visitor center next to the ranger station, at
the Hike-Bike Trail. Begin walking east. Around the bend you will
come to a fork where you will go left. Continue walking to the next
fork where you will again go left. Cross over the bridge and continue
on the trail along the lake. Pass by the 2 benches and follow the
curves - see the shelterhouse up on the hill? At the bottom of the
hill look to your right to the end of the fence. There are 2 logs at
the east end of the fence. Next to the hill behind the eastmost log
you will find what you seek. Be discreet this is a busy trail. You
may continue on around the lake for a really nice walk, or return the
way you came.
