From: (cadenza74@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-09-14 22:44:22 UTC-06:00
just a note, since many of the recent viruses that have been flying around
have been attached to files with "pictures" or "pics" or such phrases in the
subject line, I have made the decision to delete any emails that have
something of that sort in the subject line without reading them if it isn't
made clear that the pictures are letterboxing related.

If you are attaching pictures or links to pictures, could you just take a
moment to label them in a way that makes it obvious that they are
letterboxing related? eg. "letterboxing pictures, gathering pictures,
lovely Molly pictures, " etc. I suspect I'm not the only one who has grown
cautious over unlabeled files being sent to me by email with no explanation
other than "new pictures here."

thanks tons,
