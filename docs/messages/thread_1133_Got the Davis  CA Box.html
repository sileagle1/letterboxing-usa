Got the Davis CA Box

From: Matthew Sparks (makaalasparks@hotmail.com) | Date: 2000-08-22 13:24:22 UTC-07:00
Hey I finally found my first Box.
Today I got the U of CA Davis box.
I first tried last week and took a wrong turn.
Today I jumped ahead to where I made the mistake and then
off I went.
I got on the right track and ended up near the box.
Today was cleaning day! there were people everywhere.
I was able to retrieve the box and opened the journal.
I was First! I stamped up and went to put the box back
There were people sitting right beside the location!!!
I had to wait until they shut the door and the cleaners moved
away.
I put the box back quickly and left the area.....
Phew... this cloak and dagger stuff is murder..

Is the placer of the Davis Box on-line, If so I would love to ask a couple
questions about your clues? I want to make sure I got them all right.

Matt
