Just stopped by Trish's Twist to check on its status. I thought that
it was in an area that would not be inundated by poison ivy but I was
wrong! I had planned on hiding it better but I think that the
surrounding foliage will deter all but the avid seeker. It is still
possible for you to get it without getting slimed but only if you are
very careful. I would recommend long pants. I will try and update
the clues accordingly but won't get to it for a week or so.

BTW, it's a very lonely letterbox. Only I seem to visit it.
