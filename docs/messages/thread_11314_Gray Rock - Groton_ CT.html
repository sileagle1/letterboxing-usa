Maddie and I found the Gray Rock boxes in Groton today, definately a
nice walk though my legs are still tired from that trek up the big
hill! What a view from the top! Both boxes were in fine
condition. Found our first HH - the Vagabond Vulture at box #1. It
will make its way into circulation early next week!

Spring is definately upon us today.... saw plenty of robins, some
woodpeckers, and the 2nd bluebird of the season! (First one was last
weekend in East Lyme.)

I was amazed by how much dirtbike and mtn bike activity the trails
at Gray Rock get. It made for some very muddy and slick area so use
caution on the first 1/2 of the journey should you try it.

-Steve and Heidi (and Madras the wonder dog!)
