These are not updated on the LbNA or my website yet:

Update / Slight Corrections to be noted for:

GREEN LAKES STATE PARK:

-#1:The Ace of Spades, Clubs and Hearts: Upon entering the park and
after passing the ticket booth, you cannot take the first road to the
right because it is closed. Park in the small lot across from it and
walk in. It's an extra 1/4 mile (at the most).

-#2: We replaced the "THE ACE OF HEARTS", it was MIA as reported. And
while there discovered that one of the clues had fallen down. Where
the clues say in French Script:
"fungus-among-us / woodpecker holes = between"
the "woodpecker holes" are still there but a big part of it has fallen
on the the little path mentioned. You will have to approach your
target area from below. We also noticed a big dead branch hanging up
in one of the live trees so be aware of it. It looked like it would
make a lot of noise on its way down.

-#3:**MICRO-BOX NOTE / THE ACE OF CLUBS: these containers will crack
with heavy rocks placed on them. I had covered "THE ACE OF CLUBS"
with a LOT of heavy rocks and cement chunks when I changed its hiding
spot. When checking on it today we found it had some cracks, so we
replaced the container. It doesn't matter if these containers get wet
or freeze as long as the caps are screwed on tight.

*******************************
