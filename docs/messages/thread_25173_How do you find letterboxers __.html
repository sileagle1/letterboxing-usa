Re: [LbNA] How do you find letterboxers ??

From: John Chapman (john@johnsblog.com) | Date: 2010-02-23 08:38:17 UTC-05:00
Hi digitalgoddess,

I suggest looking at who has planted and found boxes nearby. As a registered member on AtlasQuest, you can view the profile of the members that have planted or found boxes nearby, where members have the option to list their location. I don't believe there is a specific search tool to find members based on location.

Another option is to search for a specific Yahoo group of letterboxers in your area. Searching for "letterboxing Kansas" shows the "LetterboxingMidwest" group. Of course, Midwest is a pretty big chunk of the country.

You could also join a letterboxing event and meet people directly. Atlas Quest lists events and includes an option to search events by location. You'll need to recheck it regularly, as some events are hosted with fairly short notice.

Happy Hunting,
Choi
