Terry Childers planted on April 20, 2002

Watertown, NY is in Jefferson Co.

 

Take I81 to exit 45. Go east on 3. Follow the signs. Pass the lady on her pedistal and the clock tower. If you dodge the king and the redhead you are on the right path.  Follow the signs.Go around the circle and up the hill. Park in the parking lot on your left. Walk to the gate entrance, but don't go in. Turn 200d. and walk across drive to the corner. Turn left and proceed to where black meets grey. From there count 13 left steps and turn to 240d. Walk until you see some concrete slabs hiding in the brush. Hiding among these is what you seek.

This is a multiple box letterbox so you want to bring some paper and a pen to write down the clues.
