Hi!

I was in Maryland on business last month & my husband joined me for a weekend of letterboxing. He filed a report on our finds (and misses). You can go to the message archive and search for message #40061 to get the full list (35 found and a bunch of "not found")

Since you mentioned Laurel & Columbia specifically, I'd recommend the Supplee Lane Series, Stanley's Surprise and Turtle's Gift. I wouldn't recommend the Riverfront Park series since the trail area has been cleared in places and most boxes seem to be missing and/or in areas that are now posted as private property.

Another recommendation would be Scot's Cove and Rocky Gorge sereis. They are both in the same area so you can hunt for them simultaneously. 2 of the boxes are actually very near each other.

Good luck!

Wisconsin Hiker
