From: letsracedennis44 (letsrace@netacs.net) | Date: 2004-06-15 03:50:21 UTC
Hi folks,
I've been letterboxing for about 6 months. I really enjoy finding
boxes and the art in carving the stamps. I'll be placing a microbox
in Chichen Itza, Mexico this week. It's a popular spot to visit
Mayan ruins. I will possibly never return to check on the box, but
it would be interesting to hear from anyone who visits there via E-
mail. Would that be the ultimate "First to Find"? I promise to carve
a nice stamp....and I'll leave a rare coin for the first finder.
I'll be posting the clues after the 23rd of June when I return.
Keep those boxes coming...

Sorry to be the bearer of bad news, but you won't actually be the
first planter in Mexico. I live in Mexico City, and was hoping for
