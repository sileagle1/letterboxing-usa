This box was placed on 2-2-03 in Austin, Tx by Ants1. the name of the
box is "box of ants" You will need to do some hiking, will encounter
some rough terrain, and be able to hoist yourself up a rock wall at the
end.
Clues.
1. a good place to eat soup on the greenbelt
2. east side of creek, about 180 paces downstream from the top of the
rapids.
3. look for a dropoff of about 4 feet, look for a "ramp" to get down.
4. 30 paces to the troll's staircase leading into the forest.
5. about twenty five paces from top of staircase, look to your left
6. one of these things is not like the others
7. from here go straight to base of cliff
8. find flat rock at base, about 1 foot tall and 3 to 4 feet wide/long.
you will know its the one if you can see a "curb" to your right.
9. look up.
