to understand how one approaches a clue like this correctly...in case
I came across such a clue again.

mention of AT was giving secrets away. Too many suggested newbies
should just tramp around looking for it, figure out the compass

consider disabling the photo until the owner of the box verifies if it
is missing or indeed intact.

If I had known the clue was so old, with no finds reported I would
have bypassed this search. We were clearly not the first to wander
around there and if the signs for reforestation up ahead are any
indication they might not be pleased with Letterboxing as a hobby,
esp. "wild goose chases". :)
