Well, seeing that I am moving from Minnesota to New Hampshire, and
that Minnesota only has one existing letterbox, I felt that I should
put a letterbox in the Twin Cities for any letterboxers coming out
this way.

The box is fairly easy to find---for now, I figure that anyone coming
to the Twin Cities to letterbox has already put in enough effort. If
this one gets a decent number of visits I'll put another one in.

The letterbox is the "Battle Creek Regional Park Letterbox", and is
located in a park in the SE corner of St Paul, MN:

http://www.kaszeta.org/rich/letterbox/mn.html

Enjoy!
