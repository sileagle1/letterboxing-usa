Buchanan Woods Letterbox
 
Planted 2-24-01
 
Rated: easy  1  1/2  hour
 
Cock-A-Doodle-Moo 4-H Club Box
 
Take rte 195 to Rte 89 at Mansfield Center   Proceed on Rte 89 to the Mansfield Library, park here at the back of the lot.  Look for the Joshua's Trust sign. You will begin here on the yellow marked trail. The trail goes into the woods and out onto a little stretch of Crest road before re-entering the yellow trail and skirting along Edgewood Lane . go left on pink and then right on  yellow again. Note the "Kettle Holes left by  glaciers that passed through  hear long ago." To a triple pine and down a small slope ( It echos here mom! ) Must be why we are near Echo Lake.  To the little bridge up and over.  We spot a silver state land marker on a tree we follow the yellow  right - through the pines. At the next intersection we chose left on the white trail into the big pine wood follow to the " T "  ( 20 min so far) .
Go right , on white, after awhile come to a branch of a Y -  go right here.   Walk to a large bulging shaped pine very close to the left edge of the trail next look on the right for a mossy stump. Behind the stump in a  campers woodpile you will find your letterbox. 
       Please stamp and rehide the box privately. 
To finish your walk in the pines continue down this track pass by a left . Feathery white pines reach for you along the next stretch.  Pass the next left (which would take you to Echo Rd. )   Pass the next right, recognize this spot we've passed this way earlier.  Ahead turn to the left on yellow over the bridge again and up the hill.  Yellow turns to pink take a right stay with pink a large Kettle Hole on the left. This takes you to a large hemlock tree a very beautiful specimen.  Follow pink to return to view of road then right on yellow and back to the library.
   Hope you have a great walk in this very distinct section of Mansfield.
