Re: Geocities to close down ;( - Questions

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2009-05-13 23:52:18 UTC
As one of the letterboxers affected by this, I have been weighing my options ever since I heard about this. I could find a new free web hosting site and copy all my clues over to it, but the same thing
might happen in the future and I would have to copy them again. Or, if I'm no longer around, the clues would just disappear and there would be many hundreds of my orphaned letterboxes out there. Therefore, I have decided that I will copy my clues to LBNA where they will (hopefully) last a long, long time. Since LBNA accepts HTML tags, I should be able to keep most of my clues the way they are, minus the pictures. It will be a lot of work, but I will only have to do it once. I probably will still create a new website for my letterboxing links, photos and other information, probably at Webs: http://www.webs.com/

I have a couple of questions of the webmasters though. What will happen to all the clues currently on Geocities owned by letterboxers no longer participating, and does LBNA have enough space to handle all the clues currently in Geocities? Not sure if it would be feasible to copy old clues to a new basement or something before the end of the year, and not sure how to identify non-participants, but it would be a shame to lose all those clues.

Thanks,
Silver Eagle
