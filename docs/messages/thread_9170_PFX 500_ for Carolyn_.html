PFX 500+ for Carolyn!

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-11-14 15:07:51 UTC

Very happy to hear that Carolyn Stearns' recent trip to Kentucky,
with letterbox planting along the way, has set her well over the
PFX 500 mark!!! Carolyn, Leader of the Pack, has done so much
to promote letterboxing as a fun, friendly family activity in the
Mansfield,CT area - and beyond! The Winter Gathering she
organized in Tolland,CT on January 27,2001 was what first really
got us "hooked" on the hobby, with her warm welcoming spirit,
creative fun and games, and kind words of encouragement for
everyone! Thanks, Carolyn, for all you've done for letterboxing,
and congratulations on hitting 500! (we'll add your name to the
"PFX 500 Club" list on our web page as soon as we can!)

Looking forward to more letterboxing fun,

Wanda and Pete
