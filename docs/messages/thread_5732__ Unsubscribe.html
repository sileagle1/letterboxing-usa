Ridley Creek Box


Southeastern Pennsylvania, Delaware County, near Gradyville, PA, placed by
Arbo on 6/12/02
A 2-3 hour hike located in the outer Philly suburbs in beautiful Ridley
Creek State Park.
Getting there: From I-476 South, Take exit 3 (Rt.1 South). Turn right onto
PA 352 North. Pass by Granite Run Mall. At 4th stoplight turn right onto
Gradyville Rd. At stop sign turn right. Continue on that road past the park
office to picnic area #14. From there the games afoot.
