Hi!

I thought I already emailed her about how to find them (we're "thinking" of making a diversion to Oaxaca to find her box in early March).

Here's at least one way I know of to find International boxes on letterboxing.org:

1. Go to "Search for Boxes"
2. Click on "View all Boxes" (near bottom left of page)
3. Scroll through list to find the country you are looking for. The countries are listed alphabetically, but are interspersed with the U.S. States. That means Mexico shows up between Massachusetts and Michigan.
