If you want detailed info on the Kernville Gathering 4/24/04 you can either go to the LBNA links section and then scroll down to and click on Silent Doug's Letterboxing Info. or go directly to Silent Doug's web page http://www.letterbox.info. For those that want to go on the free raft trip make sure you read all of the information , print out a copy of the waver, read the waver and bring it with you. The signed copy of the waver for each participant is their ticket to go rafting. You get no washy without tickey.
