If anyone else is interested, Michael's has plastic reusable buttons.
They are made from two lense-shaped clear pieces of plastic that snap
together. There is a pin on the back for affixing. I stamped my
personal stamp on a nice piece of paper, then cut to match the button.
Snap together. Voila, and it can be pulled apart later for other uses.
