Chris and Heather,
Three generations of my family and I spent part of our last day on
PEI this past week on your letterbox hunt. We really enjoyed the
nature trail and learned a few things on the way. We found the
letterbox exactly where you had planted it. Lovely stamp! However,
the ink pad had gotten wet and ink had smudged the notebook and the
inside of the plastic bag. I replaced the inkpad with a blue ink pad
and wiped out the ink from the bag with a paper towel. I also added
another plastic bag to keep the notebook separate from the stamp and
pad. We were about the 11th group to stamp in. Thanks for the quest.


Ginger from Massachusetts
