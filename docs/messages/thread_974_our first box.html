you'll need this tool to find the stamp.

A round of a Colt .45

Stand at Mr. Miagi's Pride.
Find the Bearing of 325
on a red lid your eyes should arrive.
Walk to that lid,tacked to a psuedo-pine.
Bear to 62. You should derive
a place you can in or down, do climb
Walk down 3 "steps",to feel a thermocline.
Turn around now, face where you arrived.
Sight in a stash not to high,
But level, just to the eye.
And there it will be, YOUR PRIZE!
