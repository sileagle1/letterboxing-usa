TX: 4ID Chalk Ridge found

From: Celtic Relics (celticrelics@commandnet.net) | Date: 2002-12-29 21:40:14 UTC-06:00
On Boxing Day we went out to Chalk Ridge Falls to seek the 4th ID letterbox.
Easy stroll through a beautiful state park. I was shocked to reach to final
point in the clues and be able to see the letterbox from the trail. It was
just sitting on top of a log. The good news is that it was in perfect
condition. We hid it behind and under the log when we were done. We took
my parents and my niece. It was their first letter box. No one else had
stamped in it, so our group were the first three. I don't know if family
counts, but we also stamped exchanges in our books. I was very disappointed
to get home tonight and discover the new Turtle series in that area. My
parents collect turtles and decided to use one when they created their
stamp. I guess I will have to find a way to check my email while I am away
so I don't miss out on any great opportunities. The new ones will have to
wait until next Christmas.
