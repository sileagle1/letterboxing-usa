From: (cstearns@neca.com) | Date: 2001-08-09 04:32:40 UTC
Hi Everyone! Just a reminder this weekend is the Tolland County 4-H
Fair where you can drop in enjoy a little fair and do 10-12 boxes and
collect the special event stamp! 4-H Families are preparing boxes and
writing clues already! Some of the boxes were placed tonight. We
will have a guest book so please stamp that too. You should be able
to pick up some good exchanges if you don't catch us in the middle of
the dairy or sheep show ( Fri 11-2) . Friday after 4 there is a
$10.00 fee for the country music concert on the lawn. Wonder what the
cows will think of that! The rest of the fair the parking fee is
minimal and children are free. Fees go to support the 4-H program in
our county.
We are discussing donating the fair boxes to a special care
hospital so they can be used there for the patients to seek.
Hope to see many of you there. Carol - leader of the pack
