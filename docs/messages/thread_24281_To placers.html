I wouldn't mind going on longer hikes but one of the reasons those
boxes get less visitors could be that some of us do the activity with
children or significant others who don't have the same gusto as we
might like;-)

we know there is a neat waterfall or scenery at the end or like

families. I am very happy to be able to sneak in a quick box or two
between all of life's demands. Or on the way to something. If there
weren't any nearby I wouldn't get to box very often:-(
