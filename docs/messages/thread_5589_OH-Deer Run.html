Name:Deer Run at East Fork Lake

Location: Batavia, Ohio

Address: Take SR 125 Amelia from I-275 about 11 miles, Bantam Road is Left at the
light and SR 222 is to the Right. Goto The Left, The park is about .5 Miles to the
Left. Once on the Park Road, Be on the lookout as after you pass Newman Trail
you will need to start looking for Deer Run

Level: 2 (Dirt path with a hill)


Need: Stamp Pad, Pen

Clues: Find the Water Fountin. Take a reading of 300' and you are off. You will be
taking about 69 steps to the log at the top of the trail head. Guess What, You
are going down the hill. As you look around, it looks like a war zone on the tree's
as you will see downed tree's all over the place. 41 steps down the path, The path
will make a sharp 90' turn to the right. STOP here. Take a reading of 280' and start
looking for a wooden Bridge. See the Bridge, Make your way down to it (Watch your
step)

From the 14th Plank in the bridge, Take a reading of 240' and then start walking
up the hill. at the top of the hill you will see a BLUE trail marker on a tree. From this
marker take a reading of 330'. Take about 19 steps in that direction. You should
a few stumps on the ground. The Box you are looking for is in the short stump.
