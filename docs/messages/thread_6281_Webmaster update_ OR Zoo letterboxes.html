This series was in the Portland area. I'm sad to say that only the
Winking Eagle is still on zoo grounds. We knew this was a risky
series when we placed them but decided to go for it anyway. Once
spring sprung the very diligent grounds crew got to work and the
hordes of visitors arrived. They have taken their toll.
Congratulations to the early stampers who collected the now defunct
series but the remaining stamp is a beauty. Come and get it. Funhog of
The Traveling Circus.
