From: bartgrubbs2000 (kgrubbs@anteon.com) | Date: 2003-05-12 16:42:45 UTC
Just wanted to drop a line to let everyone know the status of some
boxes that Karen and I searched for this past weekend in Southestern
Virginia.

At York River State Park in Croaker: The York River (Cardinals Rule),
Taskinas Creek, and Pupa boxes were all found in good shape.
Mattponi Trail LB was MIA.

At 1st Landing/Seashore State Park in Virginia Beach: Fox Run, Long
Creek Seahorse, and the Witch of Agnesi were all found in good
shape. Ovals of Cassini, Hot Spot, and Broad Bay Shells seemed to be
MIA.

Kim & Karen
