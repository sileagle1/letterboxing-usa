Hey All!

John and I headed out to the Tillamook State Forest yesterday to hunt
for the University Falls Letterbox placed by Ryan. We wanted Brown's
Camp, also, but the GPS didn't have fresh batteries and we didn't have
the topo map. Funhog informed us that a GPS is not necessary but for
future Brown's Camp hunting the map is a must.

Anyway...We didn't do the LONG 8-mile loop. We drove straight up to the
University Falls Trailhead and made it a minor 2-mile round trip hike
instead and successfully found Porky!

This waterfall is magnificent!!! The letterbox is wonderful. Ryan
carved a fantastic stamp. Porky was happy to see us. However, he has
been there so long without ANY visitors (it was placed on 9/11/01 - very
sobering to know that) that he wants to stay happy where he is, but
hopes others will come say hi and stamp in! He's worth getting.

Just a note: If you're looking for a peaceful walk in the woods this
ain't gonna be it, folks. This area is multi-use and is shared by
hikers, equestrians, dirt bikes, motorcycles and ATVs. No motorized
vehicles are allowed on the trail itself, though, so you won't be
runover by motocrossers. You'll hear them now and then, though if you
hike on the weekends. Don't let this stop you, though. The rewards are
worth it! University Falls is a sight to behold and Ryan's stamp will
make you smile.

Best regards,
