Dear Letterboxers,

Braving a sleety Thanksgiving morning and treacherous roads, I set
off to the top of Braintree Hill this morning to salt the third of
the Braintree/Randolph letterboxes. The clues may be found on the
Vermont map at Letterbox USA.

So, Danielle, if there is a break in the weather, you may find a
2-hour drive (up I-89 from Concord, off at exit 4) repaid with three
easily accessible (though not necessarily findable) letterboxes.
But unless you are quick, you will probably have to wait till April.
