Since people seem to be wringing their hands over how to get clues, I
thought it might be a good idea to start just such a topic!

Atlas Quest has already come up and Yes, you can view the clues with
the cache button.

I, and others like me, have also filed our clues on the various
regional group sites, like LbGLK (great lakes). That would be another
good place to start.

And I am sure that there are individuals out there with their own
sites and stashes of clues. hint, hint...

From: xxxx (PonyExpressMail@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-08-05 10:10:52 UTC-05:00
Mine here in northern IL are on my own website. The Freewebs folks makes it
incredibly simple for even the most internet incompetent person like me to
have a website, albeit nothing fancy.

And I know other folks put clues on their blogs at places like blogspot. So
maybe do a search for "letterboxing" or "letterboxes" on some of the major
blog sites.

Also maybe do a search for "letterboxing" at yahoogroups and run thru the
list of what comes up. Some people "own" a discussion group just to list
their clues. There's no actual posting of "discussion" among the group
members. It's just a repository for the listowner's clues.

~~ Mosey ~~
