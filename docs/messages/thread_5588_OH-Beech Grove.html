Name:Beech Grove at East Fork Lake

Location: Batavia, Ohio

Address: Take SR 125 Amelia from I-275 about 11 miles, Bantam Road is Left at the
light and SR 222 is to the Right. Goto The Left, The park is about .5 Miles to the
Left. Once on the Park Road, Be on the lookout as after you pass Newman Trail
you will need to start looking for Beech Grove

Level: 2(Dirt Trail, small hill)


Need: Stamp Pad, Pen

Clues: Find Water Fountin #5 From their take a heading of 140' and head for
the woods. Once at the woods, Head on down the trail. You will not walk far and
you will come upon a trail that crosses the one you are on in a North/South Direction.
You will turn South on that trail and walk about 17 steps.

There is Twin tree's to your Left and a Fallin Tree to your right. About 2/3's the
way up that tree and under the Y, You will see your box.
