Redwall Series begins in San Luis Obispo County

From: jugglermouse (jugglermouse@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-31 20:57:12 UTC
Yesterday I planted the first of what will be a series of
letterboxes around the county based on Brian Jacques's series of
rodent fiction, Redwall. The first in the series, clues posted on
the website, is Martin the Warrior. I plan to base each box on a
different character in the series, and plant one box a month. Since
there are literally hundreds of characters in the books, I could go
on for years.

Tomorrow, June 1st, I will plant the second in the series:
Sunflash the Mace- my favorite fictional badger.

-Jugglermouse
