New Series: Avery Tract, Waterford CT, 8 boxes !!!

From: Steve (shol3039@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-17 19:38:30 UTC
Ok, its been over a month since I started working on this series and
I've kept the secret this long (except a select few people who
carved and planted with me)!!!

Announcing the new letterbox series "Avery Tract" located in the
Quaker Hill section of Waterford, CT. Its 8 boxes, 3 miles, give it
2-3 hours to hike and stamp everything. The land is owned by West
Farms Land Trust and I have been working with their board member
John Bodin (Bee Keeper on the trails) to plant all these boxes.

John has had many volunteers working to clear and blaze the trails.
As of now they are well cleared and most are marked. Some of the
trails this voyage takes you on are NOT marked, but we feel they are
adequately cleared and the detail to the clues is sufficient to get
everyone through the loop! There are a few spots that you will back
track through to continue the hike, but overall it is a nice loop
trail.

Enjoy!!!!

-Steve, Heidi, Madras (with help from Bee Keeper and plants by
Patrick and Amy, Butterfly, and the Drews)
