Title: Pandamonium. Placed 3-28-04 by Salamander. Location: A.W.
Marion State Park, Circleville, Pickaway County, Ohio. Difficulty:
Easy. CLUES: Take the blue-marked Perimeter trail which starts at
the marina parking lot. Follow the trail until you come to a set of
wooden steps which lead down a slope to a wooden foot bridge. Stop
immediately before the first plank of the bridge and take a reading
of 29 degrees. Three paces at this heading and you will arrive at a
group of rocks of various sizes. Smallish rocks against bigger rocks
combine to make an interesting hiding place.
