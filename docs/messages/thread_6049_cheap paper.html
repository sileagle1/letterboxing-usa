I don't know if this is just a regional thing, but in CA Michael's
has their Strathmore pads for 50% off this week. A good time to
stock up on those nifty 3x5 pads, tracing paper, etc.
And, if you're REALLY cheap (like me), you can remove the binding
from those 3x5 pads & divide the sheets up into several smaller pads,
for several letterboxes. You can re-bind them with twist-ems.

Information for the uninitiated:  Dollar Tree and Dollar Bills stores, owned by the same parent company, are WELL worth the regular stroll for boxing supplies.  To date, nearly everything in my boxes -- including the Rubbermade-esque boxes and the Hefty ziplog bags they're buried in -- have come from Dollar Tree: bound notepads, mechanical pencils, colored stamp pads, 10-packs of small folded napkins for clean up.  Also try privately owned/operated dollar stores in your area.  Why pay more?  : )
