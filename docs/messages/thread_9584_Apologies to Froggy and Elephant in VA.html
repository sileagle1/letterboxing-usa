From: Mary from Virginia <daughteroftheolddominion@yahoo.com> (daughteroftheolddominion@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-12-07 21:13:01 UTC
We figured out the problem with letterboxing in the snow. It's not
the finding that's the problem - it's the rehiding.

Today Schizokeet and I did the Froggy series and found all four [nah
nah nah, BeMa :)]. But we can't tell if they are rehidden properly.
Sure, they are covered now - by 6 inches of snow - but will they be
visible after the melt? We just don't know. We did the best we
could but it's very likely that the next people searching will have
an easy time of finding them. So we ask that the next folks please
hide them better than you find them.

We also were the first to find Elephant Walk and the Wise Old Owl
hitchhiker. Same problem with the snow. Schizokeet will replant the
hitchhiker.

Mary
