Cypress Nature Center, Cypress, Calif.

From: Gwen & Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2002-08-06 16:25:22 UTC-07:00
City of Cypress, (Orange County)  near the Long Beach city limits, Placed by Don & Gwen on August 6, 2002.
 
This location is open on Tuesday and Thursdays from 1 to 4 and on Saturday from 10-2.  No dogs and no fee.   If you are boxing in Long Beach area this box is a real quick like a bunny one to add to your book as the hike is very short.  Wardlow Road in Long Beach changes to Ball Road in Orange County.  The Nature Center is on Ball Road and Via Largo, Cypress.
 From the single entrance take the north bound path as far as you can, then turn east at the fence line and walk about 55 paces to Bob Cat Trail marker.  This parallel trail goes down, up, down and up to the main trail.  Follow the horizontal retaining logs to the very last one before the trail rejoins the main trail.  At this last log (the eastern most edge of Bob Cat trail), note the 2 upright logs, (1 shorter than the other) holding the horizontal log in place.  At the eastern edge of the smaller upright log look in the cavity created where the upright and horizontal logs almost join.  Probe under the leaves with a stick.  You are looking for a small black cylinder. 
 .
