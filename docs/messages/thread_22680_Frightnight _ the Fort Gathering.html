Frightnight @ the Fort (A New England gathering)

Come one , Come all to a Halloween themed gathering for letterboxing
enthusiasts ( young & old)!! Plus we love to meet new letterboxers!

Our Gathering will be on October 22, 2005 at the Fort McClary picnic
area in Kittery, Maine. The event will be a potluck dinner so please
bring something to share. Our event
is scheduled to run from 9AM to 4PM. It should be a full day with a
lot of letterboxes for everyone to find & enjoy!

We will have a lot of event boxes and special surprises! Including a
costume contest for adults & one for kids!!!
We have set up a RSVP list on www.atlasquest.com on the event
schedule.
Or feel free to email me at mainekokopellian@yahoo.com or
oceanwytch@yahoo.com
