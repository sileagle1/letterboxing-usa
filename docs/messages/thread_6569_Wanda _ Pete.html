Heehee...just a little sidenote about Wanda & Pete:

When I first started this letterboxing thing and got on this list, I
heard about this illustrious couple who went around and got tons of
letterboxes and all that jazz. People had mentioned "Wanda & Pete
Sightings".

I thought they were like Bigfoot. A myth we made up for the "perfect
letterboxers" or something. A euphamism for something else. :) :) :)

(Then, of course, they stamped in to my Ravenna Park box and I stood
there kind of gape-mouthed and stunned saying, "WOW....they're
REAL!".... Like finding out after all these years that Santa Claus
really DOES live at the North Pole or something. :>)

heehee!

They're everywhere!

I just wandered down to Pt. Judith (RI) to see if anyone had stopped by my
new box, and who should I see on the very first page? Yup, none other than
Wanda and Pete! F1288? 1288???? It boggles the mind! Are you guys sure
that there aren't several teams of people wandering around the country using
the same stamps pretending to be a couple named Wanda and Pete? ;>)

I'm stuck at F4, waiting for a friend to figure out why my Jeep is dead, and
if it's worth pouring more money into it. I was getting pretty bummed out
until I saw that 8 people have stopped by my letterbox in it's first week.
Cheered me right up. Guess I'll spend my time carving and wandering around
the neighborhood looking for new hiding places. This part's fun too!
