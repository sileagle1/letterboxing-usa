Fire-Lady,

I don't know about LBs outside this country (obviously they're
there), but I do know a little about London...

Do you like fish and chips? Good! Go to the Rose&Crown near
Hyde Park (I think). It's THE absolute best fish & chips ever.
It's right next to the original Hard Rock Cafe. Across the street is
a stop for the BigBus...
http://www.bigbus.co.uk/uk/html/uk_home.html.

I highly recommend the double-decker ride through London.
The light-hearted British-humour tour guides will make you laugh!

Good luck LBing in the "home of letterboxing" (close enough!)!

SteveS
