From: mothermoo2001 (cstearns07@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-06-14 01:28:13 UTC
Hi All , Long time - no see....... I miss everyone and will be on the
trail again next week with the last day of school only 3 1/2 days
away tick tick tick!

To kick off summer come to the Summer Solstice event at Mansfield
Hollow State park in Mansfield , Ct. Come to play and stay all day it
wil be great. Here is the scoop. Letterboxing will be in the main
picnic area off Bassetts Bridge Rd. Under the Pines starting at
10:15 a.m. There will be a couple of one day only boxes and a special
solstice event stamp. The Kettle Hole trail which opened last year
will be revamped and available if you missed it at last summers 300
th event. There will be 17 boxes on the historic trail there if the
rain stops -this is a flood control lake hard at work at the present!
We will have a event sign in board so you can see who else is in the
park and some intersting letterboxing diversions. Bring a carving
for a competition the theme - Summer.

Bird walk at rte 6 dike time T.B.A.
Paddle at 6 p.m. B.Y.O.BOAT at the launch
Hikes:
10 from picnic area car pool to dog trial area the wild side (
depends on water height what trail will folow)
11 a.m. red trail to dam area
10:30 Corp of Engineers led hike at dike Rte 6
12:30 dike hike, meet at picnic area ramp.
Mountain Bikes: leaving from picnic area frequently

Other Community Organizations too. bring a friend
Bring a picnic bring area clues See you there , Leader of the
Pack - carol
