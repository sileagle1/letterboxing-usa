OH - Hamilton Co. - Great Oaks Trail Letterbox

From: Cheryl Mandrackie (teammaddawg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-07-20 05:48:08 UTC-07:00
Name:  Great Oaks Trail - Winton Woods

Address:  From I-275 go south on Winton Road. Turn right into the park (before the bridge).  Great Oaks Trail is on the right side.

Level:  3  (Beware - slippery when wet!)

Need:  Stamp Pad, Pen, Compass

Clues:  From the trailhead follow the trail to the fork.  Take a compass reading of 20.  At the first erosion bar take a reading of 80.  Go 6 paces.  With your back to the log walk to the yellow "Unauthorized Trail" sign.  Take a reading of 230 and follow the trail.  At the top of the second set of steps, from the end of the railing take a reading of 12 and go 4 paces.  There you will find the Great Oaks box.
