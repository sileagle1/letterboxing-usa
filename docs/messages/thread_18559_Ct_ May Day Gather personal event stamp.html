I posted this to the LBNE site earlier, and forgot to post it here
for anyone coming from out of the area for the May Day Gather in
Connecticut. I carved this personal event stamp in honor of the
Nipmuc indians,whose ancient fishing grounds were located at the
gathering's location. To receive this stamp, tell me the name of the
Nipmuc indian chief killed in 1619. This stamp will be altered after
the event and planted as an ongoing box for all to search for.
Safe Trails to all,
