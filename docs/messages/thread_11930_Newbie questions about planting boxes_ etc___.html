Hi Kay, you should try Terre et Eau after a rain. Gwen and I had several inches of mud and couldn't go by the bike jumps. How close can boxes be planted ? There are no rules ! Our Summer Storms box in Ariz. is an orphan as are some of our other boxes since we have moved. Many of Amanda's, Ryan's and other folks plant them across the country and they become orphans . HHers can be a stamp, book in a zip lok placed along with another LB or it can be a stamp, book, zip lok inside a Tupperware. You can also include stamp pads, pre paid postcards particularly if you want to track some travels. As far as the P, F, X, V, L,C listings I'll let someone else jump in as I tend to be a non counter. About a year ago I put the C listing up on a post as a joke and it got picked up and it then got a life of it's own. Don
