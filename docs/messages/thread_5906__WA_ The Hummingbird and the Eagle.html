The Hummingbird and the Eagle.

Located in Tacoma, south of the Tacoma Narrows Bridge.
Follow 6th Ave to Titlow Park

Fairly easy terrain, one slight hill.



* Hummingbird Box

Fitness it is the key to starting any physical activity correctly.

Turn 360 and go that way.

If you make it past that right pool without looking like a fool the remains
of slaughtered and carved up trees lead you across the water on the left.

Mr. T says, "Go right fool!"

Keep to the high road and soon the track will be left to you. Are those
Letterboxers signs on the tracks? He placed 50 and found 60 and what is
that last one? How curious.

Bends are for divers so you must be okay!

Leaping logs stampers, from the "boarder" north is it T/4 or T/2 down the
trail to get to the right?

I know it has to be the two out of one! The hummingbird is free for the
moment.


* Eagle Box

Continue down the trail and then left

Pull?

Sit-up?

Pushup bars right. Right?

Go left

See asphalt? Left.

Over the steel rails and down past TOA you go.

Second right.

When you see the water best to be left.

Always choose to be close to the water

Covered area, Picnic tables, a wood bench and bench and BAR-B-Q's you will
pass.

A Madrona tree divides the trail but if not careful, you will go left so be
it!

Seven paces to a larger Madrona tree and you are there.

The Eagle has landed.
