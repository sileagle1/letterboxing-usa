If you look at the bottom of the map on LbNA you will find a small
globe. Click on that and you will have an International list. AQ has a
listing for International boards and I know Ryan has atleast one
letterbox planted in Costa Rica. Try using the country search at AQ.
One other method you can do for a search at LbNa is to got to the
search for boxes function and type in 'Costa Rica'. When we planted
letterboxes in Sweden we used Sweden in the title just for the reason
of being able to locate it by using the country name.
