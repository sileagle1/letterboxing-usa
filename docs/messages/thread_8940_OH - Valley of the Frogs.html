It was a dreary rainy afternoon. Great time for having a state park
to oneself.

I went over to John Bryan Park and talked to the ranger staff...
everything is going well with the letterboxes in the park as far as
they are concerned.

I replaced 2 froggie boxes I knew were missing and found a third
which needed to be replaced. It is nice to have a camper on the back
of the pick-up to sit in and carve a few stamps.

So, the whole series of frogs is complete again, all 27. That in
addition to the other 5 or so other boxes scattered through the park.
