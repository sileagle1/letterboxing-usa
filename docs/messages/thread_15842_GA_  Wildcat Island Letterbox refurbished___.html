GA: Wildcat Island Letterbox refurbished...

From: James D. Satterfield (jsatt@gsu.edu) | Date: 2003-09-17 17:42:10 UTC-04:00
The Wildcat Island letterbox was thought to be destroyed this spring when
Allatoona Lake level went 9' above normal summer pool level. SpikesMom and
I paddled our "six hour canoes" to the Island this last weekend, and I
replaced the letterbox. The clues are the same, and they can be found at

http://po8.com/letterbox/wildcat/


I did find the original stamp which I cleaned up. I placed it in the box
along with the new stamp.

I'm pleased to see that more letterboxes are being placed in Georgia. I am
hopeful of finding a place to put the "Triangulation Letterbox" soon, the
original location having been destroyed by construction activity.

Best wishes for good letterboxing.

Jimmus and SpikesMom
