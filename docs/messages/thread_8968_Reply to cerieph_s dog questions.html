From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-11-06 15:45:36 UTC-08:00

There are orange vests made for dogs. They are held on with velcro. They work fine. They get used to it quickly. Put it on the dog as you get ready to go on a walk. The dog gets to think of the vest as a good thing because it means a walk. As for rolling in stinky things, Molly when she was a puppy rolled in a nice big fresh pile of bull stinky stuff. The only water was a trickle of a stream. It was not a nice ride home. She still sometimes rolls in deer or horse stinky stuff. Thats what dogs do. It's a dog thing I guess.
