CA: Survivor Series a WINNER!

From: (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2003-06-08 15:20:29 UTC-04:00
Princess Lea has a winner of a series commemorating the "Survivor:
The Amazon" TV show. Old Blue and I are die-hard "Survivor" fans so
it's a wonder we didn't get them earlier!

The preserve was wonderful to drive to, with the best views in the Bay Area.
Likewise on the trail! We were surprised at how many people were out on the
cool, (well, cool for June in Calif., 65 degrees) windy day. We passed about 45
people and 15 dogs in the 1 1/2 hours it took us to enjoy the easy hunt for
these 4 boxes. We highly recommend the trip! The stamps are great too, even for
those of you who CAN survive without "Survivor".

Thanks Princess Lea for a great afternoon!

~~Doublesaj & Old Blue~~
