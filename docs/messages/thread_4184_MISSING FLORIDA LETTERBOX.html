Since that is my box, I guess I'll give the best answer. No, as far
as I know it is still there. If you go there I would STRONGLY SUGGEST
you take the short drive from there to Blackpoint Wildlife drive and
get that box also. This time of year there are often many tens of
thousands of migratory birds of all type to be seen at Blackpoint. It
is an amazing sight on a good day. That box is also intact as far as
I know and recently seen. The one I reported missing is near Sarasota
in Myakka park (the skyfish box).
