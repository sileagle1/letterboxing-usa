So this is what I am going to do with this planed series.

It is going to be 5 boxes log, each box is going to be placed at a

fun.

From: bret bridwell (dekulink69@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-06 20:48:27 UTC-07:00
baker,
you know the funny thing about letterboxing?? it is
played how "YOU" want it played. you make the boxes
for others to find. you design the stamp. you make/buy
the logbook. you come up with the clever clues to hide
your box.
it is all up to "YOU" how you play the game. if you
want to plant boxes with NO logbooks, then do so. if
you want a series to have only one logbook at the end,
then do it that way. if you want same series to have
logs in every box, then do it that way. you are
creating these little treasures for us to find. and
for the most part, letterboxers are quite content to
find any sort of letterbox. just explain in the clues
how you are playing the game. no logs, or every box
has logs. but be prepared for the backlash if others
think you are straying from the norm.. we all have our
opinions, right or wrong...
so dont take it too personal.
then it is up to the letterboxers if they wish to find
these little gems you worked so hard to create. and i
bet you any amount of money that many boxers will
praise you for planting your letterboxes for all to
find.

and LBNA or AQ dont care how you create your boxes.
they only store the clues for us to find your boxes.
so make those gems and plant them and let us find
them. wish i lived in your area, so i could find these
"controversial" boxes you are going to plant.

later,dragonrider
