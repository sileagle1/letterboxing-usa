From: Dan (danzad_99@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-12 22:35:52 UTC
We The People Letterbox

Planted by: Teach & Preach (help from Little Piggy & Nappy Kitty)
Location: Pomfret Ct. Windham County
Rated: easy

Directions: From the intersection of Rt. 101 & Rt. 169 go north 169.
Follow for .3 miles and turn into grassy parking area on the right.

Walk the grassy trail with the stream being on your right. You'll
pass a tree house. Walk over wooden plank and then a wooden bridge.
Up a little hill. At the T go left. You'll walk through a stone
wall. At that T go right. Follow the path with a stone wall on your
right. As you walk and the stone wall and path turn towards the
left. There is a hugh (I think Oak) tree with a great big hole in
it. The We The People Letterbox is behide the tree under 2 bricks
and sticks. Be careful there is barbwire behind the tree.

Have fun: Teach & Preach
