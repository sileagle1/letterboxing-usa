From: (needeeps@webtv.net) | Date: 2003-04-12 20:51:03 UTC-04:00
Eastford CT. Rated Moderate
Planted By The NeeDeeps 4-12-03
Directions: From RT.44 or RT.6 turn onto RT.198 and pull into the
Natchaug Forest drive over a bridge and up a winding hill pull into the
first parking area on the right and park.From here enter the trail and
you will soon come to a Y take a right and go down the hill to the
river.Follow the trail along the river and you will come to a stream
feeding into it,go up stream a little ways and you will cross over a
wooden bridge once,twice,three times.After the third bridge follow the
trail down to the river and continue on the trail by the rivers edge.In
a little while you will come to a big dead pine tree growing on the
rivers edge that splits into two sisters,just beyond that is a giant
white pine tree with a dead log behind it.In the end of that log behind
a wooden door is The Test Tube Baby Letterbox.(This stamp is meant as a
joke for when cloning goes bad.) Don't forget the clues for The Teddy
Bears Picnic & #1 Teddy. For they are close by.The NeeDeeps
