Concord, MA- Henry's Sawmill Site LB Recovered

From: Purple Larva Team (whistlegrunt@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-11-04 03:22:10 UTC
The Purple Larva Group went to Henry's Sawmill Site in Estabrook
Woods. Instead of finding the LB, we found a hitchhiker (a nice
surprise) but Henry's itself was not where it should have been. It
was found, intact, some 20 feet away beneath some leaves.

It is in fair shape, a little damp though. The cover is not fitting
well. After reading the book and making some comments in it, we
replaced it in its proper hiding place, then scurried out with the HH.

Future visitors will find it in its listed receptacle.
