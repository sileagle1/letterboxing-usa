From: Susan Johnson (susan@kuku.org) | Date: 2003-07-18 17:44:03 UTC
This is a gentle reminder to put the state or state abbreviation in
the subject line of posts regarding boxes. It's easy to forget
something like that -- I know I am guilty of things like this-- but a
little reminder now and then can't hurt.

I live in Portland, OR, not on the East coast where you are tripping
over boxes (though Portland has apprx. 200, so I shouldn't complain!)
and when some of you are referencing boxes, I don't know where they
are. Even if someone has mentioned it before, I have probably
skipped over the original post because of the state reference.

This is not a rant, this is not a finger pointing the first time ever
someone forgets (because, like I said, we can all be there), this is
just a gentle reminder.

I am fairly new to this sport, and I have been loving the fact that
we are connected from all across the country here at this board.

I wish I could have gone to the Prayer Rock to honor the Vermont
Viking, but I was able to hear about it here. That meant so much to
me.

For those who have been doing this longer: Thank you. I am enjoying
this so much. And, I am overdue in posting my recent Coastal boxing
experiences.

You'll be hearing from me soon!
