A Visit in So New England

From: (defygravity@snet.net) | Date: 2001-03-19 02:01:40 UTC
Yesterday we visited the Hezekiah's Knob box on Sleeping Giant. It
had a number of entries from non-letterboxers who had discovered the
box while sitting in the little sheltered area participating in
illicit recreation. We also looked for the Right Knee, but there is
still a lot of snow up there (a foot or more deep in places) and we
were unsuccessful on that one. The orange trail is pretty much a
stream too--pondlike where it levels out.

We've been dying to get outside and the predicted rain didn't happen,
so we had quite a nice day anyway.

Aili, Bruce & Erin
