Hey, all of our family will be out there supportin' ya! :-) There's enough
time to box the rest of the weekend. And, frankly, it's one of the best
people-watching days of the year.

Decades ago, the day after Christmas used to be the best people-watching day
of the year. But Black Friday has overtaken it. After the first major
crushrush, I like to sit in that little cafe inside Fry's Electronics, have
hot chocolate and an avocado sandwich for breakfast, and watch folks.

Y'know, that's something to think about for next year. A letterbox hidden
inside Fry's that's only available on Black Friday just for kicks. With all
the commotion, a finder could probably stamp away right in the middle of the
store and absolutely nobody would pay any attention. Hide it the day before
& pick it up the day after. Gotta make a note to myself for next year. :-)
Wouldn't really expect to get any finders but it would be fun to do.
