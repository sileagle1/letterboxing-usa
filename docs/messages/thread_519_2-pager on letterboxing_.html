From: (Letterboxr@aol.com) | Date: 1999-06-08 19:30:46 UTC-04:00
The 2-page info sheet is accessable on the Letterboxing North America site.
Click on the word JOIN at the top... then click on the words "your own
letterbox".

This takes you to Erik's "Getting Started" page, which includes a link to the
info sheet. There is a web-page version for viewing, and a MS Word version,
if you have that. Also, there is a pure text version for importing into
other word processors. I recently purchased Adobe Acrobat Writer, so I'll
put it up in PDF format soon, which will make it more universally accessable
without losing the original format.

Thanks for asking.
