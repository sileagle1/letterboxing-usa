With the gorgeous weather we've had the past few days here in
Michigan, I was able to ride down and check out locations for the May
31st G.L. Letterbox gathering.

The final choice is Waterloo Recreation Area, near Chelsea, MI. This

shelter for our date at the Big Portage Lake Unit.

for those who are planning to stay overnight and would like to make
