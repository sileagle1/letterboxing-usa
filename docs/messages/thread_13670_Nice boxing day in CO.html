Nice boxing day in CO

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2003-06-19 03:29:41 UTC
Spent a nice, cool, slightly overcast day out on the trails today!
Planted another box on the same trail as the Beltane Box, so that
will be a two-box trail as soon as I get the clues posted.

Headed up to Deer Creek Canyon, and caught the recently planted "Deer
Creek" box. I was stunned to see that two people had snagged it
before me (there are so few boxers in CO). Team Min Dawg was one of
them! *waves* Then we headed up to get the Jolly G Man's "Soar
Feet" box. What a beautiful hike!

Both boxes were in great shape. I put a pen in the Soar Feet Box,
which hasn't been visited that often, but is really on a spectacular
trail!

Hey two "finds" in one day in Colorado is something to do the happy
dance about!
