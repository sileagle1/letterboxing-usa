Hi there, everyone. Personally, I enjoy seeing how
the
"sport/hobby/activity/past-time/happy-fun-time/shindig"
has taken off in the year since I started. I enjoy
meeting others in our "society" and hearing about
adventures on the trail. We all have stories to
share. I highly respect those that have helped make
LBing what it is now. Pete and Wanda are the best!
(don't blush, P & W, if you read this) I've only met

that so many of you have done in placing boxes and in
shutting off the tv and getting outside to see these
creations. BRAVO!!!
