From: Laurie (laurie62302@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-17 16:43:57 UTC
Hi All!
Just discovered Letterboxing online while looking for hikes to do with
the kids, and WOW, This is really cool! We are starting our first
search today. I was so excited about it, that I spent Saturday night
making journals for the kids. Scott and I have 8 children between the
two of us, and are always looking for inexpensive, but fun things to
do with the younger ones. The 4 youngest will be participating in this
new adventure. They are 11, 10, 7, and 6 (all girls). We do a lot of
hiking, bottle digging and making up our own adventures, but it is
going to be so much fun to "connect" with others on our outings. We
live in Western Massachusetts, so today's adventure will begin close
to home. Looking forward to sharing our "finds" with all of you! :)
Enthusiastically,
Laurie, Scott, Larissa, Summer, Samantha, and Tara
