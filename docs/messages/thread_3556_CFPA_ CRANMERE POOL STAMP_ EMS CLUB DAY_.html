From: Eric J. Eurto (enicltrbxr@netzero.net) | Date: 2001-10-24 00:26:38 UTC-04:00
Sorry you didn't find us on Saturday in Voluntown CSCM. Only a few of us
ended up showing up as a matter of fact. We have some great new friends in
our letterboxing circles! Patty Pendergast of the Ct Forest and Park
Association is now a letterboxer. The CFPA is the organization that
primarily maintains the 700 miles of CT Blues Blazed trails. They also
publish the CT Walk Book that is full of maps and trail descriptions of some
of or favorite letterboxing paths.
Come and meet Patty this saturday the 27 at Eastern Mountain Sports in
Waterford, CT as she'll have a table set up telling us all about the CFPA
and what it's about!
Also, COME SEE THE STAMP FOR THE FIRST EVER LETTERBOX IN THE WORLD FOR
CRANMERE POOL. The gentleman that published the Pequot woods series of
letterboxes made a visit to Dartmoor and brought back a copy of the Cranmere
Pool stamp. Come see it, and get 20% OFF of everything in the store that
day! As letterboxers, you all qualify for the discount in our store!

By the way, EMS has over 80 stores now and Club Day will be a company wide
event. Belong to any outdoor club or organization and get 20% off everything
in whatever store you are in! Great time to pick up new letterboxing gear!

Eric J. Eurto
