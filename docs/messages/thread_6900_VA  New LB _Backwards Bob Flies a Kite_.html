VA New LB "Backwards Bob Flies a Kite"

From: Raygan (dragonpearl@rcn.com) | Date: 2002-08-12 14:36:20 UTC-04:00
If you aren't used to alot of walking this one may wear you out. It
wore me out when I planted it. Bring plenty of water in case you
get thirsty and I guarantee you will

Backwards Bob wanted to fly a kite, so he went to the most
popular place in Virginia Beach to do so.

He parked right in front of a lighthouse and walked up a flight of
steps. "Whew, I havent done that in a while, I need to sit down."
He found a stone bench and sat a while to catch his breath. The
bench was in front of a large tree, planted by a woman's society.
As he looked at the tree, he heard the sounds of laughter and
playing to his left. He looked a saw children playing in a wooden
cove.

"That looks interesting"

He got up and walked down the mountain, heading for this cove
that isnt a true cove. He wandered around this fun place and
noticed another playground a distance away that didn't seem as
popular. "How sad."

He decided to swing on the neglected swings a bit discovered a
shady spot with a number 4 on it.

"It's getting hot, I think I will go sit in the shade"

To find the box, stand next to the pillar with the number 4 on it with
the pillar at your right hand. Look across the shade to the pillar
directly in front of you. Walk to it and turn left. Walk past 4 pillars
and at the 5th, look up. Log in and have a great time!

When you see the stamp you will understand the "Backwards"
Part of the title.
