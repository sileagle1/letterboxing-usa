darling. To rejoice this year, love, Ive hidden a valentine for you to

rocky hollow there near the pond. Not far along, on the right near the
courts, is a lovely place for a picnicanother day. Id love for you to stop
in the southEAST cornertheres a fieldstone wall and a large tree. Ive
tucked your Valentine low in the wall seven paces west of that tree.
Replace it gently and we shall visit it for years to come.
