Zodiac #11: Aquarius
Fisk Cemetary
Chester, MA (Hampden County)

Placed: Sept 18, 2003 by TehutiKa, AsetKa and kids!
Distance: Depends how long you wander around....
Time: As long as it takes to find it!
Difficulty: Hard

Directions: Fisk Cemetary is on Bromley Rd. This
cemetary was moved from it's previous location to the
current one to prevent it from being flooded over as
part of the Knightville Dam project. Considering the
age of the majority of the stones, we found it fairly
impressive that they bothered to do so. The cemetary
is still in active use.

Clue:

Tyyne Madeline Hakola
Feb 4 1903
Mar 6 1985

90 degrees
