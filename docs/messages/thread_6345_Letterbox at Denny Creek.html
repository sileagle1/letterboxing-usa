I have hidden a letterbox near near Denny Creek, Washington.  Details as follows:
 

A nice hike for the whole family to enjoy.  Bring your swim suit and towel!

Letterbox Name: The Bridge Box

Placed:               7-18-02

By:                     Deziree Iams

Located:              King County, Washington

Nearest Town:      North Bend

Clues:

Starting from the Denny Creek trailhead, (Exit 47 off Interstate 90, trailhead is past the campground) hike along the trail for about 1 mile until you see an L-shaped bridge.  Cross the bridge.  From the bottom of the stairs, go on the right trail.  Soon you wil see a trail leading to the waterslides.  Do not take it.  Pass the next one too.  When you come to an intersection by a large tree, head up the hill about 7 to 10 paces.  Turn right onto a small trail.  Go past the broken log and stop when you reach a small clearing.  The treasure is on the back side of the first stump/tree combination.  Cover with bark when finished and let me know if the letterbox is OK when you find it.
