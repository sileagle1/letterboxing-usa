Sir Balthazar's 100th Find

From: jugglermouse (jugglermouse@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-10-07 14:36:32 UTC
Sir Balthazar is of course much to shy and self-effacing to report
this himself, so it falls to me to tell you that somewhere in the
midst of planting boxes in preparation for the SLO Stampede in the
Meadow event, he also found some of the boxes that AshRaven and I
were planting, and one of these, (Rollo the Bankvole to be exact!)
was his 100th box!

I wish to publicly congratulate him, and to extoll his virtues as a
letterboxer, anxious worrywart host, and, for my money, an all around
perfect partner to spend time doing anything at all with.

Congratulations, Sir Balthazar, the achievement of the 100 box mark
is merely a numerical reflection of so many wonderful and varied
adventures, most of them shared with me, that you have chosen to
embark on in search of letterboxes in the past eight months. Let us
have many, many more such fine times.

-JugglerMouse
