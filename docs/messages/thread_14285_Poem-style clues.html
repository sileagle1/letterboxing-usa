From: Volks Boxer (volksboxer@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-07-13 00:05:59 UTC-04:00
I never much cared for poetry, couldn't write a decent poem if I tried...but
two years ago, I placed my first box in a forest named after a famous dead
poet.

I went home to write up the clues and they would *not* come out any other
way than a poem....and it reads like something T. S. Eliot,Tolkein, or e. e.
cummings would have written....the words spilled out as though writ by
someone guiding my hand. As lengthy as it is, I wrote it from beginning to
end in about 15 minutes - with no changes.

Weird and Wonder-full...
