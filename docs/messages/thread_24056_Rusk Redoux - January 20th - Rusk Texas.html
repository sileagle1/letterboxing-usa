Rusk Redoux - January 20th - Rusk Texas

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-12-26 19:16:04 UTC-06:00
The main Texas gathering was held last year in Rusk and lots of new boxes
were planted there. Right after the gathering, we had an influx of new
boxers in the area who, sadly, were a little too late for the gathering.

Because some of these new boxers are wanting to meet up and because last
year's attendees left behind such a great crop of boxes, we are going to
have a reprise of the gathering, meeting in Rusk At Rusk State Park on
January 20th at 10 a.m. and going until we all get tired and go home! Lunch
will be potluck, but we do have power available for heating. Also, there is
a big fireplace in the middle of the gathering location, so if it is cold,
we'll have a roaring fire for those who want to stay close and visit. All
are welcome, but please - RSVP on AtlasQuest for a headcount.

Any questions, email me at barefootlucy at gmail dot com.
