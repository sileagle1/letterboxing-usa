Turkey Dinner Letterbox
Planted 11/28/02
By Amanda from Seattle
Crystal Springs Park
SeaTac, WA (King County)

This is a nice short uphill climb to help work off that Turkey Dinner!
To find the Park:

Northbound on I-5
Take exit #153 and stay in the northbound exit lane;
At the signal turn right onto Southcenter Parkway;
Make another right onto Klickitat Drive. Stay in the left lane;
Continue on Klickitat Drive to 53rd Avenue South; turn left;
Turn right onto S. 159th into the park.

Southbound on I-5
Take exit #154AB, Southcenter;
Turn right onto Southcenter Boulevard;
Take an immediate right again onto 52nd Avenue South;
Follow the road to the left (narrow) onto S. 151st Street (steep
hill);
At stop sign, turn left onto 51st Avenue South.
Continue onto 51st Avenue South until it turns into Klickitat Drive;
Turn right onto 53rd Avenue South;
Turn right onto S. 159th into the park.

Eastbound on Highway 518/I-405
Take the Southcenter Mall & Parkway exit;
Turn right on Klickitat Drive;
Turn right on 53rd Avenue South;
Turn right onto S. 159th into the park.

Westbound on I-405
Take the Southcenter Boulevard and Mall exit;
At the stop sign, turn left onto Southcenter Boulevard;
Turn right again onto 52nd Avenue South;
Follow the road to the left (narrow) onto S. 151st Street (steep
hill);
At stop sign, turn left onto 51st Avenue South.
Continue onto 51st Avenue South until it turns into Klickitat Drive;
Turn right onto 53rd Avenue South;
Turn right onto S. 159th into the park.

Park by the restrooms.

Clues:
Look for the street signs S 159 St and 51 Ave S. Go to the right and
take the paved trail up the hill past the picnic shelter, play area
and artifical "pond". You will go up some steep stairs. When you
reach a bunch of choices, stay straight (approx. 250 degrees) Look
for a Magnolia on your right and a Holly bush hiding a snag on your
left. The letterbox is at the base of the snag, hidden by some pieces
of bark.
