Mystery Gathering Guest Appearances

From: Alafair (ms_alafair@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-05 12:50:07 UTC-07:00

Hello Everyone!

When we received our first invitation from Tom No one to assemble, I posed a question whether or not certain coveted letterboxing treasures and/or their creator would make their appearances. No news, so here it is again.

Amongst those is KMartha, whose namesake is steeped in controversy just now. Is this a GOOD THING, fair Martha?  KMartha, have you gone underground since your name has been sullied?  Has anyone seen you lately? *Sigh* It's been months.Perhaps if you graced us with your presence seven days hence, I am sure this would go a long way toward restoring your "tarnished"reputation. Please don't be too busy polishing the silver to come and play.

Another gem is the venerable Flutterby who I believe fluttered into Virginia just recently. Will this lovely specimen set free from the land and hand of Legerdemaine find its way north before flitting off on its next journey.

And what about you, Legerdemaine? We've not heard a whisper from you about any sort of mysterious and magical transport of your being to our midst. You are most welcome, you know.

Alafair

 Tom Noone wrote:

Gather ye weary seekers at the shores of my waters.  As sachem of the
Eastern Niantic people I welcome all those who pursue hidden treasure to
share in the bounty of our harvest by the sweet waters that have born my
name for almost  150 years now.  Come prepared to share your carvings and
your bounty at my refuge.  We will gather two hours before moonrise three
weeks after the harvest moon to celebrate a successful hunt.
