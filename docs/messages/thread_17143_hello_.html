Hi. We're fairly new to letterboxing, but have had a
great summer in New England turning over rocks. It's
also neat seeing a few familiar names on this list.

In the summer, Tak was a little wary of the
mosquitoes, and it seems that this time of year is
almost nicer for letterboxing than the summer. We've
been averaging about one a weekend as of late. Last
weekend had to be one of the nicer letterboxes we've
found. The day couldn't have been more beautiful. I
took a few pictures:

http://www.livejournal.com/users/disco_vixen/112117.html?nc=6

Still contemplating where to put our own letterbox. I
know so many precious places deep in the woods of
northeastern CT. But we want to get a good feel for it
before we do.

But that's it for now, I guess. I just wanted to drop
a quick hello and thank you to everyone who takes part
in letterboxing. It's been quite a wonderful addition
to our lives.

-kat
