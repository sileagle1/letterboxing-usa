I do understand that the Southern CA region is a long way away from
San Luis Obispo county. It would be great to attend a boxing festival
down in your neck of the woods, too. I would like to attend any that
are held in SF or Sacremento, as well.

By my quick count, there are about 76 letterboxes in the San Luis
Obispo coastal area. While that is small potatos compaired to
Connecticut (what do you folks in Connecticut have in your water,
anyway?!), it is pretty lush for boxing.

Some of these boxes, like my Middle Earth Series (10 and growing),

Even if we do not have a festival here, and we do want one, it is a
nice location to visit and to box. It is kind of discouraging to
check in on your box to find no one new has stamped in. If you are
coming to this region, please, check in with JugglerMouse or me. We
can give you some help with how difficult the boxes are for differing
physical conditions. Besides, we would love to have some more
exchanges.
