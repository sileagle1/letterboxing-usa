From: SpringChick (letterbox@attbi.com) | Date: 2002-11-06 11:50:53 UTC
Here are the ones I know (or think I know):

X - stamps exchanged while on the trail (for instance if you and I
met on the trail we would stamp each other's books and each have
an "X" -- and being you are from MI, this could actually happen!)

E - Event stamps
C - I believe some people are using this one for "confiscations"
(boxes that have been removed, primarily by the NPS)
L - Lizards

As for V, R, and TS... I haven't a clue
