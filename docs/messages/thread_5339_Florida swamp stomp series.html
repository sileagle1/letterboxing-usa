From: (wburke46@comcast.net) | Date: 2002-05-12 22:35:52 UTC-04:00
The Naples Tribe is proud to present our first in a series of Swamp Stomp letterboxes, the first of which is placed in Collier County.  All the hand-carved stamps in this series depict an element of our native culture.  The boxes have brush pads but not pens.  Compass directions are approximate.  Have fun, and bring your bug spray!
