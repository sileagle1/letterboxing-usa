Butterfly and I had fun-but-exhausting day in RI today. After
dropping off her grandson at the airport (new convert to boxing)
we visited various bike trails, regular spots and a new mystery
that really had us squealing (just as I was getting spoken to
about my co-piloting skills I spotted the exact thing we were
looking for!) as we got first-find and even a special prize in the

for tomorrow. It's getting hard to keep up with all the new plants!
Speaking of plants, finding the engangered pitcher plant really
made the day special.
See you on the trails.
