Since I'm "another boxer" that Talking Turtle was discussing this with ;-),
I'd like to just clear something up: I have nothing against the exchange of
stamp images. Andy & I had written back & forth and agreed to exchange
stamps and I was thrilled!

We are relatively new boxers, so what I was questioning was whether it's
appropriate for one person to send all of the images carved for the Four
Directions gathering to someone else?

I would have been more comfortable if the organizers had wanted to send Andy
the entire series - in fact, I think that would have been cool.

But the other part was that my son was feeling pretty proud of his carving
(thanks to very kind words from Koyote!) and had wanted to send the Caddo
stamp image to Andy along with our own personal stamp images.

Somehow I would be uncomfortable sending copies of other folks' stamp
images, but that's just my own personal feeling, and I'm very open to
learning the etiquette of the sport from the greybeards!
