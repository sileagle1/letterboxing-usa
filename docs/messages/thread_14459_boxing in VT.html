From: irishcod5 (IrishCOD5@aol.com) | Date: 2003-07-21 01:58:47 UTC
After reading about the significance of Prayer Rock and the legacy of
Erik Davis I decided to include a trip to Bristol this weekend while
visiting my sister in VT. Hope everyone finds the opportunity to
come to the birthplace of North America letterboxing. I found myself
sitting by the river, enjoying the solitude and contemplating how
much my life has changed since I started letterboxing. A worthwhile
visit on many levels.
While in VT I also visited the Man in Stone letterbox and West
Arlington letterbox - both in good shape and both near nice water
attractions.
