ROAD TRIP!! We've just returned from a 4,222 mile road trip that was a
blast!! No reservations (except in Yellowstone), just went where our hearts desired,
boxing all the way with a lot of Lewis & Clark and other historical sites
along the way. These are the following boxes we were successful at finding:

We also got 2 mystery boxes: Buffalo Warrior and Montana Mystery #1.

Our vote for the best location for a box, beauty-wise: Boulder River Secret.
Our vote for the best carved stamp: Sacagawea's Brother. Both of these by Don
& Gwen.

We also placed 6 boxes on this trip, 5 of which have been posted.

A big "thank you" to all who planted and helped us have a VERY memorable
vacation!!
