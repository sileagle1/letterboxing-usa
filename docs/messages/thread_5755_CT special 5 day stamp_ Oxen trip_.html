CT special 5 day stamp. Oxen trip.

From: woodschuckstraub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-06-15 23:33:20 UTC
Went out today and found the Stearns Family oxen trip as they were
passing through Elliott Station in Pomfret. If you get a chance, you
have to see this and get a copy of this stamp. For those that don't
know the eastern part of the Air Line trail, It is rough and not
developed as the Western sections are. This is a very hard trip and
quite an adventure. The stamp they are carring is only going to be
used for 5 days. The time of the trip. it is a great stamp for a once
in a lifetime trip. Don't miss out on getting a copy of this stamp.
Best of luck to the Stearns family and the adventure you are
taking....Chuck & Molly
