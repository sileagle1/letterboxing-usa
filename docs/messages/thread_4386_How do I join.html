Beverly, By signing up for the yahoogroups (what you had to do in order to send this) you have already joined. Letterboxing has no joining requirements, in fact you don't "join" letterboxing. You just enjoy letterboxing. When you access www.letterboxing.org you can click on the big book in the left hand corner and then click on the map.. the state of Rhode Island specifically... and that will list all (well maybe not all) the clues for Rhode Island. You can also check out my website, listed below, which has links to many popular letterboxing websites, some aimed specifically at R.I and the rest of New England.
