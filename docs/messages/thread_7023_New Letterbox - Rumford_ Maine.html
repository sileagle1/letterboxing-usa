Rumford Letterbox
Rumford, Maine

Planted by "the Family"(Yuzi, CERK, VFTR, butterbaby, spunky spaniel,
and rtrw) on August 16th, 2002 at the end of a lovely vacation.

Distance - 15 feet
Time - as long as it takes you to stamp up
Difficulty - my cat could find this one



This box is located on the west side of Rumford, Maine. Turn off
Route 2 across from the public boat launch onto Dragoon Road. This is
near the McDonald's restaurant. Make your first and only right and
watch on the left side for Grandfather Maple. Behind him, covered
with two brook rocks and a few sticks is your prize, thought up and
hand carved by the spaniel. Enjoy. Email me at rtrw@attbi.com with
any updating that needs to be done. I'll arrange it.
