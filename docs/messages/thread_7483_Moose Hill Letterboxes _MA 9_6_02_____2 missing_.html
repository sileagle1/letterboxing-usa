We got back today from a wonderful hike at Moose Hill. We found
the first two letterboxes in great shape! (Boulders and Hobbs Hill),
however the Moose Hill Letterbox (near the fire tower), and The Bluff
Letterbox appear to be missing. We saw two fawns on the way back
from the Boulders Letterbox! They just stayed there on the trail!
Let us take their pictures! They even crept closer! 20 minutes!
Unbelievable! After searching thru the posts after we got back, I
found that the one near the fire tower was reported missing. A
little disappointed but still a great hike and a wonderful view
from "The Bluff". The admission has gone up $1 by the way, to 4$.
Quite the bargain. Tons of great trails!
