Revered Beetle,

My tribe will joyfully welcome you at our gathering. I have sent
you information for resting your weary band at your email account.

We have been forced to retreat to a white man's lodge for the day.
It is a strange sight for my brethren's eyes as it has a kitchen
lodge in the building. It is a strange place filled with mystical
wonders - a fireplace for cooking that is a big white box but does
not use fire or smoke! There is another white box that turns
everything put into it as cold as a winter's night! I am not sure if
I dare eat food that has been enchanted in such a strange place but I
will try. We welcome whatever drink or food you can provide us from
your bounty.

When my tribe has met before we have been amazed by the bounty
offered by all journeyers. I am sure that the food, the travellers
and the tribal carvings will once again be wonderful to behold.

I have heard from sister Alafair that few have chosen to learn to
make the traditional tribal journals. She fears that many will show
and want to do so but she will not have gathered the supplies to help
them.

Tom Noone
