At long last --- last night I found my 200th letterbox. Went to
Brenton Pt in Newport, RI after work. It was fairly dark out when we
got there. If you have never been to Brenton Pt it is a beautiful
spot to watch the sunset over Narragansett Bay. Last night was no
exception, the sunset was magnificant!

The box, Brenton Reef (the first box in the Fast Passage series) was
in excellent shape. This box is 3 years old and still has the
original log book in it. Lots of letterboxing history here.
