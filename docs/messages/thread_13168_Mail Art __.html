yes franz

been doing it for years when i feel like decorating an envelope......i use rubberstamps (store bought) and color the images in....making a scene or just to match the card i made....

i also one time.....took some foam core...cut a big size (after measuring out say like 6 sizes of the limit of post office.....took the whole big size piece and rubberstamped...colored...a scene....then i took the measurements of post office limit and cut the big size piece into about 6 pieces with a piece protruding into the next piece...(like a puzzle piece)...i did that to all the pieces.....it is now puzzle pieces.....then i put a stamp on it and addressed the back and mailed it to my sister where she assembled the puzzle pieces as they came in ....i mailed a piece from different sections twice a week......extra postage is required for this.....it was neat....the artwork wasnt hugely great but for a "trial" it worked good.....

do a search engine for MAIL ART and you will see some really wierd...odd ....things people have mailed thru the post office....makes you smile at some of it.....

wanda
