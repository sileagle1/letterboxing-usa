Bounty! Fair trade offered

From: Sam F.Purre (wiccaboxer@occultmail.com) | Date: 2002-11-26 04:52:47 UTC-08:00

Whereas, a certain friend of mine, a letterboxing recluse to be
sure, is in dire need to deliver a certain payload to a certain
place, has charged me with obtaining the services of
"Flutterby", an object I know by name but otherwise know
very little of.

Whereas, it is well known that such objects are in high demand,
he has authorized me to offer a bounty for the delivery of
said object: choice of either a WoM clue that very few have
seen, or the key to a certain unsolved mystery on a certain
"Wachusett Mountain", a place I've never seen but have heard
wonderous things about.

Finally, he has authorized me to offer _both_ bounties should
said object be delivered by December 7th, as there is great
urgency in this matter. He has further requested that all
further communications in this matter be taken off-list, and
that all such communications be made through me.
