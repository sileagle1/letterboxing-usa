NJ Lemon Squeezer

From: (PPogie@aol.com) | Date: 2002-04-21 07:35:54 UTC-04:00
Don,  Mary and I found your Lemon Squeezer Letterbox today.  Although it's not the goal, it is fun to be the first. :)

We drove to the trail head but were unable to find parking ........... gotta get there early I guess.  We checked the map and decided to park at Lake Skannatati and go up that way. We've been up the red dot trail past Black and Bald Rocks before so I was disappointed we couldn't go up that way today .............. it's a neat hike,  but I was also very please to see this other part of the Park.  There had been a fire recently just past the Cape Horn Mine on the Long Path.  Between the fiire and the old mine it was quite an interesting place.  

Soon after we arrived at the Lemon Squeezer it began to rain and there were rumblings in the distance, so we didn't hang around very long.  We hiked up to the top, had no trouble finding the box (which is in great shape), and stamped in.  

Don, we had a great hike to another place I probably would never have gone without a letterbox to find.  Thanks!

Looking forward to your next one.

Priscilla
P of M & P
