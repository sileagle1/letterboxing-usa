Where to get Joshua's Trust Book

From: Valerie Gilson (glassreed@yahoo.com) | Date: 2000-12-29 16:19:08 UTC
Hi all...

I got my copy (the 1999 edition) of the Joshua's Trust book directly
from:

Joshua's Tract Conservation and Historic Trust
PO Box 4
Mansfield Center, CT 06250
(860) 429-9023

This organization is truly a gem and a worthwhile cause. The book is
nearly 300 pages (287 to be exact) and is ONLY about $9.00. The
proceeds benefit the Trust.

The book is easily available throughout the "Joshua's" towns of
Ashford, Chaplin, Columbia, Coventry, Eastford, Franklin, Hampton,
Lebanon, Mansfield, Scotland, Willinton and Windham and Tolland. (And
Mystic's Denison Nature Center, too!)

The folks at the Trust are friendly and quite helpful. Also, if you
get the book directly from them, you may get a really nifty decal for
your car with the Joshua's Trust logo on it! Membership to the Trust
is also available at $10/year.

Please consider *paying* for the book; it's not alot when you
consider the treasures it contains.

-Valerie Gilson
