SoCal update

From: The McQuades (mcquades@twcny.rr.com) | Date: 2003-07-03 11:22:32 UTC-04:00
Strawberry Girl, Lone Wolf, Joe Crow and I found the Two Cats and a Troll
box in LA's Griffith Park earlier this week with little problem. I winced a
little when I saw Pandora's stamp and note about boxing with her aunts from
a few weeks earlier, knowing how the rest of their day ended up. As Pandora
noted in her later update of this box, the clues note two boxes but if
there's really a second one there, we too were unable to find it. It's
possible to interpret the clues as leading to a second box, but the spot
indicated seems to be the exact same location as the first box.

The next day I went to check on my orphaned Terre et Eau box in Costa Mesa.
Kept company by the howls of nearby coyote as I read through the log book,
I was pleased to see how many people (and one HH - Virginia is for Lovers)
have visited. Many people noted how mucky the trail gets when it rains, so
be aware of this if you plan to visit some day. I added a new stash of
postcards to the box, but otherwise all was well.
