Has anyone been to this box? I checked on the other 2 Hoffmaster
boxes this weekend, both of which had weathered the recent storms
wonderfully -- one had been visited, the other had not. But I didn't
check on Seagull Shadows because I didn't want to climb the huge sand
dune (Note to self... don't put boxes in locations that you don't
want to have to go back to).

I received an e-mail today from somebody who had went hunting for
Seagull Shadows this weekend and wasn't able to find it... Just
wondering if anyone had been there (perhaps the people who had been
earlier to Spirit of the Dunes?) and could possibly save me the dune
climb to verify if it is okay or missing.

Really, I'm not lazy -- just would rather devote my hiking time to
scouting out new locations ;-)

Thanks!
