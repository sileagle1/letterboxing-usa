The main problem with double boxing is the size. The largest notepad
that I use is a 5"x7" spiral made by OMNI which has side pouches on it's
covers. These are perfect for putting extra "give away" things like
instructions,maps,whatever. This fits inside a rubber-maid rectangle box
just fine along with a pad , stamp, and pen. This is as big as I feel
comfortable to hide at a public/state park/preserve.
