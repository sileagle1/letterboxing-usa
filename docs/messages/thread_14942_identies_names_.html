Hi Lauren.
I'm glad you can join us for letterboxing fun.
I think the creative names are fun. I chose Bird Stamper because I

with a number. So I got a stamp with a bird on it so I could stamp
the log books with a bird also. :)

down menu for "Pick a placer", and you will get a list of the names
registered on the web site.
Use your own name or pick a funny one. Being creative is the fun
part.

Hi!
First of all, I'd like to say "thank you" for being a newbie that has
an interesting question (nothing against the newbies, just have read
the subject lines a 100 times). You made me think. When I started
lboxing I had the same problem. In my case, I chose to just go by
"Mark", which of course is my name. My e-mail address said more about
me (ndnboxing). Say it fast...
But, I've come to learn that I love those crazy names out there. I'm
still glad I chose to use my real name, but I really love all the
psychomommy, SpringChick, Rustypuff, Autumn, Amanda from Seattle,
Funhog, leger de maine, catbead, etc monikers out there. I love 'em
all!

If you want to take the time to scrutinize it, or something in your
life denotes who you are (activities, personality, so on), use it to
make a name just to keep the scene interesting. If not, just be
"Lauren", and be the best you can be at it. Have fun!

Thanks for making me remember all this,
Mark
