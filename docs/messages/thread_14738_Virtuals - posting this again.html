Hey there! The subject of virtual boxes comes up from time to time.
I am hopeful that at some point Wes and his wonderful crew of
webmasters and mistresses can put this information up on the LbNA
site.

Meanwhile ... here is my list of Virtuals. Apologies if the links
get cut off. You may have to cut and paste the links since some of
them are long:

There's one more, but it's a real box that is/was also available as a
virtual on the old site. For some reason, that box is not listed on
the new lbna site, so I'm not going to post that one. I did email Wes
with this list, and that additional box. He can contact that person
to see if they still want their box (virtual or otherwise) to be
listed.

Here's the list:

http://www.ejletterboxing.bravepages.com/introlb.htm
EJ's Letterboxing Links. There are five here I think.

http://weatherwitch.net/candlelight2/
Candlelight's Letterboxes. There are nine here.

http://www.geocities.com/imacoldplaya/virtuallb1.htm
Fish 'n Chips Virtual. There is one here.

http://www.livejournal.com/talkread.bml?
journal=moderngypsy&itemid=137881
Modern Gypsy's Virtual. There is one here.

http://home1.gte.net/res0fpfm/letterboxes/emerald-city.htm
Mischief's Boxes. There is one here. (Emerald City)

http://www.geocities.com/codybcleo/Chippy.html
CodyBCleo's Chippy. There is one here.

http://www.geocities.com/codybcleo/Sunshine
CodyBCleo's Sunshine Series. There are four here.

http://www.confessionsofdecember.com/lbna/virtual/moomahh.asp
SpringChick's Moo-Mah Series. There are four here. These four were
tough, and were my favorite of the virutal series so far.

There's also a new series by Buddy&Piggy. There's only one so far,
and I don't have the url on hand for it. Sorry.

Cheers!
Autumn
