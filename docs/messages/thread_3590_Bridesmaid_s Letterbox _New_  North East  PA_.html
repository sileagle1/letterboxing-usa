"Bridesmaid's"
Bushkill Falls
Bushkill, Pike Co. PA.

Planted Oct. 28th 2001


Directions: Rt.80 to exit 52 to Rt.209 north, follow signs to left at
blinker light at Bushkill.

Home: Rt.209 "south" is a crawl all the way to Rt. 80 on weekends!! So, head
south on Rt.209 and at "Fernwood Resort's Golf Course" turn left onto "River
Road". Stay on River Rd. through Shawnee and to Rt.80

Type: Can be just about anything you want. Short and easy or longer and more
strenuous.1-3hrs.

Terrain: Walkways or not,it comes in flat,with roots and rocks, and with
stairs to ascend and decend. Who ever built the wood bridges and such did a
great job!! Very beautiful!!!

"Bridesmaid's" #10

"Happy for her best friends bliss
Her own tears fall because she missed,
Her turn at love not yet to be
This water flows for her and thee...."


Under the sign, on the tree, it marks this spot for you and me. Here the
bridge, the first cross beam, tucked under the boards above the stream.
CAREFUL!!!Lots of folks around, carefull in finding or "you'll" be found...

Be the first to stamp in Nov. 2001 or you will have to wait till April 1st
2002 for a chance.

This is a beautiful spot worth the price of admission, $8.- for adults, $2.-
for childern, April 1ST through October.>>>OPEN NOVEMBER PERMITTING>(like, no ice or snow) TILL THANKSGIVING!!!

Dogs on leashes were seen.

Do not go during a drought if you want to see waterfalls...
