Hi you people are alot funnier than my other Yahoo group-the New

is how I got hooked.

idea what white PV is , I use pencil erasers for stamps. I am addicted
now .
Thanks alot ,like I have nothing to do with my time 60 hour week job,
a home , a dog to find a forever home for , looking for a mate , doing
Yoga , going to Kripalu center , cleaning , paperwork and now
Letterboxing oh my.
