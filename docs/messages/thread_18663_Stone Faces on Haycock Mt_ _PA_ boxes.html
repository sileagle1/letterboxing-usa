Stone Faces on Haycock Mt. (PA) boxes

From: Anna Lisa Yoder (annalisa@fast.net) | Date: 2004-05-05 14:09:47 UTC-04:00
Anyone here have knowledge of the placers of the "Stone Faces on Haycock Mt." series here in Eastern PA? I'm unable to find contact info. for the placers on the website. The clues are in great need of revision, as trail marks have faded beyond recognition. Some pacing or other distance-related clues would also be of great assistance, especially since the trails are evidently little-traveled. None of us could figure out what was meant by the "tacky trail". We were unable to find any of the boxes, but will try again when we have more time.... We figured out just before giving up, that we were reading one of the clues wrong-- it can be interpreted a couple different ways but I don't think the authors intended to be confusing in this case. It was disappointing, and we were unable to let a HH loose, but what a great & little-known spot for a hike anyway, and one little lone toad agreed! --lunaryakketyact
