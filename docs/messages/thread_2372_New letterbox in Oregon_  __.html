***** Los Osos Oaks Letterbox Series Update *****
I noticed the letterbox series was posted to the website, but two
things:

1. There's four letterboxes in the series, not one. (It really is a
series!)

2. If "San Luis Obispo" is too long for the name of the county, I'd
suggest "San Luis" instead. That's what it's called locally. Or
even "SLO" will work. Nobody ever calls it S.L. Obispo. Actually,
nobody ever calls it San Luis Obispo, either, even if that is the
full, proper name of the county (and city). Well, out of towners do,
but locals always call it either "San Luis" or "SLO" (pronounced just
like SLOW).

That's about it for me. Enjoy!
