CT Gathering-May 11

From: defygravity2001 (defygravity@snet.net) | Date: 2002-03-04 03:47:31 UTC
We're beginning to gear up towards the May 11 Gathering in Hamden, CT.
I've made some map copies and started marking them up, but I'm afraid
I'll be updating them up to the last minute as we have an abundance of
new letterboxes in south-central CT! There are certainly enough boxes
right in Sleeping Giant to keep a body busy, but a lot of us have
already visited most of them, so sojourning off-site will be easier
with maps.

But anyway, I was figuring on an all-day potluck style picnic going
on, so that whenever anyone gets to stop in they can eat. I'll hang
about the picnic area all day to hold down the fort. There are also
grills available so if we just start up some charcoal it will be ready
if anyone wants to bring their own burgers/dogs to have a hot lunch
(and marshmallows for dessert).

I'll be making one event stamp, and everyone is, of course, invited to
make another or to place some boxes-for-the-day too. This is gonna be
a blast!

Cya there,
