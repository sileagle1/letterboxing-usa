We visited Dinosaur SP in Rocky Hill, CT this weekend, found the GREAT boxes
and even made a cast of one of the dino prints! I think we were the only
ones there without kids! The Exhibit Center is pretty fascinating, too.

Anyway, we picked up a Dinosaur SP Day flyer while we there, and I thought
I'd share the details: Saturday, August 18, 10am-3:30pm, rain or shine.
Music, kids activities, magicians, refreshments, grab bags, treasure hunt
(we know ALL ABOUT treasure hunts :), balloons, face-painting. Outdoor
activities are free, the Exhibit Hall has a small admission fee. (It was $2
for adults.) It sounds like a great time for the kids!

Take care,
Carrie & Nate
