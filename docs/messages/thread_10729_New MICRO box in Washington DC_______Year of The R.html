In honor of the Chinese Lunar new year I have placed a new LetterBox
in Washington DC.

DC Webmaster...please link the clues to the following page:

Chinese Lunar New Year 2003
Year of the Ram Letterbox

PLEASE use caution when retrieving/replacing this box;
as it is in a very public place.

When you visit Washington DC,
Chinatown is a place one must see!

On the corner of 7th and H you will see a large arch,
this is where your journey will start!

As you walk under the arch remember the year,
2003 symbols that the RAM is near!

Looking to your left you will see a sign not new,
written upon it is 80 P2 X2!

These are not letterboxing numbers to gaze upon,
yet they are numbers of navigation!

As you sit near the numbers it may seem as a tease,
look at the arch to your NorthEast!

Count 1, 2, 3 support beams from the arch,
and you will see where I am!

Purched above your head towards the heavens,
you will find your festive Ram!

Enjoy the hunt.........BOX THE GLOBE!

If you find the above mentioned box damaged,
destroyed, or missing PLEASE notify me. NewBe1
