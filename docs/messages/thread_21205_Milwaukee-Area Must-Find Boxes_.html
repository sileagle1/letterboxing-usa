It really depends on when you'll be comin' through. We just got
buried in 5-10" of fresh snow. The problem with the CC county and
Weapons of Math Instruction Series (thanks for the plug WI Hiker)
right now is that the park that they are in will now have the trails
off limits to hikers as they will be groomed for CC skiing only. The
rangers are very particular about keeping hikers off their groomed trails.

The only indoor box available right now that I can think of is
Creative Writing: The Never Ending Story which is right next to the WI
HH Hostel, and (humbly speaking) is I think near the top of the "don't
miss" boxes of WI. (The REI box is temporarily down.)

If you're coming in March, the PI Day series (available only in March)
is a quick series of 6 stamps that are "don't miss" boxes (again,
humbly speaking). Hopefully we'll be done with the snow then too....
