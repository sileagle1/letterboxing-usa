Before I get into this let me warn you, the wooded trail part of
this journey is VERY slippery when wet, leaves on top of rocks some
of which are loose. As you walk up the *trail before you get to the
top you may want to walk to your left about 15 yards before
continuing up. There is a very steep drop off. Also from time to
time there are paintballers here. Although not posted near the box
there are some areas that are restricted, please be careful. If you
feel the need to replace the stamp, I wont be offended, lol.

Adequate parking as there is a park before the road closes.
