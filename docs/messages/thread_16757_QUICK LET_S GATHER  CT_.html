QUICK LET'S GATHER CT.

From: mothermoo2001 (cstearns07@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-11-08 12:27:25 UTC
Well this was not meant to be so last minute but things have been
crazy here. Cheryl of Teammaddawg - Cinncinatti Ohio is here in Ct.
for a boxing weekend Lets all gather to give her a frenzy of
exchanges. I have tried three different buildings for a after dark
gather to no avail and it is a bit "brisk" out there but nothing like
the cold of last winters gathers so I propose we meet up Sunday
between 4-6 at Eagleville Dam Rte 275 in Mansfield. Eagleville
preserve is there and there are a few boxes . I will see that Sit and
Relax is replaced for this event as I have been meaning to. I will
bring an event stamp and have a HH for you to stamp before it moves
on. There will be a couple of special boxes and we can all have
fun.
To get to Eagleville Dam take rte 32 to the junction of rte 275.
Turn there near the stone church toward Coventry. Go slow around the
sharp bend ansd under the train tracks parking on the right next to
the river and Preserve on the opposite side of the road. We will
have only and hour pof light but we can enjoy exchanges even as the
light fades. If you have a camp lantern bring it along! Hope to see
a few of you there. Carol - Leader of the Pack
