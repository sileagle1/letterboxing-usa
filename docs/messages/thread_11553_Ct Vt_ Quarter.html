Hi needeeps and all, When we visited the Vt. Quarter yesterday we
noted that the degrees on the compass did not line up. I figured the
degree needed was 20 degrees. Pink trotters checked it and came up
with the same. Anyway since there is ony one wall up there it is not
to hard to figure out .
When we found it WOW! the carving was great!!! We just loved it
and can't wait to pick up a few more quarters.
You can go back now as I had carved a stamp for that area over the
winter and planted Rock Climber yesterday. I'll post it next. The
day was perfect not only did Pink Trotters get her HH but when we
came out of the trail we were able to exchange with Cootie and Kyle
who were there in the car with the Plane Spotter box. Avery good day
for leader of the Pack, Pink Trotters and Cowboy and Cody.
