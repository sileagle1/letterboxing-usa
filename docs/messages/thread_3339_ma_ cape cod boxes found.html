From: (Christopher_Martin@harvardpilgrim.org) | Date: 2001-09-17 11:53:52 UTC-04:00
I spent this past weekend on the Cape and took the opportunity to find
several letterboxes. The following are all alive and well:

The Reunion
Chatham Light
Highland Light
Nauset Light

I looked for Three Women Singing (Light, Love, & Joy), but it appears to
have gone missing. I'm 95% certain I found the right spot, but the area
has been recently cleared of underbrush, and the tree that I identified as
apple is dead or dying, with a small drift of termite-created sawdust at
the base. One branch of the trunk has snapped off and I don't think the
rest of the tree will stand for much longer. No box was in evidence within
a 15-foot radius of the tree.

Thank you to Leader of the Pack, Lonemasswolf, and You Inspire My Spirit
for helping to enliven my Cape Cod weekend! I look forward to meeting you
all in October!
