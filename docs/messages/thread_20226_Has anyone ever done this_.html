From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2004-09-07 22:16:02 UTC
Small trinkets, etc have been left as a first finder reward. We found
a WOM box first and there was a little certificate from the placer;
it had the box name, and the image on it. It was very nice, and is
mounted in our logbook (we're on # 3 now).

We tend to stray away from including trinkets...personally we feel
thats more along the style of another hobby. As far as first finding
itself, we've only be the first finders twice, once WOM and once for
Steve's B-Day box.

Good discussion topic though!
-Steve, Heidi, and Madras
