Chatham Rooster
Placed: April 12, 2002
By: Deanne & David Besnia
Harding's Beach, Chatham, MA (Cape Cod)
 
Take Route 28 East to Chatham.  Look for "Shop Ahoy" shopping center on the right. Immediately after Shop Ahoy, at blinking light, take a right onto Barn Hill Road (there is a sign indicating it is the turn for Harding Beach). Follow Barn Hill Road until you see the sign for Harding Beach on the right. Follow the road to the first parking lot and drive to the left to the second parking lot.  Look for a sign indicating the walking trail.  We walked this 2 mile loop through a salt marsh, dunes and salt-water inlets looking for a spot to plant this box which honors our friend, Chris Soares, with whom we spent many enjoyable hours in Chatham.  Finding no suitable hiding spot we landed back in the parking lot. Look for a sign that reads "No Dogs Allowed on Beach 4/11 to 9/15--$25.00 Fine" to it's left you will see a log blocking vehicle entrance to a parth.  Walk up to the log and look on the far right end of it for a loose board forming the coop for the Chatham Rooster--long may he crow!
We'd love to hear from you if you find this deebes@net1plus.com
