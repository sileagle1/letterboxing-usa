What can/should please be fixed about "the site":

Northern Illinois clue designation.... For some reason, DuPage
county clues pretty much always get shuffled into the "Illinois With
No Region Assigned" category, instead of "Northern Illinois" Clues.

Used to drive me batty to see my clues land into that weird "no
region" never-never land (who would want to look for clues there?),
so I moved out of DuPage county because I could no longer face life
without a region.

But all silliness aside, I think it would help folks make up their
mind about visiting northern IL for 'boxing if the page was an
accurate reflection of how many boxes really are in that area.
