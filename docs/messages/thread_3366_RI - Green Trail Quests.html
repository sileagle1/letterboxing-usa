RI - Green Trail Quests

From: Trish Kurdziel (trishkri@yahoo.com) | Date: 2001-09-25 09:11:36 UTC-07:00

I just had some qualified good news.  I just spoke with Dave Monks of the Friends of the National Wildlife Refuge and Salt Pond Coalition.  He is the person currently responsible for the Green Trail Quests in Charlestown, RI.  He was surprised to hear that so many people were interested in letterboxing in the area and in finding the Green Trail Quest boxes.  He currently has the remaining boxes at his house for repair but was not sure when he would have a chance to replace them.  He also mentioned that he has three more to place.

I invited him to the upcoming Gathering at Burlingame.  He mentioned that he would try to come and would also invite the representative from the National Park Service in RI that he worked with in placing the original Green Trail Quest boxes.

I found Dave through a link on the Friends of the National Wildlife Refuge website that still has the clues for the Quest boxes.  When the boxes are replaced it should be a good link for those seeking them.

http://www.friendsnwr-ri.org/salt_ponds_coalition.htm

Trish Kurdziel

We were also checking on the status of the Green Trail Quest boxes this
