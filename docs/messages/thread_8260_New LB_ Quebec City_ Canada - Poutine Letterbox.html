Poutine Letterbox

Where: Quebec City, QC, Canada
By: Ellen Mountain Maple and Nitsa
When: October 5, 2002

English

On the plains of Abraham you'll find a nature trail that runs along the cliff
above the St. Lawrence. On the west end of the nature trail, go 26 steps down
or 27 steps up to find the seven trunked tree. Look inside, under the rocks,
for your prize! You might have to dig through leaves or snow!!!

Franais

Sur les paines d'Abraham, on trouve un sentier de nature, qui lange la falaise
au-dessus du fleuve Saint-Laurent. l'extrmit la plus l'ouest mantez 27
marches ou descendez-en 26 por trouver l'arbre au sept troncs. Regardez
l'intrieur sous les roches. Vous aurer pettre creuser dans les feuille ou
la neige!

What's Poutine?

Poutine is a typical French Canadian food consisting of french fries, gravy and
cheese curds. We recommend finding the closet AshTon to cap off a day of
sightseeing and letterboxing in the beautiful city of Quebec!
