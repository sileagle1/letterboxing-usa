Eyes Out Monster Letterbox

Placed by J&A-bear (jedaryn02@yahoo.com) on 23 March
2003
Garden City, ID
Ada County

Directions: Take Exit 46, Eagle Road, north. Turn
right onto Chinden Blvd. Turn left onto Glenwood.
Turn right onto Marigold St. and park in the dirt
parking lot next to the river.

Description: Another letterbox strolling along the
Greenbelt/Boise River. It took us about 30 minutes
round trip.

Clues: Take the bike path west along the Boise river.
You will pass the River Pointe Park on your left and
keep next to the river. Cross a bridge and hang a
right. Once the path turns into a dirt/rock path
start counting 33 paces/66 steps until you come to a
cluster of about 8-10 trees varying in size on your
right. 4 more paces and descend to a the 2nd sitting
rock. Reach behind you under 2 small boulders for the
Eyes Out Monster letterbox.
