Hi John!

It's nice of you to stop by our e-group! Letterboxing has taken off
here, as well, especially on the East Coast of the USA. The rest of us
are doing our best to catch up with the pros in Dartmoor and here in the
New England region of the United States of America. I live on the West
Coast in a marvelous place called Portland, Oregon. Keep tuned in, and
feel free to come to America. We're not quite as sophisticated as
Dartmoor, perhaps, but I'll bet we have just as much fun hunting for and
hiding letterboxes!
