Lucky's Lost

Can you find Lucky? Lucky is a cute little pooch who wandered off in
search of a bone near an old Jackson county "hot spot." Now he's
lost (or is he hiding?) and is waiting for someone to come and find
him.

Start your search for Lucky at the Buckeye Furnace State Memorial.
To get there, take State Route #32 to the intersection near Wellston
where State Route #124 separates from State Route #32. Take State
Route #124 east about 4 miles and look for the brown and white sign
pointing to the Buckeye Furnace State Memorial 3 miles down Buckeye
Furnace Road. Follow the signs to the memorial. (If you look
carefully, you may see a beaver or two in the wetland along the road
to the furnace!) Drive past the furnace and park at the picnic area
across the road from the company office/store. Go to the barbecue
grill. Take a bearing of 212 and walk 6 paces (1 pace = 2 steps).
You should find yourself by a large tree with four trunks. You
should find Lucky hiding in a little hollow under the tree under some
leaves.
