FLORIDA'S 50TH
Welcome! I've designed this letterbox to be a little bit different from the
ordinary. It is meant to commemorate the milestone of the 50th letterbox in
the State. To find it you will need two very important pieces of information.

1. A compass heading
2. A distance

Here, I'll provide you with clues to the location of the compass heading.
There you'll find clues to a second location, where I've placed the distance.
For this adventure you'll of course need a compass as well as a 100 foot
length of twine, string, rope or line. Be sure to also bring bug spray.
Good luck! Here are your clues:
