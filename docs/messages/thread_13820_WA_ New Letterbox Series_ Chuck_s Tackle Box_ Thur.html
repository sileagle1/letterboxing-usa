WA: New Letterbox Series, Chuck's Tackle Box, Thurston/Pierce Counties

From: Nail Family (c.nail@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-06-25 20:04:16 UTC
Finally got the chance to start placing a series I have had in boxes
since last November. It is called Chuck's Tacklebox Series and
combines my love for both freshwater fishing and letterboxing.

I have 12 stamps/boxes in all ready to go and just placed the first
2. Each set in the series will be 2 boxes placed at one of my
favorite fishing lakes in Thurston and Pierce Counties. There will
be one box hidden at the public launch area for those without access
to a boat and one or more boxes placed somewhere on the lake itself
(usually hidden along an undeveloped shoreline). I hope to get at
least 3 more sets placed by the end of this weekend so watch for them
soon.

The first set is located at Lake St. Clair near Lacey in Thurston
County.

Have fun and let me know how you like the boating adventure!
