I have been living in this adopted state for long enough to know of
the Ohio-Michigan rivalries.

The football teams and other sports teams of each state battle out
this rivalry every season of the year, but no more effectively than
during the fall.

In good natured fun, let me point out that the number of Ohio
letterboxes matched the number of Michigan boxes with the posting of
my latest box this afternoon. It is now 35 all.

I have privately appreciated my established goal of exceeding
Michigan over the last few weeks. Now if any intrepid Michigan
boxers would like to engage in the contest, I believe we have the
ball with a first down and two time outs remaining.

If you take up the challenge, hurrah! There will be more Michigan
boxes around, and I can take a trip there in the spring to stamp them
in. Win-win!

Flyfisher
