A Boxious Weekend in RI with Amanda from Seattle (et al)

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-05-11 21:44:56 UTC-04:00
This weekend, I was totally rewarded with meeting Amanda from Seattle! She
flew into Providence for an overnight, so a bunch of us met her at her hotel,
then took her out boxing. Those who were there included Team Green Dragon,
Moonstone Baby, Rustypuff, CSCM, and Laurette. After exchanging, we found
The Case of the Missing Mascot, both boxes in Central Falls, Dexter in North
Providence, and Going Downhill. We also came across the First Snow HH while
out at Dexter. We had a great time and hope Amanda will come and visit again
soon! Hopefully she will be able to get a layover at Bradley so we can show
her around CT!

On Sunday, Moonstone Baby, Indy and I ventured out and found the Wooden
Carousel series, and Westerly Woods. Jay: Westerly Woods box is cracked. We
put it in a large baggie to hopefully keep the water out. We then went back
to our respective houses to recoup! THen, we went out and found #3, #4 and
#1 of the Lil' Rhody Runaround series and Falling Leaves. We tried for the
Great Gathering, however I believe it to be missing. We did find the
Sanctuary HH from Chicago at Falling Leaves, and the Up, Up and Away HH at
Box #3 of Rhody.

All in all, a great weekend! Thanks to everyone in RI for a great time!

Music Woman
from CHarlestown, RI and Manchester, CT
