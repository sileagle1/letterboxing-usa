New letterbox in Washington County Rhode Island.

BILLIONS OF BLISTERING BLUE BARNACLES
Jerusalem, RI

Placed by: Laurette
      September 15, 2002
Rating:  Easy

Driving directions:  From Rt. 1 in South Kingstown, RI, take the East Matunuck Beach Exit.  Follow Succotash Road past the beach to its end at the DEM Division of Fish and Wildlife Marine Fisheries Building.  Park your car on the road and head out on the dirt road to the left of the building.  This is the Succotash Marsh Wildlife Management Area.  About half way out the peninsula there is a turnout and a sign identifying the area and its regulations.  Take 56 steps along the road in a northerly direction.  (The osprey pole is @27).  On the left of the road is a silverberry bush growing up over a patch of bayberry.  On the right is a juniper.  Turn right off the road to the south side of the juniper.  Take 10 steps around the juniper.  Look low on your right under an arborvite.
