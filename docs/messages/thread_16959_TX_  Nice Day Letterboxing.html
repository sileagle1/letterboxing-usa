TX: Nice Day Letterboxing

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2003-11-23 18:29:38 UTC
We decided to enjoy the Spring-like weather yesterday and
went to Varner-Hogg State Park to gather pecans before they
were all gone. We managed to get two grocery bags full of
nice nuts and found Baby Bear's Hogg Pecans letterbox, too.
I found the "Mighty Oaks From Tiny Acorns Grow" Hitchhiker
appropriately hidden in the Pecan box and will release it
soon. We then ate a picnic lunch and headed for the Sea Center
in Lake Jackson. After viewing the many free aquariums
I placed my "Gordon Grouper" microbox and we headed home.
If you live in the Houston/Galveston area, I highly recommend
that you visit these places soon. Not only were they fun, but
they were FREE. As extra incentive, I left a surprise in one
of the boxes for the first person to find, so get out there
and enjoy!

Silver Eagle
