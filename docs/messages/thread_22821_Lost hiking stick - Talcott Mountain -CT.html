Hi!

I lost my hiking stick on October 27 while hiking the Metacomet blue blazed trail across from the Penwood Park parking lot. I'm fairly certain I had it when I got up to the powerlines. I put it down somewhere along the grasses where the powerline trail goes to the left. It is stained brown, has a black rubber tip on the bottom, is a twisted design, and has a rawhide loop near the top of the stick.

I've gone back twice to look for it and a fellow letterboxer also scoured the area and didn't find it. If you see it, please let me know and I will pick it up.

Thanks and Enjoy!
