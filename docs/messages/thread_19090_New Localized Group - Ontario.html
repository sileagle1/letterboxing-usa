From: Terre (b1kegrrl@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-06-08 00:27:04 UTC
Queen B

There are no letterboxes listed in Niagara just yet. About 1.5 hrs
west of Niagara is Guelph and there are a few there. Here is a listing
of current Ontario letterboxes:

http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxByRegion.asp?region=Ontario,%20CAN

Wawa is way the other side of the province and Ottawa is north, 6
hours from Guelph. Elora is 10 minutes from Guelph, and is also beautiful.

I hope this helps

Cheers,

Terre
