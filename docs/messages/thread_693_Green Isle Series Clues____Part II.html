Directions: Walk back to your car either taking the
path backwards or walking out of the park and turning
Right. Follow E. River Drive to Allouez Ave. Turn
Right and there is your car. Drive your car back to
the park. Upon entering the park, drive to the lot
near the Tennis Courts. It is the last lot on the
Left before the road goes into trees.
Continue walking on the paved path...The next four are
on this latter part of the walk.

Clues:
Box E: Once under the First of two overpasses for 172
E/W, on the right side you will see some rocks in the
tall grass. There is a grouping of three that hold
your prise for getting this far. The one hidding your
box looks like it was placed there. :)
Box D: Continue under 172 following the river more
closely now. On your right before the diamonds and
playing fields, there is a lone pine tree. Under some
rocks before the tree the next box is placed. You
will have to get on your hands and knees for this one.
Sorry.
Box C: Again continue on the path. After passing
some homes, you will come to a wooden area near the
bank of the river. There U will find a few trees that
learned how to write. They form a U. Under this
letter the box is kept. (U may not see the letter
until after U pass it, but it is there.)
Box H: Coming around a curve in the paved path, you
will see another overpass in the distance. This is
Humbolt Road. The last box is placed there. A Big
boulder on the Right is housing your box. There is a
smallish rock protecting it.

Congradulations! You have done it. The whole walking
time should only take you two hours. On the walk back
to your car from the first four I'm thinking of
placing a bonus box. You pass a small park during
this part of the trip. I'll let you know when I place
it.
Love you all. Die xo
