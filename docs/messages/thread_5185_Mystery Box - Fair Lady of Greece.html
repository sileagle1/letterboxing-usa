Letterbox Name: Fair Lady of Greece

Placed: 29 April 2002 by the Publican

Location: Virginia, South West

Fair Lady of Greece
Drink, fair lady of Greece, for pleasant specters guard thy gate, while from
thy bosom flows austerity's dew. Its meager fare, guided south by west,
wanders not a mile and passes silently under oaken eyes watching atop a
single unassuming western rise but a stone's throw hence. Upon its very
apex, twin beams joined at birth stand erect, one bearing the gaze which
does fix the course and whose backward glance the measure from which an
ageless treasure can be rediscovered.
The Publican
