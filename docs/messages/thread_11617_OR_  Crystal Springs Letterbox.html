Hey All!

John and I impulsively took a late afternoon drive over to SE Portland
and found the Crystal Springs Letterbox.

Crystal Springs Gardens is such a beautiful and amazing place. I had
never been there before. So many beautiful plants and trees. It smells
so good there. A huge abundance of waterfowl, as well. A romantic
place to be at dusk, as well.

The letterbox is doing fine. Seemed a bit soggy and some of the
stamp-ins were a bit faded. We enclosed it in an additional ziplock and
hope that helps. Looks like lots of non-letterboxers have found it,
too. We very much enjoyed finding this letterbox. Thanks to Funhog for
placing!
