Hi Carol-

That road isn't plowed, so it only opens after spring melt-off. We can
be dry in town but still have large drifts further up that keep it
closed. Usually March is a bit early for the melt above Durango, but
this year has been very dry so far.

That's a long weay of saying, "I don't know".

All 5 of those boxes were alive and well as of November.
