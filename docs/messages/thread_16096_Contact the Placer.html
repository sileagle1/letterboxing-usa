I always contact the placer when I visit a letterbox. I wondered why
I hadn't heard back; now I see that my messages may have been lost in
cyberspace. Newbie error.

I'd like to post the boxes I've visited that are in need of more or
less maintenance here on the open list. (I'll spare you my cheery
greetings to those whose boxes are in good shape). All of these boxes
are in southern Wisconsin or Northern Illinois, visited in the last
two weeks.

The Dragon - Estabrook #2 - missing

Moofie & Blaze, adoptive parents for Bob Birkie's Boyhood Adventures
#2 - Only four pages left in logbook, box was wet inside but contents
not damaged recently; put everything in a new Ziploc for insurance

Matt the Rat - Dead River - minor problem, stamp separated from block

Baby Bird's Dad - The Flower - Water damage to logbook; replaced
Ziploc but pages still very slightly moist

I'd like to thank the list members for all of their postings on
re-hiding LBs and LB etiquette. You've inspired me to carry paper
towels for drying damp boxes and new Ziplocs to replace nibbled
baggies. Next time out, I'll carry spare logbooks to add to those
that are running out.

Leslie "Acorn"
