Quabbin Mystery

Date placed: 10/18/03
Terrain: Easy to moderate
Clues: Easy to moderate
Time: 4 to 5 hours round trip
Number of boxes: 2

I listed these as mystery boxes, but they are not a mystery. The reason that
I posted these as mystery boxes is because they are places that I do not
the general public to find. (I DO NOT WANT THESE CLUES POSTED ANYWHERE ON
THE WEB). To get the clues, you must e-mail me and I will e-mail them to
you.
These are 2 of the most interesting places that I have found in the Quabbin
Reservation.
This hike will take 4-5 hours and does involve paying close attention to the
clues. There is one section that you will need to do some bushwhacking.
You could bicycle part of the way, which would take 2-3 hours. You would
have to leave your bikes in the woods on the side of the trail.
There is also one other box on the way to these boxes, so you could get 3
boxes on this hike.
Sorry, dogs are not allowed at the Quabbin.
To get the clues, email me at dbastarache@hotmail.com and put Mystery Boxes
in the subject line.
