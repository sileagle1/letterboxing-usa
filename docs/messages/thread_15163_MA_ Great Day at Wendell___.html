MA: Great Day at Wendell!!!

From: monotropa (bsennott@crocker.com) | Date: 2003-08-17 16:40:31 UTC
I would like to thank Deanne and Ginger for organizing the gathering
at Wendell State Forest yesterday, celebrating five years of
letterboxing in Massachusetts. It was so much fun meeting other
letterboxers, exchanging stamps and stories, and finding the special
boxes Deanne placed for the day. And, the food was delicious.
Obviously, letterboxers are great cooks!

I had no idea back when I hid those first two boxes at Wendell in
August 1998 that only a few years later there would be so many people
enjoying the hobby and finding hundreds, even thousands, of
letterboxes.

A lot of the credit goes to everyone who has helped create and
maintain the LBNA Web site. And a big thanks to everyone who hung in
there and kept hiding boxes those first few years!

I feel psyched about getting out a doing more letterboxing soon. This
morning when I bought a bag of cat litter, somehow two Rubbermaid
containers also made their way into my cart. How did that happen????

Bonnie
