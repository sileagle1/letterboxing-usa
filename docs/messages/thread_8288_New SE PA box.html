"Exeter Scenic Trail Rocks"

Exeter Scenic River Trail

Gibralter Road, Exeter Twp, Berks County, PA

Gibralter, PA

Planted 10-09-02 by DiverDM

Clues: Easy

Hike: Easy 2-1/2 miles

Enter and follow the path, after a while you will cross a footbridge, at the end of the bridge is a choice, the right path is the wrong choice, unless you feel like playing baseball.  Further down the trail is the nicest part of the hike, the Schuylkill river is almost the same elevation as the trail.  Soon you will pass under a large overpass, 70 paces past the overpass, on the right is a large boulder that is split in two, perhaps by the last ice age, it sits only a few feet from the trail.  50 yards Directly behind the split rock is a large pointed rock outcropping with a cavity.  Inside is a box.
