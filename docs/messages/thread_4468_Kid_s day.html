A mini Letterboxing Gather for kids and those who think as one, is being
planed to gather this Tuesday [school vacation for some] Feb. 19 at
Rocky Neck St Park . Meet in the parking lot out at the beach next to
the bath house [closed ] Hope for a sunny day. We will also travel on
to Old Lyme and 'do ' the lovely little park there.[Whimsey Walk and
Village] Two very different spots here on the shore and so far a total
of 6 boxes for those to enjoy finding. Picnic of course in either park
at the wishes of those who come. The plan it to meet at Rocky Neck at
10:30.
Butterfly wishes to all .
Butterfly
