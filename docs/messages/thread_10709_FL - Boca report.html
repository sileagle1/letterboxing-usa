Greetings fellow LBXers-
yesterday 1/2 of the TerrapinTraveliers was out and about checking on our
Boca Boxes
- here's our report:

Florida Friends: Intact and well hidden

Tiki Idol: Intact and well hidden

Loggerhead: Intact and VERY well hidden- some folks have had trouble
locating this
box, it is quite well hidden under some debris, you may have to dig a little
for it,
don't give up! - last entry was from Dec. 2002 (Wanda and Pete!)

I also had a chance to check out the recently placed "Snowflakes are
falling" letterbox
in Deerfield Beach- thanks DandShoo- haven't had snow in South Florida since
'77!
Great box and I picked up a HH during my visit. I'll bring "BannyrCai" on my
next journey
- hopefully that will be the final move up north to reunite with the other
half of the Team.

Unfortunately, I ran out of daylight and didn't get over to check out C.L.
Miller's Morikami box
to verify it's status- we did stamp in back in December, but it's possible
that it's been removed
since then.

Also picked up Disney series "Caribbean Beach" and "Celebration" last
weekend while
visiting Mickey and friends- great fun, thanks!

Keep on boxin'
cheers!
- Mike
