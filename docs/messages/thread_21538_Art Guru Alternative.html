OK everyone... All of you folks who got skunked
on the Art Guru event by Ma Nature,
hop on the next plane to Oregon. We've been
suffering from unseasonably warm and

next summer, having 70 degree days and

couple of barrels of snow as luggage to melt
for water. There may not be enough on hand
here to drink.

the land of the microbrew. First round is on the
Hog!

Keep warm, Funhog
