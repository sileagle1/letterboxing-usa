Just a reminder that a great way to help spread the word about letterboxing
in your area is to volunteer to be a State Contact.

At least one person has volunteered to accept inquiries for each of the 50
states. Some states have several contact persons. If you would be willing to
add your name to to the list of contacts for your state, please contact me at:
letterbox-states@egroups.com

Becoming a contact person is not a big responsibility. It simply gives you
the opportunity to be one of the first few people to welcome newcomers to the
hobby, and to help point them in the right direction in their search for
further information.

Also, feel free to volunteer for more than one state if you have interest in
several areas.

Thanks,
Der Mad Stamper
