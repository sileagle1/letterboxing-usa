An alarmed citizen witnessed "suspicious" activity around this box
and removed it to the Rye , N.H. police dept. upon investigation the
box was opened and they saw the Cock-A-Doodle-Moo 4-H note. Being a
former 4-H sheep club member the police kindly went in the web site
checked us out and sent notice that they were holding the box for my
retrieval. My cousin will pick it up today. I will rehide it on my
next trip north and post when it is back in circulation. A side bar:
having never heaerd of letterboxing they feel it the perfect activity
for an 8 year old grandson another convert through a most incredible
twist of events. Leader of the Pack - Carol
