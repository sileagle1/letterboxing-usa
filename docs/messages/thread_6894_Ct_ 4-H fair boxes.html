Ahhh! The fair is over we are so tired and happy. Thanks to all who
came out and for those that couldn't this year the boxes remain on
the grounds and soon I will post new clues and info to the series
there and you will find then a mailbox on the grounds with a guest
book and clues to one of the boxes and info sheets to teach others
how to enjoy letterboxing. This will be a great starting point for
newbies as we get many school groups and such on the nature trail
which is being linked to the Snipsit trail soon.

Also from the fair a new hitchhiker called To big for a Cage left the
grounds and should be seen again pecking about the countryside.
Thanks to all. Leader of the Pack
