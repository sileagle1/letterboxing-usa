Eoghan wrote:

<< On a related note, does anyone know of a way to search the e-list
postings short of perusing them one month at a time? It seems
that a keyword search capability would be really handy. >>

Go to the JOIN page of the LbNA website, and click the words TALK LIST
ARCHIVE in the tan box. On the page that follows, find the table called
MESSAGE ARCHIVE BY MONTH and click on any of the numbers in the table. This
will take you into the archives. Once you are in the archives, the search
function in the upper right corner will allow you to search the entire
talklist archive by keyword.
