ATTENTION - Project White Sands! - ATTENTION

From: gingerbreadjunk (gingerbreadjunk@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-11 18:41:43 UTC
This past Sunday, while checking up on the condition of my White
Sands letterbox, it came to my attention that the area has become so
littered with trash. When I placed this box, the area was quite
nice, but not as nice as I wish for it to be. Since I have found the
area in such a condition, I have decided to call out to the
letterboxing community. I am planning a get together/gathering, May
7th called Project White Sands. I am asking that anyone who is
interested, to come down and help myself clean up such a beautiful
area. I will bring pizza from my work, which I'm sure my boss will
greatfully donate, as well as hefty trash bags. If anyone is
interested in joining in my efforts to keep New Hampshire clean can
email me. If enough people offer thier services, the location of
White Sands with no longer be a mystery.

I plan on carving a special stamp for Project White Sands, for those
who come and join my efforts. There are also many boxes within 20
miles of White Sands.

All the help and will be greatfully appreciated.

Keepin New Hampshire Clean
David (team new hampshire)
