Thanks for the positive comments on this project! I hope that it will
enable people cruising the web to easily find letterboxers in their
local area.

Let me say,.. you haven't seen anything yet. Ever gone to a general
internet search engine (such as altavista or google) to try to find
letterboxing related info and sites? If so, you realize that most
listings have nothing to do with our hobby and are related to a film
industry technique with the same name.

Wouldn't it be cool if there was a Letterboxing internet search
engine that _only_ included sites that were related to the hobby of
finding letterboxes? Then you could search on whatever interested you
and be confident you wouldn't have to sift through thousands of
listings unrelated to our hobby.

Wouldn't that be cool! :-)

Again, anyone interested in being involved in the site development
list is welcome to subscribe by sending a message to:

lbcommunity-on@letterboxing.com

Ideas wanted, no technical knowledge is necessary to participate.
