A PERSONAL comment about stamps:

It is my personal opinion, that as long as you have created your stamp to the best of your: ability, capabilities and satisfaction, it does not matter what it looks like. I am in no way shape or form an artist... I'm arts and crafty, but most of my drawings are piteous at best. We cannot compare our stamps to anyone else's as we are all so different in our abilities, whether they be physical, mental or financial.

I'm now sorry we posted the images, as I'd hate to think the ones posted so far have deterred anyone from sending what they were previously satisfied with.

Warrior Woman, I love yours... it is simple, elegant a straight to the point. When a person sees it, unless the live on a small island off the coast of Pago Pago, they should recognize it.

Kerri, I'm sure your stamp is great, please don't change it on account of something you've seen posted.
