We went out on Sunday to Willard Brook State Forest in Ashby, Ma. and found
Sister Solstice and The Solstice Dance letterboxes. Solstice Dance was our
100th find!!!!!! These 2 boxes haven't seen many visitors. It is a really
nice park, and the boxes are great. I highly recommend these boxes. They are
both easy short hikes. (We also left a suprise for the next visitor).
