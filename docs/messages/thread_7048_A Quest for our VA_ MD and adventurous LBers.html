Ok, here is your assignment if you choose to accept it. Be one of
the few, the brave, the adventurous!

Visit the Prince of Power letterbox placed by Two Gray Squirrels in
Pocomoke State Forest--it's not a difficult find at all. You will
find that only three have stamped in before you. You will also find
a surprise inside, a surprise that is aching to be helped upon it's
way. (is that a big enough hint?)

While in the area travel over to Assateague Island to see if you can
find Barefoot placed by Jolly G-man--*no one* has ever found that box-
-wouldn't it be cool to be the first?!? Also check out the Dunes box
placed by The Usual Suspects...how many have that stamp in their
collection??

So- end your summer with a bang and with 3 (or more) stamps very few
have inside their books. The ponies are especially nice, too!

Any takers??
