Hi again all!

Clan Jiggs made another foray into Tilden Park in Berkeley (spelled
it right this time!!) and can report on the following...

Haunted Carousel - in fine shape and well-visited - book just about
full though.

Pony Ride - also in good shape!

Wildcat Summit #2 (Peace Grove) - nice hike! Box was fine, if a
little soggy (book was new - replaced?)

Wildcat Summit #3 (LUD) - trail was soggy but passable (and miserably
muddy to reclimb!!) box was a little damp, but readable. First
vistors since July!!)

Wildcat Summit #1 - will remain a mystery as time (and daylight) were
in very short supply.
