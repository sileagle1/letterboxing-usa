Has anyone found Ringing Rock box #2 anytime soon? This was the very first
box I found (Feb 2002); however, at the time I didn't have a stamp or log
book. Since I was back in the area (found the 3 Haycock boxes), I decided
to stop by and stamp the Ringing Rock boxes. Ringing Rock #1 was still in
the same place it was in Feburary, however, we were unable to locate box #2.
Since we had found the box before, we were sure we were looking in the
correct place; however it wasn't there. Acorrding to box #1, the last group
of letterboxers came through on 30-Mar-2002. Has anyone seen box #2 during
the past two month?

Just a note on Haycock box #2. It is now sharing its home with a Turkey
Vulture. Before you go in after it, you might want to make sure the Turkey
Vulture isn't home.
