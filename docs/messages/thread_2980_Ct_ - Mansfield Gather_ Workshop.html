Ct. - Mansfield Gather/ Workshop

From: (cstearns@neca.com) | Date: 2001-08-01 04:43:25 UTC
Well more old than new letterboxers showed up at tonights workshop
but the ink and journals were flying as everyone tried to be sure and
get all the exchanges.Cowboy and leader of the Pack now have 64!
There were about 25 folks who stopped in. The group included Melissa
and Dan, Wanda And Pete , Mama Bear and Mama Duck, quite a few
younguns' and lots of new friends. Most gathered the event box called
Sunset Trails. The image will now be altered so a variation will
remain in that location and will be posted soon. The Willimantic
Chronicle Newspaper sent a photographer it will be interesting to see
what we get for coverage.
Thanks to all who came out for a fun evening. Many stopped by the
mansfield Centre General Store box after dark and had a snack for the
road too. Thanks to all hope to see you atthe County 4-H fair.
Nice to meet Bill and Val from letterboxing Central hope you
enjoyed the evening. Leader of the pack- Carol
