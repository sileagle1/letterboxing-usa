Dear Letterboxers,

Back from a visit to Minneapolis, Minnesota over the holidays and placed a new letterbox there. I'm
not sure if the two letterboxes on Pike Island are still there (some earlier posts seemed to indicate
they were washed away by spring flooding) but my letterbox is located not too far from there. Here
are the clues:

Rock of Racial Harmony Letterbox
Minneapolis, Hennepin County, Minnesota

The Rock of Racial Harmony is an immense boulder on the edge of a meadow atop a cliff overlooking
the Mississippi River. To find the Rock, start from the trailhead at 54th Street. Follow the bike path
southward toward Fort Snelling, but shortly you will come to a gravel road leading down toward the
river. Follow the road, taking the left branch past the electrical tower and around the meadow. The
Rock is located on the far side of the meadow.
