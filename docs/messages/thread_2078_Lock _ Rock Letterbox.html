Hi all!!Went out this morning and planted a letterbox that I've had ready to
go for sometime now but just haven't had the time to hide. Hope everyone is
having a fantastic 'boxing weekend!! Below I submit my clues for your
enjoyment..



Delaware Canal State Park and
Nockamixion Cliffs Natural Area
Bucks County,PA
Nearest town Kintnersville,PA

Directions:Start point at the junction of RT. 412 and RT. 611

Planted March 17th 2001
By See Joy

LOCK>ROCK

At the lock called 21
Head downriver and you've begun
The path is flat with lots to see
Run, walk, ride or even ski!
Under the bridge
To the farm road above
Make a stop at the stand
For veggies you'll love...
Then meander along
Past the Federal style home
Keep your eyes on the woods
your not alone!!
A bridge over water
That carries the same
And on towards the smoke stack
That has no name
These "rocks" are high
It's an ancient place
Look at them softly
They may show their face..
Stop at the lock called 20
Take the time
And you can learn
Plenty!
Falling water
Heard in spring
Once ice they climbed
With ropes and things
Past the white fleshed
Double tree
Then to a single with mail slot
You'll see!!
CAREFUL NOW
Passing eyes...
In the slot on right
You'll find the prize!!
