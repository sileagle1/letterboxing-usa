Hi,
Just read your message and may be too late! There aren't any boxes
right in Boston. You could take a bus from Harvard Sq. to Watertown
Sq. and find the Heron on the Charles in Watertown. There is a bus
that leaves Copley (in front of the library) that also goes to
Watertown Square. I'm sorry to say I have blanked on the Bus numbers
and will have to reply again. You could also take a bus to Belmont
Center and walk to the Habitat for the letterbox there or a bus to
Waverly Sq. in Belmont and walk to Beaverbrook to find the box there.
If it's cold it could be a not-so-fun walk. I'm not sure if there are
busses to Blue Hills in Milton. There are several boxes there.

I'll try to get back to you soon on the bus #'s, but can't promise
(very busy day!).
