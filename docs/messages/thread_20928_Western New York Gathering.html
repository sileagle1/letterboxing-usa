From: teamdakota5 (teamdakota5@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-14 02:56:46 UTC

This was our second event in western New York. The first was
informal and only had 2 teams at it. Dagonell and us. The gathering
today was a far cry from that. We had a turn out that surprised me.
At the event we had letterboxers that were out for the first time.
We also had some that were working on their count in the Thousands.
We have been letterboxing for just a year now, it is nice to see it
take off in this area. We want to give everybody a big heart felt
THANK YOU for your attendance. For those who were not able to
attend, we are sorry that we missed you. We look forward to meeting
you at the next gathering. The gathering goes down in our book as a
RED LETTER DAY. Pat and Michelle 2/5ths of Team Dakota
