From: cadenza74 (jjcadenza@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-22 19:20:37 UTC
It sounds to me as if you are thinking of LBNA and the list as one and
the same. They aren't. LBNA is the main website for letterboxing
information and you can be contacted through that. It is completely
separate from receiving emails from the letterbox-usa list. I suspect
you gave LBNA your other address to register there and those are the
emails you are receiving.

If that isn't the case and you are just asking about stuff from the
list, Yahoo only keeps addresses you give. You can give a couple of
different addresses. If you look in your membership details it tells
you what addresses they have listed. I log in on my Yahoo address for
isntance, but there is a separate line where I list an alternate email
address to receive email. You can see if there is more than one
listed. I do suspect you are registered under the different email
address with LBNA though.

Let me know if this answers your question. If not, I can see if I can
find out anything more.

Cadenza
