From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2003-07-31 20:39:48 UTC
No rules is right! We count event stamps as finds -- mostly because
we got a stamp and could care less as to how many events we attend.
(Currently at E1, and an additional P+1 for the event we held). We
have 4 virtuals from the CodyBCleo Sunshine series and count them as
V = Virtuals. Make sense? We did print out the images on a color
printer and glue-stuck (word?) them into our logbook for safe-keeping.

You'll often see HH for hithchiker found, we call them F(inds). And
FF for first finder -- though a certain Rhode Islander keeps beating
us to them, so we have no "FF"'s .

-Steve and Heidi (and Madras)
