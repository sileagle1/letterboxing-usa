Well we managed to pick up all the suspects yesterday before the rain
set in, but today we weren't so lucky. Despite waking up to heavy
rain we decided not to spend another day indoors rather than getting
wet and we went back to Richmond in search of the Rooms. We have now
become pros at keeping logbooks dry while stamping in.

Found all however the Dining room was floating in a huge pool of
water in it's hiding spot. It already had a bit of water damage so
we piled up rocks/bricks where it should be and placed the box on top
of them. You can't see the box so you'll have to reach on top of the
stones to get to it, but it's still exactly where it's meant to be..

Unfortunately we couldnt get down to the weapons today as well, but
soon!

Thanks for a fun day out!
