From: Sheila Cunningham (sheilac@airmail.net) | Date: 1998-11-29 01:49:43 UTC-06:00
Sylvia, Junanne(a friend of Julies') and I went looking for all
four of the Duck Creek boxes. Julie went with us but wouldn't
give any clues away. First, of course, we had to have a North
Texas Letterboxing Summit(we missed Shannon! that will teach us to be
so spur of the moment!). Then it was off to the boxes. We
found all four and had a great time. It was an absolutely
beautiful day - perfect for letterboxing.

We did talk about Deborahs' box and were sorry to hear of the
loss. We took extra care to make sure that people
had passed us before actually pulling a box out of hidding in
front of them - just in case(sad, isn't it?). Extra care was
also taken to replace so not easily seen.

I am off to work on my stamps for a second and third box......

Sheila
