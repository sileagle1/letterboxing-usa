Sarah is going to kick me once she reads this e-mail! She told me
time and time again that the clues for these boxes were too confusing
but I didn't change them.

It is very rare for me to write anything but straightforward
directions to our boxes so I am sorry to say that I will not change
the clues, but I will offer you some hints.

Remember that this is a story and that all items mentioned in the
story do not appear on the path exactly as they are listed in the
text. Pay careful attention to the wording of each direction. These
boxes are not far from Firefly's set and they are not far off of the
path.

Firefly herself had some difficulty finding the first box but
eventually located it once she freed up her thinking a bit. Keep
trying and you will be rewarded.

Welcome to the fray!
