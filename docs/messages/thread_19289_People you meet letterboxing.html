People you meet letterboxing

From: teamdakota5 (teamdakota5@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-26 03:05:47 UTC
On my way home from work I travel throgh the park where we have a
letterbox placed. On 6/24/04 I was in the park when I passed two
ladies by a suv with out of state plates. As I passed I said hello,
but curiosity got the better of me and I had to turn my bicycle
around. I had to ask Letterboxing? They both said yes. For me, it was
a thrill to meet someone looking for one of our boxes. Sharon and
Dorothy from Bloomsburgh PA. We talked along the trail till they
found our box. After they stamped the log and their log books I
invited them to our house so we could exchange our stamps. We shared
some of our stories. It was a nice per chance meeting
