the longest letterbox series on my to-do list? I couldn't drum up
interest from the poolside Drew kids, so set off alone to find Pete
and Wanda's "Lil Rhody Runaround" in Burlingame RI.

Wow! This is my version of the perfect letterbox series: excellent

theme that lead straight to well hidden boxes that'll last for ages.

Wanda!

Thank you so much, P&W for the great afternoon and for the
