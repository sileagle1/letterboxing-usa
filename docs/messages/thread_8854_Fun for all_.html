Hope this will be a helpful message about where you can find
additional clues and comments on letterboxes in the Catskill Mountain
area of New York. .
There is a cork board display which is posted on the second
floor of a local Outdoors/Safety Supply Retail Store at the foothills of
the mountains. There is a "Map of the Catskills" on the front of this
display and a small cork board for any messages & comments under it. On
the bottom is a small wooden box containing a free "Catskill Forest
Preserve" official map and guide provided by the New York State
Department of Environmental Conservation. Lots of good hiking
information too!
