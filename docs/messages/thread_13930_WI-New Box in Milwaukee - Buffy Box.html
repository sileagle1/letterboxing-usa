From: Tim Kuehl (tkuehl@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2003-06-30 18:12:59 UTC-05:00
In honor of the final episode of Buffy the Vampire Slayer, my step-daughter
(Rua) who is a huge fan (me too), and my Vampire-loving wife, I have placed
a new box in Milwaukee - the Buffy Box!

As an extra incentive, the first finder will also find my new hitchhiker -
Baby Jinks!

You can find the clues here:
http://home.wi.rr.com/dragonlber/buffy_box.htm

Side note for the Traveling Trouts (BuffyVH) - This box is for you too! Now
you have an incentive to come up this way again and put your great stamp in
my box :-) ! Fishin' is great in Wisconsin!

The Dragon
