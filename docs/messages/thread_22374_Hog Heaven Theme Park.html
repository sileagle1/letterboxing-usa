Hog Heaven Theme Park

From: grumpygrinchy (ffuselier@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-08-02 03:27:13 UTC
For those of you who are real letterboxing fanatics, you have to come
to northern California. The Hog Heaven Theme Park is a DO NOT MISS
experience, with boxes carved by 15 well-known boxers from around the
country. You will find the details in the northern California area,
Tehama County, Red Bluff. We visited the "Theme Park" today and were
blown away by the clever carvings and boxes. This is a relatively new
letterboxing treasure!
