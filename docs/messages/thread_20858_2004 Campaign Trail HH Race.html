From: monique kramer (thetrekkingshoe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-03 16:42:36 UTC-08:00
Now that the election is over, it is time to see which HH won the 2004 Campaign Trail HH Race. It has been reported that the Kerry HH campaigned in many states--VA, PA, DE, MD, DC, IN, and OH. He was last reported seen in the Buckeye State of Mind in Powell, OH. On the other hand, very limited information has been released about the Bush HH. He was last spotted in Goat Hoof Trellis, Mt. Jackson, VA. But I checked that location yesterday and he is no longer there. He has moved on to an undisclosed location...hopefully not a Kerry supporters backpack!! If you have any information about these HH's, please let me know. I would like to count up the electoral votes, and declare a winner.
Thanks,
The Trekking Shoe
