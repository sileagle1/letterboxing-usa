Atlas Quest is another very popular sight to post and retrieve
letterbox clues. I've found that it's more popular in other parts
of the country. However, when I post clues for my boxes on LbNA, I
also post them on Atlas Quest by using a link to LbNA. I've had
responses from people who got clues from Atlas Quest and left
letters for me. It's fun to get feedback. I personally check LbNA
first, then AQ.
Goose Chaser
