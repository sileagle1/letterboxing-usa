Western MA and CT Boxing Updates

From: Mike S (tehutika@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-10-22 17:13:11 UTC-07:00
Greetings,

Over the last week we've been out and about, enjoying
the fall weather and hunting up a box or two.

Finally got around to the Sunday Drive in CT. We
picked a perfect day for it, too. Wonderful drive, and
the final hike up the trail for the last box was
fantastic! I can't do the views justice, so I'm not
even going to try to describe it. If you've been, then
you understand. If not, go see it.

We also went out and visited the Troll King in Forest
Park. Not only did we find him, but we also found his
crown, and the promised surprise!

We wound up our day checking on a number of our boxes
in and around Westfield. We are still eagerly awaiting
the first person to complete the Zodiac series. Come
on people, what you waiting for!? :-)

Finally, we've come across quite a few HHs in the last
month. We left some of them scattered in our wake. The
question is, where? The weather is supposed to be good
this weekend...

Mike
