From: melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2007-08-10 19:40:11 UTC-07:00
I'm assuming this has been dealt with on other forums, but just in case it
hasn't, the trash you may or may not be finding in areas that there are
letterboxes is NOT left by letterboxers. Letterboxers for the most part are
conscientious people that do more than their share of cleaning up the woods,
not trashing it. Unfortunately, some of the hiding places that are excellent
for letterboxes are also excellent places for teenagers and other hoodlums
to hang out with their friends and picnic or get drunk. I've found numerous
places that were great when the box was planted but later were found to be
homeless camps or locations of drunken orgies. The two are not necessarily
connected.

Maiden
