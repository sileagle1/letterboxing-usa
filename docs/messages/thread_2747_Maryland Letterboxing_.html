Hi All!
We had a great trip to Maryland, and a fun adventure taking the Metro from
Greenbelt to DC !! I got motion sick (didn't chuck, just felt yuck), on the
Metro and had to close my eyes the whole way down...thankfully the trip back
was better...
It was wonderful to see the Memorials again as an adult. For some reason the
Air and Space Museum seemed a little smaller then I remember...must be the
span of time between 8 and 45 yrs.old!!!
We stamped into 4 of Squirrel's letterboxes and were the 1st to stamp into
"Hughes Hollow Pine Grove" in Potomac, Md.!!! What an interesting place...I
started looking for transient alligators as we walked along!
Thanks to those who e-mailed with ideas for our journey :)
