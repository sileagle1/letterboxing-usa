The Backpacker Hitchhiker!

From: (trishkri@yahoo.com) | Date: 2000-12-02 22:39:31 UTC
We packed up our three kids (2, 5 and 7 but already veteran
letterboxers) and took a friend
with three kids for their first letterbox. We have been letterboxing
casually since Easter but everyone really got the bug today. It was
a perfect day for a hike. We went to Westwoods and when we stamped
into the Lost Lake letterbox we found the Backpacker Hitchhiker. Our
friends are definitely hooked. They were able to stamp in several
times on just one hike.

Also, we planted the first of our letterboxes at "Dinosaur Rock" in
Westwoods. More to come on that when I write up the clues tonight.

Trish
