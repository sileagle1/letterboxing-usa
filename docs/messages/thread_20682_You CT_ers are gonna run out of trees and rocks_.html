You CT'ers are gonna run out of trees and rocks!

From: Jeff LaCoy (jlacoy82@attbi.com) | Date: 2004-10-12 00:45:09 UTC

Sheesh! My entire life I've had an aunt and uncle who live with my 4
cousins in Madison, CT. I live up in Western Mass, and we would
drive down 5-6 times a year regularly to go visit. Now that I see a
gathering down in that area for next month, I figured I'd just run
through the boxes on letterboxing.org quick to see what was in that
area, figuring I might spend the weekend with them. After a quick
count, its safe to say that if I only drew a circle around their
town representing a 25 minute drive in any given direction, theres
probably ONLY 150 boxes in that area :) I was planning on going down
that day for the gathering, spending the night saturday and spending
some of the day sunday boxing, but after seeing that list, I may
just have to get friday off from work as well! I think I might go
crazy knowing all the boxes I wont be able to get in those few
days :)

Jeff
