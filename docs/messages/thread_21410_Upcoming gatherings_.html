From: TurtleMcQ (mcquades@twcny.rr.com) | Date: 2005-02-18 17:26:02 UTC-05:00
At 04:05 PM 2/18/2005, Pete wrote:
>We've heard some rumblings about NY and MI in June

The second sorta-annual Finger Lakes, NY gathering will be held in the
Ithaca area on June 11th. I'll be posting updates to both the Central NY
list and AtlasQuest (on the NY and the Event News & Planning boards):

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbCNY

http://www.atlasquest.com/

Both Silent Doug and Mark & Sue also maintain event listings on their sites:

http://www.letterboxing.info/events/

http://gatherings.blogspot.com/

For anyone interested in coming from afar and enjoying our beautiful land
of gorges and waterfalls for more than the few hours of the gathering, be
aware that Cornell's Reunion weekend is taking place that weekend as well.
Lodging in the Ithaca area may be scarce if you wait until the last minute
to make reservations. You can find all sorts of information about local
lodging, camping, places to see and things to do here:

http://www.visitithaca.com/

I'm looking forward to seeing letterboxing friends old and new at our
gathering, so put it on your calendar now. (That means you, Jiggs Clan!)

TurtleMcQ
