From: acahilly@prodigy.net (acahilly@prodigy.net) | Date: 2002-09-04 03:24:51 UTC
A funny thing happened the other day. We were boxing/caching (it's
become a her/his, mom/dad thing in our family to do both together)
and found an odd thing in a geocache (well, they're ALL odd, IMO, but
this was REALLY unusual). It was steel, about an inch square and an
inch and a half tall, with a raised plain cross on one end. Hmmm, sez
us, could it be a piece of typeface? Is not typeface kinda lika a
stamp? WILL it stamp? Well, let's just pull out the old inkpad and
see. Yep. What a nice X! So now that I have this really neat X in my
logbook, does it count as a find?
Aud
