MA- At long last - announcing Moose Hill Mach 3 gathering!

From: Kim Calcagno (hannahkat@cox.net) | Date: 2005-07-24 20:06:47 UTC-04:00
Come one, come all to the third annual letterboxing gathering at Moose Hill
Wildlife Sanctuary in Sharon, MA!
Find directions at
http://www.massaudubon.org/Nature_Connection/Sanctuaries/Moose_Hill/index.ph
p

This event will take place on Sunday, August 21, 2005 beginning at 12 noon
and lasting until round about 5 p.m. or so. Our past two have been great fun
and this year should be no different.

There are several permanent boxes on the sanctuary and many more within
close proximity at nearby parks.

Thanks to the generous folks at the sanctuary (doing me a favor yet again,
since I no longer work there), we will be allowed to use the canopies and
picnic tables in the field adjacent to the nature center and parking lot. (I
hope folks will consider a small donation to the sanctuary as a way to say
"thank you" for this courtesy.)

A few items of note:
The restrooms are in the nature center, which closes at 5 p.m.
Alas, as always, dogs are not allowed on the sanctuary.
Smoking and alcohol are also not permitted. (The town of Sharon has a no
public smoking statute)
We will have a hitchhiker hostel and will observe strict "bring one to take
one" rules as well as "no double, triple etc dipping" to prevent the
over-exploitation of hitchhikers.
CSCM and I will be hosting and will provide cold drinks and we welcome folks
to bring their favorite dish.

I will carve an event stamp but certainly welcome any other event stamps or
traveling event stamps to join us that day.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions, comments or
concerns....or if you'd like to RSVP.
We hope you'll be able to join us on this relatively short notice.

Cheers!
-Kim (Rustypuff)
