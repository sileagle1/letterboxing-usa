I visited Wickaboxet for the first time Wednesday afternoon with my
favorite but seldom available letterboxing partner, Mr. Grumpy Turtle
(who is not grumpy at all.) If you never have been to Wickaboxet it
is quite a treat. It has pretty dirt lanes through the woods and
neat rock ledges.

The Wickaboxet first letterbox is alive and well. The second
Wickaboxet box is missing which is reported in the clues. The newest
Wickaboxet letterbox, The Fox, is in a really nice spot with a cool
stamp. I could have sat & listened to the babbling brook it is by
all day!
