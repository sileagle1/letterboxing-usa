From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2002-10-07 01:10:10 UTC
I spent the weekend boxing with today spent in and around Olympia
WA. Wonderful boxes in Tolmie, Woodard Bay, Olympia and the Olympia
Watershed Park.

Also the "worlds most boring" LB in Lacey. Blah Blah Blah :-)

The Nail Family and DVN2R SKR have done a great job at placing boxes
with good, sometimes challenging, clues. The parks are all beauties
( except perhaps that blah blah blah place which can be said to be a
pretty little gem) with Tolmie SP presenting a good and hefty hike
when both boxes are tackled back to back.

I gave an info sheet to a family who passed by when I was stamping
in. I think the two young boys would love this activity. Many Wooly
Bears seen including some young ones. Douglas Squirrels are hard at
it getting ready for Winter. Mushroom Lovers are in heaven right now
with varied colorful species all over the place.

I picked up Ocean Sunrise HH and will place it again by next weekend.

Hope everyone had a great Fall weekend out and about.
