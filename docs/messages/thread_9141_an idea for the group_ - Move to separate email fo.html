Everyone has a different way of reading and working.

I manage all those emails by creating a separate folder under my
InBox called LBNA and a rule that automatically moves all subjects
containing "LBNA" into it. My regular email is free of LBNA stuff
all mixed up in it. My LBNA stuff is all together in one place.
Then it is easy to go through and delete most posts from other
states and subjects that don't look interesting.
