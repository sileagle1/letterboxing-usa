On Sunday, I (lonemasswolf) went out looking for the two boxes in Hell Hollow. These being the Devil's Den and on the Road To Hell. I did find the box at Devil's Den, even without traces of the seasonal brook, and with being chased by all those Devil's Machines. I, however, was not as fortunate on the Road To Hell. The somewhat confusing clues; Devil's Crossroads, 66 steps (how long are your steps) and bushwhack off trail at 6 degrees (for how far, 5 feet, 50 feet); will require a return trip.
 
Undaunted I traveled on to Revolutionary Fire. A word of caution to future summer time seekers. WEAR LONG PANTS. The brambles and stickers are at least two feet high. Keep a brave heart and a keen eye and you will persevere.
