From: Brian, Ryan & Lori (bconnoll@marksonrosenthal.com) | Date: 2002-11-18 15:05:01 UTC
TeamGreenDragon was out in the rain on Saturday at the Norman Bird
Sanctuary in Newport RI and picked up "Craig's Crag" (Logbook was a
little wet:(, we added an additional ZipLock :)), "Norman Bird", and
"Hanging Rock"(What a great place!!). We where unable to locate the
third in this series, the one on the ridge. Does anybody know if this
is missing? We ended the afternoon at the "Red Parrot" with "Ultimate
Irish Coffees".We had the whole team together on Sunday and headed
for the Cape to get both the "Cape Cod Canal Bourne Bridge"
and "Railroad Bridge" Boxes (Both in fine shape:)).Stopped by "The
Art Experience" in Avon MA to pick up some of "the pink stuff"(this
is a great store)and headed home.

TeamGreenDragon
