Go to the top of the municipal elevator. Take the path at 240 degrees. Stay on this path until you see a cable growing out of a tree at 110 degrees. From here, you'll see a high group of rocks at 180 degrees. Climb to the top of these rocks. Scrub is under a rock, under a rock.
 
*NOTE: Scrub is the nickname of a friend of ours that was recently deployed to the Middle East. We came up with the idea for a military-themed stamp when we heard he had been activated. He's in our thoughts daily.
