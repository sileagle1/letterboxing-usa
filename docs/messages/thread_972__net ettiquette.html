For the interested, RFC 1855 suggests some guidelines for 'net
ettiquette. Rather than debate them here (or anywhere for that
matter) I would suggest looking at the RFC (since alot of people
have already debated it) and deciding if you want to follow it
or not. The RFC is at ftp://ftp.isi.edu/in-notes/rfc1855.txt

(For those who don't know, RFCs propose standards, best common practices,
and the like for the 'net, both in terms of protocols, usage, and other
dimensions).
