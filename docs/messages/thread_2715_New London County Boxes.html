From: (egd1125@aol.com) | Date: 2001-07-05 22:15:41 UTC-04:00
We are finally going to get to visit the "Mecca" of letterboxes next week.
With 90 + letterboxes in New London County, CT, one would think it would be
the most letterbox saturated County in New England. We have decided to
"vacation" in the area and have listed boxes that we would like to visit. If
anyone knows of any of these boxes that may be out of commission, please let
us know.

Green Falls Pond - 5 boxes
Metamorphosis - 3 + Drew Clan 3
Parallel Play - 2 boxes
Haley Farm - 2 + Canopy Rock
Celestial Motion - 3 boxes
Benedict Arnold Letterboxes - 2
Gray Rock - 2 boxes
Fowler-Grindstone Letterboxes - 3

Actually, any information as to the condition of these boxes will be greatly
appreciated.

Thanks in advance.
