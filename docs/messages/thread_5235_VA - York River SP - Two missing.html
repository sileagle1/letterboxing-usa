VA - York River SP - Two missing

From: qocmike (qocmike@msn.com) | Date: 2002-05-06 05:03:41 UTC
Unfortunately, I have to report a second box missing at York
River. York River #2 (near the picnic shelter) was not there today.
It was in the central area for park activity. I'm surprised it lasted
as long as it did.

Mattaponi Trail is already posted as missing. It's hiding spot is
a pure mess of standing water and mud. Fortunately, within 100
yards...

Pupa Letterbox is still there, in good shape. It had four other
visitors today.

I didn't get to York River #1 in the western part of the park. If
the other boxers are on the list, maybe they could send an update.
Their logs mentioned 4 attempts today.

Sorry for the bad news.

Mike
