Well, I bet you didn't know there's been a geocache right where
you take out of the water for the Upper Batsto River letterbox.
http://www.geocaching.com/seek/cache_details.asp?ID=15802
The directions are as follows:
After you take-out of the Batsto River as described in the
"Upper Batsto River" directions, you are on the west side of
Quaker Bridge. There are a series of short telephone poles
along the right side (south), leading up to the bridge. The
cache is located between the 2cd and 3rd pole, covered with
pine needles. Small log book to stamp into, but no stamp.

------------------
There are 3 more placed along the mullica.
http://www.monmouth.com/~tneigel/GeoCache/index.htm

Feel free to stamp into any if you wish. Maybe I should
place a few hybrids. We do both,
T&E Letterboxing (Tom)
