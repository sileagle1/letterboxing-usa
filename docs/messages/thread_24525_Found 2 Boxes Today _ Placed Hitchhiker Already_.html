From: Robyn (fivepucks2@aol.com) | Date: 2007-08-17 13:15:34 UTC
Joy,
Welcome to Letterboxing! Yes, HH should be released soon after you
find one and I think someone else wrote that one week is acceptable to
hold onto one. Not all boxes are listed on both LB.org AND on AQ.com.
So neither site will ever have your true counts. This doesn't make a
difference to me as my personal log as my acurate count. You can
contact the placers by clicking the links from either group's website.
Locate the page for the box or hh and you should find the contact the
placer link there.

I hope this helps. If you have any other questions about getting
started with letterboxing, feel free to email me.

Robin's Nest
