Hi, all. Today, Sunday the 2nd, the letterboxing.org website is
temporarily down. Sorry, we're working on it!

There is worse news for the few of us lazy folks who kept clues (or
other web content) on the free America Online service. I didn't realize
it, but they've been planning for a month to drop the service
permanently. Eeek! All the aol-based clues are gone forever. Luckily
for us we have backups. If you need any Drew Family clues in the next
few days, please use the wayback machine to drudge them up.

Sorry for the bad news, Jay in CT
