From: Susan/Erik Davis (davisarc@wcvt.com) | Date: 2001-03-21 10:55:29 UTC-05:00
Thanks for contributing your thoughts and please continue to do so.

Yes, There has been discussion among the Webmasters regarding the
concept of symbols to further describe hikes. We decided that we could
not imply a level of oversight that does not exist. We cannot ourselves
know if the hike is such, as the poster of the clue makes a statement
and we forward their opinion along to the group. We feel that we are an
information conduit but not an overseer.

I hope you will continue to enjoy letterboxing even if you may need to
contact some box owners in advance.
