> It appears that the capacity is shared by everyone who posts pics,

Yep, it's the quota for the board, not individuals. The only way
around it is to find someone who can take off old or unnecessary
photos, or at least shrink the size of larger ones.

If you have your own website, it might be easier to post them there
and just provide a link for everyone else to use. That'll get your
around the Yahoo Groups limitiation. You could also try
http://photos.yahoo.com if you'd rather not make up your own website.

-- Ryan

photos that you have uploaded are taking up over 900kb of space each.

*might* contain clues, etc.

removing any old/out dated prints that you have posted, that would help
too!
