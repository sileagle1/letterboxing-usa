I found The Acorn Doesn't Fall Far From the Tree, Friendship Knot, and Witch Way at Mason Neck.  Can't believe I missed the Jimmy Buffet series and the Nesting Eagles!  I need to start printing out ALL the clues and keeping them in a binder or something so I don't miss any!
