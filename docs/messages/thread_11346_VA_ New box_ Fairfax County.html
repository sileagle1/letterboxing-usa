Billy Goat Gruff Microbox (velcro edition)
Fairfax County, Virginia. Great Falls. Riverbend Park
Planted 3/22/03 by Waterbird

Difficulty: A walk-up. An easy find on your way to Bee's Knees and Who
Cooks For You?

Follow the directions to Riverbend Park in Great Falls, VA. Go past the Visitors
Center entrance to the Nature Center and park in the lot. Find the trail just
past mailbox 8314; walk up this trail and turn left on the Paw Paw Trail. When
you come to a footbridge, look around the SW corner, where Billy Goat Gruff is
keeping an eye peeled for trolls.
