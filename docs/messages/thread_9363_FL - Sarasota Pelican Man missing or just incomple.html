Re: [LbNA] FL - Sarasota Pelican Man missing or just incomplete directions

From: thedoubtfulguests (thedoubtfulguests@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-11-28 20:23:08 UTC
I've replaced the Pelican Man letterbox. Included an appropriate
stamp.

Here is the information needed to adjust the clues. I just ate
Thanksgiving dinner so I am not my most coherent.

The "Recycle a Can for the Pelican Man" sign has all the lettering
torn off but the word "Can". The recycling center is still there
though and the road has a sign to the "Recycling Center" that
includes the Pelican logo. The site is the staff entrance at the
rear of the Pelican Man Sanctuary. To the right, as in the original
clues, there are some clusters of palm trees. Look in the first big
cluster of palm trees, close the staff entrance. The letterbox is
hidden under some bark and leaves in the middle of the cluster.

Happy Hunting
Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
