New Traveler: GeoAlterEgo

This traveler is a stamp and log book released with the purpose of
giving me the chance to vicariously visit as many letterboxes and
geocaches as I can. The wording on the bottom of the stamp says
Flyfisher so it is my alterego.

The traveler is also marked as a geocache travel bug to facilitate
computer tracking of the stamp and log. I hope that being able to
track the stamp on the internet will allow a letterboxer to find the
stamp more easily.

I released the traveler into the new hybrid geocache/letterbox in
Cedarville Ohio on 17 November 2001.
