I just spent an absolutely wonderful weekend in the Hocking Hills
Region of Ohio. It is a beautiful area, and the weather was just
wonderful. The reason for this post is in hunting the letter boxes in
that region there seemed to be a very dominate name in box placement,
which was trailtracker. I'd like to extend a thank you to them for all
of their time and effort in the thinking up, carving, hiding and
posting the 33 or so boxes they have listed for this area. My family
and I had hours of fun letterboxing. And not to offend anyone else
from the area that has placed boxes....... thank you too. I just
wanted to acknowledge the significant effort by this boxer. :-)
