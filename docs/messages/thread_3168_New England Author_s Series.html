Hello All,
We just spent a beautiful Sunday late afternoon of letterboxing in Tobiasson
Memorial Forest in Tolland, CT. I actually got the hubby to come, too! All
4 boxes are there, however the books are very, very full. Churchill or
Pinecone, if you check in you might want to replace them soon! Thanks for a
really nice set of LBs.

I got stung by a yellow jacket while coming back from the Shelter Falls box
on Wednesday. I didn't see a hive or anything but I didn't stick around to
see either! Possible a nest in the ground that was disturbed by Larry Boy
and Dino Boy tromping by. They were very impressed that I didn't cry.
We're very blessed that I got stung in the back of the leg and not Baby
Brother Bear sleeping in his backpack. That would have been a nightmare!

We also enjoyed the Bird Walk boxes in Storrs on Friday. That was a nice
walk and the stamps are beautiful!!! We are looking forward to Fall and
cooler weather for more comfortable letterboxing!
