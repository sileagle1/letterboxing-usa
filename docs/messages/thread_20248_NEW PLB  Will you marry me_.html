From: Desiree and Darren (drgdlgletterboxers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-09 22:17:45 UTC
We have been talking about it and working on it, and Darren and I are
ready to announce our first postal letterbox!

The idea of the box is to share the story of how you became
engaged. We have been engaged for one year September 20, and the
idea is to get this box sent out on that day.

We want to hear stories of how you became engaged and we would also
like to share pictures. You can include pictures of then (if it was
a while ago) Now, or both.

We will include an ample logbook so that you can share as much as you
please.

We would like this PLB to include 25 people (October 25, 1998 is the
day we became a couple). So, email drgdlgletterboxers@yahoo.com asap
to sign up.

I am very excited about this letterbox and I am certain you will
enjoy it!

Desi and Darren (100 days until the wedding!!)
