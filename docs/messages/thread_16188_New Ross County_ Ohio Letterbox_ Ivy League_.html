Title: Ivy League. Placed 10-3-03 by Salamander. Location: Ross
County, Ohio (Chillicothe). Difficulty: Easy. This letterbox can be
found in Grandview Cemetery in Chillicothe, Ohio. CLUES: Follow the
entrance road into the cemetery. Making no turns, at about .1 mile
past the entrance gate, you should be able to look to your left and
see a gray monument with 8 columns a short distance away. From this
vantage point, you can also see a large monument in the shape of a
tall white cross ,which appears to the right of the other monument.
You need to go to the monument with the 8 columns. At its
southeasternmost corner, take a reading of 183 degrees. Go 15 paces
at that heading to a large Maple tree. Check out the base of the
tree on its western side.
