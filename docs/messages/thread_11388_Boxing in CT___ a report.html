Boxing in CT... a report

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-03-23 18:46:20 UTC-05:00
Hello all!  What a wonderful day today was!  I wish I could have spent yesterday (Sat.) boxing too, since it was also wonderful!  Yesterday was All-State Concert day  here in CT, so I was conducting one of the bands (dress rehearsal in the AM, concert in the afternoon), so it was a LONG day!  Thankfully, today Butterfly and I were able to get out and box, finding the following boxes safe and sound:

Little Dam Series (4)

Yawbux Series (first two only... we didn't try to get #3 today since we didn't know if we would be able to get back up the hill..... bad knees and all.  We will try for #3 when they reroute the Narragansett Trail on the Wyassup Lake Road side.)

Revolutionary Fire (Needs a new stamp pad.  The old one is so wet and inky!  We were covered in ink when we left)

Amy's Sweet Valentine Mystery Box (we were only the second group to stamp in behind a certain duo who should remain nameless!!!!!)

A great day for boxing!  I hope everyone gets out to these boxes soon!  Many have not been stamped into since July and September!!!!!!!!  

Happy Trails!
