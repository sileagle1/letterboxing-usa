Back on the trail again

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-11-30 23:39:20 UTC-05:00
Thank you everyone for your emails and asking how I'm doing. I'm doing much better and actually very lucky not to have broken anything. Thought it was time I got out there and find the "Climb Every Mountain" box today. Same box I was looking for when I took my fall. Chickabirdie, Molly and I went out and ended up having a very good trip. We found that box and finished finding all rest of the series.A few of them were pretty tough. We also found the new hitchhiker "The Duffer" which was planted by Team Green Dragon at "Calamity Jane". We think we were not far behind Team Green when they were up there. Thanks to an email tip off, we also found a extra box near the spring. Word is that there is still another extra box further along. Finished up just in time and got back to the car just before it was too dark. I had overworked a muscle I hurt last weekend and was supposed to take it easy but it was worth it for this trip. Chuck, Molly and Chickabirdie
