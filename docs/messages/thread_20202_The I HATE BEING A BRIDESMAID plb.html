From: (davyschris@aol.com) | Date: 2004-09-06 21:14:43 UTC-04:00
On October 15, I am going to embark on my fourth trip down the aisle.
As a bridesmaid. By this time next year, I will have done it six
times. And every time I do it, I think, "This is the LAST time!"

I mean it. Number six is the last time. Three times a bridesmaid,
never a bride. Six times... I'm SOL as it is. I don't need to go
for seven.

I have burgundy, navy, lavendar, hunter green, periwinkle and some
shade of red called "clarion." With shoes and purses (and gloves, in
one case) to match.

Anyway, every time I lay down my cash, the bride says the SAME THING.
"You can wear it again!" I think I wore one again, once, as a
Halloween costume.

So let's have it. You can sign up for this PLB, slated to be mailed
out around the time of my fourth trip down the aisle, in mid October,
if you've ever had to buy something to wear in a wedding that "you can
wear again!"

Email me at davyschris@aol.com. I'm thinking 20 people or so? Let's
see what happens. :)

In this box, I want to know whose wedding it was and what color it
was. And if you ever did wear it again!
