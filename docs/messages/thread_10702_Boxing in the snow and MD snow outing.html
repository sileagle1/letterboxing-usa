I was boxing in the snow the last couple of days and yes, I was
nervous about leaving tracks. Before I left the area I tracked in
other directions leaving a bunch of confusing tracks.

It sure is harder to find something under 6 inches of snow! Searched
and searched, which isn't very good, all that tramping around. I'm
even more worried about re-hiding the boxes. Can't really see how
the sticks and leaves will work after the snow melts. I'll choose
snow boxes carefully.

I enjoyed Bernie the Tree Frog and Sherlock. D and Shoo, if they
over nearby, might want to check Lilies of the Valley before the snow
completely melts to be sure it is still hidden alright.

Celtic Pride, I really enjoyed Sherlock. Great theme and a terrific
stamp. I can't wait to bring the wife and dog back to do the rest.

Happy hunting,
Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
