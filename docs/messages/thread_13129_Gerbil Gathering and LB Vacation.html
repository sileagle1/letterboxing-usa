Gerbil Gathering and LB Vacation

From: Brian, Ryan & Lori (bconnoll@marksonrosenthal.com) | Date: 2003-05-27 17:41:46 UTC
TeamGreenDragon had a great time on vacation last week ! We found 62
boxes spread out over five states ( Tom Cooch's " Beehive " being my
favorite find ( the box has been there three years and we where the
second finders : ) ) We would like to thank Memlili and Legerdemain
for a wonderful tour of Mt. Desert Island . Also want to thank the
people who planted " Blue Bears Adventure " ( Great Stamps !! ) as we
had a lot of fun doing these ( just the thought of you guys running
around the woods in costume is way too funny ; ) and also Allen (
letterboxer2002 ) for some very amusing logbook entries ( Get some
sleep my friend ) . As for " Stalin " , he stayed in our possession
for only a brief time , and he is not in Maine ( or maybe he is .....
I don't know , but we did not take him there ! ) . Beth I can not
thank you enough for all the work you did for the Gerbil Gathering (
by the way that pizza was awesome !! ) we had the best time and it
was great to see some familiar faces and meet some new enthusiast .
We also once again failed to find " Dancing Men " : (

Thanks To All !!

Brian
TeamGreenDragon

P.S.

Pam and Mark ,

I will be in touch
