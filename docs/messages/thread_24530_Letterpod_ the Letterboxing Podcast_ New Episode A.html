Hello everyone, this is your new host of the Letterpod, a podcast
about letterboxing.

A podcast is like a radio show for your computer. You don't need an
IPOD to listen to it, you can listen to it right on your computer.
You can download it to an iPod as will to listen, but that's optional.

Choi started the Letterpod last year, but because of personal reasons
wasn't about to continue to do it. So I've taken over the job and
hope to get lots more participation from YOU with future episodes.

In the newest episode we have a segment we hope will be an ongoing one
from Orion, called "Tales from the Trails". I also conducted a phone
interview with wassamata_u. I think its a fun episode, and have lots
of ideas of ways to make it better for the next.

We can't do it without YOU though, so listen and tell us what you think.
http://letterpod.podbean.com/2007/08/19/episode-6/

We also have a Yahoo Group, is Letterboxing_Podcast so if you want to
help out..we can use you...learn more here:
