I wrote "another letterboxing forum" off list. I was met with some sarcasm and a reluctance to properly discuss the subject of advertising one's product, i.e.. their chat list on the LBNA chat list, so even though I hesitate to write as the ' Flame War" may start, I do feel it necessary to make some comments. Since I may be told to "put a sock in it" I might as well make my comments public. I find it a little problematical when one is bold enough to utilize a LBNA logo patch on their own chat list then put a call out on the LBNA chat list to join. Why not create your web site with your own logo and if it is really good folks will find it and use it? The discussions at the site regarding getting more members have been generally centered on putting out the word on the LBNA chat list. You have 45 members and have had 14127 registered visits so obviously folks know about the site and have decided for what ever reason not to join. I have no attachment to the LBNA chat list other than just being another letterboxer ,and I like having other forums for letterboxers , I just think one should not try to compete in this manner. Now that being said I believe my comments will only draw some folks to the web site out of curiosity
Don.
