Hi, group.

You deserve an explanation about the decals. It is
unfortunate that I need to do this on the list, but I
dont want anyone to think I made any changes or
unilateral decisions regarding what we ordered.

When I received the order, I called the embroidery
shop in IL and told them that the decals were not what
we ordered.

The shop told me that Kay Mohmers(who did the first
patch order) made the changes to the design after we
sent in the order. To my knowledge, the group was
never consulted on this. Believe me, I would not dare
make a change like that without first taking a group
consensus and receiving everyones permission!

Because of that change, I could not use the postage or
envelopes that everyone generously sent and I did not
want to fold the decals to make them fit. That is why
most of you got your orders in a different (oversize)
enveloope.

Kay Mohmers, if youre out there...did the embroidery
shop tell me correctly? Did you change the decals and
not check with any of us who sent in money?. If so,
shame on you. If not, do you know who did then?

-Valerie
