What is an Exchange - answer...

From: Dog Scouts Troop (DogScouts@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-06-13 22:34:39 UTC-04:00
An exchange is when you meet another letterboxer and they stamp their
signature stamp into your log book & you stamp your sig stamp image into
their log book.
An exchange can occur on the trail, by chance encounter or at a gathering or
any time two boxers meet and exchange images.
