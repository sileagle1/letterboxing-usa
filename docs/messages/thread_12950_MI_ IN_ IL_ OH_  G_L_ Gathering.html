MI, IN, IL, OH: G.L. Gathering

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2003-05-19 01:09:08 UTC
Just wanted to post a public notice that over that past week or so I
have sent out a couple of e-mails with detailed information regarding
the Great Lakes Letterbox Gathering being held at Waterloo Rec Area
(Chelsea, MI) on May 31, to everyone who had e-mailed me expressing
an interest to attend, and a few others I was aware of in the area.

If there are any letterboxers out there in or out of the Great Lakes
region, who were not on my mailing list, and are interested in
further information, please e-mail me at:

springchick at letterbox-mi.com

You can also check out the event information page here:

http://www.confessionsofdecember.com/extras/lb_gather.asp

There are only a couple of spots left at the carving workshop and the
log books workshop, so if you have not already contacted me about
attending these and would like to do so, please e-mail me soon.

And also, I would like to say a huge thank you to those you
contributed Visit Michigan letterboxes for the gathering. There is a
great variety of themes and all the stamps are great! Thanks for
your part in making our first event sure to be a success!

Looking forward to seeing everyone there!

Deb (SpringChick)
