Charter Oak Landing, Hartford, CT
>From North of Hartford, take I-91 South to Brainard Road, exit 27.
At the end of the ramp, turn left. At the light in front of McDonald's
turn left. Follow to the next light and turn left onto Reserve Road.
Pass CT Regional Market on your left, the entrance to the park will
be on your right.

>From South of Hartford, take I-91 North to exit 27, Brainard Road.
Turn left at the light and stay in the right hand lane. Go straight
through the next intersection at McDonald's. Follow to the next set
of lights and turn left onto Reserve Road. Pass CT Regional Market on
your left, the entrance to the park will be on your right.

Earlier this summer I saw about 15 bunnies as I drove in.

Park your car at the northern end of the park. Head in the general
direction of the children's playground.

You will come to a place where you need to choose a path. There is a
paved walkway and a paved driveway. You want the third and leftmost
option of a stone and dirt path.

As you head up the path you will find a bunch of coffin shaped stones
on your right. I counted about 15 of them and a piece of Jersey
barrier.

>From the big pieces of stone keep heading down the path a short bit.

When you reach the telephone pole next to the large metal box take 3
big steps and look left. The Sharp Dressed Man is under some rocks
at the bottom of the hill/levy type thing.
