Mystery Gathering: Wow!

From: mulbie (mulbie@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-14 11:46:01 UTC
The Yachtsman and Mary Manatee would like to thank Tom No One and
everyone else for a great day, great food, and a chance to meet a
host of other letterboxers. By our count we had 10 finds (including
2 we walked to after the event), 37 exchanges and 2 wonderful event
stamps. We were a bit nervous about appearing at our first
gathering, but everyone was very friendly if somewhat mysterious.
The arrival of "K-Martha" was a real hoot. We also have some
pictures, but I haven't had time to post them as we are in the
middle of getting our boat ready to come out of the water today.
I'll try to get them posted by this evening.

Thanks again to all who planned, and all who came and made the rainy
day a great success!
