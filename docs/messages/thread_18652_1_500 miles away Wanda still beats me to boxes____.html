From: irishtinker (irishtinker@charter.net) | Date: 2004-05-04 12:30:38 UTC-04:00
Here is my status report for Lincoln, NE

Son of Corn Husker - The bushes were trimmed so far back there is no way the box was still there. Probably discovered by landscapers.

East Meets West - Heavy construction in this park. Couldn't find box in rubble.

Journey of the Saints Box 1 - Alive and well!!!! This is the one Wanda beat me to!!

Journey of the Saints Box 5 - Could not find it.

Sarah Plain and Tall - 1-3 were missing (confirmed to me by planter)

4 couldn't find but saw reference in a later logbook that it was found thrown all over the field.

5 - great shape. I was the first one to stamp in got the HH "Skylark"

6&7 Great shape with lots of visitors. Note lots of visitors stated #5 was missing but it is there and it had a HH to boot!!

Wanted to do more but ran out of time!
