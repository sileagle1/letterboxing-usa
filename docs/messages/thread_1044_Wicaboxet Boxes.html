From: (RHopk71446@aol.com) | Date: 2000-07-15 16:24:55 UTC
This is just to inform any and all that the Wicaboxet boxes, (2) in
RI are still right where they are supposed to be, however, the
Rattlesnake Ledge one is looking a little worse for wear. I guess
from now on I'll carry some ziplock bags to help keep some of the
older boxes weatherproof. This was a short little hike, to collect
two stamps, in the state of RI. The discovery of some blueberries
gave me cause for pause as well. And yes Bug, I got my two stamps.
Hope your trek in Virginia was a succesful one too. BTW, it was all
ledge, and no Rattlesnakes. Recommended to anyone looking for an easy
2 hour trek, but beware, the horseflies are huge, and you can't shake
em. They were with me all the while that I was walking, but gave me a
break while I sat and stamped in. Still, it was worth the trip. Enjoy.
