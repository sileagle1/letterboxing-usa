Oh, Funhog! I had such high hopes of taking my new friend on her first Lb
find! Alas, after looking for 40 minutes, we tromped down the dusty trail
stamp-less! The good news though, was it was the Summer Solstice, on Mt. Shasta and

empty-handed.
