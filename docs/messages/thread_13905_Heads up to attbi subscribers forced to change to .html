Heads up to attbi subscribers forced to change to comcast

From: cavy_lovers4 (adelcoll@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-30 01:25:31 UTC
Due to the sale of attbi to comcast, my website will be changing
providers from attbi.com to comcast.net after June 30. [I don't know
if this was all of attbi or just our area].

I changed my letterboxes on the LBNA site so they no longer directly
link to the web page as I am not sure that the switch will be hitch
free. (The clues should now show up with the normal LBNA parchment
background).

If you are an attbi subscriber in the same situation, you may want to
take steps to ensure your clue are still available despite the
change. Those who are not such worrywarts as myself may just want to
try viewing their clues on the LBNA website after the changeover.
