From: BB (bburk@harbornet.com) | Date: 2002-05-30 21:22:36 UTC-07:00
If in the process of checking the boxes you placed, you discover a Hitchhiker has been left in the letterbox.
Do you count that as a find, or did you not *notice* it?
 

Re: [LbNA] Hitchhiker and Finds

From: Melissa Harvey (kiwi-1@snet.net) | Date: 2002-05-31 07:27:48 UTC-04:00
 

If in the process of checking the boxes you placed, you discover a Hitchhiker has been left in the letterbox.
Do you count that as a find, or did you not *notice* it?
 
Hi,
    I think that if people find a hitchhiker in their own letterbox, they should stamp in and count it as a find, but leave it for the next letterboxer to find.  There is nothing more disappointing than reading that a hitchhiker was there, but taken by someone just checking on the box.
 
Just my opinion on the subject,
Melissa
