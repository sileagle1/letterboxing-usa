Hi to all in letterbox land! - What a beautiful day to be out hiking -

We had just got to the Boulder Rock letterbox and someone (name not
mentioned) had signed in today! Wow were we surprised . We have never yet
run into any other letterboxer. This was very close for sure because it
is right on top of a mountain. We maybe passed them on the
trail...........Found the box a bit damp but otherwise ok. Everyone has

checkout the letterbox website.
Budster stayed home resting but we walk him a lot over by
the "Bridge to Nowhere" letterbox otherdays
Back to work tomorrow and will be thinking of our next letterbox
adventure


Enjoy the outdoors! J&G
