In reply to the question of what is the C in the count. We started using it
mostly in jest when several of our boxes were confiscated by National Park
Service. Now it us part of our p/f category. Our son had one picked up too
as did a fellow boxer on Central Coast-Ca. It just seemed like a distinct
way to title a "Lost" box. We knew who took it as we were notified. We
will continue to hide boxes on PUBLIC land with masked clues, Gwen
