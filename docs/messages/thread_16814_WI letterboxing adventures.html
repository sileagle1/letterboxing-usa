Well, I did the two new boxes in Brookfield today after school. (Thought I'd
take advantage of the nice weather and do some much needed running to boot!)
Wisconsin Hiker says that the railroad tracks are overgrown, but I found a
nice path along the way to run on. Unfortunately, someone placed a white
PVC pipe sticking out of the ground I didn't see (in the middle of the
tracks near the hiding spot) and I took quite a tumble. But I found both
boxes in great shape and the spots and stamps are great! Got to see a bunch
of trains too!

On the way home I stopped to check on the progress of The Neverending Story
Letterbox. It was great to see that 4 letterboxers had already found it!
Thanks for your contributions! The box is working out great!

Also a note about my letterboxing webpages. I keep uploading the new pages,
and road runner keeps reinstalling my old ones. I have no idea why they are
doing this, but if it continues I'll probably move my webpages elsewhere.
If you try a link that doesn't work, just drop me an email and I'll see what
I can do.
