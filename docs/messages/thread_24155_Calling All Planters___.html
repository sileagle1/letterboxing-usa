Calling All Planters...

From: infinitylovesletterboxing (infinitylovesletterboxing@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-02-26 19:56:02 UTC
Hey, Everyone!

This is an all-call to anyone who would like to carve a stamp / make
a box for my Bachelorette Party Gathering on 12 May. The theme is
love/marriage/dating/engagement/divorce/etc. So, anything at all
that has to do with that theme would be greatly appreciated.

Now, if you live close, then you obviously have more control over
what happens to your box after the gathering. But, if you live far
away, then there are two ways you can do it:

1) You can send me the stamp/box and after the gathering, I will find
it a permanent home here in upstate NY. And, if you like, I will
maintain it for you, but give you ownership so you can have an out-of-
state plant on your tally. :-)

or 2) You can send me the stamp/box and after the gathering, I will
simply mail it back to you. So, you can do this for event stamps or
postals and such... or traditionals that you just feel like launching
out here to NY for a bit. :-)


So, thanks in advance for your thoughts and contributions!!!!

take care,

Infinity
