Chickadee Dee Dee
Placed by Harpers Square Explorers
Established August 24, 2002
Suffolk County
Noyack, New York
No pets. Easy. Hand made Stamp.

This is a very nice path through a sanctuary. There are birds and critters
everywhere if you stay quiet and walk slowly. Walk a ways down the main
path, hold your hand open and away from your body offering your sunflower
seeds (None of that parakeet stuff). Hold very, very still, and see what
happens. If you are with a group spread out a bit so that you aren't too
intimidating. This is truly an experience, communing with nature.

After your experience, walk on down the main trail until you come to a bench
on the right. Turn around and walk 12 paces back. On your left is a large
tree. On the opposite side is a small multi-trunk tree. Look behind that
under bark and leaves.

Do explore deeper into park. Someday I will return and move the box to a
more interesting hiding place. If someone lives near there and would like
to move it call me or post a message for the Chickadee Dee Dee on the board.

Morton Wildlife Refuge. On the southern neck of eastern Long Island, NY.
