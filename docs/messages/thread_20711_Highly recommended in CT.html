I'm sure most folks in CT are justifiably concentrating their autumn
boxing in the Mansfield area, but I've just got to point out a
wonderful new letterbox by some new friends. When I first met this
couple in Salem, I was struck by how much they were obviously in
love. It's kinda like when you see Warrior Woman these days: that
sparkley happy excited delight that just bubbles out and makes
everyone else all breathless and goose pimply and a little jealous.
There's just something thrilling about lovers. It was only later that
I learned that this couple goes by the fitting trailname "The
Lovers." Anyway, be sure to check out The Salem Star Donkey, and not
just because of the embarassing dedication at the beginning of the
clues. This one is a "how to" lesson in great boxing.

Thanks, Lovers! Jay in CT
