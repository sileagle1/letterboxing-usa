Hi Graham!! My name is Joie, aka " see joy". It was a pleasure to learn  about "you" yesterday on our trip up to the gather with Judi the Space Traveler. Judi and I met via her letterboxes, which happened to be my first finds, and the internet. To our surprise we live within miles of each other and we got together yesterday "in person" to share the journey to Vernon, Ct. along with my friend Sue Clausen , who introduced me to 'boxing, and my son Austin aka lockmaster.We were all intrigued as Judi shared her stories from Dartmoor and the miles and hours just flew by.We truly had a wonderful day! The differences  in  'boxing  on your side of the pond and ours are "way cool" as my son would say . I love the fact that many of your shops and pubs have stamps. I think i'm going to create a house stamp for our home.You will have to come for a visit to collect it when you travel our way to letterbox!!Thank you for"being there" with us yesterday in story and in spirit!!  How many boxes have you found? best regards, see joy and lockmaster
