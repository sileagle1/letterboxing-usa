Hi Psychommy!  Thanks for the warning!  We're still spinning from the Bull Run spiders, prayers and ballet dancers (not to mention the horses, firearms and Civil War battles!)...

We're glad you had some luck that day.  Nothing bums us out more than a total miss on several attempts (I guess some of us never get over that childhood low threshold for frustration which is why we no longer take piano lessons...)

When we get really stumped, we humbly correspond with the box planter.  We explain where we searched and why and he/she normally tells us if we were on the right path or not.  It helps a lot!

We're so happy that this weekend was warm (relatively) and sunny 'cause we're plunging back down into the 30's again today!  At least the snow is almost all gone!  It was great to get outside again and hit the trails!  Good luck and let us know any good hints you get!  BeMa
