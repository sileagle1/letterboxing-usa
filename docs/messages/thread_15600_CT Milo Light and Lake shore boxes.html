Toad Dog and I went out today, Swamp Yankee had to work :0(
We discovered the Milo Light Preserve. Does anyone know why it is
called that? Anyway,all 4 boxes were found exactly where they were
supposed to be and in fine shape. thanks to Drewclan, Butterfly, and
Lobsta Lady. From there it seemed logical to go get Lake Shore box at
Hopemead State Park. This was a piece of cake, and we continued down
the hill to check out the lake- very pretty. We saw a cool cave
built into a bank, but without a flashlight, I wasn't about to
venture inside. there was alot of broken glass in the vicinity, and
I was worried about Toad's feet, so we left carefully. It looks like
a popular party spot. It's strange,isn't it,that beer bottles aren't
too heavy to get somewhere, but seem too heavy to cart back out!!???
Hmmmmmm. Happy Boxing!- Sunshine and the Toad Dog
