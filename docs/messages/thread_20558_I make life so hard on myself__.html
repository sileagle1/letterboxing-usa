I make life so hard on myself!!

From: Jeff LaCoy (jlacoy82@attbi.com) | Date: 2004-09-30 00:58:59 UTC
I made a very simple "positive" stamp for my first personal with a
rubber stamp, it was pretty rough, but not bad. I put in an order
with stampeaz and got my order in today. I scanned out my picture
(Calvin of Calvin & Hobbes) and traced it and transferred it. LOL,
I'm laying on my floor carving it now, had to take a break before my
back seized up on me. I have almost all the inside parts carved out,
then I'll just have to "trace" it with the #1 blade, and gouge out
the blank spots around it. I'll probably scan it and upload it, see
what you guys think :) I couldnt have just done a big easy negative
stamp, I had to make it hard on myself :)

Jeff

P0 F39 X1 HH2

Started 9/5/04 :)
