CT- Salem Quest

From: Melissa Harvey (kiwi-1@snet.net) | Date: 2002-10-06 20:54:38 UTC-04:00
Last spring I took on a letterboxing project with a small group of 5th graders at Salem School.  Through this  project, my students learned about the history of their town, created maps, carved stamps, and wrote clues to a series of letterboxes in and around Salem.  We published a small book of maps and clues similar (on a much smaller scale) to the Valley Quest Series.  After distributing the books to the students we have a limited supply of books left to share.
 
    If you are interested in a Salem Quest Book, please e-mail me off list at kiwi-1@snet.net with your name and address and I will be happy to send you one.
