Mid-Atlantic Camping Weekend, May22-23

From: celticprideltrbxr (eileenbriley@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-19 17:44:59 UTC
The campsites have been reserved and the cicadas are waiting...Here is
the agenda that has been hammered out for the upcoming weekend.
Please come by for the day, even if you don't want to camp!

Mid-Altlantic Camping Agenda May 22-23, 2004

10:00am - 10:30am Meet at Patapsco Park, McKeldin Area
Trailhead---see attached website link for directions!

10:30am Hit the trail and find some boxes---until check in time!

Check-in time for all campers is 1:00pm--this would be a great time to
do lunch. We'll be camping in the Hollofield Area - Pet loop

3pm--6pm Boxing

7:00pm gather at Site #461 (Celtic Pride) for pot luck dinner and
desserts. We can have a smorgasborg of foods and adult beverages. This
will be a great time to hang around, eat, get to know everyone, & tell
cicada stories.


Sun. morning May 23rd, 2004

Breakfast on your own.

9:30am--10am ---Meet to continue boxing, cicada dodging, and having
fun!---until we drop!!!

http://www.dnr.state.md.us/publiclands/central/patapscovalley.html



Any comments, suggestions, witty remarks?

See you Saturday!

Celtic Pride
