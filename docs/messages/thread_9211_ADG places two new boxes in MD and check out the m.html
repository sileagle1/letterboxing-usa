The ADG placed Out of the Frying Pan and Into the Fire, a series of
two boxes, at Savage Mill. To view the clues, please visit:

http://wildwinds.tripod.com/adg2.html#pan

For those of you on the East Coast, be sure to check out the Leonid
meteor shower on Tuesday, November 19. The shower will be most
visible from 5-6 AM that morning. For best viewing conditions, leave
the city lights behind and find an open area preferably at high
elevation. I have never seen a meteor shower before and am very
excited!
