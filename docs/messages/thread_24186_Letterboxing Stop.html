Letterboxing Stop

From: ponyexpressinfo (ponyexpressinfo@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-03-22 23:01:40 UTC
Hi! We have just added an official Letterboxing stop in our store! We
are Pony Express, 16 North Main Street South Yarmouth M) 02664 (off 28
where Station Ave meets North Main). We have beginner information on
letterboxing from the lbna site, and we sell stamps, ink pads, and
journals. Please stop by and add information. We also offer a list of
local letterboxes in Yarmouth!

We just love letterboxing and want to spread the word, help out new
letterboxers, and be a place for the dedicated letterboxer to stop by
and share stories.

So stop on by.....
