SLO County, CA- Two missing Ryan boxes:

From: jugglermouse (jugglermouse@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-25 15:21:18 UTC
Yesterday I was by the "garden" mentioned in Ryan's Run For the
Border Series - the garden has been completely removed, and the box
must be gone.

And on Sunday, Emily Rose, Sadie, and I walked the first loop of The
Montana de Oro Series. The first box, School of Fish, is definitely
gone. It appears that probably a further crumbling in the rock wall
where it was placed may have caused it to tumble out and then be
carried away. We had the right spot because both Emily and Sadie had
been there before and found it. The second box, The Dolphin, was in
place.

Though we completed the whole loop, we can not actually verify the
rest of the boxes- we realized at some point that we had not given
ourselves nearly enough daylight hours for the hike, and so though we
noted the places where the next boxes might be, we REALLY needed to
keep moving and get ourselves back to civilization. I want to take
the walk again sometime giving myself more time to complete it.

Sigh- two Ryan boxes found missing in a week. And there he is off
tramping the country, not worrying about us at home and our troubles
at all...

-Jugglermouse
