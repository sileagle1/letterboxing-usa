New Year's Eve is the best time to letterbox.

We go to Steve Cochran's stand-up Comedy Show For People Who Can't Stay
Awake Til Midnight (tho last year the date got a little messed up, but this
year the show's producer got the date right), then we go out boxing after
the show so that we get a box before midnight on Dec 31st, a box right at
midnight, and a box after midnight on Jan 1st.

Then we head home to hot chocolate and marshmallows.

For me, that's the best of winter boxing.
