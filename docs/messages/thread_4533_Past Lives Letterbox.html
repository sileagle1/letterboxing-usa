Past Lives
Owensville, OH  Clermont County
Stonelick Twp. Cemetery
Placed: Feb. 22, 2002 by Team Boji (ZAX31@AOL)

Driving Directions:  Stonelick Twp. Cemetery is located on S. R. 50 approx. 0.6 miles west of Owensville.

Clues:  In a past life you may have been a cannoneer.  Choosing an appropriate artillery piece, you sight your weapon well.  Being careful not to clip the tops of any head stones, you fire!  (If you so choose, now would be a good time to yell BOOM!)
Your round goes all the way to the tree line.  Good shot!  Your cannon ball now lies at the root system of a midsize fallen tree that touches a large fallen tree.  Be very careful in choosing a downhill path to the large fallen tree.  Toward the top of the tree, in a "Y" above two saw cuts, you may find the Past Lives Letterbox
