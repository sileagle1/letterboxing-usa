Hi Boxers:

I received an email from Turtle McQ who saw we were planning to
attend the ME gathering later this month. She asked if we made it up
to Rockport before or after the gathering, would we be able to
replace her tupperware container at Bull Frog Box, or drop it into a
new zip lock bag as a favor. Apparently, she must have received an
email that water was leaking through a hole in the top of the
tupperware container.

That got me to thinking - could we use a resource where all
letterboxers who have a few long distance boxes that might need some
attention could email and ask for help from the boxing community?

Well . . .

http://fixbox.blogspot.com/

Ta da - visit our new Box Maintenance Page, check out to see if a box
is nearby that need some help and give a hand to a brother or sister
letterboxer. If you too have a box in need of some help, email me
from the link on that page.

And, as always . . .



Have fun and just get out there and box!!!

Mark Pepe
