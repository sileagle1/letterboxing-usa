Just got back from a great day of Letterboxing and thought we should
pass on some updates. We went looking for the two boxes in the
Washington Monument State Park in MD. Two Grey Squirrel's box is in
great shape but the log book is almost full.

See Joy's box (Spirit Walk) appears to be DOA. We found the hiding
place mentioned in the clues (at least we are 99% sure it was the right
place) but didn't find the box. At least not intact. We found a box,
but no lid, stamp or log book. We really hoped we are wrong, but an
exhaustive search of the area turned up no further evidence.

A little bit of a disappointment to any otherwise great day, but I guess
that's all part of the experience.
