We Live & Breathe XX - Texas Style!

From: (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2016-12-12 20:25:06 UTC-08:00

We are thrilled to announce that Texas will be hosting the 20th anniversary Event of American letterboxing in October, 2018.  The event will be at a beautiful facility close to Austin, TX. We will have 18 miles of trails, optional group lodging, optional full meals onsite, and camping, RV, and hotel options nearby. The date will be in the second half of October. We plan to keep this tentative until the Formula 1 race dates are announced so we can avoid the insanity it causes in Austin and surrounding areas.

We have created an event listing on AQ for information purposes:
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=3784

This event will require registration and advance payment, so the AQ listing is for announcements only. A dedicated website, pricing and registration information, facebook page, and more will be announced next year. For now, we just wanted to give a heads up so you can put it on your long range calendar. Oh, and if anyone has doubts about my sincerity, this is not a prank!

Silver Eagle
