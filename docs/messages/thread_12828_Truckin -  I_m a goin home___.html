Truckin - I'm a goin home...

From: Terrapin Traveliers (terrapintraveliers@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-05-12 18:02:48 UTC-04:00
Our house in Boca Raton, FL has been sold and on Friday I'll be heading to
the biggest little state in the union and reuniting with my better half!
We're looking forward to many weekends searching out letterboxes and
planting new ones along the way. Stay tuned for our upcoming letterbox
series based on my family history in Rhode Island (Kent Bros. Dairy, Kent
Heights, Kent Heights General Store, etc.) Also looking forward to enjoying
some brown eggs, johnny cakes, coffee milk frappes, bubblahs, etc. :-)

However, this means our Boca boxes (loggerhead, boca friends and tiki idol)
will become orphans :-(
We'll probably only be able to get down south once a year to check on them,
but perhaps others could look in on them for us and give us an occasional
update.

Oh, and if anyone in the Providence/Boston/Worcester area knows of a company
looking for an Art Director/Graphic Designer, please let me know!

- Mike (1/2 of the Terrapin Traveliers)
