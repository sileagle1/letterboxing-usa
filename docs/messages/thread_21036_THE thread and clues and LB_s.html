I would like to think of myself as an average Lb'er.

I check the weather to see what to wear. Don't ask...

I check the car to be sure I have enough gas to get
home again. Don't ask.....

I check the BP to be sure everything we need is in
there. HA! This is one we haven't forgotten...yet.

BUT FIRST we pick the LB we will look for today.
Is it easy or hard to get to and can we even begin to
understand the clues or do we need to figure out or
research them BEFORE we go?

I READ the clues to see if we can handle the walk
BEFORE we leave. DS is just off of back surgery.

Why complain about an box not being what you want if
you didn't read it BEFORE you went? Besides I am
always grateful someone took the effort to put a box
out for ME to look for.

If we can't find the box I would email the placer, say
we could not find the box, state what we did and ask
nicely if we could have a teensy, tiny, itty bitty,
clue....Okay we would actually throw ourselves on the
mercy of the placer and ask for help.

I've heard grovelling is helpful in some cases....
