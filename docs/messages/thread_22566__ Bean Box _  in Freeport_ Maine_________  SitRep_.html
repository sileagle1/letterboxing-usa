They counted five rocks from each end, looked in both places, and could not find it, so they just kept looking behind neighboring rocks until they did. Then they replaced it exactly where they found it.

I can ask them to check on it for you.

(By the way, these friends learned about letterboxing when we visited them on 8/6. We got them addicted too!)
