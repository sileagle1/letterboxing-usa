From: mepanj (mepanj@hotmail.com) | Date: 2006-05-28 00:40:14 UTC
I finally got the chance to do some maintanence on these boxes. Only
th Shuttle Craft was in serious need. A new log book was added to
replace the waterlogged one and the box was replace to replace the
cracked one.

Also - I moved the location of the box and have changed the clues to
reflect that change. I am hoping that this will help that box to
have a longer life.

These trails are truely amazing. During my travels today I saw many
different species of birds, lots of plants in bloom, huge frogs and
tadpoles. In general, life is in full bloom on these trails. It was
a real pleasure (wet log book aside) to walk these trails and witness
all this. I was also very happy to see so many 1st time boxers
logging in to the DAR box. My sincere apologies to any one who was
disappointed about the second box and hope that it's problems have
been solved.

The shuttle pines are an amazing thing to think about. Seeds having
traveled so far and then growing strong makes all things seem
possible.


The weather is finally improving and I wish you all a happy boxing
weekend, with special thoughts to those who this weekend is intended
to honor.

Mepanj
