Great Day on Sat

From: celticprideltrbxr (eileenbriley@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-21 16:24:00 UTC
Daughter of the Old Dominion and ourselves had a great day on Saturday.
We enjoyed the Accotink Wildlife Refuge and the wonderful boxes that
were there. Unfortunately, Mary had to leave us after finding four of
the boxes so Bern and I continued the journey. We are sorry to report
that we could not find "Sir Mooseness" in the McCarty Farm Series. We
found the spot, the indentation in the earth where the box was, and the
rock used to cover it, but no box. I guess he decided to wander off
into the woods. Do mooses migrate? :) The other two were in great
condition and were wonderful to find.

We then headed off to Mason Neck to enjoy a little Jimmy Buffet and
after getting a clue wrong and much hiking, we were finally successful
and elated to discover a little "Chinese Food To Go" waiting in
"Letterbox in Paradise"! Our first ADG find!!! Woo-hoo!!! We also
found the "Nesting Eagles" box in the park.

After getting 10 boxes Saturday (our record for one day) I feel the
urge to try for 11! I'm so addicted!
