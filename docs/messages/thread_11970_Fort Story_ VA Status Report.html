Fort Story, VA Status Report

From: Kathy (Btrplc2b@aol.com) | Date: 2003-04-15 11:44:39 UTC
I've received a report that this box is missing. The Fort Story
letterbox was recently replaced by a friend who was very familiar
with the location original location. This particular box has been
reported missing before because when returning the box under the
walkway, boxers have been very diligent in making sure it remains
out of sight and they look for the most secure spot to put it in.
Don't give up on this one, and if any of my letterboxing friends in
VA Beach who are very familiar with this box have the time to swing
by Fort Story to confirm it missing or not, I'd be very grateful.

The security is at a very heightened state at Fort Story and I was
told that welshchick's car was searched, to include a mirror-check
under her car...so be advised.

Good luck and I hope to be able to provide definite confirmation of
the status of this box soon.

Be safe,

Kathy (Ladybug)
