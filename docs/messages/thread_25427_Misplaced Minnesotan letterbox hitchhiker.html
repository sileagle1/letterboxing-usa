From: Libby Hunt (libby.bloom.hunt@gmail.com) | Date: 2016-07-30 13:22:21 UTC-04:00
Hi there,

I'm embarrassed to say how long I had the hitchhiker, but I recently rediscovered it as we were cleaning out my old car to sell. It traveled with me from NH to OH when we moved, and then all over Columbus with my family. It was a quiet stowaway for over 10 years. 10 YEARS! And now I am happy to tell you that it has been placed out in the wild again. About two weeks ago, I placed it in the House of the Rising Water Riverbox in Dublin, OH. Hopefully it gets picked up and logged in somewhere new by someone more timely than me!

Happy travels!
Libby
