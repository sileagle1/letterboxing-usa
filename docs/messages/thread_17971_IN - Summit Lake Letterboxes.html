IN - Summit Lake Letterboxes

From: barbw427 (barbw427@insightbb.com) | Date: 2004-03-01 01:01:20 UTC
We took advantage of the beautiful weather this weekend and went out
hunting some new Indiana letterboxes. We found all 4 of the Winter
Wonderland series at Summit Lake State Park and enjoyed some nice
hiking. The first two boxes in this series are going to be quite a
challenge when the trees leaf out! We were the first finders of this
series -- we think that is a first for us! I recommend that anyone
who finds themselves in the East Central part of Indiana check these
boxes out.
