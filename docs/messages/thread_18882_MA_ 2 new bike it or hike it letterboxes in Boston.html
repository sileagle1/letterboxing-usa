I've added 2 new boxes in Boston; Franklin Park in West Roxbury and
Southwest Corridor Greenway in Jamaica Plain. These 2 will companion
with my Arnold Arboretum box in Jamacia Plain, which I've also
updated and redid its map. You can bike all 3 from any of the
starting points. The Southwest Corridor Greenway is one of my
favorite urban bike trails. Well kept and it brings you through the
historic South End of Boston. A side note, please let me know if your
unable to print or view any of the maps that I've included with my
letterboxes. I've tried several different formats (jpg,gif,tif) and
would like to know which ones have the least problems. Thanks
