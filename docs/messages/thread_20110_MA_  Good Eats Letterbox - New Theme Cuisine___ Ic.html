The Good Eats Letterbox, in Acton, MA, now has the Theme Cuisine of
Ice Cream!

The Good Eats Letterbox is a cuisine-oriented letterbox and features
a new cuisine every few months, errr, every once in while... ;-)

favorite eating spots for the featured cuisine. For this theme I'm
particularly interested in the best spots for Mocha Malted
Frappes!!! To find out more, check out the clues page on:

http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?boxnum=6197&boxname=Good_Eats

Sorry it's taken so long to get out the lastest cuisine change. I'd
hoped to get the Ice Cream theme out earlier in the summer, but with
a scorcher day like today, it seems an appropriate theme!

Enjoy and Good Eats!
