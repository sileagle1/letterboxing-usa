Is it too dark to go boxing now?

From: adglbna (ADGLBNA@aol.com) | Date: 2002-09-15 02:26:22 UTC
There's this little voice in the back of my head screaming, "Go to
Patapsco Valley State Park right now! Who cares if it's dark!
You've got a flashlight a stamp pad and bear repellent!"

I was just researching camping in St. Mary's County and stumbled
across a site that offers reservations for camper cabins in
Patapsco. Unfortunately they are only available through September
30. What wise park official decided that no one should be able to
hole up in a cabin in October? Fall is prime time for boxing!

So, Psychomommy, when can you put Sarah, Cat, and I up for the
night? We won't take up too much space and if we get bored, we might
place a couple of boxes in your yard, crawlspace, toilet, etc.
(hee, hee)

In all seriousness, the park is not far from Frederick and its
magnetic pull is getting stronger and stronger every day. I don't
think I need a fortuneteller to tell me that the gang will be
visiting very soon.

Tammy,

ADG #1
P11, F23

>
> Box 3
> SHEEP GO TO HEAVEN, GOATS GO TO HELL

> your friend. Head for the bottom of the hill. WATCH WHERE THE'RE

> stream, flowing or not. Start counting left blazes only. After the
7
> blaze look to the right for the old rock wall. Now count 2 right
> blazes. See the hanging extraterrestrials!!!! Go 2 more right
blazes.
> STOP Look back to ET and take 6 steps- look right in the middle of
3
> you will find what you seek.
