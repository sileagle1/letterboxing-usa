I wanted to thank each and every one of you that took the time to write
to me with ideas, suggestions, and support about my cats and my husbands
asthma.

We have tried many of your suggestions... the ones working so far are:
Acepromaxzine - this is a tranquilzer that is diluted 30 times and is
proven to change the ph of the cats saliva.
Brushing - we are brushing 1-2 times daily and have them down to almost
no shedding
Baths - oh yea, the cat LOVES this one! HA Anyway - once a week he
gets a bath w/ an anti-dander shampoo... this is helping, but he still
needs work
Bath wipes - mid-week he gets wiped down with a wipe that helps w/
dander and shedding.
Shed-Free tablets - crumbled on food, they are supposed to help reduce
shedding
Hairball Food - this may take a while, but this combined w. the brushing
is working - not a hairball in a week!
