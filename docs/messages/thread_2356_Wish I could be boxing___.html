Between proposals and travel for work, visiting relatives, gardening
and kids' soccer schedules I haven't had much time for 'boxing. I
did take my Brownie troop to Fisherville Brook for their first LB
outing. They had a great time finding the Knight and the Mummy (no
time for Renee). Fisherville Brook is close to where we live and I
was surprised that only two out of fifteen kids had been there
before. Hopefully we will get a few new converts to letterboxing
from the outing.

I have several boxes ready and waiting to be planted with more on the
drawing board. Still waiting for someone to decipher Trish's Twist.
