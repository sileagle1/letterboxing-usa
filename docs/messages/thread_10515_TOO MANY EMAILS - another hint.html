A very good way to deal is to make a separate folder in your email
box just for Letterboxing. Then make a rule that all email with
subjects containing "LBNA" be automatically moved there. Look
under "ools, Message Rules, Mail" in Outlook Express.

Then it doesn't clog your personal mail. In the Letterboxing folder
you can delete all of it at once if you want to because it's only
LBNA and not your personal stuff.

The one precaution is that if someone sends you "personal" mail with
LBNA in the subject it will go to the new folder too.

Good luck,
