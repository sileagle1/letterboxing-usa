From: thedoubtfulguests (thedoubtfulguests@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-04 01:03:54 UTC
After such a boxious weekend I am full of ideas. (dangerous)

I propose a local award for boxing the area. One, to reward local
efforts and Two, to reward those who place or exchange as much, or
more, than find: recognizing that it's all good.

I think it would be grand to have a stamp for those who have a
combined FPeX 250, (Found, Placed, event, eXchanged) of all Potomac
River area stamps.

I propose a map stamp in five parts: DC, NernVAsouth, NernVAwest,
MDnorth, and MDeast. I suggest an emphasis on water, mountains and
parks rather than borders and roads. Each stamp could be made by
someone different. Those who make Potomac FPeX 250 could stamp the
stamps at gatherings or through the mail. Just maybe it would be a
cool stamp.

Any comments?
