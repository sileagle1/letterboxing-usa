Hi Jude:

The two stamp pads Team Bindlestiff uses are:

1) StazOn brand by Tsukineko. This is a solvent-based ink which makes
it pretty permanent and super quick to dry. These are very vibrant colors.
http://www.tsukineko.com/store/cart.php?m=product_detail&p=3247

2) The Colorbox Chalk inks by Clearsnap. These are pigment inks that
dry as fast as a dye based ink. They come in more subtle colors.
http://www.stampit.com/clearsnap/chalk.html

Enjoy!
