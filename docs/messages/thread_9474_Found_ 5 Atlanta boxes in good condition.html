From: roberta a stillson (Rubaduc@juno.com) | Date: 2002-12-01 07:34:26 UTC-05:00
Since I was spending Thanksgiving with my son and his family, they had to
be introduced to letterboxing. On Wed. we started at Emory University
with a nice walk around Chandler Lake in Lullwater Park and got the
Easter Egg. Then we headed for Stone Mt. to see how many of those 4 we
could get before we ran out of time. Picked up Jolly G-Man's Strength
pretty easily (the 10 year old actually found it) and started around the
Mt. to Ryan's Stone Mt. box. Because of our schedule we had to turn back
before we got there, not knowing where "there" was exactly and the 10 yr.
old was wearing out. Prior plans included a hike on the A.T. in northern
Georgia (mother and son deal) but my son decided he wasn't going to be
out done by letterboxers, so off we go back to Stone Mt. and nabbed The
Stone Mt. box, Eagles Home and Whiskers not all of them easily. The
distances there were much longer than in Ct. but good planting and lots
of fun. I may have convinced my son to plant some and next time I'm there
I'll definitely bring along a couple to leave behind.
Thanks for the boxes.
