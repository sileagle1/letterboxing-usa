From: irishtinker (irishtinker@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-08-07 08:09:18 UTC-04:00
Just a gentle reminder

Please remember when rehiding a box make sure everything is in the box correctly (not crammed so the stamp could break) and make sure the lid is on tight. Please look back & make sure you can't see the box in it's hiding place. In the past week & a half I have found 2 boxes left out in the open, 1 with it's lid just sitting on top of the box not snapped on, & one stamp broken. Remember all the work the hider has put into creating this treasure for you to find and handle with care.
