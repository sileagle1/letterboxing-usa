Hitchhiker & Westwoods

From: Melissa Harvey (kiwi-1@snet.net) | Date: 2000-12-11 16:57:26 UTC-05:00
Dan and I went out to Westwoods yesterday for an awesome day of letterboxing.  I was psyched because Westwoods is one of my favorite places to hike in CT.  While we were there we not only found the Drew's boxes and the new Dinosaurs Before Dark box, but we also found a new hitchhiker that we didn't even know existed!  It is the Magic Tree House Hitchhiker left by the Kurdziels (the same family that made the dinosaur box).  Our lucky day!!!  We were shocked though that the Westwoods boxes had very few visitors.  For those of you in SE CT I would highly recommend this hike it is terrific.  To the Kurdziels:  Your spot was so cool.  We really felt like we were being transported back to the ages of the dinosaurs!
 
Melissa & Dan in muddy CT
