     Have just taken a look at your site,  I have been letter boxing on Dartmoor for about ten years and have collected about 400 boxes  there are about 8000-10000  boxes on Dartmoor which covers an area of 365 sq. miles quite small by your standards.  some boxes are vandalised by small minded people with a low intelligence threshold other boxes are placed on the moor without notifying the organisation that collates and publishes the location boxes.
 
  I am a "traveller" I carry a stamp with me to exchange with other travellers but only if I am asked - are  you a traveller?  some boxes are static and are placed in pubs hotels campsites cafes and tourist attractions these are published in the book but you still have to ask!!  I am sure it will take off over the water but only if you can stop it becoming commercialised as money will remove the fun of discovery
