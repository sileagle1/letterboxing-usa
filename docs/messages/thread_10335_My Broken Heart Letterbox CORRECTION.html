My Broken Heart

 

Although it hurt to let you go I knew you wanted to be free,

I longed to keep you by my side I wanted you for me.

 

The tighter I held the more you pushed, my strength too soon wore thin,

So I sent you far away from me, to start your journey again.

 

I will pray for God to keep you and for safety along the way,

And maybe by chance you will return to me after many a day.

 

Full of adventures and tales to tell of trials great and small,

You’ll be welcomed with opened arms, I want to hear them all.

 

Be safe my love, tread carefully and be sure to take a good look,

Not everyone will be good to you or want to stamp your book.
