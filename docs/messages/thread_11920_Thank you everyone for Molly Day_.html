Thank you everyone for Molly Day!

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-13 21:48:53 UTC-04:00

Thank you everyone for making the Molly Day Gathering a success. Yesterday,
I tried to make a rough count on how many people might be there. I figured
about 14. We were overwhelmed when the attendance was between 35-40 people
and a couple dogs. It was so nice to see the faces to match the stamps we
have been seeing in so many letterboxes. Four Hitchhikers were also there:
The Worlds Fishiest Hitchhiker, Spare Tire Hitchhiker, Support The Troops
Hitchhiker and the debut of Molly Looking For Ginger Hitchhiker. A special
thanks to Carolyn, Leader of the Pack, Butterfly and Irishtinker for all
their time and efforts for the box planting and this great gathering. We
were impressed with the work and quality of the stamps made for Molly. The
boxes were "Molly Key To My Heart", "The Key To Mollys Success","Molly Our
Hero" and the Molly Day event stamp. The weather and day turned out perfect.
Thank you to everyone who came. We had a wonderful time! Thank you on behalf
of Molly.....Chuck, Molly and Chickabirdie

The pictures we took are on my web site and can be viewed at:

http://www.chuckstraub.com/mollyday.htm
