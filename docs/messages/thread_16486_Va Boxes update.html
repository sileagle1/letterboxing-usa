Va Boxes update

From: Kerri (kerripaul@peoplepc.com) | Date: 2003-10-20 20:37:20 UTC-04:00
Hi all!
We had a great Sunday this weekend, even though we only increased our "F"
count by one!

We found the "Green Tea" box in Norfolk. It was there and in good shape, for
which we were thankful after the long roundabout way I steered us to it.
(oops!)

While there, we planted a new box. We will have the clues up shortly.

Then we headed of to Red Wing, to check up on the "Old Maid" and the
"Japanese Dragon". Much to our great surprise they both survived the
hurricane and the clean up crews. We were a very happy car-ful on the ride
home!

I just want to thank all the boxers who visited these boxes, as they have
converted my kids for life.

Thanks to all, and happy boxing,
Kerri
