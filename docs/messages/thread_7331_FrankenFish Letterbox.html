For those that are unfamiliar with FrankenFish (as it is called
here in the Balto/Wash area) it has to do with some fish that a man
bought for medicinal purposes. I think the fish originates in China
and the people there make a soup out of it for some kind of illness.
So this man in Crofton, MD didn't use all the fish so he threw them
into a pond. They multiplied and ate all the good fish. These ugly
fish can live out of the water too! Some travelled to other ponds.
Now the trouble is how to kill them. The End.
