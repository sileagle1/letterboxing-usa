Well....Couldn't we all have had a little letterboxing reunion in
Washington County today! John and I went out and nabbed Chief Kno-Tah
and Indian Feather. Bethany and Co., plus Brett, Penny & Grace had been
there ahead of us!

By the way, folks, the talk about the excellent carving of the Chief
Kno-Tah stamp is no exaggerated rumor. This truly is an excellent stamp
that I'm SO PROUD to have in my letterboxing logbook!

Indian's Feather is a sweet little gem and in a terrific hiding place
with great clues. We appreciate Wingnut (Cyndie) for placing this just
in the nick of time for us to stamp in.

Thanks all for a wonderful letterboxing adventure. Even people on
crutches can easily obtain these stamps once they figure out where they
are!

Best regards,
