great day boxing in Coventry CT

From: Odilio (odiliofinder@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-18 08:08:12 UTC-07:00
We had a great day boxing in Coventry CT yesterday. Licks our cousin from NY and Irish TInker and I found the brand new Constitution State box and the Coventry Grammer School box (this one without clues. Irish Tinker has an amazing sixth sense for where boxes may be!)
Licks and I then did Irish Tinkers Presidential Animals and The Actors Colony. We loved the history attached to both series and had a lot of fun. The boxes were all in great shape and in really great locations.
I finally reached my 200th find!!!
Thanks to everyone who plants boxes and givers opportunities to go to great places!!!
