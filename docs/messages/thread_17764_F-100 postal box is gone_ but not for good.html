F-100 postal box is gone, but not for good

From: Dog Scouts Troop (DogScouts@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-02-08 16:19:47 UTC-05:00
Well, it seems that the F-100 box is gone. I know who mailed it out prior to Christmas and who did not get it (that should have), so I will be carving a replica that will be sent to the place it was supposed to visit before going missing. This is an example of why the 'delivery confirmation' is such a good idea & it doesn't cost much. Having that number of the package allows the post office to see where it is/went. I have no control over how the postal boxes are mailed once they leave my home, but I do request that anyone sending off a box of my creation gets a delivery confirmation (& I'll do the same for any boxes I pass along). Look for this box to start it's rounds again soon!

If anyone is on the list and has moved in the past year or no longer wants to be on the list, just let me know.
Thanks,
Scoutdogs
