I found the boxcaching site to quite hilarious.

That being said, Jeremy is completely within his rights to request the removal of the logo and name as he is one of the owners of geocaching.com and they have a copyright on the logo and name.

One of the responsabilities that goes with obtaining a copyright is the enforcement of the copyright. Failure to do so may result in the copyright being withdrawn. There are posted usage guidelines on the geocaching.com site that indicate that the logo may be used on non-commercial pages as long as it portays geocaching.com in a positive light and is linked to the geocaching.com site.

While I personally don't see this portraying geocaching.com in a bad light, that is a decision that is up to the owner of the copyright. There is already bad blood between some members of both the letterboxing and geocaching communities and I would hate to see this spill over into a legal battle.
