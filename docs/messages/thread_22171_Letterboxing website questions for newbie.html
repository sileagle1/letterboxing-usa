Hi B-N-B,

Welcome to the addiction. While there are a number of letterboxers
that will probably post an answer here on this list, I would rather
refer you to a number of great sites where you can find your
answers. I tend to be from the point of view that will "teach you to
fish, rather than give you fish"

Here are a few sites you can check out and find most of the answers
to your questions.


Letterboxing.org:
http://www.letterboxing.org/faq/faq.html

Silent Doug's site:
http://www.letterboxing.info/
Doug probably has the best info site on Hitch hikers

AtlasQuest:
http://www.atlasquest.com/
Ryan has a link set up "Code of Conduct" that is really good for all
boxers to read

newboxers' group:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/
Where there is no dumb question, and is "the" place for many
newletterboxers.

>
> Also...is there a way/place I can read past comments from others who
> visited the letterbox?

There isn't a listing of comments like the geocaching site. There are
regional list as well as the newboxer list and this list.
