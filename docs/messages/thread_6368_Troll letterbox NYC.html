Troll Letterbox

NYC

 

Away in the Big Apple,

A green thumb you'll find.

Tucked under a bridge,

A troll that is kind.

 

A movie you must see,

An answer you'll get.

The trolls name is Stanley,

Can you guess where he sits?

 

A child you can ask,

His name they will know.

You must watch this movie,

Then you'll know where to go.

 

Film stock from '94,

Warner Brothers did make.

Go ask a child,

And his answer you'll take.

 
Placed on July 21, 2002
