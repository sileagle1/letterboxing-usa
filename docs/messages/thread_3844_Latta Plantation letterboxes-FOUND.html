Latta Plantation letterboxes-FOUND

From: Chip Cranfill (ccranfill@cadrawer.com) | Date: 2001-12-03 14:31:26 UTC-05:00
Both the Fleur-de-lis and the Noah's Ark letterboxes were found yesterday by the charter members of the yet to be formed, unofficial Davie County letterboxing club. These are North Carolina's two newest boxes. The Latta Plantation is a great place to spend a Sunday afternoon. With many riding and walking trails as well as the Carolina Raptor Center there is plenty to do. What a great idea using scripture to "fill in the blanks" in the clues. We can't wait to hit the trails again on our next boxing adventure. Everybody "stamp up".
