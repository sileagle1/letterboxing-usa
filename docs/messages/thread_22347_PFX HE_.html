My 2 cents:

H = Hitchhiker. (Many people do not include these in their "F" counts,
but instead maintain a separate count of these)

E = Event. A letterboxing gathering or event with a special event
stamp. (Some people only count actual events they have attended.
Other use their "E" count to include all traveling stamps that may be
at an event.)
