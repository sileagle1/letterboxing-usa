Directions:

The "Texas Jackalope" letterbox is located in Lockhart State Park,
close to Lockhart, Texas. From Lockhart and Hwy 183, go south on Hwy
20 for 1.7 miles to the park entrance on the left. After entrance
station, stay on main road toward "Recreation Hall', traveling up the
hill to the end of the road. Park near the Rec. Hall.
