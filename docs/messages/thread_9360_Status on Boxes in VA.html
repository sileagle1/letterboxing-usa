Status on Boxes in VA

From: Mary from Virginia (daughteroftheolddominion@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-11-25 15:24:12 UTC
This past Saturday morning I met up with The Knights of Columbo and
Witchiepoo for a little local letterboxing. Brrr, was it cold.

We were the first to log in to Bananaconda. It's a great stamp - ya
gotta see it.

We also found Mother Goose and a hitchhiker with an unusual name that
we took to be BannyrCai. Mother Goose had become dislodged from her
hiding place because of all the recent rain but we placed her back and
weighed her down with a rock. The stamp is a hand carved picture of
you know who. Witchiepoo will move the Hitchhiker. We also found, by
complete surprise, a geocache.

We then went over to Ghost Castle. We think we were the first to log
in there but the Unlucky Hitchhiker was already there. We don't know
who moved it or when because there was no log in. The Ghost Castle is
a nice stamp too - The Doubtful Guests have become pretty intricate in
their carvings. Knights of Columbo will move the Hitchhiker.

So in about 2 hours time we found 3 boxes at three different sites,
were the first to log into 2 of them, and found 2 hitchhikers.

Mary
