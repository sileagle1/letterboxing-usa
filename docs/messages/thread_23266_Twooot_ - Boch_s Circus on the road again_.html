Spring has sprung and so has Boch's Circus from winter hibernation.

I went down to the train station yesterday. Amidst the excitement of
the busy platform I waved "bon show" to the departing circus train.
The ears of the Dancing Dog flapped in the wind as it hung out the
window. Boch's Traveling Circus and Wild Menagerie is back on the
road for a new season of regaling an appreciative audience with some
of the finest impressions of physical entertainment from around the
country. ["And more blather from his Ringmaster
