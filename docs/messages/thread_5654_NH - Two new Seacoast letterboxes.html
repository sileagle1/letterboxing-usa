Hi,
 
Just in time for this weekend (I hope), I've posted the clues to my two Seacoast letterboxes. 
 
One is actually on the trail to the drewclan box at Odiorne Point Science Center's park.  After finding my first one, be certain to continue up the trail to the drewclan box, which contains a lovely stamp and the views from its hiding spot are magnificent!
 
The second letterbox I've planted is at the Urban Forestry Center in Portsmouth.  It's a lovely place to hike and learn more about Nature, so be sure to pick up the guide booklets that are free at the parking lot and in a mailbox at trailside as you cross the meadows.   There is more than one booklet, so you'll learn more if you pick up one of each.
 
All of my letterboxes contain handcarved stamps, btw. 
 
For my clues, see this page at my website:
http://www.aisling.net/lb/NH/seacoastclues.htm
