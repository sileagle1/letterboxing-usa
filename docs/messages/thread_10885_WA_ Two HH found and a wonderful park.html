WA: Two HH found and a wonderful park

From: hoopoe4me <scribenbirder@msn.com> (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-02-22 20:33:18 UTC
I paid a visit to "Dancing at Bougival" in Lacey WA. First and
foremost, a wonderful box. Stamp well done! Poem, a stunning
tribute to the Head Nails one True Love , Happy Mommy.

And what an interesting park. I sort of fell off the clues so busy
looking at interesting trees growing in an interesting fashion. I
had to wonder if one area of the park had some standing flood water
at one time. Many trees that obviously grew out of nurse stumps, but
no stumps to be seen. Just not something I have seen much in this
region.

Anyway got back on course to the tune of many Spring-ing birds.

The box is a gem and inside it was heavy with TWO Hitch hikers. One
by Head Nail himself is a mini version of his Dancing at Bougival, so
it has hit the road so others may see this great stamp and read the
lovely poem.

The other HH was "The Lost Hiker" HH by Eliza B the Modern Gypsy.

Thanks heaven for a sunshiny day. The stamp is elaborate and
required some coloring at the handy lunch table.

Cheers to all.

Hoopoe
Federal Way WA
