Re: Letterboxing Pub Crawl.

From: Integrity Homes NW (integrityhms@verizon.net) | Date: 2005-12-02 00:33:12 UTC-08:00
Funny this should become a topic.
I planted a box in a TexMex Sports bar a couple of months ago in the
damp Pacific NW.
Not because I'm big into bars but because this place has great food,
service and a fun atmosphere. They have a separate area that is kid
friendly with Xbox and big screen TVs, etc.
I enjoy letterboxing because it's fun to figure out the clues and I
discover many places I wouldn't have whether they are in doors or out.
So I hope you all keep planting boxes even in doors if you are so
inspired. Another part of the beauty of this hobby is that everyone can
choose to hunt the boxes/clues that look interesting to them.
Happy Hunting!
LadyB
