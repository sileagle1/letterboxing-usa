After taking my grandsons to the BMX track, we headed to Naugatuck to snag the What's the Scoop? Ice Cream box. Mountain Girl (she'll always be Just Beth to me) has done a stupendous job making this a unigue ice cream parlor. Even the boys reveled in the decor. When you get hot and tired this summer or you're just passing through, stop in to see this place and get the box. You won't be disappointed.
