Family Fun Recruit

From: eriesistiblemom (bonniese@mail2mom.com) | Date: 2003-08-03 20:38:31 UTC
Our family ventured out yesterday for our first ever LetterBoxing
excursion. I squealed in excitement when I spied the container but
let my son retrieve the sought after prize. Mom was more excited to
look through the contents of the box than the kids (6 and 4) who were
digging in the sand because we were at the beach. It was our first
hunt and to our amazement we were the first to log into the journal
of the Presque Isle Lighthouse in Erie, PA. Being somewhat of a
scrapbook enthusiast too, DH snapped our picture in front of the
lighthouse to put in our new LB/Stamp journal.

Happy trails, ERIEsistibleMOM
