David et al,

Beth and I just found and stamped in your box at The Covered Bridge
on August 14th! It is true the road is under construction and they
have literally moved the entire old bridge from its location over the
river to the side of the road, but despite this we were still able to
follow your clues as written and find the box. I rehid the box a bit
better as it was partially in view from above.

In our opinion, the stamp you carved for this letterbox is WELL worth
any additional effort as it is quite a beautiful stamp! For those
who wish to add this great stamp to their collection, we suggest you
visit the site on a weekend when the workers are not there, as we did
which worked out quite nicely. There are no tresspassing signs near
the heavy machinery, but you can easily get to the box without having
to go near any machinery.

Also, there are 2 other nearby boxes also provided by David that will
have you signing out old favorite tunes! It was 100 degrees and so
humid out you could cut the air with a knife when we found these
boxes, so don't let the weather or a little construction keep you out.

Best wishes,

Mark & Beth Bendickson
Hillsboro, Oregon
