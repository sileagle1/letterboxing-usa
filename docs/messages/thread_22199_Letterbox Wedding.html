Ah, a letterbox wedding. Let's help them plan it.

First, the location of the wedding and/or the reception must be a
mystery. Your invitation will have the clues.

Second, colors have to be the LbNA colors or your regional colors if
they are different.

Third, instead of a guest book, everybody gets to stamp in to the
event logbook. And they need to use their trail name, of course.

What else?
