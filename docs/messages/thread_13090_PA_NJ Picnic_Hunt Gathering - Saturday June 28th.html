One month to go! Plan on arriving around 11 am. A full kettle of
veggie chili will be waiting for you. For those not familiar with
the area, the Moravian Pottery & Tile Works is located on Route 313

the 611 by-pass). Plenty of parking. We will be in the picnic area in
back of the Pottery Works. E-mail Ed if you need additional
directions. Look for the LbNA sign.

A new stamp has been carved for the event, and new box planted on the
site (this makes three). Bring your own goodies and drinks. (sorry!
no alcohol - county park rule).

We have ordered sunshine for the event! :o)

It would he helpful to hear from those who plan to attend so enough

so someone will be there early to lay a claim.

NOTE: If it's raining buckets, there is some limited shelter by the
Pottery Works; however bring your umbrellas and ponchos anyway.)
