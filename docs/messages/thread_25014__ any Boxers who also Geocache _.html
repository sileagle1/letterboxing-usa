From: zess the treehuggers (zess.devine@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-03-13 08:06:18 UTC-04:00
If they are active on the boards you may be able to find them from that
member list (premium membership not required). Try here:
http://forums.groundspeak.com/GC/index.php?s=b7f39f1b87374578ee90311a2d38cab8&act=Members

If that link doesn't work, go to geocaching. com, sign in, and then in the
left column click on "Forums." This will take you to the Groundspeak forum
site. In the light blue bar that spans the screen under the title, on the
right side, you will see "Members" as one choice.

Good luck! I hope you are able to make contact.
