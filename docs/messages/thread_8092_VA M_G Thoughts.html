From: mlgainey (mlgainey@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-01 01:11:04 UTC
What else is there to say? Everything has been wonderfully and
amusingly stated already by other attendees.

For me personally, it was one of the best weekends I've had in a long
time. I have lost count of the number of times I have pulled out my
now-bulging logbook and leafed through the stamped pages. Everyone was
so cool and friendly. And Mother Nature looked kindly on us after all.

It's official. Rivah City is a fab place for letterboxing now! I think
we should alter the official city slogan from "Historic Richmond
Region: Easy to Love" to "Historic Richmond Region: Easy to
Letterbox"! I mean, we did that with the official state slogan on an
HH: "Virginia Is for Lovers" became "Virginia Is for Lovers. And
Boxers."

Again, much props to those who organized and attended in both body and
spirit.
