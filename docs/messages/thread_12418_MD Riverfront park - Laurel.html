Just a note to Chessie and the Mobots that I have returned the third Easter
series box to it's original position. So everyting is back the way it should
be!

The stamps are so fun. I did all nine boxes in the park today. The Dam
Trail series stamps are wonderful! And I really enjoyed the hike too. I was
wishing that I had brought my bike for the Easter series. That's a nice
trail. I'll have to go back.

People searching for these boxes should be VERY careful of poison ivy. It is
everywhere. Especially the last box of the Easter series - the tree that the
box is hidden in is literally COVERED in poison ivy. Of course, I wasn't
really paying attention when I found the box - I only noticed when I went to
put the box back. I am going to be very sad if I get poison ivy again! I'm
just recovering from a recent run-in with the evil plant!!

Thanks Chessie for some great boxes!
