Or, re: Unclaimed letterboxes

Thanks, Dragon, for adopting these letterboxes. Of the 7100
letterboxes on the LbNA website, there are still about 150 that have
not been claimed by their owners. If anyone is interested in seeing
a list of them organized by state, go to letterboxing.org and click
on Search For Boxes. Pull down the Placer list and click on
Unclaimed, and you'll see the lonley orphans. We've been culling
through them assigning them to their owners (you'd be surprised how
many people have signed up for a letterboxing account but have not
claimed their own boxes yet), but it's a big job. Anyone who wants
to adopt an unclaimed box can write us here at the big list or
directly at webmasters at letterboxing dot org.

Jay in CT
