Maine: Ogunquit/Freeport Area, and a good story

From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2003-10-20 01:50:03 UTC
The LLBLB, Nubble Light, and Goldeneye boxes were found safe, sound,
and in good condition by my non-letterboxing parents this weekend in
Maine. They wrote in the logs and I have the stamp images on the clue-
sheets here in CT now : )

I guess we can add an "S" to the PFX for images received as
Souveniers? LOL

A funny story about the Nubble Light box.... the first day they went
to try for it the waves were crashing up on shore really high so they
skipped it. The second time the York (I think thats the town) Fire
Department and Ambulance services were on scene for a technical
rescue of some vacationer that had fallen and torn her knee out on
the rocks. My dad (We're both firefighters and EMTs -- he's a
Lieutenant) got asked by a firefighter to assist in carrying the
stokes litter with the lady in it across the rocks and to the waiting
EMS unit. Kind of funny how those in the trade are always on duty!
