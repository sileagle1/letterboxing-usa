Congratulations to LBCNY!!!

From: Cyndie (lockwench@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-04 13:47:14 UTC
Just wanted to shout out to Letterboxing In Central NY group...a big
congratulations for their One Year Anniversary. (I even have the stamp
to prove it! lol) I came into this group just recently but have been
sooo impressed by the caliber of these boxers. Great boxes...great
people...and best of all they set a great example for new boxers. I
recently attended the One Year Anniversary Party hosted by Paul in SF
and his family and neighbors in Oriskany, NY. They made special boxes
for us but were generous enough to leave some in place to encourage
other boxers to visit the area. I highly recommend "The
-------, The Baker, The Candlestick Maker." Clever and lots of FUN!!

When I first checked boxes back in 1998-99...there were only 3 boxes in
my area. Through the efforts of LBCNY boxers, there are now over 150!!
The group continues to grow and hosts visiting boxers from all over the
country. I am proud to be a part of it.

Thanks to the founder, Paul in SF..he truly is an inspiration and a
generous bi-coastal boxer.
