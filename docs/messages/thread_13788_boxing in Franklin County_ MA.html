Hello all,

Binx and I went for a rainy camping weekend in Franklin County and
picked up the following boxes:

-Captain Tupperware
-Poet's Seat Tower #1 and #2 (alas, #3 is missing! We were
disappointed especially since the three stamps are supposed to form a
complete image.)
-High Ledges
-Dragonfly (replaced a leaky ziploc that the stamp was in and wrapped
the stamp in a paper towel. The log was dry.)
-Acrophobia
-North Mount Sugarloaf
-Pig Pen Ledges (what a cool place!)

The downpours on Sunday finally dampened our spirits and we headed
home a bit early but had a wonderful time. What a picturesque part of
the state! Thanks to all who placed these boxes.
