Happy Birthday Jay

From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2003-09-05 10:45:01 UTC
After doing a little research and web browsing we found out that
today is a special day for someone. This person has some of the best
carvings we've seen and has become a true family friend in the year
we've been letterboxing. Along with Patrick and Amy, Butterfly, and
Psychokiwis, Jay Drew has been extremely helpful in providing hints,
carvings, and general guidance as we explored southeastern CT. As
part of our grattitude for his help, we just wanted to openly send
Jay a birthday greeting and pass on a little nonsensical poem that
Steve, Heidi, Patrick, and Amy contrived : ) Enjoy! And have a fine
day Jay!

-S+H


"Have a righteous time making a new find. Red or Mauve, let it lay.
Yet, remote or close, keep your ears listening low over white. Take
off, go right around south southwest. You sure I got new boxes out? A
real doozy. His endeavor as Drew feeds our undying requests and
teaches new ways to overthink. Fore losing a trail, retrace over
certain knowledge."
