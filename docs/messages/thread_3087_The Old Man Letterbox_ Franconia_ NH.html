The Old Man Letterbox, Franconia, NH

From: Mark S. Fitton (mfitton@snet.net) | Date: 2001-08-13 21:45:54 UTC-04:00
The 4-hearted Fittons have planted a letterbox while vacationing in the
White Mountains. This is a good one for families with kids.

After you take in the southbound View of The Old Man, go back to the upper
parking lot.

Take the Pemi trail to your left.

Short walk.

Split tree on left. Stop at it.

Look to big rock on right, slightly off trail, with tree growing out of it.

Go left around the rock to near the back of it.

Look low, down into opening.

Stamp up and return.


Mark, of the 4-hearted Fittons
