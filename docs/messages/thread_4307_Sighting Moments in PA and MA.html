Sighting Moments in PA and MA

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-01-28 16:43:30 UTC
Ever say hi to fellow hikers on the trail? Then, when you reach the
next letterbox, realize the people to whom you just hi and the last to
sign in the letterbox log were one and the same? We have started
humorously calling such a missed opportunity for personal stamp
Exchange a "Sighting"!!!

We did indeed find "Moments to Celebrate" and felt it was an
appropriate place to celebrate after having recently completed an
inspiring "Dreamery" adventure! We also located a little bird in "Grey
Reservation" and "Seal With A Kiss" has hitched a ride to RI with us.

We were not so lucky searching for "Whale of a Time" in Garfield Woods
(Berlin, MA). Its big old cave seemed bare and empty. Perhaps if the
water level had been lower and the Sun hadn't been performing its daily
disappearing act, a more thorough search would have yielded results.

Thanks to the MA folks for a great day out and hope to meet some of
them at one of the upcoming gatherings.

Wanda and Pete
