From: joie christensen (see_joy29@hotmail.com) | Date: 2001-10-28 18:34:43 UTC-05:00
Hope everyone enjoyed this beautiful fall weekend!
Today Floating Feather and I took a trip up into the Poconos and to Bushkill
Falls.
We were on the hunt for the letterbox planted there and are pretty certain
we found the spot, but alas, not the box. The spot is very close to the
"trail" and we checked out what we thought were all possiblities...
Anyway, a beautiful location and well worth the $8.- to get onto the trails,
once you get in and "on board",you understand and appreciate the fee. Even
the traffic on the way home was worth it, tho, I would suggest when you
go,if on a weekend, to return from Bushkill Falls via something other than
Rt.206 south, north is ok, but south is a crawl at best.
We decided to plant a box of our own since we had come up stampless and the
clues will follow soon.
Has anyone found the Bushkill box in the past few months? Would be thankfull
for any response.
'Box On!! See Joy
