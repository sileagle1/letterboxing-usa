The following letterbox was placed Saturday, 4/26/03 in Holly Springs,
NC (Wake County - 12 miles southwest of Raleigh) in Parrish Womble Park.
Take either US-1 or 401 to Hwy. 55 to Holly Springs. Look for signs to
Parrish Womble park & sports complex.
------------------------------------------------------------------------

In the Town of Holly Springs,
At Parrish Womble Park,
Where the kiddies play ball
And the pups run and bark...

Between the diamonds and the lake
Sits a small concrete "house,"
With a door on the side
That is fit for a mouse.

Inside, to the left
Sits a box full of cheese.
Make your mark, take a print,
Leave a note if you please!!!

Enjoy!
