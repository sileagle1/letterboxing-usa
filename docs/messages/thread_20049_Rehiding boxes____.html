Rehiding boxes....

From: Steve (boxdn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-24 01:08:46 UTC
Not a rant just an obseveration.

The past two weeks I've been all over our area hutning and checking
and out of the twelve boxes hunted two were missing and six were
hardly covered. The two missing ones had been bulldozed and
ironicly they were 150 miles apart. The hardly covered ones, a few
of them mine, started to give "trail rage". After stamping in I
noticed the last stampers were all different. Could everyone be
doing a bad job rehiding? Last night watching a thunderstorm roll
through I obsevered how the recently cut grass clippings were blown
around and then washed away by the rain. I could visulize a box
covered by only pine straw or leaves being uncovered naturally.
Could the one responsible for leaving our boxes out in the open be
Mother Nature herself?
