Title: A Loon Alone. Placed by Salamander on 3-31-03. Location:
Yoctangee Park, Chillicothe, Ross County, Ohio.*** This is a
microbox; please bring pen and stamppad. Difficulty: Easy, if you
find the correct wall. CLUES: Go to Yoctangee Park. You must find a
stone wall somewhere near the park lake.Stand facing the wall. Look
about 6 feet in towards the center from the right side of the wall,
and about 6 inches up from the ground. The lone loon is hiding in a
crevice behind a clump of wild onions.
