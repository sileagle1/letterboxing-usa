The meaning of "X"

From: (mirkwood3@aol.com) | Date: 2001-03-02 20:34:45 UTC
X is when you run across another Letterboxer on the trail (or
wherever) and you swap personal stamps (well, the images, NOT the
stamps!). For example, last week my siblings and I all met up and
placed a memorial box in Florida, and we each stamped our personal
stamp in the others' journals.
