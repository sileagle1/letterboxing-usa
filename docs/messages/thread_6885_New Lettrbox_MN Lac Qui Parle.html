Lac Qui Parle Letterbox
Western Chippewa County, near Montevideo, MN
Placed by Dunkproductions.

Find the historic Lac Qui Parle Mission Site. It is
about 1/2 mile east of Lac Qui Prarle State Park. The
mission and park are separated by the county line
between Lac Qui Parle County (park) and Chippewa
County (mission). When facing the mission house, look
to your right and find the walking path leading up the
hill. Take this path to the Ferry Road site marker.
