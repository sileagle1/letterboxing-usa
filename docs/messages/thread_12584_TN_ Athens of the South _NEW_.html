Athens of the South Letterbox
Nashville, TN
Placed 3 May 2003
by A-Bear (jedaryn02@yahoo.com) & Quilting Noni

Directions: Take exit (209??) from I40 to
Broadway/70S. Head southwest on Broadway which
eventually turns into West End. Turn right onto 25th
Ave. Turn left on Park Plaza and then turn left into
Centennial Park and find a good spot to park near the
Parthenon.

Difficulty: Drive-by and use of a compass.

Clues: From the tunnel like entrance to the Parthenon
on the north side, head about 45 paces/90 steps at 20
degrees to the tree with many exposed feet across the
street. Up its leg on the northeast side rests the
Athens of the South Letterbox.

This is a busy place so try not to be detected as you
reach in for your letterbox find. Let me know how it
goes.
