MA Tobacco River update

From: defygravity2001 (defygravity@snet.net) | Date: 2003-04-18 23:37:34 UTC
Bruce & I did Tobacco River today. Let me say there is some deep,
COLD water on the very last section of the trail....knee-deep, in
fact (BURRR). But, the box is in good condition, last people there
was in August. We saw Eastern bluebirds, a red tail hawk, and a Canada
goose followed us a ways toward the box after the water-crossing, and
watched us stamp in.

If anyone is going to attempt this while it seems the water table is
high, email us, we discovered a dryer way (on the way out :( ).

~ Aili & Bruce
