From: (davyschris@aol.com) | Date: 2006-09-22 15:34:36 UTC-04:00
I was just in San Fran in April.

My Off to See the Wizard box is a short walk if you park near the
Conservatory of Flowers. Practically a drive-by.

There is a box on a certain island off the cost of San Fran as well... peruse
those clues!

The Ortus Depths one is also a short walk, if you figure it out first and
learn its location. I think the walk only took us about 10 minutes.
