From: rscarpen (letterboxing@atlasquest.com) | Date: 2004-09-07 00:22:22 UTC
Specific questions about Atlas Quest should be directed to me. =)
I've been on vacation and out of touch for the last several days, and
it's easy for me to miss a message on the talk list. If it wasn't
for the great title, I'd have missed it completely! (There's over
250 new message to wade through!)

> I have tried the contact us and received a reply but it was not
> helpful.

I do my best! Really! But as much as my reply may not have been as
helpful as you wanted, nobody else can fix these issues.

> Why would one box and not the other 41 record-not all one series.
> As well as 7 HH that won't record!

Could you e-mail (no reason to clog up this list) me the names of
some boxes you want to record as a find so I can try it too?
Sometimes, it helps to figure out a problem if I can recreate it.

> I have also noticed that someone-not Warrior Woman-is claiming to
> have planted the Blade Runner Series in Westerly, RI.

I can find no record of this series on Atlas Quest. Presumably, the
person who added it has deleted it.

Any boxes that are listed without the owner's permission will be
deleted. I cannot personally verify every letterbox that is entered,
so it's up to people like you to keep them honest. =) If you see
boxes listed that should not be, let me know. I'll investigate and
either delete the box if it shouldn't be listed, or leave it if the
author of the box okays it. Atlas Quest assumes any box you list was
created by the person who entered it--which may not be the case for
adopted boxes, so sometimes you might see a mismatched name but it's
still okay. (For the record, the website DOES distinguish between
the author of a letterbox and the owner of a letterbox, and I can
manually change one or the other as necessary. I'm working on an
interface so users can do that themselves, however. So for those of
you who have adopted boxes listed on AQ, let me know and I can change
the author to the proper person--or to nobody at all if the user
doesn't have an account.)

Hope I've helped a bit more this time, but if not, please e-mail me.
There's not much anyone else can do to help, as I'm the sole creator
of the website.

Happy trails!

-- Ryan
