From: Steve (shol3039@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-19 00:50:48 UTC
Wow we had a long day! Started late... at 1:30 ish with the Hoffman
Evergreen series. Great hike! One of our new favorites actually! We
provided a little maintanence on some of Jay's boxes as far as
replacing ripped baggies and damp paper towels. All the log books
in all 6 boxes are full/near full and we're having to stamp on the
flip side of pages.... so if you owners havetime to place #2 books,
It would be a good idea!

We tried to go do all the DPNC boxes and arbitrarily picked the DPNC
#2 series to start with. LONG hike but incredible stamps!!!! Use
caution if you choose to try these, as 1) there are 2 places to turn
left onto the white trail from the red trail's long loop.... and 2)
the yellow trail going to the power lines, along with the orange
coming back from the powerlins are now WHITE! Rumor is there's a
new map being printed and it should be up soon, but definately get a
map at the Nature center and USE it!

We finished the series at 6:30 and did not have the gusto to even
think about the DPNC #1 series or Judi's boxes in that area. Also
wanted to go to Captain's Walk but we were toooo tired. Probably did
~ 6 miles today, so the hot tub got some use after dinner!!!

Thanks to Melissa and Dan, The Drews, and the Kurdziel's for great
spots!

-Steve, Heidi, and Madras
