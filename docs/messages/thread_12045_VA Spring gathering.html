Okay, all interested parties! I went so slowly on this and I haven't said a
thing about it lately and I'm very, very sorry and wish to most earnestly beg
all your pardon, but I want to announce that the PA/DE/MD/DC/VA/WV/NC/SC and
anyone else who wants to come spring 'boxing gathering will be at Riverbend
Park in Fairfax Co. VA (www.co.fairfax.va.us/parks/nature.htm) on Saturday,
May 31, exact location in the park to be announced. We will start very
informally at 10:00 a.m. and go until everyone is ready to be done or the
park closes, whichever comes first. 'Boxers of all ages and species welcome!

Persons willing and able to do boxes for this gathering, an event stamp and
anyone willing to speak on a related subject or show others how to do
something are urgently solicited. This event is potluck, so if you can bring
something please let me know and anyone RSVP ASAP.

Merci! Mother of Crickets

From: (gbecket@aol.com) | Date: 2003-04-18 06:59:37 UTC-04:00

Yay, Bema! It will be great to meet you all! Yes, trailer trash macaroni
salad sounds awesome and I'm sure many people will want it. That name gives
me a particular mental image picture of the salad and we'll see if it's the
same. I hope the Happy Homeschoolers will also come and lots of people with
kids. I've met a few other boxers only in person and none with children and
I'm eager to meet some other families and compare fun-creating ideas for us
all.

I don't know what you put in your salad but if it has mayo, I wanted to give
you an idea: last summer I went to a barbecue with some southern potato salad
I made which included mayonnaise in the recipe and people got sick as it was
sitting out on the picnic table for hours so I now use Nayonnaise, which is a
soy-based "mayo" and doesn't include the raw egg. I love potato salad and
macaroni salad and that seemed to make it safer for outdoor gatherings, which
to my mind must include potato and macaroni salad.
