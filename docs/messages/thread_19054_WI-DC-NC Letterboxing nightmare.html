WI-DC-NC Letterboxing nightmare

From: Volks Boxer (volksboxer@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-06-04 07:57:32 UTC-04:00
Woke up in a cold sweat.

Last night I dreamt that two large groups of people, children as well as
adults, were letterboxing in a locale that was downtown Milwaukee, the
Smithsonian grounds, and the Biltmore Estate all rolled into one. Many of
the letterboxes were hidden in fake rocks.

Everytime they found a box, they taped a note to the top of it that read 'WE
FOUND THE LETTERBOX!" After I discovered the first note, I tried to remove
all the notes and find the boxers to tell them they were missing the point
entirely. They also were leaving boxes, but each box was identical - a
bright white round hollow plastic rock embossed on top with a crescent moon
and stars.

Lucy (Mountain Scorpia)

Oh thank god I am not the only one who dreams about letterboxes.
I had one last week, it went like this.
After a grueling 2 hour hike, uphill, in the snow I finally found

sandwich in it. After such a long hike, I was hungry but, did not
have any milk. It was horrifying.

Then I woke up, and went downstairs to make a real one. All's well
that ends well!!
