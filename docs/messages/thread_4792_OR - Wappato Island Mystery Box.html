The Forest People trekked to the Wappato Island Mystery Box (recently
placed by Ryan) yesterday, and snagged the box, hot on the heels of the
Traveling Circus. This was a great stamp, in a great location. Even
though we didn't have multicolor pens with us, we were able to take
advantage of the coloring job of the previous stampers and got a
beautiful multicolor image.

We will have to go back again and hike around the area and explore more
of the island.
