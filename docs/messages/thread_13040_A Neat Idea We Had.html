I planted a box on private property on a farm with a really neat
country barn gift shop (Peace on Earth). At first, the owner and I
were going to do the password thing, but that was limiting because
sometimes he was out in the fields and keeping his staff up to date
would be too much. Also, the farm closes for the winter which would
restrict access. We ended up placing it on the grounds of the farm
and let the caretaker know the location of the box so when folks went
into the property off season, he'd know why.

According to the log book, folks enjoy finding the box during the
winter (it is safe from the elements and is a hitchhiker magnet) and
often return during the regular season to shop and enjoy.

It's a mutually beneficial proposition for both you and the shop
owner. Best of luck to you!
