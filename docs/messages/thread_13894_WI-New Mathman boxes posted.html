Mathman 2, 3, and 5 boxes have now been posted! (Mathman 4 should be ready
soon!) For those finding the Mathman series challenging, please note that
Mathman 3 is an Elementary level one (good for kids too).

In response to those who want the answers to the clues, I'll refer you to my
new policy posted on the updated Mathman site
(http://home.wi.rr.com/dragonlber/mathman_series.htm):

"Letterboxers are welcome to find these boxes if they're up to the
challenge! Feel free to email me at tkuehl@wi.rr.com with any math
questions, or if you want me to check your answers! Remember that many
letterboxes are mentally challenging, so please at least try the problems
before asking for help! ; - ) The math teacher in me will only allow me to
give the answer keys in response to an attempt at the math. Also, look for
some links to sites that may help you with the math."
