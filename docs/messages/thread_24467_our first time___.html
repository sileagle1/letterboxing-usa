our first time...

From: Shawna Farley (shawna@shawnawrites.com) | Date: 2007-07-23 02:33:26 UTC-04:00

Hi, we are the treehuggers_lbna Our kids kind of voted on the name... and well today we had our first time and first find. We recorded everything at atlasquest... and we made a family page to log stuff. Anyhow, won't give all details but, if you want to know (I did not give any clues away) our new page is at http://www.myspace.com/treehuggers_lbna I am thinking that not only will we have our journal books, but, we will have the log for other boxers to look at. I put on there how we came about our name, who got us started, and last...our first attempt and adventure which was today/yesterday (I am up late) We had a blast... kids loved it. My son cleaned up the area from litter too. LOL I didn't ask him to...he just did. I took pictures, my daughter picked up special rocks, and my other daughter walked my golden retriever and allowed him to meet friends and sniff a couple dog butts...it was great for her being at the park because there were painters and she is a painter... last my husband LOVED IT TOO. He packed the lunch and many other things lol. Great family day.

I'm happy to say our first attempt we found the box. It did help that we kind of already knew the park and it has a very special meaning for us anyhow. The funny part was we picked this because going to a park is free...but it just so happens that today was a motor boat race soooo... they had a 5.00 fee...thankfully if we walked to the park it was no charge.

This was the Elizabeth Bridge box and in Trenton Michigan (yes the letterboxer gives this info away anyhow.)

Our next thing is to try and make a stamp.
