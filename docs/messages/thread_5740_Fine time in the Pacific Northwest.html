Just wanted to thank all those Pacific Northwest letterboxers
whose interesting plantings added so much to our recent trip out
there. We found time for boxes by Amanda A, Amanda B,
Funhog, Mitch, Ryan, Gypsy, Mischief, Bill, Buckaroo and quite a

Flies when your having fun and just don't seem to know where
the time went!

Thanks again, guys, for all the fun!
