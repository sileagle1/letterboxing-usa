My son and I went hiking at Cape Henlopen this past
Friday. We found the Lighthouse series all in good
shape. When I got back to the car I found the bag for
the log book of #4 in my back pack! I'm going to try
to get back out there in the next few days, if any one

other boxes listed, all were OK. I thought there was
one more box out on the point but the clues are not
listed anymore. Did it go missing? I had tried to
find it once before and came up empty. I'm not sure
if I held onto the clues or just thought I'd look them
up, but now they are gone.
