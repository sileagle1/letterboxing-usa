RI: boxes found in Barrington, Warren, Bristol

From: cscm88 (cscm@toast.net) | Date: 2003-04-14 14:00:08 UTC
Rustypuff and I went box-crazy in Rhode Island's East bay yesterday.
After a winter of crappy weather, it was great to get out in the sun
again.

Nockum Hill Turtle in Barrington was found in place, dry, and intact.

Meander to the Marsh: Great Blue Heron and Red Fox in Warren were
found in place, dry, and intact.

The World Needs Peace in Bristol was found in place, dry, and
intact. A washed-up beam was the perfect place for a picnic by the
bay.

East Bay Bike Path Series I: Chair Wheeler, Coffee Break, Roller
Blader, Tired Cyclist, and Walker were all found in place. The box
for Roller Blader was damaged and full of water, so we replaced the
box and bags. The log was a little wet, but not bad. The other four
boxes were dry and intact.

Thanks to all!
