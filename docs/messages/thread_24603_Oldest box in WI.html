Oldest box in WI

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2007-09-26 13:15:28 UTC
Just wanted to mention that today is the 9th anniversary of the oldest
box in Wisconsin! The original placer (a "Steve") planted it on
September 26, 1998. He is no longer in the game, but "The Dragon"
adopted the box several years ago (as he did with several other older
boxes in Wisconsin).

The ORIGINAL logbook is still in the box, and 2 other logbooks have
been added to the box over time. The original custome-made stamp is
also in the box. Over the course of 9 years, 80 different
individuals/groups have signed into the logbooks.

I know this isn't the absolute OLDEST box in the USA, but I think it is
one of the older ones. Can anyone provide info on what other boxes are
older than this one?

Wisconsin Hiker
