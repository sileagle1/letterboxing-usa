The Antietam Bridge Box in Fredericksburg has been pulled for
relocation. I checked on it yesterday, and it was out in the open and
scattered. The stamp and logbook were there, so I will relocate it in
the same park. A new hiking trail has been recently opened in the
park. It will be in a more out of the way spot. This is an unclaimed
box. If you are the placer, please get in touch with me. Thanks.

closed. The recent hurricane wiped out the bridges on the trail, and
they think it will take a year to replace them.

Just wanted to let people know.
