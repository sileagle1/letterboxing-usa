WILD EYED THINGS THAT PEEK THROUGH THE LIMBS MYSTERY LETTERBOX

CLUE DIFFICULTY: We just think we are so clever :)

(If you find any problems with this box or wish to share any successes, etc.,
please email Annette at ARoseLb@aol.com.)

CLUE:
Just feel lucky that you aren't one of the Wild Eyed things that peek through
the limbs of those four fir trees over there. Imagine, every time a baseball
got hit over the left field fence, your life would be in danger! The
fountain is nice, but those crazy fisherman could start casting, the hook
might go hither and thither, landing under the branches and hooking onto
something, er, personal, if ya know what I mean. I like the volleyball
courts too. Its just that those volleyball players miss sometimes and the
volleyball rolls into the fir trees, messing up your home. Let's just say, a
Wild Eyed thing has the right to a safe home, just like any other person.
The worst thing is that compass! That W points at my Wild Eyed friends --
You might as well just label it Wild Eyed Things over there and have done
with it. At least living under the tallest of the four firs holds some extra
comfort.
