letterboxing in Arizona

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2002-02-11 18:34:26 UTC
I have just returned to the cold, foggy Northwest from warm, cloudless
Arizona. I am pleased to report that the South Mountain Park box
(Phoenix) and the Summer Storms box (Tucson) are both alive and well.
Furthermore, I took the opportunity to avoid working for a couple of
days while I was there to increase the box count for the state by three
while I was at it. Anyone travelling to AZ will enjoy the lovely places
that are home to Arizoa letterboxes. Funhog
