Re: [LbNA] Tracking down some Hitchhikers

From: Poison Ivy (poizniv@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-10-20 19:28:15 UTC
Trailer Hitch - passed in person to another letterboxer - similar to
a Cootie, but doesn't have to be clandestine.

Cooties are placed on a boxer's person or in a boxer's things without
them knowing.
