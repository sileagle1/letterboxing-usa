As we promised the Psychokiwis, we have placed a box they carved
called "I love ice cream!". For those of you living under rocks

native land of New Zealand. As a last favor from us, Judi asked me to

treats once the leaves turn green again.

for a lovely stop over when collecting stamps from Walden Pond,
Hartman Park, Devil's Hopyard, etc etc etc in the summer!
