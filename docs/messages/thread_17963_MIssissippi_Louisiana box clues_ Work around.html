To answer your question, yes there are. A friend of mine just
found Silver Eagle's Mudbug box and had a report of the
condition of the box. I advised him to contact the placer. We
looked up the box and it wasn't there. Here's the work around: Go
to Search for boxes and then search by state. It will still let you
access the clues that aren't displayed on the site, for whatever
reason. Silver Eagle responded within minutes and all is well.

Hope this is beneficial to ya,
Mark
