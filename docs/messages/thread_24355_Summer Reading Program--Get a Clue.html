Summer Reading Program--Get a Clue

From: walkaboutletterboxer (walkabout@columbus.rr.com) | Date: 2007-06-06 13:47:33 UTC
Lots of public libraries coordinate their Summer Reading Programs
through a library consortium called the Collaborative Summer Reading
Program. (Sharing resources and reducing redundancy in this fashion
reduces each library's costs.)

This year's CSRP Summer Reading Program is "Get a Clue." Ideas that
local libraries may be following can be found at this link:
http://www.cslpreads.org/2007/cp07.htm.

I'm posting this on this letterboxing listserv in the off chance some
of you would like to contact your local children's librarian to offer
to host a letterbox, a letterbox hunt, or a program. This summer is
the perfect opportunity to do so. (In my case, a local librarian
called me about the Get a Clue program when she discovered I'd planted
the Wagnalls letterbox near her library.)
