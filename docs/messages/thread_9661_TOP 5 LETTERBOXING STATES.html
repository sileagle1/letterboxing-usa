This was interesting to read! Of course, Connecticut is tops! They
deserve that honor, half-baked pie or not, per Ryan's post.

I can't believe Oregon has so many now. Just a couple years ago Der Mad
Stamper and Thom Cheney's letterboxes were all that were happening.
They were the spark that made it happen out here! I hid some boxes here
and there...Then along came the amazing Ryan and Funhog. Not to mention
others who have graced our presence with sprinklings of their beautiful
boxes!

The one thing about Oregon is that it's so perfect for this hobby! And
so often we have gone out searching for letterboxes and if it weren't
for letterboxing, we might not know some of these places exist or if we
knew they existed, may not necessarily have bothered to make a focused
effort to check them out.

I don't care about the numbers, but I do love it that I live in a
wonderful state where there are lots of beautiful places to go and so
many letterboxes to seek. This sport has really come alive in the
Pacific NW! Thanks to all who contribute!!!
