N.J./PA get together March 2nd

From: joie christensen (see_joy29@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-02-25 14:49:29 UTC-05:00
Hi All!
This is what Floating Feather, Lockmaster and I plan on doing March 2nd. We
will meet Pete and Wanda in Easton, PA. at the "Fish Passage way" at the old
Canal Museum off Rt.611 at 4pm to meet, greet and exchange stamps. If the
weather is awfull we will meet in the lobby of "The Best Western" just
around the corner from that location where they are staying in town at the
same time.
We hope that many other 'boxers will be willing to do the same. Looks like
Muk-Luk, Mary and Priscilla and the Borzoilady will be there too.
Afterwards our contingent of three will head downriver on Rt. 611 approx. 5
miles to Raubsville and to the Raubsville Inn along the Delaware River.
There we will get a bite to eat and share letterboxing stories with all
comers!!!
Hope to see lots of folks there!
Could we start a list of people who are planning on coming to meet Pete and
Wanda at 4pm?
See you soon!
See Joy
