Eric,

I usually find all can use at local camera stores. A couple have a box
behind the counter with a bunch. If your area one's don't, then you might
order some bulk from a science supply company like Carolina Biological or
Forestry Suppliers and just sprinkle the beads in the bottom of the box or
ziplock bag. They turn pink when saturated and can be roasted back to health.
