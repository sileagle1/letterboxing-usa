Wet Weather Loop
An easy stroll placed by John and Stephani on 12/29/01.

CLUES: Along one of the many blue trails in Eastern CT take a left onto the
Wet Weather Loop. You will soon pass a large but fallen bird house on your
right and a small but intact tree stand on your left. In a short while you
will cross a foot bridge. From the end of the bridge sight a large but
leaning three trunk tree. From the base of the tree look to the stone wall
at due East. Your prize lies in the far side of the wall under the large
chunk o' quartz.
