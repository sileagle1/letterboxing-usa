And yea, verily they did walk into the valley and down the path and
past the large birch tree just as the Great and Wonderful Creator had
instructed. And though they were sorely tested and their bodies were
much abused by the trip, they found themselves rich at the end of
their travails. For in the grey rock's nook they didst find the Carving
in rubber lying next to the Logbook, both enclosed in the Tupperware of
Safety and camouflaged by the Holy Detritus. And Lo, they didst have
upon them the Sacred Colors and were thus rewarded with the ability to
collect the Inverse Image they had so dutifully sought.

Blessed are the Box Planters for they shall be rewarded with Signatures
of the Seekers by the multitudes.
