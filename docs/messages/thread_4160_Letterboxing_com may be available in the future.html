From: Eric Mings (elm@letterboxing.com) | Date: 2002-01-11 23:52:30 UTC-05:00
Hey all,

I have decided I do not have time to maintain the Letterboxing
International site (letterboxing.com). I am way too busy with other
things and don't have the time or energy to keep it up to date. I am
willing to consider turning over the domain name free of charge at
renewal time (this spring I think)to some other persons who would
like to maintain a letterboxing related site. There are only a couple
of firm requirements that I would need to do that. The site must
remain noncommercial, must be completely independent of
letterboxing.org site, and should be developed by the consensus of
all members who wish to participate in it's development. If any group
of people is interested you can contact me directly. I am _not_
guaranteeing I will turn it over to just anyone. It would have to be
a situation that I feel comfortable with. Please contact me off list
for any questions rather than burdening the main list with such
discussion. Thanks.
