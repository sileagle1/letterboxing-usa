All this talk about minding your P's and F's got me thinking. How can I
muddle the issue more? :-) (like I said. I'm interested in this stuff ...).

If you find a beldin box, you can change something -- its stamp, its clues
or its location (if you change the location, you usually change the clues
also). You just can't change everthing at once. So it could move around
like a hitchhiker, but be a static box. It never lives in a box unless it
morphs into a HH and the clues say it does. Or it could just sit there and
gather water, like most of my boxes. :-)

In any case, I created such a box at http://www.mapsurfer.com/boxes/box44.html

Its current location is French Creek State Park in southeast (Berks Co) PA.
If you find it, you can change something about it if you want to. More details
are on the clue page.
