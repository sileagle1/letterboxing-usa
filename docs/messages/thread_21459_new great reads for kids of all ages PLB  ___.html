The snowy mountains of Colorado mixed with some serious letterboxing cabin fever prompted me to think about starting up a new postal letterboxing ring. I thought that it would be great fun to pick out some of our favorite kids books and pass them around with a stamp. We could all enjoy a new read or two (little kid or BIG kid) and maybe find a new favorite that we didn't even know was out there.

I have dibs on Where the WIld Things are and another one called Whisper in the Woods.

If you would like to participate in this ring email me at jodiemkern@yahoo.com. I will take responses for a couple of weeks and then post a list with those that replied and we can get started.

Thanks! Jodie
