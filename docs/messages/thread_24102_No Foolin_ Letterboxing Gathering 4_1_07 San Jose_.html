This event is posted on Atlas Quest too, but wanted send another
broadcast announcement with updates!

We already have more than 40 folks signed up to attend!!

Successfully recruited several travelling event stamps (and the
groveling has just begun!).

Rumor has it some of our attendees are carving new stamps for this
event, but I don't want to leak names just yet.....but these will

lettering. I don't think it will disappoint.

Food provided (just bring a dessert to share), lots of good
letterboxes nearby, playground adjacent to our covered picnic area,
wheelchair accessible--all in sunny San Jose. Free parking. No
dogs.

Please check out our listing and consider making the drive! Should
be a fun day of letterboxes with some surprises.
