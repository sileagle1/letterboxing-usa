Okay, I found out later that it didn't burn down, but there was a bad
fire at the post office. From what I hear some guy that was not
entirely with it decided he needed to pour gasoline down the mail
slots inside the local post office and then set a match to it. I've
been told that a lot of the mail in my town was lost. Several days
later, they did get the office up again, but the thought occured to
me that I better check to see if anyone sent postal boxes.

If anyone sent me anything recently and has not heard from me yet,
please write so that I can look out for it and confirm delivery.
