HI RoseWood, Butterfly & Ying Yang!

Finally took the my kids and the 3 neighbor girls out and FOUND your
Jefferson Barracks Box. We thought the stamp was well chosen for the spot!
Of course there was too much walking involved as far as one or tow of them
was concerned! :o)

We did make the mistake of following the Grant Street in the National Cem.
first ... which also had a very scenic Circle Drive, a maintenance garage, a
pine tree lane, a view of the Mississippi & a rock wall! The clues just
didn't fit in the right place. We almost gave up, thinking (of course) the
problem was with the clue writer not the clue readers.

But alas, one more try - we head north think the park (duh) might have a
Grant Street also. We pulled in at the Cannon Gate with the rifle barrel
fence to let the kids play and almost quit for the day when I noticed the
street signs...we were in the right place! I scared my kids when I stopped
the van. :o)

We thought we would be the first there but NANCY DREW had stamped in as had a
Letterboxing family just a few days later!

Thanks for the fun. :o)
