From: forestfrogs (forestfrogs@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-02 02:31:11 UTC
Hey, everyone - just a quick note to let those of you who knew of
his impending arrival that our newest forest frog, Owen William
Seftor, made his entrance on Thursday, May 22nd at 12:30 pm (a full
two weeks late!) If you'd like to see pictures, you can check out
http://www.thatsmybaby.net/home/nursery.asp and enter the code 064-
15456, or visit http://seftor.com/owen

We can't wait to get him out on the trails with us, once I heal and
the rain stops falling! I've really missed letterboxing these past
few months. But other than needing to take it easy, baby and I are
doing just fine. :) Hope to see you DC-area boxers soon!

Erika, aka Mama Frog
