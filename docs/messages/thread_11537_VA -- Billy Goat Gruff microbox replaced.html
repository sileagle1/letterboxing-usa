From: virginiawaterbird (virginiawaterbird@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-03-28 18:16:45 UTC
VA -- Fiarfax County. Great Falls. Riverbend Park.
Billy Goat Gruff Microbox. This was intended to be velcro'd in place, but that
idea had to be abandoned. It is in a plastic baggy.
Planted 3/29/03 by Waterbird
Difficulty: A walk-up, an easy find on your way to Bee's Knees.

Go past the entrance to the Visitors Center and follow the signs to the Nature
Center. Park in the lot. Take the footpath just past mailbox #8314 to the Paw
Paw Trail and turn left. When you come to the footbridge, look under the NW
corner for Billy Goat Gruff, who is keeping an eye peeled for trolls.
Note: It is tucked WAY up and behind leaves.
