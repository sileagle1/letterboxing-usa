A "Cuckoo" Clue is a variation on the hitch hiker theme where the
letterbox stays in one place and the clue sheet travels from host to
host. I think Randy or Sarah had planted one a couple of years ago,
but I've never heard if it is still current. Well, we liked the idea
so much, we went and planted the Cuckoo's Nest --somewhere-- and
stashed the clue sheet in a fun letterbox in New England for the next
visitor. Hope you enjoy!
