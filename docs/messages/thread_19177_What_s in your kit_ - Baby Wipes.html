Hi folks,

Just a friendly reminder that a wet towel or sponge is a better way
to clean a stamp than a baby wipe -- wipes have a scent, which can
attract critters. They also can wear away the surface of the stamp,
which can result in losing detailed carvings. We typically just
take a wet towel in a ziploc with us -- just a thought!
