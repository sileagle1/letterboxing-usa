How do I avoid lame clues?

From: bookeditor42 (bookeditor42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-04 17:10:44 UTC
Am I the only one who has trouble writing clues? As I leave the
plant location, I think I'm doing great. As I type up the clues, I
worry about being too confusing or not challenging enough.
When I read the clues a week after they are posted, they sound
extremely lame.

We have two planted boxes that have not been posted yet
because of my frustration. (You'd think it would be easy for me
because I'm an editor.)

I think part of my problem is I get frustrated when other people's
clues don't make sense. I hate going through all of the clues
thinking we are solving the puzzle only to realize we're in the
wrong spot and we don't know where we went wrong.

Any suggestions?

Newt

decribed differently etc.
