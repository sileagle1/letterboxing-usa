Hi all. Wanted to report that last week I found Shark's Tooth alive
and well in Venice Florida! As for today...ugh, lots of rain put a
bit of a damper on our boxing excursions. The rain finally broke in
the afternoon, so we headed to Chillicothe to look for the stamps
there. Unfortunately, the rain and nightfall hampered our efforts.
We did manage to find 'You take the High Road, I'll take the Low
Road " that was planted by Salamander. Very nice stamp :) We got up
to the HIGH flag for GrandView...but couldn't tell which slope
contained a tree stump, and it was pretty dark. We will try again,
hopefully on a nicer day. My 4 year old really wanted to play at the
wonderful playground there.
