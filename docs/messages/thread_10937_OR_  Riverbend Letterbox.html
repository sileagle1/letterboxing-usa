It was a lovely afternoon and John and I headed out to Oxbow Park. We
had already gotten Kara's Oxbow #1 letterbox ages ago, and, of course,
her Oxbow #2 is missing.

Funhog had placed the Riverbend Letterbox in this park a while back and
we decided to check it out. Only three people besides us have checked
it out. Tammy, Jason and a baby footprint stamp. The clues are
excellent and the stamp is wonderful (we would expect nothing less from
the happy hog!)

Oxbow is an excellent park. Way beautiful with tons of letterboxing
potential. We may have to place some letterboxes there ourselves one of
these days.

Meanwhile...I'm working on a new stamp for a letterbox for a
newly-discovered state park we stumbled upon this past weekend. Found
the perfect spot for the letterbox, just need to get the masterpiece
completely in rubber and head out that way again and viola! It will be
another one of those boxes that takes you to a pretty cool park. I'll
keep y'all posted when it gets placed.
