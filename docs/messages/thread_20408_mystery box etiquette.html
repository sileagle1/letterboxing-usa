When was the box placed? Or last confirmed? Each time I email
someone, I keep in mind it may have been a few years since the
person placed the box, and since that time they may have lost
interest in the hobby, or for whatever reason arent monitoring that
particular email address, or arent involved whatsoever. I really
wouldnt take it personally, I dont see how you could have leaked any
info using the lbna site to send an email. Good luck in your
searches :)
