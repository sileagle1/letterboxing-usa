The Map Is Greener In TX

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2009-09-17 13:41:31 UTC
I just wanted to let everyone know that the TX Clue map is now completely colored green! In other words, at least one letterbox has been placed in all 254 counties. We started this quest in late 2006 and I never dreamed it would be completed in less than 3 years. Now, no matter where you are in TX, a letterbox is relatively close (relatively close since a few counties are bigger than some states). So come for a visit!

Silver Eagle
