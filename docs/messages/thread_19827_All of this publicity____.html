I seem to be turning into one giant public safety announcement.... (:

We've all been talking a lot lately about the loss or destruction of
boxes due to all of the newfound publicity. Rightfully so, I think,
but just remember to look up from your clue sheet from time to time
to see if there is anyone else around you. Think about it....people
not really paying attention to their surroundings, sometimes out
where no one else can hear or see you....the perfect place for some
of the world's scary people to find us unsuspecting, good
hearted, "wanna' exchange stamps?" letterboxers.

Sorry for the scariness....my husband is a police officer and has
been on my case about this lately...he won't let me go any more
unless I agree to leave him a copy of the clue sheets for the boxes
that I'm going to find!

Happy SAFE trails,
Kerri
