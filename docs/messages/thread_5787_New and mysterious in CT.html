On 6/12/02, the Drew family was out in force:

First Thirds, Second Fourths
Eric can take heart that something in man keeps us searching. Cave
people and the cliff dwellers to the east could find these. Facing
toward the right opening in the big wall, look for tree hugging
hunters among the beech.

Then Fifths:
The Ram and the coyote climb high up the cliff and take in views from
horizon to the sun's fire. Knowing that the Ram's ring of power is
found thirty degrees west and twenty steps beyond, coyote can look
east. Howling at the moon to call Ram, the coyote rounded the corner
to the boulder.

And Sixths:
"Howling," thought Eric? "A coyote's snout in the air? These are
points to help me find happiness? It must be easier to figure out!"

Upstream:
It is. From Eric the Ram's hidden letterbox The Books, "an
interesting geologic formation," upstream between the third and
fourth cascades, lives a rocky island. At the topmost point, between
fallen forks, find bonus treasure.

Uphill:
At the top of the hill scramble above The Books, before "the smaller
path leading toward the sound of running water" drops down, a rocky
knoll lives to the southwest. The best bushwack up is to the left.
Again, look at the topmost point, in the north base of a big oak.

You can also find these clues on our website at:
http://members.aol.com/_ht_a/drewclan/mystery2.htm

Enjoy! (And thanks to Eric the Ram).
