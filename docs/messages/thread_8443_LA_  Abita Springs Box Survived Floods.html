LA: Abita Springs Box Survived Floods

From: n0kwa (n0kwa@bellsouth.net) | Date: 2002-10-16 21:54:32 UTC
I just got a chance to check the Abita Box in Louisiana. Despite
recent floods from Tropical Storm Isidore and Hurricane Lili, the box
was still there. The only damage was to the ink pen I placed in the
box. I didn't have a pen with me to replace it, so if you search for
it, be sure to bring something to sign the log.

The hiding area was unreachable until just recently. I was pleased to
see that a hitchhiker paid a visit and has since moved on.
