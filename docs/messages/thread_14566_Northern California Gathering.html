The 3 coordinators of the Northern California gathering (Sept 6th) met yesterday and
hammered out some of the details for the much anticipated event. I have compiled a
brief web page summarizing everything - content will be updated as things develop.
I have also provided a link to the gathering page from several of my Bay Area

Nice information site on the No.CA gathering. Thank you!

I would like to introduce the "You've Got Mail" mailbox at the No. CA
gathering. The mailbox will be a drop-off, and/or pick-up for HHs. It
will be left on one of the picnic tables for all to enjoy exchanging
HH stamps, etc. This is the standard, average size US mailbox, which
in the future will be hidden somewhere in the East Bay area. So,
bring your HH for an exchange.
