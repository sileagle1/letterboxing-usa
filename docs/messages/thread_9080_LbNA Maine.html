Yes, we did happen to be up in Maine this past long weekend!
Got up to Freeport on Friday night, and took a nice little walk
along Casco Bay by moonlight, but thought we'd wait for morning
for more letterboxing. Nothing doing! - a big running race was
going on near Wolf Neck Woods, and we couldn't get anywhere
near the place! We scooted off to Skofield Preserve in nearby
Brunswick, did a few non-letterbox nature walks, and then the
delightful Boothbay area boxes. Had a pleasant visit with
RIBNAG Ben and Laura, whose new box in the Blue Hills area
we visited on the way home, along with several others, but not
before a brief foray onto Mount Desert Island for a couple of
legerdemaines and the Algerine Coast. Algerine was a little
damp but dried out OK.

It was a lovely little vacation break, with great weather, too, and
we'll be looking forward to getting back up there again
sometime, but for now we are turning our attention southwards!!!

Thanks and cheers,
Wanda and Pete
