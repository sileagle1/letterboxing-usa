It has been brought to my attention that Senator Kerry has found a
platform here.

I would like to take the time tell you, my letterboxing voters, that
I too am an avid letterboxer, but have been prevented from doing so
since its inception due to the pressures of running this, your
country. I have a personal stamp carved and carry it with me always
in the hopes my campaign trail will take me near another letterbox
which I have no time to find.

My daughters, Jen and Barb have kept me informed me of the plight of
orphaned and sabotaged letterboxes and ones that go MIA. And I want
you to know, every letterbox that is MIA is one more missing
letterbox. Sabateurs never stop thinking about ways to harm your
letterboxes, and neither do I. It is because of that, I signed the
Healthy Forests Restoration Act in 2003 to significantly reduce the
threat of letterbox destruction caused by natural disaster.

To Mr. Pepe, you have interviewed Senator Kerry. Give me equal time.
Give me, as America's opponent, an interview.

Remember, a vote for me, is a vote from you.

Thank you,

W.
