Hi there! My name is Bonnie and I am the hider of the This Little Pig
and Mount Sugarloaf boxes. I am so happy to hear you had a good day
of letterboxing. Yes, those trails on North Mount Sugarloaf are not
great, but the views are worth it!

I, too, have found the Mount Pollux box, but that was back in March,
well before poison ivy would have been a problem. That one, and some
other local boxes, was hidden by the "F Troop." Other than that
family, and myself, I don't think there are a lot of us letterboxers
in western Mass. My sister Betsey mostly hides boxes near Boston but
also has a couple out this way. So I hope you will join in and hide
some boxes, too.

I hope you continue to have a fun time letterboxing!
