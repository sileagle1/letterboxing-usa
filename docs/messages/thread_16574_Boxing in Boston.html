Boxing in Boston

From: Mike S (tehutika@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-10-25 21:16:00 UTC-07:00
Greetings,

Last spring, we bought tickets for a comedy show in
Boston. Yesterday, we made the trip out a little
early, and grabbed a few boxes before the show.

We were the second people to find the Battle of Bunker
Hill. The first finder was there only the day before
we were. Thanks to Ryan and Amanda for our first taste
of big city boxing.

Surprisingly enough, we found parking near the Public
Gardens right away, and stamped into both This
American Life and Head By The Charles. Both of these
boxes have seen numerous visitors, including one group
from Kansas. Thanks to the placers of both of these
for their creative ways to hide in such public places.
Let me tell you, trying to discreetly get them, stamp
in and rehide wasn't easy, but was the best part of
the experience!

Of course, the best part of the day was the show.
First row balcony for Eddie Izzard, two hours of all
new material. Fantastic jokes! Fantastic make-up! :-)
