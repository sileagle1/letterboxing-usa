From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-05 16:44:18 UTC
Hi all!

Just couldn't resist taking advantage of the fabulous fall weather
earlier this week to go on another little letterboxing spree! Just
realized it had been over 10 years since we'd last climbed a former
favorite of ours, Mt. Monadnock in southern NH, so that was the goal
of the day! Then, after a few more "outlying boxes" in the area, I
decided to scoot on over to VT, where legerdemaine's Toad became my
F#1500! What a hoot - felt like hitting the jackpot! (But then any
time I find ANY box - no matter what the quality of the stamp,logbook
or container, seems like hitting the jackpot to me!! :-)Thanks again
to all you wonderful planters!

Wanda from RI
