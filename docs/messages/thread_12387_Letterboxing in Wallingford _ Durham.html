Letterboxing in Wallingford & Durham

From: Judi Lapsley Miller (judi@psychokiwi.org) | Date: 2003-04-27 20:27:18 UTC-04:00
Spent a lovely day boxing out Wallingford. The Great Tree Adventure is
particularly awesome - wonderful old trees and woods filled with spring
wildflowers (trout lillies, Dutchman's britches, and many more). If you're
wondering where to go boxing next, we reckon you couldn't go wrong with this
series (Butterfly, the inkpads are getting a bit knackered though). Also got
Hope, Double Happiness, Light the Way, and Tyler Two, then had a pretty
jaunt around Miller Pond in Durham doing the Story Teller Series.

I think we may have hit 300 today - though we've still got some way to go to
get to the magic 5-double-oh!

Cheers,

Judi
