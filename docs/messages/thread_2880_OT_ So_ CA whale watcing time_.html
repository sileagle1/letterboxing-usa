Dear Cowpost,

Generally in Southern CA you can see Grey Whales from the end of December
through March. They are at their "thickest" in February.

Let me know where in So Cal you plan to be and I'll try and get you more
information on Boat Trips. Also let me know if you are looking a few hours
of Whale Whatching or would like to take a week tour down to Mexico to see
the Whales up close and personal. My sister and parents took this trip and
got to pet the whales and even the baby whales.

We can take this off list if you like, my email address is
wendygault@earthlink.net

Wendy
