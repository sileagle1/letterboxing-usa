[L-USA] Photos of Kids

From: Klink, Mitchel (Mitchel.Klink@sulzerpumps.com) | Date: 1999-02-02 14:25:45 UTC-08:00
Several people have expressed interest in submitting photos of kids hunting
for and/or placing letterboxes. Such pictures would be great additions to
the Kids Corner feature of Letterbox USA.

I am hoping to use some of the pictures to help illustrate the concept of
letterboxing for kids. Some of the other photos that are submitted will
appear on a separate scrapbook/photo album type of page.

Please let me know the names of the children in the pictures, and I will try
to include this information on the web pages. Also please let me know what
state the pictures were taken in. Other info, such as the name of the
letterbox, etc. is welcome, but optional.

If your pictures are already in electronic form (such as JPG, GIF, or BMP
files) please attach them to an email and send to me at:
LETTERBOXR@aol.com

If you prefer, you can send the actual photographs to me via ordinary USPS
snail mail. My home address is:
Mitch Klink
20546 NW LaPine Way
Portland, OR 97229

I will be happy to scan the photos for you and return them. A SASE will be
appreciated, but is not neccessary. I will take very good care of your
photos, but (of course) can't be held responsible for lost or misdirected
mail.

I am also looking for images of rubber stamps that have been made by
children.

Also, please let me know if you do not have such photos at the present time,
but would be interested in submitting them later in the spring when the
weather clears up.

Sincerely,
''Der Mad Stamper''
