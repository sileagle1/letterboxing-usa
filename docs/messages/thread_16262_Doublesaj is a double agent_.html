I don't usually choose to denounce people in a public forum, but I
can't stay silent.

Yesterday as AshRaven and Sir Balthazar and I were retrieving the
last of our stamps hidden in Meadow Park for the event, we took a
quiet moment to look through the logbooks to learn who had found
which boxes. Of course, Doublesaj and Old Blue were part of that
ruthless team who joined forces and insisted on finding ALL of them.
I was very pleased when I looked through the book from my Yes on
Cheeseracing box and saw that D.S. had penned next to her stamped
archery design a small, (but SINCERE seeming) pro-cheeseracing
sentiment. Pleased, at least, until I happened to see what she had
penned in Sir Balthazar's anti-cheeseracing book!!!!

This is a woman so unafraid to take a stand that she will take
several mutually exclusive stands on the very same afternoon, with
seeming conviction! It is this kind of moral uncertainty that I am
taking exception to as I state that while she, at her last report,
had 130 finds, I went out purposely yesterday for Buzzard's wonderful
Halloween Box so that I no longer have to share that figure with her:
131 for me, Doublesaj- take THAT!
