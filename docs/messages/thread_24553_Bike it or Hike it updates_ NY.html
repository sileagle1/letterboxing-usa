Bike it or Hike it updates; NY

From: haalck (haalck@snet.net) | Date: 2007-09-05 03:55:09 UTC
It only took 3 weeks, but I finally finished updating clues and adding
4 new Letterboxes from my NY trip. All 4 new boxes are in the Albany
area; see "Whats New". I also replaced 3 missing boxes and updated
clues for a few others. Check out the Bike it or Hike Update Page at
http://www.geocities.com/haalck@snet.net/update_page.htm for more
info. I also wanted to send out a big THANK YOU to the many fellow
boxers who have replaced logbooks for some of my more distant boxes.
It really helps when you have over 100 boxes planted and your trying
to keep them all active. It makes it easy for me to just send out a
new log or stamp to someone living in the area or about to visit my
box. I've met some great people over the years through boxing.
Happy Hunting!
Bill & Craig
