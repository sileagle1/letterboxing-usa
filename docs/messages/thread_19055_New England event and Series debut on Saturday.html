New England event and Series debut on Saturday

From: Shannon Donovan (sciencepup@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-06-03 22:32:48 UTC-04:00
Greetings all! Pup of RIBNAG here. I've been away from this list for a
number of years, but I'm back in the groove now. I want to let you know
about a new series that has been placed for the 2004 RI Sustainable Living
Festival in Coventry, RI. There will be a special event stamp available on
Saturday, June 5th only. The Sustainable Living Festival is conducted at The
Apeiron Institute for Environmental Living. Unless you call ahead to
volunteer(401-397-3430), you need tickets to attend the festival. Visit
http://apeiron.org/ri_sustainable_living_festival.htm for info and
directions. Clues to the Circle Series Letterboxes will be available at the
Children's Activity Area on Saturday. After the festival, the clues will be
posted as usual. I hope you can make it to this great event. Stop in the
blue info booth to say "hi!" Happy Boxing!
-pup
