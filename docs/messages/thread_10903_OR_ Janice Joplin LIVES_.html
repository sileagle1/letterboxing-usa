From: Mark Bendickson <nomad_pnw@hotmail.com> (nomad_pnw@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-02-24 03:05:56 UTC
Just got back today from a nice hike after finding all 4 of the
Forest Park Musician Series, west of Portland, Oregon.

For those fans of Jancie Joplin who thought she was gone, think
again! She is alive and well living in a little box along the Leif
Erikson Trail (old defunct road) in northern Forest Park.

We were surprised to see that we were the 1st to bag this series, but
I'm sure there will be others now as the stamps all make a nice
addition to our log book and will to yours too.

If you are having problems solving some of the clues, as Janis would
say, just "Try A Little Harder"! ;o)

Mark & Beth
