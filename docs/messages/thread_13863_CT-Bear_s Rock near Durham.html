CT-Bear's Rock near Durham

From: Alafair (lkazel@mindspring.com) | Date: 2003-06-27 05:53:44 UTC-07:00
Last evening, my letterboxing partner and I took the short hike to Bear's Rock near Durham. Since it stays light so late in the evening, this Drew Clan letterbox can easily be done after work. When we were there, there was not another soul around.

The Mountain Laurels were in full bloom and the trail was a fairy-land of white and pale pink. In addition, the view is not to be missed.

This box, placed in 2000, has a nice stamp, is in good shape and hasn't had many visitors lately. Take a few moments to look through the logbook at all the box finders' marvelous stamps that have come to grace its pages over the last three years.

Jay, this one's a gem. Thanks for a perfect evening!

Alafair
