From: BB (bburk@harbornet.com) | Date: 2003-03-10 20:23:48 UTC-08:00
Headed south today and visited Amanda's Borst Park LtrBox and Fudrick's Cowlitz
Falls LtrBox.

Had to wait for some ball players to leave the area before retrieving the Borst
Park box, then found it to be in great shape. My stamp made 5 visitors.

Fudrick's box also showed no signs of undue wear, my stamp made 4 visitors.

The weather cooperated and bestowed upon us a beautiful day while at Cowlitz
Falls Park, actually quite warm and almost sunny. We saw three Eagles, two
juveniles and one adult and a couple Kingfishers, oh, and a Pileated Woodpecker
too.

Proved to be a great day to be outside!
