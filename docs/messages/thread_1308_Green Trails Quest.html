It was a gloriously autumn day yesterday and Margaret and I had a
couple of hours of together time in the morning, so we headed over to
the Trustum Pond Quest ( http://www.tontlamets.org/finding.htm ).
There's nothing we like better than salt ponds on an early fall
weekday! We've done a couple of these "new" letterboxes, but we could
tell from the clues that this was to be a special one. It is! If you
get a chance to be in Rhode Island, it's most definately worth the
trip: beautiful, easy, creative, funny!
