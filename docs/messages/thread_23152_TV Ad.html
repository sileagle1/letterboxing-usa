From: Cire Otrue (cireotrue@hotmail.com) | Date: 2006-03-14 21:20:53 UTC-05:00

Fascinating, this ad brings back memories. Memories of a time a few years
ago in eastern CT before the "Courant Article". It was a time when news of a
new box posting was acted upon almost immediately with some (including
myself) going out even into the dark to be the first finder of these new
boxes. There were some fights, some biting, crying, but in the end there was
laughter and fellowship. We are a more civilized people now and have not the
need for first findership. Our developed letterboxing ways have purged the
yearning of first findership from our emotions. Through meditation we have
found peace withourselves and with each other.

Box Long and Prosper

~The Ram~
