East Coasters Rock!

From: adglbna (ADGLBNA@aol.com) | Date: 2002-09-30 22:20:24 UTC
What a fabulous weekend. This was much better than being eaten alive
by mosquitoes!

I am actually kind of sad that I won't be running into all of you on
the trail every day. Group boxing is a lot of fun and really helpful
too! I don't think we would have found boxes 3 and 4 if we hadn't
had help. We didn't find number 1...Firefly did so we were thrilled
to find her on that rock when we headed back down the trail. Coleen
rules!

Thanks to NewBe for setting this up and it was really cool to meet
you all. We will definitely be heading back to Richmond to hit the
rest of the boxes in the area. The campground was great and so was
the rum. Celtic Pride, you really missed the boat on that one! Next
time we have a meet and greet, you all need to stay in the park so we
can party after we walk our butts off!

Thankfully, we did get one group of visitors. Poor Psychomommy and
Turtle stumbled into our campsite after traipsing all over the park
for hours. I think because so many different people placed boxes at
different times that the amount of walking required was enormous. We
lucked out and came in backwards on a couple and saved ourselves
quite a lot of time and pain. That said, Sarah and I were out cold
by the time we headed back yesterday night.

I haven't even been home yet! I am heading out now to do some laundry
and admire all of my new stamps. Sarah is going to the Renn Fest
this weekend so I am on my own. I plan to place a couple of boxes
without her so expect some new postings soon.

Thanks again for a lovely weekend and a truckload of laughs!

Tammy,

TOO TIRED TO HAVE ANY STATS YET, TUNE IN NEXT WEEK, SAME BAT TIME,
SAME BAT CHANNEL!
