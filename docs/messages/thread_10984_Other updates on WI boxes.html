Other updates on WI boxes

From: Tim Kuehl (tkuehl@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2003-03-02 11:34:34 UTC-06:00
As long as I'm giving updates on Tyranena and Fort Atkinson, I thought I'd
finally take the time to announce my findings (or lack thereof) of some of
the other WI letterboxes.

Tyranena - I forgot to mention that it is at the city park in Lake Mills.
It is NOT a State park.

E-Way Trails Box - fine as of 12/14/02

Cam-Rock Canoe Launch - fine as of 12/14/02

River Watch (Ft. Atkinson) - missing as of 12/14/02 (at least I couldn't
find it)

Eagle LB - fine as of 12/14/0. Picked up Robot 1 HH.

Boyhood Adventures LB - were fine back in 8/6/02. Went to find Mt.
Bieterman LB with some students after a math meet on 11/25/02 but it was
missing. Noticed that BirkieBob was in the area at the time and figured he
was checking on it. I have not gone back to check if it is there again.
(Note to BirkieBob: I live very close to all three of your boxes in WI. I'd
be happy to keep an eye on them for you if you wish.)

Boerner Botanical Gardens LB - fine as of 12/15/02 - I was the first!

Wehr Nature Center - fine as of 11/23/02

University of Green Bay LBs-

Cofrin Arboretum #1 - went looking for it three times and was not found.
Last looked in 12/27/02. Tried both old and new clues all three times.

Cofrin Arboretum #2 - also searched three times without any luck. I believe
it is also missing. I assume the "another observation spot" is the tower in
the middle of the Corn Maze. Is this right?

Bay Shoreline - still missing as of 11/8/02. Good hiding spot - shame it's
gone.

Under the Overpass - still there as of 11/8/02. Had been cleaned up by
previous person, but I found it completely soaked again. It is in poor
condition. You might move it to a higher location.

Shorewood Golf Course - still missing as of 11/8/02

High Cliff series - found all 3 on 11/9/02. All in good condition.

Algoma Pierhead Lighthouse LB - I could not find it on 12/26/02. I think it
is missing. I searched the rocks for an hour.

La Salle Park - I could not find it either on 12/26/02. The clue is very
vague. Still, I think it is lost.

Sturgeon Bay Shipping Canal LB East - I searched the rocks for an hour and
could not find it on 12/26/02. Clue to the bonus stamp would be lost then
also. :(

Sturgeon Bay Shipping Canal LB West -found in good condition on 12/26/02

Chanticleer Guest House LB - couldn't find a place to park except the
Chanticleer itself. Are we allowed to park there?

Cave Point LB - found in good condition on 12/26/02. I thought it was
interesting that I had found the box one day before the ranger at the park
complained about the box being there. I'm sure they did not see me there.

Old Bailey's Harbor Lighthouse LB - still missing 12/26/02. The light house
is even gone now too!

Bailey's Harbor Range Lights LB - still missing 12/26/02. Has NOT been
replaced.

Cana Island LB - found in good condition on 12/26/02.

Mink River LB - found in good condition on 12/27/02. Placed Robot 1 HH
here.

Sherwood Point Lighthouse LB - still missing on 12/27/02.

Cabot Point LB - I could not find it in the rocks. I looked for over an
hour on 12/27/02. I assume the end of Cabot point is the last place the
public is allow to drive to on the road. There is a private drive that goes
out further.

Holy Hill LB - found in good condition on 8/7/02

Green One/Caltrava - found in good condition on 8/8-14/02


Tim Kuehl
"The Dragon"
