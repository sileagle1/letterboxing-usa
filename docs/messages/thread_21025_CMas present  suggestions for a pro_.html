I'm looking for a great Christmas present for someone I love who is
addicted to letterboxing. She's very in to it so I don't need advice
for something for a novice. PLEASE - any suggestions would be so
greatly appreciated. She's my only sister!







RE: [LbNA] CMas present suggestions for a pro!

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2004-11-30 08:16:12 UTC-08:00
Here's some ideas:

Ink pads (Cat's eyes or Duo Daubers)
Pens
First Aid supplies (especially the creme that takes the burn out of stinging
nettles)
Nice carrying pack
personalized pack?
Hiking stick
Carving material
novelty boxes
Hand made log books
paper for making log books
Stickers for log books or boxes


On the other side of the coin.. the best birthday present I ever got was
letterbox related.
My best friend spent weeks carving a series of boxes that she planted just
for me. Each stamp depicted one of my favorite things. She then planted them
around town and we spent the entire day with a group of letterboxing friends
searching them out. I even had to answer clues to get my other presents :)

So there's a few ideas for you..

Hope that helps.

Maiden

I have a nephew in NC who carves some really great walking sticks. They make beautiful gifts. Most have faces at the top, and a smooth place for your hand, and a leather loop for hanging. He has taken them a few steps further than many I have seen. My favorites still have some lichens spots, as the trees in NC have. If you'd like his contact info., email me privately and he could probably send you pics of the ones he has already made-- they're already photographed.
