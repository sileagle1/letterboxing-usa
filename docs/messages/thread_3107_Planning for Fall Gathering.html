Planning for Fall Gathering

From: (trishkri@yahoo.com) | Date: 2001-08-16 13:08:30 UTC
Yes, Oct 6 is the Saturday of Columbus Day weekend. In the original
poll I indicated that it was a 3day weekend. I know that a few
people have conflicts but hopefully most will be able to attend.

I was hoping to get an indication from everyone how they want the
planning handled. I know that many people who read the list will not
be able to come and probably don't want to sift through lots of
postings about something they have little interest in. Here are some
of our options:

1) Continue to do all planning via postings on this egroup.
2) Establish a new egroup only for the planning, open to all for
posting and reading.
3) Establish an email list for those who plan to come and copy all in
the group for every message.

My personal preference would be 2) in consideration for those that
are not coming. I know some people on the list have limited internet
access and may not care to read 30 postings on one food suggestion
vs. another. That way anyone who is not coming but is interested
will have the option of checking out the other egroup. The down
side, of course, is that those of us who are will have to check two
different egroups (or just sign up for the daily digest). Let me
know what your preferences are.

Thanks!
Trish
