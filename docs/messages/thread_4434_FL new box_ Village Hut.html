FL new box: Village Hut

From: Mountain Scorpia (mountainscorpia@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-02-10 23:26:36 UTC-05:00
Volusia County
nearest city: DeLand

First find Swamp Owl, Clay Dog, Clay Acorn, Swamp Fox, and Bannerstone, Nos.
1-5 of Seven from the Florida Ancients.

NO. 6 - Village Hut

Stop at post with yellow arrow and FVA. Take 10 small-woman steps and look
left between two palms to straight tall dead oak about 50 away that has a
lovely hidey hole. Walk to it, then look left and low in the roots for
another hidey hole on the ground. Therein lies the Village Hut.

Return to the trail and turn left to continue.
