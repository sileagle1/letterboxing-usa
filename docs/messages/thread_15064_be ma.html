Hi Pam! Glad to hear from you! Be is 8 and Ma is 6 and we have a lot of
fun letterboxing. There are many other families with young kids and it's
fun to share tales!

Please don't let the bickering that has been going on lately discourage you
from posting your adventures, questions, whatever! Every once in a while a
conflict stirs up -- sort of like the daily special! We just treat it as
part of the daily update call to hubby regarding what is new in the world
"what's the list uproar about today?!..."

Thank you for emailing! It's wonderful to hear that you enjoy the stories
-- sometimes I get to wondering if my bantering is driving everyone up a
wall! But - we've got to be ourselves and live and let live.

By the way, that is how most of these little "controversies" end -- everyone
agrees to disagree and respect the fact that we are all different in little
ways (life is much more interesting that way!)

Happy boxing! BeMa
