From: psycomommy2003 (ktborrelli@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-08-04 23:31:28 UTC
The boxes are being pulled by the FBI. I'm not sure of all the
details but this is what I do know. Some guy from the MD/DC area got
busted for espionage. He buried alot of the stolen files in MD and VA
state parks. They dug up quite alot of documents where I had planted
some boxes in Patapsco State Park in MD. He also planted in
Pocahontas State Park. If a box was discovered, during the search, it
was pulled. Now the state of MD has a form you must fill out if you
plan on planting a box.
Psychomommy
