To the Groton/waterford LB thief

From: Kelli (k2xcthcxnep@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-22 20:25:28 UTC
I went to Bluff Point today to pull any remaining boxes and of all my
Pirate series there were only 4 left.I want you to know that I spent
almost a year collecting those boxes from some of the best and nicest
LBers across the globe,there were boxes from England,Ireland,and the
Netherlands as well as every corner of the US.I went to great lengths
to put that series together to give people a chance to see what boxes
were like all over the world.I dont know what your reasons are for
what you've done to all my hard work but I implore you to please give
the boxes back to me so that I might send them home to the people who
were generous enough to make them for me.Email me for my address or I
will meet you anywhere you like to pick them up,if you want just drop
them in a garbage can somewhere and then send me an email of where I
might find them.Thanks, Kreepy Kelli aka the Angry Irish Warrior Maiden

I am involved in a campaign to save an historical site in Ireland from
being bulldozed to make way for a highway.To sign the petition and
help save the Hill of Tara from being destroyed by corruption and
greed go to Tarawatch.org Please help spread the word,inform others
who may be interested in Tara's plight,time is running out.Ireland
needs our help,give voice to those who have none!
