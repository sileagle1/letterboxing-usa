From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-06-04 04:43:14 UTC
I'm not sure about the"God Bless America LB" but since Gwen & I
started "Honor Liberty HH" I will do the honor of bringing you up to
date on her. You can find her travels on Pete & Wanda's HH page. On
September 11, 2002 she was launched at Point Vicente Lighthouse near
the LA , Calif. harbor. The instructions requested that her present
location be posted so that she could move quickly across the country
visiting many letterboxes. This is contrary to how we generally
treat a hitch hiker as normally the location of a HH is kept secret.
Each letterboxer not only stamps in but is encouraged to write a few
lines concerning their feelings in her log. Now occasionally there
have been races to obtain the HH ,and other times she gets stalled
out and her journey delayed. At the end of her first year she was
mailed back to us and I posted the various comments on this chat
list that were in her log. Hopefully the same procedure will occur
after this years travels. If you would like to read the first year's
comments see message #34174, dated 10/3/03. She has been at her
current location for nearly one month, or at least no one has
reposted a new location.

Don
