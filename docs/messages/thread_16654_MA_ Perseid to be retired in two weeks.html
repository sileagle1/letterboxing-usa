MA: Perseid to be retired in two weeks

From: Mike S (tehutika@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-10-29 19:42:30 UTC-08:00
Greetings,

Recently, a boxer reported that the stamp at Perseid,
in Stanley Park, is starting to crack. When I first
started carving, all I could find was the Speedball
white rubber, so naturally, it's deteriorated over the
last year.

The stamp needs to be replaced, and, with all of the
construction going on near the box, it should be moved
anyway, so Perseid is going to be officially retired
as of Nov 11, 2003, two weeks from today. If you don't
have this one and were planning on getting it, now is
your last chance. This email warning is going to be
added to the clues.

The box will be replaced by another, similarly themed
box, sometime before the end of November.

Mike S.
