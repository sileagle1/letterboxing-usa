From: Nisa (teapot@teapot.mv.com) | Date: 2002-01-11 15:42:59 UTC-05:00

My Peterborough boxes (Shieling Forest and Noone Falls) are about an hour away from Manchester.  I think you're pretty central to where many of the letterboxes are in NH - if you go about an hour east, you'll be in the Rye Beach area where there are several as well.  If you visit the Peterborough boxes, give me a call and maybe we can get together for a cup of coffee?  I've yet to meet more letterboxers after my initial visit with Susan and Erik Davis almost 4 years ago.

Right now, the roads are pretty wet, there's some snow, but it's a little dense due to the precip. today.  It's not that cold either.

Nisa
