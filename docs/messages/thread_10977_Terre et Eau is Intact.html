Kate and I enjoyed the walk through a part of Orange County I had
never seen, despite being an OC resident for the last 35 years. I had
no idea there was open land anywhere in OC.

We trudged through a bit of mud, due to the recent rains, and then
mistakenly followed the chain link fence a bit too long, going to the
right of the BMX hills. Upon finding nothing, we doubled back, found
the trash can on the left, ddged a few bikers (literally) and
13-year-old Kate found the 5-trunks and the box. Although I must say
that those were 8 very LONG paces from where we entered the thicket.

In any case, we were both thrilled and stamped in. Thanks for a
challenging hunt and a pleasant walk! We'll send a postcard.

Zeek and Kate, March 01, 2003
