There's not a lot in the immediate vicinity of Jim Thorpe (just 2 in Carbon
County), but within a one hour radius you'll find the boxes in Northampton,
Monroe, Lehigh and Schuylkill counties. Check the clues carefully -- a
number of the Northampton County boxes are MIA.

For some reason, the Mt. Minsi box (Monroe Cty) isn't listed on the Eastern
PA page, but it's still active (I was just there a couple of weeks ago),
though it is a bit of a hike:
http://www.geocities.com/RainForest/Canopy/7962/minsi.htm
This is right at Delaware Water Gap at I-80.

On the other side of the Delaware River in Northern NJ, there are 2 more
letterboxes in Warren County (DWG & Mt Tammany).

There are also a number of boxes in NJ that are pretty convenient to I-80
and I-78. Holler if you want some suggestions.

Also, if you're up for a mystery, my new Bull Hill box is north of
Scranton, and I've got a couple more planted in Scranton for which I'm
finishing up the clues:
http://www.letterboxing.info/box_bullhill.shtml
