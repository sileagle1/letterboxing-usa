"Little Feet"
Placed by A-Bear on 9 Apr 03
Colorado Springs, CO
El Paso County

Directions: Take Exit 146, Garden of the Gods, from
I-25. Head west to 30th Street. Turn left on 30th
St. Turn right on Gateway Road which leads to the
Garden of the Gods Park. As you enter the park, park
in the North Main Parking Lot which is the first one
you will come to on the left.

Trail: Flat for the most part and wheelchair
accessible. 1-1.5 mile loop to find this
micromagnetic letterbox.

Clues: I'm so much smaller than the incredible rock
formations surrounding me. As a result, my LITTLE
FEET had to sit down and take in all the surroundings,
incredible rocks, rock climbers, rapelling,
wildlife...to find me read on.

Start at the Central Garden Trail sign and continue
down the walkway. Hang a right at the Signature Rock
to start the loop. After 3 rock benches along the
loop, you will come to a bench unlike any of the
others. Take a seat on the southern end and find
LITTLE FEET hiding in its base.
