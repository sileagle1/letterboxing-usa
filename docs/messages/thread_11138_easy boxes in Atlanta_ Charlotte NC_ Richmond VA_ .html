My Charlotte, NC Mystery Box is an easy drive-by (if you can figure
it out) and can be gotten after dark too!! :-)

The Latta Park boxes in Charlotte, NC are off of I-77 about 15 to 20
minutes N of I-85 and then require about an hour or so of walking
around the park. (Also, Dogwood and Noah's Ark are missing and won't
be replaced until later this month)

The Father's Day Box in Steele Creek is quick and easy. The Visitor
is also in the same park, although I haven't gotten that one yet,it
was hidden in February of this year.
Also Max'a Birthday box is in the same general area. (these are store-
bought stamps)


And that about does it for quick boxes in the Charlotte area.
