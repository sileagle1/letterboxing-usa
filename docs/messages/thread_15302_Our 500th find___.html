From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-24 05:23:39 UTC
After a few months of very sporadic letterboxing (read major hiatus),
we were able to finally find our 500th letterbox today. It is only
fitting that we found one of A-Bear's letterboxes as our 500th
find!!! The Star in Star, ID was the lucky letterbox. Thanks A-Bear
and all of the other awesome letterboxers past & present who have
placed so many cool and interesting letterboxes at such incredible
places around the country (& the world for that matter--although we
haven't yet found any international boxes). Without letterbox
placers, where would letterboxers be? Lost in the woods? ;)

God Bless & see you on a letterboxing trail someday!
