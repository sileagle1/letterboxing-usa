Name: Beavercreek Fen
Location: Greene County Ohio
Placed 14 Nov 2001
Distance: mile round trip from parking

No need to get your feet muddy here. This park is a wetlands park you
can explore on a boardwalk. It is a great place to go birding or see
some of the wetlands grasses without mucking about in the goo.
"The Fen" is on Fairgrounds Road.

Great for strollers or even wheelchairs. You can get to within 60
feet of the cache without leaving the boardwalk. Even then you do not
need to get your feet wet. It will be obvious where you should leave
the prepared path. Just go to N 39 44.530 W 084 00.541.

Cache/letterbox contains toys for boys and girls of all ages. More
details and hints can be found by looking for placename GC26D2 on the
www.geocaching.com website.

I am sorry to say that I have not fished the stream along the first
part of the path. Looks like a few bluegills could be pulled out of
there.

Have fun in this park which remains easy when all the other trails
will leave your shoes caked with mud.
