Greetings to all you letterboxers in Maryland from Martini Man, a
Wisconsin letterboxer! My spouse, Wisconsin Hiker, and I took up
letterboxing last summer on the advice of a friend. We have been avid
hunters and we also have started to place some in our area. Wisconsin
Hiker was recently scheduled to go out of town to the Baltimore area
on business for a couple of weeks. She had a great idea and asked if
I could come out over the weekend for some letterboxing in Maryland.
Well I agreed and it was a great experience. From Saturday, April17,
till Sunday, April 18, we boxed until we dropped. Here's the
blow-by-
blow of how we did. We thought this would be of interest to our
Maryland colleagues in regard to the status of their boxes. For the
ones we could not find.well, it might be us, but we looked HARD.
