Forcebox #2 can be found at Pierce Park in Pierce Twp) (Stamp has been replaced 5-21-02)

(Trail, short hike with a few hills)
You will need: Stamp Pad and Writing Tool

From I-275 take SR 125 Eaxt (East to Amelia)

about 5 miles on SR 125 you will see a water tower to your right. At the water tower turn right onto White Oak Street. You will dive on White oak to you come to a 4 way stop. Turn left onto Merwin Ten Mile. You drive on Merwin ten mile till you come upon a Golf course on the right. The next street on your right will be Locust Corner Road. You will go about a mile or so and the Park will be on your Left. (You will know when your coming up to the park as they have rumble strips in the road)

From the Parking lot you will see a map of the trails. You will take the RED trail to the left and down the hill. At the bottom of the hill you will see a dirt path to the left. Take the path. You will will cross two Steal pipes that are under the trail , at the 2nd pipe, The trail splits, go to your right.

Head down the trail about 90 paces, You will come to a round Rock
in the path. To the left of the rock is a dead tree on the floor, about 4 paces
off the trail. along side the tree is a rock, Under the Rock is Forcebox #2.
