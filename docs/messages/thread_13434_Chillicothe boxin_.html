CardinalMom, Buddy, and I found time to look for a couple of
Chillicothe, Ohio boxes yesterday evening. We found "Grand View"
and "Little Peeper, Petite" to be dry and in good condition. We did
have to search for a bit for the "Little Peeper" - teaches us to read
and follow the clues carefully and not jump to conclusions. A good
hide. We suspect things have leafed out since "Grand View" was
hidden. If we hadn't known what we should be seeing we might not
have found it. it also doubles as a geocache - first one of those
we've encountered. Surprised to find such a BIG box after all those
microboxes! Nice relaxing Sat. evening! What a great hobby!
