I hope you get good weather. I actually spent Labor Day weekend
traveling from Indianapolis to Columbus, then Chillicothe,
Portsmouth, through Cincinnati and then back to Indy. Just me and my
dog, letterboxing away. Chillicothe is a great spot, but don't
forget to stop at Scioto Trails State Park, just south of
Chillicothe. There are several boxes there. If you are coming up and
can pass through Portsmouth, there are at least 4 good boxes there
and a few in the Waverly area, between Portsmouth and Chillicothe.
All are pretty accessible from Rt. 23. You will find a mixture of
hand-carved and store-bought stamps in Chillicothe. What a great way
to bring in the new year!
