Binx and I went camping for Memorial Day weekend at Black Rock State

-Paradise Lost
-Claddagh
-Cunningham Tower
-St.Veronica
-Wild Walk series

We found a couple hitchhikers as well which I'll mention in a
separate posting. Many thanks to all who placed all of these boxes!
