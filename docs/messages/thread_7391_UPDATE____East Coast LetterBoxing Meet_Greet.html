UPDATE....East Coast LetterBoxing Meet/Greet

From: Kristoffer J Buquet (KBuquet@ApertureFoto.com) | Date: 2002-09-02 22:03:01 UTC-04:00
Alright LetterBoxers......exciting new UPDATE here!

The East Coast LetterBoxing Meet/Greet is an official GO.......

Date:          Saturday 29 September 2002
Time:          All day (exacts to be determined)
Location:     State Park in the Richmond, VA area (I can't give it away yet....still working on the clues)
 
First, I have a few questions/challenges for all those that wish to participate in the shindig!
 
a. Food: Eat @ a local place or Pot Luck
 
b. Submit a carved stamp to me (or the list) for judging by the list to be THE representative stamp of the "1st Annual East Coast Letterboxing Meet/Greet" Submit stamp (image) No Later Than 12:00 PM FRIDAY 7 SEP 2002. Judging will be throughout the weekend of 8-9 Sep 02. With the winning stamp presented to the list on Monday10 Sep 02.
 
c. Submit brief bio of yourself, LetterBoxing family/group for inclusion into the "program" that I am doing up for the shindig. (name, when started LetterBoxing, why started, boxes P/F/X, 1 Unique LetterBoxing story, etc....please limit it to 1 typed page....preferably contain a photo of yourself, some of your stamps, etc...)
 
OK, if you have any questions PLEASE feel free to contact me.....I am open to most all complaints, suggestions, gripes, and tips :-)
 
I look forward to hearing from everyone....
 
P.S. Please submit your bio information directly to me..NOT THE LIST.... KBuquet@ApertureFoto.com
 
NewBe1
