On March 18, 2002 Funhog placed the Papillion Letterbox near Papillion,
Sarpy County, Nebraska. Papillion is a town just south of Omaha that has
one of the prettiest water towers to be found.

to the trailhead: Go South out of Omaha on 84th to the town of Papillion.
Find the park with a nutty name. Go North on the park road that makes you
want to eat too much and watch a football game. A short distance along this
road you will see signs for a nature trail. Park at the trailhead.

to the letterbox: Traverse the span. Head for the spot best for those with
an elevated intellect. From the letterbox you can see a barn at 345 degrees
magnetic, a water tower at 75 degrees and an avian abode at 200 degrees.
Crawl on your belly like a reptile.
