DRAGON IN THE ROCK


Washington County, near Rockville, RI


Hidden 10/18/02
Clues: Easy
Hike: no more than 2 miles round trip, moderate to strenuous, lots of
rocky ravines which can get slippery. Take your time and all should
be well.


Directions: Follow 138 in RI until you reach Wincheck Rd in
Rockville. Take a left on Wincheck followed by another left onto
Canonchet Rd. Drive .5 miles to North Rd. and follow it one mile to
the parking area. A little before the parking area the road turns to
dirt....There will be a sign at the parking area.
