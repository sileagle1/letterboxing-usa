Had a busy few hours boxing the Jackson area yesterday. Found Fossil
Finders and Just Passin Through, then Order in the Court (after
creeping around the courthouse trying to look inconspicuous while
wearing a flourescent green T-shirt---poor choice of attire on my
part). Also found Sacred Symbol, Leo, and Leo Too. No luck finding
Political Graveyard, Hero's Rest or the Ridgewood Flower but very
interesting cemeteries all the same! It got dark before I could get
To Lake Alma so that particular adventure will have to wait for
another day. Thanks to Bigguy, Scoutdogs and Franzsolo for planting
great boxes! Salamander
