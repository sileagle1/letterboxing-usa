Hi all,
I finally have the clues to my first box up on the site. I was
thinking of making it a mystery, but it was too easy to find out
about this place online. LOL

We spent the last 2 days doing some hunting. Sunday we went to PA and
got Spirit and Honor. Both are in great shape and active. :) Then we
headed back to DE for Serendipity's Sister and Along the Way.
Serendipity's Sister is in a beautiful location. I can't say where.
It's a mystery. SpringChick, be prepared for an email from me. I want
to know more about that place!

Yesterday we went to PA again for the Wissahickon boxes. Boy were
those ever a hike! And with a 3 yr. old to boot! Body Parts was the
hardest to find. I wouldn't want to do that one again. LOL
We looked for the Eastern State Penitentary box, but we believe it's
MIA. We KNOW we were in the right place, but no box. :(
So we only went 3 for 4 yesterday but that's cool. We had fun, even
though we got stuck in the woods during a major thunderstorm. We were
muddy and drenched! LOL We were hoping to get to The Portia boxes in
Fairmount Park, but were too beat after the Wissahickon. We're off
again tomorrow, so hopefully we find them then. :)
