We've just returned from our Oregon trip - empty-handed re:
letterboxing! :( We went to Multnomah Falls and looked for one of the
boxes there, but couldn't find it. There were tons of people there the
day we went so I felt a little self-conscious looking for the box. My
brother and his girlfriend were also there and they weren't very
interested in looking for the others. Sooooooo, no letterboxing! We
didn't look for any of the others in Oregon either. We hope to go out
again next year (for a wedding, we hope!) and perhaps we'll have more
time and better luck!

Sorry to read of all the discord in the letterboxing community - I still
consider it a fun hobby and will continue to do it (though I've done so
little...) whether there is a cohesive group of letterboxers or not.
