Great day for boxin' in Southern Ohio!

From: BigGuy_45640 (bigguy_45640@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-25 00:08:38 UTC
After so much gloom and rain, today was a sunny and glorious 70
degrees here in southern Ohio so CardinalMom, the kids, and I
journeyed down to Clermont county and found 10 of franzsolo's
wonderful stamps. We found Owl's Nest, Footprints, Stumped Parrot,
Up A Tree, Oh300io, In Flight, ZooBox, Raccoon Ridge, Happy 2003, and
Wild Things. In addition, we were blessed with a hitchhiker
(Witchcraft) and planted 2 new boxes while we were there. Some of
the boxes were a little soggy but the stamps were all in great
shape. It's worth the effort to find great stamps like these! Hope
you all had similar success.
