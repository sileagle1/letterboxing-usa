From: Irene Martin (hikersandhounds@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-16 20:32:07 UTC-08:00
Greetings,

Hikers & Hounds are happy to announce our very first planted boxes in beautiful Bensalem, Pa. Clues are posted on the website. Also I have changed email address for this list from my personal, ireneemartin, to this new address for my team. I deleted my old LBNA membership and we are now officially Hikers & Hounds.

A HUGE thank you to Dan and Joyce Kelly - TEAM KELLY - for meeting us in the chilly winter weather to "test drive" our clues and be the first to stamp our boxes. We really had a great time and appreciate them answering all of our questions. Really great people.

Good luck on your search and hope to meet you all on the trail some day!

Hikers & Hounds
