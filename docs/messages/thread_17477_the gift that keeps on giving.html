From: Takn'kat (takkat_attack@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-05 18:23:24 UTC-08:00
For Christmas this year, I made my mother a
letterboxing stamp, and got her a bag, a journal, "The
Letterboxers Companion" from the local Borders, and I
printed out some local clues in a little booklet.
She's a big fan of ferrets, so I thought I'd make a
cute little ferret stamp.

What do you think?

http://stainlessgrace.com/letterboxing.jpg

This photo is included in my portfolio under "logo
design" since that's the closest thing I could think
of relevant to someday landing a good design job.

Tak and I brought her on her first letterbox the day
after Christmas at Bigelow Hollow Park in CT. And
amazing day. She was so excited, and hasn't stopped
talking about it since. Add another letterboxer to the
world!

Anyone go letterboxing in RI the weekend after
Christmas? We did the Loch Ness Monster and Nokkun Wud
letterboxes. It was an amazingly warm day. And what a
beautiful walk in the woods!

Here's to a mild winter!

-takkat
