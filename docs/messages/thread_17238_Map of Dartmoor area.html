Mapquest.com has maps from all over the world, not just the U.S. On the
main page of their site, just select the UK as the country, then enter
Topsham or Exeter. That enormous green area on the left side of the map
(zoom out if you need to) is Dartmoor National Park.

Also, using the driving directions feature on the site, I found out that
it's 33.21 miles from Topsham to Princetown, which is roughly in the middle
of the park.
