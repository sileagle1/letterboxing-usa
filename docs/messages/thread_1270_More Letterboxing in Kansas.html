From: (Letterboxr@aol.com) | Date: 2000-09-23 21:33:00 UTC-04:00
Der Mad Stamper and his trusty sidekick Littlefoot have been adventuring in
the land of sunshine again. Here's a progress report: The Waconda Lake
letterbox in Mitchell County has been replaced. It had been unearthed
recently by park officials, who were gracious enough to arrange for its
return. The revised clues can be found at:
http://members.aol.com/letterboxr/usa/wacondlk.htm

Der 26th Mad Stamper box was placed last Sunday at the birthplace of the
Kansas state song. Clues to the "Home on the Range" Letterbox can be found at:
http://members.aol.com/letterboxr/usa/hotr.htm

Box number 27 is located near the World's Largest hand-dug well, at
Greensburg, in southern Kansas. Clues for the Big Well Letterbox are at:
http://members.aol.com/letterboxr/usa/bigwell.htm

More to come!!!
Der Mad Stamper
http://members.aol.com/letterboxr/derboxes.htm
