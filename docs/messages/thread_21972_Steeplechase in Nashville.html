Oh DebBee, you brought back memories with that one. I was just getting over a 3-week flu, still weak as a kitten when I tackled that one. Parked way before the road split and flagpole and walked (meadered would be a more apt term), pausing to speak to the many mini-dachshund owners. The lack of physical health prompted me to stop three or four times - but the photos are pretty from that beautiful October day, multi colored leaves strewn at random. At the top of the steps, I looked back to where the Durango was parked and reached in my pocket to discover the clues not there. A moment of epiphany, remembering putting the printout on the passenger seat. So I tried anyway, stepping over a scantily-clad college aged couple engaged in intimacy, but resulted in no find. A very funny day. I did find the Mystery box that trip though, so that was a plus too.
