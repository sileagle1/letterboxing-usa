Let's see: I've been a pauper, a puppet, a pawn, and a king...

I started out as a prelaw student. Switched out of that really
quickly when I saw what lawyers do.

Next, I happened upon a young Army recruit. Wound up in Vietnam, not
a good choice.

Switched back into the body of a biology major. Got the BS and MS
degrees and got really tired of hurting mice in PhD research.

Switched into a Health Physicist. Got an exciting trip up to help
recover the mess at Three Mile Island.

After tiring of the long hours (10 hr days, 7 days a week), I found a
body of a teacher at a technical junior college in Texas. That was a
sweet gig.

Unfortunately, I was hijacked to another body where I found myself in
Louisiana designing training for a reactor. I learned to bite the
tails and suck the heads on dem crawfish.

I found a letterboxer hitchhiking to California and I managed to wind
up here on the coast. Now I teach at a nice reactor, beautiful but
expensive area where I can not afford a house, plant stamps with
JugglerMouse, Emily the Rose, Sade from Nebraska, Pandora, and of
course Ash Raven. Pretty sweet deal but I may now start having to
feed those danged toy poodles. Gack!
