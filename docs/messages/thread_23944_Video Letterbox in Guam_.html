From: Jeremy Chapman (bravecomet@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-11-11 12:26:10 UTC+09:00
Hey,

My name is Jeremy Chapman; I discovered letterboxing a couple years ago,
and it's awesome. I hid my first letterbox last month, and I have two
questions about it.

1) I hid it while I was visiting the U.S.-owned island of Guam.
Understandably, Guam is not shown anywhere on the website's North America
map, even though it is technically part of America. Should I just try to
add the box in the "outside of North America" section?

2) Also, I have pictures and even video footage of the walk along the beach
that leads up to the spot where I hid the Guam box. I'd love to post the
video-hints on YouTube and then get a link to it from
letterboxing.org....what do we think? Possible?

Again, I've really enjoyed letterboxing with my girlfriend Amy - so thank
you!

I look forward to hearing from you...

Best,
Jeremy

Hafa adai, Jeremy! Guam, huh? Was that Taragi Beach? I have been
lucky enough to spend almost 10 years in Guam, and even met the love
of my life and had babies there. Always thought it would be letterbox
heaven.

Anyhoo, Guam is on the list of places for letterboxing.org, so just
enter your box normally. (It's down at the end of the list of
states). And if you want to add external links to you-tube, go right
ahead. You can make them html tags in your clues or just type them
out longhand. Great idea.
