Just got back from Long Beach and I found two terrific new boxes by
Chowder. Wonderful artwork and they take you to the cutest little
area along the water in California. It was a lovely day to be sipping
on a Jamba Juice and walking along the docks of the Marina. Getting a
gorgeous letterbox stamp was icing on the cake. :-)

For folks traveling to the LA area this summer, I would definitely
put these two boxes on my "must have" list. Naples Canal Conundrum
and The Marina Pacifica Plumbing Problem.

Also the Plumbing Problem should be studied by all serious urban
letterboxers! Very Clever!
--Amanda from Seattle
