DE, NY, RI boxes found

20030523

Along the Way (Port Mahon, DE): In place, dry, and intact.

20030524

Vanderbilt series (Hyde Park, NY): All four boxes in place, dry, and intact.

20030525

Seamon Park (Saugerties, NY): both boxes in place, dry, and intact.

Poet's Walk (Red Hook, NY): In place, dry, and intact.

20030527

Carolina South (Richmond, RI): In place, dry, and intact.

Wizard of Oz series (Richmond, RI): Scarecrow, Tin Man, and Cowardly Lion are
in place and intact. The Tin Man box was a little wet inside, but the
contents were safe inside plastic bags.

We had come back to finish the Oz series this visit, but had a scary,
unfortunate encounter with a fellow hiker. We had brought the Nina dog with us this
trip. A woman came along with two big dogs. They were a bit too much for her to
handle and one got the better of her and bit Nina pretty good. The poor
woman got bitten several times trying to get her dog off. Nina needed stitches and
the poor woman was absolutely horrified and apologized up and down (while
searching for her wedding ring she lost in the scuffle...thankfully she found
it). I tried to give her first aid while Christopher held the offending dog, but
all she wanted to do was get herself and the dogs home (they were dragging her
all over). I'm sure both of our dogs believe they saved us from some great
evil. Nina slept all the way to the vet's. So our boxing quest for today was cut
short, but we are just thankful it wasn't worse.

CSCM & Rustypuff

From: (budster2@juno.com) | Date: 2003-05-28 07:03:24 UTC-04:00
Quite an experience with those dogs! One of the hazards of lbing. Only
one in 100 dogs on the average (my statistics) will be that way. Our dog
"Bud" (he's blind) usually stops in his tracks and lets other dogs smell
him out, but a small few are hostile from the start. You probably will
not encounter such a situation again in your travels but if you do I was
thinking that a small plastic tarp that could be carried and used as a
shield ( like a matador uses) would be good to include in your lbing
kits.

of this yahoo site. But we are back in the loop again.

it.... Please Sun Shine in NY again........Happy letterboxing!!!
