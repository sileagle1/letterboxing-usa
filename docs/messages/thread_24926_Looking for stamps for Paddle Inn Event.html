Looking for stamps for Paddle Inn Event

From: Robyn (fivepucks2@aol.com) | Date: 2008-08-14 01:22:15 UTC
Hi all,
I thought I would post an invitation for anyone who may have in their
stamp stash or if anyone may be interested in carving a stamp for the
upcoming, Paddle In, event to be held Labor Day in Maine.

Stamps should be of a "Lake" nature. Things you would find in the
water, on the water, around the water. After the event, stamps can be
returned to you, forwarded to another event, or placed in more
permanent spots in Central Maine.

Thanks!
Robin's Nest
