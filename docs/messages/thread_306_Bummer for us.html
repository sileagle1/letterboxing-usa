[L-USA] Bummer for us

From: Thom Cheney (tcgrafx@imagina.com) | Date: 1999-02-26 20:29:13 UTC-08:00
Well... the letterbox hooligans have struck in Oregon. Brian, Maggie
the wonder beagle & I went out to check the Bethany Lake bunch of
l-boxes since we may get a visit from across the state this week. 2 of
the 4 are gone without a trace. One of mine & one of Mitch's. I called
& left word with him and have since received e-mail acknowledging.

Not bucking for pity here, Mitch has several other boxes out there & I
still have 3 left (big assumption, Brown's camp is undoubtedly under
quite a bit of snow right now).

So, being the stalwart l-box pioneers that we are, we will tough this
out & hustle to replace the missing boxes ASAP. A good opportunity to
carve a new stamp, although the logbook in RC#1 had Dan'l's stamp &
comments and will be missed.
