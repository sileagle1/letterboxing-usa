Dana Peak Letterbox
Placed: 30 Nov 02
Placed By: Samara
Location: Dana Peak Park, Harker Heights, Bell County, Texas

Dana Peak Letterbox

Dana Peak is located in Harker Heights, TX. To get to the start point
from I-35, take US 190 West and exit FM 2410 East. Continue East for
3.5 miles and turn right onto Commanche Gap (Follow the sign to "Dana
Peak"). Continue 2.5 miles, while you pass the "Goats for Sale," and
park at the bend in the road with the gate leading to a large path.
This is the start point.

Pass the gate and walk up the hill on the large path until you come
to a large washout on the right. Follow the path at 130 degrees.
Once the path narrows down, see the path up the hill to the right?
Don't take it, but start counting. Take 30 paces straight. Stop, turn
to 30 degrees and walk 5 paces to a clump of fallen trees. Look
inside the trees to find the Dana Peak Letterbox.

Stamp away! Due to the large amount of beer bottles atop the actual
peak, I didn't place the box there, but if you follow the path up the
hill, you'll see the small trail to the top, where there is a great
view of Stillhouse Hollow Lake. Enjoy.

Please let me know how the box is, as this is my first placement.
