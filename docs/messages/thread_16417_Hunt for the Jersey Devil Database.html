Hi all,

I decided to make a database for the NJ gathering so that we know who
all is coming and what they're bringing. I believe the original
intent was for it to be a potluck. I think there may have been some
mix up with that (not sure though) so I decided to bring burgers and
rolls for everyone so that no one is without lunch. We'll also be
bringing rice krispy treats. We'll be bringing our own beverage and I
suggest that everyone do the same. It makes it easier that way since
alot of people only drink diet drinks or don't drink soda or what
have you. I've been to other potlucks where everyone brought their
own drinks, and it's worked out beautifully. :)
If you plan on attending the gathering, please add your info to the
database. I hope letterboxingbee doesn't take any objection to me
creating the database. I just thought it would come in handy when
trying to plan on what to bring.

Hope to see alot of you there!
