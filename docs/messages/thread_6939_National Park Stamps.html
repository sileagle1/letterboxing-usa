Hello...
I've been reading the treads about putting the boxes on National Park Lands.
While I find it disappointing, I can understand their position.

However, if one must have a stamp from a National Park, go to the Visitor's
Centers. The NPS has a Passport Cancellation program where they sell
"Passports" and you take them to the various National Park Sites to have
them cancelled. You don't have to have the passport to use the cancellation
stamps.

While this isn't the best answer to the situation...It still gives us hikers
a reason to visit the National Parks.
