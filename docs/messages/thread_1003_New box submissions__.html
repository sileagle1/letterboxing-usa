Clues go to the list. This allows clues to be acted upon by all, and to
be put into the archives, without depending on a webmaster. So if your info
is necessary, interesting, etc in relation to a clue, send it to the list.
If it is technical, you don't like the formatting of your clue, etc, send
this off-list to the webmasters (webmasters@letterboxing.org).

The webmasters are a secret organization. It is not possible to reveal
who they are. All the names we use are aliases anyway. We set the rules,
pull the strings, and share the clues to secret boxes. We take volunteers,
so if you want to enjoy all of these benefits and more, contact us :-)

Currently, due to circumstance, all updates to the site go through me.
I travel quite a bit and have alot of other stuff going on, that is why
they take a long time to happen. I am expecting the whole process to
improve soon but I have no personal control over when (or if) that
will occur, so I cannot promise it.

I apologize for the inconvience but that is why clues should go to the
list. A good letterboxer will find them even if the webmasters are
a bit slow.
