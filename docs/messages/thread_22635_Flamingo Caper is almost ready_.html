Hi boxers:

The Lazy Letterboxer has not been living up to her name. Leominster,
MA is a flurry of activity, and she's been non-stop for days!

We look forward to spending the day with you all this Saturday,
October 1. Just a few reminders:

* Meet us in the parking lot of the National Plastics Museum [see
Atlas Quest link below to sign up if you haven't and for more info!]
at 9am.

* NO CLUES WILL BE HANDED OUT BEFORE 9am. You will understand why
once you begin your hunt. You may arrive before 9 to greet/exchange
with others, buy your raffle tickets, make up your car pool, buy a
boxing band, etc.

* Don't forget a potluck item that you can leave with us before you
register for your clue dossier.

* We encourage you buddying up with others for fuel conservation and
to help limit traffic. Your cars left behind in the parking lot will
be safe. There will always be someone there at the museum.

* You MUST stop your search at 2pm and report back to the Plastics
Museum, (follow signs in the building for our room's location) where
the potluck will begin at 2pm, raffle at 3pm and we MUST leave the
building by 4pm - since this is when the museum closes.

* Lastly, SAVE ALL OF THE PAPERS IN YOUR CLUE DOSSIER UNTIL THE
RAFFLE! :-)

Have fun and just get out there and find Percival, Luvvy's pink
plastic flamingo!!!
