From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-06-16 23:08:46 UTC
I am not sure specifically but in any case you should check in with
the staff of the refuge.

Discuss letterboxing with them and the desire to place a box in a low
impact fashion.

Emphasize that the goal is to get people to come and enjoy the
environment.

In the case of a NWR you need to be sure that the area you use is not
part of a fragile site that supports nesting in breeding season. At
those times the access might be restricted. Remember too that there
is often an open hunting season on NWR's and you need to take care in
planting.

The staff can guide you in choices.
