For those who really like being FIRST to get a box, we just wanted to
say that one of our boxes has been out for over a month now and still
has had no visitors! (as of yesterday anyway)

Also, for those of you interested in hitchhikers, we do try to keep a
pretty steady stream of travellers passing through our boxes, often

in one place for too long, we just might have to take them to NJ or
something! [ :-) ] Also, theres always the possibility of
finding an unlisted surprise, like Wandas first attempt at a
hand-carved stamp (The Nurse Log). So, its up to you to figure out
just where these things might be!
