From: Savannah Georgia (hawaii_gator@hotmail.com) | Date: 2001-03-08 18:42:04 UTC
We went out on our Snow Day (yeah!!) Tuesday and hiked around the pond at
Fisherville Brook, finding the Kurdziel's Knight at Dawn and Mummies in the
Morning and the Wiz's Gift to Renee. This is such a beautiful, peaceful
refuge (at least when covered in snow!) and we saw many birds, including an
owl! What fun it was to make the first tracks in the newly fallen snow, and
what a workout! Thanks for introducing us to another fantastic place.
