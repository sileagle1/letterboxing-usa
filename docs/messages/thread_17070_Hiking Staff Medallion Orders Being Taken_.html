Hi All!

I will go and coordinate a bulk order. I figured I will be able to
order the medallions and send them out for $3.35 each. I anticipated
the possibility of having to pay the $10 fee to have the image
redrawn as well as the shipping. I am not in this too make money.
If there are no objections, if there is money left over, I will use
it to plant a couple more letter boxes.

Please send your payments to:

James Dillon
PO Box 15
Talisheek, LA 70464

or paypal them to me at n0kwa@bellsouth.net (no credit cards).
If you don't have a paypal account, check them out at
www.paypal.com. Consider listing my email as the one referring you
if you set up a new account.

I would prefer payment by paypal or with bills registered with
www.wheresgeorge.com.

I also collect stamps, so interesting stamps on the envelop are
appreciated.

I will take orders through December 15, 2003, at which time I will
send the order to Hermann Werks. They estimate a 2 to 4 week turn
around. I am guessing that you will have your medallions by the end
of January or beginning of February.

If anyone wants to order a large number of medallions, let me know
and I can figure a discount for shipping and such.

Please feel free to email me at my personal address with any
questions.

Jim Dillon
