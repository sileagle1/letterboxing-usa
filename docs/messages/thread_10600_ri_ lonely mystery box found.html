I have looked for Dingos at Dinnertime several times with no sucess.
Some people are better at solving mysteries than others. I am not
very good at mystery boxes. When I make several ( or many) attempts
without sucess I put the clues away for a while. Otherwise I just
spin my wheels. This has happened to me with Dingos & with Lone Mass
Wolf's mystery. I will pick them up again some day & work on them
some more but there is only so much energy you can put into it
without results before you must move on.

It is funny... I tried to do Dingos thinking it would be fairly
easy. I went to where I thought it would be... but boy did I get
lost. None of the street names matched any of the street names on my
map. I was confused just on finding my way back home never mind
finding the box LOL. It takes a lot to get lost in RI but some how I
managed to!
