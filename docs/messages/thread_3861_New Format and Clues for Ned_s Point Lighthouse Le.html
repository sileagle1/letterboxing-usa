Letterbox Name: Ned's Point Lighthouse



Location: Entrance to Mattapoisett Harbor, Mattapoisett, Plymouth County, Massachusetts

Accessibility: The tower is open during July and August, Thursdays, 10 a.m. to noon. The grounds are open to the public every day.

Difficulty: Easy, this is a "drive by" box.

Date Placed: 03 August 2001 by the Lonemasswolf and You Inspire My Spirit.

All questions and comments should be directed to lonemasswolf@covad.net

For a Light History and Photographs please visit www.lighthouse.cc/nedspoint/index.html

Driving Directions:
(a). Take US Route 6 North to North Street in Mattapoisett, Massachusetts.

South on North Street for 0.7 miles to Water Street.

East on Water Street for 0.5 miles to Ned Point Road.

South on Ned Point Road for 0.7 miles to Ned Point Park.

(b). Take US Route 6 South to Marion Road in Mattapoisett, Massachusetts.

Southwest on Marion Road for 0.5 miles to Ned Point Road.

South on Ned Point Road for 0.7 miles to Ned Point Park.
