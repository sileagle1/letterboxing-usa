WA/OR: Silver Eagle Trip

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2003-11-16 23:35:04 UTC
I just got back from a great business/pleasure trip to
Seattle and wanted to thank all of you that placed so
many great boxes. I'm not used to so many boxes being
available in such short distances and it has spoiled me!
I managed to hit quite a few diverse areas of Washington
and Oregon (Portland area) during my whirlwind tour and
I ended up placing 5 (including 1 HH) of my own. I also
got to meet several letterboxers at the two events
organized in my honor thanks to DVN2R SKR, who also carved
the first Event stamp (Oyster Exchange). Special thanks
also to Rayvenhaus for carving the second Event stamp
and giving it to me as a keepsake. Both stamps are
awesome and I will treasure my memories of both events
forever. If you ever get a chance to visit this area, be
sure to let them know. They love letterboxing and are
some of the friendliest people I have ever met! Thanks
again y'all, for making my visit special.

Silver Eagle (P68 F292 X25 E2)
