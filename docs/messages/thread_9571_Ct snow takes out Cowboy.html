From: mothermoo2001 (cstearns07@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-12-06 20:54:13 UTC
Not even this snow but the previous fall on the eve of Thanksgiving
and in the category of "boys will be Boys "the cowboy and his cousin
had a great snowball fight at our farm. Of course like sword duels
the fight went up and down hill around vehicles and then fatefully
out onto the loading dock where the Cowboy lost his footing in the
snow and ice and fell 8 feet. He army crawled to the top and the
fight resumed from knees the balls flew until the older cousin gave
in. With victory in hand he limped off to find the older sister and
get a ride home. Now the Cowboy awaits a MRI to get a better look at
a chip in the ankle bone....HMMM. So Mom has become an attendent to
the one on crutches a system he is most in favor of! I have been
spending down time carving , look for my Town Gazebo coming soon.
This slowed me but I've done 2 seasons in the snow. Its no big deal
even the Bic pens worked under a foot of snow and ice! Once in awhile
a clue is covered up or a box is frozen in - Oh well . Ink pads
slipped into an inner pocket work fine after just a minute in the
body warmth. Oh how I missed getting out this week but maybe a little
over the weekend. Leader of the Pack
