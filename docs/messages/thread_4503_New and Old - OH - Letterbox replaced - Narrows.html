This note is about a hybrid letterbox/geocache of mine.

The old box location, known as "The Narrows" on the geocache pages
and as "Little Miami Respite" on the letterboxing pages was popular
enough that a bit of a worn down trail was being formed from the main
trail to the box. That is too much of a good thing.

I have moved the box and written new clues for both the geocaching
community and the letterboxing community. I have marked the old
clues for both communities as being permanently retired.

The clues for letterboxing are at

http://www.qsl.net/ws8g/narrows2.htm

For fans of Shavian, it is another entry for you to practice your
reading.
