New New York Letterbox at the Glen

Start at the Starting line.
At Mario Andretti bear 240, to the parking lot, and thence upstream,
entering the tunnel.
Count 574 steps going always upstream.
Turn and head dowmstream while continuing to climb along the native
course.
Pass 611 steps and then encounter a stone wall on the left.
Bear north at the stump and pipe, along the fallen logs, 52 paces.
Behind the remains of an old stump.
Shhh...... If the time is right, you can hear the engines!


Return either the way you came, or follow the native course.
