Ingram's pond is just west of Millsboro DE, which is on the inland side of the bays west of Bethany Beach. Bethany is south of Rehoboth. Check out this link for a map. http://oaspub.epa.gov/pls/tmdl/waters_list.control?state=DE&wbtype=LAKE%2FRESERVOIR%2FPOND Click on the Ingrams pond link.
