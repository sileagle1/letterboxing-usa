Angel's Kiss ***new box in GA!!!!***

From: angelyn_adams <design@clovegirl.com> (design@clovegirl.com) | Date: 2003-02-24 00:01:20 UTC
Angel's kiss is only difficult because to get it you have to find the
car that it is hidden in. This is a nighttime excursion, as the only
way to find it is at night, when Angel is working at the copy shop.
In Norcross, GA there is a Road named after a very HAPPY EX-PRESIDENT.
It ends at a road named after a BRIDGE. The two meet at a corner and
there is a COPY SHOP there. Behind the COPY SHOP look for a BURGUNDY
SATURN that says ANGEL in the back glass. Next year, the tag of the
car will be ANGELYN. Under the bumper the box is velcroed. Be gentle,
it's a handcarved stamp of my signature logo, hence "Angel's kiss."
I will keep purple ink in the box for you, and soon I will have
notecards for reports. Thanks, and enjoy your adventure!


PS if you should happen to find it during the day, I'm thinking of
employing a daytime stamp :)
Angel Adams
