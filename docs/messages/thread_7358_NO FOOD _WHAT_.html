Hey Letterboxers.

Team Jedi tried something..

We put out 3 sets of two boxes. In each area we had 2 boxes. One of the
boxes we set out and the other one we set out about 5 feet away from the first box BUT we handled the box after eating something. There was nothing
inside these boxes. (Just Empty Boxes)

GUESS WHAT!!! All 3 of the boxes
that had the smell of the food from our fingers were gone or Chewed up!! The other 3 did not have a nick in them and were still there!

Make sure when placing or hunting
you do not have the smell of food on
your hands. The Oils will hold the oder
of the food for a long time. With critters and their very good nack for
smelling, They think it's Lunch time..
