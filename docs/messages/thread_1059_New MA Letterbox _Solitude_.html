Solitude
This is an easy cryptic letterbox somewhere in the State of
Massachusett. Should be quite easy for those who like good quotes. Don't
forget a trail map.

LOCATION: Somewhere in MA
ESTABLISHED: 23-JUL-2000
DIRECTIONS: You will need to figure this out yourself.
CLUE DIFFICULTY: Cryptic Very Easy
TERRAIN: Easy

CLUES:
"I went to the woods because I wished to live deliberately, to front
only the essential facts of life, and see if I could not learn what it had
to teach, and not, when I came to die, discover that I had not lived.
- ????"

Standing on the granite "cliff" facing south, under the top ledge.
