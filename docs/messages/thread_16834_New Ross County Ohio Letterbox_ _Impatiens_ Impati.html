Title: Impatiens, Impatience. Location: Chillicothe, Ohio.
Difficulty: Easy. Placed 11-13-03 by Salamander. CLUES: Go to
the Northside Branch of the Chillicothe/Ross County Public Library.
(This is located at 550 Buckeye Street, Chillicothe.).Find the pay
phone near the parking area. Stand two feet in front of the phone
and take a compass reading of 120 degrees. Walk in that direction to
a single evergreen type shrub in the landscaping near the patio.
The camo-taped container is hidden at the base of the shrub.
