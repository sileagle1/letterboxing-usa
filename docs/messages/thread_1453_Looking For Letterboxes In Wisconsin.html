Hello Fred,
Since I'm living in Green Bay, and placed a good
portion of the boxes you're looking for, I'll do my
best to update you here.
The Door Co. Boxes should all be there except for the
Sherwood Point Lighthouse box. (The property got sold
and developed before I knew it and could yank the
box!)
-Oh, and last time I went to look for the LaSalle Park
box I could not find that one either.
The UWGB boxes are fine except for the one by the golf
course. I went looking for it myself not long ago and
could not find it. I found a golf ball, but no
letterbox.
Dierdra placed a series of 8 letterboxes along the
East River Trail which combine to form a larger
picture. These haven't been posted to the site yet,
nor do I know if they are all there. I found them
last year and have not had the chance to look since.
I think you can find the clues archived on e-groups.
I'll have her re-post them just to be sure.
Let me know if there's anything else I can do to help
you out in your hunt.
Good luck!

Steven Stary
