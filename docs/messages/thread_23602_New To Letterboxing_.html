From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-07-25 18:30:49 UTC-04:00
Hi Cinema Boxers,

Just wanted to invite you to join the Letterbox Great Lakes Yahoo! group. We are a regional group of letterboxers in the western Great Lakes states -- MI, IL, IN, OH, WI, MN. The regional group is a great place to get to know others in your area, learn about gatherings near you and just keep up with what is going on in letterboxing at a level a little closer to home.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/

Also, if you are interested, there is a Yahoo! group for new letterboxers that is a good place to get all your questions answered and interact with other boxers who are where you are with the game.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/

Welcome! Sounds like you are already having a great time with it!

SpringChick

I am SOOOO excited about all these midwest newbies!! Welcome to
letterboxing. It's an obssession I'm not afraid to admit that I
love! One word of advice about those preprinted clues in
folders...take a look at the clues on line before you head out. You
never know when they might change. I've had to change some of mi
clues due to animal invasion or other legitimate reasons and I hate
to do it thinking about those preprinted clues leading someone
astray. I've learned to check my preprinted clues before I go. You
might be glad you did.
Goose Chaser
