Hi Sam ~

I just wanted to reassure you.  Not everyone finds 100's of boxes, or even a box every time they go out.  Boxes are still few and far between here in Maine and those that are here are nice and challenging.  There have been several times that my husband and I have made 3 tries before finally nabbing a particular box.  Don't give up.  I am just of the opinion that the boxes are greater prizes when they are hard to find!  Keep it up.  (By the way, I have never found 5 in one day or even in one weekend, probably rarely have I found that many in one month!)

Memlili
