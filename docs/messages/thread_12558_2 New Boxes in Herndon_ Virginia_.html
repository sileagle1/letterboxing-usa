Hi all, below are two new letterboxes placed in
Fairfax county, in the town of Herndon.

http://www.mindspring.com/~alberth/lbna/letterboxes.html#homestead

The Ol' Homestead and Ice Cream Sundae were placed in
Folly Lick Stream valley park, May 1st. They are part of
a group of boxes, all placed along the banks of two streams
that run through Northeast Herndon.

Their neighbors:
A Slice of Cake at Sugarland Run park
Warm Apple Pie at Sugarland Run park
Frosted Cupcake at Runnymede park


On the same page as above, you can find the clues to all
