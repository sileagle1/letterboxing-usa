new letterbox near Bellingham, WA - Blanchard Hill

From: Christine Stewart (clstewart29@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-03-22 12:29:17 UTC-08:00
I submitted this a few months ago, but it has not been posted, yet. I am
not sure if it fell through the cracks. It was planted by Michaela (7) and
Isaac (5). If anyone visits it, please let me know how it looks. We
haven't been back to check on it yet.

Take the I-5 to the Alger exit (240) west. Turn left at Barrell Springs Rd.
Pass Shaw Rd and turn right on B-1000 Rd to Blanchard Hill. Pass the
first parking lot and keep driving up the hill. Park at the second parking
lot. Walk back down to Lily and Lizard Lake trail. Follow the trail.
Cross the bridge and keep following the trail. Turn right at the Y in the
trail. Follow up the hill. Cross a small creek bed. Just on the left, up
the hill is a huge stump. Behind the stump to the right, under a root is
the letterbox.

We look forward to checking on it and finding lots of new stamps in it!!
Christine Stewart
