When you are scanning, pay attention to the resolution of the scan. If
you are scanning for use on the www, you needn't make it any better than
100 dpi. Most monitors display below that level. JPEG's are normally
compressed and have the added bonus of adjustable resolution. I am
using Photoshop & all of this is inherently adjustable and tunable...
not sure about the program you are using.

Good luck & contact me offlist if you have more questions!
