MoJo JoJo is the arch villian of the Powerpuff Girls! He was sent traveling
by a mighty >punch< from a flying ButterCup! At the same time 3 travel bugs
were released from three separate "lettercaches" and a traveling letterbox
[Professor Utopia At Large] was kidnapped!!!! The three travel bugs,
ButterCup, Blossom & Bubbles are hoping to rescue Professor Utopia and
capture MoJo JoJo!
...................................
..........................................................
MoJo JoJo would like to steer clear of these four. But if you are an upright
citizen you will do your best to see that MoJo JoJo is brought to justice.
Please keep him captive and release him only into another properly secured
letterbox or geocache/letterbox combination.


If , by chance, MoJo JoJo should come into contact with Prof. U. or any of
the Powerpuff girls .... then I beg you to record their adventures in MoJo
JoJo's log book. .......................................................
Also please stamp MoJo Jojo into any log books you encounter along the way.
It is possible that his pursuers might visit that very location. Your
diligence in this matter will surely aid them in their important mission!!
.......................................................

Last but not least, after properly securing MoJo JoJo into another letterbox
or "lettercache" please send a note stating who found him, when and where to
letterbox-usa@egroups.com . But do not state where you dropped him off !
That could be used by his co-villians in a rescue attempt!
