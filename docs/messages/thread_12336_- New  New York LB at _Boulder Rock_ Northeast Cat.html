Boulder Rock Letterbox
(elevation 2400 feet)

Placed by J&G&"Bud" on April 20,2003 @ 2pm.

Location: Northeast Catskills of NY - Green County

Closest Town: Haines Falls

Trail Map #40 and #41 (detail insert) Grid 3.5 P

Stamp: Owl (originally at the Overlook Hotel)

There are many ways to get to this Lb. You can start at the Schutt road
parking area which is on the outside of the park and hike the 2 or so
miles following the blue and red markers which lead to the LB.
or
The Scenic way is to walk through the park to the Catskill Mountain House
Site and hike up from this side of South Mountain to the LB. Views all
over the place from this approach.

This picture and this clue should make it easy to find this LB.

"On the top where no body should go is 1912 etched in stone,
If you push the LB too much it will fall and be out of touch,
So follow the channel in the shadow and you will gain
Another map showing an "x" where there is a downed plane"
