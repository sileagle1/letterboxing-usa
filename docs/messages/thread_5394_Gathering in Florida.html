Hi,
 
Once you're used to the Florida heat, summer may not seem that bad, but I lived in Dunedin (just north of Clearwater) for three years and vastly prefer the sunshine state in January.
 
In fact, I put on an arts event at DisneyWorld last January, and it was gorgeous!
 
I know that January is hit-or-miss, but it's my favorite time of year in Florida.  And the crowds at the theme parks are nearly as low as in that wonderful vacant time between Thanksgiving and Yule/Christmas. 
 
So, by all means, schedule a summertime letterboxing/BBQ in St. Pete!  It sounds fabulous!  But I'll lean towards January, and the middle of the state.
