(CT) great day of hiking

From: Phyllis (rel@neca.com) | Date: 2001-11-09 21:31:36 UTC-05:00
We went hiking today in Vernon Connecticut. The first hike was the
Rockville spur rail to trails that got us warmed up for the next two. We
did the Tortoise and the Hare that brook is so beautiful. My son who is
6 just loved jumping over the brook and trying to find a place he could
cross back over. And the Brail trail has alot of history about Valley
Falls that was great.. We also did the Little Bird on a Branch which has
a really great place for trout fishing. The brook is so nice that I want
to come back and walk it in the summer time. We also invited 3 family
members to come with us letterboxing and I think there hooked. So maybe
there are three more letterboxers to add to are gang.
What a great day in Vernon Ct.
