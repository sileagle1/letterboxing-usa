PO,
Even if they are straight Geocaches, I would not worry too much about
needing a GPS.
Having pretty much exhausted the nearby letterboxes, I thought I would try
some local Geocaches.
I found 18 out of 19 this week without a GPS, and barely using my compass. I
still think that one cache might be missing.
I am not big on the Treasure Trade, but I do like the custom buttons
that many of the caches builders supply.
It is also as good as getting a stamp image!
Remember, the first thing they tell you is EXACTLY where it is.
