Exeter, RI letterbox succumbs to backhoe?!

From: Bernie Mulligan (bfmulligan@mindspring.com) | Date: 2002-04-15 20:31:10 UTC-04:00
We hope we're wrong. After all, we've struck out on several recent attempts
for various reasons. We tried to find the Mercy Brown, Exeter Vampire
letterbox today. The clues were easy until we reached the back of the
cemetery. Then we saw signs of recent heavy equipment work, apparently for
the purpose of expanding the cemetery. We fear that the big rock that was
hiding the letterbox was one of the ones that was rolled over, with much
movement of earth around it. That would be sad, considering that Mercy was
a real historical character with a great supernatural legend built up
around her life, death, and thereafter. This box was also a group effort.
Can someone give us good news?

We took pictures of the grave and mausoleum. Nice idea for a letterbox
theme.

Bernie and Mimi
