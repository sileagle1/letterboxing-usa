Even though we only had one day, it was well worth the many miles and hours
to make the trip. Thank you Sir B and Jugglermouse for hosting such a fun

Leader plans to plant one in our area and maybe we'll see plenty of
letterboxers out our way. We're looking forward to your next event. We're already
talking about a return trip.
