RI letterboxing report: Beautiful day of boxing

From: mulbie (mulbie@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-03-24 13:32:42 UTC
Hi folks. Just wanted to report that The Yachtsman and I, along with
Tweety and Mr. Coon had a glorious day of letterboxing in southern RI
yesterday. Visited The Drew Clan's "Wooden Carousel" series in
Westerly, and found both the Napatree Pt letterbox and the Watch Hill
letterbox in fine shape. Wow was the area busy with people. Little
Narragansett Bay had a gaggle of Brants and a few swans bobbing around
in the choppy water. We also finally found the Great Gathering stamp
(winter 2001) now that the ground is unfrozen. Nobody had visited
since April of 2002! Finally stopped in at the Quonochontaug mystery box.

Also wanted to tell Team Greendragon that we loved their "No _ _ _ _
Sherlock" stamp which all four of us have visited sometime in the last
week. On Saturday The Yachtsman and I also found the two "Meander to
the Marsh" boxes at Touisset marsh in good shape. Altogether
wonderful to be back at letterboxing in the nice spring weather.

Mary Manatee
