A report on and photos of the first Mid Missouri Letterboxers gathering is
posted at http://mymissourian.com. If it is not on the front page, check
the sports section. We had a great time, thanks to Ron.

By the way, citizen journalism sites like MyMissourian are springing up
all over the country. Anyone can submit material to such a site, so it is a
very good way to talk about letterboxing without having to maintain your own
blog. Ask your newspaper about it.
