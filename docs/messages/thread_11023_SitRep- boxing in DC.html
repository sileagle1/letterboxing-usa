SitRep- boxing in DC

From: thelionandsanddollar (pmoriarty01@snet.net) | Date: 2003-03-05 04:20:29 UTC
We just returned from a (mostly) fruitful trip to Washington DC where
we picked up 10 boxes. The boxes we found in good order were:the
Ram, Inventor, Independence, Mr Jefferson's Box, Taft Memorial, Skull
& Bones, Orion, and It's a Holiday. We also came across the Columbia
HH, but we left it there to continue it's travels rather than return
it to CT. We did not find Teddy's Box- too much snow and mud to
really look. We also missed the Egypt Explorer, but I think we were
in the wrong place for that one. I must say that urban boxing is
VERY different than what we are accustomed to. It takes a whole
different set of skills. A lot of fun, but we're glad to be home!
Thank you so much to Scarab of the Doubtful Guests! We had a
wonderful lunch talking about boxing. It's always great to meet
other boxers. It's nice to know we're not the only crazy people out
there. Maybe we should set up a support group for LB addicts!
OK, so I'm a little tired from the 8 hour drive. Anyway, we had a
great time, so thank you all of the planters down in DC!
Patrick & Amy
