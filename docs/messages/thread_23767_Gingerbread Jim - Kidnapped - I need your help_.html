Introducing:

The Adventures of Gingerbread Jim - Box #10 - Kidnapped

At the age of 65, Gingerbread Jim at spent most of his years
wandering around the country, visiting people he knew and meeting
people he never met. Somewhere down the raod, he must have met
someone that didn't like him very much. Sent to me this weekend, was
a ransom note and what I thnk might be a clue to his where-abouts.
The ransom note states that Jim is safe, but if we ever want to see
him again, we need to send $40,000. The clue is a picture.

To help find out where Gingerbread Jim is being kept, go to my blog
and look at the picture clues. http://teamnewhampshire.blogspot.com
They all have something in common. Not the location itself, but in
which town/city it is in. When you visit my blog to see the clues,
post your suggestions as to where you think he is, using the
comments at the bottom of each post. Only a few posted suggestions
will be used on the main messageboard to assist others in finding
Gingerbread Jim. The better the suggestions, the better the clues.

There is only a month before something bad is to happen to
Gingerbread Jim. I don't know what it is, and I don't know if I want
to find out. Help Gingerbread Jim come home.

david (team new hampshire)
http://teamnewhampshire.blogspot.com
