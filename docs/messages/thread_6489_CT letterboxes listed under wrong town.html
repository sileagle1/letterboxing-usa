Dear Web "Master" Drew  :)
 
The "Seven Falls" letterbox is listed under Hadlyme, but it's really on the Haddam/Middletown border.  This is an easy mistake to make, considering that most of the towns along this stretch of the river are named either Haddam, Lyme, or some combination of the two (Middle Haddam, Haddam, Haddam Neck, East Haddam, Hadlyme, Lyme, East Lyme, Old Lyme.... have I forgotten any?).
 
Also, the "Bear Hill" letterbox is really in Middletown, not Hadlyme.
 
Kudos to some really nice letterboxes!!
 
-Laura
