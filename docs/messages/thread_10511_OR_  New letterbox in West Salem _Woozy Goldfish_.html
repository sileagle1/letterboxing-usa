Woozy Goldfish Letterbox

Placed: 29 Jan 2003
By: dvn2r ckr, rnrB & trkr

Wallace Marine Park
Glen Creek Road NW (500 block)
West Salem
Polk County
Oregon

CLUES: This little caper occurred in Wallace Marine Park so that's
just where you'll have to be to solve it. It seems a little
goldfish escaped from the Willamette River and is feeling a bit
woozy these days. He really needs your help getting him revived so
he can soon return to the water. He so much wanted to go on an
adventure and do what 2 legged 'air-breathers' do for a bit but his
adventure has gotten him into a bind (or a box, that is). Armed
with a sense of adventure and not much else--he jumped out of the
river, up the boat launch ramp and onto the street. He wriggled
down the road, heading West under the trestle (as the vehicles go).
He wriggled and flopped along the road veering to the right but
staying left of the grand trees, boulders and curb. He flopped and
he flopped past 5 'no parking' signs. At the fifth sign he heard
something odd, so he flopped right around and hopped 15 times back
retracing his tracks thinking he could hear it a bit better from
there. At that moment, he was on a little striped mound. He heard
something else that really startled him, so he dashed into the woods
toward the river and hid in the entangled roots behind (NE side) the
nearest large tree on the left. Here he remains feeling a little
scared, quite woozy and desperate for your help. Please discreetly
find him, stamp a little life into him and return him to his hiding
place. He's a little orphan so take good care of him. Email
comments to blee@pobox.com
