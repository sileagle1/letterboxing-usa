CT - update on Mansfield boxes

From: Small Bear (summerlove23@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-03-22 20:19:06 UTC
Wandering Frog, Jessie, Small Bear and Hummingbird hit the trails in the
Mansfield meca of letterboxing today. We visited the Pink Panther series
(all in good shape - beautiful area to hike in), Air Mail (hey Chuck, you're
log book is almost full!), Tiger Cubs at Mansfield Middle School, Plane
Spotter (pretty wet, we dried it out, this log book is also almost full),
and Bed Rug (also wet and now dried out). We had a great time, though we
were sad not to have found any hitchhikers. Some day! Be sure to visit the
new box in Hebron at Gay City - it was planted by Jessie and Wandering Frog
for my 21st b-day. Have a great weekend everyone, it's finally time to take
advantage of this beautiful weather!
