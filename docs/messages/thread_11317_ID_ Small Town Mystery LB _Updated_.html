Small Town Mystery Letterbox (Updated)

Placed by A-bear (jedaryn02@yahoo.com) on 20 March
2003
Somewhere in Idaho!!

Description: Easy once you figure out the town.

Small Town Mystery Clues: In the small town of _ _ _
_, she waits for you to find her. She is a Young
Adult book title of eight letters _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
written by Jerry Spinelli. Once you know the title go
to the town in Idaho with the first half of her name.
Find the House of Knowledge and the isle which holds
her book. At the end of her isle, sit on the red seat
by the window. Reach below with your right hand and
find the micromagnet-letterbox.

Be discreet, as the owners of this establishment do
not know she is there. Have fun!!
