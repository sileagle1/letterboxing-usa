Oh, now I get it. The skeeters were FIERCE. The heavy rains coming after
summer of almost no rain in the south part of town this summer seems to have
brought a season's worth of mosquitoes all in one day. Folks, don't even
think about boxing around here without mosquito repellent, and if you are in
frail health you might want to give the wetlands areas a pass, what with the
threat of West Nile. I doused myself with Off, including under my shirt,
and they still found a couple of spots to nip me.
