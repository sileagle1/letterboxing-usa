From: browjaso62 (browjaso62@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-09-09 17:10:22 UTC

On Sunday my wife and I went to Troutdale to search for Letterboxes.
Last week we found Love Hitchhiker #3 at ponytail falls. We dropped
it off in one of the boxes we found on our recent trip.

Here is what we found.

Blue Lake - Box was in the right place but was on top of some logs.
We hid it a little better than we found it.

Broughton's Bluff - The hike to this box was more steep and with
difficult terrain than we expected but we made it to a live box.

Beaver Creek #1 - lots of steps. Box alive and well.

Oxbow Series - Box 1 was there but we couldn't find box 2. We found
a what we think the spot was. There was a good place for a letterbox
there but it was empty.

Riverbend - A recent plant from Funhog. We found the box alive and
previously unfound.
