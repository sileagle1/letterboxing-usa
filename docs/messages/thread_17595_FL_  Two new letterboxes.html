Hi folks. The Yachtsman, our son, Moonshine, and I just posted the
directions to two new letterboxes in Florida. Hope anyone else
headed down there this winter will also add some boxes -- the state
doesn't seem to have enough for those of us used to the density of
letterboxes in New England.

One letterbox is outside of St Augustine in Guana River State Park
(Guan River State Park Letterbox); the other is southeast of Kissimee
in the Disney Wilderness Preserve (Snowy Egret Letterbox). Have fun
and let us know if you find them, please!
