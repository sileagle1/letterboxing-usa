MD: The Box Skippers find a hitchhiker in their very first box

From: theboxskippers (sas1470@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-08-06 01:43:22 UTC
Well, it took the inexperienced but enthusiastic Box Skippers 2 days
(8/2/03 - 8/3/03) to successfully complete Mighty Mouse's Pilgrimage
(our first) battling thunderstorms, man eating gnats, and apparent
blindness. (I swear I looked in the same spot the day before and it
was not there!)

Our initial victory was made even sweeter when we found The World's
Slowest Hitchhiker with Mighty Mouse. He made the trip to the next
box we found.

By the way, that box would not be South Mountain Trinity #1, since we
could not find it and it seems to be widely reported as MIA..
