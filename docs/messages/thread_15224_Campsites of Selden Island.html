Campsites of Selden Island

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-08-20 06:34:35 UTC-04:00
Yesterday, SY and Sunshine ferried Wanda, Pete and I over to Selden Island to
get their new stamps! What a great series and hard boxes to get to! The
island is fairly rugged outside of the campsites, which makes getting to the
boxes harder, but not difficult. This is not your average "take this trail"
search! We had loads of fun and had a nice mini picnic afterwards, thanks to Wanda
who brought some munchies for the event! Hitchhikers were broken out, and we
stamped away!

A very special THANK YOU to Sunshine and SY for helping us get out to the
island! I had a great time and it was nice to get out on the water again (I
haven't been ALL YEAR.. sigh)!
