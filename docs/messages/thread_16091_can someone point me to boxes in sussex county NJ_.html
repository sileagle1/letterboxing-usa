Letterboxing fact about NJ: By the lates count on the LBNA list,
fully 20% of the boxes in NJ are mystery boxes, and would not be
listed by county. See if you and your host can figure any out.
Geographical fact about Warren County, NJ: It borders on both NY and
PA. Silent Doug's boxes in Dingman's Ferry, PA are right over the
mountain and river from Augusta, and Bushkill is then down the river.
