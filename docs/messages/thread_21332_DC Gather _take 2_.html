Kreepy Kelli, Chrissy and I ventured down from the frozen north to the 35
degree land of DC to attend the DC Gather at the Pentagon City Mall. We had a
great time, meeting up with old friends (Scarab, Tempus Fugit, and Sewsobizzy),
and meeting many new friends! We played a great game called Clue! Many people
who attended the gather carved people, weapons, and rooms from the game "Clue"
and we had to find out "who done it." It was SOOOOOO much fun, though as we
were wrapping up, the security guards kicked us out of the mall for loitering
in the food court. Grrrrrrrr! (There were PLENTY of other places for people
to sit!)

Anyway, Scarab then called Chevy's Mex (which Scarab had reservations for at 5:
30) and asked if we could come early (say 1:00????), and they were gracious
enough to have us for lunch instead of dinner! We had a great time, and they
didn't have to kick us out!!!! :-)

Thanks Scarab for a GREAT DAY!

Music Woman, Kreepy Kelli, and Chrissy
