A hearty thank you to ESAK for the well placed Giuffrida park letterboxes.
Nice carvings too!

I found Chauncey Peak, Timber and Nosebreaker this afternoon in a snow
and ice covered challenging climb. Though I was careful to take a
roundabout approach to the boxes as possible - light falling snow
and winds promised that any footprints would be quickly covered.

My faithful canine companion was a willing partner and made the steep
climb up to chauncey peak more easily than I!

This was outing number two for me, and man am I ever hooked!
Anyone considering a couple of hours in Central Connecticut should check
out these boxes! Nicely done!
