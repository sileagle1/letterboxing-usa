From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-09-01 04:26:04 UTC
Yesterday, on August 30th, the VERY FIRST THING I did upon
returning to Portland was to head off to Gabriel Park and replace
the two missing boxes. If you saw a strange guy who looked like
he hadn't taken a shower for several days and was sleeping in
his car walking around with a letterboxing kit, THAT WAS ME! =)

Anyhow, the two missing boxes have been replaced and I
checked up on the others and can report they're still alive and
well. The series is complete for those who want to go back out
and catch the killer.

None of the clues have changed, but for those who've already
found the boxes before, the "button" letterbox is in a new location
since the old one is no longer feasible. It's in the same area,
though.

Enjoy!
