A short time ago a letterboxer named Glenn rocked the LBNA boat (again).
Randy Hall declared a Poll to see if Glenn should be banned but a the same
time unannounced he banned Glenn. Glenn won the poll 51% to 49%. It didn't
matter, seems it was a Randy Hall dog and pony poll!

This was followed by Monica, A.D. and a few others catty types accusing Bill
Burk of evil. A.D. posted a message Bill had sent another user in jest and
twisted it into an evil threat to others. A.D. now knows what she posted was
not true but doesn't feel the need to right her misinformation Monica spent
a week showing the true hateful person she is by kicking the dead horse
again and again.

The end result, Glenn and Bill's clues have been pulled off the LBNA site.
Bill still has access to the LBNA group (??) but his clues aren't allowed on
the LBNA site. It is odd that a site claiming to be the "official"
Letterboxing site would blacklist users clues due to their group politics or
personality. It was one thing to ban Glenn from the talklist after Bill is
really a sorry situation. To remove all of their clues from the website is a
bit much. Now they have taken the away the pleasure many could have received
from looking for these Washington boxes. The clues caused no harm yet it
seems everyone must be punished. Bill is punished because he is Glenn's
friend. Pathetic

Neither Randy Hall or his webmasters will respond to any mail or posts
seeking more information on this issue. At least to those that question what
happened. I wonder why? Randy had no problem making a public condemnation of
Glenn why not do the same to Bill if he has done something wrong? Something
in LBNA land stinks!
