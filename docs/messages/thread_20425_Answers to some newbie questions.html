We seem to have a lot of newbies joining us in the last few days.
Welcome, all. We're glad you could join us.

There are many fantastic resources out there to help answer some of
your questions, including FAQ and Getting Started pages at:

Letterboxing.org:
http://www.letterboxing.org/faq/faq.html

Silent Doug's site:
http://www.letterboxing.info/

AtlasQuest:
http://www.atlasquest.com/

Also, if you look in the files section for this group
(http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-usa/files/), the top
folder -- "A Folder of Useful Information" -- is chock-full of really
helpful tips and info. The file called "terms.txt" will go a long way
toward helping you decipher a lot of the terms you'll see people
use on the list.

You may also want to check out the fantastic newboxers' group:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/

Happy boxing!
