CT Gathering, Hampton, 9-13-03

From: Chuck Straub (woodschuckstraub@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-30 21:47:30 UTC-04:00
The Ginger Meets Molly gathering will be held at Goodwin Forest in Hampton
CT 11:00 am, Saturday September 13th. This may be the last time to get both
the Looking For Molly HH from Oregon and the Molly looking for Ginger HH.
Both will be retired soon after this event. There will also be about 10
boxes planted along the trails for the event and will only be out a limited
time before they are removed. Boxes are being made and stamps carved by 6
different CT letterboxers. Hot dogs, soda and bottled water will be
available. More details will come along with directions. Hope you all can
make it to the gathering......Chuck and Molly
