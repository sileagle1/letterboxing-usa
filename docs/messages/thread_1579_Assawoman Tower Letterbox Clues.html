Assawoman Tower Letterbox Clues
Assawoman WMA
Near Ocean City, Maryland
Placed by Squirrel on November 25, 2000
Easy

Finding the letterbox is not nearly as difficult as finding Assawoman WMA but
it is well worth a visit. There are lots of ponds and places for birds to
hang out and for you to find when you are not looking for letterboxes. Stop
at the tower (duh!) and begin your search. Go to the tower leg on the right
side that is closest to the water. From there you will see a faint trail
leading into the woods. Just after you enter the woods take 13 steps at 340
degrees to a tree. Look to the base of that tree down deep under the pine
needles for the letterbox.
