I'm New, Found my 1st one in ME and got a Hitchiker to boot!

From: strout99 (strout99@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-07-09 23:56:44 UTC
Hi All,
Well, I learned about Lbing from an article in Yankee Magazine.Needless to say I was intrigued. Got some supplies together and went in search of my first letterbox in Brunswick/Topsham, ME....Rings in Heaven (directions in LBNA site). First try was a complete dud, 2nd try was successful and to my great surprise I discovered the FFA Hitchiker that was originally placed in KY, travelled to VT then to ME. I'm "hooked" and am planning to place a few in southwestern Maine from Brunswick south, so keep your eyes peeled.

Marilyn

Welcome, and I am glad to hear you enjoyed the Rings
of Heaven box. I didn't plant it - but I did stop in
to find it on a trip up this winter. It really is a
wonderful location and neat hike!

Another great and fun place to check out is the one in
the Kittery area. Last I heard some or all of the
ones in Freeport (Wolf Neck State Park) had gone
missing. Maybe someone here can give you a update -
or do a quick search of the archives. I seem to
remember it being posted in very early spring.

Good luck, welcome, and happy boxing!!

Tig
