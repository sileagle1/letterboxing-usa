And along those lines, we've (more or less) completed our Conanicut
Island Series, with 4 boxes on Jamestown in Rhode Island. All have
beautiful views of Narragansset Bay and several scenic lighthouses, and
all are quite easy both for clues and for hikes. One of the boxes may
disturb, due to it's position on "nonpublic" land, which is sort of a
letterbox rule breaker....but the site is just too breathtaking to
leave out of the series. We may plant 2 more in this series this
winter, but it's good to go as is.

There are a couple of other new boxes with views of the spots on
Jamestown as well, all on the "clues" map at letterboxing.org and at
