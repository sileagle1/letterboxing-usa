From: Malinda Welte (mwelte@kc.rr.com) | Date: 2004-03-21 12:16:58 UTC-06:00
I have followed the conversation about PZKut not stamping evenly and had
the trouble myself. I tried several of the suggestions yesterday and the
one that works best for me is to sand the stamp with 220 grit sandpaper
after it's carved. It didn't affect the carving and I got nice, even
impressions after. I'm going through all my stamps and sanding them this
weekend! Thank you for all your suggestions!
