To Janice and Linda -
Yup, California's a whole lot bigger than most other states, but you
just gotta do it! Put a letterbox in the field and they will come
(eventually).

In some ways, I sort of like the idea of salting letterboxes all over my
state, long before it becomes well know locally, and then, sometime, off
in the future, someone does a human interest article about the hobby,
and, lo and behold, it is discovered that this crazy old man has been
way out there ahead of them, stashing these quizzical carvings
cryptically clued in crannies and crevasses, laughing to himself all the
way. The fool on the hill is as crazy as a fox.

Just do it. Someday, they'll be found.

Erik
