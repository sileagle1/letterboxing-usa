OR First Time's the Charm

From: Mariette (dbltall@speakeasy.net) | Date: 2003-07-16 13:21:41 UTC-07:00
This letterbox was placed during the Rubber Stamp Camp Out July 11-13,
2003 at Milo McIver State Park in Oregon.
The stamp was carved during the stamp carving class and is the first
stamp ever carved by Lucky Charm of Team Garlic. The logbook was made by
DblTall in the bookbinding class. Many thanks to all the wonderful
people who made the campout such a fun experience!

First Time's the Charm

Milo McIver State Park, Estacada, OR

Go east from Group Camp Site H2.

In the clearing, take the trail past the volleyball poles.

12 steps before the trail meets up with the horse trail,
there is a giant of the forest on your left.

This lucky letterbox is next to the giant in a little grove.
