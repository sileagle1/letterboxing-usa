Hi All
        On the boxes going missing issue. It has happened many times over here. We have had one or two cases of deliberate vandalism but most are taken by ordinary people who do not know what they are a sort of holiday souvenir. Stick a label inside the lid explaining that the box is put out for may peoples pleasure and that taking it or destroying it will cause people wasted time and effort. Include your phone number so and tell people to ring you if they want to know more. This is how i got started.
