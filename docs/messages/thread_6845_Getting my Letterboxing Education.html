Well, I'm a newbie and this week I've learned so much
1 A hybrid is a combination box of stamping & geocacheing
2 Not to use fingernail polish remover to clean my stamps
3 How to write clues
4 What "monroe bragging" rights are!
5 That when I plant a box it might be a good idea to include a
stamppad for the box so my inks don't get mixed because the previous
soul didn't have any cleaning tool with them.

Here's what I'd like to share..........
To follow mailing list rules that were established 10 yrs
ago...when something upsets you, write a reply but DON"T send it!
Wait 24 hours read the post again, read your reply but DON"T send it.
Wait 24 hours and then read the post and your reply a 3rd time. If
you're still inflammed, then send it, BUT NOT TO THE LIST. Send it
privately.

From: psycomommy2003 (ktborrelli@hotmail.com) | Date: 2002-08-11 18:56:10 UTC
- TO answer your question
1-ESAK- Ihave no idea
2-P- planted
3-F-found
4-X- exchanged- meet another letterboxer and excange stamps
5-V- virtual- there is a section in this site reg. vituals. They
are challenging. We do them when it's too hot to go out.
6-C- confiscated as reported in post # 14389
7-L- that's my nephews own personal addition because he likes
lizards and the word googol. I on the other hand have now
encountered 3. 2 real and 1 stamped courtesy of twogreysquirrels. L
is just to add a little fun and humor to a sport (that is as I was
told to be fun). You don't need to add it to your list. IT was all
meant to be fun...
psycomommy2003
