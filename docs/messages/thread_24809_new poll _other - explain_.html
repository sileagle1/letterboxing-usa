I chose "other" too. I nearly picked "plant near bike trails" since I do a lot
of my letterboxing by bicycle and I like those, but there's a less limiting and
more versatile option. I would like to see the directions include public
transportation and/or bike directions included along with driving directions
(when appropriate, of course). Something like this:

By car: from I-5 take exit #___ and go south on ______ ...

By bus: from downtown take #62, 64, or 66 to ______ and change to #68 to go
south on _______ ...

By bike: from the downtown transit center, take the ______ Trail to the ______
trailhead and head south on ...

I hope to plant my first box this summer and have planned all along to include
a non-car alternative. It seems only right in a hobby that includes so many
hikers and admirerers of the outdoors.
