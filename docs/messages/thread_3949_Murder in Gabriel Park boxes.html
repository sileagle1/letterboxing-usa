Murder in Gabriel Park boxes

From: K.L.Sinizer (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2001-12-16 17:20:01 UTC-08:00
The Traveling Circus pooled their brilliant minds on Saturday, headed
out into the rain (brilliant?) and spent a couple of hours searching for
evidence and interviewing witnesses. The good news is that we succeeded
in finding all the evidence (six boxes) needed to convict the
perpetrator. The bad news is that one of us recently spent time in the
company of Joe, the murderer, having picked him up as a hitch hiker.
Fortunately, no one was hurt but he made his escape and is again on the
loose. All letterboxers should be on the lookout for this dastardly
villain. Funhog
