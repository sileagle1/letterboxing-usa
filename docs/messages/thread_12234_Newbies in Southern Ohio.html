My family and I went letterboxing for the first time last Saturday (a
beautiful day!) and I think we're hooked! We found the Pike Co. box,
2 in Scioto Trails S.P., 2 at OU-Chillicothe, and a couple in
Yoctangee Park. Not bad for a first effort although there were 3 in
Yoctangee we couldn't find - Hin Mallet, Bearfoot in the Park, and
Yoctangee Baden. The park was really crowded and we didn't want to
be too obvious. We'll gettem' next time! We're already planning to
plant some boxes of our own so watch this spot! Thanks to you
creative types out there who carve some really great stamps and put
them out there for us to share.
