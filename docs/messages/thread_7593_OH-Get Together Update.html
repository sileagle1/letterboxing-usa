From: geoflyfisher (geoflyfisher@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-09-10 12:11:07 UTC
It is exciting to see that this will happen.

If it is going to be near Dayton, I'd definately suggest John Bryan
where we have a strong relationship with the park management.
Huffman Dam works, but I have had to go out and show the manager
where each box is.

Besides there are 30 some boxes in John Bryan and only a couple in
Huffman!

I can be back in Dayton from the Soccer by afternoon.

If I can be of any help in setting up a reservation for a picnic
pavilion, I will do so. A nice one is in the lower parking lot at
John Bryan.

Coolwan, if you preplace boxes, please stop by the park office and
let them register you as a box placer. They do have a restriction
that the boxes need to be within 10 feet of trails or within 10 feet
of any of the edges of the public areas and picnic grounds.

Flyfisher
