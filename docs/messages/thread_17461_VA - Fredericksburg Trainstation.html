VA - Fredericksburg Trainstation

From: J. BRIDGMAN (jmbridgman@snet.net) | Date: 2004-01-04 19:00:57 UTC-08:00
Wanted to let the placer of Fredericksburg
Trainstation know that the box is in excellant
condition. We found it in the dark on 12/30 and had
an interesting time trying to be descreet in the
parking lot. There was a person in a car practically
in front of the box but one of us snatched it while
the other stood so as to block the view from the car.
Then while we were stamping, another car drove in and
parked and probably watch what we were doing. Don't
think he could see the hiding place though as we
replaced it. Hope they didn't get curious as to what
we were doing.
