From: irishtinker (irishtinker@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-04-25 15:11:45 UTC-04:00
Pick a name that has something do with you likes or personality or something
from your past.
I picked Irish Tinker which is an Irish Gypsy because I am 100o/o Irish
American and I like to wander around in the outdoors like a gypsy.
My sister picked Odillio because my daughter started calling her that when
she was learning to talk.
My husband picked French Fox but just changed it to Freedom Fox because of
his new patriotic stamp. The Fox part comes from his real name, Todd which
means Fox.
My three year old goes by Ladybug98 because she loves anything with ladybugs
on it.
My three year old goes by Rosie Bunny because when we went to pick out
stamps she really wanted a bunny stamp and her nick name has been Rosie
since birth.
