Due to some confusion with regards to my Paint Branch Box (Silver Spring,
MD) I have made the following update on the clue. Please update it on the
website whenever you get a chance. Please also note that if you go
searching for the box and come across a white stormbox, you've gone too far.
Just turn around and find the meeting of the two trails. Thanks goes out
to the "Usual Suspects" for sending the E-mail about having a hard time
finding it.

Paint Branch Box Clue (Revised 7/26/02)

Down this path to where trails cross,
Find the car and pass it by,
Continue on past two metal disks,
A few more cars, touch by death's kiss.
Across the stream, take a right..
Find the bug and stop on site.
Thirteen Paces and stop in view of me,
Way to your left, I'm hiding at the base of that large rotting tree.
