Title: Beacon Hill. Placed 12-9-03 by Salamander. Difficulty: Easy.
Location: Bobblett Cemetery, Old Rt. 50, AKA Co. Rd. 13A, Vinton
County Ohio {between Londonderry and Allensville, Ohio).
This letterbox is not hidden on a hill as I had originally planned;
however, it is in an area surrounded by beautiful hills. CLUES:
Find a blue water pump next to a white flag pole, basically right in
the center of the cemetery. Standing just south of the pump, take a
compass reading of 232 degrees. Go 23 paces at this heading to a
large, tree-like evergreen shrub. The box is hidden in a crotch
formed by several of the "tree/shrub"'s thin trunks, under some
small rocks and branches. (Small, camo-taped container).
