Amanda from Seattle

Go to the Ecola State Park in Cannon Beach. A day-use fee is charged.
Take Hwy 101 to the north exit for Cannon Beach and follow the signs.
Park at the lot for the Ecola Point Picnic Area.

As you enter the parking lot, look for a trail sign on the right.
Take this trail to the first viewpoint. Stop and check out the view
of the Tillamook Rock Lighthouse. The lighthouse is no longer
operating. The island was bought by funereal entrepreneurs who bring
urns of cremated remains in by helicopter. What a stormy final
resting place!!
