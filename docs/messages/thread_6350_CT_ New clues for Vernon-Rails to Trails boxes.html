Webmaster,
Could you please change the clues for the Vernon-Rails to Trails
letterboxes located in Vernon, Tolland County, Connecticut. Box #1
was soaked and had to be replaced. We transfered as much as we could
read from the soaked book. As a precaution, we double bagged the
logbooks, hopefully future stampers will continue to do so. We also
moved box #2. We recently checked that box and found that some non-
letterboxers found it and drew some inappropriate pictures in the
logbook. We are hoping that it won't be spotted as easily in it's
new location. Here are the new clues:

Vernon-Rails to Trails Letterbox
Vernon, Tolland County, Connecticut

3 mile long hike (or bike if you'd like) down the track and back.
Approximate time: 1 to 1 1/2 hours, depending on how fast you walk.
Originally planted by Team Weems on 9/30/01. Re-planted on 7/20/02.

Directions: If you're on I-84 East, take exit 65. At light take a
right onto Route 30. At the first light take a right onto Dobson
Road. If you're on I-84 West, take exit 65. Take a left onto Route
30. At the first light take a left onto Dobson Road.
