Hello Everyone!

First, I would like to thank Tom No one for a wonderful gathering! The rain did nothing to dampen the spirits of all who were there. Ninigret Pond was the perfect setting. I cast my vote for another gathering there in the future. Great choice!

Second, I would like to thank Legerdemaine for those delicious seasonal confections as well as sharing surprises aplenty with everyone. Your cookies were such a treat. Flutterby flitted from one log book to another in all her colors and brought many smiles, beautiful box, log book and all.

I was priviledged to present KMartha to everyone as a surprise. I think we all enjoyed this very unexpected appearance!  It was a "Good Thing!"

Last and by no means least, our Mystery Guest from afar, Funhog, was warmly welcomed by all. Thank you for joining us!

All in all, a superb day. It was such a pleasure to meet everyone and make friends with those whose posts I read every day. Thank you all!

Lastly, I've posted a few photos in the Photos section. Here is the link (and I hope it works).

http://photos.groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-usa/lst?.view=t&.dir=/Photos-Mystery+Gathering-12+Oct+2002

Hope to see many of you next week at Rocky Neck. Thirteen new boxes for the occasion! Come one! Come all!

Linda a/k/a Alafair
