New letterbox - Castries, St. Lucia

From: rtrwathome (rtrw@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-03-14 15:01:13 UTC
Maine Black Bear Letterbox

Planted by rtrw on December 2nd ,2002

Difficulty - You need to get to St. Lucia. After that it's easy
Time - 10 minutes
Directions to starting place - Find your way to Point Seraphine
Shopping area in Castries on the beautiful island of St. Lucia.
Outside the shops is a big boat dock. Go to west end of the boat
dock
overlooking the water. There are two giant red things to which the
ships can be anchored. Stand behind the red thing and look pretty
much to the north. You will see another red thing with steps leading
to it. This is how you know you are in the right spot.

Details you should know - I'm not sure if the shopping area is open
if
the ships are not in the port. Sorry for not getting that piece of
information.

Details you don't need to know -
St. Lucia is 27 miles long and 14 miles across at the widest point.
I
wish we had gone to see the volcano while visiting. I could be
wrong,
but this may be the first letterbox planted on St. Lucia.

Clues:
Turn and head 5 steps at 20 degrees to a shallow blue wall. At the
southern end of the wall on the ocean side you will find your
treasure
beneath some rocks. Please be as discreet as possible, as people
will
surely wonder what on earth you are doing. This is an orphan box.
Please care for it as best you can if it has needs. Thanks.
