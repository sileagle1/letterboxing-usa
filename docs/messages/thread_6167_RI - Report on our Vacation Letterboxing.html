Hello All,

I thought I would let those of you who are interested know how we did
with our Rhode Island boxes.

6/23 Asa Pond - We found only the second one. Really fun trail for
the kids with all the little bridges.

6/23 Fast Track To Your Degree - we found only the first one, tried
for all three. Beautiful campus.

6/25 Signs of Life - We found 3 but had to leave to meet someone for
lunch before we found the 4th. There were lots of ticks and scratchy
plants!

6/25 Dolphins at Daybreak - What a beautiful place! Found the box.

6/25 Midnight on the Moon - Yes, the poison ivy is unbelievable! We
didn't go far enough along the trail last time we tried. We found it
successfully this time.

6/26 Southeast Lighthouse on Block Island - Letterbox still intact.
We took the tour of the lighthouse, well worth the $5 for adults.
Got to take the circular stairs all the way to the top of the
lighthouse and see the light up close and the view. It was great and
well worth the walk. We look forward to going to Block Island again.

Thanks for a bunch of great boxes! It sure broadens our experience
while we are on vacation.
