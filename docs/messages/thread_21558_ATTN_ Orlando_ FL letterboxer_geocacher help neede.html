OK, I know there's a certain amount of rivalry between letterboxing
and geocaching, but bear with me, as there's also a lot of
crossover. Hopefully, we can find someone who does both in the
Florida area.

A geocacher was out in the Disneyworld area geocaching and
accidentally found a letterbox. Not realizing it wasn't the geocache
he was looking for, he left a travel bug in the cache. Travel bugs
are like LB hitchhikers, except instead of being a stamp and book,
it's a tag with a serial number that you log at the geocaching
website.

Unfortunately, his attempt to email the LB owner bounced, and
there's no guarantee the LB owner would be able to get out there
anyway.

I offered to post here looking for someone who letterboxes and
geocaches in the area and who would be willing to pick up the bug
and move it to a geocache (any geocache will do) and log it on the
geocaching.com website.

So, is anyone willing and able to do that?

Thanks,
Stephanie
