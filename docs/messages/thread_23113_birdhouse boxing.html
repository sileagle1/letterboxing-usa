so, listening to everyone come up with all these crazy ideas about
where to put boxes, doesn't make me feel so weird. somehow i want to
place birdhouses in the woods and inside these houses would be
stamps. i am trying to figure out how that would work with out
playing with birds minds. i thought that if i put a few birdhouses
out in the woods and blocked off the house hole, so no birds
entered, then the placed stamp inside the birdhouse would be safe.
but then i thought about the poor birds. with the increase birdhouse
prices in the state, lol, i would hate for a bird to try and move in
and become disstressed with confusion. then i thought, well i could
place several birdhouses and then plant boxes near the trees the
birdhouses reside. is it a bad thing to place birdhouse in random
places or should i consider asking? i wouldn't consider placing
these birdhouse in parks run by towns or the state. i don't know
where i would place these houses yet, but i was thinking of placing
the birdhouses for boxing. and/or consider a birdhouse series like
wanda and pete's holiday ornament series, where other boxers can add
birdhouses/boxes to the series. what does everyone think?
