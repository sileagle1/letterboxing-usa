Wow!! I was gone for a couple of days and look at all the posts. It
is wonderful that this is such an active group. I'm glad to see some
newbies like me. Hey, Busy Bee from TX glad to see your still here.

Went to Orlando to visit my sister. Actually I think I have her
hooked. I took my 4yr old to get our starting equip. Stamps, inc and
journal. Came back and started to hunt for the Disney Series. Our
first find was at Celebration. Found it. Hurried to look thru it and
sent kids and bro-in-law back to van before the sky opened up and it
poured rain. My sister and I looked like we just jumped in and out
of a pool when we arrived back at the van. Off to Fort Wilderness.
I didn't realize we needed a compass so we have to go back. We saw
some good places it could of been but everyone was chicken to stick
there hands in wet slimy stumps. Of course one had a HUGE frog in
it. 2nd box we found at the Caribean Resort. Found the ButterFly
HichHiker in it. What a great surprise! Rain Rain Go Away!!! Yep had
that inky fingers, legs, shorts thing going on. What fun:)

On to Ichetucknee Springs. After a discouraging forcast of 80%
chance of rain and the storm in the morning. It cleared up when we
hit the Spring. 8:30 in the morning the mist on the Spring was
enchanting. We had the area almost all to ourselves. Rain scared
everyone off. Around 4:30 out of the spring to enhale our lunch and
off in search of Drangonfly LB. All the boys ate and fell asleep and
the girls went on a hike. Great spot!!!!! (Came prepared with
gloves this time ;)

We were going to do more LB thru Gainsville, Ocala and Lake Wales but
it really was rainy and dreary and my 4yr was whiny. Next trip.

I'm excited and have some good ideas for planting my own boxes.
Hopefully people will come find them.
