RI &MA letterbox updates

From: (DebTeix@aol.com) | Date: 2002-03-11 09:08:46 UTC-05:00
Saturday afternoon saw a change of plans and off dad and I went to find the Hours of the Olympics. All three boxes were safe and in good condition. This is a great little park.

After a quick stop at the A&W Rootbeer on rte 44 for some weiners and Rootbeer went off to the Scituate Reservoir to retrieve the Naked Woman in the Waterfall. Another beautiful site to visit. Seems that we had just missed "TNT" who had visited this site early in the day.

Sunday saw an early easter trip to Hebert's Candy Mansion then a visit to Farmer Harvey for a little "Peace on Earth" for Dad. I had visited him in February on another trip to the area. Here too everything was fine.

It was a great weekend for letterboxing.

Debbie (The bee) P0F38
