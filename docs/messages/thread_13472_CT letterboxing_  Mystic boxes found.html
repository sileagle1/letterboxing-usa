CT letterboxing: Mystic boxes found

From: mulbie (mulbie@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-09 17:53:25 UTC
Mary Manatee, The Yachtsman and Moonshine went out with friends this
weekend and found the following letterboxes in Mystic in good shape:
DNPC -- Series One -- all boxes; Denison Pequotsepos Wildlife Series
#1: Joe Pyeweed. We also visited the Drewclan's "Birdhouse"
letterbox. It was missing a log book, which had been removed because
it was wet. We added a new, dry log book and a new bag to (hopefully)
keep it dry. Some folks had stamped in on pieces of letterboxing
instructions that they had brought with them. We left those in the
box also.

What a muggy, buggy day yesterday! One of these days we'll go boxing
in good weather again!
