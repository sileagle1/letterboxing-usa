mansfield CT caution

From: Small Bear (summerlove23@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-07-25 03:12:45 UTC
Just a caution to all those boxers who frequent mansfield. There has been a
raccoon spotted near and around Bicentennial pond, which is off of Clover
Mill Rd, during the day recently. I work at the day camp that is held there,
and have seen it twice in the past two days. The town personnel responsible
for the area has mentioned that it may have rabies, so please be careful out
on the trails! This area is close by School House Brook Park, and is loaded
with boxes, like Chuck's elephants, air mail, squirrel's treasure, in search
of sasquatch, Irish Mum's the totem pole, and Cock-a-doodle-Moo's
bicentennial pond, silly songs and poets box. Be careful everyone!!
~Small Bear
