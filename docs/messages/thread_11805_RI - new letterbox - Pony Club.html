Pony Club Letterbox
Goddard Park
Warwick, RI
Kent County

Park your car at the boat ramp.
Follow the trail from the NNW corner of the lot. Trot right at the fork.
Enjoy the trail high above Greenwich Cove. Stop at the first left. There
is a knee-high boulder just visible at 165. Canter 65 paces toward it and
look under the overhang. To return, it's left at the T and right at the Y.
Happy Trails!
