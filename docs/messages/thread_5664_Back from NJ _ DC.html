Just returned to CT from a trip down to DC. Hit the "Toe Path" Letterbox in NJ and happy to report it's in fine shape. Added my own box along the canal and will send in new clues later. While in DC, we hid a box along the Mount Vernon Trail and were able to find "Runway" and "Knowhadamean Fern", but not the "Riverside Park" box. The "K. We also found "Clark's Crossing" in Vienna, VA while visiting my old neighborhood of 30 years ago. It's in good shape. We hid two more Letterboxes in the DC area, but it may take a bit to get all 4 new box clues sent in. I wish we had more time to hit the other boxes in the area, but the humidity killed us!
