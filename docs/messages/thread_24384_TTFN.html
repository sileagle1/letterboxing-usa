Dear Letterboxing Community,

To all, a thank you for a game well played.

The culprit, you ask? Investigator Bacon did not
share this information with me, saying that it was up
to you, the letterboxing community, to restore the
missing Store of Good Manners. I would like to take
this time to encourage those of you who already have
the key to this mysterious case to continue in your
diligence toward the final solution. For those of you
who are just beginning the quest, send word to me and
I will share what information Investigator Bacon has
gathered in his investigation.

For all the points of etiquette that have been
mentioned, I wish to thank the following players of
the game: Der Mad Stamper, Stealthboxxer, Mark Pepe,
Archimedes Screw, Mountain Scorpia, Gallant Rogue,
Dude the Wonder Dog, Hikers & Hounds, Safari Man,
Barefoot Lucy, Silver Eagle,Don, Wassamatta_u,
Wisconsin Hiker, Lnd-Crzr, Y-Nought, Dragonrider, Knit
Wit, Eidolon, SpringChick and Speedsquare. They are
indeed worthy of their reputations as caring members
of the letterboxing community, with very good advice
for those who care to listen. These individuals gave
of themselves, without knowing what their roles would
be, who the guilty parties are, or even who
Investigator Bacon and I are. Yes, these esteemed
individuals are worthy of your praise, for they embody
the very essence of the mysterious nature of
letterboxing.

Now, as Mark Pepe would say, "Have fun and just
get out there and box!!!"

Truly,

Ms. Emily Post
