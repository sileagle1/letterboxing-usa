We have a confirmed first finder for my Haunted Shipwreck box -- the
one which required a 100 ft wreck dive in Lake Michigan.

It was found in Oct by a geo-cacher who sometimes letterboxes. I had
left something in the dry-box for the first finder to mail back to me
so I could confirm that the box was actually found, and this unique
item arrived back to me via USPS safe and sound.

Cool, hey? Though I don't think I'd have picked Oct to dive the Lake!

Enjoy!
