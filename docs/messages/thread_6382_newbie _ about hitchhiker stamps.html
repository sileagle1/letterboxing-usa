>I think I am supposed to place it in the next box I find but what
>if the next box is too small? Do I hold onto it until I find a box
>big enough to put it in.

The HH's we've found in the past have had their own "homes", so that
when placed at the next box they didn't have to actually go into the
box but be placed with it. In fact we liked the idea so much that
for the HH's we've put out we also put them in their own boxes.
So in response to your question, you could get one of those cheapy
boxes from the store and give it a traveling home or just wait until
you find a box big enough to hold it.
