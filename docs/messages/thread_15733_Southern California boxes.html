Hi,

The Three Furies' Crone Tree box clue was sent to me directly. It is
being redone because of some problems. This was their very first box
and they wanted me to check it out. Really nice folks who are local
to San Luis Obispo area. They are going to be an incredible force in
the boxing community for years, I predict.

And yes, there are times when the clues to a box are private, not
posted anywhere or only posted on a private site or only in another
box or travel around from box to box (cuckoo clues.)

As far as running out of local boxes, hmmm. Have you got all of the
Redwall boxes yet? If you have at least two, you can ask JugglerMouse
for her large Martin the Warrior and ask me for my Urthstripe stamps.
We carry them with us. How are you doing on the Camelot series at El
Chorro park? Have you got the ones out at Montana del Oro? How about
the Haunted Mine?

Other than that, there are the ones that Princess Lea and Sleepy Cat
put in up highway 101. And if you have not been down to get the
Critters of Ennisbrook, hurry down there right away.

If all else fails, you could plant some while you wait for October
4th SLO Stamping in the Meadow. I understand there will be quite a
few in that meeting for you. Or, you can arrange for some exchanges
if you know of any other letterboxers in your area (*ahem*)

Glad you are so avid; me, too,
Sir B
