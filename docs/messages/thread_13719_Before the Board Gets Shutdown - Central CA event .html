Before the Board Gets Shutdown - Central CA event ~ Oct 4th

From: Sir Balthazar (neovolatile@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-22 03:58:22 UTC
Googled invites letterboxers to a letterboxing event around Oct4th in
San Luis Obispo, CA. We are planning a potluck, some special stamps,
multi-exchanges, maybe a contest or two and a wonderful day (or two)
in the letterbox-rich area around the central coast of California. We
should be over 100 boxes in a 50 mi diameter soon.

No, Ryan Carpenter cannot attend any event in Oct or Sept in this
area. He may stop in November. The rains should not hit us in early
October. Later, we get our yearly dose in a few months.

Those needing to find out about lodging or campgrounds, please feel
free to contact me.

This is a preliminary notice. More details will be posted to
letterboxing.org as long as that site stays up and active.
