From: Gwen & Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-06-16 11:58:25 UTC-07:00
Eric,
If you are going to be in Grants Pass you should go for the Rainey Falls Letterbox and some others. You can drive to the Grave Creek river put in , and it would be a beautiful 2 mile to Rainey Falls . Just make sure you know which side of the river the box is on. I think there is one at Indian Mary Campground area also. Don't think about hiking to our Paradise boxes from this area unless you are planning several days on the trail it's 24 miles from Grave Creek to Paradise. Ryan did the Paradise boxes a few years ago - he did the 12 in and 12 out in one day from Illahe . If you are going to Gold Beach you could take a great jet boat ride to Paradise lodge , have lunch, nab two boxes with a short hike and jet boat back to Gold Beach. Just thinking about the Rogue makes me want to pack up my boat and head north. Don
