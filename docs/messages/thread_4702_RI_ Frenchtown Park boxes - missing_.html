From: Kate (mainetig@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-03-15 15:29:44 UTC-08:00
Well, AD and I finished up our Letterboxing vacation
today (total of 18 plus 1 HH)...she heads for home
tomorrow.

We ended at the Frenchtown Park boxes that Trish
planted. The one up in Frye Family Preserve was
fine..thought the hike was quite a bit further than we
had anticipated from the clues.

After returning to the car for the second sheet of the
clues that we had forgotten (and a short cut drive to
the rec department ;o) ), we attempted to search out
the box at the mill. All we managed to find, however,
was a ton of high schoolers smoking and huffing, and a
group of Johnson and Wales Geocachers. Neither they
or us had any luck in our hunts. The teens told the
geocachers that they had found a box a while ago, but
down the trail a ways from where we thought it was
suppose to be.

Trish, we are pretty sure that we were in the right
spot, but we looked all over. If you want to email me
specifics as to where the box is, maybe I can confirm
if that is where we checked.

I will send another message to the board with updates
on the other boxes we found in our journey.
