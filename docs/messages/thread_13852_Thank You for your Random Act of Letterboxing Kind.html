Thank You for your Random Act of Letterboxing Kindness Letterbox

www.psychokiwi.org/letterboxing/ralk.htm

This special box is to give thanks to those kind and generous letterboxing
souls who commit random acts of letterboxing kindness. This is a box that
searches for you. If it finds you, your job is to find someone who has
committed a RALK and pass it along. Please write a note in the logbook
singing their praises, stamp in, and add one to your 'F' count.

To all recipients - a big thank you from Team Psychokiwi for your kind
deeds. We have got more out of this hobby than we could ever give back,
thanks to folk like you.
