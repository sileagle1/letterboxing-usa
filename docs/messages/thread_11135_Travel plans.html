Hey, Mike --

Panther Swamp is one of mine, and I live about 15 miles south of the park.
There's still two feet (or more!) of snow in my yard right now, so I would
expect that those boxes are still pretty snowed in. The temperature is
supposed to get up to the 50s this weekend, so we may get some thawing. On
the other hand, temps have been in the single digits at night these last
couple of days, so the snow is pretty solid. In fact, I was out today
checking a box and the snow was frozen so hard that my feet weren't even
breaking the surface of the snow (and I'm 6'2" and no lightweight). I
haven't heard any recent reports on the Panther Swamp box, but perhaps
someone else on the list has been to one or the other of the boxes this year.
