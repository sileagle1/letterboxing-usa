Hi,
 
I'm delighted when I can be helpful!  For letterboxing, I mount my stamps on a thin piece of wood, bought in bulk at Michael's Arts & Crafts.  You know, those precut little pieces of wood that they sell for crafts projects?  I get the ones that are about 2" x 3" or thereabouts.  They come in small bag of three or four, for about $1 total. 
 
I glue the stamp to the wood with a glue called Household Goop.  So far, this works well.
 
This gives me a nice, small stamp that'll fit nicely inside the fairly flat container of my letterboxing supplies.
 
Cheerfully,
Aisling
