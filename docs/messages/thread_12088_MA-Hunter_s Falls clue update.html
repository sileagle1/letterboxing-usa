MA-Hunter's Falls clue update

From: warrioringilead (warrioringilead@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-18 23:24:34 UTC
Mary(RI) and I had a great deal of fun on Wednesday. She came to
visit and to take advantage of my semi-yearly "care and feeding" of
my letterboxes. I made Mary follow my clue sheets to take advantage
of a fresh pair of eyes to see if the clues were still relevant.

Hunter's Falls Letterbox needs *one* tiny change in the clues. It is
amazing how quickly the decomposition of cut trees occurs in a single
year in New England. Simply eliminate the word "FRESHLY", as
in "freshly cut tree" from the clues. The previously "freshly" cut
tree is now looking more like a rotten old tree stump, and this clue
threw Mary off, and rightly so.

All the rest are still dead on accurate, and Mary even found the
Woody Hitchhiker at Marathon (waves to CSCM and Rustypuff).

Also to CSCM - Yes, the box at Angel's Lookout was broken, but
fortunately all the extra bagging kept the contents dry. I replaced
the box. And I also added a few dozen more stickers to the Peace on
Earth Box - thanks for the heads up.

The Woody Hitchhiker has been let loose today, at one of my boxes -
but which one? You'll have to search and find out!
