A little further up Rt. 16 will find you in N. Conway. My husband
and I just returned from a trip there and were able to find a few
letterboxes in Conwy. As for easy to find boxes, I would
suggest "Eastern Slopes Inn" (about a ten minute walk) and "Diana's
Baths" (which is a mile round trip). We also really enjoyed the
three box series at Cathedral Ledge (not to mention the view from
the top). This one takes a little longer but is kid friendly. Hope
you enjoy your trip!

Happy trails,
Kerri
