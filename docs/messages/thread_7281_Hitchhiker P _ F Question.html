Planted, and we only Find other people's HHs once even though
we may come across the same one at another location some
other time.

Also, moving a HH seems a personal decision. If its at your own
box or you have seen it before or don't feel in the mood, you
might just leave for the next adventurer. On the other hand if it
has been waiting for a long time at a seldom visited location, you
might want to move it elsewhere so others can find and enjoy it.
