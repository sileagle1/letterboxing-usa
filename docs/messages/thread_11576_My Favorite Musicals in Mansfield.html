My Favorite Musicals in Mansfield

From: rospa10 (wilmes2@mindspring.com) | Date: 2003-03-30 01:57:54 UTC
Today I spent about 2 1/2 hours out in the wonderful Coney Rock
Preserve hunting for musical letterboxes placed by Pink Trotters and
Leader of the Pack. Sound of Music and Coal Miner's Daughter needed a
little camoflauging after I stamped in. All the boxes I found were in
excellent shape. Last stamp-in was Dec. 23rd. May have been earlier
stamps for the first box..I was too busy watching out for other
hikers, as this is near the start of the trail. Besides the 2 boxes
already mentioned, I found Phantom of the Opera, Friedrick, and Kurt.
Liesl and Louisa escpaed my detection..this time;-) So, 5 boxes found
and 16 to go!! The threatened rain started to gently pitterpatter
down so I beat a hasty retreat. Made it to my car just as the
downpour started. I'd love to see a trail map of this preserve so I
can get a sense of it. Many thanks to the letterbox hiders for well
thought out clues and creative handcarved stamps.
