From: gbheron77 (gbheron@netzero.net) | Date: 2002-01-25 13:56:42 UTC
A gathering in MA suits me just fine. Especially since my recent
injury suffered while particpating in the extreme sport of
Letterboxing. My confidence charged after planting my first box on
Martin Luther King Day(clues to be forthcoming shortly),I planned an
extra long lunch yesterday and set out for the Bowers Springs LB. Not
50 paces from my car, I slipped on the snow covered trail which had
become a smooth surface of ice. With compass in my left hand, I
reached out with my right to break my fall and my wrist. My coach has
placed me on the bench for the remainder of the winter season.
However, stamp carving may be incorporated in my rehab plan. At any
rate, an LB gathering could be just the right treatment for this
frustrated armchair letterboxer. See previous e-mail
regarding "accident" for more info on yesterday's incident.
Wing Clipped Heron
