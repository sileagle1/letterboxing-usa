The World's Most Boring Letterbox, Blah Blah Blah.
Location: Brooks Park, Lacey, WA, Thurston County
Placed 9/12/02 by The Nail Family

To get there: From I-5 take exit # 109 to Martin Way. Head West
on Martin Way to College St and turn left.
Follow College to intersection with Lacey Blvd. Cross Lacey Blvd
and look for the brown sign on the immediate right directing to
Brooks Park behind a yellow building (Qwest). This is a very
small rectangular city park located between the
backyards of a few homes and directly behind the Qwest service
building.

Clues:
Enter the parking lot and park.
Walk to the chain link fence on the north side of the park.
Walk westward along the park border.
Count 3 railroad ties.
World's Most Boring Letterbox is behind the
3rd railroad tie near the base of the cedar tree.
Blah Blah Blah.

Warning: It is a boring stamp in a boring park but you'll probably
go find it anyway since you can't pass up such an easy find.
Blah Blah Blah.
