My New Years Resolution was to fit letterboxing into every possible
facet of my life. So I planted a box close to my work place to give
me incentive to walk over there and check on it as often as possible

Coffee Break
Wakefield, Rhode Island

Placed by: Laurette
When: January 14, 2003
Level: Easy 20 minute walk

Driving Directions: Take Rt. 108 to the intersection of Curtis
Corner Road. Go east on Curtis Corner. When you see the CC Middle
School (I don't work here), pull into the lot and follow the road
around the left of the building past the administration building.
Park near the tennis courts.

Clues: Across the street from the tennis courts, a path runs east.
Take it. Follow the pink as it takes the 2nd right, over some
bridges, past lots of briars and through a stone wall. At the, Y go
left. 35 paces from the Y, on the right side of the trail stands a
tree covered in briars, with a hollow at the bottom. It's a good
place for a break.
