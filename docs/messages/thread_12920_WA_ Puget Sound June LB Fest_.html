Hi all--

We're trying to coordinate a sort of 'low-key' Puget Sound
letterboxing fest sometime during June for any letterboxers who are
interested in meeting other letterboxers from around the region.
Tumwater Historical Park (just at I-5 & Hwy 101 interchange in the
Olympia area) offers a nice facility with picnic tables, playgrounds,
running water/toilets and is centrally located to numerous 'quicky'
letterboxes in the region.

Here are the dates/times we can be available for planning such an
event:

14 June (Sat)--afternoon, anytime after 3:30pm
15 June (Sun)--afternoon, anytime after 1:00pm

22 June (Sun)--afternoon, anytime after 1:00pm

28 June (Sat)--all day
29 June (Sun)--afternoon, anytime after 1:00pm

If there is any interest out there, please drop us an email, post
your comments to the board, or check out the Yahoo Groups LbPNW group
and post your comments there.

Hope to see you soon,

dvn2r ckr & gang
DuPont, WA
