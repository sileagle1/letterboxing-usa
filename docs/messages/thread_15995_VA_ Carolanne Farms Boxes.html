Went out today to check on the Carol Anne Farms boxes after the worst of the
devastation from Isabelle has been cleaned.

ARA Cowboys: Great shape though we had to pull a tree off it to get to it.
All the clues still relevant.

The YIN and The YANG boxes: Still there and in breat shape. Your can no
longer count the paths though, as the hurricane flattened most of the
defining landmarks. Take the path after the "6" marker to find them now.

The Jolly Rodger: Still there and in good shape, but the rating for the box
has changed! Three big trees are fallen across the path. One you can duck
under, two you have to climb over, one of those is all wrapped in a thorn
bush, so beware!

Hope to check our Redwing boxes tomorrow,
