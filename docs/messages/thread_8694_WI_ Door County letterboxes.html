From: Steven Stary (kurrwic@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-10-26 12:37:34 UTC-07:00
Hi everybody,
Long time no write. Those Door Co. letterboxes
are/were mine for the most part, and I've been a busy
busy man this past year and haven't had a chance to
replace and repair any. To the best of my knowledge,
the letterboxes at Cave Point, Cana Island, Mink
River, and Sturgeon Bay Canal Light are still there.
The others were all lost or removed and not yet
replaced. If anyone does go looking for them, I'd
welcome a report on their condition. If any new boxes
should happen to get placed, well, so much the better
as well.
Happy letterboxing,
Steven
