I dare say that there are thousands of boxes available on the moors which are not in range of gunfire. Did you aquire a copy of the catalogue as well? I would suggest getting the catalogue and making a color copy of the part of dartmoor you are going to visit. Take the color copy and encase it in a clear plastic sheet protector, and seal up the edges with clear tape. That way your map won't get wet if you are out in the rain.
