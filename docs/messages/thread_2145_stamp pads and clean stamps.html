stamp pads and clean stamps

From: Jay Chamberlain (ae4mk@1bigred.com) | Date: 2001-04-03 13:52:45 UTC-04:00
I went out on Sat. and checked on a couple of my boxes that been out since last Aug. I found that I my first couple of boxes I neglected to put the stamp and logbook in separate zip-lock bags. The stamps get quite messy after several visitors so to keep the logbook presentable it's a good Idea to keep everything in separate bags.
 
I also carry a fanny pack/purse size refillable container of baby wipes. Just the thing to clean old stamps and ink off fingers.
