I rode Caveat to the blue aerodrome
And flew Up, Up and Away and landed far, far from home
Where the Hemlock trails wind
For my one hundredth Find!

Scarab with Night Owl of the Doubtful Guests,
wandering with Wanda.
F102 P14 X46 E4

P.S. The new Hemlock series by Sam I Am follows a beautiful trail. It
has everything: fields, trails, creeks, river, a cool crossing,
passing trains (Ok, I like trains), gorgeous stamps, 6 herons, 1
cardinal, a little creek waterfall, old ruins, ancient ford, an old
abandoned worn road, all in a nice loop. And did I mention gorgeous
stamps? What a great place to get my 100. ~Tj
