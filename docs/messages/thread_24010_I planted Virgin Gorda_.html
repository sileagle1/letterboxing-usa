Since there weren't any letterboxes to be found during our recent
vacation to Virgin Gorda we decided to leave one behind! I don't
expect it to get much trafic but if you're staying in the American
Virgin Islands or taking a cruise, Virgin Gorda is a wonderful place
to visit and Gorda Peak (which takes second billing to the Baths as
the place to visit) has an incredible view of the island. It's worth
the rocky hike just for this view, the letterbox is just a bonus.
Remember your passport, Vigin Gorda is part of the British Virgin
Islands.
