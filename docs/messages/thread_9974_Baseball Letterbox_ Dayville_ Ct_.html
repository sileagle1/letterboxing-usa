Baseball Letterbox
Placed: 1-1-02
Planted by: Teach & Preach
Rated: easy 5-10mins.
Location: Dayville, Ct. Windham County

This is the second box in our sports series. Growing up, I have always
loved baseball. First watching the St. Louis Cardinals(who I'll be able
to see at Fenway this year) in the 82 World Series, and cheering them
on ever since. I play in softball leagues now and get together every
weekend with friends from work to go against my twin brothers work team
for the last 7yrs. (I'll admit his team has the better record).
Directions: From 395north exit 93 go left park will be on the right.
395south exit 93 go right park will be on the left. It is on rt. 101

Pull into the first parking area on the left. Park over by the
basketball courts. Walk past the basketball courts to the Savings Bank
Of Manchester Pavilion. At the 4th post on the left, take a reading of
285 degrees. Walk up the hill to a headstone that looks like a bench.
You have a great view of the ballfield from here. From there head north
down the hill. Line yourself up straight across from the Community Hall
Stage. Right behind you will be a rotten log at the edge of the woods.
Inside the log from the back is your prize.
