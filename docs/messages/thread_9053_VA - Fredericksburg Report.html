From: thurston_1701 (jmatthew@thurston.org) | Date: 2002-11-09 23:06:20 UTC
Had another great Autumn day on the trails. We spent the day in
Fredericksburg tracking down as many boxes as the daylight would
allow. Found Blue Heron, Dead Snellings and Weeds, Bi nka bi, Sankofa
and Confederate Cemetery, all in good shape. Antietam Bridge appears
to be MIA. The spot was easy to get to and the clue was really clear
but no box. Anyone else found it recently?

On the Hitchhiker front, we found Halloween Night and sent Honda Road
Again back into the wilds.

We're hoping the weather holds tomorrow so we can head back out and
get some more.
