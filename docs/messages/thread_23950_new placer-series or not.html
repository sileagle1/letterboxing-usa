I have an answer for you both that will satisfy both of your needs.

When I plant a series (but this is just how I do it), I give it a
series "umbrella name", then give each box an "individual name".
Look at my clues for "Blade Runner" and "RI Refreshments" as
examples. This works well if you plant *all* the boxes in the series
all at once and post the clues all at once.

Then there is the option of planting a series one by one, adding
boxes as you go. The "umbrella name" for the series and
the "individual name" for each box is the same - BUT - you can enter
the boxes one by one as you go. Look at the clues for "Native Son"
in MA for this type of example. Each box was planted individually at
different times. The individual boxes are named "Native Son #1: John
Smith", "Native Son #2: John Doe", "Native Son #3: Jack Black" (not
the actual names, just examples).

I hope this helps!

Warrior Woman
who doesn't claim that these are the ONLY ways to do it - just ways
that I have used/seen in my six years of experience
