                    Horseshoes

The place: The City of Preston

The time: 1871

The conversation:

"Mr. Robbins, my horse needs shoes."

"I will make some for him, Dr. Downer."

"Will it take long?"

"No, not in my new shop!"

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

"There. How are they? Take your horse home to test them out. Ride past the 3 maples, then rest a while on the porch swing and watch the sun set. Maybe Mr. Doane will sing you a song."

" Thank your Mr. Robbins. I will remember to write about our conversation in my journal tonight. I keep it hidden nearby at 260

°. Although it is just 14 paces from my swing, I keep it safely hidden from the curious worshippers nearby."

"Good thinking, Mr. Downer. Must go stoke my fire."

"Good day, Mr. Robbins."

                "Good Day."
