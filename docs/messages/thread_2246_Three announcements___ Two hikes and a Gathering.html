The members of RIBNAG wish to invite those letterboxers nearby
to come to a small gathering in Western Coventry RI. On Saturday
28 APR 2001 we will have an event stamp and a short hike for you

on having a pot-luck dinner about 7ish so feel free to bring

If you have any questions, directions etc... feel free to email
us at ribnag@users.tmok.com
