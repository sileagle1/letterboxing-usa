Hi ~*Sailor Moon Beam*~


No-one else has answered you yet ..so here's a reply from over the pond !

I'm only a Dartmoor Letterboxer ..but the answer to your conundrum is,
"Both answers right!"



1. The letterbox itself contains a stamp which "you" the letterboxer use
to take an impression.............

............This goes in your COLLECTORS book and proves that you've been
there (to the box) and "you" get a nice "impression collection" to show
off ..and get a nice F count.



2. You have a personal ID...(Sailor Moon I presume ! ) and "you"
make "your own" personal stamp for your identity...


3. When you find a box ..You should find that it also contains a
VISITORS book ! SO...then also impress "your ID" stamp into THE BOX'S
vistors book !

This proves to the next letterboxers that you have been there..and you
might leave them a message too !

A stamp impression exchange in fact ..! Nice !


On Dartmoor we pull in our boxes ..so as not to be litterbugs ..after say
2 years ..and the box owner gets to keep their VISITORS BOOK...

The record in the vistors book makes a cool record of all the letterboxers
who followed their clues to their box too !


Easy Peasy !

And its so much just plain crazy fun!

The game ..(as someone here says most times) ..is afoot


... Bilbo to the recsue !
