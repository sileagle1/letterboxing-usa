Soaring Seagull Hitchhiker

From: The Christmas Elves (schuppner@uwalumni.com) | Date: 2003-11-01 21:41:36 UTC-06:00
We found the Soaring Seagull Hitchhiker in the new Wirth Park (WI)
letterbox today (11/1/2003). It experienced a good soaking at some
point (very dry today), so nearly all of the placers comments are
illegible and some of the stamps are blurred. A separate typed note
asks finders to leave a note on the letterboxing e-mail list with
when and where it was found. We hope that this is the list the
placer intended since a quick archive search didn't turn up any
messages about this hitchhiker and Soaring Seagull wasn't carrying an
e-mail address.

We noticed that Soaring Seagull is not on Wanda and Petes site.
Would the placer like an off list update about Soaring Seagulls 2
years on the road? We would be happy to share the information with
Wanda and Pete if theyre interested and the placer doesnt object.

The Christmas Elves
