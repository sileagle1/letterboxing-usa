From: Forest People (forestpeople@zilknet.net) | Date: 2005-06-26 23:02:13 UTC-07:00
There is good news and bad news about our two Thanksgiving Letterboxes.

The good news. After being alerted by Wild Rose that the 1st Annual
Thanksgiving Letterbox in Olympia, WA was soaked and needed attention,
we have completely revamped it. New box, new bags, new logbook, and
most importantly a new stamp! Now all of you who have found this box
already will have an excuse to go visit it again. The only thing that
isn't new is the location.

The bad news. The 2nd Annual Thanksgiving Letterbox in Lake Oswego, OR
is missing. We intend to replace it, but don't know yet when that will
happen. We have updated the LbNA website with the current status, and
will send another message out when it is back in business.
