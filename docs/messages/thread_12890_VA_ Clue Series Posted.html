From: mlgainey (mlgainey@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-15 23:51:38 UTC
The Clue series in metro Richmond, Virginia has been posted to the Web
site! A humonguous thank you to all who contributed as well as to the
letterboxing community as a whole for being so patient as we gathered,
planted, and tested -- and tested some more! -- 24 stamps.

The Richmond club is offering a prize to the first boxer who can prove
to us that he/she found all 24 stamps in all 4 parks. You'll have to
send us an electronic scan or a fax showing all 24 stamps. When you
think you've found all 24, let us know. The prize hasn't been
finalized, but it will definitely be boxing related.

Questions, comments, and contest entries can be sent via e-mail to any
of the planners listed in the clues.

Happy trails!
