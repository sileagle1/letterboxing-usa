While checking on a few of my boxes over the summer, I found a love
letter in my Point Judith, RI letterbox! No, not to me - it is
addressed to:

"Cindi Morris of Savannah, GA"

It is signed:

"Always, Ralph M."

and dated June 19, 2005.

I copied down the contents of the love letter, but I left the
original in the letterbox just in case Cindi Morris of Savannah, GA
actually does somehow find the box.

Cindi Morris - if you are out there and want to know the contents of
your letter, please contact me offline.

How delightful to find something *nice* in a letterbox!
