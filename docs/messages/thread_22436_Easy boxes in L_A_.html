There's a whole bunch of boxes in Griffith Park, which is on the north
side of Hollywood. Trekking around the park trying to find those is a
good day's adventure. Public transportation is pretty good--subway and
bus are both fairly reliable and regularly scheduled, a $3 day pass is
your best bet, though really you might want to rent a car if you're
going to do the Griffith Park boxes because the park itself is so vast
I wound up driving from site to site, then doing the separate hikes
for each box. You could get *to* the park on pub-trans, but getting
*around* it is another story.

I can't speak to other boxes in LA because i moved away before i could
hunt for any others elsewhere in the area. (Hence, i wouldn't be able
to meet up either, but good luck and have fun!)
