Santa Fe NM Letterboxing event announcement

From: Diane (dforsdale68@comcast.net) | Date: 2009-06-10 02:06:14 UTC
The Santa Fe NM letterboxers are hosting a letterboxing event in Santa Fe on Saturday, September 26, 2009. The event will be on the grounds of the lovely, historic El Rancho de las Golondrinas, on what was once the "royal road" to Mexico City (the Camino Real). For all the details and to sign up, please see
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1287

Come join us for some history, some exchanges, and some letterboxing fun! Hope to see you there.
