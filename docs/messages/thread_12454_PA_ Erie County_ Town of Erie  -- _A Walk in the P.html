I just got back home from Zuck Park in Erie PA. I went on "A Walk
in the Park", but I couldn't find either letterbox.

I went over the trails several times counting out paces both as
single-step and as double-step. Ended up searching *every* tree
stump on either side of the trail. Admittedly, there aren't that
many and the one that's 70 single-steps down the trail is the only
one where the description "under the stump" is possible.

I couldn't find the second box either. Counting out single and
double step paces takes you to the head and foot of the same fallen
tree, so I searched it from end to end. No box. It's possible I
simply missed both boxes, but I'm reasonably sure I found the
correct locations.

Is there going to be a webpage on the new site for reporting missing
boxes?
