We, too, would like to thank the gracious people of Tom Noone's clan for
hosting us at the white man's long house. We shared a great feast and traded many
goods. We were pleased to be reunited with many of our friends from familiar
tribes and even had a few new travelers from near and afar.

Upon our travels with Mary Manatee and the Yachtsman we met up with Chuck and
Chickabirdie at the trading post of many dyes, and while there we met a
stranger to our clan who showed great interest in what we were doing. We welcomed
her into our tribe and gave her a log book of her own and we traded marks. I am
sure we will see her (calling herself "Happy Stamper" for now) at future
gatherings.

It was a wonderful conclave and we look forward to meeting Mary Manatee and
the Yachtsman again soon at their longhouse in the land of the Wampanoags and
Pokanoket.

-Rustypuff and CSCM
