Whitecliff Course
by Crestwood Parks & Recreation

N 38 33.414 W 090 21.895
In Missouri, United States
Hidden: 4/8/2002

(ratings out of 5 stars. 1 is easiest, 5 is hardest)
Difficulty: Terrain:

This box course is sponsored and maintained by the Crestwood Parks and
Recreation Department and is intended to introduce visitors to Whitecliff
Park. It is open to families with children, and anyone who wants to visit and
explorer the park.

Use a gps to locate the hidden clues or stop by the park office for a clue
map ... traditional letterboxing.

> Community Center hours are:
> Mon-Thur: 8AM-10PM
> Friday: 10AM-6PM
> Saturday: 9AM-12:30PM
> Sunday: Closed
> The park hours are still 7AM-11PM 7 days a week.
>
Each of the five waypoints/ hidden clues consists of a 6" square brass plaque

> imbedded in concrete and engraved with the coordinates of the next hidden
> clue coordinates along with a single letter.
>
The cache contains special Crestwood Geocache pencils, buttons,
letterboxing stamp and a logbook. Each cacher may take just one pencil and
button and leave a log entry. Nothing should be left in the cache box except
a log entry.
