It's registered on LbNA I know. But it's only been there less than a week
and this is a holiday week when not that many people might be out boxing.
Being one of the gourmet cooks in the family, I know I've been stuck pretty
close to the kitchen yesterday and today. :-) Plus I've been trying to
bond two mini-lop bunnies to see if they can become friends -- this is also
a big time of year to dump unwanted animals at shelters and animal
controls -- both these bunnies were recently dumped. A lot of folks are
also lining up their Black Friday moves since BF starts at midnight this
year so there's more planning needed!

I think once Thanksgiving is over and Black Friday is over there might be a
lot more boxers out there to salvage the rest of the weekend for boxing.
The weather is supposed to be rain tho in the Chicago area. I had hopes to
do me some powerboxing! 5 boxes! One Friday, one Saturday, one Sunday, one
Monday, and one Tuesday. :-) For me, that's powerboxing!
