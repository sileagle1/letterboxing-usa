I went letterboxing in Lincoln Nebraska on 12/30/02.   I found Son of Cornhusker,  The box is in perfect shape, dry and completely in tact.  We were surprised that there was only one other boxer who had stamped in.

I introduced by sister-in-law and nieces and nephews to letterboxing and they are hooked.   We planted a new box in Lincoln the same day curtesy of Irishtinker's carving. Expect to see new boxes being planted in the area of Lincoln soon.
