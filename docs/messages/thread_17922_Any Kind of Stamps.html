From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@pocketmail.com) | Date: 2004-02-24 21:07:40 UTC
> What do you think of a Stamp Hostel at Gatherings?

Hmm... I kind of like the idea. It lets people who'd rather not
carve still hide hand-carved stamps. (Or those that are ashamed of
their carvings, sad as the case may be, but those people do exist!)
It lets people who like to carve but not enjoy the work of hiding
boxes give their stamps to those that would.

So I like that idea. The only string I'd attach is that nobody is
allowed to stamp the stamps! Except for the person who 'adopts' the
stamp for hiding elsewhere.

It's not something I personally would get involved with. I *like*
carving stamps, and can do a fine job myself. But I also like the
thrill of hiding my own stamps and except in rare instances, wouldn't
want others hiding my stamps. So while I think the idea is good,
it's not necessarily one I'd participate in either.

Carpe diem!
