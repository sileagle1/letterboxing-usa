                               Inherit the Earth
 
    From rte 195  between Mansfield and Storrs turn into the Old Town Hall / Historical Society opposite  Spring Hill Rd.
  At the rear of the Parking lot the sign marks your start. Pick up trail and take the abandoned town road to the right.   Pass the tree house long vacated.  Long ago farmers  stacked walls to form a corner here.  Onward into the depth of woods  play dot to dot as you walk.  Stay with the blue.  cross a corduroy bridge then a plank one too.   Through the weeds to a T . On the right we go , we're close now .  HOLD CHILDREN'S HANDS AT APPROACH TO  CLIFF  IT'S HUGE!
    Enjoy the view you have to drive all the way to Vermont to find a better one!
Now the search:   from the charred remains at the top of the cliff go due north 35 paces.   Set your course 40 degrees now and step off 24 paces, to the wall. Along the wall the way a squirrel runs go 30 paces. Stop!  Up and over , down and in.
Simple right?  
     To return: go to the  cliff top pick up the blue dot / Nipmuc trail again and return the way you came, or  follow the trail  down toward the hollow or north toward Gurleyville.   Have Fun , and remember   who the earth is for.
