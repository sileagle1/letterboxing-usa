#3. Slug Box (placed Sept 30, 2001 - corrected Dec 24, 2001)

This box is a little more of a hike (at least coming back) and with less
explicit directions. From the last box, find your way to the bluff and head
down to the beach (look for signs to the Colman Pool). From the Colman pool,
head down the path, along the beach, North. Follow the path until you are a
little ways past the end of the sea wall. You will see a path on the right
that heads up the steep bluff. Go only a short distance up the path and you
will see a few logs on your right and a less distinctive path that goes to
the right. You will find a large mossy tree on your right. The box is hidden
at the base.
