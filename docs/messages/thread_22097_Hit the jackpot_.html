From: goyanks1175 (goyanks1175@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-07 15:15:06 UTC
Though new to this list, I'm not a newbie. I've been following the
recent threads on this list via another letterboxer's computer.
Hopefully, the recent wars are over and everyone can get back to what
matters.....boxes....right?

Anyhow, the other day, while melting and doing some boxes in the 90
degree heat, I happened upon some other people in the woods. This
was the first time I had run into other boxers in the woods.

Here's the amazing part: They were Funhog, Mark and Sue Pepe, and
Jenn from Jenn and Silent Doug. What are the chances of running into
these heavy hitters? Moreover, they were all together. Probably
should have bought a lottery ticket, but didn't think of it. What
amazing people they are. Funhog's from Origyn, (this is how it should
be spelled) right? How lucky to be around the same time she was. And
Mark and Sue....awesome. We even went out for pizza afterwards where
we met with JAY DREW and some other old-schoolers...can you even stand
it? Holy Cow!!

Look, is it possible to wipe the slate clean and maybe pretend last
week didn't happen? It shouldn't be too hard. It was a dark time and
hopefully, it is forgetable.

We're all abunch of crazies running through the woods looking lost and
flipping over rocks. Normal people would think we're maniacs.

That's what I'm counting on. The fact that we are different and
willing t forgive and forget and not carry grudges. It's too much
work to stay mad, don't you think?

Let's all be friends again and put our differences aside. REMEMBER,
WE'RE ALL A BUNCH OF LOONIES WHO LOOK FOR TUPPERWARE. Do we need
anymore madness, besides this insane addiction? I've got no room left
for any "extra" craziness. I'm dealing with enough just finding and
planting boxes. Is there rehab for box adicts. And does Blue Cross
cover the treatment?

Respectfully,
Go Yanks
