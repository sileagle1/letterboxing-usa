"Wellies" are just plain old rubber boots - (originally named after the
Duke of Wellington, who designed a prototype - I think I remember that
he was just too impatient to wear the very tight-fitting boots of the
fashionable gents, because they required a valet to put them on & pull
them off: Wellington was in the midst of fighting Napolean and couldn't
be bothered!)
