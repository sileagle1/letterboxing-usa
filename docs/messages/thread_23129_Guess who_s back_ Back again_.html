so im back from the dirty south. now i know why they call it dirty. my
car was so dirty from all the clay. dang! well, i didn't find as many
boxes as i had hoped. who knew that a box that looks on a map to be an
hour and a half away, would actually be three! everything is so spread
out. but none the less, i has found boxes in Vicksburg Mississippi,
Hot Springs Arkansas, Shreveport Louisiana, and Jefferson Texas. I
would suggest all these places and others, to others to go to. All
these places had lots of history and a couple boxes. You may find
yourself struck with wonder instead of wondering where the box is
located. I have placed a box in Shreveport Louisiana and it is
temporary. When my brother moves, he will pick it up and place it
somewhere else. This box is called Fur's Class Stamp, and will
continue to move with him as he will for his job. I hope that everyone
someday gets to travel somewhere and experience what i had last week.
Oh and Mardi Gras was awesome.
