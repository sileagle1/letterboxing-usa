Get your mountain bikes prepped, your hiking shoes tied and Fido leashed for it is time to follow the yellow brick road in search of the Wizard of Oz.  After a year in the planning and planting this series of 8 boxes carved and planted by eight different groups or individuals is ready for the 'boxing public. 

Special thanks to Melissa who made several trips over the 8 miles of dirt roads and trails to the boxes to link them all together.

The Wizard of Oz series is A-#1 & a 10 in my book. Riding your bike
through the dirt roads with a dog at your side it doesn't take much

constraint I only got to do the first 4 boxes but I absolutely loved
it! I can't wait to go back and finish and do the Emerald City.

are beautiful & the trails great.

Thank you so much to the organizers and stamp carvers.
