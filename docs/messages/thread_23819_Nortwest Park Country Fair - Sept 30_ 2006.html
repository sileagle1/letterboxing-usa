Nortwest Park Country Fair - Sept 30, 2006

From: Bob Gresham (bobgresham@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-09-24 12:16:10 UTC-07:00
Northwest Park County Fair - Windsor, CT Saturday September 30, 2006

There are about 28 LBs throughout beautiful Northwest Park in Windsor CT. At the Country Fair this weekend. we will host a Letterboxing informational "booth". The plan is to have copies of the clues for the NWP LBs, literature about Letterboxing, and a "non-event" stamp, and what would a "non-gathering" be without a special one day only Letterbox.

For those who are not attending the gathering on Cape Cod, this is a great alternative. This will be the 24th edition of the Country fair and will be packed full of exhibits, activities,and refreshments.

We hope to see some of our LBing friends.

Bob & Carolyn
