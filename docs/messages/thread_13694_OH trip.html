OH trip

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-20 14:29:46 UTC

Just heard from Wanda. She's on her way home with muddy
boots and a broken tooth after several days of letterboxing in
Ohio, starting with her first Ohio box on Saturday and the Miami
Whitewater gathering on Sunday. Then she had a couple of fun
days letterboxing with ScoutDogs, including an exchange with
Wild Woman and Wild Child at the Cincinnati airport on Tuesday.
Yesterday she started heading back east , and found 30 boxes in
one day, including her 100th in Ohio and 2700th overall! Looks
like Mansfield, CT is getting some competition for letterbox
abundance not only from areas like Portland, OR and greater
DC, but also the greater Cincinnati area!

Pete
looking forward to Wanda's return
