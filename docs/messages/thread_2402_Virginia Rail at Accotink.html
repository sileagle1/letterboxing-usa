Under sunny skies, with tournament back in full swing, just enough
time between games to head back to Fort Belvoir to hunt the 3rd box
hidden in Accotink Wildlife Refuge. The turtles were loving the

it's spot. Had to be real careful to follow The Piper's precise
compass instructions, as there are many 4-in-1's, 3-in-1's, and 2-in-
1's that can mislead you. This was another fun hunt!
