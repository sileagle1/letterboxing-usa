Firefly's Centennial Lake Letterbox
Planted July 29, 2002
Howard County
Maryland
Nearest City: Columbia, MD

Off of Route 108, enter Centennial Park from the South Entrance. Park above
the boat house. Walk down to the lake and turn left onto the paved path that
circles the lake. Cross two bridges. After the red foot bridge, enter the
Centennial Arboretum. Brush up on your tree identification skills!

Locate the Mockernut Hickory Tree. Behind the tree, see a rotting stump.
What you seek is behind the stump.

This is a high traffic area. Please be discrete!
