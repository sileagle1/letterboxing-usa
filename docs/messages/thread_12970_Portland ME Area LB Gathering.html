If people wanted to have a gathering here on Mount Desert Island (Acadia) I would be most happy to help organize it. There are some early Tom Cooch boxes here (which only appear on the old site), a few of my own and lots of Legerdemaine boxes. There are several campgrounds and soon to be lots of tourists. Anyone remotely interested in a Mount Desert Island gathering can be in touch with me through here. There are also a goodly number of boxes in the midcoast area, I believe. Though both on the coast, Acadia and Portland are a good 3 hours from one another, so would really require two different gatherings.
