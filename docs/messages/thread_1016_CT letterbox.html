Hello to everyone out there in letterbox land!
We went on a great little walk today in New London.  We found the Science of Letterboxing box.  The hike was easy, with lots of tiny wood frogs along the path.
The clues were really fun to figure out, they were science trivial questions which when answered directed you down the right path.  Lots of fun!  We were really surprised to see that the only other visitors were the Drews.  We thought we would let local letterboxers know about this hike, since the clues are only available at EMS and not on the internet.  We would recommend this box to anyone looking for a nice level walk, especially those with children.  We took the kids that we watch every now and then, they had a blast (but they always love letterboxing).  Well, hope a few more of you check it out.
        Melissa and Dan
