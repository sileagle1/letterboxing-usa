The Lion Letterbox -- tag a long box to Honey Bucket

Planted by Bookworm and rtrw sometime in the early winter of 2002
It should be easy to find

After finding the Honey Bucket, continue following the directions
back place you began on the purple trail.
A while after the junction with the light blue trail you will find
yourself near a very skinny tree, blazed with the lovely purple, on
the right side of the trail. From this pathetic little tree, count 3
more purple blazes. After the 3rd blazed tree you will see a double
trunked deciduous tree with a log at its foot.
Be careful. The Lion is waiting to ambush you there. Subdue the
lion by stamping into the log book, and continue on your merry way.
Thanks to the Grandbees for bringing us to this wonderful game refuge
with room for so many boxes.
