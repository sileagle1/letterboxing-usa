Hello Luluphi,

Just wanted to let you know that SunvalleySu, JoltionJess and
Jakizard finally found Charge. We tried last October but didn't get
that we needed to cross the river so we didn't get the find!

This time we found a low water cement bridge and even though the
freezing cold water was rushing across it we waded on over in our
sandals. Of course, par for the LB course (or curse) as soon as we
got across we heard the thunder start and by the time we got to the
box the heavens opened up. My kids are convinced that Letterboxing
will end a drought.

We were the first finders! We also found the Silver Bullet HH which
will travel with us next weekend across state lines! Thanks for
planting one in our backyard. We are doing our best to up the
numbers in the Shenandoah Valley. Plant them and they will come!

Box on,
