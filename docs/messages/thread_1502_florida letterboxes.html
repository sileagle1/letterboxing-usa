Ok, in the spirit of the times- here is the recount of the Florida
letterboxes. Mind you there will be no late submissions accepted here
by the Florida Secretary of Letterboxes (that's me elected by a
unanimous vote of Florida Letterboxers - 1 to 0). There are currently
two boxes in the Titusville area (unless they have recently gone
missing). The ones in the Canaveral Marsh and the Blackpoint Wildlife
Drive are valid and as far as I know and do not have any dimples or
hanging chads. The squirrelfish box was previously vandalized in
clear attempt by the throw the numbers off by some unknown nefarious
party (thought to be the ALFP - Anti Letterboxing Florida Party).

There is a chance I may be able to replace that one this week. There
is also a good chance that I may place one in Sarasota over the
Thanksgiving weekend. If so, I will overrule myself and add them to
the count and send the clues to the list ASAP. I have also heard
rumors a certain Amy is threatening to break my exclusive collection
of Florida Letterboxes by adding one or more during an upcoming trip.
I hope so, I have waited a very long time for someone else to plant
some down here for me to find!
