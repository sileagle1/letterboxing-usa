I've taken the picture of the bird in question straight from the state governments website. So, in the case of the meadowlark or the cardinal, I have different images for each state.

Given that each image is different, This constitutes a different stamp as the poses are different and the state is different. At least that's how I'm looking at it, your mileage may vary, some settling may occur during shipment, void where prohibited. (smiles)

I'm really looking forward to do this and getting someone from each state to help us, for, with out you, there's be not TR stamp in any states other than Washington, Oregon and maybe Idaho.
