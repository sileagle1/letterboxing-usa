New Box In So. Nevada

From: (realroadrunner@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-12-26 18:37:50 UTC-08:00
I have placed a new box in southern Nevada and posted it to the LBNA site.
I guess I though it got to the list automatically, but perhaps not. So,
here's the anouncement and clues:


Ghost Rider
Placed by: Suzanne (contact the placer)
Placement date: Dec 21 2003
State: Nevada
County: Nye
Nearest city: Beatty
Number of boxes: 1

Clues
Difficulty: Easy
Status of Box: Alive and Well on 12/21/03

On August 9, 1904
Two men, one short, one cross,
Struck gold
And founded a city with 12,000 souls.

Only ghosts remain to mine memories,
including the ghost with a bike,
That he keeps at the ready
for when inspiration strikes.

Start here, and walk southwest 75 paces.
Encounter the thirteen without any faces.
Count one from the left, or 13 from the right,
Go behind the big platform and stay out of sight.

Three boards are curled up,
Reach in and reach right.
Make your mark and replace
With it standing upright.

Please give me an update after you find: realroadrunner@earthlink.net

NOTE: Always take adequate precautions (such as prodding with a stick and/or
wearing gloves) before reaching into dark crevasses and holes in the wild.
Rattlesnakes do inhabit the area, and particularly during the summer the
temperature can reach well over 100 degrees. Be prepared!
