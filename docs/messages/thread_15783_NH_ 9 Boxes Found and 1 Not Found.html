We did a few boxes in New Hampshire this weekend and here are the results:
Found NH Forest in Concord
Found Tombstone in Manchester (we appear to be first finders on this,
but perhaps the logbook was changed out since it's been in place since

Found Gilson Road Cemetary and NH Ghosts in Nashua
Found Mine Falls Park in Nashua
Found Sea Song Mystery in Portsmouth
Found Portsmouth Harbor Light in New Castle (the stamp pad is dead in
this one- it needs to be replaced. Also one of the compass bearings is
off as multiple people have noted in the logbook. This may account for
some of the missing reports.)
Did NOT find Hear the Sea in New Castle (this one has several other
missing reports and according to those who had found it before was an
easy find, so my guess is that it is gone)
Found Live Free or Die in New Castle (the bottom of this stamp is
broken off, but it is still stampable)
