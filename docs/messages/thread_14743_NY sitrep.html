Tom's Letterbox in Fayetteville, NY, Onondaga Co.
Anyone within driving distance of Syracuse should visit this box,
which is in the Green Lakes State Park. This box is alive & well and
is in a peaceful, beautiful location. While we stamped in a Bard Owl
flew up to us and perched on a branch above to watch, or maybe it just
wanted the little mousies that live under the bird watching platform.
It was not at all bothered by my flashing camera, or our barking dog.
