New Format and Clues for Orient Point Light Letterbox

From: fred (lonemasswolf@covad.net) | Date: 2001-12-04 18:18:25 UTC-05:00

Letterbox Name:  Orient Point Light Letterbox

Location:  Orient, Suffolk County, Long Island, New York

Placed by:  Lonemasswolf and You Inspire My Spirit on 18 May 2001.

Clues:   Easy.

Terrain:  Easy.

 

Clues 

Once you have found the Orient Point Letterbox placed by Ladybug, return to the last telephone pole on Orient Point.

 

Step off 30 paces at a bearing of 100 degrees.

Orient Point Light should be at a bearing of 75 degrees.

New London / Orient Point Ferry at a bearing of 220 degrees

Cable station directly behind you.

Look down to the left for two pieces of gray slate.

The box lies below the slate.
