Greetings from Maine... I just discovered letterboxing a couple of
weeks ago. I grabbed my husband and a friend and off we trekked, with
our brand-new stamps, across a swinging bridge toward our first box. My
friend and I were shreekingly (a word?) delighted when my husband rolled
over the cement block. It was dusk and we had almost given up--glad we
didn't!

heading down to visit the family... So, I HAD to stop by... glad I did.
