From: zed_boxing (szorzi_1999@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-10 05:49:22 UTC
I planted 3 new boxes in Baton Rouge, Louisiana today:

Big Bang in a Little Box (ultra-micro box)
Breaking News (with my best carved stamp so far)
"Taking Orders" Series - One from Column A (B is already placed and
C will come this weekend - all are microboxes)

The clues for all three are on www.letterboxing.org. Remember that
to find the listings for Louisiana boxes you can't just click the
map. You must use the "Search for Boxes" link at the page and
choose Louisiana for the state. That will give you about 30 boxes
as opposed to the 5 you get from the map.

Sadly, I found that my first plant - The Old One - has gone missing
after just 2 months, and with only 2 visitors. C'est la vie. I
left it on the clue site but marked it as missing in both the Name
and the Clues. I hope to recarve and replant nearby (I like the
clues.)

I've also had a report that Fleur de Lis Pizza is also missing,
after only about a week and with no visitors at all that I'm aware
of. I will try to confirm that this weekend. I've marked it as
reported missing on the clue site.

The next plant in this area will be "Stinkin' and Drinkin'",
hopefully to be planted this weekend.

Happy hunting and planting!

Zed
