Once Again I am sorry about the gathering

From: exit9maine (exit9maine@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-11 21:31:21 UTC

Hello everyone,

I would just like to let you all know I cried and cried for hours before I
decided to post pone this gathering. I put so much work into it. I would do
anything to change the weather. I didn't want to inconvience anyone, really,
I'm not a harble person. I just didn't want people driving in the two feet of
snow we are going to get up here and get into an accident and me feel
horable for the rest of my life that i put someone in danger.
They have already closed the University for tomorrow and we havn't gotten
a single snow flake. That says something because it has to be bad to shut
down the school. My family down in Mass said they are getting two inches so
please see the big difference between two inches and 24. There is no way
even if everyone fought the snow that the boxes would be accessible anyway.
I really do hope that you all don't hate me forever but I thought this was
the best thing.

Merenwen
