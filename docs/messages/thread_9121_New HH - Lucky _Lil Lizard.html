New HH - Lucky 'Lil Lizard

From: SpringChick (letterbox@attbi.com) | Date: 2002-11-11 19:54:33 UTC
All the talk of lizards and snakes reminded me that I had once heard
it was lucky to have a lizard cross your path. Since in MI we don't
have lizards, I felt deprived of the chance to run across this
unexpected bestowment of good luck... Thus the Lucky 'Lil Lizard --
for those who don't have the fortune to cross paths with a lizard,
and an added bonus for those who do!

I had thought about carrying him around in my pack as a traveler
stamp, an always-present lucky companion, but alas, the little guy
slithered out of my pack in into a letterbox. I don't think he has
any idea how cold he's going to get here in Michigan this winter!
Hopefully somebody will be able to come to his rescue and carry him
to a new home before that!

Now the only thing you need to figure out is how to score him -- an
F, an HH or a L? Guess that's up to you ;-)

Deb
