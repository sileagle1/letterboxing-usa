I can't restrict myself to just 10. Off the top of my head, here are
my top 15, in no particular order:

Birdhouse, Needham, MA
Loma Prieta Earthquake, Aptos, CA
Pirates of Hingham Harbor, Hingham, MA
Palindromic Harmony, Mystery, RI
Cape Henlopen Devil, Lewes, DE
Ghost Train, South Dennis, MA
Wolf Mystery in RI, Mystery, RI
Secrets of the Knob, Woods Hole, MA
North Quabbin Reservoir: Salamander, Pelham, MA
Hancock Hill, Milton, MA
The Phantom, Milton, MA
The Pig is back, Portsmouth, RI
Dancing Men, Mystery, RI
Let It Be, Mystery, MA
The Duality of Nature aka The Witches, Richmond, RI

Why these? Some have great stamps, some are in great locations, some
are great adventures, some are great puzzles. To be more specific
might give too much away.

The Pig is Back is no longer available, but as far as I know the rest
are still in the field and awaiting your visit.
