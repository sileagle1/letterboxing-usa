For one of my boxes, you need to go to three places and solve two
different kinds of codes.

There are no 'letterbox clue' police (grin) I suggest that you go
ahead and make the clues to your boxes any way you want to. If
people don't want to take the effort to search for your box, they
wont. However, some people like clues that have more challenge than
those that are straight forward, follow the directions.

Enjoy!
---Bindle Babe
