Boxing Weekend

From: forestfrogdad <dad@forestfrogfamily.com> (dad@forestfrogfamily.com) | Date: 2002-12-30 14:20:05 UTC
The Forest Frog Family hit the trails (okay, sidewalks) again this
Saturday after a few weeks sans letterboxing. First, we stopped at
Lily of the Valley, and despite the warning, got covered with ink!
Next, we headed downtown to check on Mr. Jefferson's Microbox, and
we're happy to say that it's still in fine condition. Our next stop
was a second attempt at finding Orion ... and I must say it's easier
in the daylight! We headed across the Mall to plant the Independence
Microbox (clues forthcoming), and then down to the waterfront to find
the Ghost From the Titanic as the sun set over the river.

Looking forward to the DC area gathering in 3 weeks ...
