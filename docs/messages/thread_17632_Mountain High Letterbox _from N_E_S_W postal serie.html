I am pleased to announce that the second installment in the N,E,S,W series
was launched, of all days, on Boxing Day!! (I was pretty excited about
that).

I have had many, many requests to be on the list and didn't want it to take
five years to get around :-) So, I followed Martha's example and carved two
stamps. They have been traveling around for a bit to various letterboxers
that were first on the list, but for a very short time (as in, until
tomorrow or so), I have them both in my possession. So, I'm sending out the
confirmation of who was on the mailing lists. Let me know if you have any
problems with it, and please keep my address if you are on the list. If
anything changes in your address status or such, we need to know!

I hope everyone enjoys the letterbox!
Cadenza
