Subject: IA - Toledo/Tama letterbox

Hello,

I planted my first letterbox this weekend. It is in Toledo, IA.

It is fairly easy to find.

Here are the directions/clues:

Drive to Toledo IA. Take Highway 30 West towards the Mesqwauki
Casino. On the edge of town there is the Toledo City Park. Pull in
the park and drive west on the road that runs parallel to the highway
until you reach a small picnic shelter.

Get out of your car and walk straight from the shelter into the
trees. No more than twenty paces in (If you reach the creek you have
walked too far), you will come upon a large fallen tree. In a hole
in the base of the tree is the Tama Letterbox.

Happy hunting.

Chris Goudy
cagoudy@hotmail.com
