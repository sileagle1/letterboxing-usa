(From friends who can't get up on the list just now...):

The Davie Co. letterboxers stamped up at this box last Dec. We became
confused but were very fortunate to finally get on the right course before
darkness caught us. The box was intact and the stamp is great. A couple of
hints: Find the swimming pool off Benjamin Parkway. It's actually across
from Grimsley H.S. And also don't become confused by the street named
Mimosa. After that it's actually very simple. Good luck and good hunting!
