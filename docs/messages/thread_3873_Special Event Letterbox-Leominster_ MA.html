Special Event Letterbox-Leominster, MA

From: Deanne & Dave (davebes@net1plus.com) | Date: 2001-12-04 13:31:05 UTC-05:00
Native Son #7, Louis Charpentier
Leominster, MA (Worcester County)
Planted by David & Deanne Besnia
This is a special event letterbox--it will be available for one month only.
To find this letterbox take Merriam Avenue out of downtown Leominster. Head west and go through one set of lights. Be on the lookout for 522 Merriam Avenue, which is on the left. Park along Merriam Avenue and enjoy the beautiful Christmas display all hand carved by Leominster's Native Son #7, Louis Charpentier, a delightful 91 year old who invites young and old to walk through the decorations, take pictures, sign his guest book and while you're at it........stamp into the letterbox that you will find when you stand in front of the menorah and look down to your left. Underneath the small bush at your side is Native Son #7, honoring a man who is as well known for his kind and generous spirit as he is for the bowties he wears!
