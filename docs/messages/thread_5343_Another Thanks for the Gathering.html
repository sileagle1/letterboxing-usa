Another Thanks for the Gathering

From: gbheron77 (gbheron@netzero.net) | Date: 2002-05-13 12:54:56 UTC
I too had a great time at Sleeping Giant Saturday meeting boxers from
our own backyard as well as out of staters. Of course I think we
all have to be a little bit 'out of state' to be letterboxers! We
enjoyed hunting with Ginger and John in the morning while catching up
with Butterfly and her group. After the noontime fest and stamp
exchange frolic, Deanne and I decided to hit a quieter trail in the
afternoon and were delighted to find Aili's artful Merlin's Grotto.
Terrible at remembering names and faces, adding a letterbox handle
made this even more challenging for me. So if we meet again, forgive
me if I need another introduction. However, I know that I too missed
exchanging stamps with some that I thought I had gotten. Hope there
will always be a next time.

Most of all I want to thank Aili and Bruce for their faithful
presence throughout the day - Without them we would have been lost.

Ginger from Massachusetts
