There have been several comments lately on the
sheer size of the archives, and the impossibility
of searching out information there. Having read
many of the posts over the last year or so, I
have decided to create a series of narratives
which provide a sampler of interesting, relevant,
and/or historical posts to the archive. My intent
is to document some of the "firsts" of
letterboxing in the US, as well as to provide
some of the flavor of letterboxing as it
was then - compared to how it is now.

Episode one may be found at
home.gwi.net/~moxie1/Clues/letterbox-usa-history.html
