Burr Oak Woods Conservation Area
20 miles east of Kansas City, Missouri

Open daily from 8 a.m. until 8 p.m. during Daylight Savings Time and
from 8 a.m. until 6 p.m. during Central Standard Time. Closed
Thanksgiving, Christmas Day and New Year's Day. The area is composed
of 1,071 acres of woodlands, fields and ponds, and includes five self-
guided nature trails, two of which are paved and fully accessible.
Two sack-lunch picnic areas include shelter, tables and a privy.
Pets, even leashed pets, are NOT allowed.

Please Note: Specific permission for letterboxing has been neither
requested of nor granted by the area administrators, so discretion is
appreciated. The information desk in the nature center has available
a very nice (and free) tri-fold with a good map of the area.

Bethany Falls Trail is 1.3 miles of easy wood chip-covered terrain,
dependable in all weather.
