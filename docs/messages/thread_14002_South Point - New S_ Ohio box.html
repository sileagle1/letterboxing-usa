South Point

Difficulty: easy (once you find the park)
Terrain: easy
Placed by: Bigguy, CardinalMom, Pack Rat, & Trail Buddy
Date placed: July 3, 2003

Go as far south in Ohio as it's possible to go. There you'll find a
tiny little park with a very nice view of three states. Stand at the
barbecue grill as if you were going to cook yourself a burger. Take
a bearing of 304 and go about 14 paces (1 pace = 2 steps) to a small
tree. You should find South Point at the base of the tree under some
rocks.
