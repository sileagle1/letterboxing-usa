Logo Design Contest

From: Mark Pepe (mjpepe1@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-12-01 14:12:25 UTC

Hi boxers:


There's a contest presently underway to design our own LbNE logo for
the Northeast letterboxing community. You are invited to check out the
details at our website. While I realize that this is a national list,
I also know that there is some great national talent out there as
well and want to offer the design aspect to all letterboxers.

Eventually, this new logo will be use in a full line of clothing,
backpacks, etc. While we are not involved in the sale or profit of
these items, we've offered our website as a medium to facilitate
these sales as a favor to a local CT boxer.

Have fun and just get out there & box!!! [And design a logo!]


Mark Pepe
http://markandsuepepe.blogspot.com/

Good question Jim. I would think the final size for manufacturing would
be somewhere in the range of 3 x 3 or thereabouts.

But, the image should be provided say twice that size. It's easier to
reduce a larger image and still maintain the quality than to have to
enlarge it.

The winning entry designer may have to resubmit in the needed size but
with technology today, I think we'll be fine.
