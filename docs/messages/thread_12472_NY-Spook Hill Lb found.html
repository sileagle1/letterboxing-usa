What a beautiful Spring day yesterday was in Upstate New York. The
puppies and I headed down to Middlesex near beautiful Canandaigua Lake
to look for JeLyBean's letterbox on Spook Hill. And Spooky it sure is.
It's quite a sensation to have your car, in neutral, slowly roll to a
stop on what appears to be a down hill section of road, then, still in
neutral, begin to roll backward up the hill. Really strange! Anyway,
there it was, a pristine virgin Letterbox with a beautifully carved
stamp that was perfect for the location. And what a lovely view from
that spot. The Finger Lakes section of upstate NY is a world all its
own. Thank you JeLyBean.
