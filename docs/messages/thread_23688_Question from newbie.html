I believe they were my Battle of the Bands clues. You mentioned looking at some of them when you contacted me. I use camo colored burlap to wrap my boxes in. I have seen boxers using camo tape and I am pretty sure I will switch to that, just having trouble finding some right now. Should be in stock in my Kmart in a couple of weeks.

A great resource is the newboxers list. Check it out at:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/

Don't forgot to join your local boxing community (Mid-Atlantic Region) at:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/lbma/

Another site you should join is www.atlasquest.com , another source of clues, talk lists, event information.

And Silent Dougs site www.letterboxing.info is very helpful. Plus, he has some plants in our area.
