......and ferrets. In November my husband and I went to go letterboxing and

Hiker!

The cat I saw on the AT was always carried on the owner's back, then
was allowed to run loose while taking a break or at the shelters.

It was very popular among hikers since the cat was great and getting
rid of the mice in the shelters at night. That cat may not have
done much hiking, but it earned its keep. =) I've got a picture of
him and his cat somewhere around here. *digging around* Okay, I
uploaded it to the Photos area. Check out the last picture.

Another hiker apparently was carrying a real, live turtle, although
I admit I didn't actually see that personally.

On an unrelated hike, I did once see a ferret being walked on a
leash.

One person e-mailed me privately about a famous letterboxer in
England that would take his chicken with him. (Reputedly handy for
finding those tricky to locate boxes...)

In all honesty, I didn't personally have an issue with the term 'dog
friendly' just like I don't have an issue with the term 'mailman'.
But in this world of political correctness run amok, it doesn't hurt
to consider such issues. =)
