Sligo Creek Letterbox
Wheaton, Maryland
Hidden by Squirrel and McCoy October 23, 2000
Dogs on leash ok
twograysquirrels@aol.com

Begin your search at University Blvd. and Sligo Parkway. Park in the first
parking lot on the right. Go to the sliding tube. Sitting at the bottom of
the tube look to the right for the green arches and go there and read the
instructions.

The number of beginning leg vaults is the number of bridges you must cross.
The number of beginning balance walks is the number of bridges you must pass.
Now begin by crossing back over the road and take the trail to the left.
After the last bridge in the instructions you should come to a bench on your
right.
Look behind that bench for a short log, a little ways back into the woods.
The letterbox is behind that short log.
