To unsubscribe, send a message to:
letterbox-usa-unsubscribe@yahoogroups.com

But you might also want to get the Daily Digest - I really like that - it
saves on a lot of email.

To get a digest:
1. go to www.yahoogroups.com
2. login - If you haven't registered, do it with the email address that is
used when you receive the letterboxing email
3. Once you are registered and you login, a list of "My groups" is listed
on the left.
4. Click on letterbox-usa group
5. On the right of the Letterboxing home page, Click on "Edit my
membership".
6. On the next screen, select "Daily Digest" and scroll to the bottom to
"Save Changes". That's it.

Good luck!
Pat
