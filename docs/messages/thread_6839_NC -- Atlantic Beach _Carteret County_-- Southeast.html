Off of Ft. Macon Road in Atlantic Beach...
look for signs of General John G. Parke.
He led union forces near Hoophole Creek.
If the road is gated...have no fear.
There is another option.
You'll find it near Hardee's.
Enter the rope lined path.
Stop when you get to the marker that
is the same as "Lilybelles" age.
(She drew the pic that is on the stamp.)
Look at the water, turn around to your
right...behind you find a stump...
wedged beneath it you'll find "Clear Water"
under a piece of felt. 
(Be careful..it's protected by a thorny vine.)
If there are little ones in your group...
please feel free to have each of them liberate a sea creature.
Also make sure it's covered well when you return it to its
hiding place, it's a tricky spot.
Thanks and have fun.

The Russ Clan
