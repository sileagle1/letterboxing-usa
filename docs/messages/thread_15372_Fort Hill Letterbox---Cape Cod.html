Fort Hill Letterbox---Cape Cod

From: the5coolcats (the5coolcats@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-27 04:23:04 UTC
The5coolcats visited and refreshed the letterbox planted at Fort Hill
in Eastham on Cape Cod last weekend. We enjoyed reading the many
messages and greetings from those who followed our clues and took a
walk at this wonderful place. Since our last visit this spring
the "sunny pin" has apparently been removed but you still should be
able to find your prize without too much trouble as many apparently
have recently. Try doing what we did last Sunday---get up early, go
to the Hole in One Donut shop on Route 6 for some fresh Jelly Sticks
and coffee, take your walk and find your prize, then treat yourself
to breakfast with a view from the bench at the top of Fort Hill. It
always reminds the5coolcats that life is good!
