I thought I had been sent this earlier.

It might be a good idea to be careful regarding where Xacto blades
(& similar equipment) are carried or sent. We travel quite a bit &
also go to meetings in federal, state, county & city buildings where
there are security check points. I've adjusted to making sure all
our small scissors, knives, etc. are not going through a security
check point.

There could be a problem with blades sent to certain countries.

And, speaking of metal detectors, don't forget, don't put metal
items in the LBs. People who go treasure hunting with metal
detectors would get excited unnecessarily.
