Finally had a few minutes & an extra adult to find the quickie Blake
Lane Dog Park mini-box (#2 in the park series).

Picked DH (Dear Hubby) up at Vienna Metro this afternoon - our first
time near there in a long while. Beautiful, sunny but cool spring
afternoon. On the way back, nobody minded - at last! - so we parked and
I took a look. Quick & easy, as advertised. Initial question was "5
o'clock relative to what?", but that resolved itself. Most recent dated
stampers had been by in January; pawprint stamp after those was undated
and uncommented. Box was very slightly moist inside plastic container;
wiped it with a tissue. Otherwise in good shape. Replaced where/as
found & very well covered.
