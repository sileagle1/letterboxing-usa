As winter comes, harsh reality sets in!

From: nh_local_newspaper (gingerbreadjunk@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-21 04:24:58 UTC
New Hampshire - AP

Today, a spokesman from the local community involved with the
search of Gingerbread Jim, has started to come to the harsh reality,
that time may be running out. With only 11 days left before funds run
dry from the search party fund, volunteers has become frustrated by
the lack of clues found during the search.
"This weather seems to be getting worse everyday we come out
here to look. We are limited to several hours before retreating for
warmth." says the spokesman. "If it would only warm up. Things would
be much easier on us."
It seems to be a growing concern as it was in the beginning,
except now, winter has finally shown her ugly face. Gingerbread Jim
is somewhere in the woods of New Hampshire, but where? The
temperatures have dipped close to 0 degrees in most parts of the
state over the past week, and the forcast doesn't look to be any
better.
There has been some good news though. A few days ago, a cookie
crumb was found. Unfortunately no other details as to the location of
the finding have been released due to the kidnapper being still at
large.
If you would like to volunteer in the search for Gingerbread
Jim, you are asked to be aware of your surroundings. With the ice and
thin layer of snow on the ground, it can be slick at times. So please
take your time, but hurry up as well.

For more information, please visit http://savingjim.blogspot.com
