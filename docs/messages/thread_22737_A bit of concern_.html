From: gingerbreadjunk (gingerbreadjunk@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-23 21:19:53 UTC
Unforunately, living in new hampshire, for the past several weeks,
it as rained and it has poured. my efforts to letterbox has been
hampered by swollen water ways and flooded paths. my concern lies
with any or all boxes that maybe prone to flood waters of every
sort. the thought of going out to search for a letterbox that
doesn't exist, is not a thought i really enjoy thinking about. if i
may send out this message to all letterboxers, that you check up on
your boxes placed. if you know of boxes in your town even if they
don't belong to you, to please check on them and report to the
placer. i would hate for the art of letterboxing to go with the
weather. i plan on doing my part to rebuild new hampshire, one box
at a time.

david (team new hampshire)

p.s.
i apoligze in advance for not mentioning the unfortunate owners of
any property, damaged or lost, in the floods of new hampshire. the
hardest hit area is the south west corner of our wonderful state. as
i have, if you would like to help in any way, please donate whatever
you can to the american red cross, designated for the new hampshire
floods. you and i may not have been affected by the floods directly,
but we all are affected in some way or another. thank you.
