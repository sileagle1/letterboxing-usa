From: ribnag (ribnag@entropy.tmok.com) | Date: 2001-09-10 19:12:35 UTC-04:00
RIBNAG has planted its first box in Maine, and as two members
recently moved there, you can expect many more in the future.
Yes, we considered changing the name to "NEBNAG", but since
one of the people who moved also maintains our web-pages (namely,
myself), we decided that laziness dictate that the name not
change. ;-)

Anyway, the box:

Dust To Dust Letterbox
Bangor City Forest, ME
1 box (hopefully more to come), at least 2km hike.

The clue lives at:
http://entropy.tmok.com/~ribnag/lb-dusttodust.shtml

And of course, as always, you can access it through the main page:
http://entropy.tmok.com/~ribnag/index.shtml

Don't let the name of the location fool you... Bangor City
Forest has very litle "city"-like about it, other than the
name and its proximity to Bangor (which also doesn't look
very city-like).

Also, a small request... We have confirmed the "Wilcox Park"
box as missing. Unfortunately, it seems that by some bizarre
oversight no RIBNAG'ers actually kept a copy of the stamp image
(I say "bizarre" due to the OCD-like nature of this webmaster...
you should see my spreadsheet of found boxes, I probably have
more data about each box than the people who planted it ).
So, if anyone managed to get it during its very short life
(first report came in within a month), could you email a scan
of the stamp image to ribnag@users.tmok.com? We would like to
replace it.

Finally, at least one person has asked permission to use the
"standard disclaimer" RIBNAG has at the top of each clue. We
don't mind this at all, just give us credit (a link to the
RIBNAG homepage would suffice) and realize that none of us
have a law degree, so it "just sounds good" but probably has
no legal validity at all.
