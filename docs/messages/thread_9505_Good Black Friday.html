From: celticprideltrbxr (eileenbriley@yahoo.com) | Date: 2002-12-02 15:12:20 UTC
Bern and I introduced a few friends to LBing on Black Friday (better
than shopping!) We headed out to find the Creature of Fountainhead
Park and were rewarded with all 5 boxes - yes Scooby is there! - and a
geocache! Our first! We then left our friends and headed into DC to
see some monuments and nabbed the Taft Memorial box in the pitch dark.
It was in o.k. shape - a bit soggy. The ink has run on the pages, but
you can still make everyone out. We did put it in a plastic bag before
returning it to it's hiding spot. We forgot to bring the Einstein and
Nat'l Geographic clues with us, so we'll have to go back for those and
also nab the new Smithsonian box. What a great idea that is! Thanks
leger de maine!
