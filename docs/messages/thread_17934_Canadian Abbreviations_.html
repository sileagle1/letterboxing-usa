Hi all.

As Canadians living here in the USA, we keep track of the (pitifally
few) LBs in Canada. I know it's probably picky to a lot of you, but
we'd really appreciate it if the site would reflect the proper
abbreviations for the 10 provinces and 3 territories...

BC - British Columbia
AB - Alberta
SK - Saskatchewan
MB - Manitoba
ON - Ontario
QC - Quebec
NB - New Brunswick
NS - Nova Scotia
PE - Prince Edward Island (PEI is still widely used)
NF - Newfoundland
YT - Yukon Territory
NW - Northwest Territories (NWT is still widely used)
NU - Nunavut

Thanks, eh?
