The Sounds of Siren letterbox in Gordon's Pond in Rehoboth, DE is
alive and well as of 10/5/03. Team PresSinOn took a trip to the beach
again this weekend and found it along with the Seaside Nature Trail
and the Cape Henlopen Devil. We found the other Henlopen boxes a few
weeks ago.
Anyway, the reason for posting is to let you know that the log book
for Sounds of Sirens is full. We didn't have a spare one on us, but
we took a few pages from one of our logbooks to log in, and leave a
HH (I only mention the HH as an incentive to get people to come visit
DE. LOL). The next finder has nothing to stamp into unless they have
a spare book or some spare pages.
Just thought I'd let ya know.

Box on,
NeverEnuff of Team PresSinOn
