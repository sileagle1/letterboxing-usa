From: (bastiality@aol.com) | Date: 2002-07-31 01:19:42 UTC-04:00
Some friends and I hit Cape Cod a couple weeks ago. The boxes we found were
Chatham Light, Three Sisters Light, Nauset Light, Highland (Cape Cod) Light,
Heron's Haven and Secrets of the Knob. We also placed a box at Nobska
(Nobsque) Light in Woods Hole.

We did not find the Fort Hill letterbox, though the site was enjoyable.

And if you visit Providence, I recommend the box there. The hunt was a lot
of fun and the stamp was beautiful. It takes a little knowledge of the city
layout, but once you're on the right track the clues fall right into place.
The the "bonus" box, however, was not to be found.

Hope it helps. Happy hunting!
