I was in Plymouth this past Thursday for a business retreat and I
took advantage of the opportunity to finish tracking down the Bunny
in a Briar Basket. Once I located the correct property the box
parctically jumped out at me, but I made extra sure it was well
hidden when I left.

On my way home on Friday I attempted to find the Scituate Light
letterbox, without success. I'm sure it's there, but with the sun
going down, and me needing a bathroom, I just didn't have the
patience and concentration that were required.

Kim and I spent part of Sunday and Monday in the Moose Hill Wildlife
Sanctuary in Sharon, MA. We found The Bluffs, Moose Hill, and The
Boulders letterboxes on Sunday, and Hobb's Hill on Monday.

Once again, thanks to Lonemassworlf and You Inspire My Spirit for
getting us out into the open spaces of Eastern Massachusetts!

Kim and I plan to be at the Big E in Springfield on the 30th, where
we hope to come across Leader of the Pack's letterboxing booth.
Maybe we'll see some of you there as well.
