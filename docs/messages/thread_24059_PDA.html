From: Geoff Tucker (toejamlbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-12-28 16:25:34 UTC-08:00
I learned how to use my PDA for letterboxing. First, copy and paste clues from LBNA into my word processor. I then save all boxes pasted as a .txt file. I infrared the file to my Z22 Palm and it receives it as a memo. I open the memo while boxing and can access clue info while I am searching for the boxes. I just thought it cool. No more ink printed paper clues for me!!!!
