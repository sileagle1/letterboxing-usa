Who is "Just Beth"?

From: rabbit_relations (milneelizabeth@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-17 15:00:23 UTC
I am hoping someone can answer this question... how can I get in touch
with "Just Beth" or find out where her valley might be?

We have been letterboxing for only one year (our anniversary is this
weekend!) but have had many rewarding adventures. This past April I
came across a tiny HH in a small plastic bag. No logbook, just "Help!
Please, Murder!" written on the outside, and a letter of the alphabet
on the other. Wanda and Pete's HH page cleared up some of the
mystery, as I realized that this stamp was a symbol in a coded
message. Now that I have decoded the message, I am still puzzled as
to the starting location. Was I supposed to be able to figure it
out? Or do I need to know who Just Beth is? If anyone could help,
I'd certainly appreciate it! I have no way of knowing how far from
home this HH has already travelled...

Thank you!
One of Rabbit's Friends and Relations
