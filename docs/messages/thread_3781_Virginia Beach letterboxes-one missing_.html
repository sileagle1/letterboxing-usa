Virginia Beach letterboxes-one missing?

From: A.D. (alwayschaos@yahoo.com) | Date: 2001-11-23 14:56:46 UTC-08:00
Hi all!

Just checking in to let you know that Ovals of Cassini
and the Witch of Agnesi were doing well as of last
week, though they did seem a bit damp. Also picked up
a hitchhiker- Palantin! :o)

Got the Long Creek Seahorse today with my parents (who
very much enjoy this new hobby) but sadly could not
find the Broad Bay Seashell box. It looked as though
something/one else had been there before but, look as
we might, we were unable to come up with it. Does
anyone know the status of this box?

We were rewarded with a very late in the season show
put on in Broad Bay by a pod of four very playful
dolphins so the hike was a good one even though the
box was no where to be found. And hitchhiker Palantin
is on his way ready to be found again.

Amy :o)

Hi Kathy and Amy,
I meant to check on Broad Bay Shells a couple weeks ago but didn't
get out that trail. After reading your posts I decided I needed a
good long jog after Thanksgiving weekend. I've always wanted to do
Osprey trail from the Shore Drive entrance to the park. Fort Story
Lighthouse, Long Creek Seahorse and Broad Bay Shells were the first
Letterboxes I found over a year ago. You may still want to check for
yourself but Broad Bay Shells is not where it was 13 months ago. The
tangle of fallen trees is no longer a tangle. They're spread all over
now. Just thought you might want to know what I found.

Mark
