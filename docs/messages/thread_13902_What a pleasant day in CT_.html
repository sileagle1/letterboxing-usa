What a pleasant day in CT!

From: (mindizney@aol.com) | Date: 2003-06-29 19:08:38 UTC-04:00
Hello all! Since today was the last day I could box for a whole week (due to
my grad school class starting tomorrow), I thought I would go out and do some
boxing today! Today was a bit different than other days... I had a theme
boxing day. I wanted to find as many Letterboxer2002 boxes as I could!

I started out in Norwalk and found the Norwalk Historical Society and
Jonathan's Birthday box, (I already have the Buteo one, and I don't think the first
Disc Golf one has been replaced yet) then headed out to New Fairfield and found
Woody Woodpecker and Lady of the Lake (YES!!!!! IT LIVES!). I also found
IrishCOD's Fox View Letterbox, and the third of the 3 Boxes in Danbury series.
I also found four other boxes that I can't talk about online..... :-)

BTW.... Friday, I hit my 800th box at the Hanging Hiker on the My Favorite
Musicals loop. THere were a few boxes that I hadn't gotten from that series, so
I thought that I would do the loop again. This time, I was victorious with
all of the boxes I was looking for, including Spring, Spring Spring, and
another unpublished box: Love Labour's Lost.

All in all! A great summer so far! Too bad I have to go to grad school this
week.... the weather's supposed to be GREAT!

Happy Hunting Everyone!
Music Woman
in CT
