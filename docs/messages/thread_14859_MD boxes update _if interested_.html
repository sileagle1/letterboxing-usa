From: bassackwardsfrommd (bassackwardsfrommd@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-06 01:34:06 UTC
We found two more boxes today! We didn't have much time, so we did
two easy ones. The Clustered Spires of Frederick is still safe and
sound at the library. Very cool idea! It's the second library box
we've found. We also found the New Jersey in Maryland Box. I found
the clues on the old LBNA website. I think it was listed as Maryland
Monkey box, or something like that. I'm not sure why they're not on
the new site. The box is still there, very safe and dry. And
current, too. The logbook said #2, and was placed in June of this
year. Very cool spot for a box! I'm getting ready to carve my next
stamp. I may make this one a mystery box. Hmmmm, haven't decided
yet.

THe Basses
