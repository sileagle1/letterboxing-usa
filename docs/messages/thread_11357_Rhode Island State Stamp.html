East Killingly Conn. Rated Easy
Drive-up Planted By The NeeDeeps 3-22-03
Directions: On RT.101 heading east just past East Killingly RD. and
before the welcome to Rhode Island sign there is a pull off on your
right pull over and park. heading west just before East Killingly
RD.cross over and park in the little pull off and park.Walk between the
rocks and you will see a little stone wall to the left,near the end of
this wall is two big oak trees growing from the same trunk and one
splits off about 10 feet up.Even with this tree on the other side of the
wall buried in the wall down low is the R.I box.Take a moment to walk up
to the top of the hill.This pine grove used to be nice and clean and
filled with picnic tables and built in fire places,my parents used to
take me here when I was a kid (Lenny) it's too bad some people have to
ruin everything.The NeeDeeps
