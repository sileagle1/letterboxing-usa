Solstice Dance Letterbox
Willard Brook State Park
Ashby,MA  Middlesex County
Planted by Deanne Bellefeuille-Besnia and Patty Finneron
June 21, 2002
 
Willard Brook State Park is located on Route 119 in Ashby, MA.  Enter through the main gate. In summer there is a $5.00 parking fee.  The path you seek is at the far end of the parking lot and has a chain across the entrance. It is a blue blazed trail.  Follow the path up the hill.  There will be picnic tables on your left.  You will go around a large rock in the path as you enter the woods. Stay on the path as it winds it's way through pines and laurel.  When you get to the fallen log in the path stay alert, your treasure is close at hand.  Look to the left in the woods and you will see rocks roughly in the formation of a semi-circle. Step off the path and find the Solstice Dance letterbox nestled under the back of a flat topped rock with a removable smaller rock in front of it.
