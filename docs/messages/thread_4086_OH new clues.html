Mystery Hiker 2002
Near Yellow Springs,Green County,OH
Difficulty: easy
Placed :January 3,2002

Best parking is a the Wingo Picnic Area of John Bryan Park.
Follow the signs from Yellow Springs for the park,68 North from town to
343 just outside town,then 370 to the entrance of the park. Go thru the
park toward and past the day lodge to Wingo.
Park closes at dusk, dogs are to be on a leash.
Coordinates for parking are N 39.79054 and W 83 .85640.

Take the gravel path from the parking area south for (23) paces ( 1
pace=2 steps,about 5 feet ) . On your left you will see (3) large logs.
