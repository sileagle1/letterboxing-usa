Utah , here they come ! We have 12 new Lettrboxes all packed and ready
to plant , all are hand carved stamps by Chuck Straub, Leader of the
Pack, Steve and Heidi, The Needeeps, Bookworm , Lobsta Lady, Slo Camel

called GIFTS FROM CONNECTICUT and numberd with the name from each box.
So as they are found, the number will tell how many more you have to
hunt !!
Hope to see you in Utah and a big big thank you to all the CT
letterboxers.
