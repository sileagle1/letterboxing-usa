I thought I would jump in here as I'm sure iohC is very busy gluing
some of the stuff that needs it. If you go to "Member Services" you
will find at the bottom a line for "Letterbox Status". You can disable
or enable the function by either clicking on this line. The flag on
the clue sheet lets a placer list the status of their clue. The flag
also allows a finder to list the date of the find or attempt. If the
Flags are a nuisance you can disable. I found it easier to record
finds on those clues not hosted by LBNA. In those cases I didn't have
to go to the clue sheet to record, but could record on the list of
clues for that area as those flags were on the larger list. The only
thing I have yet to figure out is the red markers. I guess it helps a
placer find their own clue sheets?
