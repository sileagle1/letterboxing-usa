We've got Bugs! We've got Parasites! We've got a Postal Lb. that's
really a Hitchhiker Hostel ready to make the rounds to a mailbox near
you. The "Who's Got a Bug Up Their Butt Hitchhiker Hostel" PLB that's
been . . . *GASP* . . . infested with the Little Parasites That Could
from Leapin' Lizards.

This Letterbox Hostel comes equipped with Hitchhikers .... and right
now there is a parasitic convention going on inside!

Leapin' Lizards and I hunkered down, plotted and planned, and now are
just itchin' to bring you a whole new concept in Postal Letterboxes
(and Parasites)!

HOW PARASITES WORK:
Parasites are little critters that infect the host Letterbox logbook
and Hitchhiker logbook (with its stamp impression) and then moves on
with the Hitchhiker.
- Or, when dropped off in a HH Hostel the Parasite infects _ALL_ the
HH in the Lb. while waiting for the next ride out.
- The Parasite always infects every logbook it comes in contact with.
- The Parasite can only move with Hitch Hikers (sort of a HH to a HH).

HOW THE HITCHHIKER HOSTEL WORKS:
-It starts out salted with Hitchhikers and parasites.
-The first 5 people on the list get to stamp the parasites but can't
take one (this way more people get to see them) even if they exchange
Hitchhikers.
-The next 5 people on the list each get to take one if they have a HH
to exchange.
-The 6th and last parasite will always stay with the HitchHiker Hostel.
-You can only take a HH if you leave a HH; exchange one for one. If
you don't have one (HH) then treat this PLB like any other PLB with
the "Who's Got a Bug Up Their Butt" PLB stamp, exchange stamp images
and mail it on to the next person on the list.
-You are encouraged to make a Hitchhiker, even a Parasite, for the
occasion, so start plotting.
-The "Who's Got a Bug Up Their Butt HitchHiker Hostel" PLB can only
stay for a day or two because this box intends to find out just
exactly Who's Got a Bug Up Their Butt !

There is limited space so sign up now at: catbead@catbeadmischief.com

First come, first bitten!
