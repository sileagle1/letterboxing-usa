Please replace this portion of the clues:
"From the disk below the steps on the trail, take 80 paces North to
a multi-trunked tree to your Southwest."

With this correction portion:
"From the disk below the steps on the trail, take 85 paces North to
a multi-trunked tree to the West."

dvn2r ckr

> Cold Moon Over Puget Sound Letterbox
> Placed By: DVN2R CKR
> Please send comments regarding this letterbox to: blee@p...
>
> Start at:
> Cormorant Park
> intersection of Chapman Loop with itself
> Steilacoom, WA
> Pierce County
>
> From I-5 North & South: Take Exit 119 `Steilacoom/DuPont Rd.
> Exit'. Turn west off the highway and at the first 4 way
> intersection (Steilacoom Hwy) after the railroad tracks, turn
> right. Continue on this road for 6 miles. You will be driving
> through North Fort Lewis at this point. Eventually the road will
> take you through a residential area that is the quaint seaside
> village of Steilacoom. Immediately turn left (West) on Cormorant
> Drive (the 1st residential street once you start down the hill in
> the village of Steilacoom about the time you can see Puget Sound
in
> front of you)this turn will happen right after you see the speed
> limit reduced from 45 mph to 35 mph. Continue straight until you
> reach a stop sign (the intersection of Chapman Loop with itself).
> The park is immediately on your right. Park legally where you can
> and your trek will begin at the playground.
>
> Average roundtrip time for the letterbox: 5 minutes
> Roundtrip distance: maybe 1/16 mile, depending on where you park
> Degree of difficulty: EASY but includes stairs or a slope on a
> gravel trail!!! Suitable for very small children and pets.
Other:
> Bring a picnic lunch and enjoy time at the park or visit one of
the
> nice eating establishments in Steilacoom.
>
> Cold Moon Over Puget Sound Letterbox
>
> Placed: 7 January 2003
> December's Full Moon is also known as the `Cold Moon'. This stamp
> was inspired by a front page photo in one of the local newspapers
in
> December. Enjoy!
>
> CLUES: Slide down the slide. From the disk below the steps on
the trail, take 80 paces North to a multi-trunked tree to your
> Southwest. Your treasure is hidden in a nice little cubby hole
> behind the tree. Please stamp in discreetlythis is a popular
trail
> for the locals!!! Use the markers enclosed within for a multi-
> colored image similar to the actual photo!
>
> NOTE: For a nice longer stroll with multiple letterboxes(4), you
> can link this letterbox trek with those of Saltar's Point and the
> Old Town Steilacoom Series. To do this, after Cold Moon, continue
> North on the trail and continue on the road (1st Ave) until it's
> Northern most terminus. This will be the entrance to Saltar's
Point
> Beach. Then after that letterbox, head East until you meet Union
> Ave. Turn North until you bump into Pioneer Park which is where
the
> Old Town Steilacoom Series begins. Linking all of these
letterboxes
> would make it about a 3-1/2 mile casual walk through a nice,
quaint
> village that offers great views of Puget Sound and some of its
> islands.
