From: Randy Hall (randy@mapsurfer.com) | Date: 2002-08-30 08:34:57 UTC-04:00

> can you post a picture of the stamp - might make it easier to ID

Don't do this.

We can only hope that it is part of some elaborate, intricate scheme
that is just begininning to reveal itself. :-) Even if it isn't,
you shouldn't post images of other people's stamps unless you have
their permission. That is just plain rude, IMHO ...

If you find something unusual, imagine first the possibility that
it could be part of someone's game. It prolly isn't, but there are
things going on, and the people behind them often don't chit chat
about them. I think they think its more fun that way ... (at least
that is what my sources tell me).

> Here's an ethical Q. If I were checking on one of boxes and there was a HH
> there can I take it.

IMHO, you can take it, but if you don't want to, don't.
