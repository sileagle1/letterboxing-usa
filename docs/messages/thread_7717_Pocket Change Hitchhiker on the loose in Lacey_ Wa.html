Just finished launching our first Hitch Hiker called Pocket
Change.
It is in its own micro box that should be small enough to fit inside
most host boxes.
It is a hybrid letterbox/cache box: there is a coin inside the box
along with the stamp and log book. When found, take the coin to
keep and replace it with another coin. Log the type of coin and
it's date and mint mark (D for Denver, S for San Francisco, etc.)
in the log book, stamp your personal signature stamp and any
comments.
Please email us when you find our hitch hiker and keep us
posted on its travels.

Happy Hunting,

Nail Family
