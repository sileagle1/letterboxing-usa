National Park letterboxing

From: Drew Family (drewclan@aol.com) | Date: 2009-03-31 21:54:37 UTC
Just a reminder from your friendly letterboxing.org webmasters: please do not post clues to letterboxes placed in National Parks lands unless you have specific permission from the local park rangers to do so. It is possible to work with the NPS in planting letterboxes if you do it out in the open, but in general it is much safer for LbNA if you "plant" only virtual boxes. Thank you very much, Jay in CT
