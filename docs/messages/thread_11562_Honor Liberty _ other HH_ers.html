Last Sept 12 I put out a different kind of HH. This one was set up so that it's current location would be posted so that it's 1 year travel would hit many boxes. A note was put along with it that included that info. It apparently stalled out somewhere in Ohio ? My feeling about that HH or any other HH or letterbox for that matter is that once you plant it or launch it you have no control . You only have a responsibility to keep it going or in the case of a letterbox to take care of it if it needs repair or putting in a new book if it needs it,even if it is some other letterboxer's box. If the HH gets lost in the shuffle ,gets stalled somewhere , goes missing , or is being passed along without notification don't worry about it - carve another stamp. Don
