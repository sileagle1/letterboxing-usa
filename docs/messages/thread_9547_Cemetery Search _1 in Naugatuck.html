I just wanted to let anyone who might be interested in searching for this box that sometimes in the winter the gates to the cemetery are closed and locked. There is a sneaking back way if you happen to be in this area and just have to have this stamp I can certainly give you exact directions to the box. Or feel free to email me and I will be happy to let you know if the cemetery is open, as I can see the gates from my house.
This is an easy letterboxing walk for the cold weather.....for those of you going through letterboxing withdrawal!!
 
Thanks for visiting and happy letterboxing.
