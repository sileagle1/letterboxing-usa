Hey All!

Thanks to some diligent letterboxers giving me a heads up (i.e.,
CampFire Lady, Carie of Lennens 3, Wild Woman and She Bear of 3) John
and I hiked on the Dogwood Trail and checked out Who Let The Dogs Out!

Needless to say the reports are true and confirmed. My easy clues had
turned difficult (thanks to Mother Nature being herself and a missing
mutt). With specific landmarks gone and the replaced letterbox of Dog
#2 once again history I have revised the clues. This is now a Who Let
The Dogs Out - 3 Dog Night. In other words. Dog #2 will not be
replaced AGAIN and I've eliminated landmarks that no longer exist.

Hopefully, my clues are pathetically easy once more and people will be
able to have fun looking for this series without scratching their heads
in confusion and dismay.

Thank you!!!

Best regards,
