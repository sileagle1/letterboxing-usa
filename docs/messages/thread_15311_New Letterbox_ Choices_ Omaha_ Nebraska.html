Choices

Hidden by Scott Redd, August 24, 2003.

Level of difficulty: Easy

This letterbox is part of the Big Papio Trail letterbox series.

The Location

Omaha, Douglas County, Nebraska. Richard & Annette Bloch Cancer
Survivor Park

The Richard & Annette Bloch Cancer Survivor Park is located near One
Pacific Place at 105th and Pacific Avenue. This park is dedicated to
fighting and surviving cancer. Learn more about the Cancer Survivor
Parks at the following link:
http://www.blochcancer.org/virtual_park/welcome_vp.html

The Clues

* Locate the Party of Eight. Traverse the maze from start to finish.

* Walk south to the Tower of Light. Nearby stands a Muted Sentinel
(unless he's been re-voiced)

* Walk north. Seek and accept support from your left.

* Continue north until you find a qualified doctor, but stop a few
steps short.

* Have a seat with your back to the setting sun.

* Actually, you need a second opinion. Try sitting with your back to
the rising sun.

* Most choices are yours to make freely. This Choice, like iron to a
magnet, is already made, and lies just beneath you.
