Bluebird and I had a fun day Sunday driving a big circle and picking up
10 boxes. Four of them shall remain nameless as they were mysteries, -
we love them - Peggy Dow , pretty place ,pretty stamp, pretty windy !!
Indian in a Cupboard,!! Well, bring your River Raft !! Actually it was
only a stream, but fast moving and coming from a magical place that even
with her Headlamp, Bluebird could not see the back of the cave. No
problem but lots of giggles. Read the Log Book.!! The Bike trail,
which we walked to off Silver Lane. Nice wind there too and there was a
Walk A Thonn going on . We were the only ones going in that direction,
so it looked as if we were all finished and on the way back. !! Got the
box and it is hidden so great. No one except a boxer would pick up
trash. Music Woman's house box she so kindly left out for us as she was
in church praying for us. Her cat Sammy would not come to the window,
but we tried, so did stamp into his box too. A little cheating there
Got to finish up two bonus clues that we had before Christmas and the
snow kept us away. All in all A great day and thank you Bluebird for
driving into places that I dare not drive !! She is a Cowboy !!
Smile, Butterfly
