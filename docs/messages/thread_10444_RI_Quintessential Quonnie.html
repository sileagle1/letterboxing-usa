Quick! Quipping quietly, a quixotic quid-pro-quo in a quadralateral
quontainer quakes near the quai. Questions? Quizes? Qualms? Quaff
your queenly quality without quandry or quashing the queers! Quench
your quest near the quahog's quarry. Quirky? Don't quit! All a-
quiver? Make your quota! Bring quinine, quince, quarterhorses. Don't
quail! Don't quarrel! Don't take the QEII! A quarter of the
querulous quadrapeds quaver. It's quartzy, I quip. It's a quilt of
quizzical quotes, quite? QED!

Quonochontaug, replaced this morning somewhere in Rhode Island. This

1999 and gone missing, perhaps taken by space aliens or quirky FBI
agents or even more mysterious smoking men but now back in
circulation. Good luck!
