What sort of rubber stamps should we send to Dartmoor, UK, to be used in letterboxes that commemorate the formation of the Letterbox USA group? You may vote for as many of the ideas as you like.
----

Please select one or more of the following:

o map of USA listing our founding year "1998"
o tribute to Prayer Rock, 1st US letterbox
o dedication to Smithsonian article that introduced the hobby
o a stamp for each Region of the country
o tribute to our online virtual letterboxing community
o "thank you" to our friends in the UK
o humorous "L-USA invades Dartmoor" stamp
o copy of stamp from (or a tribute to) the first box in each of first 13 states


by going to the following Web form:

http://www.egroups.com/vote?id=920016131846

Thank you!
