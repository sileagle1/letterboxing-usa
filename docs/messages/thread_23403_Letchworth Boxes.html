There *were* two boxes in Letchworth; they are both gone.

One has been missing for about 2 1/2 years due to trail changes and
paving on the perimeter of the park. The other, which we were able

the area and bragged about it.

We haven't put anything there due to the volume of
people/geocachers/destructive noxers. It's a shame because it is
such a beautiful place! Very middle-earthy. Definitely worth a
visit even though there aren't any LBs.
