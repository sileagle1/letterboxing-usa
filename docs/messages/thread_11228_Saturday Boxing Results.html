Saturday Boxing Results

From: Eric Polk (ericpolk@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-03-16 10:37:04 UTC-08:00
Braved the pounding rain and went out to find the Cal State Long Beach boxes.  We found two : '49er Mascot' and 'Catching Some Sun in the Quad'.
 
We tried to find the Real 49er but had no luck.  Ironically, when we were looking for the Real 49er, we ended up within feet of 'Catching Some Sun in Quad'.  We had the wrong number in mind when we were looking for Custer's number and that brought us almost right to the Sun box.  A phone call to my Mom gave us the number that was more likely to be Custer's.  For anyone looking for this box, do your research BEFORE you are standing in the pouring rain, ankle deep in a puddle holding a soggy piece of paper.
 
As for the Real 49er, we think we were in the right place.  The three adjoined metal cones on a road was a good clue.  That was what made us think we were in the right spot.  There was an open gate right next to the spot we thought was the location so someone may have been there recently.  We didn't find the box and were getting drenched and hungry so we called off the search.
