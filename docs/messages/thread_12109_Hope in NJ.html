Yep you're going to be hooked. =-) My sister got me hooked last
summer/fall, and I do love it. Even when you go out to find a box
and perhaps it's not there or you can't quite get the clues figured
out, don't give up because there are plenty mroe out there or you

people along the way. Be creative and learn from others, because
there's so much to learn, and it's so fun. Some people create these
awesome elaborate boxes while others like myself are very basic. I
love finding them all and getting different ideas.

Enjoy!! See you on the trail and Welcome to the adventure of
letterboxing!!
