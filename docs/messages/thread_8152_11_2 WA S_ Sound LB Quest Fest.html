> (ranging from extremely interesting history, many trails, a lake, a
> cemetery, an old farm with silo, playground with playing fields,
> trees, old mental asylum buildings, model boating, picnic area, you
> name it). It is home to many ducks, geese, rabbits, fish, snakes
> (we found them yesterday), blackberries and myriad trees of NW
> origin. We've also seen Bald Eagles and Mt Rainier (weather
> dependent) while visiting. Historic Ft Steilacoom and Western WA
> State Hospital are located directly across the street. This is all
> great fodder for some interesting letterboxes!!!
