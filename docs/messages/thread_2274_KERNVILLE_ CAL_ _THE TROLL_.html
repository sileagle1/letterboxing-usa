KERNVILLE, CAL, "THE TROLL"

From: Gwen & Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2001-04-30 16:50:24 UTC-07:00
The sticky box holding the "Troll of Kernville" was planted on 4/28/01 by Don & Gwen (Kern County, California)  The best time to search for this box would be in the evening as it is very exposed.
 Find Deb's Bench in Riverside Park.  As you are sitting there contemplating nature, take out your compass.  The box is at 53 degrees magnetic.  Before you leave Deb's bench count the number of letters in her full name.    Find the entrance to the "Troll's" domain at 20 degrees magnetic.  Walk to the north side and continue east to the end.  From the north east end count the railing supports towards the middle of the structure.   The box is hidden behind the railing support that corresponds to the number of letters in Deb's full name. Careful, now, don't lean over too far!  The box is very small (1" x 3"x 4").  There is no book nor stamp pad....just the stamp.  The box is silver in color and is attached to the location with Velcro.  This is a very busy location during the warmer months due to the rafting and kayaking season.  Be discrete when you seek this stamp.  Have fun!. 
Deb would have loved this game!
