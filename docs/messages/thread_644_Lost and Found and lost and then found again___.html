From: Thom Cheney (tcgrafx@imagina.com) | Date: 1999-09-05 20:45:44 UTC-07:00
Weird news here in little Dartmoor (Rock Creek, Oregon). I was out at
the creek throwing rocks and sticks with the boys & decided to see if
anyone had signed in to Rock Creek #1 , now that I have replaced it...
again! Mitch actually dropped the box off for me... I had been trying
to get my "mother of all sheds" complete among other things... So...
anyway, as we were sampling blackberries & noticing that Mitch was the
only one who had been by, lo and behold, the original box was sighted in
the bushes nearby. We opened it and found that the little so-and-so who
took it had returned all of the pages that had stamps (thank goodness)
and 2 of the 3 stamps I carved at one time or another for that box. The
otter has returned!! As has the heron. Unfortunately they took the
goose stamp and all of the rest of the little notepad, but for some
reason returned the rest. I was really happy to get back the stamped
pages, Daniel Servatius had visited with a beautiful carved stamp by
Susan Davis and Mitch had been by at least twice & probably three times.

I have decided to move this box to a less tempting spot. I'm kinda
ticked off about that, because the location is really perfect for a
letterbox. I have a sneaking suspicion who is taking & returning the
box, but since I have no proof, I can do nothing except move along.
Anyway.... the box has been relocated & when I dream up some
appropriately clever clues, I'll get Mitch to change them.
