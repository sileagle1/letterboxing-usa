From: Steve and Heidi (stephenholyfield@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2004-09-01 05:53:24 UTC
Just a quick reminder as we quickly approach 2000 members on the list
(1968 right now):

* Read the FAQ's
* Check out the links section
* Ask questions! (If you think its a dumb question, then email us
with it!)
* Check out some of the other groups - There's a new member list,
regional lists, state lists, a hitch hiker list (traveller), and a
Postal Letterbox list. Each of these have a little different spin
on this "big list" and you will certainly find some different
information on those lists.

And like Mark says, have fun and get out there and box!

-Steve, Heidi, and Madras in Gales Ferry CT
