Title: Bearfoot in the Park. Placed 7-19-03 by Salamander.Location:
Paint Creek State Park, near Bainbridge, Ohio. Difficulty: Easy.
Start at the large parking area below the spillway. CLUES: At one
end of the parking area, there is a large sign reading "WARNING-
BLAST OF HORN...etc.". Take the trail past this sign. At 46 paces
down the trail, you should see a large boulder to the right of the
trail. The box is hidden at the base of the northwest side of the
boulder, under two smaller rocks. Note: this is the second version
of this box--the first was in a completely different location which
became unsuitable due to recent new landscaping.
