Dear Letterboxers
   Yesterday, we did the Three Score Letterboxes and we were upset to see that others before us had , in what may have been their zeal to find the letterbox, collapsed a portion of a magnificent old stone wall on the Preserve. It was most disheartening to see. We tried to pick up some of the stones but had great difficulty- we are not spring chickens. Please remember that it is imperative that we
always leave a place , not the same, but better than when we found it-  that means ourselves as well as the grounds.   Thanks, Nancy
