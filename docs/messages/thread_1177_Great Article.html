[John De Wolf] The standard is not to include an inkpad.  The asumption is that the letterboxer brings his/her own (maybe several for multi-colored effects).  Many CT letterboxers have been generous by placing inkpads in their boxes, but I think we shouldn't expect that to be the case in all boxes.  In addition to the incremental cost to the box, there's the issue of how long the pad will stay good in outdoor conditions.  How well do they freeze. etc...
