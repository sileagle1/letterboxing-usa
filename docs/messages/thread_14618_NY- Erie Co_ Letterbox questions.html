From: thenextrushdie (jelybean@stny.rr.com) | Date: 2003-07-28 23:04:46 UTC
Hi Delerium, I am the owner of five of these and have found the
other two. As far as I know they are all still there, though I
found Sinking Ponds about a year ago. They are all fairly easy,
short hikes.

Easiest and shortest would be:
Buffalo Wings, if you park in the right place
Charles Burchfield
Chestnut Ridge (at least going down the steps)

After that, the others are a little longer walks but mostly level or
with minimal slopes. Eternal Flame may be the longest and most
challenging, probably still less than a mile but with a decent hill
or two, but worth the hike to see the flaming waterfall.

Email me off list if you want more details: jelybean@stny.rr.com
