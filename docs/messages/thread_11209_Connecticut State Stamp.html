Coventry Conn. Rating Easy
Planted by The NeeDeeps 3-15-03
Hi this is the very first box planted from our State Stamp collection.On
the Ct. state quarter it features a charter oak so we thought it would
only be fitting to plant this box near one.On south st. in Coventry go
past the Hale homestead,down the road a little more is the Strong Porter
House directly across the street is the place to pull in and park.The
same starting place as some of the best Letterboxes around like Charter
oak's Great Grandson,Women of the Revolutionary War and Pumped up. After
you park your car walk down the trail soon you will see a stone wall on
your right,follow the wall until it comes to a corner.Take a moment to
look at the big charter oak off to your right.Now on your left you will
notice that there is another stone wall that also came to a corner,in
the corner of the stonewall on your left behind a stone door is the
CT.State Stamp.please rehide well.The NeeDeeps
