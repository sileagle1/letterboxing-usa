John and I went out today to check on "Who Let the Dogs Out!" and to
replace Dog #2. Since we were in the vicinity, it seemed like the
perfect time to finally hunt down the "What's For Dinner" series.

just had a blast. This would be a good one for families for sure.

A HUGE THANK YOU TO FUNHOG for placing these. What a hoot! We had a
really enjoyable afternoon thanks to these letterboxes!
