Whale Trail Tales from New England

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-15 21:33:07 UTC


We really liked Jay Drew's idea of planting boxes to go along with the various
whale sculptures temporarily being displayed along the coast from Old Lyme,
CT to Boston, MA. So, we have picked out a whale to plant a box near too!
Jay choose "Health and Whaleness" at Flanders Fish Market in CT (great fish
and chips) for his original whale box (named Free Welly), and now we've
decided to take "Get Whale Soon" in Westerly, RI. Hope some of those other
whales get "adopted" by boxers in the area, and then we could have quite a
whale fest here in southern New England.

Thanks, Jay, for sharing this whale of a project :-)
